---

## Girardian Framework Reminder

A scapegoat cycle is NOT mere negative sentiment. It requires all four stages:

1. **Diffuse anxiety** — the market is stressed but the cause is unclear
2. **Convergence** — blame focuses onto a specific, identifiable target
3. **Collective blame** — the community unifies around its opposition to the target
4. **Cohesion through shared opposition** — the in-group identity solidifies against the scapegoat

Where you see unusual alliance formation (people who normally disagree suddenly agreeing), disproportionate emotional responses, and language like "we all agree," "finally someone said it," or "they should be banned," you are watching Stage 2–3 unfold.

**Convergence Mechanism:** The anxiety crystallized around a specific target pattern: the "overnight consultant" who learned from YouTube and now charges enterprise rates. **Blame Narrative:** "These people are exploiting real anxiety. They learned ChatGPT prompting for free on YouTube and now they're packaging it as a $5,000 course. **Urgency:** HIGH. DeepSeek's January 2026 release accelerated this cycle dramatically.
