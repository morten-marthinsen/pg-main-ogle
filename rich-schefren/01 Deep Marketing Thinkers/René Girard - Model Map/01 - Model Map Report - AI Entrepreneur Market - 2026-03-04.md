# Model Map Report — AI Entrepreneur Market
## Who Online Entrepreneurs ($250K–$5M/year) Are Actually Modeling Themselves After
### Produced 2026-03-04 | Girard Model Map Skill v1.0.0

---

> **Market Definition:** Online entrepreneurs and business owners generating $250K–$5M/year, interested in AI-powered business growth, marketing strategy, and operational leverage. Sophisticated buyers who have already tried multiple courses and programs. The Strategic Profits / ZenithPro / Force Multiplier buyer.
