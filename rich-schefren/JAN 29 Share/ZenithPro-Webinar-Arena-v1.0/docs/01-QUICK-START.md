## You Just Installed Something Powerful

You now have access to the methodologies of six legendary webinar experts, encoded into AI skills that you can use instantly. Let's get you creating better webinars in the next 5 minutes.

## Your First Competition (5 minutes)

### Step 1: Open Claude Code CLI

Open your terminal and start Claude Code:
```
claude
```

### Step 2: Start the Arena

Type:
```
/webinar-arena
```

### Step 3: Provide Your Brief

When prompted, provide your webinar challenge:

```
Brief: Create a webinar selling a $997 online course to small business owners
Price: $997
Market sophistication: 3
Format: Automated
Rounds: 1
```

### Step 4: Watch the Competition

The Arena will:
1. Have all 6 experts + Synthesis draft their webinar structure simultaneously
2. Have each version critiqued by its methodology's critic
3. Have all experts revise based on feedback
4. Have the Marketplace Judge declare a winner
5. Show you all 7 versions

## Understanding the Results

### The Winner

The Marketplace Judge declared a winner based on predicted marketplace performance. The winner is the webinar structure most likely to convert if presented to real buyers.

### The Scores

Each entry is scored on 7 criteria:
- Stopping Power (20%)
- Believability (15%)
- Desire Activation (20%)
- Objection Handling (15%)
- Offer Clarity (10%)
- Risk Reversal (10%)
- Creative Strategy (10%)

### Your Choice

You're not required to use the winner. Pick the version that resonates most with your market and style.

## Context Variables That Matter

When providing your brief, these variables shape the competition:

| Variable | Options | Effect |
|----------|---------|--------|
| Price | <$500, $500-$2K, $2K-$10K, >$10K | Affects expert biases and weights |
| Sophistication | 1-5 | Affects complexity level |
| Format | Live, Automated, Hybrid | Affects delivery recommendations |
| Product Type | Info, Transformation, Service, Physical | Affects approach |
| Goal | Conversion, Relationship, Both | Affects evaluation weights |
