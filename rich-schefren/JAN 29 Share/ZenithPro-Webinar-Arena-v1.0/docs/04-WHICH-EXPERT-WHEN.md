# Which Expert When
## A Decision Tree for Picking Webinar Methodologies

---

| If your situation is... | Start with... |
|------------------------|---------------|
| Automated funnel, mass market | **Fladlien** |

## Decision Tree

### Start Here: What's Your Price Point?

**Under $500?**
-> Fladlien is your default. His efficiency and commitment building work best when the buying decision is low-risk.

**$500 - $2,000?**
-> Multiple experts compete well here. Consider:
- Fladlien for automated
- Brunson for live
- Kennedy for precision

**$2,000 - $10,000?**
-> This is where methodology matters most:
- Kern for complete campaigns
- Brunson for live paradigm shifts
- Cage for sophisticated buyers

**Over $10,000?**
-> High-ticket has specific requirements:
- Joon for live events with table rush
- Kern for relationship-based selling
- Cage for deep psychology work

## Expert Strengths Deep Dive

### Fladlien: The Efficiency Expert

**Pick when:**
- Automated funnels
- Mass markets
- Under $500
- You need conversion NOW
- Direct response culture

**Avoid when:**
- Sophisticated B2B
- Relationship-first markets
- High-ticket transformation
- Markets that hate "selling"

---

### Cage: The Psychologist

**Pick when:**
- Sophisticated buyers
- Markets that have "seen everything"
- Need deep understanding of desires
- Retention is key
- Long-term customer value

**Avoid when:**
- Need quick conversion
- Mass markets
- Low-ticket offers
- Audiences with short attention spans

---

### Brunson: The Energy Machine

**Pick when:**
- Live presentations
- Paradigm shift required
- Emotional connection matters
- Community-building offers
- Belief change needed

**Avoid when:**
- Automated webinars
- Sophisticated markets that hate hype
- You can't deliver energy
- Conservative industries

---

### Kern: The Campaign Architect

**Pick when:**
- You need complete system (pre/during/post)
- Relationship matters more than quick sale
- Markets hate pushy tactics
- Long-term value focus
- Can invest in full campaign

**Avoid when:**
- Need just the webinar
- Quick turnaround required
- Low budget for production
- One-off promotions

---

### Joon: The Event Master

**Pick when:**
- Live stage presentations
- High-ticket ($5K+)
- Table rush mechanics possible
- Transformation-based offers
- Group buying dynamics

**Avoid when:**
- Automated webinars
- Solo online watching
- Under $2K offers
- No physical/hybrid element

---

### Kennedy: The Precision Engineer

**Pick when:**
- Any format (media-agnostic)
- Tight timing required
- Professional audiences
- Efficiency valued
- Checkout prevention critical

**Avoid when:**
- Need emotional dynamism
- Transformation-heavy offers
- Casual, entertainment-seeking audiences
- You can't follow tight scripts

---

## When to Run the Arena

### Always Run Arena When:
- Multiple experts could work
- You're unsure which methodology fits
- Stakes are high (major launch)
- You want to learn what works for YOUR market
- You have time for multiple rounds

### Skip Arena When:
- You know exactly which expert fits
- You've run similar briefs before and know the winner
- Time pressure requires quick single-methodology output
