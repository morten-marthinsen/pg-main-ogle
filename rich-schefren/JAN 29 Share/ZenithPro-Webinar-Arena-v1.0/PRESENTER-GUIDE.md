## HANDLING QUESTIONS

### "How long does it take to run?"

"A single round with all seven competitors takes about 15-20 minutes. Three rounds takes longer. This is thoroughness, not a bug - you're getting seven complete webinar approaches, each critiqued and revised."

### "What if I don't like the winner?"

"Override it. Tell Claude which one you prefer and why. The system logs your override and learns from it. Your judgment shapes future competitions."

### "Can I use this for [specific thing]?"

"If it's a webinar, yes. Live, automated, hybrid. Any price point. Any market. The experts have different strengths - that's why they compete. The right expert rises for each situation."

### "What if I want to use JUST Fladlien's approach?"

"You can. Just call `/webinar-fladlien` directly instead of running the Arena. Each expert is available as a standalone skill. The Arena is for when you want to see all approaches."

### "Do I need to know anything about webinars already?"

"No. The experts know. Your job is to provide a good brief - who you're selling to, what the offer is, the price point. The clearer your brief, the better the output."

### File Locations After Install

| What | Where |
|------|-------|

| Agents | `~/.claude/agents/webinar-*` |

### The 7-Phase Competition Flow

1. **Setup** - Create project folder, parse brief
2. **Drafting** - All 7 competitors create simultaneously
3. **Critique** - Each critic evaluates its expert's draft
4. **Revision** - All experts revise based on critique
5. **Judgment** - Marketplace judge declares winner
6. **Learning** - Win records updated, patterns logged
7. **Evolution** - Skills updated, spawning checked
