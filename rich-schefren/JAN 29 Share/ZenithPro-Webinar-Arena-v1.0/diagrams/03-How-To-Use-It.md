# How To Use The Arena

Your first competition in 5 minutes.

---

## The Simple Version

```mermaid
flowchart TD
OPEN[Open Claude Code] --> TYPE[Type: webinar-arena]
TYPE --> BRIEF[Provide your brief]
BRIEF --> WATCH[Watch competition run]
WATCH --> PICK[Pick your favorite version]
```

That is it. Four steps.

---

## Step 1: Start The Arena

```mermaid
flowchart TD
CLAUDE[In Claude Code] --> SLASH[Type: webinar-arena]
SLASH --> READY[Arena is ready]
```

---

## Step 2: Provide Your Brief

Tell the Arena what you need:

```mermaid
flowchart TD
BRIEF[Your Brief] --> WHAT[What you are selling]
WHAT --> PRICE[Price point]
PRICE --> WHO[Who is buying]
WHO --> FORMAT[Live or Automated]
FORMAT --> ROUNDS[How many rounds]
```

**Example Brief:**
- Brief: Webinar selling $997 course to business owners
- Price: $997
- Market sophistication: 3
- Format: Automated
- Rounds: 1

---

## Step 3: Watch The Competition

```mermaid
flowchart TD
RUN[Arena Runs] --> DRAFT[All 7 draft]
DRAFT --> CRITIQUE[Critics review]
CRITIQUE --> REVISE[All 7 revise]
REVISE --> JUDGE[Judge picks winner]
```

---

## Step 4: Get Your Results

```mermaid
flowchart TD
DONE[Competition Done] --> V1[Version 1: Fladlien]
V1 --> V2[Version 2: Cage]
V2 --> V3[Version 3: Brunson]
V3 --> V4[Version 4: Kern]
V4 --> V5[Version 5: Joon]
V5 --> V6[Version 6: Kennedy]
V6 --> V7[Version 7: Synthesis]
V7 --> WINNER[Winner Declared]
```

---

## What You Receive

| Output | What It Is |
|--------|-----------|
| 7 webinar versions | Complete structures from each expert |
| Rankings | Scored 1-7 with explanations |
| Judge reasoning | Why the winner won |
| Learning briefs | What each expert learned |

---

## Your Choice

```mermaid
flowchart TD
WINNER[Judge Declares Winner] --> AGREE{Do you agree?}
AGREE -->|Yes| USE[Use the winner]
AGREE -->|No| PICK[Pick a different one]
PICK --> OVERRIDE[System records your choice]
OVERRIDE --> CALIBRATE[Future judgments improve]
```

You always have the final say.

---

## Multiple Rounds

For important projects, run multiple rounds:

```mermaid
flowchart TD
R1[Round 1] --> L1[Everyone learns]
L1 --> R2[Round 2: All are better]
R2 --> L2[Everyone learns more]
L2 --> R3[Round 3: Even better]
R3 --> BEST[Best version ever]
```

Each round makes everyone better.
