# How Judgment Works

The Marketplace Judge picks winners based on one question:

> "Which would generate the most revenue if presented to the same audience?"

---

## Not Methodology - Marketplace

```mermaid
flowchart TD
WRONG[Does NOT Ask] --> M1[Did they follow rules?]
M1 --> M2[Is it technically correct?]
M2 --> M3[Did they use all frameworks?]
```

```mermaid
flowchart TD
RIGHT[DOES Ask] --> R1[Would people stay?]
R1 --> R2[Would they believe?]
R2 --> R3[Would they buy?]
```

---

## The 7 Scoring Criteria

```mermaid
flowchart TD
SCORE[100 Points Total] --> C1[Stopping Power: 20 pts]
C1 --> C2[Desire Activation: 20 pts]
C2 --> C3[Believability: 15 pts]
C3 --> C4[Objection Handling: 15 pts]
C4 --> C5[Offer Clarity: 10 pts]
C5 --> C6[Risk Reversal: 10 pts]
C6 --> C7[Creative Strategy: 10 pts]
```

---

## Criterion 1: Stopping Power (20%)

```mermaid
flowchart TD
STOP[Stopping Power] --> Q1[Do they stay past minute 5?]
Q1 --> Q2[Does it grab attention?]
Q2 --> Q3[Would they keep watching?]
```

---

## Criterion 2: Desire Activation (20%)

```mermaid
flowchart TD
DESIRE[Desire Activation] --> Q1[Does it awaken wants?]
Q1 --> Q2[Even wants they did not know?]
Q2 --> Q3[Does it create urgency?]
```

---

## Criterion 3: Believability (15%)

```mermaid
flowchart TD
BELIEVE[Believability] --> Q1[Do they believe the promise?]
Q1 --> Q2[Do they believe it for THEM?]
Q2 --> Q3[Is it credible?]
```

---

## Criterion 4: Objection Handling (15%)

```mermaid
flowchart TD
OBJECT[Objection Handling] --> Q1[Are real objections addressed?]
Q1 --> Q2[Before they think them?]
Q2 --> Q3[Convincingly resolved?]
```

---

## Criterion 5: Offer Clarity (10%)

```mermaid
flowchart TD
OFFER[Offer Clarity] --> Q1[Do they know what to do?]
Q1 --> Q2[Is the offer crystal clear?]
Q2 --> Q3[No confusion about next step?]
```

---

## Criterion 6: Risk Reversal (10%)

```mermaid
flowchart TD
RISK[Risk Reversal] --> Q1[Is yes easier than no?]
Q1 --> Q2[Is risk removed?]
Q2 --> Q3[What do they have to lose?]
```

---

## Criterion 7: Creative Strategy (10%)

```mermaid
flowchart TD
CREATIVE[Creative Strategy] --> Q1[Right angle for this market?]
Q1 --> Q2[Fresh or tired approach?]
Q2 --> Q3[Will it stand out?]
```

## Context Adjustments

Weights shift based on your brief.

```mermaid
flowchart TD
HIGH[High Ticket over 10K] --> PLUS1[+5% Believability]
PLUS1 --> PLUS2[+5% Objection Handling]
```

```mermaid
flowchart TD
LOW[Low Ticket under 500] --> PLUS3[+5% Stopping Power]
PLUS3 --> PLUS4[+5% Desire]
```

```mermaid
flowchart TD
SOPH[Sophisticated Market] --> PLUS5[+5% Believability]
PLUS5 --> PLUS6[+5% Creative Strategy]
```

## You Can Override

```mermaid
flowchart TD
WINNER[Judge Declares Winner] --> YOU{You Agree?}
YOU -->|Yes| DONE[Use It]
YOU -->|No| OVERRIDE[Pick Different]
OVERRIDE --> RECORD[Override Recorded]
RECORD --> BETTER[Future Judgments Improve]
```

Your override helps calibrate the system.
