# Sample Brief: Mid-Ticket Coaching Program

## Copy This Into Your Arena

```
Brief: Create a webinar selling a $2,000 group coaching program for small business owners who want to implement AI into their operations. Target audience is service business owners doing $200K-$1M/year who know AI is important but feel overwhelmed by the options and don't know where to start. They're practical, time-pressed, and skeptical of hype.

Price: $2,000
Market sophistication: 3
Format: Hybrid (live with replay)
Rounds: 3
```
