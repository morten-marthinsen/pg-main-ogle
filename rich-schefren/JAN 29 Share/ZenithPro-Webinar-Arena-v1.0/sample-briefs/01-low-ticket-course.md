# Sample Brief: Low-Ticket Online Course

## Copy This Into Your Arena

```
Brief: Create a webinar selling a $197 online course teaching freelancers how to raise their rates without losing clients. Target audience is designers and developers earning $50-100/hour who feel stuck and undervalued. They've tried generic business advice but nothing worked for their creative services context.

Price: $197
Market sophistication: 3
Format: Automated
Rounds: 1
```

---

## Expected Expert Tendencies

**Fladlien** should perform well here:
- Low price point is his sweet spot
- Automated format matches his methodology
- Value stacking can make $197 feel like a steal

**Brunson** may struggle:
- His high-energy approach is harder automated
- Paradigm shifts need more engagement time

**Kennedy** should be competitive:
- His efficiency resonates with busy freelancers
