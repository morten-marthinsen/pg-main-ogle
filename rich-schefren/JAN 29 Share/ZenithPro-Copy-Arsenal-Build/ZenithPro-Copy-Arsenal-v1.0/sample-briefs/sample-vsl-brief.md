# Sample Arena Brief: VSL (Video Sales Letter)

**Use this brief to test the Arena before using your own project.**

Run: `/arena sample-vsl 1`

---

## Project: 15-Minute VSL for Positioning Workshop

### Product/Service Description

A live virtual workshop called "The Positioning Intensive" where strategic consultants:
- Clarify their unique positioning in 4 hours
- Walk away with their "Core Concept" defined
- Get templates for messaging based on their positioning
- Receive 30 days of follow-up support

**Price:** $497 (or 2 payments of $297)

### Target Market

**Primary Avatar:** Strategic consultants who:
- Downloaded the Positioning Gap Assessment
- Scored "positioning" as their primary gap
- Are ready to fix the problem (not just diagnose it)
- Have budget for investment in their business
- Are skeptical of courses but open to workshops

**Awareness Level:** Product-aware (they know what positioning is, now evaluating this solution)

### Key Constraints

- 15 minutes maximum (attention spans are short)
- Must work without showing face (slides + voiceover acceptable)
- Hook must be strong enough for paid traffic
- Include at least one "proof" element
- Clear CTA to registration page

### VSL Structure Required

1. **Hook (0-2 min):** Pattern interrupt, promise
2. **Problem (2-4 min):** Agitate the positioning gap
3. **Solution (4-7 min):** Introduce the workshop
4. **Proof (7-10 min):** Results, testimonials, credibility
5. **Offer (10-13 min):** What they get, price, bonuses
6. **Close (13-15 min):** Urgency, CTA, risk reversal

### Copy Type

VSL script

---

## Success Criteria

The winning entry will:
1. Hook within 30 seconds (no slow builds)
2. Make the problem vivid and personal
3. Position the workshop as the obvious solution
4. Include believable proof (not hypey)
5. Create urgency without being pushy
6. Make the price feel like a steal
