## Project: Strategic Consultant Lead Magnet Landing Page

### Product/Service Description

A free PDF guide called "The Positioning Gap Assessment" - a 15-question diagnostic tool that helps strategic consultants identify why their marketing isn't working despite their expertise.

The assessment reveals whether the consultant's real problem is:
- Positioning (they haven't articulated what makes them different)
- Messaging (they're speaking to the wrong beliefs)
- Or tactics (they're using the wrong channels)

### Target Market

**Primary Avatar:** Strategic consultants earning $5K-$50K/month who:
- Have deep expertise but struggle to communicate it
- Have tried multiple marketing approaches without success
- Own a "course graveyard" of marketing programs they never finished
- Feel frustrated that less qualified competitors seem to win clients easily
- Are skeptical of "marketing gurus" but open to strategic frameworks

**Awareness Level:** Problem-aware, solution-skeptical

### Price Point

Free (lead magnet) - goal is email opt-in

### Key Constraints

- Landing page only (not full sales letter)
- Must work for cold traffic from paid ads
- Headline + subhead + 3-5 bullet points + CTA
- No video required
- Must feel strategic, not "markety"

### Mandatory Elements

- Reference to the "Course Graveyard" concept
- The idea that positioning is the "cracked vessel" that makes marketing tactics fail
- Clear articulation of what they'll learn from the assessment

### Copy Type

Landing page / opt-in page

---

## Success Criteria

The winning entry will:
1. Stop the scroll (compelling headline)
2. Create identification ("this is about ME")
3. Promise a clear outcome (what they'll know after taking assessment)
4. Reduce friction (easy yes, obvious next step)
5. Feel sophisticated, not hype-y
