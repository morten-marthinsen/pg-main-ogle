## Project: 5-Email Welcome Sequence

### Product/Service Description

A welcome sequence for new subscribers who downloaded "The Positioning Gap Assessment." The sequence should:
1. Deliver the lead magnet
2. Build relationship and credibility
3. Introduce the Core Concept (positioning before marketing)
4. Seed the paid offer (a positioning workshop or course)
5. Drive to a sales page or application

### Target Market

**Primary Avatar:** Strategic consultants who just opted in because:
- They resonated with the "Course Graveyard" message
- They suspect their marketing problem is deeper than tactics
- They want to diagnose what's actually wrong

**Awareness Level:** Now solution-aware (they have the assessment), moving toward product-aware

### Key Constraints

- 5 emails over 7 days
- Each email should be 300-500 words
- Subject lines critical (these people get a lot of email)
- Must feel like it's from a peer, not a "guru"
- Balance value with sales progression

### Email Structure Required

1. **Email 1 (Day 0):** Delivery + quick win
2. **Email 2 (Day 1):** Story + credibility builder
3. **Email 3 (Day 3):** Core Concept introduction
4. **Email 4 (Day 5):** Case study / proof
5. **Email 5 (Day 7):** Offer introduction + CTA

### Copy Type

Email sequence

---

## Success Criteria

The winning entry will:
1. Have subject lines that get opened
2. Create a relationship arc across 5 emails
3. Teach while selling (not pitch-heavy)
4. Make the offer feel like a natural next step
5. Sound like one consistent voice throughout
