# Practice Brief #03: AI Implementation Sprint

**Type:** PRACTICE (Sandbox Competition)
**Price Point:** $1,997
**Context:** Launch
**Focus:** 30-day intensive implementation

---

## The Offer

### Product: AI Implementation Sprint
A 30-day intensive program to go from "curious about AI" to "AI implemented across your team."

### Positioning
"30 days from now, your team will be using AI daily—or you don't pay."

### Structure
- Week 1: Assessment + Strategy (which AI where)
- Week 2: Owner Implementation (you first)
- Week 3: Team Rollout (systematic adoption)
- Week 4: Optimization + Habit Formation
- Daily accountability check-ins
- Live hot seat calls (3x/week)
- Done-for-you prompt templates by role
- 30-day implementation guarantee

---

## Audience

### Who
- Business owners who've been "meaning to" implement AI
- Have bought AI tools but not using them
- Teams of 5-20 people
- Action-oriented, deadline-driven

### Psychology
- Frustrated with their own procrastination
- Know AI is important but keep putting it off
- Need external structure and accountability
- Respond to intensity and deadlines
- Want to just "get it done"

### Primary Objection
"30 days? That seems too fast for real implementation."

---

## Competition Instructions

Create a webinar that:
1. Leverages the intensity and speed positioning
2. Uses accountability and structure as key value
3. Overcomes "too fast" objection with methodology
4. Creates urgency (limited cohort, start date)
5. Makes the guarantee central to the offer

---

## Learning Goals

This brief teaches the system:
- Sprint/intensive program positioning
- Accountability-based offers
- Bold guarantee usage
- Cohort/start-date urgency
- Action-oriented audience psychology
