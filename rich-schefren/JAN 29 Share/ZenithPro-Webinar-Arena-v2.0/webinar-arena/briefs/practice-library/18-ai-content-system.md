# Practice Brief #18: AI Content System

**Type:** PRACTICE (Sandbox Competition)
**Price Point:** $997
**Context:** Evergreen
**Focus:** Content team operations

---

## The Offer

### Product: AI Content System
Turn your content team into a content machine—10x output without 10x effort or 10x headcount.

### Positioning
"The content system that lets a team of 3 produce like a team of 30."

### Structure
- The Content Engine Framework (systematic production)
- AI for Content Planning (topics, angles, calendars)
- AI for Content Creation (drafts, variations, formats)
- AI for Content Optimization (SEO, engagement, conversion)
- AI for Content Repurposing (one piece → many formats)
- Content workflows and SOPs
- Prompt library for content teams
- Quality control checklists
- Tool stack recommendations

---

## Audience

### Who
- Content managers and directors
- Small marketing teams
- Business owners doing their own content
- Teams producing blogs, social, email, video scripts

### Psychology
- Content demand exceeds production capacity
- Quality inconsistent when rushed
- Repurposing happens rarely (no bandwidth)
- Knows AI could help but worried about quality
- Needs system, not just prompts

### Primary Objection
"AI content is obvious/generic. Our brand needs authentic content."

---

## Competition Instructions

Create a webinar that:
1. Shows systematic content production
2. Addresses authenticity/brand voice concerns
3. Demonstrates quality with examples
4. Uses 10x metaphor with specifics
5. Positions as system, not just tool training

---

## Learning Goals

This brief teaches the system:
- Content team psychology
- Authenticity/brand objections
- System-based positioning
- Production scaling messaging
- Quality control concerns
