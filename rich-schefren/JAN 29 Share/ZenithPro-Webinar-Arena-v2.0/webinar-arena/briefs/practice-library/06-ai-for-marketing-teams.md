# Practice Brief #06: AI for Marketing Teams

**Type:** PRACTICE (Sandbox Competition)
**Price Point:** $1,497
**Context:** Evergreen
**Focus:** Vertical-specific (Marketing)

---

## The Offer

### Product: AI for Marketing Teams
Complete AI implementation program specifically for marketing teams—content, ads, email, social, analytics.

### Positioning
"Turn your marketing team into an AI-powered content machine—without replacing anyone."

### Structure
- Module 1: AI Content Creation (blogs, social, email)
- Module 2: AI Copywriting (ads, landing pages, sales copy)
- Module 3: AI Analytics & Insights
- Module 4: AI Research & Competitor Intel
- Module 5: AI Workflow Integration
- 100+ marketing-specific prompts
- Tool stack recommendations by budget
- SOPs for AI-assisted marketing
- Monthly marketing AI updates (12 months)

---

## Audience

### Who
- Marketing directors/managers
- Business owners with marketing teams
- Agency owners
- 2-10 person marketing teams

### Psychology
- Under pressure to do more with less
- Seeing competitors use AI for content
- Worried about quality control with AI
- Need brand consistency across AI outputs
- Team may be threatened by AI

### Primary Objection
"AI content is generic and doesn't match our brand voice."

---

## Competition Instructions

Create a webinar that:
1. Shows how to maintain brand voice with AI
2. Positions AI as amplifier, not replacer
3. Demonstrates dramatic productivity gains
4. Addresses quality/originality concerns
5. Uses marketing-specific examples and results

---

This brief teaches the system:
- Vertical/department-specific positioning
- Brand voice and quality concerns
- Team threat/job security psychology
- B2B marketing language
- Productivity multiplication messaging
