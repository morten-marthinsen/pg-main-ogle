# Practice Brief #17: AI for Agencies

**Type:** PRACTICE (Sandbox Competition)
**Price Point:** $1,997
**Context:** Evergreen
**Focus:** Vertical-specific (Agencies)

---

## The Offer

### Product: AI for Agencies
Complete AI implementation for marketing, creative, and digital agencies—deliver more, charge more, stress less.

### Positioning
"The agency AI playbook: produce more creative work, deliver faster timelines, and actually keep your margins."

### Structure
- Module 1: AI for Creative Production (design, copy, video)
- Module 2: AI for Client Strategy (research, planning, briefs)
- Module 3: AI for Project Management (timelines, resources)
- Module 4: AI for Client Reporting (analytics, presentations)
- Module 5: AI Pricing Strategies (value-based pricing with AI)
- Module 6: Client Communication About AI (transparency/positioning)
- Agency-specific prompt library
- Client deliverable templates
- AI disclosure/positioning scripts
- Agency owner mastermind access (6 months)

---

## Audience

### Who
- Agency owners and partners
- Creative directors managing teams
- Digital/marketing agency leaders
- Revenue: $500K - $10M
- Team: 5-50 people

### Psychology
- Squeezed margins, clients want more for less
- Competitors using AI to undercut pricing
- Team burnout from tight timelines
- Worried about commoditization
- Some clients asking about AI, others skeptical

### Primary Objection
"If we use AI, won't clients think they're not getting real creative work?"

---

## Competition Instructions

Create a webinar that:
1. Shows AI as margin-saver, not margin-killer
2. Addresses client perception concerns
3. Demonstrates quality maintenance with AI
4. Positions early adoption as competitive moat
5. Shows how to price AI-enhanced work
