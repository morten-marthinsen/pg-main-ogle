# Practice Brief #11: AI for Service Businesses

**Type:** PRACTICE (Sandbox Competition)
**Price Point:** $1,497
**Context:** Evergreen
**Focus:** Vertical-specific (Service businesses)

---

## The Offer

### Product: AI for Service Businesses
AI implementation specifically designed for consultants, agencies, coaches, and professional service firms.

### Positioning
"AI that works for service businesses—where relationships matter and every client is different."

### Structure
- Module 1: AI for Client Delivery (enhance what you deliver)
- Module 2: AI for Client Communication (proposals, reports, updates)
- Module 3: AI for Business Development (prospecting, follow-up)
- Module 4: AI for Operations (scheduling, admin, documentation)
- Module 5: AI-Enhanced Client Experience
- Service business prompt library (150+ prompts)
- Client deliverable templates
- Proposal and SOW generators
- Implementation roadmap for service firms

---

## Audience

### Who
- Consultants, coaches, agency owners
- Professional service firm partners
- Revenue: $300K - $5M
- Team: 1-25 (often lean)
- High-touch client relationships

### Psychology
- "AI seems impersonal, and we're a relationship business"
- Worried clients will feel they're getting AI, not human expertise
- Already stretched thin—no time to figure this out
- Needs AI to enhance, not replace, human judgment
- Skeptical that AI applies to their "unique" services

### Primary Objection
"My business is relationship-based. AI will make us seem impersonal."

---

## Competition Instructions

Create a webinar that:
1. Shows AI enhancing (not replacing) human touch
2. Uses service business specific examples
3. Addresses the "impersonal" objection directly
4. Demonstrates client experience improvement
5. Positions AI as competitive necessity in services

---

## Learning Goals

This brief teaches the system:
- Service business psychology
- Relationship/impersonal objection handling
- B2B service provider messaging
- "Unique business" objection patterns
- Enhance vs. replace positioning
