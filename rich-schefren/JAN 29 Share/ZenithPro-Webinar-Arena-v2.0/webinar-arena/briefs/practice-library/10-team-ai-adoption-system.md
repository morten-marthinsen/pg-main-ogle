# Practice Brief #10: Team AI Adoption System

**Type:** PRACTICE (Sandbox Competition)
**Price Point:** $1,997
**Context:** Launch
**Focus:** Change management / adoption

---

## The Offer

### Product: Team AI Adoption System
A change management system specifically designed to get resistant teams actually using AI.

### Positioning
"The missing piece in AI implementation: getting your team to actually use it."

### Structure
- Phase 1: Adoption Readiness Assessment
- Phase 2: Change Management Strategy
- Phase 3: Champion Development (train your internal AI leaders)
- Phase 4: Rollout Sequences (department by department)
- Phase 5: Habit Formation & Sustainability
- Resistance-handling playbooks
- Team communication templates
- Progress tracking system
- Manager coaching calls (4x)
- 90-day adoption guarantee

---

## Audience

### Who
- Business owners who've tried AI and failed adoption
- Have resistant or skeptical teams
- Managers responsible for implementation
- HR/Operations leaders

### Psychology
- Frustrated that tools aren't being used
- Team is "too busy" or "doesn't see the point"
- Wasted money on AI tools nobody uses
- Knows the problem is people, not technology
- Needs a people solution, not more tech training

### Primary Objection
"We've tried. Our team just doesn't want to change."

---

## Competition Instructions

Create a webinar that:
1. Reframes AI adoption as change management problem
2. Shows why tech training alone fails
3. Addresses resistance psychology
4. Demonstrates systematic adoption approach
5. Uses guarantee to overcome "we've tried" objection

---

## Learning Goals

This brief teaches the system:
- Change management positioning
- Adoption/resistance psychology
- "We've tried and failed" audience
- People-focused vs. tech-focused messaging
- Guarantee-centered offers
