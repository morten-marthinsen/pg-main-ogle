# Practice Brief #12: AI Productivity Multiplier

**Type:** PRACTICE (Sandbox Competition)
**Price Point:** $997
**Context:** Evergreen
**Focus:** Efficiency/productivity

---

## The Offer

### Product: AI Productivity Multiplier
Get 10x more done with AI—without working more hours or hiring more people.

### Positioning
"What if your team could produce in 1 day what used to take a week? That's not hype—it's what AI makes possible."

### Structure
- Module 1: The Productivity Audit (find your 10x opportunities)
- Module 2: AI for Writing Tasks (10x content output)
- Module 3: AI for Research (10x speed to insight)
- Module 4: AI for Communication (10x email/messaging efficiency)
- Module 5: AI for Decision Support (10x analysis capacity)
- Module 6: AI Workflows (automate the automation)
- Time tracking templates (measure your gains)
- Productivity prompt library
- Weekly implementation challenges

---

## Audience

### Who
- Overwhelmed business owners
- Teams doing too much manual work
- Anyone feeling behind on AI
- Productivity-focused buyers
- May have tried "productivity hacks" before

### Psychology
- Working too many hours
- Team is at capacity, can't afford to hire
- Knows AI could help but hasn't figured out how
- Responds to concrete metrics (10x, hours saved)
- Skeptical of "too good to be true" claims

### Primary Objection
"10x sounds like hype. What's the realistic improvement?"

---

## Competition Instructions

Create a webinar that:
1. Makes bold productivity claims believable
2. Uses specific, concrete examples
3. Shows the math (hours saved, output increased)
4. Addresses "hype" skepticism directly
5. Creates urgency around competitive productivity gap

---

## Learning Goals

This brief teaches the system:
- Productivity/efficiency messaging
- Quantified benefit claims
- "Sounds like hype" objection handling
- Time-saving value propositions
- ROI-focused selling
