# Practice Brief #19: AI Customer Service

**Type:** PRACTICE (Sandbox Competition)
**Price Point:** $1,497
**Context:** Evergreen
**Focus:** Customer service team

---

## The Offer

### Product: AI Customer Service
Transform your customer service with AI—faster responses, happier customers, lower costs.

### Positioning
"Give your customers instant, accurate help 24/7—without hiring a night shift."

### Structure
- Module 1: AI-Enhanced Support Strategy
- Module 2: AI for Response Generation (email, chat, social)
- Module 3: AI Knowledge Bases (self-service systems)
- Module 4: AI for Ticket Triage and Routing
- Module 5: AI for Customer Insights (patterns, sentiment, opportunities)
- Module 6: AI + Human Handoff Protocols
- CS prompt library (200+ scenarios)
- Response template library
- Escalation workflow templates
- CSAT improvement tracking

---

## Audience

### Who
- Customer service managers
- Operations leaders overseeing support
- Business owners with growing support volume
- Teams of 2-20 CS reps
- E-commerce, SaaS, service businesses

### Psychology
- Support costs growing faster than revenue
- Response times too slow
- Quality inconsistent across reps
- Customers expect instant responses
- Good CS people hard to find/keep

### Primary Objection
"AI customer service is frustrating. Customers hate chatbots."

---

## Competition Instructions

Create a webinar that:
1. Distinguishes good AI CS from bad chatbots
2. Shows human + AI collaboration model
3. Addresses "customers hate AI" objection
4. Demonstrates cost savings AND quality improvement
5. Uses specific response time/CSAT metrics

---

## Learning Goals

This brief teaches the system:
- Customer service team psychology
- "Chatbots are frustrating" objection
- Human + AI collaboration messaging
- Cost reduction + quality improvement
- Service industry specific concerns
