# Practice Brief #08: AI-First Company Blueprint

**Type:** PRACTICE (Sandbox Competition)
**Price Point:** $3,997
**Context:** High-ticket
**Focus:** Strategic transformation

---

## The Offer

### Product: AI-First Company Blueprint
A comprehensive strategic program to transform your company into an AI-first organization.

### Positioning
"Stop bolting AI onto your business. Rebuild your operations with AI at the core—the way the most valuable companies in the world are doing it."

### Structure
- Phase 1: AI-First Assessment (where you are, where to go)
- Phase 2: Strategic Roadmap (12-month transformation plan)
- Phase 3: Foundation Building (infrastructure, governance, culture)
- Phase 4: Department Rollouts (systematic implementation)
- Phase 5: Optimization & Scaling
- Executive strategy sessions (monthly)
- Implementation team training
- AI governance framework
- Vendor evaluation support
- 12-month advisory access

---

## Audience

### Who
- CEOs and executive teams
- Companies $10M-$100M revenue
- 50-500 employees
- Recognize AI as existential opportunity/threat
- Ready for significant investment

### Psychology
- Thinking 3-5 years ahead
- Worried about disruption
- Want to lead, not follow
- See AI as strategic, not tactical
- Ready to invest at scale

### Primary Objection
"This is a big investment and commitment. How do we know we're ready?"

---

## Competition Instructions

Create a webinar that:
1. Paints vision of AI-first companies dominating
2. Shows the risk of incremental vs. strategic approach
3. Justifies premium price with transformation scope
4. Uses case studies of AI-first transformations
5. Addresses readiness and timing concerns

---

## Learning Goals

This brief teaches the system:
- Transformational offer positioning
- Premium price justification
- Strategic/visionary messaging
- Fear of disruption leverage
- Big commitment handling
