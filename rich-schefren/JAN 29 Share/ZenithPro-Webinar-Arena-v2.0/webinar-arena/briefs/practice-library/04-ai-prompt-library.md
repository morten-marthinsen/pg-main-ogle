# Practice Brief #04: AI Prompt Library + Training

**Type:** PRACTICE (Sandbox Competition)
**Price Point:** $297
**Context:** Evergreen
**Focus:** Product + training hybrid (digital product)

---

## The Offer

### Product: AI Prompt Library + Training
500+ business prompts organized by department, with training on how to customize and use them.

### Positioning
"Stop spending hours crafting prompts. Get 500+ proven prompts organized by role—copy, paste, profit."

### Structure
- 500+ prompts in searchable database
- Organized by department (marketing, sales, ops, HR, finance)
- Video training: How to customize prompts
- Video training: Prompt engineering basics
- Monthly updates (new prompts added)
- Lifetime access

---

## Audience

### Who
- Business owners and team leaders
- Already using AI (ChatGPT, Claude)
- Frustrated with prompt quality
- Want shortcuts, not theory
- Price-sensitive but willing to pay for convenience

### Psychology
- "I know AI could do more but my prompts suck"
- Wants plug-and-play solutions
- Values time over money (at this price point)
- Skeptical of "prompt" products (seen bad ones)
- Needs proof the prompts actually work

### Primary Objection
"There are free prompt libraries everywhere. Why pay?"

---

## Competition Instructions

Create a webinar that:
1. Demonstrates prompt quality live
2. Shows organization/usability advantage
3. Emphasizes business-specific (not generic) prompts
4. Uses low price as impulse-buy trigger
5. Creates evergreen urgency (price increase, bonus removal)

---

## Learning Goals

This brief teaches the system:
- Low-ticket evergreen psychology
- Digital product positioning
- Competing with free alternatives
- Live demonstration in webinars
- Impulse purchase triggers
