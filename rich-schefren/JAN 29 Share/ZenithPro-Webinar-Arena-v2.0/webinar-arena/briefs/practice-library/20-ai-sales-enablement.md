# Practice Brief #20: AI Sales Enablement

**Type:** PRACTICE (Sandbox Competition)
**Price Point:** $1,997
**Context:** Evergreen
**Focus:** Sales team

---

## The Offer

### Product: AI Sales Enablement
Arm your sales team with AI—more qualified leads, better proposals, higher close rates.

### Positioning
"What if every rep on your team performed like your best rep? That's what AI sales enablement makes possible."

### Structure
- Module 1: AI for Prospecting (research, outreach, qualification)
- Module 2: AI for Discovery (preparation, questions, note-taking)
- Module 3: AI for Proposals (customization, speed, quality)
- Module 4: AI for Follow-up (timing, messaging, persistence)
- Module 5: AI for Sales Coaching (call analysis, improvement)
- Module 6: AI for Forecasting (pipeline intelligence)
- Sales prompt library by stage
- Email/message sequences
- Proposal templates
- CRM integration guides

---

## Audience

### Who
- Sales managers and directors
- VP of Sales / CRO
- Business owners who sell
- Sales teams of 3-30 reps
- B2B sales organizations

### Psychology
- Top performers do well, others struggle
- Too much time on non-selling activities
- Proposals take too long to create
- Follow-up is inconsistent
- Want to scale performance, not just headcount

### Primary Objection
"Sales is about relationships. AI will make us seem robotic."

---

## Competition Instructions

Create a webinar that:
1. Shows AI enhancing relationship selling
2. Positions AI as leverage for more relationship time
3. Addresses "robotic" objection with examples
4. Uses performance gap narrative (best vs. rest)
5. Demonstrates specific sales metrics improvement

---

## Learning Goals

This brief teaches the system:
- Sales team psychology
- "Relationships, not robots" objection
- Performance elevation messaging
- Sales metrics and KPIs
- B2B sales context
