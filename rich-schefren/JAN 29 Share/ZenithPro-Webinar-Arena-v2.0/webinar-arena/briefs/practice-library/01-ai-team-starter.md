# Practice Brief #01: AI Team Starter

**Type:** PRACTICE (Sandbox Competition)
**Price Point:** $497
**Context:** Launch
**Focus:** Low-ticket team training entry point

---

## The Offer

### Product: AI Team Starter
A low-ticket entry point to get small teams (3-10 people) using AI within 2 weeks.

### Positioning
"The fastest way to get your team actually using AI—without the overwhelm, resistance, or wasted time."

### Structure
- 4 core training modules (video-based)
- 10 ready-to-use team prompts
- Team implementation checklist
- 2 live Q&A calls
- Private community access (90 days)

---

## Audience

### Who
- Small business owners (2-10 employees)
- Revenue: $200K - $1M
- First-time AI buyers
- Budget-conscious but ready to invest

### Psychology
- Want results fast
- Don't want to figure it out themselves
- Team is skeptical but owner is motivated
- Need something they can implement this month

### Primary Objection
"$497 for AI training? I can learn this on YouTube for free."

---

## Competition Instructions

Create a webinar that:
1. Justifies $497 against free alternatives
2. Creates urgency for a launch
3. Overcomes team resistance objection
4. Promises quick wins (2 weeks)
5. Positions as gateway to bigger results

---

## Learning Goals

This brief teaches the system:
- Low-ticket launch psychology
- Competing against free content
- Small team dynamics
- Speed/simplicity messaging
- Entry-point positioning
