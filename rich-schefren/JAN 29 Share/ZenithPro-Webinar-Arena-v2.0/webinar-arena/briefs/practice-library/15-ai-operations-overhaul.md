# Practice Brief #15: AI Operations Overhaul

**Type:** PRACTICE (Sandbox Competition)
**Price Point:** $2,497
**Context:** Launch
**Focus:** Operations/back-office

---

## The Offer

### Product: AI Operations Overhaul
Transform your back-office operations with AI—reduce costs, eliminate errors, scale without hiring.

### Positioning
"The unsexy AI transformation that saves you $100K+ per year: back-office operations."

### Structure
- Module 1: Operations Audit (find the waste)
- Module 2: AI for Admin (scheduling, documentation, filing)
- Module 3: AI for Finance Ops (invoicing, reporting, reconciliation)
- Module 4: AI for HR Ops (onboarding, policies, documentation)
- Module 5: AI for Customer Ops (support, follow-up, retention)
- Module 6: Automation Buildout (connect the systems)
- Operations playbook templates
- SOP library for AI-enhanced operations
- ROI tracking dashboard
- Implementation support (8 weeks)

---

## Audience

### Who
- COOs, Operations managers
- Business owners drowning in back-office
- Companies growing faster than ops can handle
- Revenue: $1M-$20M
- May have hired ops people who are now overwhelmed

### Psychology
- Operations is a bottleneck to growth
- Spending too much on admin/ops labor
- Making errors that cost money and reputation
- Wants to scale without proportionally scaling ops team
- Sees operations as necessary evil, not competitive advantage

### Primary Objection
"AI for operations? Our processes are too messy/custom for AI."

---

## Competition Instructions

Create a webinar that:
1. Makes ops transformation exciting (not boring)
2. Shows massive ROI potential (cost savings)
3. Addresses "too custom/messy" objection
4. Positions ops as competitive advantage
5. Creates urgency around scaling constraints

---

## Learning Goals

This brief teaches the system:
- Operations/back-office positioning
- "Unsexy but valuable" messaging
- Cost-savings ROI arguments
- Scaling without hiring narrative
- Custom/messy objection handling
