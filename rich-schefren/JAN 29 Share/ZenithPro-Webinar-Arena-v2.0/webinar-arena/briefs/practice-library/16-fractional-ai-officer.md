# Practice Brief #16: Fractional AI Officer

**Type:** PRACTICE (Sandbox Competition)
**Price Point:** $7,500/month
**Context:** High-ticket retainer
**Focus:** Ongoing advisory/retainer positioning

---

## The Offer

### Product: Fractional AI Officer
Get a dedicated AI executive for your company—without the $300K salary. Fractional CAO on retainer.

### Positioning
"The AI leadership you need without the executive price tag. Your Fractional Chief AI Officer."

### Structure
- Monthly AI strategy sessions (4 hours)
- Unlimited email/Slack access
- Quarterly AI roadmap reviews
- Vendor evaluation and selection support
- Team training oversight
- Implementation accountability
- Executive briefings for leadership team
- 6-month minimum commitment

---

## Audience

### Who
- CEOs of $5M-$50M companies
- Can't justify full-time AI executive
- Need ongoing strategic guidance
- Have budget for significant monthly investment
- Making multiple AI decisions quarterly

### Psychology
- Knows they need AI leadership
- Can't find/afford full-time AI talent
- Wants accountability and continuity
- Values strategic thinking over implementation
- Needs someone to own the AI agenda

### Primary Objection
"$7,500/month is a lot. How do we know we'll get enough value?"

---

## Competition Instructions

Create a webinar that:
1. Positions fractional as smart alternative to FTE
2. Shows the cost of NOT having AI leadership
3. Justifies monthly retainer with scope of value
4. Uses executive decision-making examples
5. Creates urgency around AI talent scarcity

---

## Learning Goals

This brief teaches the system:
- Retainer/ongoing service positioning
- Fractional executive messaging
- Very high-ticket justification
- Strategic advisory value props
- Long-term commitment objection handling
