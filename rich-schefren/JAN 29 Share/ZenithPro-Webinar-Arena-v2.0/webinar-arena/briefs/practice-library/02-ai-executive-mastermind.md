# Practice Brief #02: AI Executive Mastermind

**Type:** PRACTICE (Sandbox Competition)
**Price Point:** $5,997
**Context:** High-ticket
**Focus:** C-suite AI strategy

---

## The Offer

### Product: AI Executive Mastermind
A 6-month mastermind for CEOs and executives implementing AI strategy across their organizations.

### Positioning
"The AI strategy room for executives who need to lead—not just understand—the AI transformation."

### Structure
- Monthly 2-hour strategy sessions (small group)
- Private 1-on-1 strategy call (quarterly)
- AI strategy assessment and roadmap
- Executive briefing documents (monthly)
- Direct access to AI implementation advisors
- Peer network of implementing executives

---

## Audience

### Who
- CEOs, COOs, Presidents of $5M-$50M companies
- Responsible for company-wide strategy
- Board asking about AI
- Competitors starting to use AI

### Psychology
- Don't want to learn prompts—want strategic clarity
- Need to lead, not just participate
- Time-poor, need curated intelligence
- Want peer validation and benchmarking
- Fear being disrupted or left behind

### Primary Objection
"I can hire an AI consultant for less than this."

---

## Competition Instructions

Create a webinar that:
1. Speaks executive language (ROI, competitive advantage, strategic positioning)
2. Differentiates from consulting (peer learning, ongoing access, implementation focus)
3. Justifies $5,997 as investment, not expense
4. Creates urgency around competitive window
5. Uses exclusivity and selectivity positioning

---

## Learning Goals

This brief teaches the system:
- High-ticket executive psychology
- Mastermind/peer group positioning
- Strategic vs. tactical messaging
- Premium price justification
- Exclusivity and scarcity tactics
