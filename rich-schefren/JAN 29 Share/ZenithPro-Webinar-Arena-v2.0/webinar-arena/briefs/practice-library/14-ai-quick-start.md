# Practice Brief #14: AI Quick Start

**Type:** PRACTICE (Sandbox Competition)
**Price Point:** $197
**Context:** Tripwire
**Focus:** Entry-level / gateway product

---

## The Offer

### Product: AI Quick Start
Get started with AI in your business this weekend—no tech skills required.

### Positioning
"From AI-curious to AI-capable in one weekend. $197. No excuses left."

### Structure
- 4 core video modules (2 hours total)
- Quick Start workbook
- 25 business prompts (plug and play)
- Tool setup guides (ChatGPT, Claude)
- Weekend implementation challenge
- Community access (30 days)

---

## Audience

### Who
- AI beginners
- Business owners who haven't started yet
- Price-sensitive first-time buyers
- People who've been "meaning to" learn AI
- May ascend to higher-ticket offers later

### Psychology
- Intimidated by AI
- Doesn't want to look stupid
- Wants the absolute basics first
- Responds to low risk, quick win
- Procrastinator who needs a push

### Primary Objection
"I can learn this for free on YouTube."

---

## Competition Instructions

Create a webinar that:
1. Makes AI approachable/non-intimidating
2. Competes with free content on structure/speed
3. Uses ultra-low price as action trigger
4. Creates weekend challenge urgency
5. Hints at larger transformation (ascension opportunity)

---

## Learning Goals

This brief teaches the system:
- Tripwire/entry-level psychology
- Competing with free
- Beginner-friendly messaging
- Low-price impulse triggers
- Ascension ladder positioning
