# Practice Brief #13: AI Strategy Intensive

**Type:** PRACTICE (Sandbox Competition)
**Price Point:** $4,997
**Context:** High-ticket
**Focus:** Done-with-you strategy

---

## The Offer

### Product: AI Strategy Intensive
A 2-day intensive workshop (virtual or in-person) to build your complete AI strategy with expert guidance.

### Positioning
"Walk out in 2 days with your complete AI strategy—built with expert guidance, not figuring it out alone."

### Structure
- Day 1: Assessment, Opportunity Mapping, Prioritization
- Day 2: Implementation Roadmap, Resource Planning, Quick Wins
- Pre-work: Business assessment and preparation
- Deliverables: Complete AI strategy document
- Deliverables: 90-day implementation roadmap
- Deliverables: Tool recommendations and budget
- Post-intensive: 3 follow-up strategy calls
- Post-intensive: 60 days of email support

---

## Audience

### Who
- CEOs and leadership teams
- Companies at a strategic inflection point
- Ready to invest but need the plan
- Revenue: $5M-$50M
- May have failed at self-directed AI implementation

### Psychology
- Values expert guidance over self-learning
- Wants concentrated progress (not dragged out)
- Needs a plan before investing in implementation
- Has budget, needs confidence it's spent wisely
- Decision-maker who can commit to 2 days

### Primary Objection
"$5K for 2 days? That's expensive for a workshop."

---

## Competition Instructions

Create a webinar that:
1. Justifies workshop pricing vs. ongoing programs
2. Emphasizes deliverables (walk away with complete strategy)
3. Shows cost of bad strategy vs. cost of intensive
4. Uses time-compression as key value
5. Creates urgency around limited seats

---

## Learning Goals

This brief teaches the system:
- Workshop/intensive positioning
- High-ticket concentrated delivery
- Deliverable-focused value propositions
- Executive buyer psychology
- Premium workshop pricing justification
