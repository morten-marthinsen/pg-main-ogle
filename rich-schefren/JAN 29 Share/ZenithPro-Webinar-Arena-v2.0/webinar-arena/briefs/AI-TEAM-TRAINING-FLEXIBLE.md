## Audience Intelligence

### Who They Are
- Established business owners ($500K - $10M revenue)
- Have teams (3-50 people typically)
- Strategic thinkers who want leverage, not just tactics
- Likely Rich's existing audience or similar

### Their AI Status
- **Curious but not implemented** - Heard the hype, overwhelmed by options
- **Tried and failed** - Bought tools, team didn't adopt, felt like waste
- **Using personally, not team-wide** - Owner uses ChatGPT but team doesn't

### Their Fears
1. "My team will resist / won't adopt it"
2. "It's too technical for my industry / business type"
3. "We tried AI tools and it didn't stick"
4. "It'll take too much time to implement properly"
5. "The technology changes too fast—what we learn will be obsolete"
6. "It'll replace jobs and create team anxiety"
7. "I don't understand it well enough to lead the implementation"

### Their Desires
1. Competitive advantage before competitors figure it out
2. Team efficiency without adding headcount
3. Looking innovative/forward-thinking to clients and industry
4. Reducing their personal bottleneck on tasks
5. Finally understanding AI well enough to make strategic decisions
6. Clear implementation path (not just theory)
7. Confidence that this will actually work for their business

### Their Skepticisms
1. "This is probably just ChatGPT tips I could learn on YouTube"
2. "Generic AI training won't apply to my specific business"
3. "Rich is a strategy guy, not an AI expert"
4. "My team is different—older, less technical, etc."
5. "We're too busy to add another initiative"

## Competitive Landscape

### What Exists
- Tons of generic "AI for business" courses (mostly cheap, surface-level)
- Enterprise AI consulting (expensive, wrong scale)
- Tool-specific training (Jasper, ChatGPT, etc.—fragmented)
- DIY YouTube/blog content (overwhelming, unstructured)

### The Gap
- **Team-focused** (not just owner-focused) AI implementation
- **Strategic framework** approach (not just prompts and tips)
- **Implementation-oriented** (not just education)
- **Right-sized** for small-to-medium businesses with teams

### Positioning Opportunity
"The first AI training designed for business owners who need their TEAM to adopt AI—not just learn about it."

## Product Discovery Prompt

When creating this webinar, you are not just writing copy—you are **designing the optimal offer**.

Before writing the webinar, produce a "Product Strategy" section that includes:

1. **Recommended transformation promise** - Specific, measurable, desirable
2. **Recommended product structure** - Modules, timeline, deliverables
3. **Recommended price point** - With justification based on value and audience
4. **Recommended bonus stack** - Each bonus should address a specific friction point
5. **Recommended guarantee** - Type and terms
6. **Key differentiator** - What makes this offer obviously different

Then write the webinar selling THIS offer.
