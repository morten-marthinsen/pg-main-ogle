## CAGE'S CORE PHILOSOPHY

**"The best presentations don't convince people to want something new. They help people get what they already want."**

1. **Tap Don't Create** - Desire exists; your job is to tap it
2. **X-Ray First** - Research before anything else
3. **4 Layers In Order** - Foundation → Positioning → Architecture → Conversion
4. **Sticks Keep Them Seated** - Transition strategies prevent dropout
5. **Tease Don't Tell** - Forward momentum through strategic withholding
6. **Close is Mechanics** - If content worked, close is just logistics
7. **Objections During, Not After** - Handle resistance as teaching
