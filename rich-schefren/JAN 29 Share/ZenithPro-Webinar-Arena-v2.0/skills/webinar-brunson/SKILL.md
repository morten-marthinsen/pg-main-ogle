### STEP 4: BUILD THE CLOSE SECOND (Internal Only)

**The Stack is everything. Russell tried skipping it once - webinar bombed.**

1. Master The Stack presentation (recap full offer 6+ times)
2. Prepare If-All statements to justify price
3. Design price drop sequence
4. Select which of the 16+ closes fit your offer
5. Add Voicemail Close as final slide
6. **VALIDATE:** Does The Stack make buying the obvious choice?
