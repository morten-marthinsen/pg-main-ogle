## What This Is

A self-evolving competitive webinar creation system. 6 world-class webinar experts compete to write the best webinar for your offer. The system learns from every competition, tracks real-world outcomes, and improves itself over time.

**v1.0 was a tournament. v2.0 is a tournament that studies its own game film, hires researchers when it spots weaknesses, and rewrites its playbook based on what actually works.**

## Installation (2 minutes)

### Mac/Linux
```bash
cd path/to/ZenithPro-Webinar-Arena-v2.0
chmod +x install.sh
./install.sh
```

### Windows
```powershell
cd path\to\ZenithPro-Webinar-Arena-v2.0
.\install.ps1
```

The installer copies skills, agents, and the arena infrastructure to your `~/.claude/` directory.

---

## Your First Competition

After installation, open Claude Code and type:

```
/webinar-arena
```

You'll be asked for a brief describing your webinar. Include:
- **What you're selling** (product name, price, what's included)
- **Who you're selling to** (audience, their situation, their fears/desires)
- **Context** (launch vs. evergreen, warm vs. cold audience)

The arena runs a full competition: all 6 experts create webinars, critics evaluate them, a marketplace judge picks the winner.

## What's New in v2.0

### It Learns
- Every competition adds to the knowledge base
- Reports real conversion data with `/arena-outcome` and the system calibrates
- Pattern detection across competitions spots what works for YOUR audience

### It Evolves
- Wild Cards: inject external methodologies (`/arena-wildcard [file]`)
- Breakthrough extraction: when something wins, the system codifies why
- Expert spawning: winning combinations become permanent new experts

### You Can See Everything
- System health dashboard: `/arena-health`
- Real-time learning feed: `/arena-feed`
- Weekly digest: `/arena-digest`

### Practice Mode
- 20 pre-built practice briefs for sandbox competitions
- Located in `~/.claude/webinar-arena/briefs/practice-library/`
- Run practice competitions to build up the learning system before your real webinar
