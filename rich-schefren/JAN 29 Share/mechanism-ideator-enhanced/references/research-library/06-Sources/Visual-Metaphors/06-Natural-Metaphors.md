# Natural Metaphors

> **Purpose:** Document growth, decay, seasons, renewal, and organic metaphors used to explain health cycles, healing processes, and natural transformation.

---

## Why Natural Metaphors Resonate

Nature mirrors human life patterns - growth, decline, change, and renewal. These shared rhythms create deep intuitive understanding. Natural metaphors are often more holistic and less mechanistic than machine metaphors.

### Advantages of Natural Metaphors

1. **Cycles normalize struggle** - Winter always leads to spring
2. **Growth mindset** - Living things can heal and adapt
3. **Patience framing** - Natural processes take time
4. **Holistic view** - Everything is connected
5. **Universal experience** - Everyone observes nature
6. **Emotional resonance** - Deep connection to natural world
