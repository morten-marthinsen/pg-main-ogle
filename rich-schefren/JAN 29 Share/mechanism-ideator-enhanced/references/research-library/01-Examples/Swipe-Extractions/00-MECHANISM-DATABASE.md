## Core Mechanism Frameworks

### 1. The One Belief (Evaldo)
> "This NEW OPPORTUNITY is the key to THEIR DESIRE and it's only attainable through my NEW MECHANISM."

**Components:**
- New Opportunity (not improvement)
- Prospect's Desire (emotional benefit)
- New Mechanism (unique delivery)

### 2. UMP + UMS (Stefan Georgi)
**UMP:** Why they failed before (the missing 1%)
**UMS:** Why they'll succeed now (what you provide)

**The Connection:** UMP reveals the gap; UMS fills it.

### 3. Hook-Story-Offer (Russell Brunson)
**Hook:** Pattern interrupt/attention grab
**Story:** Emotional connection through narrative
**Offer:** Clear call to action

### 4. Problem-Agitate-Solution (Dan Kennedy)
**Problem:** State it clearly
**Agitate:** Make them feel the pain
**Solution:** Present your answer

### 5. Fascination Accumulation (Boardroom)
Stack curiosity bullets that:
- Tease valuable information
- Create open loops
- Promise specific page numbers
- Never reveal the answer

## The Enemy Identification Framework

Every great mechanism identifies an enemy:

| Market | Common Enemies |
|--------|---------------|
| Financial | Wall Street, Big Banks, Fed, Government |
| Health | Big Pharma, Ignorant Doctors, FDA |
| Weight Loss | Diet Industry, Food Companies, Processed Foods |
| Dating | Society, Movies, Bad Advice |
| Business | Gatekeepers, Competition, Outdated Methods |
| MLM | Traditional Methods, Cold Calling, Rejection |

**Why This Works:**
- Externalizes blame (not your fault)
- Creates us-vs-them bonding
- Positions you as ally against enemy
- Provides emotional fuel for action
