## By Mechanism Type

### Problem Mechanisms (UMP)
These mechanisms explain the ROOT CAUSE of the prospect's problem:
- [[Gundry-Leaky-Gut-Lectins]] - "Lectins punch holes in gut"
- [[Health-Dirty-Genes]] - "Dirty genes causing disease"
- [[Fitness-Leptin-Resistance]] - "Leptin resistance blocks weight loss"
- [[Health-Brain-Fog]] - "Inflammation causing cognitive issues"

### Solution Mechanisms (UMS)
These mechanisms explain HOW the solution works:
- [[Hopkins-Schlitz-Purity]] - "Live steam sterilization"
- [[Hopkins-Pepsodent-Film]] - "Removes film from teeth"
- [[Fitness-Muscle-Confusion]] - "Keeps muscles adapting"
- [[Fitness-Metabolic-Switch]] - "Switches body to fat burning"

### Delivery Mechanisms (UMD)
These mechanisms describe the VEHICLE for delivering results:
- [[Carlton-One-Legged-Golfer]] - "Simple Secret" system
- [[Albuquerque-16-Word]] - "16-Word formula"
- [[Georgi-RMBC-Method]] - "Research, Mechanism, Brief, Copy"
- [[Brown-E5-Method]] - "5-step framework"

## Quick Reference: Mechanism Components

Every documented mechanism includes:

1. **UMP** (Unique Mechanism Problem) - The root cause
2. **UMS** (Unique Mechanism Solution) - How it's fixed
3. **UMD** (Unique Mechanism Delivery) - The product/system
4. **Visual Metaphors** - Mental images used
5. **Psychological Hooks** - Emotions triggered
6. **Market Context** - Audience and competition
7. **Results** - Documented success metrics
