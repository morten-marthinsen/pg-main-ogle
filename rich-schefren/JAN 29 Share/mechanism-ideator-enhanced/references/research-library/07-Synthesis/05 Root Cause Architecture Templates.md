# Root Cause Architecture Templates

> **Purpose:** Frameworks and templates for constructing root cause arguments that shift beliefs, destroy false solutions, and restore prospect self-esteem.

---

## Core Concept

Root cause architecture is the systematic process of:
1. Destroying the prospect's current (incorrect) beliefs about their problem
2. Introducing the "real" root cause (your mechanism)
3. Explaining why all past solutions failed (they didn't address the real cause)
4. Restoring self-esteem (it wasn't their fault)
5. Generating hope (this new approach will work)

---

## The Belief Shift Framework

### Structure

```
OLD BELIEF → REFRAME → NEW BELIEF

Components:
1. Articulate the old belief clearly
2. Challenge with evidence/logic
3. Introduce new explanation (mechanism)
4. Support with proof
5. Make new belief inevitable
```

### The Belief Shift Process

**Step 1: Name the Old Belief**
What do they currently believe is causing their problem?

**Step 2: Validate Their Experience**
Acknowledge their struggle and past attempts

**Step 3: Introduce Doubt**
Evidence that the old belief is wrong

**Step 4: Present New Belief (Mechanism)**
The real cause they didn't know about

**Step 5: Support with Proof**
Evidence for the new belief

**Step 6: Make It Inevitable**
Logic that makes new belief the only conclusion

**Step 7: Generate Hope**
Connect to solution that addresses real cause
