## The exact process

### Step 1: Find your goldmine thread

Go to any subreddit related to your topic, industry, or interests. Look for threads with 100+ comments (I found one with 507 comments about AI writing). The more heated the discussion, the better.

Good threads to target:

  * "What's your biggest frustration with [topic]?"

  * "Has [technology/trend] ruined [something]?"

  * "Unpopular opinion about [topic]"

  * Weekly rant threads

  * Beginner question megathreads

### Step 2: Feed it to NotebookLM

  1. Go to NotebookLM

  2. Create new notebook

  3. Click "Upload sources"

  4. Paste your Reddit URL

  5. Let it process




[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!G41D!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F060be024-4f57-4510-bb3d-c27b661ceee8_946x426.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!G41D!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F060be024-4f57-4510-bb3d-c27b661ceee8_946x426.png)

> **Pro tip:** Add 3-5 related threads for deeper patterns. NotebookLM handles multiple sources beautifully.

### **Step 3: Run the Content Intelligence Prompt**

Copy and paste this exact prompt into NotebookLM's chat:
    
    
    Analyze this Reddit discussion and extract:
    1. Top 5 pain points/frustrations people express
    2. Top 3 questions (asked or implied)
    3. What makes people emotional (angry/excited)
    4. Solutions they wish existed
    
    Then generate 10 specific content ideas that address these findings.
    
    For each idea provide:
    - Clear headline/angle
    - Which pain point it solves
    - Why someone would click
    - Unique value you'd add
    
    Focus on specific, actionable ideas based on real problems from the thread—not generic topics.

## What makes this so powerful

Every content idea is pre-validated because:

  * Real people are already discussing it

  * You understand the exact language they use

  * You know which emotions drive engagement

  * You see solutions they're actively seeking




From one Reddit thread, you get:

  * Weeks/months of content ideas

  * Exact phrases the audience uses

  * Problems they didn't even articulate clearly

  * Angles your competitors haven't found
