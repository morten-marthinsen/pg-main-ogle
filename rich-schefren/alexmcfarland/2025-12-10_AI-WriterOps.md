## **The two-layer system**

Your AI writing system should have two layers:

  1. **Production layer** \- Where content gets made. Claude Code, skills, and context profiles.

  2. **Knowledge layer** \- Where thinking lives. Your drafts, notes, and published content.




Separating these lets you build a system instead of starting from scratch every session.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!qV9E!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F2d914c09-a7ae-47c0-98f2-f1294f00e71e_828x464.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!qV9E!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F2d914c09-a7ae-47c0-98f2-f1294f00e71e_828x464.png)

### **Context profiles**

Three profiles power everything:

  1. **Voice DNA** \- How you sound, your tone, what you never say

  2. **ICP** \- Who you’re writing for, their problems, their language

  3. **Business Profile** \- What you offer, your positioning




When these are structured as data (not paragraphs), AI can use them consistently.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!sxru!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fca301a40-751b-4ac6-9502-bb718f54e578_1680x1112.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!sxru!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fca301a40-751b-4ac6-9502-bb718f54e578_1680x1112.png)

### **Skills**

Skills are packaged expertise. Instead of explaining how to write a Substack note every time, you write the instructions once and use them forever.

The skill tells Claude: “When asked for this content type, follow these guidelines.”

Write once. Use forever.
