Most creators are sitting on goldmines of content and don't even know it.

That random podcast transcript? The messy interview notes? That brain dump you recorded last month? They're all packed with dozens of content angles you're completely missing. And here's the kicker – there's a stupidly simple way to extract them all in about 5 minutes.

## **Using NotebookLM as a content multiplier**

One 30-minute conversation contains enough angles to fuel content for months.

The secret? Stop looking for new ideas and start mining what you already have. NotebookLM makes this process so simple it feels like cheating. Instead of staring at blank pages, you can systematically extract every valuable angle from content that already exists.

Here's exactly how to extract 30+ content angles from any longform source.

### **Step 1: Upload your content goldmine**

Getting your content into NotebookLM takes literally 90 seconds.

Here's what works best as source material:

  * Podcast or video transcripts

  * Long blog posts or articles

  * Meeting recordings or interviews

  * Brain dumps or voice notes

  * Course content or workshops

  * Email threads with good insights




### **Step 2: use the NotebookLM 30x Content Generator**

Here's the exact prompt that turns chaos into content gold.

Copy this word-for-word into NotebookLM's chat:
    
    
    Analyze the sources and extract 30+ specific content angles organized by audience intent:
    
    PROBLEM-AWARE angles (they know they have an issue):
    - What problems/frustrations are mentioned?
    - What failed solutions come up?
    - What complaints or struggles appear?
    
    SOLUTION-AWARE angles (they're comparing options):
    - What solutions or methods are discussed?
    - What frameworks or systems are mentioned?
    - What tools or approaches are referenced?
    
    TACTICAL angles (they want how-to):
    - What step-by-step processes could be extracted?
    - What specific techniques are explained?
    - What implementations are described?
    
    THOUGHT LEADERSHIP angles (they want insights):
    - What contrarian opinions appear?
    - What industry myths are challenged?
    - What unique perspectives emerge?
    
    For each angle, provide:
    1. A specific headline/hook
    2. The key insight or argument
    3. Who would care most about this
    
    Focus on specificity over generic topics.

This prompt works because it forces the AI to think like your audience, not like a content robot.

### **Step 3: Turn AI output into content calendar**

NotebookLM will spit out more angles than you can use in a year.

Here's how to process them:

  * **Bold the winners:** Mark 5-7 angles that make you excited

  * **Assign platforms:** LinkedIn post, newsletter, Twitter thread, etc.

  * **Add your spin:** Each angle needs your experience or opinion

  * **Batch similar angles:** Group them into content series




You're not creating from scratch – you're remixing your own expertise.

## **Try these variations to extract more angles**

Want to extract even more specific angles? Try these variations.

**For personal brands:** "What personal stories or experiences in this source could become valuable lessons? Extract moments of struggle, breakthrough, or realization."

**For B2B content:** "Identify all process improvements, efficiency gains, or cost savings mentioned. Frame each as a business case study angle."

**For educators:** "Find all the misconceptions, beginner mistakes, or 'aha moments' that could become teaching opportunities."

**For controversy:** "What opinions or approaches here go against conventional wisdom? Extract the contrarian takes with supporting evidence."

Each prompt unlocks completely different angles from the same source.

Start with one source today and watch what happens.
