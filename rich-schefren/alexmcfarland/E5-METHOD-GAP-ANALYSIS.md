## TODD BROWN E5 METHOD - COMPLETE FLOW

### Module 2: Foundation Components (Strategy)

1. **Campaign Thesis** (M2B)
   - The one belief prospects need before the offer
   - Formula: "The most effective way to [primary promise] is with [unique mechanism]"
   - **CRITICAL:** This is the NORTH STAR for everything else

2. **Primary Promise** (M2C)
   - Big, bold, audacious, true, believable, specific promise
   - Must address prospect's problem (Now/After Grid, Ideal Client)
   - Must have proof

3. **Unique Mechanism** (M2D)
   - How the product/service works
   - Three types: Actual, Unspoken, Transubstantiated
   - Must be NAMED (scientific/official sounding)
   - Must have ARTICULATION (how it works)

4. **Thesis Statement** (M2E)
   - Combines Primary Promise + Unique Mechanism
   - Template: "The most effective, most efficient way to [primary promise] is with [unique mechanism]"

### Module 4: Marketing Execution Components

5. **Big Marketing Idea** (M4A)
   - The IDEA behind the campaign
   - NOT the same as headline
   - Used to get attention

6. **E5 Headline** (M4A)
   - COMMUNICATES the big marketing idea
   - Formula provided (specific structure)

7. **E5 Campaign Lead** (M4D, M4E)
   - First 350-800 words
   - Makes emotional sale
   - Must communicate: New/Different, Simple/Easy, Fast, Predictable
   - Template with specific structure

### Module 5: Campaign Argument (Education-Based Marketing)

8. **Intro & Credibility** (M5B)
   - Why should they trust you?
   - 100 words max
   - 11 ways to establish credibility

9. **Campaign Argument Structure** (M5C)
   - Education-based marketing content
   - Delivered as CPB chunks

10. **Determining CPB Chunks** (M5D)
    - Based on prospect gap questions
    - 9 core gap questions provided
    - Must answer: "What does prospect need to believe to accept campaign thesis?"

11. **Ordering CPB Chunks** (M5D)
    - Dependencies between chunks
    - Flow considerations

12. **Filling CPB Chunks** (M5E)
    - Claim (answer to gap question)
    - Proof (demonstration, reason why, examples, before/after, studies, statistics, authoritative, logic)
    - Benefit (what this means for prospect)

13. **Finishing Campaign Argument** (M6A)
    - Simple segue (from intro to CPB chunks)
    - CPB chunk connectors
    - SIN offer segue
    - Putting it all together
