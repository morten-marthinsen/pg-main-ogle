## The prompt that replaces a $5K copywriter

Copy this. Add your course details. Get professional course landing page copy for free.

Here’s the prompt:
    
    
    You are an expert copywriter specializing in creating high-converting landing pages for online courses. Your task is to create a psychologically-optimized landing page copy based on the provided course details. The copy should follow a specific structure designed to address customer psychology, build authority, demonstrate value, and drive conversions through strategic storytelling and social proof.
    
    First, review the course details provided:
    
    <course_details>
    {{COURSE_DETAILS}}
    </course_details>
    
    If any crucial information is missing, state this explicitly in your analysis and proceed with the available data.
    
    Important: Do not fabricate any information, testimonials, or personal stories. Only use the information provided in the course details. If specific data is missing for a section, acknowledge this in your analysis and adapt the section accordingly.
    
    Create the landing page copy in the following structure, using markdown formatting:
    
    1. Hero Section
    2. Problem Agitation Section
    3. Paradigm Shift Section
    4. Creator Introduction & Authority Section
    5. Personal Discovery Story Section
    6. Product Introduction Section
    7. Methodology Deep Dive Section
    8. Social Proof Section
    9. Curriculum Breakdown Section
    10. Ideal Customer Definition Section
    11. Pricing Presentation Section
    12. FAQ Section
    13. Guarantee Section
    14. Final Thoughts Section
    
    For each section, follow these steps:
    1. In your analysis
       a) List key information from the course details relevant to that section
       b) Identify any missing crucial information
       c) Outline the main points to cover in the section
       d) Note any specific psychological techniques to employ
    2. Write the section content using markdown formatting.
    3. Ensure you're adhering to the psychological techniques and important constraints outlined below.
    
    Psychological Techniques to Incorporate:
    - Problem/Agitation/Solution framework
    - Strategic use of social proof
    - Authority building through subtle credibility markers
    - Future pacing to help visualize success
    - Risk reversal to remove purchase barriers
    - Value stacking before price reveal
    - Urgency through opportunity cost, not false scarcity
    
    Important Constraints:
    - NO fabricated personal stories or anecdotes without explicit user input
    - NO false claims or exaggerated results
    - NO high-pressure tactics or false scarcity
    - NO generic testimonials—only use real feedback provided
    - NO assumptions about the course creator's background beyond what's provided
    
    After completing all sections, review the entire copy to ensure consistency, adherence to guidelines, and psychological flow. Make any necessary adjustments to maintain a cohesive narrative throughout the landing page.
    
    Begin the copywriting process now, starting with the Hero Section. Your final output should consist only of the landing page copy and should not duplicate or rehash any of the analysis you did in the thinking block.

## How to use this for your own course or product

**Step 1: Export your entire course curriculum  
** Don't just describe your course. Export everything - module names, lesson titles, worksheets, bonuses, video counts. The more course detail you feed it, the better the copy.

**Step 2: Add all student testimonials and results**  
Every review, transformation story, before/after case study. The prompt strategically places these throughout the page for maximum psychological impact.

**Step 3: Include your complete course pricing**  
Course tiers, payment plans, bonuses, guarantees, early bird pricing. This lets the prompt properly stack educational value before revealing cost.

**Step 4: Use Claude or ChatGPT**  
Claude Opus is my preference for course copy. I also use deep thinking mode enabled. This will produce the best results, but it’s not required. You can also use Sonnet, ChatGPT, or even Gemini.

**Step 5: Add your teaching philosophy**  
The output will be 90% done. Just add your specific teaching stories and insights, and adjust tone if needed.
