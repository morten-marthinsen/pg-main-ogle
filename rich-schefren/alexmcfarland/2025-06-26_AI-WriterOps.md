## Here's what we're building today

We're combining ChatGPT or Claude's analysis power with ElevenLabs' new Voice Design v3 feature to create memorable characters for your brand.

The best part? Once you create these voices, you own them. Use them for newsletter voiceovers, social content, product launches, whatever. Your brand just became 5x more interesting.

## First, let's create your characters

Start by creating a new ChatGPT or Claude project. Or use one you already have.

Upload any brand files you have — your about page, newsletters, brand guide, writing samples, whatever shows your brand's personality.

No files? Just paste in some of your best content directly.

Now copy and paste this exact prompt:
    
    
    Analyze ALL project files to understand the brand's essence, then create 5 distinct character voices that could represent this brand.
    
    For each character, write ONE PARAGRAPH describing:
    - Their speaking style and pace
    - Emotional tone and energy level  
    - Personality quirks that affect how they talk
    - Background that influences their speech patterns
    - Overall vibe and presence
    
    Focus on HOW they sound, not WHAT they say. These descriptions will be used for AI voice generation.
    
    Create these 5 character types:
    
    1. THE REBEL - Someone who challenges the status quo
    2. THE SAGE - A wise mentor with unexpected quirks  
    3. THE MAVERICK - An unlikely expert from a surprising background
    4. THE INSIDER - Someone who's seen it all and tells it straight
    5. THE WILD CARD - A completely unexpected personality
    
    Example format:
    THE REBEL: Speaks with controlled intensity, like they're always holding back from saying what they really think. Quick bursts of words followed by meaningful pauses. Their voice carries the weight of someone who's been burned by the system but channeled their anger into change. Slight edge of sarcasm that softens when they talk about possibilities. Speeds up when excited about breaking rules, slows down when explaining why it matters. You can hear both the frustration and the hope in every sentence.
    
    Make each voice distinct and castable.
    

### Now let's make them actually speak

Head over to ElevenLabs.io with the 5 outputted character voices and sign up if you haven't already. You'll need at least a Creator account for Voice Design access (worth it if you like being creative).

Once you're in:

  1. Click "Create or Clone a Voice" in the top menu

  2. Select "Voice Design"




[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!P8YJ!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd9daa498-59bf-47fd-bcde-e89fd1a8dc8f_1322x1524.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!P8YJ!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd9daa498-59bf-47fd-bcde-e89fd1a8dc8f_1322x1524.png)

  3. You'll see a "Voice Design” box — paste one of your character descriptions here in the "Prompt” field and a text for it to read. 




[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!JJsh!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb5e9f80c-bd00-47be-a188-112c5a7a8d2b_1344x1620.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!JJsh!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb5e9f80c-bd00-47be-a188-112c5a7a8d2b_1344x1620.png)

  4. Hit "Generate"

  5. ElevenLabs will create 3 different voice options. Listen to each one

  6. When you find the perfect voice, save it to your library

  7. Repeat for all 5 characters

## What to do with your voice squad

Now you've got 5 brand voices ready to use. Here's where it gets fun:

  * **Newsletter voiceovers** : Different character each week keeps subscribers guessing

  * **Product launches** : Let each character introduce different features

  * **Social content** : Multiple personalities = multiple content streams

  * **Course materials** : Match the character to the lesson vibe

  * **Customer responses** : Pick the character that matches their energy

  * **Podcasts:** Make all of your characters host a podcast together
