## Power Word Formulas

**Analysis:** [DEPTH] + [PRECISION] + subject + "to" + [OUTCOME]
> "**Forensically dissect** market data to **unearth** hidden patterns"

**Strategy:** [CREATIVE] + "a" + [STRUCTURAL] + "that" + [ACTION]
> "**Architect** a **framework** that **catalyzes** growth"

**Problem-Solving:** [DIAGNOSTIC] + problem + "and" + [SOLUTION]
> "**Diagnose** the bottleneck and **engineer** a solution"

**Innovation:** [TRANSFORM] + "by" + [METHOD] + unexpected element
> "**Revolutionize** by **cross-pollinating** with gaming mechanics"
