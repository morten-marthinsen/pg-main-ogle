# Landing Page Engine — Typography & Copy Formatting Reference

**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** 2026-02-28
**Source:** Cross-analysis of 11 landing page specimens

---

## Headline Formatting Patterns

| Pattern | Example | Specimens | When to Use |
|---------|---------|-----------|-------------|
| **Title Case, Bold** | "Bleeding edge AI ad tracking" | Hyros | Bold claims, SaaS |
| **Sentence Case, Lower** | "find more fairways. Lower Your Scores." | MAMG | Aspirational, conversational |
| **ALL CAPS with Quotes** | 'LMNT Zero-Sugar Electrolytes' | LMNT | Product name emphasis |
| **Question Format** | "Is The Tiny Challenge™ Masterclass... Right For Me?" | Richmond Dinh | Qualification sections |
| **Long-Form Promise** | "Join The Community Mastering AI Together..." | Graziosi | Info product feature-heavy |
| **Guarantee-Lead** | "Give Us 7 Days, And We'll Place..." | Closers.io | B2B services with performance guarantee |

## Subheadline Patterns

- **Benefit reinforcement:** "12-Day Free Trial for Fix Your Slice" (MAMG)
- **Guarantee extension:** "that increases ad revenue on average by 15%...Guaranteed" (Hyros)
- **Objection pre-emption:** "Even If You Have No... following, experience, or budget" (Richmond Dinh)
- **Trial/price anchor:** "$1 trial → $37/month" (Graziosi)
- **Speed promise:** "If They Don't Book 40 Sales Calls In The First Month" (Closers.io)

## CTA Copy Patterns

| CTA Type | Examples | Frequency |
|----------|----------|-----------|
| **Action + Object** | "Add 1 Lemonade Salt to cart" (LMNT) | 3/11 |
| **Start + Possessive** | "start my free trial" (MAMG) | 2/11 |
| **Join + Community** | "Join The AI Advantage Club" (Graziosi) | 1/11 |
| **Schedule + Meeting** | "Schedule Demo" (Closers.io) | 1/11 |
| **Get Started** | "Get started below" (Hyros) | 1/11 |
| **Join + Event** | "Join The Free Masterclass Now!" (Richmond Dinh) | 1/11 |
| **Shop + Product** | "Shop The Pioneer Willow" (Sunday Red) | 1/11 |
| **Add to Bundle** | "ADD TO BUNDLE" (Native) | 1/11 |

**Best practice:** Use possessive "my" in trial CTAs ("start MY free trial"). Use action + specific object for product CTAs ("Add 1 Lemonade Salt to cart" — note the quantity and flavor specificity).

## Section Headline Patterns

| Pattern | Example | When to Use |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| **"What's inside?"** | LMNT | Ingredient/feature sections |
| **"real people, real results"** | MAMG | Testimonial sections |
| **"Need proof? Here's a lot of it"** | Hyros | When proof is your primary mechanism |
| **"frequently asked questions"** | MAMG, Richmond Dinh | FAQ sections (lowercase = approachable) |
| **"Why [Brand] Is Different"** | BIOptimizers | Company differentiation |
| **"A Letter From The Desk of..."** | Graziosi | Authority letter sections |

## Trust Signal Copy Patterns

- **"No Questions Asked Refund Policy"** (LMNT) — named guarantee
- **"Try Risk-Free • Cancel Anytime"** (MAMG) — adjacent to CTA
- **"365 Day Guarantee"** (BIOptimizers) — time-specific guarantee
- **"If We Don't Perform, You Don't Pay!"** (Closers.io) — performance guarantee
- **"15% more...or you don't pay"** (Hyros) — metric-specific guarantee
- **"Satisfaction Guarantee" + "Secure Checkout" + "1 Million+ Customers"** (BIOptimizers) — triple trust badge strip
- **"FREE Shipping On All US Orders"** (LMNT) — shipping trust signal
- **"No Sugar, No Gluten, No Dodgy Ingredients"** (LMNT) — negative-claim trust (what it DOESN'T have)
