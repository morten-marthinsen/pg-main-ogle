## SECTION 11: FAQ (5 Questions)

**What supplements does ARMRA Colostrum™ replace?**
It depends on your objectives and health regimen. ARMRA Colostrum™ is a bioactive food and a source of 400+ bioactive nutrients. It works on foundational pathways in the body that translate to whole-body benefits, across immunity, gut health, energy, fitness recovery, skin health, hair health, and more. Thus, for those taking supplements to achieve such goals, ARMRA Colostrum™ can serve as an excellent supplement for whole body health.

**How is ARMRA Colostrum™ sourced?**
Colostrum is a dairy food, and we take a lot of pride in the responsible sourcing practices of ARMRA Colostrum™. The calves' needs always come first. We partner with family-owned dairy farms throughout the US, and source colostrum strictly from the surplus of grass-fed cows. After birth, cows produce colostrum in excess, with calves only consuming between 33%-50% of it. The surplus is considered a waste product in the dairy industry. Instead, ARMRA® takes this surplus and upcycles it, ensuring that calves get fed all they need first and no colostrum goes to waste.

**What testing does ARMRA Colostrum™ undergo and what certifications does it hold?**
All ARMRA® products are food-grade, made in the USA, and undergo rigorous 3rd party testing. We manufacture ARMRA Colostrum™ in FDA-registered facilities to adhere to the strict requirements of the FDA, and use Current Good Manufacturing Practices (CGMPs). Extensive audits on every production include glyphosate, heavy metal, contaminant, and microbiological testing by FDA-approved, ISO/IEC-certified third-party laboratories. Additionally, all ARMRA Colostrum™ products are officially Keto-Certified.

**What is the best way to take ARMRA®?**
Many customers enjoy dry-scooping Unflavored ARMRA Colostrum, pouring it directly into their mouth on an empty stomach or stirring it into just a gulp of water. Unflavored can also be stirred into liquid, mixed with yogurt, granola, or chia seeds. Just avoid hot foods or beverages. For all flavors, mix into liquids.
