## Feature Hierarchy

### Hero Feature

**Auto-Glide Sole**
- **Naming Format:** Action-Benefit + Component
- **Caveman Test:** Sole glides through ground → no more chunks
- **Micro-script:** "The 'Auto-Glide Sole' gives you Four-Way Forgiveness because instead of digging, its four-way cambered shape glides through the ground automatically — regardless of your attack angle."
- **Benefit headline:** "Your Fat Shot Fix"
- **Sub-mechanism:** "Four-Way Forgiveness" (named benefit of the four-way camber)
- **Copy technique:** The feature name contains the SOLUTION (glide) and the component (sole). Compare to 357 where the hero feature name is more technical and needs a nickname. Here, the name IS the nickname.
- **Naming verdict:** EXCELLENT — "Auto-Glide" is pure caveman with no translation needed. "Sole" identifies the component. The name alone communicates the full value proposition.

### Supporting Features

**Distance Control Weighting**
- **Naming Format:** Benefit + Technology Type
- **Caveman Test:** Special weighting → consistent distance even on mishits
- **Micro-script:** "Enjoy consistent ball speed across the entire face — not just the sweet spot... thanks to Distance Control Weighting."
- **Benefit headline:** "Delicate Distance Control... Even On Mis-Hits"
- **Sub-mechanism:** "Sole Stability Wing" (component within the weighting system)
- **Naming verdict:** GOOD — "Distance Control" is the benefit; "Weighting" is the mechanism type. Simple and functional.

**Square-Shoot Alignment**
- **Naming Format:** Action-Action + Feature Type
- **Caveman Test:** Square the face → shoot straight
- **Micro-script:** "'Square and Shoot' Alignment pairs a straight leading edge with center-face markings, so you instantly square the face at address."
- **Benefit headline:** "Perfect Aim — Every Time."
- **Micro-tagline:** "Square It, Swing It, Stick It"
- **Naming verdict:** STRONG — "Square-Shoot" is a two-verb compound that tells the full story in two words. The tagline extends it into a Rule of Three.

**Spin-Fast Face**
- **Naming Format:** Benefit-Benefit + Component
- **Caveman Test:** Face creates spin → ball stops on the green
- **Micro-script:** "The 'Spin-Fast Face' combines max-volume conforming grooves with laser etchings to generate maximum legal spin on full swings AND partial shots."
- **Benefit headline:** "Spin It To Win It (Legally!)"
- **Micro-tagline:** "Stop, Drop, And Roll It Close"
- **Naming verdict:** STRONG — Stacks two benefits (spin + fast) onto the component (face). "Legally!" in the headline adds personality and addresses a regulatory question.

**Motion Swing Weighting**
- **Naming Format:** Action + Feature Type
- **Caveman Test:** Counterweight stops flipping → solid chipping
- **Micro-script:** "We added 'Swing Stability Weighting' — a stepless shaft combined with a 40-gram counterweight — that stops you from flipping at impact."
- **Benefit headline:** "Stops Flipping For Better Chipping"
- **Note:** The feature name in the copy ("Swing Stability Weighting") differs from the section header ("Motion Swing Weighting"). This is a naming inconsistency specimen — shows what NOT to do.
- **Naming verdict:** MODERATE — The name is less intuitive than other ONE.1 features. "Motion Swing" doesn't immediately communicate the benefit. The headline ("Stops Flipping") is actually the better name.
