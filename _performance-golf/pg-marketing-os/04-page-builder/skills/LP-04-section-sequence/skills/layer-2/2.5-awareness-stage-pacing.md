# 2.5 — Awareness-Stage Pacing Optimizer

**Skill:** LP-04-section-sequence
**Position:** Layer 2, Microskill 5
**Purpose:** Adjust section pacing, word count proportions, and section inclusion/exclusion based on the audience's Schwartz awareness stage. Stage 1 (Unaware) needs extended education before any mechanism reveal. Stage 5 (Most Aware) skips story entirely and shows price immediately. This microskill modifies the outputs of 2.1 through 2.4 — it is the final Layer 2 adjustment pass before validation.
**Effort Level:** high
**Dependencies:** `brief-verified.md` (0.1), `word-count-plan.md` (2.1), `proof-density-plan.md` (2.2), `cta-placement-plan.md` (2.4), `template-selection.md` (1.1)
**Output:** `pacing-adjustment.md`

## Awareness-Stage Pacing Table

| Stage | Name | Lead/Story Adjustment | Mechanism Timing | Price Timing | Proof Strategy |
|-------|------|----------------------|-----------------|-------------|---------------|
| **1** | Unaware | Extend lead (+50% word count). Story is critical — never skip. | Delay mechanism until ~40% of page | No price until ~60% of page | Light early proof. Heavy proof must follow education. |
| **2** | Problem-Aware | Standard lead. Story recommended. | Mechanism at ~30–35% of page | Price at ~55% of page | Standard proof wave pattern |
| **3** | Solution-Aware | Compress story. Lead can reference the solution category. | Mechanism differentiation early (~25%) | Price at ~45–50% of page | Medium-heavy proof early — they're comparing |
| **4** | Product-Aware | Story optional or very brief. Above-fold can name product. | Mechanism in above-fold subhead acceptable | Price visible by ~30%. Above-fold price OK for Type B. | Proof density very high early — they need validation |
| **5** | Most Aware | Skip story entirely. Product intro in above-fold. | Mechanism not needed — they already know how it works | Price immediately visible. Above-fold for all types. | Proof = primary trust driver. Heavy from Section 1. |

## Execution Logic

**Step 1:** Load the awareness stage from `brief-verified.md`.

**Step 2:** Apply lead/story adjustments:
- Stage 1: Increase lead word count by +50% from 2.1 plan. If story section was optional, make it mandatory.
- Stage 2: No change to lead/story.
- Stage 3: Reduce story word count by 30%. Lead can open with solution category reference.
- Stage 4: Story section becomes optional (remove if present, or reduce to <200 words). Lead compressed.
- Stage 5: Remove story section entirely. Lead replaced with direct product intro.

**Step 3:** Apply mechanism timing adjustments:
- Calculate current mechanism section position as percentage of total sections.
- Compare to stage target. If mechanism appears too early or too late, recommend reorder.

**Step 4:** Apply price timing adjustments:
- Calculate current pricing section position as percentage of total sections.
- Compare to stage target. If price appears too early for stage, flag.
- Stage 4–5: Allow price in above-fold or very early sections.

**Step 5:** Apply proof density adjustments:
- Stage 1–2: Verify proof follows education (no heavy proof in first 25% of page).
- Stage 3: Allow medium proof earlier (comparing audiences need proof sooner).
- Stage 4–5: Front-load proof density. Move heavy proof blocks earlier in sequence.

**Step 6:** Apply CTA timing adjustments:
- Stage 1: First primary buy CTA no earlier than 50% of page.
- Stage 2: First primary buy CTA no earlier than 40% of page.
- Stage 3: Standard CTA placement.
- Stage 4–5: First primary buy CTA can appear in first 25% of page.

**Step 7:** Document all adjustments. Do NOT silently modify upstream files — produce a clear adjustment document that Layer 3 validators will check against.

## Quality Criteria
- Awareness stage explicitly identified and applied — not default pacing
- All 5 adjustment areas addressed (lead/story, mechanism, price, proof, CTA)
- Adjustments are proportional to stage distance from Stage 2–3 default
- No silent modifications — every change documented with rationale
- Stage 1 pages have extended education; Stage 5 pages are compressed and proof-heavy

## Failure Modes
- Awareness stage missing from brief → Default to Stage 2 (Problem-Aware) and flag as WARNING: "Awareness stage assumed as Stage 2. Confirm with client."
- Stage 1 page with mechanism in above-fold headline → CONFLICT. Flag: "Above-fold mechanism reveal conflicts with Stage 1 pacing. Recommend removing mechanism from headline."
- Stage 5 page with 8+ educational sections → WARNING. Stage 5 audiences don't need education. Recommend compressing or removing educational sections.
- Adjustments conflict with mandatory section requirements → Mandatory sections cannot be removed even for Stage 5. Compress word count instead of removing.
