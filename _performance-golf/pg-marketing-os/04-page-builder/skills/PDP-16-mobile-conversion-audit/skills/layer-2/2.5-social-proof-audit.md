# 2.5 — Social Proof Audit

> **Skill:** PDP-16-mobile-conversion-audit
> **Layer:** 2 (Audit Execution)
> **Position:** 5 of 8
> **Model:** sonnet
> **Purpose:** Audit the social proof / review system against Baymard best practices. Reviews are the #2 conversion driver on a PDP after the buy box. A review system without a tappable histogram, keyword filters, and UGC photos is leaving conversion on the table. This section is weighted at 15% of the composite score.

## Execution Logic

**POSITIONAL REINFORCEMENT:** You are auditing the social proof / review system. This includes the review histogram, keyword filters, UGC positioning, loading behavior, verified badges, and review card template. You are NOT auditing UGC testimonial videos in BTF sections (that is 2.4) or the rating strip in the buy box (that is 2.2). Focus on the dedicated review section.

**Step 1:** From `assembled-page-load.md`, extract review system specifications:
- Review section position in page
- Histogram specification
- Filter/sort capabilities
- UGC photo/video handling
- Review loading behavior (load more, pagination, infinite scroll)
- Review card structure
- Verified buyer badge specification
- Merchant response specification (bonus, not scored)

**Step 2:** Evaluate each criterion:

### Criterion 5.1: Review Histogram
- **Baymard (7.1):** A single number is insufficient. Users need distribution — "safe" 4.5 vs "controversial" 4.5.
- **PASS:** Bar chart at top of review section. Bars are tappable to filter reviews by star rating.
- **FAIL:** No histogram, histogram present but not tappable, or not specified.
- **Evidence required:** Quote histogram specification or note absence.

### Criterion 5.2: Keyword Filter Chips
- **Baymard (7.3):** Scrolling through 500 reviews is impossible on mobile. Users need targeted access.
- **PASS:** 5-8 keyword chips above reviews (e.g., "Taste," "Shipping," "Results," "Texture"). Tapping chips filters reviews containing that keyword.
- **FAIL:** No keyword filters, fewer than 5 chips, or not specified.
- **Evidence required:** List the keyword chips specified, or note absence.

### Criterion 5.3: UGC Above Text Reviews
- **Baymard (7.2):** Customer photos = "unfiltered truth" to verify color, texture, size.
- **PASS:** Customer photo strip or grid positioned above text reviews. Tapping photo opens full-screen gallery overlay with swipe across ALL customer photos.
- **FAIL:** UGC photos below text reviews, mixed into review cards only, or not specified.
- **Evidence required:** Note UGC position relative to text reviews.

### Criterion 5.4: Load More (Not Pagination)
- **Baymard (7.4):** Pagination forces new page loads and position loss. Infinite scroll causes position loss. Load More preserves context.
- **PASS:** "Load More" button specified (15-30 reviews per batch).
- **FAIL:** Pagination specified, infinite scroll specified, or loading behavior not specified.
- **Evidence required:** Quote loading behavior specification.

### Criterion 5.5: Verified Buyer Badges
- **Baymard (7.5):** Users are skeptical of fake reviews. Verified buyer badges build trust.
- **PASS:** "Verified Buyer" badge specified on review cards.
- **FAIL:** No verified badge specification, or badge optional.
- **Evidence required:** Note badge specification or absence.

### Criterion 5.6: Review Card Template Complete
- **Baymard (7.5):** Review cards need structure: Stars + Bold Headline + Reviewer Attributes + Body.
- **PASS:** Review card template includes: (1) star rating, (2) review headline/title, (3) reviewer name + attributes (location, verified badge, "Time Owned"), (4) body text.
- **FAIL:** Missing any of the 4 required card elements, or card template not specified.
- **Evidence required:** List which card elements are specified vs missing.

**Step 3:** Write finding for each criterion.

**Step 4:** Write `social-proof-audit.md`.

## Quality Criteria

- All 6 criteria evaluated
- Histogram evaluated for TAPPABILITY, not just presence
- UGC evaluated for POSITION (above text reviews), not just presence
- Load More specifically checked (not just "reviews load somehow")
- Review card template checked for all 4 elements
- UNSPECIFIED = FAIL
- File exists after execution

## Failure Modes

- No review section in assembled page -> Score all 6 as FAIL. A PDP without reviews is a major conversion gap.
- Review system specified but minimal (just a rating display) -> FAIL on histogram, filters, UGC, card template. Partial implementation is still a fail.
- PDP-05 output not incorporated into LP-15 assembly -> Note as root cause. The review system was designed but not assembled.
