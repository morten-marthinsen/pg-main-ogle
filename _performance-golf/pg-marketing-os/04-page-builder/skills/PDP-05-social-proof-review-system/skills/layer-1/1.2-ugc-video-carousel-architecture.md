# 1.2 — UGC Video Carousel Architecture

**Skill:** PDP-05-social-proof-review-system
**Position:** Layer 1, Microskill 2
**Purpose:** Design the UGC photo strip and video carousel system — photo strip position, full-screen gallery overlay, video carousel with muted autoplay, avatar diversity requirements, and fallback behavior when no UGC exists. UGC positioned ABOVE text reviews is a Baymard-validated trust multiplier.
**Effort Level:** medium
**Dependencies:** `input-verification.md` (0.1), `pdp-reference-load.md` (0.3), `review-system-architecture.md` (1.1)
**Output:** `ugc-carousel-architecture.md`

## Execution Logic

**Step 1:** Determine UGC availability from proof inventory:
- `has_ugc_photos = Y` AND `has_ugc_video = Y` -> Full UGC system (photo strip + video carousel)
- `has_ugc_photos = Y` AND `has_ugc_video = N` -> Photo strip only
- `has_ugc_photos = N` AND `has_ugc_video = Y` -> Video carousel only (extract thumbnails for strip)
- Both `N` or `not_available` -> Omit UGC section entirely. Do NOT use placeholder images. Do NOT use stock photos. Do NOT use AI-generated images.

**Step 2:** Design the UGC photo strip:
- Position: ABOVE text reviews, below histogram (mandatory position per Baymard)
- Layout: horizontal scrollable strip of customer-submitted photos
- Photo count: display 6-10 thumbnail squares visible in viewport
- Overflow: horizontal scroll indicator (dots or partial photo peek)
- Photo size: square thumbnails (1:1 aspect ratio), consistent sizing
- Diversity requirement: photos from MULTIPLE different customers (not 5 photos from one reviewer)
- Interaction: tap any photo -> full-screen gallery overlay

**Step 3:** Design the full-screen gallery overlay:
- Trigger: tap on any UGC photo thumbnail
- Behavior: opens full-screen overlay with the tapped photo displayed large
- Navigation: swipe left/right to move through all UGC photos
- Close: X button top-right or swipe down to dismiss
- Context: below the photo, show reviewer name + star rating + first sentence of review
- Background: darkened/blurred page behind overlay
- Performance: lazy-load high-res images only when overlay opens (thumbnails are pre-loaded)

**Step 4:** Design the video carousel (if UGC video available):
- Position: within or adjacent to photo strip (can be a separate row below photo strip, or integrated)
- Video count: 5-7 video slots (even if fewer videos available — remaining slots hidden, not empty)
- Layout: horizontal scrollable row of video thumbnails with play icon overlay
- Autoplay behavior: muted autoplay on scroll-into-view. Video plays silently until user taps for audio.
- Sound control: tap to unmute. Mute icon visible during autoplay.
- Duration indicator: small timestamp overlay on thumbnail (e.g., "0:32")
- Avatar diversity: video thumbnails should show different people (age, gender, ethnicity diversity)
- Thumbnail source: auto-generated from video frame at 2-3 second mark (not black frame)

**Step 5:** Define video carousel interaction:
- Tap on video thumbnail -> video expands to large player (inline expand or overlay)
- Expanded player: unmuted audio, scrub bar, full controls
- Auto-advance: after video ends, auto-play next video (muted) with 3-second countdown
- Swipe: swipe between videos in expanded view

**Step 6:** Define diversity requirements:
- Photo strip: minimum 4 different customers represented (no single-reviewer domination)
- Video carousel: at least 3 different people visible in thumbnails
- Demographic variety: mix of ages, genders (where available)
- Product context variety: different usage contexts (home, outdoor, morning routine, etc.)
- FORBIDDEN: using the same photo/video in multiple locations on page
- FORBIDDEN: stock photos or AI-generated UGC (if no real UGC, omit section)

**Step 7:** Define fallback behavior:
- No UGC photos AND no UGC video -> hide entire UGC section (do not show empty state)
- Fewer than 3 photos -> show what exists, but add note: "UGC section thin — consider requesting customer photos for future iteration"
- Fewer than 3 videos -> show available videos without carousel format (stack vertically or show as featured)
- UGC availability unknown (not_available) -> spec the UGC section structure as a PLACEHOLDER with clear instructions for the frontend team: "Implement this section IF UGC photos/videos are provided. Hide entirely if none."

## Quality Criteria
- UGC photo strip positioned ABOVE text reviews (Baymard mandatory)
- Full-screen gallery overlay specified with swipe navigation
- Video carousel has muted autoplay behavior (not autoplay with sound)
- Diversity requirements documented (minimum unique customers, demographic variety)
- Fallback behavior clearly specified for all scenarios (no UGC, partial UGC, unknown)
- No stock photos or AI-generated images used as UGC substitutes
- Integration with 1.1 review system page hierarchy confirmed

## Failure Modes
- UGC photo strip below text reviews -> HALT. Move above text reviews per Baymard.
- Stock photos used as UGC -> HALT. Remove and set to omit if no real UGC.
- Video autoplay with sound -> HALT. Set to muted autoplay.
- Empty UGC section displayed -> HALT. Hide section if no content available.
- Single-customer UGC domination (all photos from one person) -> HALT. Enforce diversity requirement.
