# LP-07 — Hero Section Writer

**Pipeline Position:** After LP-03 (Above-Fold), LP-04 (Section Sequence). Feeds LP-14, LP-15. Arena: 3-Lens.

---

## PURPOSE

Write the most conversion-critical copy on the page. The hero section determines whether 70-80% of visitors stay or leave within 5 seconds.

**Success Criteria:**
- 5+ headline candidates generated via 3-Lens Arena
- Pre-head, deck copy, and lead copy written (Type A) or product title, description, benefits (Type B)
- Copy answers: What is this? Who is this for? Why should I care?
- hero-section-package.json produced with all variants and Arena scoring
