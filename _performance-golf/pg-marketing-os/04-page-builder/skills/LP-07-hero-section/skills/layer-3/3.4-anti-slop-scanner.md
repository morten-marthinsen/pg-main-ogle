# 3.4 — Anti-Slop Scanner

**Skill:** LP-07-hero-section
**Position:** Layer 3, Microskill 4
**Purpose:** Systematically scan every copy element in the hero section against the 5-category forbidden word list from LP-07-AGENT.md. A single instance of any forbidden word or phrase = FAIL. This is the quality gate that prevents AI telltales from reaching the final package.
**Effort Level:** low
**Dependencies:** `lead-final.md` (3.1) OR `typeb-abovefold.md` (2.5), `HERO_SELECTION.yaml` (selected headline, deck, pre-head)
**Output:** `anti-slop-scan.md`

---

## Input Schema

```yaml
input:
  elements_to_scan:
    pre_head: "[selected pre-head text from HERO_SELECTION.yaml — or 'None']"
    headline: "[selected headline text from HERO_SELECTION.yaml]"
    deck_copy: "[selected deck text from HERO_SELECTION.yaml]"
    lead_copy: "[full text of lead-final.md — Type A/Hybrid]"
    type_b_copy: "[product title + description + bullets + badges — Type B/Hybrid]"
```

---

## Execution Logic

1. **Load the 5-category forbidden word list** from LP-07-AGENT.md:

   **Category 1 — AI Telltales:**
   revolutionary, game-changing, groundbreaking, cutting-edge, breakthrough, innovative, state-of-the-art, next-level, unprecedented, transformative, unlock your potential, harness the power, leverage, synergy, holistic approach

   **Category 2 — Vague Benefit Language:**
   feel better, feel amazing, live your best life, optimal health, overall wellness, total wellbeing, achieve your goals, on your journey, life-changing, powerful results, incredible benefits, amazing formula

   **Category 3 — Unearned Claims:**
   the best, the most effective, the #1 [without sourced data], proven [without specifying what was proven], scientifically proven [without study], doctor-approved [without named doctor], clinically proven [without citation]

   **Category 4 — Generic CTA Language:**
   click here to learn more, discover the secret, find out how, don't miss out, act now, limited time offer [without deadline]

   **Category 5 — Passive/Hedge Language:**
   may help, could potentially, might support, has been known to, some people find, many users report [in headline — acceptable in testimonials]

2. **Scan each element independently.** For each element (pre-head, headline, deck, lead/above-fold copy):
   - Check every word and phrase against all 5 categories
   - Record ANY match with: the forbidden word/phrase, the element it was found in, and the category

3. **Check for near-misses** — words that are not on the list but carry the same AI-telltale signal:
   - "Unlock" (any usage in hero copy)
   - "Empower" / "empowering"
   - "Journey" (when used metaphorically)
   - "Seamless" / "seamlessly"
   - "Robust"
   - "Elevate" / "elevated"

4. **Determine scan result:**
   - Zero forbidden words AND zero near-misses → **PASS**
   - Zero forbidden words but near-misses found → **PASS WITH FLAG** (note near-misses for human awareness)
   - One or more forbidden words found → **FAIL** (list every instance, require revision)

5. **If FAIL:** For each forbidden word found, provide a specific replacement suggestion that conveys the same meaning without the AI telltale.

## Quality Criteria
- Every copy element scanned — no elements skipped (except pre-head if "None")
- All 5 categories checked explicitly (each category listed in output, even if "None found")
- Near-miss check conducted in addition to the forbidden list
- FAIL result includes specific replacement suggestions, not just identification
- Scan result is binary: any forbidden word = FAIL, period. No "minor" or "acceptable" exceptions.

## Failure Modes
- Scanning only the headline and skipping the lead → All elements must be scanned. The lead is 300-1000 words and the most likely location for AI telltales.
- Marking a forbidden word as "acceptable in this context" → No context exceptions in the hero section. The word list is absolute for hero copy. Remove and replace.
- Missing compound phrases ("unlock your potential" scanned as two separate words that each pass individually) → Scan for multi-word phrases as units.
- Near-miss found but not flagged → Near-misses should be noted even on a PASS result. Human may want to address them.
