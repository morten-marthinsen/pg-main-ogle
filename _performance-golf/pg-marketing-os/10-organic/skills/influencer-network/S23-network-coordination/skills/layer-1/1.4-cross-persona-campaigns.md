# 1.4 Cross-Persona Campaigns

## Purpose
Design campaigns where multiple personas address the same topic from different angles simultaneously. Creates network-level impact while maintaining persona differentiation and authentic appearance.

## Model Assignment
**Model:** claude-opus-4-20250514

## Input Requirements
- Narrative arc planning (from 1.3)
- Persona Bibles (niche, archetype, voice)
- Network calendar sync (from 1.1)
- Engagement choreography (from 1.2)

## Execution Protocol

### MUST
- Design campaign concepts: topics that warrant multi-persona treatment
- Assign persona angles: how each persona approaches topic from their niche/perspective
- Create differentiation strategy: ensure personas take genuinely different angles (not surface variations)
- Design launch choreography: who posts when, over what timeframe (spread 3-7 days)
- Plan engagement coordination: how personas engage with each other's campaign content
- Include campaign messaging arc: collective narrative built across personas
- Design measurement plan: how to assess campaign impact at network level
- Create fallback options if campaign doesn't gain traction

### NEVER
- Create campaigns where personas take identical angles
- Launch all campaign content simultaneously (signals coordination)
- Design campaigns without clear differentiation strategy
- Skip engagement coordination (personas need to amplify each other strategically)
- Forget measurement plan
- Create campaigns that reveal parent brand connection

## Output
**Filename:** `cross-persona-campaigns.md`

**Format:** Markdown campaign playbook

**Minimum Size:** 5KB

**Required Sections:**
1. Campaign Concepts (3-5 campaign ideas with network-level impact potential)
2. Persona Angle Assignments (how each persona approaches topic, differentiation verified)
3. Launch Choreography (posting sequence, timeframe, momentum design)
4. Engagement Coordination (how personas amplify each other during campaign)
5. Collective Narrative Arc (story built across personas)
6. Measurement Plan (network-level KPIs, success criteria)
7. Fallback Options (if campaign doesn't gain traction)

## Quality Gates
- [ ] Campaign concepts warrant multi-persona treatment
- [ ] Persona angles genuinely differentiated (not surface variations)
- [ ] Launch choreography spreads across 3-7 days
- [ ] Engagement coordination maintains authenticity
- [ ] Collective narrative arc coherent
- [ ] Measurement plan defines success criteria

## Handoff
Feeds into: 1.5 Brand Firewall (campaigns must not reveal brand connection)
