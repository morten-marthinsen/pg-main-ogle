# 1.6 Pain/Desire Mapping

## Purpose
The core of all marketing — what hurts and what they want. This is three-layer depth (surface → deeper → root) for both pains and desires.

---

## Input Requirements
- language-mining.yaml (from 1.4) for pain expressions
- psychographic-depth.yaml (from 1.2) for motivational context
- competitor-audience.yaml (from 1.5) for validation

---

## Execution Protocol

### Step 1: Surface Pains (MINIMUM 3 REQUIRED)
```
SURFACE PAINS = What they openly complain about

EXTRACT from:
  - Language mining pain expressions
  - Competitor comment sections
  - Forum posts
  - Social media complaints

FOR EACH pain (≥3):
  DOCUMENT:
    pain: "[Exact description in their words]"
    frequency: high | medium | low (how often mentioned)
    intensity: high | medium | low (how much it bothers them)
    source: "[Where we observed this]"

EXAMPLES:
  - "I can't stick to a routine"
  - "I don't have time to create content"
  - "My engagement is terrible"
```

### Step 2: Deeper Pains
```
DEEPER PAINS = Underlying issues behind surface pains

FOR EACH surface pain cluster:
  ASK: "What's really causing this?"

IDENTIFY 3-5 deeper pains:
  DOCUMENT:
    pain: "[Underlying issue]"
    connection_to_surface: "[Which surface pain(s) this drives]"
    emotional_weight: "[How it makes them feel]"
    evidence: "[How we know this is real]"

EXAMPLE:
  Surface: "I can't stick to a routine"
  Deeper: "No accountability system — feel alone in the journey"
  Emotional weight: "Isolated, undisciplined, like a failure"
```

### Step 3: Root Pains
```
ROOT PAINS = Core fears/frustrations that drive behavior

THESE are rarely stated directly. Infer from patterns.

IDENTIFY 1-3 root pains:
  DOCUMENT:
    pain: "[Core fear/frustration]"
    rarely_stated: "[Why they don't say this directly]"
    drives_behavior: "[How this shapes their decisions]"
    connection_to_surface: "[How it manifests]"

EXAMPLE:
  Root: "Fear of being irrelevant — becoming invisible in their industry"
  Rarely stated: "Admitting this feels weak/insecure"
  Drives behavior: "Overposting, engagement anxiety, constant platform switching"
```

### Step 4: Stated Desires (MINIMUM 3 REQUIRED)
```
STATED DESIRES = What they say they want

EXTRACT from:
  - Language mining desire expressions
  - Goal statements in comments/posts
  - "I wish I could..." statements

FOR EACH desire (≥3):
  DOCUMENT:
    desire: "[What they say they want]"
    common_phrasing: "[Exact phrases they use]"
    frequency: high | medium | low
    source: "[Where observed]"

EXAMPLES:
  - "I want to grow my audience to 10k"
  - "I want to quit my 9-5"
  - "I want consistent engagement"
```

### Step 5: Unstated Desires
```
UNSTATED DESIRES = What they really want but don't say

IDENTIFY 3-5 unstated desires:
  DOCUMENT:
    desire: "[What they actually want]"
    why_unstated: "[Social pressure/awareness/fear]"
    evidence: "[Behavioral clues]"

EXAMPLE:
  Stated: "I want to grow my audience"
  Unstated: "I want recognition and status in my industry"
  Why unstated: "Sounds shallow/egotistical to admit"
  Evidence: "Constantly mention follower counts, compare to others, seek validation"
```

### Step 6: Identity Desires
```
IDENTITY DESIRES = Who they want to become

IDENTIFY 2-3 identity transformations:
  DOCUMENT:
    desire: "[Future identity they want]"
    current_gap: "[Where they are vs where they want to be]"
    marker_of_success: "[How they'll know they've arrived]"

EXAMPLE:
  Desire: "Become a thought leader in my space"
  Current gap: "Unknown, just starting out"
  Marker: "Speaking at conferences, being quoted, having a waiting list"
```

## Output
- **File:** `pain-desire-mapping.yaml`
- **Format:** YAML
- **Minimum size:** 3KB

```yaml
pain_mapping:
  surface_pains:
    - pain: "[Description]"
      frequency: high | medium | low
      intensity: high | medium | low
      source: "[Where observed]"
    # MINIMUM 3 REQUIRED

  deeper_pains:
    - pain: "[Underlying issue]"
      connection_to_surface: "[Which surface pains]"
      emotional_weight: "[How it feels]"
      evidence: "[Proof]"
    # 3-5 entries

  root_pains:
    - pain: "[Core fear/frustration]"
      rarely_stated: "[Why not said]"
      drives_behavior: "[Behavioral impact]"
      connection_to_surface: "[How it manifests]"
    # 1-3 entries

desire_mapping:
  stated_desires:
    - desire: "[What they say]"
      common_phrasing: "[Exact phrases]"
      frequency: high | medium | low
      source: "[Where observed]"
    # MINIMUM 3 REQUIRED

  unstated_desires:
    - desire: "[What they actually want]"
      why_unstated: "[Reason for hiding]"
      evidence: "[Behavioral clues]"
    # 3-5 entries

  identity_desires:
    - desire: "[Future identity]"
      current_gap: "[Current vs desired state]"
      marker_of_success: "[How they'll know]"
    # 2-3 entries

verification:
  surface_pains_count: [number]
  stated_desires_count: [number]
  minimum_requirements_met: true | false  # MUST be true
  three_layer_depth_complete: true | false  # MUST be true for both pain and desire
```

---

## Quality Gates
- [ ] ≥3 surface pains documented with frequency and intensity
- [ ] ≥3 deeper pains documented with emotional weight
- [ ] ≥1 root pain documented
- [ ] ≥3 stated desires documented with phrasing
- [ ] ≥3 unstated desires documented with evidence
- [ ] ≥2 identity desires documented
- [ ] All three layers (surface/deeper/root) complete for pains
- [ ] All three layers (stated/unstated/identity) complete for desires

**CRITICAL:** If surface_pains < 3 OR stated_desires < 3, this is a BLOCKING FAILURE.
