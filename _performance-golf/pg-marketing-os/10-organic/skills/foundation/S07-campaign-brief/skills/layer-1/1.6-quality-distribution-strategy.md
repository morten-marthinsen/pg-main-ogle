# 1.6 Quality & Distribution Strategy

## Purpose
Set quality thresholds from VSF and define distribution/amplification strategy for published content.

---

## Input Requirements

From Layer 1:
- `1.1-synthesis.yaml` (VSF context)
- `1.3-calendar.yaml` (hero content)

---

## Execution Protocol

### Step 1: Set Virality Targets

Pull from VSF:

```yaml
virality_targets:
  minimum_score: [X]  # Hard floor—below this = do not publish
  target_score: [Y]   # All content should aim here
  breakout_threshold: [Z]  # Viral potential—maximum push

  dimension_priorities:
    1: "[Most important dimension for this campaign]"
    2: "[Second priority]"
    3: "[Third priority]"

  hero_content_target: [X+]  # Higher bar for pillar content

  rationale: |
    [Why these targets based on campaign goals]
```

---

### Step 2: Pre-Publish Quality Gates

Define the process before anything goes live:

```yaml
pre_publish_gates:
  gate_1:
    name: "Quick-Score Check"
    process: |
      [Use VSF quick-score checklist]
      - All must-haves present?
      - Any red flags?
      - Count green lights
    pass_criteria: "0 red flags, all must-haves present"

  gate_2:
    name: "Full VSF Scoring"
    process: |
      [Score all 8 dimensions, calculate final score]
    pass_criteria: "Score ≥ minimum_publish [X]"
    if_fail: "Rework or kill content"

  gate_3:
    name: "Voice Alignment Check"
    process: |
      [Use voice_check_protocol from 1.5]
    pass_criteria: "No banned words, tone matches guidance"
    if_fail: "Mandatory rework—voice violations non-negotiable"
```

---

### Step 3: Distribution Strategy

How content gets amplified:

```yaml
distribution_strategy:
  cross_platform_sequence: |
    [From 1.4 platform plan]
    Primary → evaluate performance → repurpose to secondary

  amplification_tactics:
    - tactic: "Boost Budget Allocation"
      trigger: "Content scores ≥85 (viral potential)"
      action: "Allocate $X boost budget, target lookalike audience"

    - tactic: "Community Share Push"
      trigger: "Content scores 75-84 (strong)"
      action: "Share to Story with engagement prompt, DM to key community members"

    - tactic: "Email Integration"
      trigger: "Content hits 10K+ views in 48hrs"
      action: "Feature in weekly newsletter, drive more platform traffic"

  engagement_protocol:
    first_hour: |
      [From 1.4 platform plan]
      Critical window for algorithm signal

    ongoing: |
      [Daily engagement routine]
```

---

### Step 4: Repurpose Plan

How one piece becomes many:

```yaml
repurpose_plan:
  primary_to_secondary:
    - source: "Instagram Reel"
      becomes:
        - "TikTok (same content, adapted caption)"
        - "YouTube Short (if strong performer)"
        - "LinkedIn (if professional topic, text version)"

  long_form_extraction:
    - source: "Carousel with high saves"
      becomes:
        - "Email newsletter feature"
        - "Blog post expansion"
        - "Lead magnet if solves key pain"

  content_library:
    action: "Save all 75+ scored content to evergreen library"
    use: "Repurpose in 6-12 months or use in paid ads"
```

---

### Step 5: Performance Monitoring

What gets tracked and when:

```yaml
performance_monitoring:
  daily_metrics:
    - "Follower growth (net new)"
    - "Engagement rate per post"
    - "Top performing post (learn from)"

  weekly_metrics:
    - "Avg VSF score of published content"
    - "Function mix achieved (awareness/engagement/conversion/community)"
    - "Hook type performance (which hooks won)"

  milestone_check:
    frequency: "Every 2 weeks"
    evaluate:
      - "Are we on track to hit milestone targets?"
      - "What's working (double down)?"
      - "What's not working (pivot)?"
```
