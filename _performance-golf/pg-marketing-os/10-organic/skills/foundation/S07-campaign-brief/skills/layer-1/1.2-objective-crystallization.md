# 1.2 Objective Crystallization

## Purpose
Define specific, measurable campaign objectives with baselines, targets, and timeline. No vague goals allowed.

---

## Input Requirements

From Layer 0:
- `0.4-strategic-synthesis.yaml` (strategic priorities)

From Layer 1:
- `1.1-synthesis.yaml` (foundation context)

User input (required):
- Current account metrics (baseline)
- Campaign duration
- Success definition

---

## Execution Protocol

### Step 1: Define Primary Goal

The ONE metric that determines campaign success:

```yaml
primary_goal: |
  [Specific, measurable outcome with timeline]

  GOOD: "Grow Instagram from 12,000 to 50,000 followers in 90 days
  while maintaining 5%+ engagement rate"

  BAD: "Grow followers and engagement"
  BAD: "Increase brand awareness"
```

**MUST include:**
- Specific metric
- Current baseline
- Target number
- Timeline
- Quality constraint (not just quantity)

---

### Step 2: Define Success Metrics

At least 2 metrics with current/target:

```yaml
success_metrics:
  - metric: "Follower Growth"
    current: 12000
    target: 50000
    measurement: "Weekly tracking via platform analytics"
    why_this_matters: "[Connection to business goal]"

  - metric: "Engagement Rate"
    current: 3.2
    target: 5.0
    measurement: "Avg engagement rate per post (likes+comments+shares/reach)"
    why_this_matters: "[Connection to business goal]"

  - metric: "[Secondary metric]"
    current: [baseline]
    target: [goal]
    measurement: "[how tracked]"
    why_this_matters: "[Connection to business goal]"
```

---

### Step 3: Define Timeline with Milestones

Break campaign into checkpoints:

```yaml
timeline:
  start_date: "YYYY-MM-DD"
  end_date: "YYYY-MM-DD"
  duration: "[X weeks/days]"

  milestones:
    - date: "[Date at 25% mark]"
      target: "[What should be achieved]"
      checkpoint: "[What we evaluate]"

    - date: "[Date at 50% mark]"
      target: "[What should be achieved]"
      checkpoint: "[What we evaluate]"

    - date: "[Date at 75% mark]"
      target: "[What should be achieved]"
      checkpoint: "[What we evaluate]"

    - date: "[End date]"
      target: "[Final goal]"
      checkpoint: "[Final evaluation]"
```

---

### Step 4: Connect to Business Objective

```yaml
business_connection:
  content_objective: "[Campaign goal]"
  business_objective: "[Ultimate business outcome]"
  connection: |
    [How content goal drives business goal]
    Example: "Growing to 50K followers creates audience scale for product
    launch in Q2. 5% engagement rate ensures audience quality and future
    conversion potential."
```

---

## Output

**File:** `layer-1/1.2-objectives.yaml`

**Format:**
```yaml
timestamp: "[ISO 8601]"

objective:
  primary_goal: |
    [Specific, measurable statement]

  success_metrics:
    - metric: "[name]"
      current: [number]
      target: [number]
      measurement: "[how]"
      why_this_matters: "[connection]"

  timeline:
    start_date: "YYYY-MM-DD"
    end_date: "YYYY-MM-DD"
    duration: "[X days]"
    milestones: [...]

business_connection:
  content_objective: "[goal]"
  business_objective: "[ultimate outcome]"
  connection: |
    [How they relate]

objectives_measurable: true
```

**Minimum size:** 1KB

---

## Quality Gates

- [ ] Primary goal is SPECIFIC and MEASURABLE
- [ ] At least 2 success metrics defined
- [ ] Each metric has baseline, target, measurement method
- [ ] Timeline includes start/end dates
- [ ] At least 3 milestones defined
- [ ] Business connection articulated
