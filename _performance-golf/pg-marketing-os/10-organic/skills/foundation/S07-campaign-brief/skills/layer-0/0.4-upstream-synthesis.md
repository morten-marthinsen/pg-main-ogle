# 0.4 Upstream Synthesis

## Purpose
Synthesize all loaded context (6 foundation files + optional CopywritingEngine) into a unified strategic brief that will guide Layer 1 planning.

---

## Input Requirements

From Layer 0:
- `0.2-foundation-context.yaml` (S01-S06 key data)
- `0.3-copywritingengine-context.yaml` (Mode A or B)

---

## Execution Protocol

### Step 1: Synthesize Audience + Platform

Combine AIF and PSF insights:

```yaml
audience_platform_synthesis:
  who: "[target segment from AIF]"
  where: "[primary platform from PSF]"
  when: "[optimal posting times - intersection of AIF and PSF]"
  what_stops_scroll: "[from AIF emotional triggers + PSF algorithm priorities]"

  strategic_insight: |
    [1-2 sentences connecting audience psychology to platform behavior]
    Example: "This audience is on Instagram during lunch breaks (12-1pm)
    and late evening (9-11pm), seeking motivation and validation. They stop
    scrolling for transformation stories and specific how-to content."
```

---

### Step 2: Synthesize Voice + Hooks

Combine BVF and HLF insights:

```yaml
voice_hook_synthesis:
  brand_voice: "[tone from BVF]"
  signature_language: [top 5 phrases from BVF + AIF language_to_use]
  approved_hook_patterns: [top 5 from HLF that match voice]

  strategic_insight: |
    [How voice and hooks work together]
    Example: "Use conversational authority tone with direct address ('You've
    been told...'). Lead with pain-based hooks that feel like a friend
    calling out bad advice, not a guru lecturing."
```

---

### Step 3: Synthesize Content Strategy

Combine CAF and VSF insights:

```yaml
content_strategy_synthesis:
  primary_pillars: [list from CAF]
  function_mix: [percentages from CAF]
  quality_floor: [minimum_publish from VSF]
  quality_target: [target from VSF]

  pillar_function_map:
    - pillar: "[name]"
      primary_function: "[awareness/engagement/conversion/community]"
      formats: [from PSF aligned with pillar]
      quality_target: [higher for hero content]

  strategic_insight: |
    [How content strategy serves campaign goals]
```

---

### Step 4: Synthesize CopywritingEngine Integration (Mode A only)

If Mode A:

```yaml
campaign_integration:
  long_form_campaign: "[big idea + mechanism from CopywritingEngine]"
  organic_role: "Awareness + Education + Traffic"

  pillar_mapping:
    - organic_pillar: "[name]"
      supports_long_form: "[how]"
      content_bridge: "[what connects them]"

  funnel_flow: |
    Organic (Instagram Reels) →
    Landing Page (CopywritingEngine VSL) →
    Email Sequence (CopywritingEngine) →
    Purchase

  timing_strategy:
    pre_launch: "[X weeks of mechanism education]"
    launch: "[announcement + case studies]"
    post_launch: "[objection handling + proof amplification]"
```

---

### Step 5: Identify Strategic Priorities

What are the 3 most important things for this campaign:

```yaml
strategic_priorities:
  priority_1: "[e.g., Build awareness of mechanism before launch]"
  priority_2: "[e.g., Establish thought leadership in niche]"
  priority_3: "[e.g., Drive consistent traffic to landing page]"

constraints:
  budget: "[if applicable]"
  time: "[campaign duration]"
  resources: "[team capacity]"
```

---

## Output

**File:** `layer-0/0.4-strategic-synthesis.yaml`

**Format:**
```yaml
timestamp: "[ISO 8601]"

audience_platform_synthesis: {...}
voice_hook_synthesis: {...}
content_strategy_synthesis: {...}
campaign_integration: {...}  # Mode A only

strategic_priorities:
  priority_1: "[description]"
  priority_2: "[description]"
  priority_3: "[description]

  budget: "[if applicable]"
  time: "[campaign duration]"
  resources: "[team capacity]"

synthesis_complete: true
ready_for_planning: true
```
