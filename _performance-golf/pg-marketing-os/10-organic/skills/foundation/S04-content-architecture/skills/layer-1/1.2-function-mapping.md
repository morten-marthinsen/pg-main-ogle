# 1.2 Function Mapping

## Purpose
Map content functions (awareness, engagement, conversion, community) to business outcomes with specific percentages and content types.

---

## Input Requirements
- Pillar concepts from 1.1
- Business context (content goals)
- Platform context from PSF

---

## Execution Protocol

### Step 1: Define Awareness Function
**Purpose:** Introduce brand to new audiences, maximize reach

```yaml
awareness:
  purpose: |
    Introduce brand to new audiences, maximize reach
  percentage: [40-50%]

  characteristics:
    - Broad appeal topics
    - High shareability
    - Optimized for algorithm discovery
    - Lower depth, higher entertainment

  content_types:
    - type: "[Format from PSF]"
      pillar: "[Which pillar from 1.1]"
      frequency: "[Per week]"
      examples: []

  success_metrics:
    - Reach
    - Impressions
    - New followers
    - Video views
```

**Recommended:** 40-50% of content

---

### Step 2: Define Engagement Function
**Purpose:** Deepen relationship with existing audience

```yaml
engagement:
  purpose: |
    Deepen relationship with existing audience
  percentage: [25-30%]

  characteristics:
    - Encourages comments and discussion
    - Builds community feeling
    - Creates inside jokes and references
    - Personal stories and opinions

  content_types:
    - type: "[Format]"
      pillar: "[Which pillar]"
      frequency: "[Per week]"
      examples: []

  success_metrics:
    - Comments
    - Saves
    - Shares
    - DMs
```

**Recommended:** 25-30% of content

---

### Step 3: Define Conversion Function
**Purpose:** Move audience toward purchase/action

```yaml
conversion:
  purpose: |
    Move audience toward purchase/action
  percentage: [15-20%]

  characteristics:
    - Clear value demonstration
    - Social proof integration
    - Direct or indirect CTAs
    - Problem-solution framing

  content_types:
    - type: "[Format]"
      pillar: "[Which pillar]"
      frequency: "[Per week]"
      examples: []

  success_metrics:
    - Link clicks
    - Sign-ups
    - Purchases
    - Inquiries
```

**Recommended:** 15-20% of content

---

### Step 4: Define Community Function
**Purpose:** Reward and activate loyal followers

```yaml
community:
  purpose: |
    Reward and activate loyal followers
  percentage: [10-15%]

  characteristics:
    - Insider content
    - Behind-the-scenes
    - Direct interaction
    - Co-creation opportunities

  content_types:
    - type: "[Format]"
      pillar: "[Which pillar]"
      frequency: "[Per week]"
      examples: []

  success_metrics:
    - Reply rate
    - Story engagement
    - UGC creation
    - Retention metrics
```

**Recommended:** 10-15% of content

---

### Step 5: Verify Function Mix
**CRITICAL MATH CHECK:** Awareness + Engagement + Conversion + Community MUST = 100%

Adjust percentages based on business goals:
- **Lead generation priority:** Increase conversion to 20-25%, reduce awareness to 35-40%
- **Brand building priority:** Increase awareness to 50-55%, reduce conversion to 10-15%
- **Community building priority:** Increase community to 15-20%, reduce awareness to 35-40%
