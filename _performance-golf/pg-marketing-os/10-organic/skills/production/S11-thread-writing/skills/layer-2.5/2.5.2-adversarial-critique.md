# 2.5.2 Adversarial Critique

## Purpose
Run 2 rounds + audience evaluation of adversarial critique where each persona evaluates others, identifies convergence, and forces diversity.

---

## Input Requirements

From Layer 2.5:
- `2.5.1-round1-submissions.yaml` (all 7 personas' threads)

---

## Execution Protocol

### Round 1 Critique

Each persona critiques all 6 others (42 total critiques):

**Per Critique:**
```yaml
critic: "[Persona Name]"
target: "[Persona Name]"
round: 1

strengths:
  - element: "[Specific element that works]"
    why_strong: "[Reasoning]"
    score: [1-10]

  - element: "[Specific element]"
    why_strong: "[Reasoning]"
    score: [1-10]

weaknesses:
  - element: "[Specific element that fails]"
    why_weak: "[Reasoning]"
    severity: "[Critical/Moderate/Minor]"
    suggested_fix: "[How to improve]"

  - element: "[Specific element]"
    why_weak: "[Reasoning]"
    severity: "[Severity]"
    suggested_fix: "[How to improve]"

stealing_for_round_2:
  - "[Element I'm incorporating into my revision]"
  - "[Element I'm incorporating]"

convergence_warning:
  detected: [Yes/No]
  note: "[If detected, where this thread is too similar to mine]"
```

### Round 1 Revision

Each persona revises based on critiques received:

**Per Persona Revision:**
```yaml
persona: "[Name]"
round: 2

critiques_received: [Count from all other personas]

critiques_addressed:
  - critique: "[Specific critique]"
    source: "[Who made it]"
    severity: "[Severity]"
    change_made: "[How I addressed it]"

elements_incorporated:
  - element: "[What I added]"
    from_persona: "[Who I took it from]"
    how_integrated: "[How I made it mine]"

revised_thread: |
  Tweet 1:
  [Revised text]

  Tweet 2:
  [Revised text]

  # ... all tweets

improvement_notes: |
  [What's better in Round 2 and why]
```

### Round 2 Critique

Focus on synthesis opportunities (42 more critiques):

**Per Critique:**
```yaml
critic: "[Persona Name]"
target: "[Persona Name]"
round: 2

synthesis_candidates:
  - element: "[Must-include element]"
    why_essential: "[Reasoning]"
    combines_with: "[Element from another persona]"
    synthesis_potential: [1-10]

remaining_gaps:
  - gap: "[What's still missing]"
    importance: "[Critical/Moderate/Minor]"

convergence_observed: |
  [Where this output now aligns with others]

divergence_preserved: |
  [Where this output maintains unique approach]

final_recommendation:
  include_in_synthesis: "[Yes/Partially/No]"
  reasoning: "[Why]"
  specific_elements_to_extract: []
```

### Round 2 (FINAL) — Convergence Detection & Forced Diversity

Critic (dedicated adversarial role) evaluates all Round 2 outputs:

```yaml
critic_meta_analysis:
  convergence_detected:
    - convergence_point: "[Where multiple threads converge]"
      threads_affected: ["[Persona]", "[Persona]"]
      severity: [8-9 on 10 scale if problematic]
      remediation: "[Force divergence by emphasizing unique angles]"

  diversity_assessment:
    hooks:
      unique_approaches: [N/7]
      concerning_similarity: [Yes/No + details]

    structures:
      unique_approaches: [N/7]
      concerning_similarity: [Yes/No + details]

    ctas:
      unique_approaches: [N/7]
      concerning_similarity: [Yes/No + details]

  synthesis_blueprint:
    winning_hook:
      candidates: ["[Persona: Hook]", "[Persona: Hook]"]
      reasoning: "[Why these are best]"

    winning_structure:
      candidates: ["[Persona or hybrid]"]
      reasoning: "[Why this structure]"

    winning_moments:
      - moment: "[Specific tweet or line]"
        source: "[Persona]"
        why_wins: "[Reasoning]"

    hybrid_requirements:
      must_combine: ["[Element from X]", "[Element from Y]"]
      reasoning: "[Why synthesis needed]"
```
