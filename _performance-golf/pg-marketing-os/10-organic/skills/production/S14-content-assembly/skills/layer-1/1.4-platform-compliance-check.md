# 1.4 Platform Compliance Check

## Purpose
Verify each content piece meets platform-specific requirements for length, format, aspect ratio, character limits, and technical specifications. Non-compliant content will be rejected by platform algorithms or fail to upload.

---

## Input Requirements

From Layer 0:
- `0.4-cbf-context.yaml` (platform requirements from PSF)

From Layer 1:
- `1.2-content-inventory.yaml` (all content pieces with metadata)

Also requires:
- `[project]/S02-platform-strategy/outputs/[campaign]-PSF.yaml` — Platform Strategy File

---

## Execution Protocol

### Step 2: Check Video Content Compliance

For each video content piece (Reels, TikToks, Shorts):

```yaml
video_compliance:
  content_id: "[ID]"
  platform: "[Platform]"
  format: "[Reel/TikTok/Short]"

  duration_check:
    estimated_duration_seconds: [From content metadata]
    platform_min: [Seconds]
    platform_max: [Seconds]
    within_range: [Yes/No]
    issue: "[If No, describe]"

  aspect_ratio_check:
    required: "[From PSF]"
    planned: "[From visual direction]"
    correct: [Yes/No]

  caption_check:
    character_count: [From caption]
    platform_limit: [Number]
    within_limit: [Yes/No]
    overage: [If over, by how much]

  hashtag_check:
    hashtag_count: [Number]
    platform_limit: [Number]
    within_limit: [Yes/No]

  hook_timing_check:
    hook_duration_seconds: [Estimated]
    platform_optimal: [Seconds]
    appropriate: [Yes/No]
    recommendation: "[If not optimal]"

  overall_compliance: [PASS / FAIL]
  issues: []
```

**If duration out of range:** FAIL — content must be re-edited.
**If aspect ratio wrong:** FAIL — visual direction must be corrected.
**If caption over limit:** FAIL — caption must be shortened.

## Quality Gates

- [ ] All content pieces checked against platform specs
- [ ] All video durations within platform ranges
- [ ] All captions within character limits
- [ ] All aspect ratios correct for platform
- [ ] All hashtag counts within limits
- [ ] NO critical compliance failures

**If ANY critical failure:** ready_to_proceed = No. Content must be fixed before assembly.

**Minor issues (e.g., non-optimal hook timing):** Can proceed with flags.

---

## Handoff

Pass compliance results to: **1.5 Voice Consistency Check**

**CRITICAL:** Non-compliant content will either fail to upload or perform poorly on platform. These are hard requirements, not suggestions.
