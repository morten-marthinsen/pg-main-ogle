# 1.3 Virality Score Check

## Purpose
Score each content piece using the Virality Scoring Framework (VSF) and verify all pieces meet minimum threshold from CBF. Content below threshold must be flagged for revision or blocked from publication.

**This implements Gate G09 (Virality Threshold).**

---

## Input Requirements

From Layer 0:
- `0.4-cbf-context.yaml` (virality thresholds)

From Layer 1:
- `1.2-content-inventory.yaml` (all content pieces)

Also requires:
- `[project]/S06-virality-scoring/outputs/[campaign]-VSF.yaml` — Virality Scoring Framework

---

### Step 3: Score Each Content Piece

For EVERY content piece in inventory (1.2):

**Scoring process:**
1. Load content (script/caption/thread/carousel)
2. Score on each dimension (0-10)
3. Apply weights from VSF
4. Calculate overall score
5. Compare to threshold

```yaml
content_score:
  content_id: "[ID]"
  content_type: "[Type]"
  platform: "[Platform]"

  dimension_scores:
    hook_strength:
      score: [0-10]
      justification: |
        [Specific observations about hook]

    emotional_resonance:
      score: [0-10]
      justification: |
        [Specific observations about emotion]

    practical_value:
      score: [0-10]
      justification: |
        [Specific observations about value]

    social_currency:
      score: [0-10]
      justification: |
        [Specific observations about shareability]

    story_structure:
      score: [0-10]
      justification: |
        [Specific observations about narrative]

    platform_fit:
      score: [0-10]
      justification: |
        [Specific observations about platform alignment]

    specificity:
      score: [0-10]
      justification: |
        [Specific observations about concrete details]

    voice_alignment:
      score: [0-10]
      justification: |
        [Specific observations about brand voice]

  calculation:
    weighted_scores:
      - dimension: "hook_strength"
        score: [0-10]
        weight: [0-1]
        weighted_value: [score × weight]
    # [... repeat for all dimensions]

    base_score: [Sum of weighted values, normalized to 0-100]

    modifiers:
      bonuses: []  # Any special bonuses from VSF
      penalties: []  # Any penalties

    final_score: [base_score + bonuses - penalties]

  threshold_comparison:
    required_minimum: [From CBF]
    actual_score: [Final score]
    meets_threshold: [Yes/No]
    gap: [If below, how much]

  tier_classification:
    tier: [Viral/Strong/Acceptable/Weak based on VSF benchmarks]

  decision: [PUBLISH / FLAG / REVISE / BLOCK]
```

---

### Step 4: Decision Logic

**Score >= 70:**
- Classification: STRONG or VIRAL (depending on benchmarks)
- Decision: PUBLISH
- No flags

**Score 60-69:**
- Classification: ACCEPTABLE
- Decision: PUBLISH (if threshold is 60)
- Flag: "Could be improved"

**Score 40-59:**
- Classification: ACCEPTABLE or WEAK (depending on score)
- Decision: REVISE (if threshold is 60)
- Flag: "Revision required — below threshold"
- HALT assembly for this piece

**Score < 40:**
- Classification: WEAK
- Decision: BLOCK
- Flag: "Blocked from publication — major revision or replacement required"
- HALT assembly for this piece

## Quality Gates (Gate G09)

- [ ] All content pieces scored on all 8 dimensions
- [ ] Scores justified with specific observations
- [ ] Threshold comparison performed for each piece
- [ ] Decisions made based on score thresholds
- [ ] NO pieces with score < 40 proceeding to assembly
- [ ] ALL pieces with score < 60 flagged for revision (if threshold is 60)

**Gate G09 Status:**
- **PASS:** All content meets threshold OR flagged pieces have explicit approval to proceed
- **FAIL:** Any content below threshold without approval

**If Gate G09 = FAIL:** HALT assembly for affected pieces. They must be revised or replaced.

---

## Handoff

Pass virality scores to: **1.4 Platform Compliance Check**

**CRITICAL:** Any content with decision = REVISE or BLOCK cannot proceed to Layer 4 assembly. These pieces must be revised, re-scored, and re-checked before assembly.

**If multiple pieces blocked:** Recommend batch revision session rather than proceeding with partial assembly.
