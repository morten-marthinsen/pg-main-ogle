# 1.5 Voice Consistency Check

## Purpose
Verify all content aligns with Brand Voice File (BVF). Check for banned words, tone alignment, anti-voice violations, and voice energy consistency. Voice violations damage brand integrity.

---

## Input Requirements

From Layer 0:
- `0.4-cbf-context.yaml` (voice requirements)

From Layer 1:
- `1.2-content-inventory.yaml` (all content pieces)

Also requires:
- `[project]/S03-brand-voice/outputs/[campaign]-BVF.yaml` — Brand Voice File

---

## Execution Protocol

### Step 1: Load BVF

Load the Brand Voice File:
```
[project]/S03-brand-voice/outputs/[campaign]-BVF.yaml
```

**Extract:**
```yaml
voice_attributes:
  core_attributes:
    - attribute: "[e.g., Authoritative]"
      description: |
      vocal_indicators: []

  energy_level: "[High/Medium/Low]"
  formality_level: "[Formal/Conversational/Casual]"

vocabulary:
  power_words: []  # Words to USE
  banned_words: []  # Words to NEVER use
  brand_specific_terms: []

tone_by_platform:
  [platform]:
    tone: "[Description]"
    energy: "[Level]"
    formality: "[Level]"

anti_voice:
  never_sound_like: []
  forbidden_patterns: []
  avoided_phrases: []
```

---

### Step 2: Check for Banned Words

For each content piece, scan for banned words:

```yaml
banned_word_check:
  content_id: "[ID]"
  content_type: "[Type]"
  platform: "[Platform]"

  banned_words_found:
    - word: "[Banned word]"
      occurrences: [Count]
      locations:
        - section: "[Hook/Body/CTA]"
          line: "[Line number or preview]"
          context: "[Surrounding text]"

  total_banned_words: [Count]
  violations_severity: [CRITICAL if any found]

  replacement_suggestions:
    - banned_word: "[Word]"
      suggested_replacement: "[Alternative]"
```

**If ANY banned word found:** CRITICAL violation. Content must be revised.

---

### Step 3: Check Tone Alignment

For each content piece, evaluate tone against BVF:

```yaml
tone_alignment_check:
  content_id: "[ID]"
  platform: "[Platform]"

  expected_tone:
    from_bvf: "[Description]"
    energy_level: "[Level]"
    formality_level: "[Level]"

  actual_tone_analysis:
    perceived_energy: "[High/Medium/Low]"
    perceived_formality: "[Formal/Conversational/Casual]"
    tone_consistency: [Yes/No]

  alignment_score: [0-10]
  alignment_status: [ALIGNED / MISALIGNED]

  misalignment_details:
    - aspect: "[e.g., Energy level]"
      expected: "[Level]"
      actual: "[Level]"
      examples: |
        [Specific lines showing misalignment]

  recommendation: |
    [If misaligned, what to change]
```

**Alignment score < 7:** FLAG for review.
**Alignment score < 5:** CRITICAL misalignment, revision required.

---

### Step 4: Check Anti-Voice Violations

Scan for anti-voice patterns:

```yaml
anti_voice_check:
  content_id: "[ID]"

  forbidden_patterns_found:
    - pattern: "[Pattern from BVF anti_voice]"
      occurrences: [Count]
      examples: |
        [Where this pattern appears]
      severity: [CRITICAL / MODERATE / MINOR]

  avoided_phrases_found:
    - phrase: "[Phrase from BVF anti_voice]"
      occurrences: [Count]
      context: "[Where it appears]"

  total_violations: [Count]
  violations_severity: [CRITICAL/MODERATE/MINOR/NONE]

  sounds_like_competitors: [Yes/No]
  competitor_similarity_notes: |
    [If Yes, which competitor and why]
```

**If CRITICAL anti-voice violation:** Content must be revised.
**If MODERATE:** FLAG for review.

---

### Step 5: Check Voice Attribute Presence

Verify core voice attributes are present:

```yaml
voice_attribute_check:
  content_id: "[ID]"

  core_attributes_from_bvf:
    - attribute: "[e.g., Authoritative]"
      present_in_content: [Yes/No]
      evidence: |
        [Specific lines demonstrating this attribute]
      strength: [Strong/Moderate/Weak/Absent]

  attributes_present_count: [Number]
  attributes_expected_count: [Number from BVF]
  presence_percentage: [%]

  voice_strength: [Strong/Moderate/Weak]

  missing_attributes: []
```

**If < 50% attributes present:** Voice too weak, revision recommended.
**If 50-75%:** Acceptable but could be stronger.
**If > 75%:** Strong voice alignment.

---

### Step 6: Check Vocabulary Usage

Check power words and brand terms:

```yaml
vocabulary_check:
  content_id: "[ID]"

  power_words_from_bvf: [List]
  power_words_used_in_content:
    - word: "[Word]"
      occurrences: [Count]

  power_word_usage_count: [Number]
  power_word_density: [Per 100 words]

  brand_specific_terms_from_bvf: [List]
  brand_terms_used_in_content:
    - term: "[Term]"
      occurrences: [Count]

  vocabulary_alignment: [Strong/Moderate/Weak]

  recommendations: |
    [Opportunities to use more power words or brand terms]
```

---

### Step 7: Platform-Specific Tone Check

Verify tone appropriate for platform:

```yaml
platform_tone_check:
  content_id: "[ID]"
  platform: "[Platform]"

  platform_tone_guidance_from_bvf:
    tone: "[Description]"
    energy: "[Level]"
    formality: "[Level]"

  content_tone_analysis:
    matches_platform_guidance: [Yes/No]
    tone_appropriate_for_platform_culture: [Yes/No]

  platform_alignment_score: [0-10]

  platform_tone_notes: |
    [Observations about platform appropriateness]
```

**Score < 7:** May perform poorly on platform due to tone mismatch.

---

## Quality Gates

- [ ] All content pieces checked against BVF
- [ ] NO banned words in any content
- [ ] NO critical anti-voice violations
- [ ] Tone alignment score >= 7 for all pieces (or flagged for review)
- [ ] At least 50% of voice attributes present in each piece
- [ ] Platform-specific tone appropriate

**If banned words found:** ready_to_proceed = No. CRITICAL failure.
**If critical anti-voice violations:** ready_to_proceed = No. Must revise.
**If tone misalignment (score < 5):** ready_to_proceed = No. Must revise.

---

## Handoff

Pass voice consistency results to: **1.6 Cross-Reference Validation**

**CRITICAL:** Voice violations damage brand integrity. Banned words and anti-voice patterns are non-negotiable — they must be removed before publication.
