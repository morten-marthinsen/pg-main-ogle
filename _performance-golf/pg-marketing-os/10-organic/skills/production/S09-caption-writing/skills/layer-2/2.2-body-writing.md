**Layer:** 2 (Generative)
**Model:** Claude Opus

**Downstream:** 2.3 CTA Integration

## PURPOSE

Write caption body that delivers on hook promise, provides educational value, and maintains BVF voice. Use proper line breaks, formatting, and value delivery structure. Generate ONE full draft labeled PRE-ARENA for Arena competition in Layer 2.5.

## EXECUTION PROTOCOL

**MUST:**
1. Select **1 hook** from 2.1 top 5 (or generate draft for each hook if Arena needs multiple starting points)
2. Write **caption body** (500-1500 chars depending on platform + content_function):
   - **Expand Hook (100-150 chars):** Deliver on hook promise immediately (no bait-and-switch)
   - **Deliver Value (300-700 chars):** Educational content, actionable tips, story, or insight
   - **Credibility Signal (100-150 chars):** Why this matters, personal experience, or client result
3. Apply **structure from 1.2**:
   - Line break every 1-2 sentences (~40-60 chars)
   - Use emoji as visual bullets if listing (🔹)
   - Create "paragraphs" with spacing (1 blank line between sections)
4. Maintain **BVF voice throughout**:
   - Load voice descriptor (e.g., "empowering coach, science-backed, accessible")
   - Avoid anti-voice (e.g., "drill sergeant aggressive, pseudo-science, body shaming")
   - Match pacing from specimens (short punchy vs longer contemplative)
5. Apply **teaching principles**:
   - Practical Value (Berger) → Make content actionable/screenshot-worthy
   - Emotion (Berger) → Use power words, celebrate wins, acknowledge struggles
   - Clear Messaging (Miller) → No jargon before explanation
6. Format with **platform-specific norms** from 1.1:
   - Instagram: Line breaks supported, emoji moderate
   - TikTok: Short punchy lines, emoji heavy
   - LinkedIn: Longer-form, emoji minimal, professional tone
7. **Label draft "PRE-ARENA"** — this signals draft status before Arena refinement
8. Verify total character count within target range
9. Generate 1 complete caption draft (hook + body, NO CTA yet — that's 2.3)

**NEVER:**
- Bait-and-switch (hook promises one thing, body delivers another)
- Wall of text (no line breaks in 500+ char body)
- Ignore BVF voice (copy generic captions from web)
- Use jargon without explanation (accessibility required)
- Skip credibility signal (body needs "why this matters" or "how I know")
- Include CTA in this microskill (CTA is separate in 2.3)

93% of women over 30 are eating too little protein. Here's why it matters. 💪

They're hitting 40-60g per day...

When they actually need 100-120g to maintain muscle mass, keep energy stable, and stay full between meals.

Here's what's happening:

🔹 You lose 3-8% of muscle mass per decade after 30 (sarcopenia)

🔹 Less muscle = slower metabolism = harder to maintain weight

🔹 Protein is the MOST satiating macronutrient (you stay full longer)

The fix?

Aim for 0.8-1g of protein per pound of goal body weight.

That's 25-35g per meal if you eat 3x a day.

Real examples:
• Breakfast: Greek yogurt (20g) + protein powder (20g) = 40g
