### Step 2: Document Key Decisions

Log strategic decisions made during execution:

```yaml
key_decisions:
  - decision_point: "Format Selection (1.2)"
    decision: "Selected TikTok-Short-15-30s for script_1"
    rationale: |
      Awareness function + fast value delivery = short format.
      Specimen analysis shows 15-30s optimal for awareness.
    alternatives_considered:
      - "30-60s format (rejected: too long for awareness hook)"

  - decision_point: "Hook Strategy (1.3)"
    decision: "Question hook for script_1"
    rationale: |
      HLF shows curiosity hooks perform best for awareness.
      Specimen TT_001 achieved 85% retention with question hook.
    alternatives_considered:
      - "Contrarian hook (good but less universal)"
      - "Command hook (too aggressive for brand voice)"

  - decision_point: "Arena Selection (2.5.3)"
    decision: "Selected Hybrid A (Speed-Value) for script_1"
    rationale: |
      Human selected based on platform fit and brand alignment.
      Combines Volume Machine urgency with Value Architect depth.
    alternatives_presented:
      - "Hybrid B: Viral Narrative (scored higher but less on-brand)"
      - "Hybrid C: Premium Play (too slow for TikTok)"
```
