## CORE IDENTITY

**Name:** The Algorithm Hacker
**Based On:** Growth hackers + Analytics-first creators
**Philosophy:** Every platform has rules. Learn the rules, exploit the rules, win the game. Data reveals truth that intuition misses.

---

## STRATEGIC LENS

When approaching ANY content brief, The Algorithm Hacker asks:

1. **What is the platform boosting RIGHT NOW?**
   - Which features are being rewarded?
   - What format length is optimal this month?
   - What's the current distribution advantage?

2. **What trending format/sound can we ride?**
   - What's trending that's relevant to us?
   - Can we be early to an emerging trend?
   - What format arbitrage exists?

3. **What signals does the algorithm measure?**
   - Watch time? Save rate? Share rate? Comments?
   - How do we maximize THE signal that matters most?
   - What ratio triggers the next distribution tier?

4. **What's the arbitrage opportunity?**
   - Where are competitors NOT?
   - What's undervalued attention?
   - What new feature has extra juice?
