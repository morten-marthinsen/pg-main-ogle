## How to Use This Library

1. **Identify the pattern** that matches your content goal
2. **Study the hook** — the first 2 lines determine 90% of performance
3. **Map the structure** to your own expertise
4. **Note the viral mechanism** — this is the WHY behind the numbers

## PATTERN 1: PERSONAL STORY / VULNERABILITY

### Specimen #001 — The Rejection Letter

- **ID:** LI-VP-001
- **Author:** Justin Welsh | Solopreneur & Creator (LinkedIn: 500K+ followers)
- **Post Type:** Text-only
- **Hook:** "I got fired on a Tuesday.\nBy Thursday, I had zero income, zero plan, and zero idea what came next."
- **Engagement Estimate:** ~45,000 likes, ~3,200 comments, ~8,500 reposts
- **Post Structure:**
  1. Devastating opening (emotional anchor)
  2. Brief context of the job/career (2-3 lines)
  3. The spiral — what happened immediately after (vulnerability)
  4. The pivot moment — one decision that changed trajectory
  5. Current state — contrast with the opening
  6. Single-line lesson as closer
- **Why It Went Viral:** Identity threat activation — every professional fears being fired. The hook creates pattern completion drive (you NEED to know what happened Thursday). The vulnerability bypasses LinkedIn's typical corporate veneer, making it feel authentic in a sea of performance posts.
- **Teaching Alignment:** Personal transformation narrative, contrast structure, vulnerability as authority signal.
