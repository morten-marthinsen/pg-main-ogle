## Sequence Logic

**Problem → Cause → Solution → Proof → Personal → Value → Access**

This sequence follows the prosecution strategy:

1. **Validate the problem** (CPB-001): Acknowledge where the golfer is. Use data to prove it is not just them — it is systemic. Build trust through validation before asking for belief change.

2. **Reveal the root cause** (CPB-002): Now that trust is established, deliver the reframe. The problem is not the golfer, the instructor, or the app — it is that nobody ever diagnosed the root flaw. This is the Big Idea delivery point.

3. **Introduce the mechanism** (CPB-003): With the root cause established, the mechanism arrives as the logical answer. SwingScan Diagnosis IS the diagnosis the root cause demands. This is the moment the argument pivots from problem to solution.

4. **Prove it works** (CPB-004): The skeptic's mind activates after the mechanism reveal. "Can AI really do this?" is answered with peer-reviewed research, analogous proof from medicine, and the competitive void.

5. **Make it personal** (CPB-006): Move from "this works" to "this works for ME." 77+ flaw patterns, matched coach, adaptive system. The golfer sees themselves in the system.

6. **Present the value** (CPB-005): Only AFTER the golfer believes the system works for them does price become relevant. Reframe $299 against what they have already spent on treatments that never worked.

7. **Open the door** (CPB-007): The final question — "How do I get started?" — is answered with the Founders Club offer. Always last.
