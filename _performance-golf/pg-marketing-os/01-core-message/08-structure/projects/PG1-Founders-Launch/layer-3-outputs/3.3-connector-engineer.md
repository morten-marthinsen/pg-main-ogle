# 3.3 Connector Engineer — PG1 Founders Launch

## Connectors Between Chunks

Connectors are placed where natural flow requires them. They are NOT forced between every pair — only where the argument needs a bridge to maintain continuation of thought.

### Connector 1: CPB-001 → CPB-002 (Failure → Root Cause)
**Type:** revelation
**Connector text:** "So if it is not your fault — if 70% of golfers who take lessons do not improve regardless of talent or effort — then what IS the problem? The answer is something nobody in golf has ever said to you."
**Logic:** Transitions from validating the failure (data) to revealing the cause (root cause reframe). The question creates a curiosity gap that the root cause revelation fills.

### Connector 2: CPB-002 → CPB-003 (Root Cause → Mechanism)
**Type:** pivot
**Connector text:** "That is why we built something different. Not another app. Not another set of tips. A diagnosis. The first one golf has ever had."
**Logic:** Pivots from problem (treatment-without-diagnosis) to solution (SwingScan Diagnosis). The pivot is clean because the mechanism IS the answer to the root cause — there is no logical gap to bridge, just a direction change from "here is why" to "here is what."
**Source:** Directly from promise package transitions section.

### Connector 3: CPB-003 → CPB-004 (Mechanism → Proof)
**Type:** escalation
**Connector text:** "Now, I know what you are thinking. Can AI really diagnose a golf swing? Is this actually different from every other app claiming to use AI? Here is what the research says."
**Logic:** Acknowledges the skeptic's objection at the exact moment it arises (after mechanism reveal) and pivots to evidence. The question format mirrors what the reader is already thinking, which builds trust.

### Connector 4: CPB-004 → CPB-006 (Proof → Personalization)
**Type:** elaboration
**Connector text:** "So the science is clear — AI-human collaboration outperforms either alone. But here is the part that matters most to YOU."
**Logic:** Transitions from general proof (the approach works) to personal proof (it works for YOUR swing). The word "YOU" pivots from abstract evidence to personal relevance.

### Connector 5: CPB-006 → CPB-005 (Personalization → Value)
**Type:** contrast
**Connector text:** "Now think about what you have already spent trying to fix your game without knowing your root flaw."
**Logic:** Bridges from personalization (this works for you) to value (and it costs less than what you have already wasted). The contrast is between past spending on treatment-without-diagnosis and one-time investment in diagnosis-first. No forced transition — the reader naturally wonders about cost after believing the system works for them.

### Connector 6: CPB-005 → CPB-007 (Value → Access)
**Type:** escalation
**Connector text:** "And for the first 1,000 golfers who want to be part of this..."
**Logic:** Transitions from value case to the offer. The "first 1,000" introduces scarcity naturally, and "who want to be part of this" frames the purchase as membership, not transaction. From promise package transitions section.
