# 3.2 Simple Segue Builder — PG1 Founders Launch

## Simple Segue (Intro → First Chunk)

The simple segue connects the lead/intro to the first CPB chunk. Per E5 framework, it introduces the MECHANISM and PROMISE — never the product.

### Simple Segue Text

"What I am about to share with you is the reason nothing you have tried has worked — and the one step that changes everything. It is not another tip. It is not another drill. It is a diagnosis. The first one golf has ever had. And once you understand what it reveals about YOUR swing... the way you think about improving your game will never be the same."

### Components

**Promise echo:** "the one step that changes everything" → echoes primary promise (know your ONE root flaw)

**Mechanism reference:** "a diagnosis" → introduces SwingScan Diagnosis conceptually without naming the product. The mechanism is introduced through WHAT IT IS (a diagnosis), not what it is called.

**Curiosity escalation:** "what it reveals about YOUR swing" → personalizes and opens curiosity loop for CPB-001

**Anti-product language:** Does NOT mention PG1, the app, or the product. Introduces the CONCEPT (diagnosis) that the argument will prove.
