## Three Thesis Pillars

### Root Cause Connection
The entire golf improvement industry prescribes treatment without first diagnosing the golfer's specific root swing flaw. Every lesson, app, video, and training aid skips the diagnostic step. That is why 70% of golfers who take lessons do not improve and why the average handicap has not changed from 2020-2025.

### Mechanism Connection
SwingScan Diagnosis introduces the missing step: it identifies the golfer's ONE root swing flaw (trained on 2M+ swings, 96% accuracy, 77+ flaw patterns) before prescribing any treatment. Then it builds a personalized correction plan, assigns a matched PGA coach, and adapts as the flaw resolves. No competitor claims root flaw diagnosis — the positioning is entirely uncontested.

### Promise Connection
Know your ONE root swing flaw — the single issue causing everything else — identified for the first time, with a clear plan built around it. The promise is CLARITY, not IMPROVEMENT. The golfer finally stops guessing and starts directed effort.
