## Emphasis Strategy Selection

### Analysis

**Root cause type:** Systemic/structural (treatment-without-diagnosis)
**Product type:** AI diagnostic system + coaching platform
**Market sophistication:** Late Stage 3 / Early Stage 4
**Proof strength:** 68/100 (Moderate — strong mechanism proof, weak authority/social)
**Promise ceiling:** 62/100 (Moderate — clarity, not results)

### Decision: ROOT CAUSE emphasis

**Primary emphasis:** `root_cause` (60%)
**Secondary emphasis:** `delivery_tangible` (25%)
**Tertiary emphasis:** `science_conceptual` (15%)

### Bandwidth Allocation
```yaml
bandwidth_allocation:
  root_cause_pct: 60
  science_conceptual_pct: 15
  delivery_tangible_pct: 25
```

### Rationale

1. **Root cause emphasis dominates** because the mechanism IS the diagnosis — it directly solves "treatment-without-diagnosis." The mechanism's entire power comes from its connection to the root cause. The root cause scored 8.9 composite with 10.0 mechanism alignment. The mechanism doesn't need to be scientifically complex; it needs to be the obvious answer to "you've never been diagnosed."

2. **Delivery tangible is secondary** because the proof ceiling limits results claims. The mechanism must be VISIBLE — the golfer needs to picture themselves using SwingScan AI, seeing their root flaw identified, receiving their personalized plan. At Stage 3-4, showing the mechanism in action matters more than explaining the science behind it.

3. **Science conceptual is tertiary** because the audience is skeptical of tech-speak (Soul.md anti-voice #3). The AI specs (2M+ swings, 96% accuracy, 77+ flaws) provide credibility but should support, not lead. Over-indexing on science risks sounding like "another AI app."

### Primary Dimensions (optimize aggressively, target 8-10)
1. **Image Strength** — Can the prospect PICTURE the diagnosis happening?
2. **Simplicity** — Can a 12-year-old understand "diagnose your root flaw"?
3. **Differentiation** — Is it clearly different from data/tracking/coaching apps?
4. **Belief Compatibility** — Does it fit "I need someone to find THE problem"?
5. **Delivery Tangibility** — Can they SEE what PG1 does (upload swing → get diagnosis)?
