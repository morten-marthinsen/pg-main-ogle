# 1.1 Blue Sky Ideation

## Purpose
Generate maximum aspiration promises without constraint. Start with the biggest, boldest, most transformational promise possible — what would they want if you could wave a magic wand?

**Key Principle (from E5):** Don't start with a weak promise and try to inflate it. Start with a blue sky promise and back it down if necessary to make it true and provable.

## Execution Protocol

### Step 1: Extract Primary Transformation

```python
def extract_primary_transformation(now_after_grid):
    """
    Identify the core transformation from current state to desired state.
    """
    primary_now = now_after_grid['now'][0]  # Most important current pain
    primary_after = now_after_grid['after'][0]  # Most important desired state

    transformation = {
        'situation_shift': f"From {primary_now['situation']} to {primary_after['situation']}",
        'emotional_shift': f"From {primary_now['emotion']} to {primary_after['emotion']}",
        'capability_shift': f"From {primary_now['limitation']} to {primary_after['capability']}",
        'identity_shift': f"From {primary_now['identity']} to {primary_after['identity']}"
    }

    return transformation
```

### Step 2: Generate Blue Sky Promises

Think: "If I could wave a magic wand and grant them ANY outcome related to this problem, what would it be?"

```python
def generate_blue_sky(transformation, ideal_success, customer_language):
    """
    Generate maximum aspiration promises — no constraints yet.
    """
    blue_sky_templates = [
        # Complete transformation
        f"Complete {transformation['situation_shift'].split('to ')[1]}",
        f"Total freedom from {transformation['situation_shift'].split('From ')[1].split(' to')[0]}",

        # Ideal outcome
        f"Experience the {transformation['emotional_shift'].split('to ')[1]} you've always wanted",
        f"Finally achieve {ideal_success}",

        # Never again
        f"Never deal with {extract_primary_pain(transformation)} again",
        f"Permanently eliminate {extract_primary_problem(transformation)}",

        # New capability
        f"Finally be able to {transformation['capability_shift'].split('to ')[1]}",
        f"Gain the power to {customer_language['desired_outcomes'][0]}",

        # Identity
        f"Become someone who {transformation['identity_shift'].split('to ')[1]}",
        f"Transform into {customer_language['how_they_describe_success'][0]}"
    ]

    return blue_sky_templates
```

### Step 3: Blue Sky Across Dimensions

Generate blue sky promises for each transformation dimension:

```yaml
blue_sky_dimensions:
  physical:
    prompt: "What physical change would they want most?"
    examples:
      - "Lose all the weight you want — permanently"
      - "Have boundless energy from morning to night"
      - "Sleep like a baby every single night"

  emotional:
    prompt: "What emotional state would they want?"
    examples:
      - "Feel confident and in control around food"
      - "Experience complete peace of mind"
      - "Feel young and vibrant again"

  capability:
    prompt: "What would they be able to DO that they can't now?"
    examples:
      - "Eat whatever you want without gaining weight"
      - "Play with your grandkids without getting winded"
      - "Fit into clothes you haven't worn in years"

  relational:
    prompt: "How would their relationships improve?"
    examples:
      - "Finally feel attractive to your partner again"
      - "Stop being embarrassed at social events"
      - "Be the role model your kids deserve"

  identity:
    prompt: "Who would they BECOME?"
    examples:
      - "Become someone who naturally eats healthy"
      - "Transform into the fit, energetic person you were meant to be"
      - "Become unrecognizable to people who knew you before"
```
