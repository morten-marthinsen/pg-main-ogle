## EVIDENCE CHAIN

The root cause claim requires three categories of proof:

**1. The diagnosis gap exists (proving the problem):**
- MECH-01: Zero competitors claim diagnosis — VERIFIED by competitive analysis of 9 apps
- DISC-05: Handicap data from USGA — SOURCED, official data
- DISC-08: 70% failure from Bobby Clampett — SOURCED, named expert
- SOC-06: Organic audience language — SOURCED from forum analysis
- **Proof availability for problem claim: STRONG (9/10)**

**2. PG1 can deliver the diagnosis (proving the solution):**
- DATA-02/03/04: 2M+ swings, 96% accuracy, 77+ flaws — CLAIMED by PG, not independently verified
- DISC-14: Human-AI collaboration — PEER-REVIEWED, analogous
- DISC-12: AI diagnostic superiority — PEER-REVIEWED, analogous
- GAP-08: 96% accuracy methodology unverified — MODERATE gap
- **Proof availability for solution claim: MODERATE (7/10)**

**3. The diagnosis works (proving results):**
- GAP-01: No PG1 customer testimonials — CRITICAL gap
- GAP-03: No handicap improvement data — CRITICAL gap
- SOC-07: Beta user 5x engagement — WEAK (engagement, not improvement)
- **Proof availability for results claim: LOW (3/10)**

## CONCLUSION

**Proof Availability Score: 7.0/10**

The root cause CLAIM (diagnosis gap exists) is exceptionally well-proven (9/10). The mechanism claim (PG1 can diagnose) is moderately proven (7/10) through product specs and analogous peer-reviewed research. The results claim (diagnosis works) is LOW (3/10) due to critical gaps in customer testimonials and handicap data.

However, the root cause skill is validating the ROOT CAUSE, not the promise. The root cause is "nobody diagnoses" — and that claim is proven at 9/10. The promise ceiling (62/100 from Proof Inventory) correctly limits downstream claims. The root cause itself clears the 5.0 threshold comfortably.

## COUNTEREXAMPLE CHECK

**If proof were insufficient, what would be true?**
- Competitors would claim diagnosis (negating MECH-01) → They don't
- Handicaps would be improving (negating DISC-05) → They aren't
- No expert would validate the gap (negating P3, AUTH-05, AUTH-06) → Multiple experts do
- The audience wouldn't recognize the gap (negating SOC-06) → They use the language organically

**Confidence: HIGH for root cause claim. MODERATE for mechanism. LOW for results.**

**VERDICT: PASS (7.0/10)**
