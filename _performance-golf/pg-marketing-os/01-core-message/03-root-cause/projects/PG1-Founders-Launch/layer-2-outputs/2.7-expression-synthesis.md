## Complete Candidate Inventory

### Tier 1: Primary Candidates (score 8.0+)

**CANDIDATE 1: "You've Never Been Diagnosed"** (SR-1)
- Method: Simple Reframe
- Three-part: "I need better tips" → "Nobody diagnosed your root flaw" → "Every solution was treatment without diagnosis"
- Anchor: "You've never been diagnosed."
- Composite: 9.2 (CI:8, Ext:10, VS:7, FE:9, MS:10, Anchor:9)
- Strongest: Maximum externalization + perfect mechanism setup. Six words that reframe the entire category.
- Risk: "Diagnosed" could feel clinical if not delivered with warmth. Soul.md pacing (conversational, build-reveal-land) mitigates.

**CANDIDATE 2: "A Flaw vs. THE Flaw"** (SR-2)
- Method: Simple Reframe
- Three-part: "I have several swing problems" → "You have ONE root flaw causing all others; every instructor found A flaw, none found THE flaw" → "Fixing compensations is infinite without finding the root"
- Anchor: "A flaw vs. THE flaw."
- Composite: 8.8 (CI:9, Ext:9, VS:6, FE:8, MS:9, Anchor:9)
- Strongest: Highest counter-intuitiveness — the a/THE distinction is a genuine insight. Memorable, quotable.
- Risk: Slightly more intellectual; needs concrete examples to land emotionally.

**CANDIDATE 3: "Nobody Ever Ran the Test"** (MET-1)
- Method: Medical Metaphor
- Three-part: "I need the right instructor" → "Nobody ever ran the diagnostic test — every solution was a prescription without a diagnosis" → "Prescriptions without diagnosis have a 70% failure rate"
- Anchor: "Nobody ever ran the test."
- Composite: 9.0 (CI:8, Ext:10, VS:7, FE:9, MS:10, Anchor:8)
- Strongest: Medical analogy is organic to golfer language. Viscerally intuitive. 70% stat is powerful.
- Risk: Must stay conversational (Soul.md) — "ran the test" is good, "diagnostic protocol" would violate anti-voice.

- Anchor: "Which one number is wrong?"
- Composite: 7.6 (CI:8, Ext:8, VS:5, FE:7, MS:9, Anchor:8)

**CANDIDATE 6: "Treatment Without Diagnosis"** (SR-3)
- Method: Simple Reframe + Medical Bridge
- Three-part: "I need the right approach" → "The industry gives treatment without diagnosis" → "You wouldn't take medicine without a diagnosis — that's what golf has been doing"

- Composite: 7.5 (CI:8, Ext:10, VS:6, FE:8, MS:10, Anchor:7)

**CANDIDATE 7: "You Needed an X-Ray, Not Just Eyes"** (MET-3)

**CANDIDATE 8: "One Flaw, a Dozen Compensations"** (DP-1)
