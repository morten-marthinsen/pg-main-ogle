# 2.1 — Simple Reframe

**Microskill:** 2.1-simple-reframe
**Approved Concept:** A — The Undiagnosed Golfer
**Expression Method:** Simple Reframe (primary for golf niche per vault patterns)
**Soul.md Voice:** Insider-to-insider, founder revealing. Not a pitch — a reveal.

---

## Simple Reframe Variants

### SR-1: "You've Never Been Diagnosed"

**Three-part structure:**
- **What they think:** "I need better tips, a better instructor, or a better app."
- **What's real:** "You've never been diagnosed. Not once. Every lesson, every video, every app — they all prescribed treatment. But nobody ever ran the test first."
- **Why nothing worked:** "You can't fix what hasn't been identified. Every solution you've tried was a prescription without a diagnosis. That's not a failure of effort. That's a failure of the system."

**Anchor phrase:** "You've never been diagnosed."
**Reframe technique:** false_solution_expose + blame_removal
**Counter-intuitiveness:** 8/10 — Golfers have NEVER heard their problem framed as an absence of diagnosis. They think in terms of instruction quality, not diagnostic absence.
**Externalization:** 10/10 — Entirely external. The system failed them.

### SR-2: "Nobody Identified THE Flaw"

**Three-part structure:**
- **What they think:** "I have several swing problems I need to work through."
- **What's real:** "You have ONE root swing flaw causing everything else. Every instructor you've seen identified A flaw. Nobody identified THE flaw."
- **Why nothing worked:** "Working on compensations without finding the root flaw is an infinite loop. Fix one, another appears. The list never gets shorter because the source was never found."

**Anchor phrase:** "A flaw vs. THE flaw."
**Reframe technique:** contrarian_truth + false_belief_destruction
**Counter-intuitiveness:** 9/10 — The distinction between "a flaw" and "THE flaw" is a genuine insight most golfers have never articulated.
**Externalization:** 9/10 — Blames the instruction model, not the golfer.

### SR-3: "Treatment Without Diagnosis"

**Three-part structure:**
- **What they think:** "I need to find the right approach to fixing my swing."
- **What's real:** "The golf improvement industry has been giving you treatment without ever running a diagnosis. Every tip is a prescription. Every lesson is a prescription. None of them started with identifying YOUR specific root flaw."
- **Why nothing worked:** "You wouldn't take medicine without a diagnosis. But that's exactly what the golf industry has been asking you to do for years. And it has a 70% failure rate to prove it."

**Anchor phrase:** "Treatment without diagnosis."
**Reframe technique:** false_solution_expose + scientific_discovery
**Counter-intuitiveness:** 8/10 — Medical framing is intuitive once stated but nobody has stated it in golf.
**Externalization:** 10/10 — Entirely systemic blame.

### SR-4: "The Missing First Step"

**Three-part structure:**
- **What they think:** "I've tried everything — lessons, videos, apps, training aids."
- **What's real:** "You haven't tried everything. You've tried every TREATMENT. But you've never tried the one thing that was supposed to come first: a diagnosis of your root swing flaw."
- **Why nothing worked:** "Imagine jumping to Step 2 without ever doing Step 1. That's what the golf industry has been doing for decades. Every solution skips the diagnostic step. That's why talented, dedicated golfers like you stay stuck."

**Anchor phrase:** "The missing first step."
**Reframe technique:** false_solution_expose + blame_removal
**Counter-intuitiveness:** 7/10 — "Missing step" is intuitive but slightly more generic than SR-1 or SR-2.
**Externalization:** 10/10 — The step was missing from the SYSTEM.
