**Golf niche pattern:** Simple Reframe is dominant (3/4 TIER1 swipes). The root cause is stated as a contrarian truth or false-belief destruction — no syndromes, no villains as primary. The audience responds to "here's what you didn't know" energy, not "here's who's been lying to you" energy.

| Rank | Method | Fit | Rationale |

## Recommended Expression Architecture

**Primary:** Simple Reframe — "You've never been diagnosed" / "A flaw vs. THE flaw"
**Supporting metaphor:** Medical diagnosis — "Nobody ever ran the test"
**Supporting villain:** Systemic — "The Symptom Industry" (the model, not the people)
**Supporting dual:** "One flaw, a dozen compensations"

This architecture matches TIER1 golf vault patterns (simple reframe as primary) while adding depth layers that the research uniquely supports (medical analogy is organic, villain is systemic not personal).
