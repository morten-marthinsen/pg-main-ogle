**Primary root cause passes proof constraint at 8.0/10.**

The root cause claim ("the golf improvement industry operates on treatment-without-diagnosis") is exceptionally well-proven:

- Statistical proof: Handicap unchanged despite technology (DISC-05, 9.20)

The proof gaps exist in the SOLUTION layer (PG1's ability to deliver), not the PROBLEM layer (the diagnosis gap). The promise ceiling (62/100) correctly limits WHAT WE CAN PROMISE about PG1 — but the root cause itself is strongly proven.

**Recommendation:** Lead with DIAGNOSIS root cause (proven). Promise CLARITY, not IMPROVEMENT (per proof ceiling). Let mechanism proof build confidence in PG1's ability to deliver the diagnosis.
