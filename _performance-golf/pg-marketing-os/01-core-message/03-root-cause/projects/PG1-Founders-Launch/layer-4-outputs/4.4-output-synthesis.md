## Root Cause — Final Statement

**"You've never been diagnosed."**

Nobody in the golf improvement industry — not one instructor, not one app, not one video — has ever identified the golfer's specific root swing flaw before prescribing treatment. The entire industry operates on treatment-without-diagnosis. That's why 70% of golfers who take lessons don't improve. That's why handicaps haven't changed despite more technology than ever. That's why dedicated golfers stay stuck despite years of effort.

The golfer wasn't failing. The diagnostic step was missing.

Now it exists.
