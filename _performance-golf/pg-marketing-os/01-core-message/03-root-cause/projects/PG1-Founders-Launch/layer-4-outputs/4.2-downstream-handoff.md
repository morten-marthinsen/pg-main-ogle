## Handoff to Skill 04: Mechanism

```yaml
mechanism_handoff:
  root_cause_to_solve: >
    The golf improvement industry operates on treatment-without-diagnosis.
    Nobody has ever identified the golfer's specific root swing flaw before
    prescribing treatment. Every lesson, app, video, and training aid skips
    the diagnostic step.

  must_address:
    - "How does PG1 diagnose the root swing flaw? (SwingScan AI — 2M+ swings, 96% accuracy, 77+ flaw patterns)"
    - "Why is AI diagnosis more reliable than human instructor diagnosis? (scale, consistency, pattern database)"
    - "What happens AFTER diagnosis? (Adaptive plan, coach assignment, sequenced improvement)"
    - "How does the diagnosis evolve over time? (Intelligence layer — gets smarter with use)"

  match_type: "direct"
  alignment_score: 10.0

  mechanism_must_deliver:
    primary: "Root flaw identification — the diagnosis itself"
    secondary: "Treatment plan based on diagnosis — not generic, but specific to diagnosed flaw"
    tertiary: "Progress tracking BY diagnosed flaw resolution — not just scores/stats"

  mechanism_must_NOT_do:
    - "Lead with AI technology specs (violates Soul.md anti-voice #3)"
    - "Claim improvement results without data (violates proof ceiling)"
    - "Position as 'better than lessons' without comparative data"

  key_proof_for_mechanism:
    - "MECH-01: Zero competitors claim diagnosis (9.00)"
    - "DATA-02/03/04: 2M+ swings, 96% accuracy, 77+ flaws"
    - "DISC-14: Human-AI collaboration beats either alone (8.20)"
    - "DISC-12: AI outperforms dermatologists in 63% of studies (7.80)"
    - "MECH-03: Medical/X-ray analogy organic to audience (8.15)"

  metaphor_recommendation: >
    Use medical diagnosis metaphor as primary framing for mechanism explanation.
    "SwingScan AI is the diagnostic test that never existed in golf." The audience
    already thinks in medical terms (X-ray, root cause, diagnosis). Don't fight
    their mental model — build on it.
```

## Handoff to Skill 05: Promise

```yaml
promise_handoff:
  what_becomes_possible: >
    For the first time, the golfer can KNOW — with precision — what their
    specific root swing flaw is. The promise is CLARITY, not improvement.
    "Know your ONE root flaw and have a plan built around fixing it."

  transformation_arc:
    before: "Confused, stuck, cycling through random fixes, quietly wondering if something is wrong with them"
    bridge: "Diagnosed — root flaw identified for the first time — clarity replaces confusion"
    after: "One clear path, one focus, effort directed at THE thing instead of everything"

  promise_ceiling_constraints:
    overall_ceiling: 62
    can_promise:
      - "Know your ONE root swing flaw (STRONG)"
      - "AI + coaches + adaptive plan (STRONG)"
      - "Be one of the first 1,000 founders (STRONG)"
      - "$299 once vs. thousands on random fixes (STRONG)"
      - "Stop wasting time on random tips (STRONG)"
    cannot_promise:
      - "Drop X strokes in Y weeks (NO DATA)"
      - "Better than lessons (NO COMPARATIVE DATA)"
      - "Guaranteed improvement (NO DATA)"

  promise_strategy: >
    Lead with DIAGNOSIS + SYSTEM + IDENTITY. The promise is CLARITY,
    not IMPROVEMENT. "We'll identify your root flaw and give you a plan"
    not "We'll fix your game." Promise ceiling allows strong diagnosis
    and system claims but prohibits results claims until 90-day data exists.

  emotional_promise: >
    "Love your game again." This is the deepest emotional destination —
    but it must be EARNED through the diagnosis-clarity-plan arc, not
    stated as a headline claim. Per Copywriting Heuristic #10: close on
    benefit, not mechanism. The emotional promise is the CLOSE, not the LEAD.
```
