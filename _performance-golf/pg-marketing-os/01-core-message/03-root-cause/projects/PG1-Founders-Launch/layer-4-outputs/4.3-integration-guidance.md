The root cause "You've never been diagnosed" should thread through EVERY creative touchpoint in the PG1 Founders Launch. Here's how it integrates:

## Language Guidelines

**USE:**
- "Diagnosed" / "diagnosis" — the primary frame
- "Root swing flaw" — the specific thing being diagnosed
- "Treatment without diagnosis" — what the industry does
- "The test" / "ran the test" — medical metaphor
- "A flaw vs. THE flaw" — the insight
- "Prescription without diagnosis" — why past solutions failed

**AVOID (per Soul.md):**
- "AI-powered diagnosis" — lead with outcome, not technology
- "Revolutionary" / "game-changing" — unsubstantiated hype
- "You've been doing it wrong" — internalizes blame
- "The industry lied to you" — too aggressive; use "the diagnostic didn't exist"
- "Diagnostic protocol" / "assessment algorithm" — clinical tech-speak
