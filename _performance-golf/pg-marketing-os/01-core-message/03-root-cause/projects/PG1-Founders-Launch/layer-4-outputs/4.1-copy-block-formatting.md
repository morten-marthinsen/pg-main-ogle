# 4.1 — Copy Block Formatting

**Microskill:** 4.1-copy-block-formatting
**Root Cause:** "You've never been diagnosed" (Hybrid 1 — The Diagnosis)
**Soul.md Voice:** Insider-to-insider, conversational, build → reveal → land

---

## Copy Block: Full Revelation (250 words)

Here's what nobody in golf has ever said to you:

"Let me find out what's actually wrong... before I tell you what to fix."

That's a diagnosis. And you've never had one.

Think about it. Every instructor you've seen looked at your swing and told you what to work on. Every app showed you data about what's happening. Every YouTube video gave you a tip to try.

But not one of them — not a single instructor, app, or video — first identified YOUR specific root swing flaw. The one underlying issue causing everything else.

They all skipped straight to the prescription. Without ever running the test.

That's why 70% of golfers who take lessons don't improve. That's why your handicap hasn't budged despite years of effort. That's why 15 different instructors gave you 15 different fixes. They were each seeing a different symptom — because the root flaw sat underneath, invisible without the right diagnostic tool.

It's not that the instructors were bad. It's that the diagnostic didn't exist in golf.

You weren't failing. You were being treated without being diagnosed.

Every frustrating practice session, every wasted lesson, every app that promised personalization and delivered generic tips — none of them failed because of you. They failed because they skipped the one step that should have come first.

Now that step exists. For the first time in golf, there's a way to identify your specific root swing flaw — before anything else.
