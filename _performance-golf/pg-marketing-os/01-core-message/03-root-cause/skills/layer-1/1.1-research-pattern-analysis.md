# 1.1 Research Pattern Analysis

## Purpose
Mine the Deep Research output for patterns that reveal root cause opportunities. This is the foundational derivation step—everything downstream depends on what patterns you extract here.

---

## Input Requirements

From Deep Research (01-research output):
- Audience complaints and frustrations
- Failed solution attempts documented
- Belief systems mapped
- Market sophistication indicators
- Competitor promise analysis
- Emotional trigger inventory

---

## Execution Protocol

### Step 1: Complaint Clustering

Group audience complaints into categories:

```
COMPLAINT CLUSTER TEMPLATE:
├── Surface Symptoms (what they notice)
│   └── [List: "I keep gaining weight", "I can't hit it straight"]
├── Attempted Solutions (what they've tried)
│   └── [List: "Diets", "YouTube videos", "New drivers"]
├── Failure Attribution (who/what they blame)
│   └── [List: "Willpower", "Age", "My swing"]
└── Emotional State (how they feel)
    └── [List: "Frustrated", "Embarrassed", "Hopeless"]
```

**Pattern Signal**: When multiple surface symptoms cluster around the same attempted solutions, a single root cause likely explains all of them.

---

### Step 2: Failed Solution Inventory

Document every solution the audience has tried:

| Solution Tried | Why They Thought It Would Work | Why It Actually Failed | Hidden Assumption |
|---------------|-------------------------------|----------------------|-------------------|
| [Solution] | [Their logic] | [Reality] | [What they believed] |

**Critical Question**: What do ALL failed solutions have in common?

The shared flaw across failures often points directly to the root cause.

**Golf Example** (from SSTS vault data):
- Tried: Swing mechanics, positions, timing
- Shared Flaw: All assume ball is at bottom of swing arc
- Root Cause: Misconception about low point mechanics

**Health Example** (from Cinderella Solution vault data):
- Tried: Diets, exercise, willpower
- Shared Flaw: All attack fat "from outside-in"
- Root Cause: Hormonal transition (insulin/cortisol/estrogen) hardwiring fat storage

---

### Step 3: Belief System Extraction

From research, identify core beliefs the audience holds:

```
BELIEF INVENTORY:
├── About the Problem
│   └── "What they think causes their issue"
├── About Solutions
│   └── "What they think will fix it"
├── About Themselves
│   └── "What they believe about their own capability/fault"
└── About the Market
    └── "What they believe about available options"
```

**Pattern Signal**: When beliefs about causes contradict observable failed solution data, a false belief root cause opportunity exists.

---

### Step 4: Frustration Source Mapping

Track WHERE frustration originates:

| Frustration Type | Source | Frequency | Intensity |
|-----------------|--------|-----------|-----------|
| With self | "I just can't stick to it" | High | Moderate |
| With solutions | "Nothing works for me" | Very High | High |
| With experts | "They don't understand my situation" | Moderate | High |
| With circumstance | "My age/body/genetics" | High | Very High |

**Root Cause Signal**: The highest-intensity frustration sources often reveal what the ROOT problem actually is (not the surface problem).

---

### Step 5: Competitor Gap Analysis

From research, map what competitors promise vs. what they deliver:

```
COMPETITOR GAP MAP:
├── Competitor A
│   ├── Promise: [What they claim]
│   ├── Mechanism: [How they say it works]
│   └── Gap: [What they miss/ignore]
├── Competitor B
│   └── [Same structure]
└── PATTERN: [What ALL competitors miss]
```

**Critical Insight**: If every competitor addresses the same surface problem the same way, the ROOT problem hasn't been identified yet. That's your opportunity.

```yaml
complaint_clusters:

  shared_flaw: "[What they ALL got wrong]"
  hidden_assumption: "[What every solution assumed]"

  - belief: "[What they think]"
    evidence: "[What actually happens]"
    contradiction_type: "false_cause | false_solution | false_self"

  highest_intensity: "[Source]"
  most_frequent: "[Source]"
  hidden_frustration: "[What they don't say but feel]"

  what_all_miss: "[The gap]"
  why_they_miss_it: "[Structural reason]"
  opportunity: "[Root cause they ignore]"
```
