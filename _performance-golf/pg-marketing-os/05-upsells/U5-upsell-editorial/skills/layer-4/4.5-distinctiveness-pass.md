# Microskill 4.5: Distinctiveness Pass (Upsells)

**Layer:** 4 (Fix Application)
**Purpose:** Detect whether editorial cleanup has stripped upsell copy of its momentum-carrying voice, reducing it to generic "add this too" language
**Sequence:** Runs AFTER rescore (4.1), sequence validation (4.2), and output packaging (4.3)

---

## WHY THIS PASS EXISTS

Upsell editorial focuses on sequence consistency and conversion rate — both push copy toward formulaic upsell templates. This pass checks whether each upsell feels like a natural extension of the main purchase rather than a generic add-on pitch.

**References:**
- `~system/humanization-specimens/SIGNATURE-MOVES.md` (Upsells section)
- `~system/humanization-specimens/engine-packs/upsell-pack.md`
- Campaign Soul.md
