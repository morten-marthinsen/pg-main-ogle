## Lead Boundary Definition

Per Stefan Georgi's Lead Formula (see `source-teachings/Copy Internship - Leads + Open Loops.md`):

**A lead ENDS when you start to EXPLAIN.** The lead's ONLY purpose is to:
- Call out prospective customer
- Catch attention
- Draw them in to keep reading/watching

**Leads DO:**
- Call out problems
- Promise solution to problems
- HINT at unique mechanism (not explain it)
- HINT at counter-intuitive information
- BRIEFLY insert credibility/authority
- BRIEFLY tease discovery story
- BRIEFLY address skepticism

**Leads DON'T:**
- Explain HOW the mechanism works
- Explain in detail WHY old solutions don't work
- Go DEEPLY into the discovery story
- Explain what the product IS

The specimens above follow this structure — they TEASE without EXPLAINING. The mechanism science, detailed credibility, and product reveal come AFTER the lead.
