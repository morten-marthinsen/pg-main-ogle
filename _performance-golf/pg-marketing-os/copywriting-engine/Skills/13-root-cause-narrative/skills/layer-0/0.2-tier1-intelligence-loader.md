**Version:** 1.0

---

## PURPOSE

Load the TIER1 root cause narrative intelligence document and index all 12 analytical parts into a queryable structure that downstream microskills can reference. This is the TEACHING SUBSTITUTE for Skill 13 — because this is a TIER1-taught execution skill with no external source teachings, the patterns extracted from elite control analysis ARE the curriculum. Every classification, writing decision, and quality check downstream depends on this index.

---

## IDENTITY

**This microskill IS:**
- The teaching loader for a TIER1-derived skill — it replaces what source-teaching loaders do in other skills
- An indexer that creates structured, queryable access to 12 distinct analytical sections
- The bridge between raw TIER1 analysis and the microskills that need specific pattern lookups
- A schema enforcer that ensures all 12 parts of the intelligence document are present and accessible

**This microskill is NOT:**
- An analyst — it does not interpret or extend the TIER1 findings, only indexes them
- A source-teaching loader — there are no source teachings; the TIER1 analysis IS the teaching
- A pattern matcher — downstream microskills do the matching; this skill makes patterns accessible
- A generator of new patterns — it surfaces what was found in the TIER1 analysis, nothing more

## QUALITY CRITERIA

1. **All 12 sections indexed** — the loader must attempt to parse and index every section of the TIER1 intelligence document
2. **Queryable structure** — downstream microskills must be able to look up patterns by name, niche, type, or frequency without re-parsing the source document
3. **TIER1 examples preserved** — every indexed pattern must retain its concrete examples from elite controls (Gundry, Haney, Stansberry, etc.)
4. **Cross-reference capability** — the index must support cross-referencing (e.g., "which communication types are common in health niches?")
5. **Stacking/compatibility data preserved** — framing pattern compatibility and reframe stacking matrices must be queryable
6. **Critical rules fully indexed** — all 10 rules with check criteria must be available for the Layer 4 rules checker
7. **Zero silent gaps** — every missing section must be explicitly logged

---

## FAILURE MODES

| Failure | Severity | Detection | Handling |
|---------|----------|-----------|----------|
| Intelligence document not found | CRITICAL | File not at expected path | HALT — this skill cannot function without its teaching foundation |
| Section missing from document | HIGH | Section header not found during parsing | Log gap, continue indexing remaining sections, flag for human review |
| Section present but unparseable | MODERATE | Section found but content cannot be structured | Extract raw text, flag as "raw_only" — downstream skills get text but no structured queries |
| TIER1 examples missing from section | LOW | Pattern indexed but no control examples | Flag in warnings — classifications may lack vault references |
| Fewer than 10 sections found | CRITICAL | sections_found < 10 | LOG critical warning, allow continuation but flag that classification and validation reliability is compromised |
| Cross-vertical patterns incomplete | MODERATE | Fewer than 3 niches profiled | Continue with available data; flag that niche-specific recommendations may be less accurate |
