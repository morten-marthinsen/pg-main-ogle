### Swipe-Worthy Patterns to Model

**1. Massive Economic Contrast**

- Never feels redundant because framing changes

**7. Promise Breadth Validates Mechanism**
- If EDTA clears arteries, improved circulation should fix 12+ conditions
- Breadth of promises makes mechanism MORE believable, not less
- **When mechanism is systemic, promise wide**
