#### What Makes This Copy Work (and How to Apply It)

**Core Success Factors:**

1. **Authority Front-Loading**
   - **Pattern:** Establish credentials in first 10 words
   - **Application:** In health copy, answer "who are you?" before "what is this?"
   - **Template:** "[Credential] + [Years Experience] + [Specialized Knowledge]"
   - **Example:** "When you've been a board-certified cardiologist as long as I have..."

2. **Simplification Positioning**
   - **Pattern:** Reframe solution as simplification of complex problem
   - **Application:** In mature markets, simplification beats novelty
   - **Template:** "Why [painful complex approach] when [simple elegant solution]"
   - **Example:** "Why gag on a dozen supplements when ONE is all you need"

3. **Proof Density Through Brevity**
   - **Pattern:** Every sentence does persuasion work - no filler
   - **Application:** In short-format copy, pack proof into every paragraph
