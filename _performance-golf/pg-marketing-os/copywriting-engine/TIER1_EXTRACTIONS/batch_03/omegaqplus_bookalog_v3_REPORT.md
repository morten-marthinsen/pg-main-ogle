**3. WHY NOTHING WORKED (Failure Explanation):**

You may address one marker while neglecting others, or the complexity of managing multiple bottles leads to inconsistent use. Plus the cost - nearly $1,000 annually - makes it unsustainable long-term."

**Reframe Strength:** 7/10 - SOLID LOGIC but LIGHT EMOTION

**Why not higher:**
Not emotionally dramatic. Doesn't reveal shocking conspiracy, hidden villain, or paradigm-shifting science. Simply offers better approach to accepted problem. For sophisticated audience, this is sufficient. For mass market, might need more drama.
