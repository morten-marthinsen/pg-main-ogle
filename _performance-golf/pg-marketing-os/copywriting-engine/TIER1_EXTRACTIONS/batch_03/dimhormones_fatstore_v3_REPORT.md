### Three-Part Structure Quality

**What They Think:**
"Weight gain over 40 is about slowing metabolism, eating too much, or not exercising enough. Men think hormones are just a women's issue. Women think it's just menopause. Both think they need more willpower."

**What It Really Is:**
"Estrogen dominance - an imbalance where estrogen levels become too high relative to other hormones (progesterone in women, testosterone in men). This affects BOTH genders over 40. Excess estrogen signals the body to store fat, especially around the midsection, and prevents fat burning no matter how much you diet or exercise. It's a biological lock on your fat cells."

**Why Nothing Worked:**
"Diets and exercise can't overcome hormonal fat storage signals. Your body was receiving constant biochemical instructions to STORE fat from excess estrogen. Cutting calories or exercising more just made you hungry and tired while the hormonal signal kept forcing fat storage. You were fighting biology with willpower - an unwinnable battle."
