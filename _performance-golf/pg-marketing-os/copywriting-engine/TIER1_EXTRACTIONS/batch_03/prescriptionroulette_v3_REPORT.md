## ROOT CAUSE ARCHITECTURE ANALYSIS

### Three-Part Structure

#### What They Think
"Following your doctor's prescription orders is the safe, responsible thing to do. Prescription drugs go through rigorous FDA testing and are proven safe and effective. Your doctor has your best interests at heart and knows which medications are best for you."

**Analysis:** This captures conventional wisdom perfectly. Sets up betrayal narrative.

#### What It Really Is
"The medical establishment is playing prescription roulette with your life. If you're over 60 and follow doctors' orders, there's a 60% chance you'll be the victim of a medical or surgical disaster. Drugs like Vioxx and Bextra were prescribed by trusted doctors for years before being exposed as 'savage killers' causing heart attacks. Antidepressants increase suicide risk instead of lifting mood. The system doesn't protect you - it endangers you."

**Analysis:** Reframe is complete reversal. "Safe" → "roulette." "Protective" → "endangering." Uses specific examples to prove pattern.

#### Why Nothing Worked
"Your health problems persist or worsen because the prescriptions themselves are causing new problems. You're not healing - you're being poisoned. Each drug creates side effects that require more drugs, creating a deadly cascade. The pharmaceutical industry profits from keeping you sick, not making you well."

**Analysis:** Explains failure cycle. Not lack of willpower/compliance, but systemic harm. Positions continued prescription use as continuation of problem not solution.

### Villain Profile

**Villain Type:** Pharma
**Villain Name:** Big Pharma / Pharmaceutical Industry / Medical Establishment
**Villain Role:** Profiteer
**Villain Motivation:** "Pharmaceutical companies make billions by keeping Americans on prescription drugs. They rush drugs to market, hide dangerous side effects, and continue profiting even after drugs are exposed as killers (Vioxx, Bextra killed thousands before being pulled). Doctors are complicit - incentivized to prescribe, not educated on natural alternatives. The entire system profits from your illness and dependency."

**Villain Evidence:**
- Vioxx and Bextra were "savage killers" before yanking
- Antidepressants increase suicide but still prescribed
- 60% disaster rate for over-60 patients
- Pattern of approval → prescription → deaths → eventual recall

**Villain Strength Analysis:** 9/10

- Proven guilty (Vioxx scandal is public record)
- Ongoing threat (current prescriptions may be next scandal)
