## ROOT CAUSE ARCHITECTURE

### Three-Part Reframe Structure

**What They Think:**
"Arterial plaque is a natural part of aging that can only be removed through expensive surgical procedures like angioplasty or bypass surgery. Poor circulation and cardiovascular problems require invasive medical intervention."

**What It Really Is:**
"Arterial plaque can be safely dissolved and eliminated from your entire circulatory system (from tiny micro-capillaries to major arteries) using EDTA, an amino acid that naturally chelates and removes the calcium, heavy metals, and deposits that form plaque. Unlike surgery that only addresses specific blockages, EDTA scrubs your entire vascular system clean."

**Why Nothing Worked:**
"Previous treatments failed because they only addressed symptoms or required invasive surgery at specific blockage sites. Nothing systemically cleaned your entire circulatory system. EDTA works throughout your body, reaching places surgery can't, harmlessly removing plaque through natural elimination."

### Villain Profile: PHARMACEUTICAL COMPANIES & DOCTORS

**Villain Type:** Pharma
**Role:** Profiteer
**Motivation:** Protecting multi-billion dollar cardiovascular surgery industry. Bypass surgery and angioplasty are extremely profitable procedures. If people knew they could avoid these surgeries with an inexpensive amino acid already approved as safe by FDA/USDA, it would destroy their revenue stream.

**Villain Evidence:**
- EDTA has been proven in 1,820+ medical studies yet remains "little known secret"
- Declared 100% safe by FDA/USDA as food additive (it's in baby food!)
- Yet doctors don't mention it to patients facing surgery
- This pattern implies deliberate suppression

**Genius Move:** The promo never explicitly accuses anyone of anything illegal or unethical. It just presents facts that *imply* suppression and lets reader draw conspiracy conclusion themselves. Legally safer while emotionally more powerful (reader "discovers" the conspiracy rather than being told).

### Countersells (3 major)
1. **Angioplasty is unnecessary** - Bill Nichols' story proves blockages can disappear naturally with EDTA
2. **Cholesterol medications are incomplete** - Edwarda Collins took prescribed pills for years with no results until EDTA
3. **Surgery only addresses specific blockages** - EDTA cleans entire system including micro-capillaries surgery can't reach
