1. **Trial** - 1 canister: $39.99 + $7.99 S&H = $47.98
2. **Good Deal** - 3-month + 1 FREE (4 canisters): $119.99 + $7.99 S&H = $127.98
3. **Great Deal** - 6-month + 2 FREE (8 canisters): $199.99 (saves $164.77)
4. **Best Deal** - 12-month + 4 FREE (16 canisters): $299.99 (saves $414, only $0.62/day)

### Price Psychology Mastery

**Anchor Price:** $853.27/month (if buying 38 ingredients separately)

**Actual Price (Best Deal):** $18.68/month

**Perceived Discount:** $834.59 monthly savings (97.8% off!)

**Daily Reframe:** Just $0.62/day (62 cents) - less than a cup of coffee

**Strategic Analysis:**

This pricing architecture is masterful because it:

1. **Extreme Anchoring:** $853 is so high that $299 for 16 months feels almost free
2. **Daily Reframe:** 62 cents feels trivial, yet totals to $226/year
3. **Volume Incentive:** Best Deal is 16 months for $300, creating strong incentive for maximum commitment
4. **Value Stacking:** Each tier adds FREE canisters, making higher tiers feel like stealing

**Guarantee Elements:**

1. Five servings fruits/vegetables - **GUARANTEED**
2. 38 whole foods - **GUARANTEED**

Most guarantees are single-dimension ("satisfaction guaranteed"). This four-way approach pre-handles four different skepticism angles simultaneously.
