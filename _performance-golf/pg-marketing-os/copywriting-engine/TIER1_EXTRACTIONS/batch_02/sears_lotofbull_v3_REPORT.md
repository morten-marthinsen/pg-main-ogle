## Root Cause Architecture

### Three-Part Reframe Structure

**What They Think:**
"Health problems come from eating too much fat, not exercising enough, and natural aging. Low testosterone and declining masculinity are just part of getting older."

**What It Really Is:**
"Modern medicine and the food industry have conspired to feminize men through low-fat diets, grain-based nutrition, statin drugs, and suppression of natural testosterone. The same doctors giving advice are fat, unhealthy, and practice the opposite of what they preach."

**Why Nothing Worked:**
"You're following advice from fat doctors with pasty faces who tell you to eat low-fat, avoid meat, and accept declining masculinity as 'normal aging.' These doctors don't practice what they preach - they're unhealthy themselves. You can't get healthy following advice from unhealthy people pushing feminizing protocols."

### Villain Profile

**Villain Type:** Medical Establishment
**Villain Name:** "Modern Medicine" / "Fat Doctors"
**Villain Role:** Deceiver
**Villain Motivation:** "Medical establishment profits from sick, dependent patients. Healthy, vital, masculine men don't need constant medical intervention. By pushing low-fat diets, grain consumption, and statin drugs, they create lifelong patients while suppressing natural testosterone and masculinity."

**Villain Evidence:**
- Visual evidence: doctors giving health advice are "fat, with pasty faces and big red noses"
- Behavioral evidence: they don't practice what they preach
- Outcome evidence: their advice makes men softer, more feminine, less healthy
- Contrast evidence: Dr. Sears at 46 is super-fit by doing opposite of what they recommend

**Villain Sophistication:** Extremely sophisticated. Not abstract (like "Big Pharma"), but visceral and visual ("fat doctors with pasty faces"). Makes enemy immediately identifiable and creates disgust response.

### Countersells

1. **Low-fat diets:** "Low-fat diets don't work - they feminize you and make you fatter. Eating fat makes you lean and masculine."

2. **More exercise:** "More exercise alone won't help if you're following feminizing nutrition advice from fat doctors."

3. **Normal aging:** "Your declining masculinity isn't 'normal aging' - it's the result of following modern medicine's feminizing protocols."

4. **Willpower/discipline:** "You don't need more willpower - you need different information from a doctor who actually practices what he preaches."

**Countersell Strategy:** Each countersell protects belief by showing WHY past solutions failed - not because solution was wrong idea, but because ENEMY was blocking it or prescribing opposite.

**Secondary:** opposite_of_truth (what they say is healthy is actually harmful)

**Emphasis Level:** Foundation - entire copy built on this reframe
**Placement:** Lead - introduced immediately
**Repetition:** Woven throughout all sections
