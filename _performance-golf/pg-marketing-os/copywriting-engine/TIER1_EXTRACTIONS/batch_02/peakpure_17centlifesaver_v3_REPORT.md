## ROOT CAUSE ARCHITECTURE

### Three-Part Structure

**What They Think:**
Heart disease and arterial plaque buildup requires expensive medical interventions like bypass surgery, stents, or ongoing medications. High cholesterol and genetics are the primary causes.

**What It Really Is:**
Calcium plaque deposits in arteries can be dissolved naturally using EDTA (ethylenediamine tetra-acetic acid), an amino acid that chelates (grabs hold of) metals and calcium in the bloodstream and flushes them out through urine and stool. This was discovered in the 1930s and proven effective during WWII.

**Why Nothing Worked:**
Standard medical treatments focus on surgery or statins, not addressing the root accumulation of calcium plaque. EDTA chelation has been proven effective in 1,823 studies but remains underutilized because it's not profitable - 17 cents per capsule vs $117,094 for bypass surgery.

### Villain Profile

**Type:** Medical establishment
**Name:** The cardiac surgery industry and pharmaceutical companies
**Role:** Profiteer

**Motivation:** Bypass surgery costs $117,094 per procedure. IV chelation therapy costs $3,000+. There's massive financial incentive to pursue expensive interventions over a 17-cent natural solution. The medical establishment profits from expensive procedures, not affordable prevention.

**Evidence:** Mayo Clinic and Harvard University have validated the effectiveness of chelation therapy, yet it's rarely recommended as first-line treatment. Dr. Norman Clarke Sr. documented this in his research decades ago. Dr. Garry Gordon, the "Father of Chelation," has been advocating for this for years but faces industry resistance.

### Key Metaphors
- WWII sailors being saved from lead poisoning
- Calcium plaque as "unwanted deposits"
- EDTA as "grabbing hold" and "flushing out" toxins

### Reframe Technique
**Suppressed Science** - The information exists, the studies are published (1,823 of them!), top institutions validated it (Mayo Clinic, Harvard), but you haven't heard about it because of profit motives.

### Countersells
1. **Bypass surgery** is expensive ($117,094) and risky - EDTA offers safer, cheaper alternative
2. **Statins** only manage cholesterol, don't remove existing plaque - EDTA actually dissolves calcium deposits
3. **IV chelation therapy** works but costs $3,000+ - oral EDTA provides similar benefits for pennies
