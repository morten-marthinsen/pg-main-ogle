**Tactic:** Split market into types to create "you need specialized solution"
**Example:** Metabolism A vs. Metabolism B
**Why It Works:** Explains why generic advice fails (designed for other type), creates uniqueness (first program for YOUR type), removes shame (not your fault, different biology)
**Application:** Find binary or typology in your market (responders vs. non-responders, slow vs. fast metabolizers, Type 1 vs. Type 2)
