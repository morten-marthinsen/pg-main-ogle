# Microskill 4.5: Distinctiveness Pass (Ads)

**Layer:** 4 (Fix Application)
**Purpose:** Detect whether pre-launch scoring and optimization have drifted ad copy toward safe, tested-to-death generic performance marketing language
**Sequence:** Runs AFTER scorecard assembly (4.1), execution log (4.2), and checkpoint files (4.3)

---

## WHY THIS PASS EXISTS

Ad scoring optimizes for platform compliance, hook strength, and format adherence — all pushing copy toward proven ad templates that work for any product. This pass checks whether each ad variant carries a distinctive visceral image rather than diluted multi-benefit messaging.

**References:**
- `~system/humanization-specimens/SIGNATURE-MOVES.md` (Ads section)
- `~system/humanization-specimens/engine-packs/ads-pack.md`
- Campaign Soul.md
