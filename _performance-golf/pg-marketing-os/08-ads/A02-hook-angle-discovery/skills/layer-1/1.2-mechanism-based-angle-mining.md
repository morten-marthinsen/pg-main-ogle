# 1.2: Mechanism-Based Angle Mining

## Purpose

Extract strategic angles from Mechanism (Skill 04) and Research competitor mechanism quotes. These angles highlight the unique mechanism as a discovery/revelation or challenge competitor mechanisms as incomplete.

## Inputs

- Mechanism package (from Skill 04)
- Research competitor mechanism quotes (from Research Handoff)

## Process

1. **Extract unique mechanism**
   - Mechanism name (if finalized)
   - Mechanism concept (how it works)
   - What makes this mechanism DIFFERENT from competitor mechanisms?

2. **Identify competitor mechanism landscape**
   - What mechanisms do competitors promote?
   - What's saturated? (everyone talks about X)
   - What's missing? (nobody talks about Y)

3. **Derive mechanism-based angles that:**
   - **Highlight the unique mechanism as discovery/revelation**
     - "This one thing nobody else talks about..."
     - "The missing piece everyone overlooks..."
   - **Challenge competitor mechanisms as incomplete/wrong**
     - "Why X doesn't work (and what does)..."
     - "The problem with every other solution..."
   - **Position mechanism as the 'missing piece'**
     - "What if the real answer was simpler than you thought?"

4. **Plain language angle statements**
   - NO mechanism name in the angle (angles lead TO mechanism)
   - State the strategic idea, not the mechanism itself

5. **Source attribution**
   - Which mechanism element is this angle highlighting?
   - Which competitor mechanisms is this challenging?
   - How does this angle differentiate?
