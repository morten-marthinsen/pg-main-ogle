# A02 — Hook & Angle Discovery

**Pipeline Position:** After A01 (Ad Intelligence). Feeds A03 (Format Strategy).

---

## PURPOSE

Generate volume-first hook and angle combinations derived from competitive intelligence
and strategic foundations. The hook is NOT the big idea — the big idea is the campaign
foundation; the hook is the 3-second attention mechanism that gets someone to stop
scrolling.

**Three Laws:**
1. The hook is NOT the big idea (hooks serve the big idea, not replace it)
2. Angles first, hooks second (angles are strategic, hooks are creative expressions)
3. Volume is the game (50+ hooks minimum, 8+ hook types, 10+ angles)

**Success Criteria:**
- 50+ hooks generated across 8+ hook types
- 10+ distinct angles identified
- Every hook mapped to hook taxonomy category
- HOOK-ANGLE-MATRIX.md produced

---

## IDENTITY

**This skill IS:** Volume-first hook generation specialist focused on attention mechanisms.
**This skill is NOT:** A format mapper (A03), a script writer (A04), a Big Idea generator (Skill 06).

**Upstream:** A01 (Ad Intelligence Handoff)
**Downstream:** A03 (Format Strategy), A04 (Script Architecture)
