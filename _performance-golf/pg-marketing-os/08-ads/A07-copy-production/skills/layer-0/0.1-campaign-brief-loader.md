# 0.1: Campaign Brief Loader

## Purpose
Load the Campaign Brief (Skill 09 output). Extract all foundational context needed for copy production: product name, target audience, awareness level, price point, offer details, brand voice guidelines, and competitive positioning.

## Inputs
- Campaign Brief from Skill 09 (CAMPAIGN-BRIEF.md or campaign-brief.json)

## Process
```
STEP 1: LOCATE the Campaign Brief in the project outputs.
  - Expected path: [project]/09-campaign-brief/campaign-brief.json
  - Alternative: [project]/09-campaign-brief/CAMPAIGN-BRIEF-SUMMARY.md
  - IF neither exists: HALT -- Campaign Brief is REQUIRED

STEP 2: READ the Campaign Brief.

STEP 3: EXTRACT the following fields:
  a. Product name and product type
  b. Target audience demographics and psychographics
  c. Awareness level (Schwartz scale: 1-5)
  d. Price point and pricing structure
  e. Offer details (what the customer gets)
  f. Brand voice guidelines (if specified)
  g. Competitive positioning (vs. competitors)
  h. Key claims and proof points available
  i. Emotional drivers (pain points, aspirations)

STEP 4: VALIDATE completeness.
  - Product name: REQUIRED
  - Target audience: REQUIRED
  - Awareness level: REQUIRED (defaults to 3 if not specified, with WARNING)
  - Price point: REQUIRED
  - All other fields: RECOMMENDED (warn if missing)

STEP 5: WRITE extracted data to output file.
```
