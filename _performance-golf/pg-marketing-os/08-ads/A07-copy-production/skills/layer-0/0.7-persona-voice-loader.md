# 0.7: Persona Voice Loader

## Purpose
Load winning ad specimens for voice calibration. Source: A01 winning ad specimens (Section 6 of AD-INTELLIGENCE-HANDOFF.md) + ad-persona-specimens/ for relevant verticals. Hold 3-5 verbatim winning ad scripts in active context as voice calibration specimens during Layers 1-2. These are the statistical attractors that prevent generic copy. Also load Soul.md if it exists for project-level voice constraints.

## Inputs
- A01 AD-INTELLIGENCE-HANDOFF.md (Section 6: winning ad specimens)
- ad-persona-specimens/ directory (vertical-specific voice specimens)
- Vertical profile (from 0.0.1 -- for vertical-specific specimen selection)
- Campaign brief (from 0.1 -- for niche matching)
- Soul.md (if exists -- project-level voice constraints)

## Process
```
STEP 1: READ A01 AD-INTELLIGENCE-HANDOFF.md Section 6 (Winning Ad Specimens).
  - Extract verbatim winning ad scripts from the competitive landscape
  - These are PROVEN performers in this market

STEP 2: CHECK ad-persona-specimens/ for vertical-relevant specimens.
  - Path: ./ads/ad-persona-specimens/[vertical]/
  - Select specimens that match the campaign's niche and product type
  - Prioritize specimens with similar: audience, product type, price point, platform

STEP 3: SELECT 3-5 specimens for voice calibration.
  - Selection criteria:
    a. Niche relevance (same or adjacent vertical)
    b. Performance evidence (proven winners preferred)
    c. Tone match (aligned with campaign voice direction)
    d. Platform match (same platform as primary target)
    e. Length match (similar ad length to targets)
  - These specimens will be HELD IN ACTIVE CONTEXT during Layers 1-2

STEP 4: CHECK for Soul.md.
  - Path: [project]/SOUL.md
  - IF EXISTS: Load and extract voice constraints:
    - Voice register
    - Energy signature
    - Anti-voice patterns (FORBIDDEN in generation)
    - Pacing signature
    - Positive specimens
    - Anti-specimens
  - IF NOT EXISTS: WARN -- generate without taste constraints

STEP 5: WRITE loaded specimens and voice constraints to output file.
  - Include the FULL TEXT of each selected specimen (verbatim, not summarized)
  - Include Soul.md constraints
```
