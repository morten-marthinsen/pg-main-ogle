# 1.4: Visual Competitive Positioning

## Purpose
Cross-reference visual strategy against A01 competitive intelligence. Identify: where our visual approach differentiates from competitors, where it intentionally aligns (platform norms), and potential visual whitespace opportunities (styles competitors are not using). This analysis validates that our visual direction is strategically positioned, not just technically correct.

## Inputs
- Treatment type assignments from 1.1 (our treatment type distribution)
- Color palettes from 1.2 (our color choices)
- Competitive visual landscape from 0.3 (what competitors are doing)

## Process
1. READ treatment type assignments from 1.1
2. READ color palettes from 1.2
3. READ competitive visual landscape from 0.3
4. COMPARE our treatment type distribution vs competitors:
   a. Where are we aligned with dominant competitor patterns?
      - Document WHY alignment is strategic (platform norms, audience expectations)
   b. Where are we differentiated from competitor patterns?
      - Document HOW this differentiation serves our campaign goals
   c. Where are competitors NOT present (whitespace)?
      - Assess if whitespace represents opportunity or is absent for a reason
5. COMPARE our color palette vs competitor color patterns:
   a. Colors we share with competitors (category conventions)
   b. Colors that differentiate us
   c. Color whitespace opportunities
6. ASSESS visual style differentiation:
   a. Pacing/editing style vs competitors
   b. Text overlay style vs competitors
   c. Talent style vs competitors
   d. Production quality level vs competitors
7. IDENTIFY strategic adjustments:
   a. Should any treatment type be changed to improve differentiation?
   b. Should any color choice be adjusted?
   c. Are there whitespace opportunities worth exploiting?
8. WRITE competitive positioning analysis to output file
