# 2.12 — Analyst Synthesis

**Purpose:** Analyst synthesizes all audience agent evaluations into an Analytical Brief for Round 2 competitors.

**Reference:** `~system/protocols/AUDIENCE-AGENT-PROTOCOL.md`

---

## Inputs
- `audience-evaluation-raw.yaml` from Step 2.11
- Round 1 Analytical Brief

## Process
1. Cluster objections by theme and frequency
2. Identify audience segments with strongest/weakest response
3. Map dimension-level gaps (which dimensions scored lowest across agents)
4. Extract specific language/proof that resonated vs. fell flat
5. Synthesize into Analytical Brief with actionable intelligence

## Output
- `analytical-brief.md` -- Structured brief containing:
  - Top objection clusters with frequency counts
  - Audience segment response patterns
  - Dimension-level gap analysis
  - Specific resonance/failure evidence
  - Actionable recommendations for Round 2
