# 2.11: Round 3 Persona Evaluation (FINAL)

## Purpose
All 7 personas receive the Cumulative Learning Brief. FINAL competitive evaluation. Best possible output incorporating all learnings from Rounds 1-2. Full persona perspective with integrated techniques. This is the FINAL competitive evaluation -- quality expectations are highest.

## Inputs
- 2.10-round2-cumulative-learning-brief output (cumulative learnings R1+R2)
- 1.3-concept-summary-generator output (concept summaries)
- 0.5-persona-specimen-validator output (specimens still in active context)
- Round 2 results (scores, critiques)

## Process
```
STEP 1: Load Cumulative Learning Brief
  - READ: layer-2-outputs/2.10-round2-cumulative-learning-brief.md
  - ABSORB: Persistent strengths and weaknesses
  - ABSORB: Effective techniques from R1+R2
  - READ: Persona-specific Round 3 guidance

STEP 2: FOR EACH concept:
  FOR EACH persona (7 total):

    A. ABSORB cumulative learnings (effort: max)
      - Integrate techniques that proved effective
      - Target persistent weaknesses specifically
      - Maintain OWN evaluative lens (FINAL -- this is your best work)

    B. GENERATE FINAL evaluation
      - This is the PEAK quality evaluation
      - All learnings from 2 rounds integrated
      - All specimens referenced
      - Concept evaluated as complete atomic unit
      - This evaluation goes to synthesis and human selection

    C. SCORE against all 7 criteria
      - HIGHEST quality scoring with evidence
      - Scores must reflect genuine quality, NOT familiarity inflation
      - Every score must be justified with specific evidence

    D. GENERATE FINAL improvement recommendation
      - The strongest, most informed improvement suggestions
      - These go to synthesis (Layer 2.5)

CRITICAL RULES:
  - This is the FINAL round -- maximum quality expected
  - ALL 7 Round 3 evaluations are retained IN FULL (not compressed)
  - These evaluations go directly to synthesis and human selection
  - Scoring inflation is a specific risk in Round 3 -- scores MUST reflect
    genuine quality improvements, not mere familiarity
  - Effort level: max (non-negotiable for final round)
```
