# 2.13: Round 3 Targeted Revision (FINAL)

## Purpose
Final revision opportunity. Precision fixes only. Each persona addresses their Round 3 critique with the most refined, targeted improvement possible. These are the FINAL evaluations that go to scoring, synthesis, and human selection.

## Inputs
- 2.11-round3-persona-evaluation output (FINAL evaluations)
- 2.12-round3-adversarial-critique output (FINAL critiques)

## Process
```
STEP 1: Load Round 3 evaluations and critiques
  - READ: layer-2-outputs/2.11-round3-persona-evaluation.md
  - READ: layer-2-outputs/2.12-round3-adversarial-critique.md

STEP 2: FOR EACH concept:
  FOR EACH persona (7 total):

    A. LOAD FINAL evaluation and FINAL critique
    B. IDENTIFY the precision fix needed

    C. REVISE with maximum precision
      - This is the LAST revision opportunity
      - Fix must be surgical -- address exactly the identified weakness
      - Persona perspective MUST be maintained
      - Quality of revision matters most here -- this goes to scoring and synthesis

    D. RE-SCORE affected criterion
      - Update critiqued criterion score
      - Keep other scores unchanged
      - Recalculate FINAL weighted total

    E. DOCUMENT revision with full context:
      - What was changed and why
      - Score impact
      - Whether weakness was structural (acknowledged) or refinable (fixed)

CRITICAL RULES:
  - PRECISION FIXES ONLY -- no broad rewrites
  - These are the FINAL evaluations
  - If weakness is structural (concept limitation), acknowledge it
    rather than pretending to fix it
  - Effort level: max
```
