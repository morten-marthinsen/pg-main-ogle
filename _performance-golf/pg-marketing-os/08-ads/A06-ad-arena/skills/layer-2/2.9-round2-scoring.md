# 2.9: Round 2 Scoring

## Purpose
Same scoring protocol as Round 1. Score all revised Round 2 evaluations. Track improvement from Round 1 to Round 2 per persona per concept. Flag any score DECREASES for investigation (potential scoring inflation or regression).

## Inputs
- 2.8-round2-targeted-revision output (revised Round 2 evaluations)
- 2.4-round1-scoring output (Round 1 scores for comparison)

## Process
```
STEP 1: Load Round 2 revised evaluations and Round 1 scores
  - READ: layer-2-outputs/2.8-round2-targeted-revision.md
  - READ: layer-2-outputs/2.4-round1-scoring.md

STEP 2: FOR EACH concept:
  FOR EACH persona (7 total):
    A. COMPILE final Round 2 scores (post-revision)
    B. CALCULATE weighted total
    C. CHECK deal-breaker thresholds

STEP 3: RANK personas per concept (Round 2)

STEP 4: TRACK R1 -> R2 improvement
  - Per persona per concept: R1 final -> R2 final -> delta
  - Flag any score DECREASES (investigate -- genuine regression or scoring error?)
  - Flag any large increases (investigate -- genuine improvement or inflation?)

STEP 5: CROSS-CONCEPT comparison (Round 2)
  - Updated rankings with R2 scores

STEP 6: SCORING INFLATION CHECK
  - If ALL scores increased from R1 to R2 across ALL personas:
    Is there evidence of genuine improvement? Or familiarity-driven inflation?
  - Document findings
```
