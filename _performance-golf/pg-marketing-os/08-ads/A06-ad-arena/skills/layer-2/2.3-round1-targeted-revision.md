# 2.3: Round 1 Targeted Revision

## Purpose
Each persona receives their critique and revises ONLY the identified weakness. Revision must address the specific fix direction from the Critic. Persona perspective MUST be maintained during revision. Maximum scope: targeted fix, not complete re-evaluation.

## Inputs
- 2.1-round1-persona-evaluation output (original evaluations)
- 2.2-round1-adversarial-critique output (critiques with fix directions)

## Process
```
STEP 1: Load original evaluations and critiques
  - READ: layer-2-outputs/2.1-round1-persona-evaluation.md
  - READ: layer-2-outputs/2.2-round1-adversarial-critique.md

STEP 2: FOR EACH concept:
  FOR EACH persona (7 total):

    A. LOAD the persona's original evaluation for this concept
    B. LOAD the Critic's critique for this evaluation
    C. IDENTIFY the specific weakness and fix direction

    D. REVISE the identified weakness ONLY
      - Targeted fix: address the specific criterion weakness
      - DO NOT rewrite the entire evaluation
      - DO NOT change elements that weren't critiqued
      - MAINTAIN persona perspective throughout revision

    E. RE-SCORE the affected criterion
      - Update ONLY the criterion that was revised
      - Keep all other criterion scores unchanged
      - Recalculate weighted total

    F. DOCUMENT the revision:
      - What was changed
      - Why (addressing the fix direction)
      - Before score vs. after score on affected criterion
      - Updated weighted total

CRITICAL RULES:
  - Revision scope is TARGETED -- fix the specific weakness, not everything
  - Persona perspective MUST be maintained (DR Strategist still sounds like DR Strategist)
  - Only the critiqued criterion score changes
  - If the fix direction seems wrong, note disagreement but STILL attempt the revision
  - Effort level: max (revision is surgical, needs deep reasoning)
```
