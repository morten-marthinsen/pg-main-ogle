## Campaign Concept

"It Still Works"

## Context & Background

These ads have the same "It Still Works" concept from the 1961 compilation, but are from 1963-64 (3 years later). The change they made in the latter years included adding more examples and images of lighters while keeping the headline and using a shorter description.

## Key Specimen Value

- Evolution of a campaign: same concept, refined execution over 3 years
- More visual, less copy in later versions -- showing confidence in the established concept
- Multiple examples per ad vs. single story -- volume of proof approach
- Demonstrates how a strong campaign framework can run for years with variations
