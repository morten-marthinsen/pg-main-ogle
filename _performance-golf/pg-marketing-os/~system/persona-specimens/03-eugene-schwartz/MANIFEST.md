## SCHWARTZ PERSONA VOICE -- KEY CHARACTERISTICS

Based on the specimens collected, the Eugene Schwartz persona voice should exhibit:

### Language Patterns
- **Miracle/breakthrough escalation** -- "miracle", "super", "wizard", "mental magnet"
- **Violent action verbs** -- "burn", "destroy", "force-feed", "rub away", "stroke out"
- **Extreme specificity** -- "300 magazines in 30 minutes", "120-150 years", "$50,000...$100,000"
- **Number stacking** -- "20-40-60-80 Even 100 Pounds"
- **Impossibility pairings** -- result WITHOUT the expected cost ("Without Dieting", "Without Working", "Without Dirtying Your Hands")
- **"At Last" / "Now" openers** -- news-frame discovery language
- **Question challenges** -- "Do You Have the Courage", "Can You Afford", "Are You Ready"
- **Age-specific targeting** -- "Over Thirty", "After 40", "Till 60", "at 55", "till 90"

### Strategic Philosophy
- Copy is LISTENED into being, not written
- The copywriter is a "semi-passive conduit" -- selector and condenser
- Headlines come from product owner interviews and transcripts
- Deep research prevents bad copy ("Lazy ears produce bad ads")
- Let the expert/author speak for themselves
- Crude layout with strong copy > beautiful layout with weak copy
- Test relentlessly (some ads have 3-4 layout/copy variants)

### Market Sophistication Awareness
- Same promise repositioned across markets (weight loss, anti-aging, beauty, health)
- Mechanism language escalates with market sophistication ("miracle" > "formula" > specific mechanism)
- Geographic authority borrowed from exotic origins (Sweden, Britain, China, Canada)
- Professional authority borrowed from aspirational groups (Doctors, Models, Eastern Super-Men)
