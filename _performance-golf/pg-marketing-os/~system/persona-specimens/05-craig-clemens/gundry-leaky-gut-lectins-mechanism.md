## The Mechanism

### The Big Idea
**"Many common health problems - fatigue, brain fog, digestive issues, joint pain, weight gain - are caused by 'leaky gut,' a condition where your intestinal lining is damaged by 'LECTINS' in modern foods."**

### UMP (Unique Mechanism Problem)
**Root Cause:** Lectins - proteins found in many "healthy" foods like tomatoes, whole grains, and beans - are punching microscopic holes in your gut lining, allowing toxins to flood into your bloodstream.

**The Three-Part Villain Structure:**

| Component | The Lectin Villain |
|-----------|-------------------|
| **WHAT** | Lectins - plant proteins designed to stop animals from eating them |
| **HOW** | They attach to gut lining, break tight junctions, create holes |
| **WHY** | Modern foods contain far more lectins than ancestral diets |

### UMS (Unique Mechanism Solution)
**The Fix:** A "Three-Prong Defense" that:
1. Seals the holes in your gut lining
2. Neutralizes lectins already in your system
3. Restores the damaged microbiome

### UMD (Unique Mechanism Delivery)
**The Vehicle:** Total Restore - a supplement containing specific ingredients that address all three prongs of defense.

## Lessons for Mechanism Creation

### 1. Name the Enemy
"Lectins" is more powerful than "harmful plant proteins" because it's:
- Specific
- Nameable
- Searchable
- Shareable

### 2. Visual Metaphors Are Non-Negotiable
The "boat with holes" and "tennis court" metaphors make complex biology understandable and memorable.

### 3. Connect Symptoms to Single Cause
Multiple symptoms (fatigue, brain fog, weight gain, joint pain) -> ONE cause (leaky gut) -> ONE solution (Total Restore)

### 4. Explain Past Failure
Every prospect has tried something. The mechanism MUST explain why those attempts failed.

### 5. Create a Worldview, Not Just a Product
The Gundry mechanism creates a new identity: "I'm lectin-free."

### 6. Make It Disgusting
"Microscopic poop flooding through holes" creates visceral emotional response that drives action.
