## Voice Characteristics (Observable in Specimens)

- **Scientific clarity:** Complex biology explained in conversational language a 12-year-old can follow
- **Binary reframes:** "healthy" foods are actually the villain, "whole grains" are the enemy
- **Named enemies:** Lectins, leaky gut — always specific, never vague
- **Visual metaphors:** "Microscopic poop," "boat with holes," "tennis court" gut surface
- **Authority stacking:** Credentials front-loaded, then woven throughout
- **Three-prong structures:** Problems and solutions presented in threes
- **Stat cascading:** User study percentages listed in rapid succession for social proof avalanche
- **Future pacing:** Both positive ("imagine the feeling") and negative ("these problems don't go away")
