## SPECIMEN A — Emotional Beat Separation (H-01, Gold)

Study the STRUCTURE of this human-edited passage. Notice how the emotional high and emotional low live in separate paragraphs. Notice the vocal stress marker (THE). Notice the conversational concession ("And sure,") replacing a formal subordinate clause.

> Because I was the guy watching hundreds of golfers come through elite Performance Golf VIP Academies and finish on cloud nine, because they'd just had THE best two to three days of their golf lives.
>
> And sure, many kept their gains because our process is so deep... a meaningful percentage of our students would fall right back to square one.

**Structural qualities to absorb:** One emotional beat per paragraph. Conversational connectors ("And sure,") over formal conjunctions ("while"). Vocal stress on the word that carries the most weight.

## SPECIMEN B — Visceral Founder Voice (H-05, Gold)

Study the STRUCTURE of this human-edited passage. Notice how "burn almost everything to the ground" matches the magnitude of the decision. Notice the single dismissive sentence replacing a tricolon. Notice how "Because" creates causal flow between paragraphs.

> That's when I hatched what industry insiders considered to be a "diabolical" scheme.
>
> Because I looked at the entire golf instruction landscape — an industry I've been in for years, that I built a 1,400,000-golfer community inside of... and I decided to burn almost everything to the ground.
>
> No minor cosmetic changes or so-called upgrades.

**Structural qualities to absorb:** Verb intensity matches narrative stakes. One-sentence dismissals replace multi-beat negations. Third-party validation ("industry insiders") over self-characterization. "So-called" adds attitude without extra sentences.

## SPECIMEN C — Audience Engagement Over Narration (H-04, Gold)

Study the STRUCTURE of this human-edited passage. Notice how third-person narration becomes a direct question to the audience. Notice the conversational intensifier ("Seriously"). Notice the vocal stress on VANISH at the emotional peak.

> "I took lessons for years, but nothing worked."
>
> Seriously, can you imagine spending years with multiple instructors — and then having your improvement VANISH the moment you walk off the range?

**Structural qualities to absorb:** After testimonial quotes, engage the audience directly — don't narrate about the testimonial subject. Use "can you imagine" to pull the audience INTO the experience. Place vocal stress (CAPS) at the emotional peak of the sentence, not the logical peak.

## USAGE INSTRUCTIONS

These 3 specimens demonstrate the structural qualities that separate human-written VSL copy from AI-generated VSL copy:

1. **Emotional pacing** — Each beat gets its own space (Specimen A)
2. **Visceral register** — Language intensity matches narrative stakes (Specimen B)
3. **Audience engagement** — Pulls the viewer IN rather than narrating AT them (Specimen C)

Hold these passages in active context. Let their STRUCTURE influence your output's structure. Do NOT copy their content, metaphors, or vertical-specific language.
