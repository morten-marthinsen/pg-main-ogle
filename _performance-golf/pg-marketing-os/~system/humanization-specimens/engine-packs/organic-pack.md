## SPECIMEN A — Conversational Register + Bridge Lines (H-09, Gold)

Study the STRUCTURE of this passage. Organic content must sound like a real person talking, not a brand posting. This specimen shows how a single conversational bridge line frames evidence with attitude and colloquial language.

> Because here's what was making me lose my mind:
>
> It's not like I'd cheaped out on anything.
>
> I'd hired coaches who used to work for the MOST elite academies on the planet.

**Structural qualities to absorb:** "Cheaped out" — colloquial, not corporate. The bridge line frames the evidence from the audience's perspective before the evidence drops. CAPS on MOST for spoken emphasis. Three separate paragraphs for three separate beats.

## SPECIMEN B — Emotional Climax as Character Moment (H-06, Gold)

Study the STRUCTURE of this passage. Organic scripts and captions need moments that STOP the scroll. This specimen shows how to treat a turning point as a character-defining moment, not a transition.

> So I finally said: "ENOUGH!"
>
> I'm going to build the experience that should have existed all along.

**Structural qualities to absorb:** "ENOUGH!" hangs alone — quoted, capitalized, exclamation-marked. The declaration and the resolve are separate paragraphs. Ultra-short rhythm for maximum scroll-stopping impact. "So" creates causal flow from frustration to decision.

---

## SPECIMEN C — Direct Audience Question (H-04, Gold)

Study the STRUCTURE of this passage. Organic content that talks AT the audience gets scrolled past. This specimen shows how to pull the audience INTO the experience with a direct question after a testimonial.

> "I took lessons for years, but nothing worked."
>
> Seriously, can you imagine spending years with multiple instructors — and then having your improvement VANISH the moment you walk off the range?

**Structural qualities to absorb:** "Can you imagine" converts passive reading into active identification. "Seriously" signals genuine empathy — not a brand performing empathy. VANISH at the emotional peak. The em-dash creates a dramatic pause before the painful outcome.

These 3 specimens demonstrate the structural qualities that matter most for organic content:

1. **Authentic register** — Colloquial language, bridge lines, spoken rhythm (Specimen A)
2. **Scroll-stopping moments** — Character moments that demand attention (Specimen B)
3. **Audience immersion** — Direct questions that create identification (Specimen C)

Hold these passages in active context. Let their STRUCTURE influence your output's structure. Do NOT copy their content, metaphors, or vertical-specific language.
