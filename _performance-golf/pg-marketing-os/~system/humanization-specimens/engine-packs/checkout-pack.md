## SPECIMEN A — Specific Referents Over Vague Reassurance (H-02, Gold)

Study the STRUCTURE of this passage. Checkout trust copy degrades into vague reassurance ("you're in good hands," "your purchase is secure"). This specimen shows how one specific, confident statement replaces generic scaffolding.

> And the results were PERMANENT.
>
> That's right — when we checked back months later — Harry's improvement had held.

**Structural qualities to absorb:** One specific word (PERMANENT) with vocal stress. Immediate proof follows the claim ("when we checked back months later"). "That's right" as conversational confirmation. No three-beat structures — one statement, one proof.

---

## SPECIMEN B — Credibility Callback (H-10, Gold)

Study the STRUCTURE of this passage. Checkout pages are the LAST chance to reference credibility established on the sales page. This specimen shows how a brief callback loops credibility back at the decision point.

> Which I hope will be a long time, because your body will be tuned up by Dr. Troy just like he does for Scottie — and because you're enjoying the game so much more.

**Structural qualities to absorb:** Callback is ONE PHRASE — the name alone triggers the full credential. Two parallel benefits linked by "and because." Physical benefit + emotional benefit side by side. Trust the reader to remember without re-explaining.

---

## SPECIMEN C — Reframe for Confidence (H-08, Candidate)

Study the STRUCTURE of this passage. Checkout microcopy needs to reinforce value in minimal words. This specimen shows how reframing a number into concrete terms makes it feel more real at the point of purchase.

> He said 70% of golfers don't improve with traditional instruction.
>
> Yes, that's nearly 3 out of every 4 golfers seeing no real change to their game.

**Structural qualities to absorb:** Abstract statistic → concrete, visualizable terms. "Yes," opens with conversational warmth. "Seeing no real change to their game" adds the emotional consequence — it's not just a number, it's a RESULT the reader wants to avoid.

## USAGE INSTRUCTIONS

These 3 specimens demonstrate the structural qualities that matter most for checkout copy:

1. **Specific confidence** — One strong statement over vague reassurance (Specimen A)
2. **Credibility loops** — Brief callbacks at the decision point (Specimen B)
3. **Concrete reframing** — Value in terms the buyer can feel (Specimen C)

Hold these passages in active context. Let their STRUCTURE influence your output's structure. Do NOT copy their content, metaphors, or vertical-specific language.
