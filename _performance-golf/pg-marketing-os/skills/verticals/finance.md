## Finance Market Context

### Audience Profile
- Adults 30-70, male-skewed (65%+)
- Range from retail investors to active traders
- Crypto audience skews younger (25-45)
- Extremely skeptical — burned by bad advice, scams, market losses
- Sophisticated consumers of financial content (newsletters, podcasts, YouTube)
- High intent when engaged — financial products have strong AOV

### Market Sophistication
- **Schwartz Stage 5** — most sophisticated consumer market in DR
- Every claim has been made and burned
- Mechanism-stage minimum: must explain WHY your approach works differently
- Identification-stage for premium: "for the investor who..."
- Crypto sub-niche: Stage 3-4 (still some education-stage opportunity)

### Proof Architecture (Finance-Specific)
- Verified track record (audited returns, time-stamped calls)
- Historical backtesting data
- Expert credentials (CFA, former Wall Street, fund management)
- Market prediction accuracy (documented, time-stamped)
- Student/subscriber results (specific gains, dates, tickers)
- Compliance disclaimers (past performance, risk acknowledgment)
