## PROOF DRAMATIZATION PATTERNS

### Testimonial Dramatization (From Whitaker)

**Pattern:** Maximum Contrast + Specific Outcomes

```
BEFORE STATE: [Most extreme suffering detail]
  → "Raging neuropathy, constant pain, blind, can't walk"

TRANSFORMATION: [Dramatic reversal verb]
  → "reversed", "transformed", "proved impossible was possible"

AFTER STATE: [Specific functional outcomes]
  → "diabetes-free, walking, working out, vision restored, driving"

TIMEFRAME: [Specific, surprising speed]
  → "in 72 hours"
```

### Demonstration Dramatization (From Sinatra)

**Pattern:** Quantified Superiority + Comparison

```
SPECIFIC NUMBER: [Exact percentage or quantity]
  → "62% blood flow boost"

TIMEFRAME: [Measurable period]
  → "in 60 minutes"

COMPARISON: [vs. alternatives]
  → "250% MORE potassium than other electrolyte drinks"
```

### Data Dramatization (From Stansberry)

**Pattern:** Rhetorical Framework + Data Cascade

```
RHETORICAL QUESTION: [Surface concern]
  → "How can it be normal when..."

DATA POINT 1: [Shocking statistic]
  → "75% of Americans living paycheck to paycheck"

DATA POINT 2: [Compounding statistic]
  → "49% on government benefits"

DATA POINT 3: [Final proof]
  → "lowest labor participation since 1970s"
```

### Study Citation Dramatization (From Sinatra)

**Pattern:** Institution → Finding → Emotional Frame

```
INSTITUTION: [Named authority]
  → "Board-certified cardiologist F.A.C.C."

FINDING: [Specific discovery]
  → "Statins deplete CoQ10 which your heart muscle desperately needs"

EMOTIONAL FRAME: [Why it matters]
  → "that's why statin users often feel exhausted and have muscle pain"
```

## PROOF TRANSITION PATTERNS

### INTO Proof Sections

| Technique | Verbatim Example | When to Use |
|-----------|------------------|-------------|
| Skepticism Validation | "Now I realize you may be thinking... Well, fasten your seat belt..." | After bold claim |
| Don't Take My Word | "But don't take my word for it..." | Before testimonials |
| Evidence Introduction | "Skeptical? Good. Let me show you the evidence..." | Before data/studies |
| Scale Setup | "[Name] isn't alone. Not by a long shot..." | Before social proof |

### OUT OF Proof Sections

| Technique | Verbatim Example | When to Use |
|-----------|------------------|-------------|
| Future Pacing | "And you could be next..." | After testimonials |
| Imagine Close | "Imagine yourself [timeframe] from now..." | After transformation proof |
| Question Pivot | "The only question now is: will you take the step?" | Before offer |
| Pattern Conclusion | "The patterns are clear..." | After data cascade |
