# Microskill 1.2: Quick-Scan Scoring

**Layer:** 1 (Blind Read Assessment)
**Purpose:** Initial 7-criterion marketplace scoring based on first impressions

---

## 7-CRITERION MARKETPLACE SCORING

Score each criterion 1-10 based on first-read impressions. Include brief evidence.

### Criterion 1: Stopping Power (Weight: 20%)

**Question:** Would the target audience stop what they're doing to engage with this?

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 10 | Can't look away — magnetic |
| 8-9 | Strong hook, would stop most people |
| 6-7 | Decent, would stop some people |
| 4-5 | Weak, might scroll past |
| 1-3 | Would definitely scroll past |

```yaml
stopping_power:
  score: "[1-10]"
  evidence: "[Quote from opening that supports score]"
  notes: "[Brief explanation]"
```

---

### Criterion 2: Believability (Weight: 15%)

**Question:** Do they believe the promise being made?

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 10 | Ironclad proof, impossible to doubt |
| 8-9 | Strong proof, most would believe |
| 6-7 | Some proof, skeptics would doubt |
| 4-5 | Weak proof, many would doubt |
| 1-3 | Claims without evidence, feels scammy |

```yaml
believability:
  score: "[1-10]"
  evidence: "[Strongest proof element or lack thereof]"
  notes: "[Brief explanation]"
```

---

|-------|---------|

---

## WEIGHTED TOTAL CALCULATION

```
Total Score = (Stopping × 0.20) + (Believability × 0.15) + (Desire × 0.20) +
              (Objections × 0.15) + (Clarity × 0.10) + (Risk × 0.10) +
              (Strategy × 0.10)
```

### Context Adjustments

Apply if relevant:

- **High-ticket (>$2K):** Believability +5%, Objections +5%, Risk +5%, Stopping -5%, Desire -5%
- **Low-ticket (<$200):** Stopping +5%, Desire +5%, Clarity +5%, Objections -5%, Risk -5%
- **Sophisticated market (Stage 4-5):** Strategy +10%, Believability +5%, Desire -5%

---

## INITIAL GRADE MAPPING

| Weighted Score | Initial Grade |
|----------------|---------------|
| 9.0-10.0 | A to A+ |
| 8.0-8.9 | B+ to A- |
| 7.0-7.9 | B to B+ |
| 6.0-6.9 | C+ to B- |
| 5.0-5.9 | C to C+ |
| <5.0 | D or F |
