# Microskill 2.6: Kennedy Lens

**Layer:** 2 (Multi-Expert Critique)
**Purpose:** Evaluate copy through Dan Kennedy's direct response fundamentals and offer architecture

---

## KENNEDY EXPERTISE PROFILE

**Master of:** Direct response fundamentals, offer architecture, no-BS selling
**Signature Approach:** Clear, direct, urgency-driven, mathematically sound offers
**Core Philosophy:** "There is no relationship between being a good writer and a successful direct-response copywriter"

---

## KENNEDY 10-POINT CRITIQUE CHECKLIST

### 1. Offer Clarity
**Question:** Does the reader know EXACTLY what they're getting?

```yaml
offer_clarity:
  assessment: "[CRYSTAL CLEAR | MOSTLY CLEAR | CONFUSING]"
  offer_elements:
    main_product: "[clearly stated | vague | missing]"
    bonuses: "[clearly listed | vague | missing]"
    price: "[clearly stated | buried | missing]"
    payment_options: "[clear | confusing | missing]"
  confusion_points:
    - element: "[what's unclear]"
      location: "[where]"
      fix: "[how to clarify]"
  kennedy_principle: "If they have to think about what they're buying, they won't buy"
```

### 2. Value Stack Architecture
**Question:** Is the value stacked properly to dwarf the price?

```yaml

  assessment: "[STRONG | ADEQUATE | WEAK]"
  value_stack:

      stated_value: "[dollar amount]"
      credibility: "[believable | stretch | unbelievable]"
  total_value_stated: "[amount]"
  price_asked: "[amount]"
  value_to_price_ratio: "[X:1]"
  stack_issues:

      fix: "[how to strengthen]"
  kennedy_principle: "Stack value until the price looks like a rounding error"
```

### 3. Risk Reversal
**Question:** Is all perceived risk removed from the buyer?

```yaml

  guarantee_type: "[money-back | results-based | keep-everything | none]"

```

## EVIDENCE REQUIREMENTS

Every critique point MUST include:
- **Quote:** Exact text from the copy
- **Location:** Section or paragraph reference
- **Problem:** Specific diagnosis
- **Fix:** Concrete recommendation

**NO VAGUE CRITIQUES.** "Offer needs work" is unacceptable. "The offer section lists 5 bonuses but doesn't assign dollar values to 3 of them—add specific values (Bonus 3: Regular Price $197, Bonus 4: Regular Price $97, Bonus 5: Regular Price $147) to strengthen the value stack" is acceptable.
