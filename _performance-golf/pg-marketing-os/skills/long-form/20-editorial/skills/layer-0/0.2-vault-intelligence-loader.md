## BEFORE/AFTER EDITING EXAMPLES

Index specific examples of edits that elevated copy:

```yaml
editing_examples:
  specificity_upgrade:
    before: "Many people have experienced results"
    after: "47,832 people have already used this system to lose an average of 23 pounds in 60 days"
    principle: "Vague → Quantified + Timeframe"

  confidence_upgrade:
    before: "This may help improve your energy levels"
    after: "This ignites your cellular energy within 72 hours"
    principle: "Hedged → Direct with timeline"

  imagery_upgrade:
    before: "The product reduces inflammation"
    after: "Your joints feel like rusty hinges being oiled for the first time in years"
    principle: "Abstract → Visceral sensory"

  rhythm_upgrade:
    before: "The mechanism works by activating the cellular pathways that promote metabolism, which in turn increases energy production and fat burning capacity throughout your body."
    after: "The mechanism flips one switch. Your metabolism ignites. Fat burns. Energy soars."
    principle: "Run-on → Staccato punches"

  proof_upgrade:
    before: "Studies show this works"
    after: "A 2019 UCLA study of 847 participants found 94% experienced measurable improvement within 21 days"
    principle: "Vague reference → Specific citation"
```
