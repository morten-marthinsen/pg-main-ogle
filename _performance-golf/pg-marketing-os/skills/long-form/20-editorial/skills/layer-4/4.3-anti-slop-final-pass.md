# Microskill 4.3: Anti-Slop Final Pass

**Layer:** 4 (Fix Application)
**Purpose:** Detect and eliminate AI-generated patterns, filler, and generic language

---

## ANTI-SLOP DETECTION PROTOCOL

"Slop" = AI-generated patterns that signal inauthenticity, reduce credibility, and damage conversion.

---
