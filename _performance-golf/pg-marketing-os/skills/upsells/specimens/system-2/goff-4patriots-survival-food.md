- **Price Presentation:** Anchored against $804 normal price, offered at $497 ($300 off MSRP), reframed as $0.93/serving for 536 servings. 7 free bonuses stacked on top ($81 in free kits + $157 in bonus items)
- **Guarantee:** Double guarantee — (1) 365-day 100% money back, keep bonuses; (2) 300% money back guarantee if food spoils within 25 years
- **CTA Style:** "Click the orange Click to Accept button" — repeated multiple times with countdown timer urgency
- **Notable Technique:** The "warehouse overstock" frame (same as Patriot Power Greens US-23 — both are 4Patriots brands using the identical structural template). Two-presenter format: Maggie creates warehouse urgency with countdown timer, Frank delivers the emotional fear-based copy about crisis scenarios, then pivots to product details and bonuses. The emotional fear section ("imagine you're 72 hours into a crisis") is unique to this vertical — it manufactures urgency through scenario visualization rather than product scarcity alone. The 300% money-back guarantee on spoilage is an extraordinary trust device.
