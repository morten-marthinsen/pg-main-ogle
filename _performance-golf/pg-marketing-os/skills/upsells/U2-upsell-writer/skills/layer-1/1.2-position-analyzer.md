# 1.2 — Position Analyzer

**Version:** 1.0
**Skill:** U2 — 1-Click Upsell Writer
**Layer:** 1
**Microskill:** 1.2 — Position Analyzer
**Purpose:** Analyze the upsell's position in the post-purchase sequence to determine buyer psychological state, CAIRO section proportions, and opening pattern selection.

---

## INPUT

- `layer-0/0.1-input-context.md` (position, FE price, upsell price, product type)
- `layer-0/0.2-specimen-calibration.md` (specimen-derived proportion data)
- `layer-1/1.1-congruence-map.md` (congruence bridge — informs opening framing)

## PROCESS

1. **Identify position in sequence:**
   - Is this Upsell 1 (first offer after checkout) or Upsell 2 (second offer)?
   - What came BEFORE this page? Options: (a) checkout page only, (b) order bump was offered, (c) a previous upsell page
   - What comes AFTER this page? Options: (a) downsell (if they decline), (b) another upsell, (c) thank-you page (end of sequence)
   - Source: U0 handoff spec (Mode A) or standalone brief (Mode B)

2. **Map buyer psychological state** based on position:

   **If Upsell 1 (immediately after checkout):**
   - **Commitment consistency:** HIGH — they just made a buying decision. The "yes" momentum is at peak. They want to be consistent with their identity as someone who solves this problem.
   - **Endowment effect:** ACTIVE — they mentally own the FE product already. They are protective of their investment and receptive to anything that makes it work better.
   - **Trust level:** ELEVATED — they trusted you with their credit card. Trust is at its session maximum. Heavy proof is unnecessary and counterproductive (signals you don't trust the trust they gave).
   - **Momentum:** HIGH — they want to complete the transaction and access their purchase. Every word must advance the decision or create friction.
   - **Time pressure:** MODERATE — they want to finish checkout but are not yet impatient. They'll read 750-1200 words if each word earns its place.
   - **Pattern recognition:** LOW — this is the first post-purchase offer they've seen in this session. No upsell fatigue yet.

   **If Upsell 2 (after a previous upsell):**
   - **Commitment consistency:** MODERATE — still present but slightly diluted. They may have already said yes to Upsell 1 (deepening commitment) or no (creating a "no" pattern).
   - **Endowment effect:** COMPOUND — they now own FE + possibly Upsell 1. More to protect, but also more decision fatigue.
   - **Trust level:** STABLE or SLIGHTLY DECREASED — depends on whether Upsell 1 felt congruent or pushy.
   - **Momentum:** DECREASED — they've been in the checkout flow longer. Patience is lower.
   - **Time pressure:** HIGH — they want to access their purchase NOW. Every word better count.
   - **Pattern recognition:** HIGH — they've seen one upsell page already. Same opening pattern will feel formulaic. Pattern disruption is valuable here.

3. **Determine CAIRO section proportions** based on position:

   Reference the AGENT.md proportion table:
   - Upsell 1 (standard): C 50-100w / A 150-300w / I 75-150w / R 150-400w / O 150-300w = 575-1250w
   - Upsell 1 (long): C 75-100w / A 200-400w / I 100-200w / R 250-500w / O 200-400w = 825-1600w
   - Upsell 2: C 50-75w / A 100-200w / I 50-100w / R 100-250w / O 100-200w = 400-825w

   Select the appropriate row. If format classifier set a specific word count target, use the proportions that sum to that target range.

4. **Select opening pattern:**
   - **Standard congratulate (recommended for Upsell 1):** "Congratulations — you just made one of the smartest decisions..." Validates purchase, names product/mechanism, creates emotional high, opens loop. 55% of specimens at Upsell 1 position use this pattern.
   - **Warning/confession opener (option for Upsell 2+):** "I need to be honest with you about something..." / "Before you go, there's one thing I should have mentioned earlier..." Pattern disruption. 55% of specimens at Upsell 2 position use this variant. Effective because it breaks the expected congratulate pattern.
   - **Insider reveal (option for either position):** "You've just accessed a page that less than 1% of our customers ever see..." Creates exclusivity. Less common in specimens but effective for high-ticket upsells (>$97).

5. **Determine what the buyer has already been told:**
   - If an order bump was offered → the buyer has already seen one additional offer. The upsell should NOT repeat the bump's angle.
   - If a previous upsell was presented → what product was it? What angle? Ensure THIS upsell's angle is distinct.
   - Source: U0 handoff spec should contain the full sequence map.

6. **Set psychological levers for each CAIRO section:**
   - C section lever: commitment consistency ("you made the right choice")
   - A section lever: endowment effect ("here's what your purchase will do for you")
   - I section lever: exclusivity + curiosity ("there's one more thing the top performers have")
   - R section lever: Cialdini "because" ("we created this because...")
   - O section lever: framing + simplicity ("just add this to your order")

7. **Write output file.**

## CONSTRAINTS

1. Position analysis must be based on ACTUAL sequence data from U0 handoff or brief. Do not assume Upsell 1 without confirmation.
2. CAIRO proportions must reference the AGENT.md proportion table. Do not invent new proportion ranges.
3. Warning/confession opener is an OPTION for Upsell 2+, not a requirement. If specimen calibration or campaign context suggests standard congratulate is better, use that.
4. Buyer psychological state descriptions are frameworks for generation, not scientific claims. They guide tone and emphasis, not medical/psychological assertions in copy.
5. Upsell 2 proportions must be visibly shorter than Upsell 1. If the total word range is the same for both positions, the position analysis has failed.
6. If U0 handoff does not specify what came before/after this page, flag the gap and default to: order bump before, downsell after. Note the assumption explicitly.
