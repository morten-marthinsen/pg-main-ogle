# 0.2.7 — Persona Voice Loader

**Version:** 1.0
**Skill:** U2 — 1-Click Upsell Writer
**Layer:** 0
**Microskill:** 0.2.7 — Persona Voice Loader
**Purpose:** Load persona voice specimens for the 6 Arena competitors to enable voice-differentiated full-draft generation in the generative_full_draft Arena mode.

---

## INPUT

- `skills/protocols/PERSONA-VOICE-LOADING-PROTOCOL.md` (shared protocol — MUST be read first)
- `skills/persona-specimens/` directory (101 specimen files across 6 personas)
- `layer-0/0.1-input-context.md` (campaign vertical, format type, product category)
- `layer-0/0.2-specimen-calibration.md` (tone and register targets from upsell specimens)

## PROCESS

1. **Read `PERSONA-VOICE-LOADING-PROTOCOL.md`** — this defines the full loading protocol, selection matrices, and output requirements. Do not deviate from it.
2. **Identify Arena mode:** U2 uses `generative_full_draft` mode. Per the protocol, this means loading 2-3 selling voice specimens per persona (NOT 1 analytical specimen — that is for `strategic` mode).
3. **Load specimens for each of the 6 personas** (The Architect gets no voice specimens — it uses pure structural analysis):

   **Clayton Makepeace (The Momentum Player):**
   - Prioritize: Speed Profits, Shameless Sobs — both show high-energy selling flow with fast transitions
   - U2-specific relevance: Makepeace's pacing patterns are ideal for the Congratulate-to-Amplify transition. His "celebrate then escalate" rhythm maps to CAIRO's first two sections.
   - Load 2-3 specimens showing: momentum building, transition energy, celebration-to-pitch pivots

   **Gary Halbert (The Street Fighter):**
   - Prioritize: Boron Letters showing conversational selling, newsletter pieces with direct address
   - U2-specific relevance: Halbert's conversational energy is ideal for the Intrigue and Reason sections. His "lean-in and tell you a secret" voice is the Intrigue section made flesh.
   - Load 2-3 specimens showing: conversational directness, story-based selling, insider revelation tone
   - Note: Halbert corpus is instructional (Boron Letters), not ad copy. Extract VOICE patterns, not structural patterns.

   **Eugene Schwartz (The Surgeon):**
   - Prioritize: Ads with body copy showing precision language, dimensionalized benefits
   - U2-specific relevance: Schwartz's dimensionalization technique is ideal for the Amplify section. Five-senses future pacing in 100-300 words is his specialty.
   - Load 2-3 specimens showing: benefit dimensionalization, sensory language, compression of big ideas into tight space

   **David Ogilvy (The Statesman):**
   - Prioritize: Ads with OCR body copy showing authority-based selling, brand voice
   - U2-specific relevance: Ogilvy's authority and restraint calibrate the overall tone away from hype. His measured conviction suits the Reason section particularly well.
   - Load 2-3 specimens showing: authority without aggression, logical persuasion, understated confidence

   **Craig Clemens (The Producer):**
   - Prioritize: Gundry VSL transcripts showing video script pacing, visual cues, spoken rhythm
   - U2-specific relevance: Clemens' VSL pacing is directly applicable to video script format output. His mechanism-extension patterns (showing how supplement A works better with supplement B) map perfectly to upsell congruence logic.
   - Load 2-3 specimens showing: VSL transitions, spoken-word pacing, mechanism callbacks, visual storytelling

   **Gary Bencivenga (The Architect of Proof):**
   - Prioritize: Promo sales letters and Marketing Maxims showing proof architecture, logic chains
   - U2-specific relevance: Bencivenga's proof discipline ensures the 1-2 proof elements in the upsell are placed with maximum impact. His "less proof, better placed" instinct maps to U2's constraint of max 2 proof elements.
   - Load 2-3 specimens showing: proof placement, logical argumentation, economy of evidence

4. **Match specimens to campaign context:**
   - If vertical is golf → weight Makepeace (high energy, competitive context) and Halbert (direct, conversational)
   - If vertical is health/supplement → weight Clemens (VSL, mechanism extension) and Schwartz (dimensionalized benefits)
   - If vertical is finance → weight Bencivenga (proof architecture) and Ogilvy (authority)
   - If vertical is personal-dev → weight Halbert (conversational) and Makepeace (momentum)
   - If vertical is technology → weight Schwartz (precision) and Ogilvy (credibility)
5. **Match specimens to format type:**
   - If video script → weight Clemens specimens highest (VSL specialist), then Halbert (spoken rhythm)
   - If text page → weight Schwartz (print precision) and Ogilvy (layout-aware copy)
6. **Write output file** with per-persona specimen selections, file paths, and loading rationale.

## CONSTRAINTS

1. MUST read PERSONA-VOICE-LOADING-PROTOCOL.md before executing. Do not improvise the loading process.
2. The Architect gets NO voice specimens. It uses structural analysis only. Do not load specimens for The Architect.
3. Load 2-3 specimens per persona, not 1 and not 4+. The `generative_full_draft` mode requires sufficient voice signal without context overload.
4. Halbert specimens are instructional (Boron Letters), not ad copy. Extract VOICE patterns (tone, rhythm, directness) — do not attempt to extract structural sales patterns from instructional content.
5. Specimen file paths must be absolute and verified to exist. Do not reference specimens that are not in the vault.
6. Vertical and format matching are RECOMMENDATIONS, not hard filters. Every persona gets 2-3 specimens regardless — the matching determines WHICH specimens, not WHETHER to load.
7. Voice specimens inform Arena generation but do not override CAIRO structure. Persona voice applies to HOW each section is written, not WHAT sections exist.
