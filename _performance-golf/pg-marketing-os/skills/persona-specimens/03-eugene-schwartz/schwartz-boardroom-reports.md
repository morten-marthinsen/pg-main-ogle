## Context

A very successful 4-page sales letter for Boardroom Publications, written by Eugene Schwartz in 1988. Since 1972, Boardroom sold hundreds of millions of newsletters and books, all thanks to hiring the top .00001% of direct-response copywriters, Schwartz being one of them. The essence of what he's selling is high-level, time-sensitive information that only top executives and the elite few know about. The deliverable is a subscription to Boardroom Reports + a free personal business guide. The first issue is free. If you like it and become a subscriber, you get 39% off discount, paying $29.95 for 23 more issues.

## Schwartz Techniques Visible

- **Promise headline:** Massive specificity (300 magazines, 30 minutes)
- **Implied mechanism:** Condensation/curation of elite knowledge
- **Exclusivity positioning:** Information "only top executives & the elite few know about"
- **Risk reversal:** First issue free
- **Price anchoring:** 39% off discount framing
