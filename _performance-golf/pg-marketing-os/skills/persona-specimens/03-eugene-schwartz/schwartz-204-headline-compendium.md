## HEADLINE PATTERN ANALYSIS

### Schwartz's Most Common Headline Structures:

1. **"How To [Result]"** -- 40+ headlines (most common pattern)
2. **"At Last [Breakthrough]"** -- 7 headlines
3. **"Now [New Thing Available]"** -- 12 headlines
4. **Question Headlines** -- 15+ ("Do You...", "Can You...", "Is It...", "Are You...")
5. **"[Number] [Specific Things] in [Time Frame]"** -- Extreme specificity pattern
6. **"World's First/Easiest/Most Powerful"** -- Superlative + category creation
7. **"Why [Famous/Admired Group] [Desirable Result]"** -- Aspirational proof pattern
8. **Result + Time Frame** -- "In 4 Short Weeks", "In One Evening", "In 30 Minutes"
9. **Impossibility + Ease** -- "Without Dieting", "Without Working", "Without Dirtying Your Hands"
10. **Age Reversal Framing** -- "Feel 20 Years Younger", "Stay Young Till 90", "Begin Your Middle Years at 70"

### Schwartz's Signature Vocabulary:
- "Miracle" (7+ uses)
- "Secret/Secrets" (6+ uses)
- "Wizard" (4 uses)
- "Mental Magnet" (3 uses)
- "Push-Button" (2 uses)
- "Burn/Burns" (3 uses -- visceral action verbs)
- "Destroy/Destroys" (violent mechanism language)
- "Force-Feed" (aggressive transfer language)
- "Super" (intensifier -- Super Plants, Super-Men, Super Hypnotism)
