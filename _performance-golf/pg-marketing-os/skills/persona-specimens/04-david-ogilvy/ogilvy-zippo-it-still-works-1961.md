## Campaign Concept

"It Still Works"

## Context & Background

This was an incredible campaign created by Ogilvy & Mather that does a fantastic job of combining the power of storytelling and a great guarantee. They use real-life examples of lighters that have lasted through decades and harsh conditions including wars, notable figures and significant events. They're essentially case studies of how Zippo lighters are made with tremendous quality, except they are spun into interesting stories that can't help but impress and fascinate readers.

## Key Specimen Value

- Case study format: real stories as proof
- Each ad is a complete narrative arc: setup, ordeal, survival, still works
- Guarantee made tangible through dramatic real-world demonstrations
- Campaign consistency: same headline framework, endless story variations
- War stories + famous people + extreme conditions = maximum credibility
