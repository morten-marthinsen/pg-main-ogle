# Skill 2.4: Lead Generator

**Version:** 1.0
**Layer:** 2 (Generation)
**Type:** Creative Generation
**Dependencies:** candidate_architectures.json (from 2.2), layer0-output.json (from 0.6)
**Output:** candidate_leads.json

---

## Purpose

Generate 3+ fully-written lead variations for each of the 5 candidates. Every lead is ACTUAL COPY — not descriptions of what to write, not outlines, not summaries. Each lead includes a 2-3 sentence opening hook, a 1-2 paragraph Root Cause introduction, and a mechanism tease paragraph. Uses 5 proven lead techniques matched to candidate architecture.

---

## The Question

**"Are all 15+ leads fully-written paragraphs of actual copy that a copywriter could drop directly into a VSL or sales page?"**

## Lead Technique Library

### 5 Proven Lead Techniques

```
1. STORY Lead
   Structure: Personal narrative → discovery moment → Root Cause revelation
   Narrative types: TRANSFORMATION, RELUCTANT_HERO, INVESTIGATOR, ACCIDENTAL_DISCOVERY
   Best for: Hope-driven candidates, mechanism-forward architectures
   Opening hook style: "It started when..." / "Nobody believed me when..."

2. SHOCKING_FACT Lead
   Structure: Surprising statistic → "here's why" → Root Cause explanation
   Narrative types: REVELATION_INTERVIEW, RESEARCH_EXPOSE, DATA_STORY
   Best for: Scientific mechanisms, authority-driven markets
   Opening hook style: "A recent study revealed..." / "73% of people over 40..."

3. ENEMY_REVEAL Lead
   Structure: Name the villain → show how they caused harm → introduce escape
   Narrative types: EXPOSE_INVESTIGATION, WHISTLEBLOWER, CONSUMER_ADVOCATE
   Best for: Enemy-based candidates, anger-driven markets
   Opening hook style: "For the past [X] years, [Enemy] has been..."

4. PAIN_AMPLIFICATION Lead
   Structure: Vivid pain description → "you've tried everything" → Root Cause relief
   Narrative types: EMPATHY_MIRROR, SHARED_STRUGGLE, FRUSTRATED_SEARCHER
   Best for: Frustration-driven candidates, failed-solution markets
   Opening hook style: "You've tried everything..." / "If you're sick of..."

5. PROPHECY Lead
   Structure: Future warning → why it will happen → prevention mechanism
   Narrative types: EXPERT_WARNING, TREND_PREDICTION, INEVITABLE_CONSEQUENCE
   Best for: Fear-driven candidates, urgency-based markets
   Opening hook style: "Within the next [timeframe]..." / "What's coming next..."
```

## Process Logic

### Step 1: Match Techniques to Candidate

```
FOR each candidate:

  LOAD from candidate_architectures:
    - emotional_driver_combination (primary + secondary)
    - root_cause_reframe (what_they_think, what_it_really_is, why_nothing_worked)
    - mechanism (name, steps, uniqueness_claim)
    - enemy_identified (name, type, how_causes_problem)
    - promise_statement (core_promise)

  SELECT 3+ lead techniques:
    ALWAYS include the technique matching primary_driver:
      FEAR_PROTECTION → PROPHECY or ENEMY_REVEAL
      FRUSTRATION_ANGER → PAIN_AMPLIFICATION or ENEMY_REVEAL
      HOPE_POSSIBILITY → STORY or SHOCKING_FACT
      CURIOSITY → SHOCKING_FACT or STORY
      REVENGE_VINDICATION → ENEMY_REVEAL
      SHAME_DIGNITY → PAIN_AMPLIFICATION
      LIBERATION_FREEDOM → STORY
      GREED_OPPORTUNITY → SHOCKING_FACT

    ADD 2+ complementary techniques for emotional range
```

### Step 2: Write Opening Hooks (2-3 Sentences Each)

```
FOR each lead variation:

  WRITE opening_hook as 2-3 complete sentences of ACTUAL COPY:

  STORY hook example:
    "For the past 40 years, the Low-Fat Diet Industry has been giving you advice
     that's actually making your belly fat worse. Not because they're evil... but
     because they don't understand bile. And bile is the key to everything."

  SHOCKING_FACT hook example:
    "A recent study published in the Journal of Hepatology revealed something
     shocking: 73% of people over 40 have severely impaired bile production.
     And most of them have no idea."

  PAIN_AMPLIFICATION hook example:
    "You've tried everything. Every diet. Every workout plan. Every supplement
     that promised to 'melt belly fat.' And nothing worked. Or worse: it worked
     temporarily, then the fat came back with a vengeance."

  QUALITY CHECK — opening_hook must:
    - Be 2-3 complete sentences (not 1 sentence, not a paragraph)
    - Create immediate emotional engagement
    - NOT be a description ("Opens with a story about...")
    - NOT start with "Imagine..." (overused AI pattern)
    - Use the candidate's specific enemy, pain, or statistic
```

### Step 3: Write Root Cause Introduction (1-2 Paragraphs)

```
FOR each lead variation:

  WRITE root_cause_intro as 1-2 complete paragraphs:

  PARAGRAPH 1: Introduce the root cause diagnosis
    - Connect the hook to the underlying problem
    - Name the specific root cause (from architecture)
    - Explain why this changes everything
    - Use language that creates an "aha moment"

  PARAGRAPH 2: Explain why nothing else worked
    - Reference the false solution (what_they_think)
    - Show causal chain: false solution → root cause untouched → symptom persists
    - Create validation ("This is why...")
    - Bridge to mechanism introduction

  QUALITY CHECK — root_cause_intro must:
    - Be actual written paragraphs (not outline points)
    - Teach the reader something genuinely new
    - Connect logically to the opening hook
    - NOT use "You see," as paragraph opener (AI tell)
    - Include at least one specific detail (number, name, mechanism)
```

### Step 4: Write Mechanism Tease (1 Paragraph)

```
FOR each lead variation:

  WRITE mechanism_tease as 1 complete paragraph:

  STRUCTURE:
    - "But here's the good news:" or equivalent transition
    - Introduce the mechanism by name
    - Give 1-2 specifics about how it works (without full reveal)
    - State the promise in concrete terms
    - Create desire to learn more (incomplete loop)

  QUALITY CHECK — mechanism_tease must:
    - Name the mechanism explicitly
    - NOT reveal all mechanism steps (save for later)
    - Include at least one specific detail about how it works
    - End with forward momentum (reader wants to know more)
    - NOT be a description ("Teases the mechanism")
    - Be 3-5 sentences of actual copy
```

### Step 5: Source & Validate

```
FOR each lead:
  MATCH to swipe_source:
    Find swipe in vault that uses similar lead technique
    Record swipe_id and quality_score

  VERIFY:
    - opening_hook is 2-3 sentences of actual copy
    - root_cause_intro is 1-2 paragraphs of actual copy
    - mechanism_tease is 1 paragraph of actual copy
    - No section contains description language ("Introduces...", "Tells about...")
    - All sections use candidate-specific content (not generic)
    - Lead maintains consistent emotional tone throughout
```

## Anti-Exemplar: What FAILS This Skill

```
FAIL (Description/Outline):
  opening_hook: "Opens with a story about someone who struggled with weight loss
                 and then found a solution."
  root_cause_intro: "Introduces the root cause concept."
  mechanism_tease: "Teases the mechanism."

PASS (Actual Written Copy):
  opening_hook: "For the past 40 years, the Low-Fat Diet Industry has been giving
                 you advice that's actually making your belly fat worse. Not because
                 they're evil... but because they don't understand bile."
  root_cause_intro: "You see, when you cut fat from your diet (like they told you to),
                     something dangerous happens inside your liver: Your bile production
                     slows to a crawl. And without adequate bile flow, your body literally
                     cannot break down fat. Instead, it stores it. Around your belly.
                     No matter how few calories you eat.\n\nThis explains why nothing's
                     worked. Not the calorie counting. Not the endless cardio..."
  mechanism_tease: "But here's the good news: A small group of doctors has developed
                    a simple 3-step protocol that reactivates your bile flow in as little
                    as 72 hours..."
```

---

## Constraints

- NEVER produce leads as descriptions ("Opens with...", "Introduces...", "Teases...")
- NEVER produce opening hooks shorter than 2 sentences or longer than 4
- NEVER produce root_cause_intro shorter than 1 paragraph
- NEVER produce mechanism_tease that reveals all mechanism steps
- NEVER start any lead with "Imagine..." (overused AI pattern)
- NEVER use "You see," as a paragraph opener (AI tell)
- ALWAYS write actual copy that could go directly into a VSL script
- ALWAYS generate minimum 3 lead variations per candidate
- ALWAYS use candidate-specific content (enemy name, mechanism name, root cause)
- ALL leads must cite swipe_source proving the technique works

---

## Quality Criteria

1. Minimum 3 leads per candidate (15+ total)
2. All opening_hooks are 2-3 sentences of actual copy
3. All root_cause_intros are 1-2 paragraphs of actual copy
4. All mechanism_teases are 1 paragraph of actual copy
5. Zero description language in any section
6. All leads use candidate-specific names/mechanisms/enemies (not generic)
7. All leads cite swipe sources with quality scores
8. Anti-exemplar check: no lead matches the FAIL pattern
9. No lead starts with "Imagine..." or uses "You see," as opener
