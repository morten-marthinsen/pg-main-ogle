### EVALUATION Criteria

**How Customers Evaluate EMF Products:**

1. **Personal Testing:**
   - Using EMF meters to verify claims
   - Before/after symptom tracking
   - Family member blind tests

2. **Social Proof:**
   - Testimonials from people "like me"
   - Reviews from EMF-sensitive individuals
   - Healthcare practitioner endorsements

3. **Scientific Credibility:**
   - Research studies cited
   - HRV data / biomarkers
   - Third-party testing claims

4. **Mechanism Understanding:**
   - "How does it actually work?"
   - Distrust of vague claims ("harmonizes energy")
   - Preference for explainable technology

5. **Risk Reversal:**
   - Money-back guarantees
   - Trial periods
   - Return policies

**Red Flags for Customers:**
- Products that still allow phone signals through
- No scientific explanation of mechanism
- Only positive reviews (seems fake)
- Very low price (too good to be true)
- Very high price without justification

**Quote Evidence:**
> "You still received notifications and phone calls while inside the pouch... so in my opinion I don't believe this product protect you from EMF"
> "Nowhere on the Harmoni website is it explained how the pendant does this"
> "I tested the phone before, and then I put it in the bag and I test it after"
