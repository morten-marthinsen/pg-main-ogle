# Copywriting Engine Skill Sequence

The 20 skills (01-20) execute in five phases, transforming research into finished, editorially-reviewed copy.

---

## Phase 1: Research & Strategy
**Inputs → Strategic Decisions**

These skills gather intelligence and make strategic decisions that constrain everything downstream.

| # | Skill | Purpose | Output |
|---|-------|---------|--------|
| 01 | **research** | Market intelligence, avatar, competition | Market snapshot, avatar profile, competitive gaps |
| 02 | **proof-inventory** | What proof exists, gaps, promise ceiling | Proof inventory, gap analysis, ceiling assessment |
| 03 | **root-cause** | The real reason the problem persists | Root cause reframe, enemy/villain, blame transfer |
| 04 | **mechanism** | The unique solution pathway | Named mechanism, credibility anchors, uniqueness claim |
| 05 | **promise** | What we can credibly claim (constrained by proof) | Primary promise, supporting promises, proof pairings |
| 06 | **big-idea** | The central compelling concept | Big Idea, angle, differentiation frame |

**Phase 1 Gate:** Cannot proceed to Phase 2 until:
- Mechanism is named and positioned
- Promise is validated against proof ceiling
- Big Idea crystallizes the entire strategic position

---

## Phase 2: Offer & Architecture
**Strategy → Structure**

These skills finalize the offer and map the logical flow of the copy.

| # | Skill | Purpose | Output |
|---|-------|---------|--------|
| 07 | **offer** | Finalize offer using mechanism name, benefits, bonuses, pricing from Big Idea work | Complete offer stack, pricing tiers, guarantee, urgency |
| 08 | **structure** | Logical outline using CBP (Claim-Benefit-Proof) segments | Section outline, CPB sequence, story placement, reveal timing |

**Phase 2 Gate:** Cannot proceed to Phase 2.5 until:
- Offer stack is complete (product, bonuses, guarantee)
- Copy structure is mapped with section purposes defined

---

## Phase 2.5: Coherence & Creative Direction
**Structure → Brief**

This skill synthesizes ALL upstream outputs, audits coherence, sets creative direction, and produces the Campaign Brief that all writing skills reference.

| # | Skill | Purpose | Output |
|---|-------|---------|--------|
| 09 | **campaign-brief** | Coherence audit, creative direction, unified brief | Campaign Brief (summary, coherence scores, lead/story type, emotional arc, section briefs, threading guide) |

**Phase 2.5 Gate:** Cannot proceed to Phase 2.75 until:
- All 10 coherence dimensions scored >= 7.0 overall (or human override)
- Lead type selected by human
- Story type selected by human
- Campaign Brief approved by human

---

## Phase 2.75: Headline Generation
**Brief → Headline Options**

This skill generates headline options based on the Big Idea and creative direction, providing the attention-capture layer that sits above the lead.

| # | Skill | Purpose | Output |
|---|-------|---------|--------|
| 10 | **headlines** | Generate headline options expressing Big Idea | 3-5 headline options with subheadlines, pattern analysis, curiosity architecture, schema distance calibration |

**Phase 2.75 Gate:** Cannot proceed to Phase 3 until:
- Headline options generated and scored
- Human selects headline (or provides custom)
- Headline-lead connection established
- headline-package.json approved

**Key Distinction:** While headlines became less prominent with the shift from sales letters to VSLs, they remain critical:
- VSLs still have text above the video
- Headline sets the frame for the lead
- Curiosity gap architecture flows from headline to lead
- Schema distance calibration starts at headline level

---

## Phase 3: Section Writing
**Brief → Narrative**

These skills write each section of the copy in sequence, consulting the Campaign Brief for direction. The lead skill references the selected headline to ensure continuity.

| # | Skill | Purpose | Output |
|---|-------|---------|--------|
| 11 | **lead** | Opening hook (written narrative), continues headline promise | Lead copy (story, secret, prediction, etc.) |
| 12 | **story** | Origin/credibility story | Story narrative (doctor origin, patient transformation, discovery) |
| 13 | **root-cause-narrative** | Root cause explained in copy form | Root cause section (what they think, real problem, why nothing worked) |
| 14 | **mechanism-narrative** | Mechanism explained in copy form | Mechanism reveal section (naming, explanation, proof) |
| 15 | **product-introduction** | The reveal | Product reveal section (transition from mechanism to product) |
| 16 | **offer-copy** | Offer section written out | Offer copy (stack presentation, bonuses, value anchoring) |
| 17 | **close** | CTA, urgency, final push | Close copy (risk reversal, urgency, CTA) |

---

## Phase 3.75: Proof Drafting
**Structure + Inventory → Proof Copy**

This skill drafts all proof elements into assembly-ready copy blocks. It takes the proof inventory (what proof exists) and the structure (where proof goes) and writes the actual proof copy — testimonials, before/afters, study citations — so that Campaign Assembly has complete, written proof blocks ready for insertion.

| # | Skill | Purpose | Output |
|---|-------|---------|--------|
| 18 | **proof-weaving** | Draft all proof elements into assembly-ready copy | Proof blocks (testimonials drafted, before/afters written, study citations formatted), density map, assembly instructions |

**Phase 3.75 Gate:** Cannot proceed to Phase 3.5 until:
- All proof blocks drafted for each section
- Density targets verified as achievable
- Assembly instructions complete
- proof-weaving-package.json approved

**Key Distinction:** This is a DRAFTING skill, not strategic. 02-proof-inventory catalogues what proof exists; 18-proof-weaving WRITES that proof into copy ready for assembly.

---

## Phase 3.5: Assembly & Verification
**Narrative → Deliverable**

This skill assembles all written sections, verifies execution against the Campaign Brief, and produces the final campaign document.

| # | Skill | Purpose | Output |
|---|-------|---------|--------|
| 19 | **campaign-assembly** | Assemble sections, verify coherence, produce deliverable | Final campaign document (JSON + Markdown), drift report, transition audit |

**Phase 3.5 Gate:** Campaign approved for delivery when:
- All sections assembled with smooth transitions
- Open loops verified (planted and resolved)
- Brief compliance checked per section
- Drift report reviewed by human
- Final document approved

---

## Phase 4: Editorial Review
**Deliverable → Refined Deliverable**

This skill runs the assembled draft through multiple editorial personas to produce line-level revision notes.

| # | Skill | Purpose | Output |
|---|-------|---------|--------|
| 20 | **editorial** | Multi-persona editorial review of assembled draft | Line-level revision notes from multiple editorial personas |

**Phase 4 Gate:** Editorial review complete when:
- All editorial personas have completed their passes
- Revision notes synthesized and prioritized
- Re-execution recommendations identified (which skills to re-run, if any)
- Human reviews editorial report and approves or triggers revision cycle

**Status:** COMPLETE — 6-layer architecture with 29 microskills, Arena-inspired multi-expert system.

## Key Distinctions

**07-offer vs 16-offer-copy:**
- `07-offer` = Strategic decisions (what's in the offer, pricing, bonuses, guarantee terms)
- `16-offer-copy` = Written narrative (the actual copy that presents the offer)

**03-root-cause vs 13-root-cause-narrative:**
- `03-root-cause` = Strategic framework (what is the root cause, how do we reframe)
- `13-root-cause-narrative` = Written narrative (the copy that explains the root cause to the reader)

**04-mechanism vs 14-mechanism-narrative:**
- `04-mechanism` = Strategic framework (mechanism name, positioning, credibility)
- `14-mechanism-narrative` = Written narrative (the copy that reveals and explains the mechanism)

**09-campaign-brief vs 19-campaign-assembly:**
- `09-campaign-brief` = Pre-execution coherence audit + creative direction + locked brief (BEFORE writing)
- `19-campaign-assembly` = Post-execution assembly + verification + drift report (AFTER writing)
- Together they form a matched pair: brief sets target, assembly verifies target was hit

---

## Usage

To generate copy for a campaign:

1. Run Phase 1 skills in order (or parallel where dependencies allow)
2. Gate check: Do you have mechanism, promise, and Big Idea?
3. Run Phase 2 skills to finalize offer and map structure
4. Gate check: Is offer complete and structure mapped?
5. Run Skill 09 (Campaign Brief) — synthesize, audit coherence, set creative direction
6. **Human reviews and approves Campaign Brief before proceeding**
7. Run Skill 10 (Headlines) — generate headline options, human selects
8. Run Phase 3 skills in sequence to write each section (consulting the brief)
9. Run Skill 18 (Proof Weaving) — draft all proof elements
10. Run Skill 19 (Campaign Assembly) — assemble, verify, produce deliverable
11. **Human reviews final assembled document**
12. Run Skill 20 (Editorial) for multi-expert review before deployment
