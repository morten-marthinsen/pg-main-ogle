# 3.1: Compliance Reviewer

## Purpose

Review every ad in the launch package against its target platform's advertising policies. This is the final compliance checkpoint before launch. Ads that violate platform policies get rejected during upload or (worse) get approved initially then taken down mid-campaign, wasting budget and disrupting testing. Every ad receives a three-tier verdict: PASS (compliant), FLAG (needs human review -- potential issue), or FAIL (clear violation -- must be modified or removed).

This review incorporates vertical-specific compliance constraints from the vertical profile (health claims, financial disclaimers, etc.) to catch industry-specific policy issues.

## Inputs

- All packaged ad files across all platforms (from Layer 1)
- 0.0.1-vertical-profile-loader.md output (vertical compliance constraints)
- A07 AD-COPY-FINAL/ (ad copy text for policy review)
- A08 AD-ASSETS/ or Layer 1 manifests (visual assets for policy review)
- Platform advertising policies:
  - Meta Advertising Standards and Policies
  - TikTok Advertising Policies
  - YouTube Ad Content Policies
  - Google Ads Policies

## Process

```
STEP 1: COMPILE ALL ADS FOR REVIEW
  - List every ad from all platform manifests (1.1-1.4 outputs)
  - For each ad, gather:
    - Ad copy text (from A07 or variant matrix)
    - Visual assets (from A08 / Layer 1 package)
    - Target platform(s)
    - Vertical compliance constraints (from 0.0.1)

STEP 2: REVIEW AGAINST META ADVERTISING POLICIES
  FOR EACH Meta-targeted ad:

  PROHIBITED CONTENT CHECKS:
    - No illegal products or services
    - No discriminatory practices
    - No tobacco, recreational drugs, or unsafe supplements
    - No weapons, ammunition, or explosives
    - No surveillance equipment
    - No misleading or false content

  RESTRICTED CONTENT CHECKS:
    - Alcohol: age-gating required, country restrictions apply
    - Dating: prior written permission required
    - Health & wellness:
      - No before/after images implying impossible results
      - No claims that diagnose, cure, treat, or prevent diseases (unless FDA-approved)
      - Supplement claims must be truthful and not misleading
      - Testimonial disclaimers may be required
    - Financial services:
      - Must comply with local regulations
      - Investment disclaimers required
      - No guaranteed returns language
    - Political/social issues: disclaimer and authorization required

  PERSONAL ATTRIBUTES CHECK:
    - Ads must NOT assert or imply personal attributes
    - Forbidden: "Are you overweight?" / "Struggling with debt?"
    - Allowed: "Weight loss solutions" / "Financial planning tools"

  MISLEADING CLAIMS CHECK:
    - No deceptive landing pages
    - No bait-and-switch offers
    - No fake urgency (countdown timers that reset)

  TEXT OVERLAY CHECK (informational, not blocking since 2020):
    - Note if text > 20% of image area (may affect distribution)

  VERDICT: PASS / FLAG / FAIL per ad

STEP 3: REVIEW AGAINST TIKTOK ADVERTISING POLICIES
  FOR EACH TikTok-targeted ad:

  PROHIBITED CATEGORIES:
    - No illegal goods or services
    - No weapons
    - No tobacco or nicotine products
    - No counterfeit goods
    - No endangered species products

  RESTRICTED CATEGORIES:
    - Health & wellness: no cure/treat claims, no before/after that's misleading
    - Finance: disclaimers required, no guaranteed returns
    - Alcohol: age-gating and country restrictions
    - Gambling: restricted to licensed operators, geo-fenced

  COMMUNITY GUIDELINES INTERSECTION:
    - No sexually suggestive content
    - No graphic violence
    - No dangerous challenges or stunts
    - No content promoting eating disorders

  VERTICAL-SPECIFIC:
    - Apply vertical compliance constraints from 0.0.1

  VERDICT: PASS / FLAG / FAIL per ad

STEP 4: REVIEW AGAINST YOUTUBE AD CONTENT POLICIES
  FOR EACH YouTube-targeted ad:

  AD CONTENT POLICIES:
    - No misleading or unrealistic claims
    - No shocking content or sensationalism designed only to get clicks
    - No content inappropriate for general audiences
    - No targeting based on sensitive categories

  RESTRICTED AD CATEGORIES:
    - Health: claims must be substantiated
    - Finance: risk disclosure required
    - Political: authorization and disclosure required
    - Gambling: licensed operators only

  VERTICAL-SPECIFIC:
    - Apply vertical compliance constraints from 0.0.1

  VERDICT: PASS / FLAG / FAIL per ad

STEP 5: REVIEW AGAINST GOOGLE ADS POLICIES
  FOR EACH Google Display-targeted ad:

  MISREPRESENTATION POLICY:
    - No misleading claims about product or service
    - No concealing material information
    - No impersonation of brands or government organizations

  UNRELIABLE CLAIMS:
    - No miracle cures or unsubstantiated health claims
    - No guaranteed results without substantiation
    - No clickbait or sensationalized content

  RESTRICTED CONTENT:
    - Health: claim substantiation required
    - Finance: disclaimers required
    - Alcohol: age-gating, geo-restrictions
    - Gambling: licensed only

  VERTICAL-SPECIFIC:
    - Apply vertical compliance constraints from 0.0.1

  VERDICT: PASS / FLAG / FAIL per ad

STEP 6: APPLY VERTICAL-SPECIFIC COMPLIANCE
  Apply the compliance constraints from the vertical profile:

  IF HEALTH VERTICAL:
    - Check for FDA-prohibited claims (cure, treat, prevent, diagnose)
    - Check for required disclaimers ("These statements have not been evaluated by the FDA...")
    - Check before/after requirements (realistic expectations, timeframes)
    - Check testimonial disclaimers ("Results not typical" or "Individual results may vary")

  IF FINANCE VERTICAL:
    - Check for SEC/FINRA compliance language
    - Check for "past performance" disclaimers
    - Check for risk disclosure
    - Check for "guaranteed returns" language (FORBIDDEN)

  IF OTHER VERTICAL:
    - Apply vertical-specific rules from 0.0.1

STEP 7: COMPILE COMPLIANCE REPORT
  Produce the MANDATORY COMPLIANCE CHECK table from AGENT.md:

  +------------------------------------------------------------------------+
  | PLATFORM   | TOTAL ADS | PASS | FLAG | FAIL | % CLEAR  | STATUS        |
  +------------+-----------+------+------+------+----------+---------------+
  | Meta       | [X]       | [X]  | [X]  | [X]  | [X]%     | PASS/REVIEW   |
  | TikTok     | [X]       | [X]  | [X]  | [X]  | [X]%     | PASS/REVIEW   |
  | YouTube    | [X]       | [X]  | [X]  | [X]  | [X]%     | PASS/REVIEW   |
  | Google     | [X]       | [X]  | [X]  | [X]  | [X]%     | PASS/REVIEW   |
  +------------------------------------------------------------------------+

  STATUS KEY:
    PASS = All ads PASS compliance
    REVIEW = Some ads FLAGGED -- requires human review before launch
    BLOCKED = Some ads FAIL compliance -- must be modified or removed

STEP 8: WRITE COMPLIANCE-REPORT.md (Deliverable)
  - Write to LAUNCH-PACKAGE/documentation/COMPLIANCE-REPORT.md
  - Include per-ad verdict with specific policy references

STEP 9: WRITE PER-MICROSKILL OUTPUT
  - Produce per-microskill output file at designated path
```

## Quality Checks

1. 100% of ads in the package are reviewed (zero skipped)
2. Every ad receives a three-tier verdict (PASS/FLAG/FAIL)
3. Vertical-specific compliance constraints are applied
4. FAIL verdicts include specific policy references and required fixes
5. FLAG verdicts include specific concerns and recommendations for human review
6. Compliance summary table matches per-ad detail (counts are accurate)
7. COMPLIANCE-REPORT.md deliverable is written
8. A FAIL verdict CANNOT be overridden by A11 -- must be fixed or excluded
9. Output file meets minimum 5KB size

## Dependencies

- Layer 1 must be complete (all packaged files available)
- Layer 2 must be complete (Gate 2 PASS)
- 0.0.1-vertical-profile-loader.md (vertical compliance constraints)
- A07 AD-COPY-FINAL/ (ad copy text)
- Runs in PARALLEL with 3.2 (independent checks)
