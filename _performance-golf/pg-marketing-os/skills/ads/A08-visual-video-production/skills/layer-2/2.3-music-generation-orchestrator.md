# 2.3: Music Generation Orchestrator

## Purpose
Compose mood/tempo direction and call Beatoven.ai for background music tracks. Match mood to concept tone and emotional register. Generate per production schedule Phase 1. Music must be commercially safe and mood-appropriate for each concept.

## Inputs
- 1.5 Production Schedule (which music tracks to generate)
- 1.2 Tool Assignment (Beatoven.ai or stock music)
- A05 Visual Direction (mood, tone, emotional direction per concept)
- A07 AD-COPY-FINAL/ (script emotional arc -- to match music mood)

## Process
```
FOR EACH music asset in production schedule:

STEP 1: EXTRACT MUSIC DIRECTION
  Source: A05 visual brief + A07 script emotional arc
  Determine:
    - Primary mood (uplifting, urgent, contemplative, energetic, calm, dramatic)
    - Tempo (BPM range: slow 60-80, moderate 80-120, fast 120-160)
    - Energy progression (build, sustain, fade)
    - Genre/style hint (cinematic, electronic, acoustic, ambient, pop)
    - Duration target (must match video/voiceover duration)
    - Instrumentation preferences (if specified in brief)

STEP 2: COMPOSE MUSIC GENERATION REQUEST
  For Beatoven.ai:
    - mood: [primary mood tag]
    - tempo: [target BPM]
    - duration: [seconds -- must match voiceover duration]
    - genre: [style hint]
    - instruments: [preferences if specified]
  Generate 2-3 variations with different mood intensities

STEP 3: EVALUATE TRACKS
  For each variation, assess:
    - Mood match to concept tone: [1-10]
    - Energy appropriate for ad type: [1-10]
    - Commercial safety (no copyright concerns): [PASS/FAIL]
    - Duration match: [PASS/FAIL -- must match target]
    - Audio quality: [1-10]
    - Mix-readiness (can be mixed under voiceover at -12 to -18dB): [1-10]
  Select best track
  IF best score < 6.0: Regenerate or source from stock music library

STEP 4: SAVE AND LOG
  Save as WAV (master) + MP3 (delivery) with naming convention
  Log mood settings, duration, quality score
```

## Output File
- **Path:** `layer-2-outputs/2.3-music-generation.md`
- **Minimum size:** 5KB

## Quality Checks
- Music mood matches the concept's emotional direction (not generic)
- Tempo is appropriate for the ad type and platform
- Tracks are commercially safe (Beatoven.ai is Fairly Trained certified)
- Duration matches voiceover/video target duration
- Tracks are mix-ready (can sit at -12 to -18dB under voiceover without losing character)
- Different concepts have appropriately different music (not all identical)

## Dependencies
- Layer 1 must be complete (GATE_1_COMPLETE.yaml exists)
- 1.5 Production Schedule must exist
- A05 Visual Direction mood specs must be loaded
- Beatoven.ai must be available (from 0.5) or stock library accessible
