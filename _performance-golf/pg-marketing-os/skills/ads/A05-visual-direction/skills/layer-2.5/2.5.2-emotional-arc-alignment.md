# 2.5.2: Emotional Arc Alignment

## Purpose
Map the emotional arc of each script (from A04 framework analysis) against the visual arc. Check: Does the visual pacing match emotional pacing? (Fast cuts during high-energy moments, lingering shots during reflective moments.) Does the visual intensity match emotional intensity? (Close-ups during intimate moments, wide shots during expansive claims.) Are there visual "breathing spaces" where the viewer can absorb a key point? Does the visual climax align with the script climax? This ensures the visual direction AMPLIFIES the emotional journey, not flattens it.

## Inputs
- Shot lists with durations from 2.1 (visual pacing and shot types)
- Script beat structures from 0.1 (emotional content of each beat)
- Script frameworks from 0.1 (framework implies emotional arc shape)
- Treatment types from 1.1 (treatment affects pacing expectations)

## Process
1. READ shot lists from 2.1
2. READ script beats and frameworks from 0.1
3. READ treatment types from 1.1
4. FOR EACH script:
   a. MAP the emotional arc of the audio:
      - Identify emotional intensity at each beat (1-10 scale)
      - Map: hook (high) --> problem (building) --> solution (climax) --> CTA (resolution)
      - Identify the CLIMAX beat (highest emotional intensity)
      - Identify BREATHING SPACES (moments of pause/reflection)
   b. MAP the visual arc:
      - Shot duration pattern (short cuts = high energy, long holds = contemplative)
      - Shot type pattern (close-ups = intimacy/intensity, wide shots = context/breathing)
      - Motion pattern (fast movement = energy, slow/static = contemplation)
      - Text overlay density (heavy = high information, light = breathing)
   c. CHECK pacing alignment:
      - During high-energy audio moments: are shots shorter? Are cuts faster?
      - During reflective audio moments: are shots longer? Are shots wider?
      - During the climax: does visual intensity peak simultaneously?
   d. CHECK intensity alignment:
      - Intimate/emotional audio moments: close-ups or medium close-ups?
      - Expansive/grand audio claims: wider shots or motion graphics?
      - Proof moments: appropriate visual evidence?
   e. CHECK breathing spaces:
      - Are there visual breathing spaces before key revelations?
      - Does the viewer have time to absorb important information?
      - Are lingering shots placed at the right moments?
   f. CHECK climax alignment:
      - Does the visual climax (most intense shot/moment) align with audio climax?
      - Is there visual buildup preceding the climax?
   g. SCORE emotional arc alignment (1-10):
      - 10: Visual arc perfectly mirrors and amplifies emotional arc
      - 7-9: Generally aligned with minor pacing mismatches
      - 4-6: Noticeable disconnects between visual and emotional pacing
      - 1-3: Visual arc works against the emotional arc
   h. FLAG specific misalignments with revision direction
5. WRITE to output file
