# 2.15: Round 3 Final Ranking & Campaign Learning Brief

## Purpose
Retain ALL 7 Round 3 evaluations in FULL per concept (these go to synthesis and human selection). Produce definitive ranking per concept across all personas. Produce cross-concept ranking of which concepts are strongest overall. Generate the final Campaign Learning Brief documenting what the Arena learned about effective concepts for this campaign.

## Inputs
- 2.11-round3-persona-evaluation output (FULL evaluations -- must be retained)
- 2.13-round3-targeted-revision output (FINAL revised evaluations)
- 2.14-round3-final-scoring output (FINAL scores and rankings)
- All previous round Learning Briefs (2.5, 2.10)

## Process
```
STEP 1: VERIFY all 7 Round 3 evaluations retained in full
  - READ: layer-2-outputs/2.11-round3-persona-evaluation.md
  - VERIFY: All evaluations present, not compressed
  - IF any compressed or missing: HALT -- Round 3 evaluations are needed for synthesis

STEP 2: Compile definitive per-concept ranking
  FOR EACH concept:
    - Rank all 7 personas by FINAL weighted score
    - Identify winner and runner-up
    - Note score range (highest to lowest)
    - Note which criteria showed most agreement/disagreement

STEP 3: Compile cross-concept definitive ranking
  - Rank all concepts by their best available score
  - Note which concepts are strongest, weakest
  - Note which concepts meet vs fail threshold

STEP 4: Generate Campaign Learning Brief
  - What hook types scored highest in Arena evaluation?
  - What script frameworks were most effective?
  - What visual treatments worked best?
  - What criteria were hardest to meet across all concepts?
  - Which persona lens provided most unique insights?
  - What improvement techniques worked vs failed?
  - Recommendations for future campaign creative

STEP 5: Prepare handoff to Layer 2.5 (Synthesis)
  - Confirm all 7 Round 3 evaluations available for decomposition
  - List concepts qualifying for synthesis (met or nearly met threshold)
```
