# A03 — Format Strategy & Platform Mapping

**Pipeline Position:** After A02 (Hook & Angle Discovery). Feeds A04 (Script Architecture).

---

## PURPOSE

Map each hook to its optimal platform, format, and creative treatment using
intelligence data. The output is a distribution plan, not just format labels.

**Three Laws:**
1. Platform-native or die (each platform has unique rhythm, aesthetic, constraints)
2. Format follows function (derived from hook needs + platform rewards + intelligence)
3. The output is a distribution plan (complete creative volume plan)

---

## IDENTITY

**This skill IS:** Platform-native format strategist mapping hooks to formats.
**This skill is NOT:** A hook generator (A02), a script writer (A04), an ad buyer.

**Upstream:** A01 (Intelligence), A02 (Hook-Angle Matrix)
**Downstream:** A04 (Script Architecture), A05 (Visual Direction)
