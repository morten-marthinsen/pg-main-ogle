# CHECKPOINT QUESTIONS — THE MC-CHECK INTERROGATION BANK
## What Questions the Metacognitive Check Asks
## Version 1.0 — March 2026

---

## PURPOSE

This file contains every question the MC-CHECK system asks during checkpoints. Questions are organized by category and purpose. Each question is designed to catch specific quality failures before they compound.

**The Philosophy:**
- Questions force honest self-evaluation
- Specific questions catch specific failures
- YES/NO format prevents weaseling
- Questions are actionable — a NO tells you exactly what to fix

---

## CATEGORY 1: STRATEGIC ALIGNMENT QUESTIONS

These questions verify content serves the campaign objective.

### SA-01: Does this content directly serve the campaign's primary goal?
**Purpose:** Catch scope creep and tangential content
**YES means:** Clear line from content to objective
**NO means:** Content is interesting but off-strategy

### SA-02: Would the target audience (from AIF) find this relevant to their needs?
**Purpose:** Verify audience-centricity
**YES means:** Content addresses documented pain points/desires
**NO means:** Content serves creator interest, not audience

### SA-03: Does this content reinforce the campaign's core message?
**Purpose:** Ensure message consistency
**YES means:** Content strengthens overall narrative
**NO means:** Content muddies or contradicts positioning

### SA-04: Is this the right content for this moment in the campaign calendar?
**Purpose:** Check timing appropriateness
**YES means:** Content fits campaign phase and calendar
**NO means:** Content is out of sequence or mistimed

### SA-05: Does this move the audience toward the desired action?
**Purpose:** Verify conversion intent
**YES means:** Clear path from content to next step
**NO means:** Content entertains without directing

### SA-06: If we could only post one piece today, would it be this one?
**Purpose:** Prioritization check
**YES means:** This is high-value content
**NO means:** There's better content to prioritize

## CATEGORY 2: QUALITY STANDARD QUESTIONS

These questions evaluate craft and execution quality.

### QS-01: Would this content survive the Arena if submitted?
**Purpose:** Pre-Arena quality check
**YES means:** Strong enough to compete with 7 persona variants
**NO means:** Needs strengthening before Arena

### QS-02: Is every sentence in this content adding value?
**Purpose:** Filler detection
**YES means:** No dead weight, high density
**NO means:** Sentences can be cut without losing meaning

### QS-03: Could this content have been written by AI without our specific context?
**Purpose:** Generic output detection
**YES (problem) means:** Too generic, needs specificity
**NO (goal) means:** Context-specific, brand-authentic

### QS-04: Would we be embarrassed by this content in 6 months?
**Purpose:** Quality regret test
**YES (problem) means:** Standards too low, revise
**NO (goal) means:** Evergreen quality standard

### QS-05: Does this content meet or exceed the quality of our best-performing piece?
**Purpose:** Benchmark comparison
**YES means:** On-brand excellence
**NO means:** Quality is below established bar

### QS-06: Is there a single weak element that undermines the whole?
**Purpose:** Weakest link identification
**YES (problem) means:** Fix the weak element
**NO (goal) means:** Consistent quality throughout

## CATEGORY 3: AUTHENTICITY QUESTIONS

These questions verify brand voice and genuine expression.

### AU-01: Does this sound like [Brand/Creator] wrote it?
**Purpose:** Voice authenticity check
**YES means:** Voice matches BVF specifications
**NO means:** Generic voice, not brand-specific

### AU-02: Are we using the brand's power words appropriately?
**Purpose:** Vocabulary alignment
**YES means:** Power words present and natural
**NO means:** Missing signature vocabulary

### AU-03: Are we avoiding the brand's banned words?
**Purpose:** Vocabulary filter
**YES means:** No banned words detected
**NO means:** Remove banned words immediately

### AU-04: Does this content reflect the brand's values and beliefs?
**Purpose:** Value alignment
**YES means:** Content embodies documented values
**NO means:** Value disconnect, may feel inauthentic

### AU-05: Would the audience recognize this as coming from us without seeing the name?
**Purpose:** Distinctive voice test
**YES means:** Signature style is present
**NO means:** Voice is too generic

### AU-06: Does this content say something only WE would say?
**Purpose:** Unique perspective check
**YES means:** Proprietary insight or angle
**NO means:** Could be from any creator in the space

## CATEGORY 5: VIRALITY QUESTIONS

These questions evaluate shareability and spread potential.

### VI-01: What is the hook, and would it stop YOUR scroll?
**Purpose:** Hook strength test
**YES (strong hook) means:** Proceed
**NO (weak hook) means:** Strengthen hook before continuing

### VI-02: What is the share trigger — why would someone pass this along?
**Purpose:** Shareability mechanism
**CLEAR ANSWER means:** Share trigger identified
**UNCLEAR means:** Add share trigger

### VI-03: Does this content make the sharer look good/smart/generous?
**Purpose:** Social currency check
**YES means:** Sharing has social benefit
**NO means:** No social incentive to share

### VI-04: Is there a "tag a friend" moment in this content?
**Purpose:** Viral mechanic check
**YES means:** Natural referral point exists
**NO means:** Consider adding referral hook

### VI-05: Would someone save this for later?
**Purpose:** Save-worthy check
**YES means:** Reference value exists
**NO means:** Consumable but not savable

### VI-06: Is there a screenshottable moment?
**Purpose:** Visual spread check
**YES means:** Quote-worthy or graphic-worthy moment
**NO means:** Nothing visually spreadable

### VI-07: Does this create tension, surprise, or strong emotion?
**Purpose:** Emotional activation
**YES means:** Emotional engagement likely
**NO means:** Too flat, add emotional charge

## CATEGORY 8: ANTI-SLOP QUESTIONS

These questions catch AI-generated content patterns.

### AS-01: Does this content contain any banned opening phrases?
**Purpose:** Opening slop detection
**NO (goal) means:** Clean opening
**YES (problem) means:** Rewrite opening

### AS-02: Does this content contain any banned closing phrases?
**Purpose:** Closing slop detection
**NO (goal) means:** Clean closing
**YES (problem) means:** Rewrite closing

### AS-03: Does this content contain filler phrases that add no value?
**Purpose:** Filler detection
**NO (goal) means:** Dense, valuable copy
**YES (problem) means:** Cut filler phrases

### AS-04: Does this content use any generic metaphors from the banned list?
**Purpose:** Cliché detection
**NO (goal) means:** Original language
**YES (problem) means:** Replace with specific language

### AS-05: Does this content show AI-signature patterns (triple adjectives, excessive em-dashes, etc.)?
**Purpose:** AI pattern detection
**NO (goal) means:** Natural writing style
**YES (problem) means:** Revise to remove patterns

### AS-06: What is the slop density percentage?
**Purpose:** Quantified slop measurement
**≤2% means:** Acceptable
**>2% means:** Revision required

## CATEGORY 9: CONTEXT HEALTH QUESTIONS

These questions monitor session degradation.

### CH-01: Can I still accurately recall the campaign brief details?
**Purpose:** Context retention
**YES means:** Context intact
**NO means:** Reload CBF

### CH-02: Do I remember the original user request that started this task?
**Purpose:** Objective retention
**YES means:** Still on track
**NO means:** Re-confirm objectives

### CH-03: Am I still aligned with the original objective, or have I drifted?
**Purpose:** Scope drift detection
**ALIGNED means:** Continue
**DRIFTED means:** Re-align or confirm new scope

### CH-04: Have I introduced scope or features not explicitly requested?
**Purpose:** Scope creep detection
**NO (goal) means:** Disciplined execution
**YES (problem) means:** Remove unrequested additions

### CH-05: What is my estimated context load percentage?
**Purpose:** Context monitoring
**<70% means:** Continue normally
**≥70% means:** Prepare for handoff

### CH-06: Is my output quality consistent with earlier in this session?
**Purpose:** Degradation detection
**YES means:** Quality maintained
**NO means:** Reload quality standards
