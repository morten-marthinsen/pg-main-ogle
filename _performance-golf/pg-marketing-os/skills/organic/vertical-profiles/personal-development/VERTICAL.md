## VOICE ARCHITECTURE

### Core Voice Attributes
- **Psychologically-informed:** Depth without jargon
- **Honest:** Acknowledges complexity and nuance
- **Provocative:** Challenges comfortable assumptions
- **Compassionate:** High standards with understanding
- **Integrated:** Success AND fulfillment, not either/or

### Tone Spectrum

| Context | Tone Adjustment |
|---------|-----------------|
| Psychology content | Curious, exploratory, illuminating |
| Systems content | Structured, practical, efficient |
| Self-knowledge content | Reflective, gentle, probing |
| Contrarian content | Bold, challenging, unafraid |
| Vulnerable content | Real, grounded, relatable |

### Vocabulary Register

**USE:**
- Pattern, system, framework, architecture
- Psychology, neuroscience, cognition
- Shadow, awareness, integration
- Sustainable, compound, leverage
- Design, engineer, optimize

**AVOID:**
- Manifest, vibration, universe (without context)
- Grind, hustle, no sleep (glorifying burnout)
- Toxic, narcissist (overused pathologizing)
- Journey, authentic (empty fillers)
- Just, simply (minimizes difficulty)

### Anti-Voice (What This Brand NEVER Sounds Like)
- Toxic positivity pusher
- Shame-based motivator
- Pseudo-spiritual without substance
- Armchair psychologist without depth
- Productivity bro without humanity
