# 2.5.2 Adversarial Critique

**Layer:** 2.5 (Arena — Critique Competition)
**Model:** Claude Opus
**Upstream:** 2.5.1 Arena Submission
**Downstream:** 2.5.3 Synthesis

---

## PURPOSE

Execute 3 rounds of adversarial critique where each of 7 personas critiques the other 6 variants. Score on hook strength (30%), voice alignment (25%), platform fit (25%), and engagement potential (20%). Identify weaknesses, suggest improvements, and prepare for synthesis of best elements.

---

## INPUT REQUIREMENTS

```yaml
required_inputs:
  arena_submission: "outputs/layer-2.5/2.5.1-arena-submission.md"
  organic_arena_protocol: "ORGANIC-ARENA-PROTOCOL.md"

scoring_criteria:
  hook_strength: 30%        # Scroll-stop power, truncation safety, value delivery
  voice_alignment: 25%      # BVF match, tone consistency
  platform_fit: 25%         # Character count, formatting, hashtag strategy
  engagement_potential: 20% # Saves, shares, comments likelihood

rounds: 3
personas: 7
```

---

## EXECUTION PROTOCOL

**MUST:**
1. Load all 7 persona variants from 2.5.1
2. For EACH persona, critique the OTHER 6 variants on:
   - **Hook Strength (1-10):** Does hook stop scroll before truncation?
   - **Voice Alignment (1-10):** BVF match, empowering not aggressive?
   - **Platform Fit (1-10):** Character count, line breaks, hashtags appropriate?
   - **Engagement Potential (1-10):** Will users save/share/comment?
3. Generate **weighted composite score** using criteria percentages:
   - Composite = (Hook × 0.30) + (Voice × 0.25) + (Platform × 0.25) + (Engagement × 0.20)
4. Execute **3 rounds of critique**:
   - **Round 1:** Initial scores + weaknesses identification
   - **Round 2:** Deeper analysis + head-to-head comparisons
   - **Round 3:** Final scores + specific improvement recommendations
5. Track score evolution across rounds (scores may change as personas refine analysis)
6. Generate critique summary document with:
   - Score matrix (7 personas × 6 others × 3 rounds)
   - Top 3 variants by composite score
   - Key strengths to preserve in synthesis
   - Key weaknesses to fix in synthesis

**NEVER:**
- Have persona score itself (conflict of interest)
- Use identical scores across all variants (must differentiate)
- Skip rounds (all 3 required for refinement)
- Ignore BVF voice in scoring (voice alignment is 25% of score)
- Generate scores without rationale (must explain why)
