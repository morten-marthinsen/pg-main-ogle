# 1.1 Foundation Synthesis

## Purpose
Extract KEY INSIGHTS from each foundation file into concise, actionable summaries. This creates the "executive summary" section of the Campaign Brief.

---

## Input Requirements

From Layer 0:
- `0.4-strategic-synthesis.yaml`
- Access to all 6 foundation files

---

## Execution Protocol

### Step 1: Synthesize Audience (from AIF)

**CRITICAL:** Extract insights, don't copy-paste entire file.

```yaml
from_AIF:
  target_segment_summary: |
    [2-3 sentences describing WHO the audience is]
    Example: "Mid-career professionals (35-50) who consume golf content
    on Instagram during lunch breaks and evenings. They're frustrated with
    complex instruction that doesn't translate to the course."

  key_audience_insight: |
    [THE most important finding from AIF]
    Example: "They don't want more information—they want permission to
    simplify their approach. They're overwhelmed, not under-informed."

  language_to_use:
    - "[phrase 1 from AIF]"
    - "[phrase 2 from AIF]"
    - "[phrase 3 from AIF]"
    # Top 5-7 phrases only

  primary_pain: "[1 sentence from AIF]"
  primary_desire: "[1 sentence from AIF]"
```

**Maximum: 200 words for entire AIF synthesis.**

---

### Step 2: Synthesize Platform (from PSF)

```yaml
from_PSF:
  primary_platform: "[platform]"
  secondary_platforms: [list]
  format_focus: "[primary formats]"
  posting_cadence: "[frequency - daily, 3x/week, etc.]"
  optimal_times: [times]

  cross_platform_strategy: |
    [1-2 sentences on how content moves between platforms]
    Example: "Instagram Reels as primary. Repurpose top performers to
    TikTok same day. Convert high-engagement pieces to YouTube Shorts weekly."
```

**Maximum: 150 words for entire PSF synthesis.**

---

### Step 3: Synthesize Voice (from BVF)

```yaml
from_BVF:
  voice_summary: "[3-5 key attributes]"
  tone_for_campaign: "[specific tone direction]"
  vocabulary_guidelines:
    use: [5-7 phrases]
    avoid: [5-7 phrases]
  energy_level: "[high/medium/low with context]"
```

**Maximum: 150 words for entire BVF synthesis.**

---

### Step 4: Synthesize Content Architecture (from CAF)

```yaml
from_CAF:
  pillar_focus:
    - pillar: "[name]"
      description: "[1 sentence]"
      campaign_role: "[what it does for this campaign]"
    # List all pillars

  series_involved: [recurring series names]

  content_function_mix:
    awareness: [%]
    engagement: [%]
    conversion: [%]
    community: [%]

  funnel_connection: |
    [1-2 sentences on how organic drives revenue]
```

**Maximum: 200 words for entire CAF synthesis.**

---

### Step 5: Synthesize Hooks (from HLF)

```yaml
from_HLF:
  primary_hook_types:
    - "[type 1]"
    - "[type 2]"
    - "[type 3]"
    # Top 3-5 only

  platform_specific_hooks:
    - "[approved hook 1]"
    - "[approved hook 2]"
    - "[approved hook 3]"
    # Top 5-7 hooks only, ready to use

  hook_testing_plan: |
    [1 sentence on how hooks will be A/B tested]
```

**Maximum: 150 words for entire HLF synthesis.**

---

### Step 6: Synthesize Virality Scoring (from VSF)

```yaml
from_VSF:
  minimum_score: [number]
  target_score: [number]
  viral_threshold: [number]

  dimension_priorities:
    1: "[most important dimension]"
    2: "[second priority]"
    3: "[third priority]"

  must_haves_checklist: [5 items from VSF quick-score]

  calibration_insights: |
    [1-2 key learnings from specimen calibration]
```

**Maximum: 150 words for entire VSF synthesis.**

---

## Output

**File:** `layer-1/1.1-synthesis.yaml`

**Format:**
```yaml
timestamp: "[ISO 8601]"

foundation_synthesis:
  from_AIF: {...}
  from_PSF: {...}
  from_BVF: {...}
  from_CAF: {...}
  from_HLF: {...}
  from_VSF: {...}

synthesis_complete: true
word_count: [X words]  # Should be ~1000 words total
```

**Minimum size:** 3KB

---

## Quality Gates

- [ ] All 6 foundation files synthesized
- [ ] Each synthesis ≤200 words (concise)
- [ ] Key insights extracted, not copy-pasted
- [ ] Actionable summaries, not just data dumps
- [ ] Total synthesis ~1000 words
