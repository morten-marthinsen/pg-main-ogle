# 1.1 Dimension Definition

## Purpose
Define all 8 scoring dimensions with teaching-backed criteria, 0-10 scales, and specific indicators for high/low scores. This is the core of the scoring framework.

---

## Input Requirements

From Layer 0:
- `0.2-teaching-synthesis.yaml` (teaching-backed dimensions)
- `0.4-upstream-synthesis.yaml` (weighting recommendations)

---

## Execution Protocol

### Step 1: Define Each Dimension

For ALL 8 dimensions, create complete definitions using this structure:

```yaml
dimension_name: "[name]"
weight: [10-20]  # Must sum to 100 across all 8
teaching_sources: [list]
description: |
  [What this dimension measures and why it matters for virality]

scoring_criteria:
  "10": "[Exceptional — specific description]"
  "8": "[Strong — specific description]"
  "6": "[Solid — specific description]"
  "4": "[Weak — specific description]"
  "2": "[Poor — specific description]"
  "0": "[Absent or harmful — specific description]"

indicators:
  high_score:
    - "[Specific observable characteristic 1]"
    - "[Specific observable characteristic 2]"
    - "[Specific observable characteristic 3]"
  low_score:
    - "[Specific observable characteristic 1]"
    - "[Specific observable characteristic 2]"
    - "[Specific observable characteristic 3]"
```

---

### Step 2: Dimension-Specific Guidance

**HOOK STRENGTH (Weight: 20)**
- Teaching sources: Kane Viral Content Model
- Focus: First 3 seconds / first line effectiveness
- High score indicators: Pattern interrupt, immediate curiosity gap, emotional trigger instant
- Low score indicators: Slow build, generic opening, context required

**EMOTIONAL RESONANCE (Weight: 15)**
- Teaching sources: Berger STEPPS, Heath Made to Stick, Cialdini
- Focus: High-arousal emotions (awe, excitement, humor, anger, anxiety)
- High score indicators: Clear emotional trigger, makes you FEEL something
- Low score indicators: Intellectual only, low-arousal emotions (sadness, contentment)

**PRACTICAL VALUE (Weight: 15)**
- Teaching sources: Berger STEPPS, Heath Made to Stick
- Focus: Useful, actionable information worth saving/sharing
- High score indicators: Specific steps, novel insight, saves time/money, solves real problem
- Low score indicators: Vague platitudes, commonly known, no takeaway

**SOCIAL CURRENCY (Weight: 15)**
- Teaching sources: Berger STEPPS, Cialdini
- Focus: Does sharing make the sharer look good
- High score indicators: Insider knowledge, remarkable stats, counterintuitive, helpful to others
- Low score indicators: Neutral status implication, risky to share, embarrassing

**STORY STRUCTURE (Weight: 10)**
- Teaching sources: Berger STEPPS, Heath Made to Stick
- Focus: Compelling narrative architecture
- High score indicators: Clear setup/conflict, rising tension, transformation, satisfying conclusion
- Low score indicators: Information dump, confusing, no arc

**PLATFORM FIT (Weight: 10)**
- Teaching sources: Kane Viral Content Model
- Focus: Optimized for platform algorithm and culture
- High score indicators: Native format, correct length, technical specs met, cultural fit
- Low score indicators: Wrong format, platform mismatch, violates norms

**SPECIFICITY (Weight: 10)**
- Teaching sources: Heath Made to Stick
- Focus: Concrete details make it memorable
- High score indicators: Exact numbers, named examples, step-by-step, concrete scenarios
- Low score indicators: "Some people," vague amounts, generic advice

**VOICE ALIGNMENT (Weight: 5)**
- Teaching sources: BVF
- Focus: Sounds authentically like the brand
- High score indicators: Matches voice dimensions, uses signature phrases, avoids banned words
- Low score indicators: Generic, off-voice, could be anyone

---

### Step 3: Verify Weight Distribution

Sum all weights:
- MUST equal exactly 100
- If ≠100, adjust based on content strategy function_mix from upstream

**Adjustment rules:**
- If campaign is 60%+ engagement → increase emotional_resonance to 20, decrease practical_value to 10
- If campaign is 60%+ awareness → increase practical_value to 20, decrease emotional_resonance to 10
- Hook_strength ALWAYS 20 (universal)
- Voice_alignment ALWAYS 5 (hygiene factor)

**Format:**

```yaml
dimensions:
  - name: "hook_strength"
    weight: 20
    teaching_sources: ["Kane"]
    description: |
      Does the hook stop the scroll in <3 seconds and create immediate engagement?

      "2": "Poor — will be scrolled past"

        - "Opens with pattern interrupt"

        - "Generic opening"

```
