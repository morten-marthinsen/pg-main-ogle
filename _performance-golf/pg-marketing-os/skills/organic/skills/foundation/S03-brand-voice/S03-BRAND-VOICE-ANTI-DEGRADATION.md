## WHY THIS DOCUMENT EXISTS

**Anticipated Failure Patterns:**
- AI selects voice archetype without AIF/PSF context
- AI skips anti-voice definition

- AI skips calibration examples
- AI proceeds to S04 without Gate G03 passing

## MINIMUM THRESHOLDS

| Section | Minimum Requirement | Gate |
|---------|-------------------|------|
| Voice dimensions | 8/8 scored (1-10 scale) | Layer 1 |
| Signature words | 5 unique words | Layer 1 |
| Power phrases | 10 phrases | Layer 1 |
| Banned words | 5 words with rationale | Layer 1 |
| Anti-voice examples | 2 archetypes/competitors | Layer 1 |
| On-voice hooks | 3 examples | Layer 1 |

---

## FORBIDDEN RATIONALIZATIONS

1. "Anti-voice doesn't matter."
   - NO. Defining what we're NOT is as important as what we ARE.

2. "Generic banned word lists are fine."
   - NO. Each banned word needs rationale specific to this brand.

3. "We can skip calibration examples."
   - NO. Examples are how voice transfers to execution.
