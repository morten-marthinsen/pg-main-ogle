# 1.6 Audience Targeting

## Purpose
Define target followers per persona, where to find them, and engagement strategy to attract them. Create specific audience targeting plans that go beyond demographics to behavior and interests.

## Model Assignment
**Model:** claude-opus-4-20250514

## Input Requirements
- Persona Bibles (niche, audience segments)
- Platform selections (from 1.1)
- Growth strategy tactics (from 1.4)
- Upstream context (AIF audience intelligence)

## Execution Protocol

### MUST
- Define ideal follower profile per persona: demographics, psychographics, behaviors, interests
- Identify where target audience congregates: specific communities, hashtags, accounts they follow
- Design engagement strategy to attract target followers: what value persona provides, how to demonstrate it
- Create follower qualification criteria: what makes someone ideal vs. acceptable vs. wrong-fit
- Plan audience nurture sequence: first follow → regular engagement → loyal advocate
- Differentiate audience targeting across personas (different segments per persona from AIF)
- Include audience research tactics: how to discover where target audience is active

### NEVER
- Define audience solely by demographics (need psychographics and behaviors)
- Use vague descriptions like "entrepreneurs" or "health-conscious people"
- Skip "where to find them" tactical detail
- Create identical audience profiles across personas
- Ignore audience nurture beyond initial follow
- Forget to qualify audience (not all followers are valuable)

## Output
**Filename:** `audience-targeting-plans.md`

**Format:** Markdown audience targeting playbook

**Minimum Size:** 5KB

**Required Sections:**
1. Per-Persona Ideal Follower Profiles (demographics, psychographics, behaviors, interests)
2. Audience Location Mapping (where they congregate: communities, hashtags, accounts)
3. Engagement Strategy to Attract (value proposition, demonstration tactics)
4. Follower Qualification Criteria (ideal, acceptable, wrong-fit definitions)
5. Audience Nurture Sequence (first follow → engagement → advocacy journey)
6. Audience Differentiation (how target audiences differ across personas)
7. Audience Research Tactics (how to discover where they're active)

## Quality Gates
- [ ] Ideal follower profiles include psychographics and behaviors (not just demographics)
- [ ] Audience location mapping is specific (actual communities, hashtags, accounts)
- [ ] Engagement strategy articulates clear value proposition
- [ ] Follower qualification criteria define ideal vs. wrong-fit
- [ ] Nurture sequence maps follower journey beyond initial follow
- [ ] Audience targeting differentiated across personas
