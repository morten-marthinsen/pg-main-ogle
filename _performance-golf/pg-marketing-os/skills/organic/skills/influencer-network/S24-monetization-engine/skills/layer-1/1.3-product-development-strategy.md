# 1.3 Product Development Strategy

## Purpose
Design digital products, courses, memberships, consulting services per persona. Must align with persona's established authority and audience needs. Create product development roadmap.

## Model Assignment
**Model:** claude-opus-4-20250514

## Input Requirements
- Revenue model mapping (from 1.1)
- Audience readiness assessment (from 1.2)
- Persona Bibles (authority areas, expertise)
- Upstream context (audience segments, needs)

## Execution Protocol

### MUST
- Design 3-5 product concepts per persona aligned with assigned revenue models
- Verify authority alignment: can persona credibly deliver this product based on backstory/expertise?
- Map products to audience needs: what problem does this solve? What result does it deliver?
- Define product formats: course, membership, template, coaching, workshop, ebook, etc.
- Create product development roadmap: which products launch when, in what sequence
- Consider production requirements: what's needed to create and deliver this product?
- Design differentiation: how do products differ across personas (avoid identical offerings)
- Include validation strategy: how to test demand before full development

### NEVER
- Create products outside persona's established authority
- Design identical products across personas
- Skip audience needs mapping (products must solve real problems)
- Ignore production requirements assessment
- Forget validation strategy (test before building)
- Create too many products too fast (focus over breadth)

## Output
**Filename:** `product-development-strategy.md`

**Format:** Markdown product strategy

**Minimum Size:** 6KB

**Required Sections:**
1. Per-Persona Product Concepts (3-5 concepts with descriptions)
2. Authority Alignment Verification (can persona credibly deliver this?)
3. Audience Needs Mapping (what problems do products solve?)
4. Product Format Specifications (course, membership, template, etc.)
5. Product Development Roadmap (launch sequence, timing)
6. Production Requirements (what's needed to create/deliver)
7. Differentiation Analysis (how products differ across personas)
8. Validation Strategy (test demand before full development)

## Quality Gates
- [ ] 3-5 product concepts per persona
- [ ] Authority alignment verified (persona qualified for all products)
- [ ] Audience needs mapped (problems solved, results delivered)
- [ ] Product formats specified with delivery mechanisms
- [ ] Development roadmap sequences products logically
- [ ] Production requirements assessed
- [ ] Differentiation across personas verified
- [ ] Validation strategy prevents over-building
