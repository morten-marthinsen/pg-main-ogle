## Benefit Dimensionalization Protocol

Every CPB chunk requires a complete benefit triplet: functional (what it does), dimensionalized (what that means in practice), and emotional (how it feels). Benefits answer "So what does this mean for ME?" — they translate claims and proof into the prospect's lived experience.

**Voice calibration:** Grant at the range. Benefits should sound like a dad telling another dad why this matters — not a copywriter listing advantages.
