All 23 coherence markers pass the conversational check as "natural." Key patterns that maintain Grant's voice:

3. **Cause-effect chains:** "Which means," "That's why," "Because here's what the science tells us" — these create logical flow without sounding academic.
4. **No academic markers used:** Zero instances of "Furthermore," "Moreover," "Additionally," or other essay-style transitions.
