## Three Thesis Pillars

### Root Cause Connection
Every toddler golf product was designed around one assumption: that someone would be standing there giving verbal instructions. But toddlers (ages 2-5) cannot convert words into movement — that is developmental science confirmed for nearly 80 years. The equipment was speaking the wrong language.

### Mechanism Connection
The See & Swing System teaches golf fundamentals through visual-tactile cues — shapes, colors, and physical positioning — the channel a toddler's brain is actually wired for. No verbal coaching needed. The equipment teaches through its design, the way a shape sorter teaches geometry.

### Promise Connection
When the equipment teaches, the parent's role transforms. Instead of struggling to coach a toddler who can't process verbal instructions, the parent becomes a golf buddy — playing alongside their kid while the See & Swing System handles alignment, stance, and setup automatically.
