# Grant Horvat — Research Brief
**Compiled:** 2026-03-09

## Profile
- **Name:** Grant Horvat
- **Born:** August 24, 1998 (age 27)
- **YouTube:** Grant Horvat Golf — ~1.6M subscribers, 264M+ total views
- **Status:** Largest individual golf creator on the internet
- **Married:** Sadee Farinha (November 2022)

## Background
- Father Steve Horvat was a professional golfer (PGA Tour of Australasia)
- Division II golfer at Palm Beach Atlantic University
- Former member of Good Good Golf, went solo to build personal brand
- On-course reporter for PGA TOUR LIVE before full-time content creation

## Career Milestones (2025-2026)
- Won 2025 Creator Classic at TPC Sawgrass (playoff birdie on 17th)
- Competed in first Korn Ferry Tour event — BMW Charity Pro-Am (June 2025)
- Turned down PGA Tour's Barracuda Championship (couldn't document for subscribers)
- Played first PGA Tour event (January 2026)
- Became partial owner of Takomo Golf (Finnish equipment company, 2025)
- TaylorMade endorsement and content creation deal
- Has previously collaborated with Performance Golf on instruction content

## Brand Values & Identity
- **Authenticity:** Refuses to play events where he can't be himself (turned down PGA Tour invite over filming rules)
- **Accessibility:** Makes golf feel less stuffy, appeals to younger demos
- **Growing the game:** Active in charity events and youth programs
- **Family first:** Credits his father for instilling love of golf; family is central to his narrative
- **Competitive but relatable:** Rare creator who actually competes at professional level

## Audience
- Skews younger than traditional golf demographics (late 20s-30s)
- Brings in people who might not otherwise follow golf
- Younger demographic overlaps with parents of toddlers (LIL target buyer)
- High engagement — subscribers feel personally connected

## Why He Matters for Lil' Legends
- His father literally shaped his love of golf from childhood — autobiographical connection
- "I fell in love with golf because my dad gave me the game" = emotional spine
- Passion project with Brixton (PG CEO) to give back to golf through kids
- His audience IS the target buyer: young parents who love golf
- Not a paid endorsement play — genuine passion alignment
