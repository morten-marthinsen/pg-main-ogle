## NLS Adaptation Notes for LIL

The NLS framework was designed primarily for supplement/health PDPs. Physical product adaptations required:

- **Thumbnail 2 (Problem Signs):** Adapt from health symptoms to parenting frustrations (broken clubs, kids walking away, over-coaching)
- **Thumbnail 3 (Ingredients):** Adapt to product components (3 clubs, Skills Mat, carry bag) with See & Swing principle per component
- **Thumbnail 6 (What to Expect):** Adapt from health timeline to developmental milestones (Day 1, Week 1, Month 1)
- **Section 7 (Ingredients/Materials):** Repurpose as "What's In the Set" — component breakdown
- **Section 12 (Cost Savings):** Adapt from subscription math to serial-purchase-cycle math
- **No urgency mechanics:** Section 3 (CTA Box) removes countdown/urgency per branded initiative constraints
- **Pre-launch gaps:** Section 5 (UGC) and Section 14 (Reviews) are blocked until testimonials arrive (GAP-02, target April 15)
