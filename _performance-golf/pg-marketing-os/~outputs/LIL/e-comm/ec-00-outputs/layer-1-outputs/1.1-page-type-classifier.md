## Classification

**Page Type:** PDP (Product Detail Page)
**Confidence:** 0.95
**Confidence Tier:** HIGH

## Rationale

1. **Single product.** The Lil' Legends Starter Set is one product with one SKU at one price point ($149). No variant selection (color, size, material) required. This is a single-product PDP, not a collection or bundle page.

2. **Deep feature story required.** The See & Swing System has 4 mechanism components that need individual explanation. The shape sorter analogy, developmental science backing, and competitive differentiation all require dedicated page sections. This depth matches PDP architecture, not collection-level overview.

3. **Direct purchase intent traffic.** Traffic will arrive from Grant Horvat content (YouTube, Instagram), PG email, organic search ("toddler golf clubs"), and social ads. All traffic types expect a product page with buy capability.

4. **Branded initiative positioning.** The page must tell the full brand story (Grant's personal narrative, mechanism education, science, product system). This story-driven approach maps to PDP, not a minimal collection card.

5. **PG pattern match.** All PG physical products (RS1, SF2, 357, ONE.1) use PDP format. LIL follows the same architecture with adaptations for branded (vs. DR) positioning.

## Alternatives Considered

| Type | Fit | Why Not |
|------|-----|---------|
| Collection Page | LOW | Single product, not a product line. No filtering or sorting needed. |
| Bundle Page | LOW | While the set includes multiple items (clubs + mat + bag), these are sold as one unit at one price. Not a configurable bundle. |
| Landing Page (DR) | EXCLUDED | Campaign type is branded initiative. No VSL funnel. No DR urgency mechanics. |

## PDP Subtype

**Story-led PDP** — follows the Structure skill's Hybrid-1 (story-led) recommendation. The page leads with Grant's personal story and the Big Idea, then educates through the 7 CPB chunks. This is distinct from a feature-led PDP (like RS1) or a price-led PDP.

Key difference from RS1 PDP: RS1 is a premium golf club for experienced golfers (Stage 4 market, $399-429, mechanism-heavy). LIL is a developmental system for parents of toddlers (Stage 2-3 market, $149, emotion-heavy with mechanism education). LIL's PDP will be shorter, more visual, more emotionally driven, with fewer technical sections.
