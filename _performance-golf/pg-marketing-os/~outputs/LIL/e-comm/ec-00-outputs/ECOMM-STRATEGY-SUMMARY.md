```
ATF:  Hero > Copy Block > CTA Box
BTF:  Product Highlights (scan bridge)
      > Problem/Solution (Relief)
      > Why It Works (Wonder + Confidence)
      > Expert Credibility (Trust)
      > Comparison Chart (Resolution)
      > What to Expect (Anticipation)
      > How to Use (Ease)
```
