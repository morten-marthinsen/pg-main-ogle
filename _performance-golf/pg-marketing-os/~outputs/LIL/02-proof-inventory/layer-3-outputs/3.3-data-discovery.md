## Track 3: Motor Development Milestones (Ages 2-5)

### Source: CDC Milestone Guidelines

**Source:** Centers for Disease Control and Prevention. (2023). *CDC's developmental milestones*. https://www.cdc.gov/ncbddd/actearly/milestones/

| Age | Gross Motor Milestones | Fine Motor Milestones |
|-----|----------------------|----------------------|
| **2 years** | Kicks a ball, runs, walks up stairs (holding on), throws ball overhand | Stacks 4+ blocks, turns pages, uses spoon |
| **3 years** | Pedals tricycle, runs easily, climbs well, walks up/down stairs (alternating feet) | Draws a circle, turns door handles, unscrews lids |
| **4 years** | Hops on one foot, catches bounced ball, pours/cuts (supervised), uses scissors | Draws person with 2-4 body parts, uses fork and spoon |
| **5 years** | Stands on one foot 10+ seconds, hops, can skip, swings and climbs | Draws person with 6+ body parts, prints letters/numbers |
