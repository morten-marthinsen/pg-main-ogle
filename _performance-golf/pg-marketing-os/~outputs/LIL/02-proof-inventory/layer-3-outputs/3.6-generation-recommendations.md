### GEN-01: Define Guarantee/Warranty Terms

```yaml
recommendation_id: GEN-01
gap_id: GAP-01
category: risk_reversal
severity: HIGH
action: guarantee_definition
owner: Business Leadership
timeline: immediate (decision only)
effort: low
impact: high
impact_effort_score: 10.0
```

**Why critical:** RISK_REVERSAL is the weakest category (3.20 avg, only category rated WEAK). No guarantee = no purchase anxiety reduction. Defining terms is a business decision, not a content task.

**Recommended options:**

| Option | Description | Fit for LIL |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| **60-Day Satisfaction Guarantee** | Standard window for physical product evaluation | RECOMMENDED — gives families 2 months to see child engage |
| **90-Day Happiness Guarantee** | Extended window with emotional framing | Strong option — "If your little one doesn't love it" language |
| **30-Day Standard Return** | Minimum acceptable for e-commerce | Weak — too short for child engagement pattern |

**Recommended copy direction:**
> "If your little one doesn't light up every time they see their Lil' Legends set, send it back within 60 days for a full refund. No questions asked."

**Scoring impact:** Defining a strong guarantee could lift RSK category from 3.20 → 6.0+ and overall score from 73 → ~76.

### GEN-02: Beta Testing Program

```yaml
recommendation_id: GEN-02
gap_id: GAP-02
category: social
severity: HIGH
action: beta_testing_campaign
owner: Product / Creative Team
timeline: 4-6 weeks before launch (by April 15, 2026)
effort: medium
impact: very_high
impact_effort_score: 6.25
```

**Why critical:** LIL has ZERO product-specific testimonials. All 8 SOCIAL elements are proxy quotes from market research. Beta testing is the only way to generate authentic product social proof before launch.

**Execution plan:**

1. **Select 15-25 families** with children ages 2-5
   - Mix of golf-playing and non-golf families
   - Include Grant Horvat's family (built-in content)
   - Geographic diversity for photo variety
   - Request signed media release before shipping

2. **Ship beta units** 6+ weeks before launch
   - Include feedback card with guided questions
   - Request weekly photo/video updates
   - Provide dedicated email for sharing experiences

3. **Guided feedback questions:**
   - "What was your child's reaction when they first saw the set?"
   - "How long did they play with it on the first day?"
   - "Have you noticed any improvement in their swing/coordination?"
   - "Would you recommend this to another parent? Why?"
   - "What does your child call the clubs?" (organic, authentic language)

4. **Content harvest targets:**
   - 10+ written testimonials with specific observations
   - 5+ video clips of children using the product
   - 3+ before/after skill progression photos
   - 1+ "unboxing reaction" video

**Scoring impact:** Strong beta testimonials could lift SOCIAL from proxy-only to genuine product proof. Estimated category strength increase: +1.0-2.0 points.

### GEN-03: Grant Horvat Demo Video

```yaml
recommendation_id: GEN-03
gap_id: [GAP-02, GAP-05]
category: [social, demonstration]
severity: HIGH + LOW
action: spokesperson_demo_production
owner: Creative Team + Grant Horvat
timeline: 4-6 weeks before launch
effort: medium
impact: very_high
impact_effort_score: 6.25
```

**Why highest-leverage:** A single Grant Horvat video with his child fills THREE gaps simultaneously:
- **GAP-02 (SOCIAL):** Implicit product endorsement from trusted figure
- **GAP-05 (DEMONSTRATION):** Live product-in-action footage
- **AUTHORITY reinforcement:** Grant's credibility + paternal authenticity

**Video concept recommendations:**

| Concept | Description | Gaps Addressed |
|---------|-------------|----------------|
| **"First Swing"** | Grant introduces his child to golf using LIL set. Genuine reaction. | SOC + DEM + AUT |
| **"Why I Helped Design This"** | Grant explains his involvement, shows product to his child. | AUT + MEC + DEM |
| **"The Walk-In Test"** | Grant demonstrates Walk-In Frame — child walks up and swings without instruction. | MEC + DEM |
| **"Skills Mat Session"** | Grant and child use Skills Mat together. Fun, unscripted. | MEC + DEM + SOC |

**Recommended approach:** Film 2-3 concepts in one session. Use candid, unscripted moments. The authentic moments will be more powerful than polished production.

**Content derivatives:**
- Long-form YouTube video (Grant's channel — 1.6M subscribers)
- 15-30 second clips for social ads
- Still frames for e-comm product page
- Behind-the-scenes content for organic
- Quote pulls for testimonial sections

**Scoring impact:** Addresses 2 gaps simultaneously. Estimated overall score impact: +2-4 points.
