## Market Research Quotes as Proxy Social Proof

While no LIL-specific testimonials exist, Skill 01 Deep Research cataloged **1,169 verbatim quotes** from parents in the toddler golf market. These serve a different purpose than product testimonials:

### What VoC Quotes CAN Prove:
1. **The problem is real** — Parents confirm weight, frustration, broken sets
2. **The desire is strong** — Bonding/legacy language is emotionally powerful
3. **The market gap exists** — Parents describe exactly what LIL offers before LIL exists
4. **Serial purchase pattern** — Average parent tried 2-4 products (primed for "final answer")

### What VoC Quotes CANNOT Prove:
1. That LIL specifically works
2. That LIL's mechanisms deliver results
3. That LIL is worth the price
4. That kids respond to Focal Sphere or Walk-In Frame specifically
