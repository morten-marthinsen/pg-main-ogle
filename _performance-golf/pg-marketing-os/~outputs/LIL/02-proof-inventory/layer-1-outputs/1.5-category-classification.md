- **MECHANISM (14 elements, 20.6%)** — Appropriately rich. LIL's 4 unique mechanisms provide strong raw material.
- **AUTHORITY (14 elements, 20.6%)** — Grant + McGinley provide credibility scaffolding for mechanism claims.
- **DATA (20 elements, 29.4%)** — Highest count but many are specs/industry stats, not product results.
- **SOCIAL (8 elements, 11.8%)** — Expected critical gap for pre-launch. Layer 3 generation recommendations will address.
- **RISK_REVERSAL (2 elements, 2.9%)** — Must be defined before launch. Non-negotiable.

### New Product Classification Pattern

This inventory follows a predictable pattern for pre-launch products:
1. **MECHANISM-heavy** — product innovation IS the proof
2. **DATA from specs** — engineering numbers substitute for results data
3. **AUTHORITY from people** — inventor/spokesman credentials carry the load
4. **SOCIAL from market research** — proxy quotes validate the problem, not the product
5. **DEMONSTRATION from renders** — visual assets exist but no live usage footage
6. **RISK_REVERSAL absent** — business decision not yet made
