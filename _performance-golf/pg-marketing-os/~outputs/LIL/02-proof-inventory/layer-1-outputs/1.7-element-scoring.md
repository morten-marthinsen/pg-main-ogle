# 1.7 Element Scoring — Lil' Legends

## Scoring Protocol
- **Weights:** Specificity (0.25) + Credibility (0.25) + Relevance (0.20) + Novelty (0.15) + Emotional Impact (0.15)
- **Schwartz Stage:** 2→3 (mechanism proof primary — MECHANISM and AUTHORITY scores carry extra strategic weight)
- **Context:** Pre-launch product. No customer results data. Relevance scored against the core promise: "A developmental golf system that teaches toddlers through visual play."
- **Quality Tiers:** Strong (≥7.0) | Medium (4.0-6.9) | Weak (<4.0)
