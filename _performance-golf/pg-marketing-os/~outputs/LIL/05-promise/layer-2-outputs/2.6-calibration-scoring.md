## Dimension Analysis

### Strongest Dimensions Across All Candidates
- **D1 Proof Ceiling:** Average 8.8 — almost all promises stay within ceiling
- **D3 Mechanism Connection:** Average 8.5 — strong mechanism integration overall
- **D4 Differentiation:** Average 7.8 — mechanism-connected promises naturally differentiate

### Weakest Dimensions
- **D6 Emotional Depth:** Average 7.0 — technical/mechanism promises lack warmth
- **D7 Voice Fit:** Average 7.3 — some promises need Grant's voice injection
- **D2 Stage Fit:** Average 7.2 — emotional/aspirational promises don't fit Stage 3 alone

### Dimension Correlation Insight
**The strongest candidates score HIGH on BOTH mechanism connection AND emotional depth.** P-17 proves that a promise can be maximally mechanism-specific AND maximally emotional. This is the target: mechanism IN the emotion, not mechanism OR emotion.
