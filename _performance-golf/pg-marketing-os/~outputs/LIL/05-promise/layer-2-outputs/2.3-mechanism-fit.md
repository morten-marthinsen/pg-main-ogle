# 2.3 Mechanism Fit Verification — Lil' Legends

## Purpose

Verify each promise candidate is deliverable by the See & Swing System. A promise the mechanism can't deliver is a broken promise.

---

## Mechanism Capability Reference

```yaml
mechanism_capabilities:
  name: "See & Swing System"

  CAN_deliver:
    - "Visual alignment teaching through color matching (Focal Sphere)"
    - "Automatic setup positioning (Walk-In Frame)"
    - "Stance guidance through surface markers (Skills Mat)"
    - "Face angle awareness through visual cue (Face Forward)"
    - "Learning without verbal instruction"
    - "Play-based golf introduction for ages 2-5"
    - "Parent freed from coaching role"
    - "Portable practice anywhere (mat rolls up)"

  CANNOT_deliver:
    - "Full swing technique (only fundamentals: alignment, stance, setup, face angle)"
    - "Competitive golf performance"
    - "Skills beyond ages 2-5 developmental stage"
    - "Complete replacement of ALL instruction (ages 6+ will need coaching)"
    - "Guaranteed engagement or sustained interest"
    - "Specific skill timelines"
    - "Results comparable to professional instruction"
```
