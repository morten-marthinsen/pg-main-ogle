# 2.2 Schwartz Stage Calibration — Lil' Legends

## Purpose

Calibrate all 24 promise candidates for Stage 2→3 market sophistication. At Stage 3, promise alone won't sell — promise + mechanism credibility is the strategy.

---

## Stage 2→3 Calibration Framework

```yaml
schwartz_calibration:
  current_stage: "2→3"
  market_reality:
    stage_2_signals: "Everyone claims 'lightweight' and 'sized for kids' — these are saturated"
    stage_3_signals: "Parents asking for gram weights, patented mechanisms, HOW it teaches"

  strategy:
    position: "Enter as Stage 3 brand in Stage 2 market"
    promise_role: "Promise + Mechanism — mechanism MAKES promise credible"
    implication: "Promise must be INSEPARABLE from See & Swing System"

  calibration_rules:
    - "Promise must explicitly reference or imply the mechanism"
    - "Bold claims require mechanism backing, not just assertion"
    - "Vague benefit promises (Stage 1-2 energy) get downgraded"
    - "Mechanism-connected promises get upgraded"
    - "Specificity beats boldness at Stage 3"

  saturated_claims_to_avoid:
    - "lightweight" (every competitor)
    - "sized for kids" (table stakes)
    - "fun" (generic — everyone says this)
    - "real clubs not toys" (Charlie Golf, TLG already claim this)
    - "grows with your child" (US Kids standard)

  available_whitespace:
    - "Self-learning / no coaching needed" (ZERO competitors)
    - "Equipment teaches through design" (ZERO competitors)
    - "Visual-tactile learning system" (ZERO competitors)
    - "Shape sorter principle for golf" (ZERO competitors)
    - "Golf buddy, not golf coach" (ZERO competitors)
```
