## Raw Promise Candidates

### Archetype A Seeds: Golf Buddy Promise (Identity/Bonding)

**P-01:** "Share golf with your kid — starting now, not 'when they're older.'"
- Seed: Dream language ("something we can do together") + developmental window
- Energy: Hope — possibility unlocked NOW

**P-02:** "Give your kid the game your dad gave you."
- Seed: Grant's origin story (AUT-03) + "My dad gave me this game" (research #1 legacy phrase)
- Energy: Nostalgia + mission + generational continuity

**P-03:** "Golf with your kid for the first time — and love every second of it."
- Seed: "Nothing beats golfing with your family" + "some of my favorite memories as a kid were trips to the range"
- Energy: Warm anticipation — pure hope frame

**P-04:** "From frustrated coach to golf buddy — in one afternoon."
- Seed: Transformation pair theme (teaching difficulty → golf buddy dream)
- Energy: Speed + relief + identity shift

### Archetype B Seeds: Seven Words Promise (Ease/Speed)

**P-05:** "Seven words replace every golf lesson a 2-year-old will ever need."
- Seed: MEC-13 knockout ("Match the yellow ball to the white ball")
- Energy: Radical simplicity — pattern interrupt

**P-06:** "Your kid learns alignment in the time it takes to match two colors."
- Seed: Focal Sphere mechanism + shape sorter speed
- Energy: Speed + specificity — concrete mental picture

**P-07:** "'Match the yellow ball to the white ball.' That's it. That's the whole lesson."
- Seed: Direct knockout proof AS the promise
- Energy: Disarming simplicity

### Archetype C Seeds: Equipment Teaches Promise (Capability)

**P-08:** "The first golf set where the equipment teaches and the parent just plays."
- Seed: "The equipment teaches. Your kid discovers. You just play."
- Energy: Capability — new category of product

**P-09:** "A golf system that teaches through shapes, colors, and touch — no words needed."
- Seed: See & Swing System core principle
- Energy: Mechanism-forward capability promise

**P-10:** "The only toddler golf system where every piece teaches one fundamental — automatically."
- Seed: Component architecture (4 pieces, each teaches one thing)
- Energy: Uniqueness + systematic proof

**P-11:** "The first golf equipment designed for how 2-5 year olds actually learn."
- Seed: Developmental science backing + competitive gap (DAT-19)
- Energy: Category creation — "designed for how they learn" reframes everything

### Archetype D Seeds: No Coaching Promise (Relief)

**P-12:** "Your toddler learns golf fundamentals through play — no coaching, no lessons, no frustration."
- Seed: "three swings later, chasing the dog" → relief from that cycle
- Energy: Triple relief — three negatives removed

**P-13:** "No coaching. No corrections. No 'hold it like this.' Just play."
- Seed: Root cause copy block + "dude, you don't even have to teach them"
- Energy: Freedom — liberation from the coaching burden

**P-14:** "Never stand behind your toddler saying 'hold it like this, buddy' again."
- Seed: Root cause core explanation + prevention framing
- Energy: Prevention + relief — specific moment they'll never repeat

**P-15:** "Your kid's first real golf swings — without a single correction from you."
- Seed: "He can actually hit the ball" (success metric) + no coaching needed
- Energy: Capability + relief — the result WITHOUT the work

### Archetype E Seeds: Lifetime Together Promise (Emotional)

**P-16:** "Start the lifetime of golf together while their brain is built to learn it fastest."
- Seed: Developmental window + "game for life" language + "decades to come"
- Energy: Hope + gentle urgency (not fear — hope market)

**P-17:** "Watch your 3-year-old pick up a golf club and figure it out — all on their own."
- Seed: "obsessed" / "refuses to stop" / discovery moment
- Energy: Parental awe — visceral emotional moment

**P-18:** "From 'maybe next year' to 'they won't stop swinging.'"
- Seed: Transformation pair — lost interest → obsession
- Energy: Before/after without claiming transformation-level outcome

### Additional Seeds: Cross-Type Exploration

**P-19:** "A shape sorter for golf. Your kid learns the same way — through design, not coaching."
- Seed: Shape sorter analogy (Arena-validated 8.90) AS the promise
- Energy: Instant intuition — every parent already trusts this learning model

**P-20:** "Your toddler doesn't need a golf lesson. They need equipment that teaches through play."
- Seed: Root cause reframe — the insight IS the promise
- Energy: Reframe — shifts what parents think they need

**P-21:** "A golf system that speaks the language your toddler's brain already understands."
- Seed: Mechanism copy asset — "language their brain understands"
- Energy: Science-conceptual without being clinical

**P-22:** "The See & Swing System: see the colors, match the shapes, swing the club."
- Seed: Mechanism name + operating principle as sequential promise
- Energy: Step-by-step simplicity — the promise is the process
