# 1.2 Specificity Enhancement — Lil' Legends

## Purpose

Add timeframes, quantities, qualifiers, and concrete details to raw promise candidates. Vague promises fail — specificity creates mental pictures and believability.

---

## Specificity Assessment & Enhancement

### High Specificity (Already Concrete — Minor Polish)

| ID | Raw Promise | Specificity Elements | Enhancement |
|----|------------|---------------------|-------------|
| P-05 | "Seven words replace every golf lesson a 2-year-old will ever need." | Number (7), age (2), quantifier (every) | Already strong. Add mechanism name: "Seven words — and the See & Swing System — replace every golf lesson a 2-year-old will ever need." |
