### Needs Integration (Add customer vocabulary)

| ID | Promise | Enhancement | Customer Language Added |
|----|---------|-------------|----------------------|
| P-01 | "Share golf with your kid starting at age 2..." | "Finally do the thing you've been dreaming about — share golf with your kid. Starting at age 2." | "dreaming about" (hope language) |

    - P-07  # "That's it. That's the whole lesson." — Classic Grant cadence

  needs_voice_adjustment:

  anti_voice_violations: 0
