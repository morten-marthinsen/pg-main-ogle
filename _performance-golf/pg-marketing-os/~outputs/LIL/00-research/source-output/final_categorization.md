## Methodology

**Tier 1 — Physical Bucket:** Each quote is assigned ONE primary bucket from the following:
- **PAIN** — Frustration, difficulty, disappointment parents experience
- **HOPE** — Dreams, aspirations, bonding, generational themes, pride
- **ROOT_CAUSE** — Why current solutions fail; developmental/design gaps
- **SOLUTIONS_TRIED** — What parents have already tried
- **COMPETITOR_MECHANISM** — How competitor products claim to solve the problem
- **VILLAIN** — What parents blame (overbearing culture, cost, gatekeeping)
- **TRANSFORMATION** — Before/after shifts, breakthrough moments
- **OBJECTION** — Skepticism, hesitation, reasons NOT to buy/act

**Tier 2 — Emotional Tags:** Each quote receives 1-3 emotional descriptors from:
frustration, longing, pride, guilt, fear, joy, anger, hope, nostalgia, overwhelm, relief, excitement, skepticism, disappointment, determination, love, anxiety, satisfaction, confusion, desperation

**Key Phrase:** The most copy-relevant 3-5 words from each quote.
