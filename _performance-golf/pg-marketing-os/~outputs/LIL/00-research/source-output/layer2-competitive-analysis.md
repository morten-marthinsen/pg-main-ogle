### 1. TOT CLUBS

| Field | Details |

| **Target Age** | Walking to ~5-6 years old |

| **Strengths** | Ultra-lightweight (140-180g, less than half a pound). Left-handed versions available. Eight color combinations. Golf Pride grip upgrade option. Four sizes grow with child. Affordable at $75. Founder story resonates (dad-built prototypes). Featured on MyGolfSpy. |
| **Weaknesses** | Cannot hit real golf balls. Plastic heads lack "real club" feel. No training system or instructional component. No bag included. Limited brand awareness outside golf forums. |

**Threat Level: HIGH.** Tot Clubs is the closest philosophical competitor -- purpose-built for toddlers by a dad, lightweight-first design, priced accessibly. Their $75 price point could make LIL look expensive by comparison. Their MyGolfSpy coverage gives them credibility.
