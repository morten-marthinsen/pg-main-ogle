## TOP 5 EDUCATIONAL PAIRS (Sample from educational_pairs.md)

### 1. Weight Is Necessary But Not Sufficient
**Misconception:** "Just make clubs lighter"
**WHY:** Even the lightest US Kids clubs are too heavy for toddlers [EXP2-RC-002]
**HOW:** 170g is the sweet spot — light enough to swing, heavy enough to feel [EXP2-CM-003]
**Mechanism:** Weight Design

### 2. Toddlers Can't Process Verbal Instructions
**Misconception:** "Just teach them proper grip/stance"
**WHY:** Piaget preoperational stage — abstract instruction doesn't land [CC research]
**HOW:** Visual matching (shapes, colors) is how 2-5 year olds learn [B-12]
**Mechanism:** Focal Sphere

### 3. Plastic Toys Create Negative Associations
**Misconception:** "Start with a cheap set to see if they like it"
**WHY:** Cheap sets break, fail to make contact, teach frustration not golf [A-079, YF-002]
**HOW:** First impression determines whether the "golf bug" takes hold [EXP2-RC-004]
**Mechanism:** System Design

### 4. Cut-Down Clubs Are Wrong Geometry
**Misconception:** "Just cut down an old set"
**WHY:** Wrong swing weight, lie angle, grip — "like trying to swing a dumbbell" [R3-P-015]
**HOW:** Purpose-built from scratch with toddler-correct geometry [R3-RC-053]
**Mechanism:** Face Forward + Weight Design

### 5. Fun and Learning Are the Same Thing
**Misconception:** "Play time and practice time are different"
**WHY:** Structured instruction kills joy for toddlers [EXP2-P-058]
**HOW:** "Playtime or practice? With the right clubs... it's both." [EXP2-H-096]
**Mechanism:** Skills Mat + Focal Sphere
