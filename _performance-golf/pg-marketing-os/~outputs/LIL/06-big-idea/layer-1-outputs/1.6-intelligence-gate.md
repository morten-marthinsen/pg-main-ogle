### Key Intelligence Findings

1. **Whitespace is massive.** Self-teaching golf mechanism, visual-tactile learning, parent role transformation — all completely uncontested territory. The Big Idea should exploit whitespace directly.

2. **FSSIT-1 is the strongest candidate.** "You don't have to teach them. At all." — NR potential 9/10, explains past failures, removes blame, opens curiosity loop. Every Big Idea concept should connect to this FSSIT.

3. **The mechanism IS the differentiator.** But the Big Idea should be the INSIGHT the mechanism makes possible, not the mechanism name itself. "See & Swing System" is the answer. "You don't have to teach them" is the Big Idea.

4. **Emotional sequence matters.** Curiosity -> Relief -> Hope -> Pride. Big Ideas that open on hope are warm but undifferentiated. Big Ideas that open on curiosity/surprise are distinctive and pull the reader in.

5. **Multiple gaps reinforce each other.** Teaching Gap + Simplicity Gap + Coaching Burden Gap = a layered Big Idea that surprises (teaching gap), proves itself (simplicity gap), and lands emotionally (coaching burden/identity).
