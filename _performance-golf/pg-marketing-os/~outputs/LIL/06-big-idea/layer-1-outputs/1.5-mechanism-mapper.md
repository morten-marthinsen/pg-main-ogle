# 1.5 Mechanism Mapper — Lil' Legends Big Idea

## How the Mechanism Maps to Big Idea Types

### The See & Swing System as Big Idea Engine

The See & Swing System is the core strategic asset for Big Idea generation. It is:
- **Named** (ownable, searchable, brandable)
- **Demonstrable** (MEC-13 knockout proof — "match yellow to white")
- **Analogizable** (shape sorter — instantly understood)
- **Differentiating** (zero competitors have it — DAT-19)
- **Science-backed** (Piaget, AAP — AUT-11)
- **Simple** (seven words is the whole lesson)

### Mechanism-to-Big-Idea Type Mapping

**MECHANISM Type (Primary Recommendation)**
- The See & Swing System IS the star
- Pattern: "The [mechanism] that [promise]"
- LIL version: "The See & Swing System — the only toddler golf system where the equipment teaches"
- Fit score: 9/10 — mechanism is highly differentiated (D6: 9), memorable name, Schwartz Stage 2->3

**DISCOVERY Type (Secondary)**
- The mechanism framed as a "discovery" or "breakthrough"
- Pattern: "[Authority] discovers that [mechanism insight]"
- LIL version: "What developmental science has known for 80 years — and no golf company has ever used"
- Fit score: 7/10 — science backing is strong but "discovery" framing could feel clinical

**IDENTITY Type (Tertiary)**
- The mechanism enables an identity transformation
- Pattern: "For [identity] who [struggle], the [mechanism] lets you [new identity]"
- LIL version: "The golf system that turns you from coach to buddy"
- Fit score: 7/10 — emotionally resonant but mechanism is supporting, not starring

**ENEMY Type (Adapted for Hope Market)**
- The "enemy" is the design assumption, not a competitor
- Pattern: "The real reason [problem] — [obstacle] — and the [mechanism] that solves it"
- LIL version: "Every kids' club was designed for coaching. Toddlers can't learn from coaching. Until See & Swing."
- Fit score: 6/10 — viable but must be carefully framed to stay hope-positive

**TRANSFORMATION Type (Not Recommended)**
- Before/after story
- Problem: No testimonials (pre-launch). No transformation data.
- Fit score: 4/10 — proof gap makes this indefensible

**PROPHECY Type (Not Recommended)**
- Prediction/urgency framing
- Problem: Incompatible with branded initiative and hope market
- Fit score: 2/10 — directly conflicts with campaign constraints

### Mechanism Components as Big Idea Fuel

| Component | Big Idea Potential | Why |
|-----------|-------------------|-----|
| "Seven words" knockout | HIGHEST | Self-demonstrating proof. Saying it IS proving it. |
| Shape sorter analogy | HIGH | Bridge from known to new. Instant comprehension. |
| "Equipment teaches, you play" | HIGH | Role reversal. Identity shift. Emotional payoff. |
| See & Swing System name | MODERATE | Ownable brand asset but not a Big Idea by itself |
| Walk-in frame detail | LOW | Feature detail, not Big Idea level |
| Color-matching ball detail | MODERATE | "Yellow matches white" is proof, not Big Idea |

### Strategic Recommendation

The mechanism should be CENTRAL to the Big Idea but the Big Idea should not BE the mechanism name. The Big Idea is the INSIGHT that the mechanism makes possible:

- "You don't have to teach them" (insight enabled by mechanism)
- "Seven words — that's the whole lesson" (proof of the mechanism's simplicity)
- "A shape sorter for golf" (analogy that makes the mechanism intuitive)
- "Golf buddy, not golf coach" (identity enabled by the mechanism)

The See & Swing System is the HOW. The Big Idea is the SO WHAT.
