**Fun + Fundamentals.** The Big Idea is about the most fun way for a toddler to learn the fundamentals of golf. Not "you don't have to teach them" (relief/contrarian) but "here's the most fun way to give them the game" (hope/aspiration).

### STILL WANTED
- Developmental science argument (WHY it's fun: because it works with how toddlers naturally learn)
- Grant's dad story (he made it fun by instinct; See & Swing does it by design)
- "The one thing we're not taught as parents" — discovery element, but positive/helpful, not contrarian
- Seven words, shape sorter, golf buddy = supporting elements

### KEY SHIFT
From: "you don't have to teach them" (relief from burden)
To: "here's the most fun way to give them the game" (excitement about possibility)

The developmental science is now the REASON it's fun (because it matches how toddlers already learn = play, shapes, colors), not the reason you shouldn't coach.

### CONSTRAINTS (unchanged)
- Hope market: no fear, no guilt, no competitive attack
- Soul.md voice: Grant at the range, excited to share something
- Proof ceiling: Level 4
- No engineering names
- No DR urgency
