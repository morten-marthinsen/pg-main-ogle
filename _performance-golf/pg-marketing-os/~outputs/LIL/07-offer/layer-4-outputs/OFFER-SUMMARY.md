## 2. Core Offer

### What They're Buying
**The Lil' Legends Starter Set** — a 3-club toddler golf system (ages 2-5) with integrated Skills Mat and carry bag. Every component teaches one golf fundamental through shapes and colors. No verbal coaching needed. The parent's role: golf buddy, not golf coach.

### Dream Outcome
Seven words: "Match the yellow ball to the white ball." The See & Swing System handles the rest — alignment, stance, setup — all through shapes and colors. No lesson. Just a little legend who won't stop swinging. Golf buddy, not golf coach.

### Positioning
**System, not product.** This is a NEW CATEGORY — "developmental golf system." Not toy clubs. Not junior equipment. Not a teaching aid. A self-teaching system where the equipment IS the teacher.

### Core Deliverables

| # | Component | What It Teaches | Stated Value |
|---|-----------|----------------|-------------|
| 1 | **3 See & Swing Clubs** (Driver, Iron, Putter) | Setup, alignment, stance, face angle through Focal Sphere + Walk-In Frame + Face Forward | $199 |
| 2 | **Skills Mat** (8 visual guides) | Stance, ball position, alignment through visual markers | $49 |
| 3 | **Carry Bag** | Ownership, portability — child carries their own clubs | $29 |

### Competitive Position
- 4 mechanisms with ZERO competitive overlap (Focal Sphere, Walk-In Frame, Skills Mat, Face Forward)
- Zero competitors have visual-tactile teaching mechanisms
- Zero competitors have integrated teaching surfaces
- Differentiation score: 9.0/10
