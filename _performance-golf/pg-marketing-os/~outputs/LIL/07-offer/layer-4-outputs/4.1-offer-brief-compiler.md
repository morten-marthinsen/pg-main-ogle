### What It Is
A 3-club toddler golf system with integrated Skills Mat and carry bag. Every component teaches one golf fundamental through shapes and colors — no verbal coaching needed. The parent's role: golf buddy, not golf coach.

### What It Is NOT
- NOT toy golf clubs (Tier 3 plastic)
- NOT junior equipment (adult clubs shrunk down)
- NOT a teaching aid (requires no teaching)
- NOT a coaching tool (eliminates the coaching role)

### Positioning
"The only toddler golf system where the equipment teaches and you just play." This is a NEW CATEGORY — "developmental golf system" — above toys and below junior equipment. No competitor occupies this position.

## 2. CORE DELIVERABLES

### Deliverable 1: Three See & Swing Clubs

| Component | What It Teaches | Teaching Mechanism |
|-----------|----------------|-------------------|
| **Driver** | Setup position + alignment | Walk-In Frame (stands upright, child walks in) + Focal Sphere (yellow matches white) |
| **Iron/Wedge** | Alignment + stance | Focal Sphere (color matching) + Skills Mat stance markers |
| **Putter** | Face angle + alignment | Face Forward (visual face cue) + Focal Sphere |

**Key specs:** Ultra-lightweight, age-appropriate sizing (2-5), real golf fundamentals (grip, stance, alignment, swing path).

**Stated value:** $199

### Deliverable 2: Skills Mat (8 Visual Training Guides)

- Stance markers show where to stand
- Ball position guides show where to place the ball
- Alignment aids show where to aim
- Rolls up, fits in the carry bag
- Creates a practice station anywhere — backyard, garage, living room, range

**Key differentiator:** Zero competitors have an integrated teaching surface. The Skills Mat is what turns 3 clubs into a system.

**Stated value:** $49

### Deliverable 3: Carry Bag

- Child-sized — toddler carries it on their own
- Fits all 3 clubs + rolled-up Skills Mat
- Creates the "walking to the range together" moment

**Stated value:** $29
