## Problem-Solution Map (16 Problems Mapped)

### BEFORE Purchase — Problems Getting Started

| # | Problem | Value Driver | Solution | Delivery Vehicle |
|---|---------|-------------|----------|-----------------|
| 1 | **"Plastic sets break in a week"** — Parents have bought 2-4 cheap sets that fall apart. Heads pop off, shafts snap, stickers peel. | Likelihood (they'll fail again) | Real clubs built to survive toddler use. Metal construction that handles real balls and real swings without breaking. | Core product: 3 durable clubs designed for toddler force patterns |
| 2 | **"Metal clubs are too heavy"** — Adult-shrunk clubs are 2-3x too heavy. "Like swinging a sledgehammer." 25+ weight mentions in research. | Effort (too hard for kid) | Ultra-lightweight construction matched to toddler strength. Each club designed for the weight a 2-5 year old can actually swing. | Core product: weight-optimized clubs (target: match/beat Tot Clubs 140-180g) |
| 3 | **"I don't know how to teach a toddler golf"** — Parent feels unqualified. Even golf-playing parents don't know how to instruct a 2-year-old. | Effort (parent burden) | The equipment teaches — parent doesn't need to coach. "Match the yellow ball to the white ball" is the entire instruction. | See & Swing System: visual-tactile teaching built into every component |
| 4 | **"I don't want to be the pushy sports parent"** — #1 emotional barrier. Fear of forcing it, killing the joy, creating negative associations. | Dream Outcome (fear of ruining it) | No coaching, no corrections, no "hold it like this." The system is designed so the parent's only job is to play alongside their kid. | Identity reframe: "Golf buddy, not golf coach" + system design philosophy |
| 5 | **"Is 2 too young for golf?"** — Uncertainty about starting age. "Just wait till they're older" is the default advice. | Likelihood (wrong timing) | Developmental science: ages 2-5 is the optimal window. Toddlers learn through shapes, colors, and play — this system is designed for exactly that developmental stage. | Skills Mat + mechanism design matched to preoperational stage (Piaget) |
