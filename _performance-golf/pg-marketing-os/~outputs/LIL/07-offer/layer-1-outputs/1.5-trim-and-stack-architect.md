## Trim & Stack Analysis

### Hormozi Trim Framework
For each solution, evaluate on two axes:
- **Value to Customer** (LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH)
- **Cost to Deliver** (LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH)

**Keep:** High Value + Low Cost
**Consider:** High Value + High Cost (if margin allows)
**Trim:** Low Value + Any Cost

## Final Stack Architecture

### CORE OFFER (What They Buy)

```
THE LIL' LEGENDS START SET
├── 1. Driver (with Walk-In Frame + Focal Sphere)
├── 2. Iron/Wedge (with Focal Sphere)
├── 3. Putter (with Face Forward + Focal Sphere)
├── 4. Skills Mat (8 visual training guides)
└── 5. Carry Bag (child-sized, fits mat + clubs)
```

**Positioning:** "The See & Swing System" — not 5 separate products, ONE system where every piece teaches one thing through shapes and colors.

**Stated Value:** Sum of individually-priced components (see 2.6 Price Architect for value calculations)

### BONUS STACK (What They Get Free)

```
BONUSES (included with purchase)
├── Bonus 1: PG1 App — 30-Day Free Trial
│   ├── Objection addressed: #15 (engagement drops), #16 (what's next)
│   ├── Stated value: $29.99/month (first month free)
│   └── Cost to deliver: ZERO marginal
│
├── Bonus 2: "First Swings" Quick-Start Card
│   ├── Objection addressed: #3 (don't know how to start), #6 (outgrow it)
│   ├── Stated value: Included (not separately priced — avoids undermining "no instruction" message)
│   ├── Content: 5 play-based activities for the first session + milestone guide ages 2-5
│   └── Cost to deliver: Printing cost (minimal) or digital
│
└── Bonus 3: Grant Horvat Welcome Video (Digital)
    ├── Objection addressed: #4 (pushy parent fear), #10 (will my kid like it)
    ├── Stated value: Exclusive (not sold separately)
    ├── Content: Grant's personal message to new Lil' Legends families — "here's how my dad did it"
    └── Cost to deliver: One-time production, zero marginal distribution
```

## Trimmed Elements (Excluded with Rationale)

| Element | Why Trimmed | Risk of Excluding |
|---------|-------------|-------------------|
| 4th club | Adds COGS, marginal value, complicates "3 clubs = Drive, Hit, Putt" simplicity | None — 3 clubs is cleaner |
| Physical instruction booklet | Contradicts "no instruction needed" premise. Would undermine mechanism credibility. | None — the product IS the instruction |
| Online course / video curriculum | Over-complicates. A full course says "this is hard." 7 words says "this is easy." | Low — PG1 app fills this role lightly |
| Branded golf balls | Undifferentiated commodity. Doesn't solve a mapped problem. | Low — parents have balls or can buy any |
| Foam practice balls | Low cost but doesn't solve a mapped problem strongly enough to include in core. Could be added as future promotional bonus. | Very low — nice-to-have, not need-to-have |
