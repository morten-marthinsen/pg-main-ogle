The face falls toward square, not away from it.

## ARENA WINNER SELECTION

### Winner: Hybrid 1 -- "The Golfer's Reframe"

**Score:** 9.5/10 composite

**Rationale:**

1. **Experiential entry:** Opens with the felt experience every golfer recognizes (four-footer, tight hands, right-edge miss). No abstraction. No preamble. You're IN the moment immediately.

2. **The anchor phrase:** "That miss wasn't mental. It was mechanical." This is the line. It's the core reframe in 8 words. It subverts the dominant belief ("putting is mental") with a specific, provable counter-claim ("it's mechanical"). It's quotable. It's repeatable. A golfer could say this to a friend.

3. **Layered construction:** The expression builds from experience (the miss) to physics (weight behind shaft) to cliche subversion ("the arrow was bent") to carousel validation (every putter, same flaw) to resolution (move the weight forward). Each line earns the next.

4. **Soul.md compliance:** Quiet power throughout. No hype language. No exclamation marks. No "But wait." The confidence comes from the truth of the physics, not from the energy of the writing.

5. **Mechanism path:** "Move the weight forward" points directly to Forward Weighting without naming the product. Creates demand for the mechanism before introducing the mechanism.

6. **FSSIT echo:** Directly validates ELG-01 (The Technique Deflection) and ELG-04 (Confidence Attribution Error) -- the two strongest latent audience emotions.

**Three-part structure of the winning expression:**
- **What they think:** "That miss was mental. I steered it. I lost my nerve."
- **What's real:** "The weight behind the shaft pulled the face open. Under pressure, your grip tightened and the compensation failed. The miss was mechanical."
- **Why nothing worked:** "Every putter had the weight in the same place. That's why they all missed the same way."

**Primary anchor phrase:** "That miss wasn't mental. It was mechanical."
**Secondary anchor phrase:** "The arrow has been bent this whole time."
