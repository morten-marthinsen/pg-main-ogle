## Step 2: Finding the Common Denominator

**Question: What single dysfunction could produce ALL 14 symptoms?**

|---------|-----------------------------------------------|

**CONVERGENCE IDENTIFIED:** Every symptom traces back to the same underlying physical reality: **the putter's center of gravity sits behind the shaft axis, creating a gravitational force that drifts the face open during the stroke. The golfer must compensate for this force with their hands. The compensation is imperfect, variable, and degrades under pressure.**
