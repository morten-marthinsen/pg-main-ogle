## ROOT CAUSE — Explanations of zero torque, lie angle balance, face mechanics

**Bucket:** Root Cause
**Source:** GolfWRX
**URL:** https://forums.golfwrx.com/topic/2028433-lab%E2%80%99s-%E2%80%9Ctrue%E2%80%9D-zero-torque-vs-toe-up-putters/
**Quote:** "In putting, torque is the force that causes a putter's head to rotate around the axis of the shaft during a stroke. Factors which influence torque include a putter head's weight, its balance, the hosel mechanism, the center of mass, etc. When making a putting stroke, torque will act to rotate the putter face open or closed relative to the stroke path. Zero Torque putters seek to reduce or eliminate this effect."
