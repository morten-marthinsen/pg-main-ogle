## Strategic Foundation

**Market:** Premium putters, $300-500. Schwartz Stage 4 (Mechanism Fatigue). Audience has absorbed MOI, CNC milling, zero torque, AI face claims. Skeptical of mechanism claims but still searching.

**Avatar:** The Frustrated Ball-Striker Who Bleeds Strokes on the Green. 5-15 handicap. Strikes like scratch, putts like 12. Hits greens, shoots 82. Identity: "I'm a good ball-striker who can't putt." Has been through 5-20+ putters on the carousel.

**Root Cause:** Rearward CG creates face drift. Hands compensate. Pressure degrades compensation. Every putter shared this flaw. That miss wasn't mental. It was mechanical.

**Villain:** The conventional design paradigm — rearward CG. Soft villain (physics flaw, not a person). Golf's deepest belief ("it's the archer, not the arrow") shielded the flaw from scrutiny.

**Mechanism Hierarchy:**
1. Forward Weighting (hero) — 75%+ weight in front 25%
2. Face Down Balance (proof) — face sits down on flat surface
3. 74-Degree Lie Angle (supporting) — reduces stroke arc

**Dual Promise:** Technology eliminates face drift + Design assists natural stroke. No other putter does both.
