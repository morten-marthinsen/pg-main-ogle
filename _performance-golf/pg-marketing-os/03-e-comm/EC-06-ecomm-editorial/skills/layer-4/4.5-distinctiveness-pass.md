# Microskill 4.5: Distinctiveness Pass (E-Commerce)

**Layer:** 4 (Fix Application)
**Purpose:** Detect whether editorial cleanup has drifted e-comm copy toward generic product page language
**Sequence:** Runs AFTER quality scoring (4.1), feature consistency (4.2), and output packaging (4.3)

---

## WHY THIS PASS EXISTS

E-comm editorial focuses on consistency and scanability — both push copy toward safe, interchangeable product page language. This pass checks whether the campaign's distinctive voice survived cleanup.

**References:**
- `~system/humanization-specimens/SIGNATURE-MOVES.md` (E-Commerce section)
- `~system/humanization-specimens/engine-packs/ecomm-pack.md`
- Campaign Soul.md
