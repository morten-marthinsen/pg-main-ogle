### Selection Priority for This Skill

Since this skill generates HERO SECTIONS (headlines, subheads, value props, carousel copy), prioritize specimens that demonstrate each writer's approach to **opening hooks, headline craft, and compressed persuasion**. The hero section is the ecom equivalent of the first 3 seconds of a sales letter — where voice matters most because every word is under maximum scrutiny.

Hero copy is the most compressed form of persuasion on an ecom page. Headlines are 5-12 words. Subheads are 10-20 words. Value props are 15-30 words. At this compression level, voice calibration from specimens is critical — the difference between a generic "Discover the Power of..." and a Schwartz-level "The one golf club that adds 23 yards without changing your swing" is entirely about internalized voice patterns from actual specimens.

## E-Comm Hero Section Specimen Focus

When loading specimens for hero section Arena, instruct each persona to pay special attention to:

1. **Opening hooks** — How does the writer STOP the reader in the first line?
2. **Benefit compression** — How do they compress a complex promise into 5-12 words?
3. **Subhead expansion** — How does the second line ADD to the first without repeating?
4. **Value proposition clarity** — How do they answer "why this, why now" concisely?
