# EC-02 — Hero & Value Prop Writer

**Pipeline Position:** Third skill in E-Commerce Copy Engine pipeline. Executes after EC-00 (Strategist) and EC-01 (Feature Naming). Feeds EC-05 (Assembly).

---

## PURPOSE

Write the above-fold hero section: headline (5-12 words), subhead (10-20 words), value proposition (15-30 words), hero CTA (3-5 words), 10-thumbnail hero story architecture copy (NLS framework), and product highlights TLDR (top 3-5 quick answers, 50-100 words total).

The hero has ONE job: stop the scroll and earn the scroll-down. No feature stacks, no proof cascades, no story — just the promise and one visual proof point.

**Success Criteria:**
- Headline stops the scroll (5-12 words, clear product promise)
- Subhead adds specificity without repeating headline
- Value proposition answers "why this product, why now" in 15-30 words
- Hero CTA is action-oriented (3-5 words)
- 10-thumbnail copy provides hero carousel story architecture
- Product highlights TLDR gives top 3-5 quick answers
- Hero feature from EC-01 is prominently featured
