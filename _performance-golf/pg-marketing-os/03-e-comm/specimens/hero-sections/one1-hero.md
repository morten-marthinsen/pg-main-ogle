## DUAL HERO: PDP + Sales Page Variants

The ONE.1 has TWO hero sections — a PDP variant (Section 1A) and a Sales Page variant (Section 1B). This is a specimen of how the SAME product adapts its hero to different page types.

---

## Section 1A: PDP Hero

### Pre-Head
**"NEVER. CHUNK. AGAIN."**
- 3 words, all caps, periods between each word
- Format: Staccato Promise
- This is a micro-headline that functions as a pre-head — it scrolls stops BEFORE the product title

### Product Title
**"ONE.1 Auto-Correct Wedge"**
- Product name IS the value prop (see feature naming specimen)

### Tagline
**"The only premium wedge with an Auto-Glide Sole that glides through the ground instead of digging into it... even on your 'bad swings.'"**
- Word count: 26
- Formula: Only Claim + Named Mechanism + Benefit + Safety Valve
- "even on your 'bad swings'" — the safety valve that earns permission to believe

### Offer Box (ATF)
- Configuration selectors (Hand, Loft)
- ONE.1 "Auto-Correct" Wedge
- Fast & Free Shipping
- 365-Day "No Dig, No Doubt" Demo Period
- PG1 App (toggle-based membership)
- Member's Only Pricing (toggle-based)

### CTA
**"GET MY ONE.1 WEDGE NOW"**
- Format: Possessive + Product + Urgency
- "MY" creates ownership before purchase
- 6 words

### Trust Elements
- Named guarantee: "No Dig, No Doubt" Demo Period
- 365-Day duration

## Section 1B: Sales Page Hero

### Pre-Headline
**"NEVER. CHUNK. AGAIN."** (same as PDP)

### Headline
**"New: 'Auto-Correct' Wedge Glides Through Any Lie"**
- Word count: 8 (excluding "New:")
- Formula: Novelty Signal + Named Product + Core Benefit
- "New:" is a Schwartz Stage 1 signal — works because this is genuinely new to market
- The headline IS the product name + the dominant benefit in one line

### Sub-Headline
**"The only premium wedge with an Auto-Glide Sole that glides through the ground instead of digging into it... even on your 'bad swings.'"**
- Same as PDP tagline — shared across both variants

### Hero Visual
**[Auto-Glide Sole hero animation]**
- Animation, not static image — shows the sole in motion
- The visual DEMONSTRATES the feature, not just displays the product

### Benefit Stack (5 bullets)
1. "Eliminate fat shots and chunks — even when you mis-hit it"
2. "Break the 'yips' cycle — commit without flinching"

## Analysis

### PDP vs. Sales Page Differences

| Element | PDP (1A) | Sales Page (1B) |
|---------|----------|-----------------|
| Pre-head | Same | Same |
| Product title vs. headline | Product title (descriptive) | Headline (persuasive) |
| Tagline vs. subhead | Same text | Same text |
| Benefit stack | Not present | 5 bullets |
| Offer box | ATF (full pricing/config) | Not ATF (below) |
| Hero visual | Product gallery | Animation |

**Key Insight:** The PDP hero is TRANSACTIONAL (title, config, price, buy). The Sales Page hero is PERSUASIVE (headline, benefits, visual demo, then buy). Same product, different buyer intent.

### What Works
- **"NEVER. CHUNK. AGAIN."** — Staccato pre-head is the scroll-stopper. Three words name the #1 pain and resolve it.
- **Product name = headline** — "Auto-Correct Wedge" is both a name and a value prop, eliminating the need for a separate persuasive headline
- **Named guarantee echoes product language** — "No Dig, No Doubt" mirrors the "Glides vs. Digs" binary
- **Toggle-based offer box** — PG1 membership pricing is toggled ON by default, with dynamic copy changes

### What's Missing (vs. NLS Framework)
- No 10-thumbnail hero carousel (both variants)
- No product highlights TLDR (covered by benefit stack in Sales Page variant)
- Hero video described as animation, not full video
- PDP variant lacks benefit copy — relies on product name + tagline alone

### Model for EC-02
- The **dual-hero architecture** (PDP + Sales Page) is a specimen for products that need both page types
- The **staccato pre-head** ("NEVER. CHUNK. AGAIN.") is a template for any 3-word pain-elimination promise
- The **product-name-as-headline** pattern eliminates redundancy when the product name IS the value prop
- The **toggle-based offer box** is a UX/design specimen for page builder handoff
