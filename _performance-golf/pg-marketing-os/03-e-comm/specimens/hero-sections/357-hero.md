## ATF Components

### Headline
**"Introducing... THE FIRST-EVER SUPER 7-WOOD WITH TRI-FUSION TECHNOLOGY!"**
- Word count: 9 (excluding "Introducing")
- Formula: Category Claim + Named Technology
- Scroll-stop mechanism: "FIRST-EVER" (novelty) + "SUPER 7-WOOD" (new category) + "TRI-FUSION TECHNOLOGY" (named mechanism)
- Schwartz level: Stage 3 — mechanism leads because the market has seen benefit claims before

### Subhead
**"Launch, Reach, And Stick More Greens In Regulation — Without Changing Your Swing!"**
- Word count: 14
- Formula: Triple Benefit Stack + Caveat (preserves what they have)
- Expands headline with: specific benefits (launch, reach, stick) + safety valve (without changing your swing)
- The "without changing your swing" is a fear-removal that earns the scroll

### Price Display
**~~$399~~ $249 | Save $150**
- Positioned ATF with the headline — price is part of the hero, not hidden BTF
- Strikethrough anchoring: $399 reference price, $249 actual
- "Save $150" — explicit savings calculation (don't make them do math)

### Trust Strip
- Rated 4.8 based on 3,700+ happy reviews
- 365 Day Money Back Guarantee
- Free Shipping Across The USA
- 100% Secure Checkout

### CTA
**"Hurry! Claim your 38% Discount + Get 5 Exclusive Bonuses before time runs out!"**
- Urgency + specific discount percentage + bonus stack reference
- Not a standard "Buy Now" — it's a mini-offer summary in the CTA

## Scroll-Down Earner: Three Reasons Section

Immediately below the hero, the 357 uses a **Numbered Reasons** structure:

1. "You'll make solid, consistent contact" (Progressed Face)
2. "You'll launch the ball higher so it stops faster" (Power Launch Crown)
3. "You'll carry the ball as far (or farther) than a 3-wood" (C-Cup Construction)
+ 2 "Bonus Reasons" (Stability Rails + Chris McGinley credibility)

**Key Insight:** The 357 hero doesn't use a traditional carousel/highlights format. Instead, it flows directly into a numbered-reasons section that functions as both hero extension and feature introduction. This is a long-form-influenced hero — more copy-heavy than typical PDP.

## Analysis

### What Works
- **Category claim** in headline ("FIRST-EVER SUPER 7-WOOD") creates a named new category
- **"Without Changing Your Swing"** is the #1 fear-removal for senior golfers
- **Price ATF** — no hiding, no scrolling to find cost
- **Trust strip** immediately below price reduces anxiety
- **3,700+ reviews** — social proof quantity is the most powerful ATF proof element

### What's Missing (vs. NLS Framework)
- No 10-thumbnail hero carousel (this is a more traditional single-image hero)
- No product highlights TLDR section (the three-reasons section fills this role differently)
- No hero video overlay
- This is closer to a long-form sales page hero than a PDP hero

### Model for EC-02
- Best used as a specimen for **sales page** variant (Section 1B), not PDP variant (Section 1A)
- The three-reasons structure is excellent for products with distinct, nameable features
- Price-in-hero is a strong pattern for products with a compelling discount
