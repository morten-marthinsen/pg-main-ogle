# Microskill 4.4: Distinctiveness Pass (Checkout)

**Layer:** 4 (Fix Application)
**Purpose:** Detect whether editorial cleanup has reduced checkout copy to generic trust/reassurance boilerplate
**Sequence:** Runs AFTER editorial validation (4.1) and output packaging (4.2)

---

## WHY THIS PASS EXISTS

Checkout editorial prioritizes clarity, trust, and friction reduction — all of which push copy toward standard e-commerce checkout language indistinguishable from any other brand. This pass checks whether the campaign's voice persists through the final purchase moment.

**References:**
- `~system/humanization-specimens/SIGNATURE-MOVES.md` (Checkout section)
- `~system/humanization-specimens/engine-packs/checkout-pack.md`
- Campaign Soul.md
