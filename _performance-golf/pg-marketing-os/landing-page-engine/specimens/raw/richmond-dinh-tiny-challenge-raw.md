## Proof Architecture
- **Total testimonials:** 4+ video testimonials + 4+ screenshot testimonials
- **First proof position:** Section 4 (Russell Brunson celebrity endorsement)
- **Proof types:** Celebrity endorsement (Russell Brunson), stat claims ($30M+, 1,200+ coaches, 500+ videos), video testimonials, screenshot testimonials
- **Proof density:** HIGH — proof concentrated in Sections 4-5 and 11

## CTA Architecture
- **Total CTAs:** 8+ (hero, post-qualification, post-endorsement, after each lesson preview, registration form, final)
- **CTA text:** "Join The Free Masterclass Now!" / "Join Our Next Free Masterclass!" / "Join The Masterclass Now>>>"
- **Emotional appeals:** Urgency (countdown timer, LIVE event), simplicity ("3 Hours"), zero-risk (FREE), FOMO ("Starting Soon")

## Offer Structure
- **Price display:** FREE (masterclass registration)
- **Guarantee:** None needed (free event)
- **Value stack:** 4 lessons previewed with bullet benefits
- **Urgency:** Countdown timer, specific date/time, "LIVE! Starting Soon"

## Key Design Patterns
- ClickFunnels template with embedded registration form
- Countdown timer creating real urgency (live event)
- Lesson preview structure (show-don't-tell for masterclass content)
- Celebrity endorsement (Russell Brunson) as primary trust signal
- Stat bar ($30M+, 1,200+, 500+) as credibility shortcut
- Qualification checklist section ("Is this for me?") — reduces friction by self-selecting
- Free masterclass → paid program funnel (not selling directly on this page)
- Extremely long page for a free opt-in (~8,000+ words)
