## What Causes Pelvic Floor Dysfunction?

Your pelvic floor doesn't weaken overnight. **Several life events and habits can break down support over time:**

- **Pregnancy & childbirth** can overstretch and weaken key muscles.
- **Aging & menopause** cause tissue to thin and lose elasticity.
- **Obesity & chronic constipation** increase pressure and strain.
- **Rushing to the bathroom too often** can train your bladder to overreact.
- **Squeezing the wrong muscles** (or holding too much tension) makes symptoms worse.

If any of these sound familiar, you're in the right place. Because from now on **you don't have to accept these symptoms as "just part of aging."**

## Kim Vopni's Story — "The Buff Muff Method Changed My Life..."

**Until one day she told me she was scheduled for surgery** - a bladder sling for her incontinence...
