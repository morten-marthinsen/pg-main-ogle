## Proof Architecture
- **Total testimonials:** 35+ (12+ celebrity, 5+ detailed case studies, 20+ videos, 3+ screenshots)
- **First proof position:** Above the fold (Section 1 — celebrity endorsement cards)
- **Proof types:** Celebrity endorsements (Hormozi, Robbins, Frank Kern, etc.), video testimonials, detailed case studies with specific metrics, screenshot testimonials, "300+ testimonials" social proof claim
- **Proof density:** EXTREMELY HIGH — ~80% of page is proof. This is one of the most proof-dense pages in the specimen set.

## CTA Architecture
- **Total CTAs:** 3 (hero, post-guarantee, final)
- **CTA text:** "Get started below" / "Get started here"
- **Emotional appeals:** Guarantee ("you don't pay"), simplicity ("just paste our code"), specific metric ("15% more")

## Offer Structure
- **Price display:** None on page (demo/consultation model)
- **Guarantee:** Performance guarantee ("15% more... or you don't pay")
- **Value stack:** 3-step process simplification
- **Urgency:** None explicit

## Key Design Patterns
- Framer-built (modern, clean design)
- Proof-first architecture — celebrities above the fold before any product explanation
- Business model segmentation (4 use cases, each with dedicated page)
- Extremely simple product explanation (3 steps) surrounded by massive proof
- Celebrity social proof as primary selling mechanism
- Specific metrics in every testimonial (43%, 300%, 50%, etc.)
- No pricing shown — book-a-call model
- Multiple proof formats (celebrity quotes, video, case studies, screenshots)
- "300+ testimonials" claim to imply iceberg of proof
- Mobile app promotion in footer CTA
