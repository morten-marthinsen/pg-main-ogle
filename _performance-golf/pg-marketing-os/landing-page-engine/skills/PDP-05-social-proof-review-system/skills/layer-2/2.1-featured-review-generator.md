# 2.1 — Featured Review Generator

**Skill:** PDP-05-social-proof-review-system
**Position:** Layer 2, Microskill 1
**Purpose:** Generate 3-5 curated featured reviews with full persona detail — each review must have a distinct reviewer persona, specific measurable outcomes, pros AND cons, and verified buyer attributes. Featured reviews appear at the top of the review section and set the quality standard for the entire system.
**Effort Level:** high
**Dependencies:** `input-verification.md` (0.1), `research-load.md` (0.2), `review-system-architecture.md` (1.1), `reviewer-persona-builder.md` (2.3 — if available, otherwise generate inline)
**Output:** `featured-reviews.md`

## Execution Logic

**Step 1:** Determine featured review count:
- All scopes: 3-5 featured reviews
- Target: 5 for `full`, 4 for `standard`, 3 for `minimal` and `generation_only`

**Step 2:** Determine review source:
- `scope = full | standard` with existing reviews -> curate from real reviews (select best, enhance formatting to match card template)
- `scope = minimal | generation_only` -> generate reviews entirely (must follow realism constraints)
- If existing reviews are provided but too thin (< 3 that meet quality threshold) -> supplement with generated reviews, clearly marking which are generated

**Step 3:** Define featured review quality threshold:
A review qualifies as "featured" ONLY if it contains ALL of these:
- Star rating (4 or 5 stars — featured reviews are positive, negative reviews appear in the general feed)
- Bold headline (1 line, max 80 characters, specific outcome — NOT "Great product!")
- Reviewer name (first name + last initial, or full name)
- Verified Buyer badge
- Time Owned indicator ("Owned for 3 months," "Using for 6 weeks")
- At least ONE reviewer attribute (age range, skin type, body type, use case, etc.)
- Pros list (1-3 bullet points, specific)
- Cons list (at least 1 con — MANDATORY, even for 5-star reviews)
- Body text (2-5 sentences with specific, measurable outcomes)
- Helpful vote count (e.g., "47 people found this helpful")

**Step 4:** Generate or curate featured reviews. For EACH review:

**4a. Persona assignment:**
- If 2.3 reviewer personas available: assign one persona per review (no duplicates)
- If 2.3 not yet completed: create inline personas varying by:
  - Age range (spread across 25-65)
  - Gender (mix)
  - Use case (different scenarios)
  - Skepticism level (at least one initially skeptical reviewer)
  - Purchase motivation (different reasons for buying)

**4b. Headline generation:**
- Must be SPECIFIC outcome, not generic praise
- GOOD: "Finally Sleeping Through the Night After 2 Weeks"
- GOOD: "Down 12 lbs in My First Month — Not Just Water Weight"
- GOOD: "Best Investment for My Home Office Setup"
- BAD: "Great Product!" (generic)
- BAD: "Love This So Much!" (no outcome)
- BAD: "Exceeded All My Expectations" (AI slop)
- Maximum 80 characters

**4c. Pros/Cons generation:**
- Pros: 1-3 specific bullet points grounded in product attributes
- Cons: at least 1 per review — MANDATORY even for 5-star reviews
- Con examples: "Took 10 days to ship," "Wish it came in more colors," "Taste is medicinal — I mix it in smoothies," "Price is steep but worth it for me," "Packaging could be better"
- Cons must be MINOR and REALISTIC — they build trust, not destroy it
- FORBIDDEN: 5-star review with zero cons (unrealistic, destroys credibility)
- FORBIDDEN: "No cons" or "Can't think of any negatives" (obvious tell)

**4d. Body text generation:**
- 2-5 sentences
- Must include at least ONE specific measurable outcome (timeframe + result)
- Must reference the reviewer's personal context (why they bought, what they tried before)
- Natural voice — NOT copywriter voice, NOT marketing language
- Sentence length variety (mix short and long)
- FORBIDDEN words/phrases: revolutionary, game-changing, exceeded expectations, I was skeptical but, transform, unlock, journey, blown away

**4e. Attribute assignment:**
- Product-specific attributes (e.g., skincare: skin type, age, concern; supplement: age, health goal, activity level)
- Time Owned: realistic range (2 weeks to 8 months — NOT "1 day" for effectiveness claims)
- Location: city + state or region

**Step 5:** Star distribution for featured reviews:
- 5-star: 2-3 reviews
- 4-star: 1-2 reviews
- 3-star: 0-1 reviews (optional — featured reviews are positive curations, not the full distribution)
- FORBIDDEN: All featured reviews at 5 stars (include at least one 4-star)

**Step 6:** Diversity check across all featured reviews:
- At least 3 different reviewer personas (age, gender, use case vary)
- At least 2 different Time Owned durations (not all "3 months")
- At least 2 different primary benefit themes (not all about the same feature)
- At least 1 initially skeptical reviewer ("I wasn't sure this would work for me...")
- No two reviews with the same headline structure
- No two reviews with the same con

**Step 7:** Anti-slop scan — run every generated review through:
- [ ] No "revolutionary" or "game-changing"
- [ ] No "exceeded expectations" or "exceeded my expectations"
- [ ] No "I was skeptical but" as opening
- [ ] No "transform" or "transformed my life"
- [ ] No "unlock" or "unlocked"
- [ ] No "journey" (in self-improvement context)
- [ ] No "blown away"
- [ ] Sentence lengths vary (not all 15-20 words)
- [ ] Reviews don't all sound the same tone/voice
- [ ] No exclamation point overuse (max 1 per review)

**Step 8:** Compile all featured reviews into output file.

## Quality Criteria
- 3-5 featured reviews generated with complete card fields
- Every review has at least 1 con (even 5-star reviews)
- Every review has at least 1 specific measurable outcome in body text
- Star distribution includes at least one 4-star (not all 5-star)
- Diversity check passes (3+ different personas, 2+ time durations, 2+ benefit themes)
- Anti-slop scan passes all 10 checks
- Headlines are specific outcomes (not generic praise)
- Time Owned values are realistic for claimed outcomes
- No forbidden AI-telltale words/phrases

## Failure Modes
- Any featured review missing cons -> HALT. Add at least 1 realistic minor con.
- All featured reviews at 5 stars -> HALT. Change at least 1 to 4 stars.
- Generic headlines ("Great product!") -> HALT. Rewrite with specific outcome.
- AI-telltale word detected -> HALT. Rewrite affected sentence.
- All reviews same tone/voice -> HALT. Rewrite to vary sentence structure, vocabulary, and personality.
- Time Owned = "1 day" but claims long-term effectiveness -> HALT. Increase Time Owned to realistic duration.
- Fewer than 3 featured reviews for any scope -> HALT. Generate additional reviews to meet minimum.
