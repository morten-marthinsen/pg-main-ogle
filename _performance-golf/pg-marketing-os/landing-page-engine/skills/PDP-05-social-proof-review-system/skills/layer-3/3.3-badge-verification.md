# 3.3 — Badge Verification

**Skill:** PDP-05-social-proof-review-system
**Position:** Layer 3, Microskill 3
**Purpose:** Verify that every review card — featured and general — contains the two mandatory credibility badges: Verified Buyer and Time Owned. A review without Verified Buyer looks like a planted testimonial. A review without Time Owned removes the credibility signal of long-term use vs impulse-buy praise. Both are non-negotiable per Baymard UX requirements. This is the structural health check — does every card have its trust signals in the right position?
**Effort Level:** medium
**Dependencies:** `input-verification.md` (0.1), `review-system-architecture.md` (1.1), `featured-reviews.md` (2.1), `reviewer-personas.md` (2.3), `review-card-template.md` (2.5), `volume-audit.md` (3.1), `specificity-check.md` (3.2)
**Output:** `badge-verification.md`

## Execution Logic

**Step 1:** Define the two mandatory badges and their specifications:

| Badge | Field | Display Format | Position | Mandatory? |
|-------|-------|---------------|----------|------------|
| **Verified Buyer** | `verified_buyer` | Green checkmark icon + "Verified Buyer" text | Reviewer line, after name, before Time Owned | YES — every card |
| **Time Owned** | `time_owned` | "Owned for [N] months" or "Purchased [N] weeks ago" | Reviewer line, after Verified Buyer badge | YES — every card |

**Step 2:** Collect all review cards that need badge verification:
- All featured reviews from `featured-reviews.md` (2.1)
- All general pool review specifications from `review-system-architecture.md` (1.1)
- Review card template specification from `review-card-template.md` (2.5)

**Step 3:** Verify Verified Buyer badge on every featured review:

| Check | Pass Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|-------|--------------|---------------|
| Badge present | Review card includes `verified_buyer: true` or displays "Verified Buyer" | Badge missing or set to false |
| Badge format | Green checkmark icon + "Verified Buyer" text | Text-only without icon, or icon-only without text |
| Badge position | After reviewer name, before Time Owned, on the reviewer line (Section 2 of card anatomy) | Badge in body text, in header row, or after actions row |
| Badge styling | Visually distinct from surrounding text (green icon, slightly different font weight or color) | Same styling as body text (invisible badge) |

**Step 4:** Verify Time Owned badge on every featured review:

| Check | Pass Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|-------|--------------|---------------|
| Field present | Review card includes `time_owned` with a specific duration | Field missing or empty |
| Format correct | Relative time: "Owned for [N] months" or "Purchased [N] weeks ago" | Absolute date ("January 15, 2024"), no timeframe, or vague ("a while ago") |
| Duration realistic | 2 weeks to 24 months for most products | "Owned for 0 days" (just bought, no usage experience) or "Owned for 5 years" (product is 2 years old) |
| Duration varied | Featured reviews have different Time Owned values (spread across timeframes) | All featured reviews have identical Time Owned (e.g., all "3 months") |
| Position correct | On reviewer line (Section 2), after Verified Buyer badge | In body text, in headline, or missing from reviewer line |

**Step 5:** Verify Time Owned duration distribution across featured reviews:

| Timeframe Bucket | Expected Distribution | Purpose |
|-----------------|----------------------|---------|
| Recent (< 1 month) | 1-2 featured reviews | Shows fresh excitement and immediate results |
| Mid-term (1-6 months) | 1-2 featured reviews | Shows sustained use and ongoing benefits |
| Long-term (6+ months) | 1-2 featured reviews | Shows lasting satisfaction and loyalty |

- PASS if featured reviews span at least 2 of the 3 timeframe buckets
- FAIL if all featured reviews are in the same timeframe bucket
- NOTE: exact distribution depends on product type (consumables may have shorter ranges)

**Step 6:** Verify badge consistency with reviewer persona:
- Does Time Owned align with persona's stated experience level?
  - "Long-Term User" persona should have Time Owned >= 6 months
  - "New Convert" or "Skeptic Convert" persona should have Time Owned 1-3 months
  - "First-Time Buyer" persona should have Time Owned < 1 month
- FAIL if Time Owned contradicts persona archetype (e.g., "Long-Term User" with "Purchased 2 weeks ago")

**Step 7:** Verify badge specification in review card template (2.5):
- Section 2 (Reviewer Line) includes Verified Buyer badge position: [Y/N]
- Section 2 (Reviewer Line) includes Time Owned field position: [Y/N]
- Template specifies badge styling (icon, color, font): [Y/N]
- Template specifies Time Owned format (relative, not absolute): [Y/N]
- FAIL if template itself is missing badge specifications (upstream structural failure)

**Step 8:** Verify badge behavior for general pool reviews (from architecture spec):
- General pool review template includes Verified Buyer badge field: [Y/N]
- General pool review template includes Time Owned field: [Y/N]
- Architecture spec indicates badges are mandatory for ALL reviews, not just featured: [Y/N]
- FAIL if general pool reviews are specified without badge requirements

**Step 9:** Check for merchant response badge consistency:
- Reviews with merchant responses (rated <= 3 stars) — do they still have Verified Buyer badge? [Y/N]
- Low-star reviews with merchant responses — does Time Owned help contextualize the complaint (e.g., "Purchased 3 days ago" explains shipping complaint)? [Y/N]
- FAIL if merchant response reviews lack badges (inconsistent card structure)

**Step 10:** Calculate badge verification score:

| Dimension | Weight | Score Logic |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Verified Buyer present on all featured reviews | 25% | All present = 10, per missing -3.3 |
| Time Owned present on all featured reviews | 25% | All present = 10, per missing -3.3 |
| Time Owned format correct (relative, not absolute) | 15% | All correct = 10, per incorrect format -3.3 |
| Time Owned distribution varied (2+ buckets) | 15% | 3 buckets = 10, 2 buckets = 7, 1 bucket = 3 |
| Persona-badge alignment | 10% | All aligned = 10, per contradiction -5 |
| Template specification complete | 10% | All 4 template checks pass = 10, per fail -2.5 |

Badge verification score = weighted average. Minimum passing: 8.0/10.

**Step 11:** Document all findings, per-review badge status, distribution analysis, and overall badge verification score.

## Quality Criteria
- Every featured review individually checked for both Verified Buyer and Time Owned badges
- Verified Buyer badge verified for presence, format (icon + text), position (Section 2), and styling
- Time Owned verified for presence, relative format, realistic duration, and correct position
- Time Owned distribution spans at least 2 of 3 timeframe buckets (recent, mid-term, long-term)
- Persona-badge alignment verified (Time Owned matches persona archetype)
- Review card template (2.5) verified to include badge specifications
- General pool review template verified to require badges
- Badge verification score >= 8.0/10
- All FAIL items have specific remediation actions

## Failure Modes
- Any featured review missing Verified Buyer badge -> HALT. Badge is mandatory on every card.
- Any featured review missing Time Owned field -> HALT. Time Owned is mandatory credibility signal.
- Time Owned in absolute date format ("Jan 15, 2024") -> HALT. Must use relative format ("Owned for 3 months").
- All featured reviews have identical Time Owned value -> HALT. Vary durations across timeframe buckets.
- Time Owned contradicts persona archetype (Long-Term User with "2 weeks") -> HALT. Align duration with persona.
- Review card template (2.5) missing badge position specs -> HALT. Upstream structural failure — fix template first.
- General pool reviews specified without badge requirements -> HALT. Badges are mandatory for ALL reviews, not just featured.
- Badge verification score below 8.0/10 -> HALT. Address all FAIL items before proceeding.
