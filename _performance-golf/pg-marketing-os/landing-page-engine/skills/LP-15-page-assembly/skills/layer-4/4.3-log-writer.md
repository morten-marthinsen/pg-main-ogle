## FAILURE MODES

- Log written before all microskills complete -> The log should be the LAST thing written. It records what happened, not what is planned.
- Log omits failed microskills -> If a microskill failed or was skipped, log it as FAILED or SKIPPED with the reason. Do not only log successes.
- Log does not record Arena verification -> Arena verification is the most important single check in LP-15. It MUST be in the log.
