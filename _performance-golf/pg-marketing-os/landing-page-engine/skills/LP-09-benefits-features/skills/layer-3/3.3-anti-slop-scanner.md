# 3.3 — Anti-Slop Scanner

**Skill:** LP-09-benefits-features
**Position:** Layer 3, Microskill 3
**Purpose:** Systematically scan every copy element across all benefit section outputs against the 5-category forbidden word list from LP-09-AGENT.md. A single instance of any forbidden word or phrase = FAIL. This is the quality gate that prevents AI telltales from reaching the final package.
**Effort Level:** low
**Dependencies:** All Layer 2 outputs (benefit-bullets.md, ingredient-deep-dives.md, feature-explanations.md, how-it-works.md, comparison-benefits.md)
**Output:** `anti-slop-scan.md`

---

## Input Schema

```yaml
input:
  elements_to_scan:
    benefit_bullets: "[full text of benefit-bullets.md]"
    ingredient_deep_dives: "[full text of ingredient-deep-dives.md -- if generated]"
    feature_explanations: "[full text of feature-explanations.md -- if generated]"
    how_it_works: "[full text of how-it-works.md -- if generated]"
    comparison_benefits: "[full text of comparison-benefits.md -- if generated]"
```

---

## Execution Logic

1. **Load the 5-category forbidden word list** from LP-09-AGENT.md:

   **Category 1 -- AI Telltales:**
   revolutionary, game-changing, groundbreaking, cutting-edge, breakthrough, innovative, state-of-the-art, next-level, unprecedented, transformative, unlock your potential, harness the power, leverage, synergy, holistic approach

   **Category 2 -- Vague Benefit Language:**
   feel better, feel amazing, live your best life, optimal health, overall wellness, total wellbeing, achieve your goals, on your journey, life-changing, powerful results, incredible benefits, amazing formula, supercharge, turbocharge, skyrocket

   **Category 3 -- Unearned Claims:**
   the best, the most effective, the #1 [without sourced data], proven [without specifying what was proven], scientifically proven [without study], doctor-approved [without named doctor], clinically proven [without citation], studies show [without naming the study], research proves [without citation]

   **Category 4 -- Generic Benefit Language:**
   supports overall health, promotes general wellness, helps you feel your best, works naturally with your body, gentle yet effective, fast-acting formula, maximum strength, premium quality, pharmaceutical grade [without certification]

   **Category 5 -- Passive/Hedge Language:**
   may help, could potentially, might support, has been known to, some people find, many users report [without data behind "many"]

2. **Scan each output file independently.** For each file:
   - Check every word and phrase against all 5 categories
   - Record ANY match with: the forbidden word/phrase, the file it was found in, the specific element, and the category

3. **Check for near-misses** -- words not on the list but carrying AI-telltale signal:
   - "Unlock" (any usage)
   - "Empower" / "empowering"
   - "Journey" (metaphorical usage)
   - "Seamless" / "seamlessly"
   - "Robust"
   - "Elevate" / "elevated"
   - "Optimize" / "optimized" (when used as marketing rather than technical term)
   - "Curated"

4. **Determine scan result:**
   - Zero forbidden words AND zero near-misses -> **PASS**
   - Zero forbidden words but near-misses found -> **PASS WITH FLAG** (note for human awareness)
   - One or more forbidden words found -> **FAIL** (list every instance, require revision)

5. **If FAIL:** For each forbidden word found, provide a specific replacement suggestion that conveys the same meaning without the AI telltale.

## Quality Criteria
- Every generated Layer 2 output file scanned (no files skipped)
- All 5 categories checked explicitly (each category listed in output, even if "None found")
- Near-miss check conducted in addition to the forbidden list
- FAIL result includes specific replacement suggestions, not just identification
- Scan result is binary for forbidden words: any instance = FAIL, period

## Failure Modes
- Scanning only benefit-bullets.md and skipping other files -> All generated files must be scanned. Ingredient deep-dives and feature explanations are common locations for AI telltales.
- Marking a forbidden word as "acceptable in this context" -> No context exceptions. The word list is absolute for benefit copy. Remove and replace.
- Missing compound phrases -> "Unlock your potential" must be scanned as a unit, not just the individual words "unlock," "your," "potential."
- Near-miss found but not flagged -> Near-misses should be noted even on a PASS result. Human may want to address them proactively.
- Suggested replacement is another forbidden word -> Check replacement suggestions against the forbidden list before recommending them.
