# Microskill 3.4: Distinctiveness Pass (Emails)

**Layer:** 3 (Validation)
**Purpose:** Detect whether editorial cleanup has drifted email copy toward generic brand-email voice — the "any competent email marketer" problem
**Sequence:** Runs AFTER rescore (3.1) and output packaging (3.2)

---

## WHY THIS PASS EXISTS

Email editorial focuses on deliverability, pitch ratio, and structure — all pushing copy toward safe, tested email templates. This pass checks whether the campaign's distinctive voice survived cleanup, especially in subject lines and opening hooks where distinctiveness matters most.

**References:**
- `~system/humanization-specimens/SIGNATURE-MOVES.md` (Emails section)
- `~system/humanization-specimens/engine-packs/email-pack.md`
- Campaign Soul.md
