# 3.7 Implementation Guidance

## Purpose
Provide detailed, actionable implementation guides for each generation recommendation. This microskill expands the high-level recommendations from 3.6 into step-by-step playbooks that clients/stakeholders can execute.

---

## Input Requirements

From 3.6 Generation Recommendations:
- Prioritized recommendations
- Gap context
- Basic execution guidance

From Context:
- Client resources/capabilities
- Industry/niche specifics
- Timeline requirements

## Implementation Playbook Library

### SOCIAL PROOF GENERATION

#### Playbook: Testimonial Collection Campaign

```yaml
playbook:
  name: "Testimonial Collection Campaign"
  category: "social"
  timeline: "1-2 weeks"
  effort: "low"
  resources_needed:
    - Email access to customer list
    - Email template
    - Simple tracking system

  phase_1_preparation:
    duration: "1-2 days"
    tasks:
      - task: "Segment customer list"
        how: |
          Pull customers who:
          - Purchased 30+ days ago
          - Have not returned/refunded
          - Have interacted positively (support, email opens)
          - Ideally have reordered
        target: "50-100 customers minimum"

      - task: "Draft email template"
        template: |
          Subject: Quick favor? (Takes 2 minutes)

          Hi [First Name],

          I noticed you've been using [Product] for [X weeks/months], and
          I wanted to personally reach out.

          Would you mind sharing your experience? Specifically:

          1. What was going on BEFORE you tried [Product]?
          2. What specific results or changes have you noticed?
          3. How long did it take to see those results?
          4. What would you tell a friend considering [Product]?

          Your story could really help someone who's on the fence.

          As a thank you, I'd like to offer you [incentive].

          Just hit reply—I read every response personally.

          Thanks so much,
          [Your name]
          [Company]

          P.S. If you have a before/after photo, we'd especially love
          to see it! (Totally optional)

      - task: "Set up tracking"
        how: "Use spreadsheet to track sends, opens, responses"

  phase_2_execution:
    duration: "3-5 days"
    tasks:
      - task: "Send initial batch"
        how: "Send to 25-50 customers first (test batch)"
        timing: "Tuesday-Thursday, 10am-2pm best"

      - task: "Track responses"
        what_to_track:
          - Response rate
          - Quality of responses
          - Follow-up needed

      - task: "Send follow-up"
        timing: "3 days after initial send"
        template: |
          Subject: Re: Quick favor?

          Hi [First Name],

          Just wanted to bump this in case it got buried.
          Would love to hear your experience with [Product]!

          [Same questions]

          Thanks,
          [Name]

      - task: "Expand to full list"
        when: "If test batch performs well"

  phase_3_curation:
    duration: "2-3 days"
    tasks:
      - task: "Score testimonials"
        criteria:
          - Specificity (numbers, timeframes): +3
          - Named attribution willing: +2
          - Before/after narrative: +2
          - Emotional story: +2
          - Credibility (verifiable): +1

      - task: "Request permission"
        template: |
          Thanks so much for sharing your story, [Name]!

          Would you be comfortable if we shared your testimonial
          (using just your first name and city) on our website?

          If yes, just reply "Yes" and I'll make sure it helps
          others who are considering [Product].

      - task: "Format for use"
        output: |
          "[Quote]" — [First Name] [Last Initial]., [City, State]

  success_metrics:
    minimum_viable:
      - 10+ responses collected
      - 3+ testimonials with specific numbers
      - 2+ testimonials with before/after
    ideal:
      - 20+ responses collected
      - 8+ testimonials with specific numbers
      - 5+ testimonials with before/after
      - 1+ video testimonials
```

#### Playbook: Before/After Photo Collection

```yaml
playbook:
  name: "Before/After Photo Collection"
  category: "demonstration"
  timeline: "2-4 weeks"
  effort: "low-medium"

  phase_1_preparation:
    tasks:
      - task: "Create photo guidelines"
        guidelines: |
          FOR BEST RESULTS:
          - Same lighting for both photos
          - Same angle/distance
          - Plain background preferred
          - Clear, in-focus
          - Include face if comfortable (more impactful)

          TIMING:
          - Before: Day 1 or before starting
          - After: After noticeable results (4+ weeks typically)

      - task: "Draft request email"
        template: |
          Subject: Show off your results? (We'll reward you!)

          Hi [Name],

          Congratulations on your results with [Product]!

          We're looking for before/after photos to help inspire
          others on their journey. Would you be willing to share yours?

          HERE'S WHAT WE NEED:
          - A "before" photo (from before/early use)
          - An "after" photo (recent, showing your progress)
          - Brief caption about your experience

          IN EXCHANGE:
          - $[X] credit toward your next order
          - OR [free product/bonus]

          PHOTO TIPS:
          [Include guidelines]

          Interested? Just reply "Yes" and I'll send the details!

          [Name]

      - task: "Create release form"
        template: |
          PHOTO RELEASE FORM

          I, [Full Name], grant [Company Name] permission to use
          my before and after photographs in marketing materials,
          including but not limited to: website, social media,
          advertisements, and promotional materials.

          I understand that my first name and general location
          (city/state) may be used alongside the photos.

          I confirm these photos are of myself and represent my
          actual results using [Product].

          Signature: _______________
          Date: _______________
          Name: _______________

  phase_2_collection:
    tasks:
      - task: "Incentivize participation"
        options:
          - Free product
          - Store credit ($25-50)
          - Feature in "Success Stories"
          - Entry into prize drawing

      - task: "Create easy submission"
        options:
          - Direct email reply
          - Simple upload form
          - Dedicated email address (results@company.com)

  phase_3_curation:
    tasks:
      - task: "Quality check"
        criteria:
          - Both photos clear and comparable
          - Visible difference/improvement
          - Release form signed
          - Attribution confirmed

      - task: "Enhance if needed"
        allowable_edits:
          - Crop/resize
          - Adjust brightness/contrast (both equally)
          - Add before/after labels
        not_allowed:
          - Altering body/appearance
          - Combining different people
          - Misrepresenting timeframe

  success_metrics:
    minimum: "3-5 quality before/after sets"
    ideal: "10+ quality sets with variety"
```

### AUTHORITY PROOF GENERATION

#### Playbook: Expert Endorsement Outreach

```yaml
playbook:
  name: "Expert Endorsement Outreach"
  category: "authority"
  timeline: "2-6 weeks"
  effort: "medium"

  phase_1_identification:
    tasks:
      - task: "Create expert target list"
        criteria:
          - Credentials relevant to product/mechanism
          - Active online presence (findable)
          - Has endorsed products before (warm to it)
          - Tier 1-3 experts preferred
        target: "15-25 potential experts"

      - task: "Research each expert"
        gather:
          - Email address
          - Recent work/publications
          - Social media presence
          - Previous endorsements
          - Potential interest angle

  phase_2_outreach:
    tasks:
      - task: "Craft personalized outreach"
        template: |
          Subject: [Personalized - Reference their work]

          Dear Dr./Prof. [Last Name],

          I recently came across your [paper/interview/book] on
          [specific topic], and your perspective on [specific insight]
          really resonated with our work at [Company].

          We've developed [Product], which [mechanism/how it works],
          and I believe it aligns with your research on [topic].

          I'd love to send you a sample and, if you find it valuable,
          discuss the possibility of your endorsement or feedback.

          We compensate fairly for expert time, and I'm happy to
          discuss what arrangement works for you.

          Would you be open to a brief call or email exchange?

          Respectfully,
          [Your Name]
          [Title, Company]

        personalization_required:
          - Reference specific work
          - Connect to their research
          - Show you've done homework

      - task: "Follow-up sequence"
        timing:
          - Initial: Day 0
          - Follow-up 1: Day 7
          - Follow-up 2: Day 14
          - Final: Day 21

  phase_3_engagement:
    tasks:
      - task: "Send product sample"
        include:
          - Product
          - One-page fact sheet
          - Key research citations
          - Personal note

      - task: "Interview/feedback session"
        questions_to_ask:
          - "What's your overall impression of [mechanism]?"
          - "Based on the research, would you recommend this approach?"
          - "How would you explain why this works to a patient?"

      - task: "Secure endorsement"
        formats:
          - Written quote (easiest)
          - Video testimonial (most powerful)
          - Advisory board position (highest commitment)

  phase_4_documentation:
    tasks:
      - task: "Get written approval"
        template: |
          ENDORSEMENT AGREEMENT

          I, [Expert Name], [Credentials], agree to provide
          the following endorsement for [Company/Product]:

          "[Exact quote to be used]"

          This endorsement may be used in [specified materials].
          I confirm I have [tried the product / reviewed the research].
          Compensation: [Terms]

          Signature: _______________

  success_metrics:
    minimum: "1 Tier 2-3 expert endorsement"
    ideal: "2-3 endorsements across Tier 1-3"
```

### DATA PROOF GENERATION

#### Playbook: Customer Results Survey

```yaml
playbook:
  name: "Customer Results Survey"
  category: "data"
  timeline: "1-2 weeks"
  effort: "low"

  phase_1_design:
    tasks:
      - task: "Create survey"
        tool_options:
          - Google Forms (free)
          - Typeform (better UX)
          - SurveyMonkey
        question_design:
          question_1:
            type: "multiple_choice"
            text: "Have you seen improvement in [primary problem] since using [Product]?"
            options:
              - "Yes, significant improvement"
              - "Yes, some improvement"
              - "No change yet"
              - "No, I don't think it's working"

          question_2:
            type: "multiple_choice"
            text: "How quickly did you notice results?"
            options:
              - "Within the first week"
              - "2-4 weeks"
              - "1-2 months"
              - "3+ months"
              - "Haven't noticed yet"

          question_3:
            type: "rating_scale"
            text: "On a scale of 1-10, how satisfied are you with [Product]?"
            scale: "1 (Not at all) to 10 (Extremely)"

          question_4:
            type: "yes_no"
            text: "Would you recommend [Product] to a friend or family member?"

          question_5:
            type: "multiple_choice"
            text: "How does [Product] compare to other solutions you've tried?"
            options:
              - "Much better"
              - "Somewhat better"
              - "About the same"
              - "Worse"
              - "Haven't tried others"

          question_6:
            type: "open_text"
            text: "Is there anything else you'd like to share about your experience?"
            optional: true

  phase_2_distribution:
    tasks:
      - task: "Send to customer list"
        template: |
          Subject: Quick survey: We want your honest feedback (1 min)

          Hi [Name],

          We're always looking to improve [Product], and your
          opinion matters most.

          Could you take 60 seconds to share your experience?

          [SURVEY LINK]

          As a thank you, everyone who completes the survey
          gets [small incentive].

          Thanks!
          [Name]

      - task: "Target sample size"
        minimum: "100 responses for statistical validity"
        ideal: "300+ responses"

  phase_3_analysis:
    tasks:
      - task: "Calculate key statistics"
        metrics:
          - "X% report improvement"
          - "Average satisfaction: X.X/10"
          - "X% would recommend"
          - "X% saw results within Y weeks"
          - "X% rate better than alternatives"

      - task: "Document methodology"
        include:
          - Survey dates
          - Sample size
          - Response rate
          - Customer criteria (how long using product)

      - task: "Create proof elements"
        output: |
          "In a survey of [X] customers:
          • [Y]% reported noticeable improvement
          • Average satisfaction rating: [Z]/10
          • [W]% saw results within [time]
          • [V]% would recommend to a friend"

  statistical_validity_notes: |
    FOR CREDIBLE CLAIMS:
    - Minimum 100 responses
    - Note sample is existing customers (selection bias)
    - Can say "of [X] surveyed customers" not "of all users"
    - Round percentages appropriately (94%, not 94.37%)

  success_metrics:
    minimum: "100 responses, 2+ usable statistics"
    ideal: "300+ responses, full statistical package"
```

### RISK REVERSAL GENERATION

#### Playbook: Guarantee Enhancement

```yaml
playbook:
  name: "Guarantee Enhancement"
  category: "risk_reversal"
  timeline: "immediate"
  effort: "low"

  guarantee_options:
    option_1_extended_time:
      name: "Extended Guarantee Period"
      description: "Increase guarantee from 30 → 90 or 365 days"
      implementation:
        - Update sales page guarantee section
        - Update checkout/order confirmation
        - Update customer service scripts
        - Update refund policy page
      copy_template: |
        365-Day Unconditional Guarantee

        You have a FULL YEAR to put [Product] to the test.

        If at ANY point during that year you decide it's not for you,
        simply email us and we'll refund every penny—no questions asked,
        no hoops to jump through.

        Why such a long guarantee? Because we KNOW [Product] works,
        and we want you to have all the time you need to experience
        the results for yourself.

    option_2_conditional:
      name: "Conditional/Results Guarantee"
      description: "Guarantee specific results"
      implementation:
        - Define specific measurable result
        - Set reasonable timeframe
        - Create simple claim process
      copy_template: |
        The "[Specific Result]" Guarantee

        Use [Product] as directed for [X] days. If you don't
        [specific measurable result], we'll give you a full refund.

        It's that simple. Either you get results, or you get
        your money back.

      examples:
        - "If you don't lose at least 10 pounds in 60 days..."
        - "If your energy doesn't noticeably improve in 30 days..."
        - "If you don't sleep better within 2 weeks..."

    option_3_double:
      name: "Double/Better-Than-Risk-Free Guarantee"
      description: "Money back PLUS additional compensation"
      implementation:
        - Define additional compensation
        - Create claim process
        - Budget for potential claims
      copy_template: |
        "Better Than Risk-Free" Guarantee

        If [Product] doesn't [result] for you, not only will we
        refund your purchase in full, we'll ALSO [additional compensation]:

        • Keep the [bonus] as our gift ($[value])
        • Get a $[X] credit toward any other product
        • [Other compensation]

        You literally have nothing to lose and everything to gain.

    option_4_trial:
      name: "Free Trial"
      description: "Try before full commitment"
      implementation:
        - Define trial period and terms
        - Set up billing/shipping logistics
        - Create conversion sequence
      copy_template: |
        Try [Product] FREE for [X] Days

        Don't decide now—let [Product] prove itself to you first.

        We'll send you a [X]-day supply, and you pay nothing today
        except [$X] shipping.

        If you love it (and we think you will), do nothing and
        we'll send your next supply in [X] days at [price].

        If it's not for you, just let us know before your trial
        ends and you'll never be charged a penny more.

  implementation_checklist:
    - [ ] Choose guarantee type
    - [ ] Draft guarantee copy
    - [ ] Update sales page
    - [ ] Update checkout page
    - [ ] Update order confirmation
    - [ ] Update refund policy
    - [ ] Train customer service
    - [ ] Set up tracking (claims, impact on sales)

  risk_management: |
    MANAGING GUARANTEE RISK:
    - Track refund rate before/after
    - Set reasonable conditions
    - Most businesses see refund rates DECREASE with stronger guarantees
      (counterintuitive but documented)
    - Longer guarantees = people forget to return
    - Specific result guarantees filter serious buyers
```

- [ ] Detailed step-by-step playbook provided
- [ ] Templates/scripts included
- [ ] Timeline broken into phases
- [ ] Success metrics defined
- [ ] Risk management addressed
- [ ] Client can execute independently
