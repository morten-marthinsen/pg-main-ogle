5. **Lead with IDENTITY.** At Stage 5 (which PG1 wraps around its Stage 4 mechanism), the purchase becomes membership. SOC-03 (1,000 cap) + SOC-04 (badge) + DEMO-02 (leaked keynote format) transform buying into belonging.
