## Knockout Proof Identification

### Primary Knockout: AUTH-01 — Golf Labs iON+ vs ProV1 (Composite: 9.55)

**Element:** Golf Laboratories independent robot testing at 95mph. iON+ carry: 239.3 yards. ProV1: 231.6 yards. 7.7 yards more carry.

**Why it's the knockout:**
1. **Independent** — not manufacturer-claimed (SVO-01, schema violation)
2. **Specific** — 239.3 vs 231.6, not "farther" (Stage 4 demands specificity)
3. **Comparative** — directly names and beats the #1 brand (ProV1)
4. **At their speed** — 95mph is the target audience's swing speed
5. **Verifiable** — Golf Laboratories is a named, real institution
6. **Emotionally charged** — 7.7 yards over the ball they're currently overpaying for

**Best copy position:** Lead proof point. Every other claim flows from this: "How do we know? We didn't test it ourselves. We sent it to Golf Laboratories — an independent testing facility — and asked them to test it against ProV1 at 95mph. Here's what the robot measured."

### Secondary Knockout: DATA-01 — 7.7 Yards Delta (Composite: 9.30)

The DELTA is what the reader remembers. 239.3 is a number. 7.7 is a story. "Same swing. Different ball. 7.7 more yards."

### Tertiary Knockout: SOC-03 — Gold Phrases (Composite: 8.85)

"My mind writes checks my body can't cash." Not proof of iON+ performance — proof that the problem is real and that the copy speaks the golfer's language.
