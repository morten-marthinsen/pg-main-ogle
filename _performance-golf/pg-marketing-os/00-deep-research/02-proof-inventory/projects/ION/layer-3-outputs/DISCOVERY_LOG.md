#### DISC-01: MyGolfSpy Robot Testing — Spin Difference Between Ball Types (HIGH VALUE)
- **Source:** MyGolfSpy, "How Golf Ball Construction Influences Spin" (May 2025, Tony Covey)
- **Key finding:** In robot testing, the difference between highest and lowest spinning balls off a driver was ~600 RPM. On a 35-yard wedge shot, the difference ballooned to 2,500 RPM.
- **Key finding:** Ionomer = "firm-over-soft" construction = less spin. Urethane = "soft-over-firm" = more spin. This is a structural engineering principle, not a quality difference.
- **Key finding:** MyGolfSpy simulation showed softer/lower-spin balls landed only 0.3-0.6 yards closer to center line (minimal accuracy advantage) but carried 3.5-4 yards shorter. HOWEVER — this is for balls with lower ball SPEED. iON+'s speed mantle compensates for this, which is why it carries FARTHER despite being ionomer.
- **Key finding:** "Manufacturing consistency" — softer materials are harder to manufacture consistently. Inconsistent layers can cause 30+ yard dispersion. This supports iON+'s quality engineering narrative.
- **Credibility:** 9/10 — MyGolfSpy is the most trusted independent golf equipment testing publication
- **Relevance:** DIRECT — proves ionomer produces less driver spin (supports MECH-02), provides quantified RPM differences
- **Copy usability:** "In independent robot testing, the difference between ball types is 600 RPM off the driver. That spin difference IS the distance difference."

#### Testimonial Collection Template (for Ben's team)

When collecting testimonials from influencers or regular golfers, capture:

1. **Name, age, handicap range, swing speed** (if known) — for attribution and targeting
2. **What ball they were playing before** — "I switched from ___"
3. **Specific distance result** — measured by GPS, launch monitor, or course markers. NUMBERS, not feelings.
4. **Feel comparison** — "softer/firmer/same as ___"
5. **Alignment system reaction** — did they notice it? Use it? Like it?
6. **Would they switch permanently?** — yes/no and why
7. **One sentence they'd tell a friend** — this becomes the testimonial headline

**Format that converts best:** Before/after switch story with specific yardage. "I was playing ProV1 and averaging 225 off the tee. After 3 rounds with iON+, I'm averaging 235. Same swing, different ball."
