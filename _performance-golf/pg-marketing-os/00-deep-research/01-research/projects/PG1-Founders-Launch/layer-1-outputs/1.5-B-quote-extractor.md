## Quality Flags

- **Reddit scraper Villain bucket**: Many quotes in the Villain bucket (1.4-C) appear to be mis-classified. Several contain the word "generic" in non-instruction contexts (equipment questions, clothing, travel). See 1.5-C for reclassification analysis.
- **Reddit scraper overall**: Posts are full-text (not excerpted), which means some are very long. The scraper captured entire post bodies rather than extracting the most relevant sentence/paragraph. This is acceptable for the corpus but means individual quotes vary significantly in length and specificity.
