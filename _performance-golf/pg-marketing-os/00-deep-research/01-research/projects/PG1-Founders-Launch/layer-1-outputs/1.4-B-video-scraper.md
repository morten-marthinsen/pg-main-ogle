## ROOT-CAUSE — Why Golfers Aren't Improving (Structural Problems)

**ROOT-CAUSE-001** | Article | Practical Golf | Former Google employee / golf instructor | 2025
> "The engineers who create platforms like Instagram and YouTube are incredibly talented. It's the best of the best. But the problem is for golfers... there is no chance for you to make any meaningful changes to your golf swing if you've viewed 15 different videos from instructors who all have different philosophies on how to swing a club."
Source: https://practical-golf.com/youtube-instagram-golf-swing-tips

**ROOT-CAUSE-002** | Article | Golf.com | GOLF instructor | 2025
> "It's like going into a Walgreens. One new feel makes things worse, so the golfer searches for another fix. That fix might help temporarily, but it could also lead to more issues, so they go searching for another fix. Before long, the brain is overloaded with information, and the swing is in a pretzel."
Source: https://golf.com/instruction/hidden-dangers-social-media-golf-instruction/

**ROOT-CAUSE-003** | Article | Golf.com | Teaching professional | 2025
