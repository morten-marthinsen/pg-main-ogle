## Strategic Summary

**Lead with OPP-01 (Root Flaw Diagnosis)** — this is the master opportunity. It has the highest score, the largest competitive void, the strongest evidence, and the most powerful FSSIT candidates (FSSIT-01 and FSSIT-02 both score 10 momentum-adjusted).

**Support with OPP-02 (End the Search)** — this is the emotional reinforcement. After establishing the diagnosis mechanism, pivot to "and this is the last time you'll need to look." The lifetime pricing structurally delivers this promise.

**Layer in OPP-03 (Founders Identity)** — this transforms the purchase decision from transaction to membership. The docu-series and keynote build the emotional investment; the Founders Club captures it.

**Use OPP-04 and OPP-05 as proof pillars** — the AI + coaching hybrid answers the "how" after OPP-01 answers the "what." The anti-overload angle frames the "why it's different."

**Deploy OPP-06 as the opening emotional hook** — self-blame acknowledgment is the highest-impact opening line but cannot sustain an entire message. Use it to earn attention, then deliver OPP-01.

**Close with OPP-07** — "love your game again" is the emotional destination, not the starting point. Per Copywriting Heuristic #10: close on benefit, not mechanism.
