## OPP-01: Root Flaw Diagnosis

### Leading Indicators (Measurable Within Phase 1)
| Indicator | Target | Measurement Method |
|-----------|--------|-------------------|
| Docu-series Episode 1 open rate | >35% | Email platform |
| Docu-series video completion rate | >50% | Video hosting analytics |
| Keynote video play rate (of page visitors) | >60% | Page analytics |
| Keynote video completion rate | >40% | Video hosting analytics |
| Founders page visit rate (of keynote viewers) | >70% | UTM tracking |
| "Root flaw" language A/B test winner | Statistically significant at p<0.05 | Email A/B test |
| SwingScan diagnosis completion (within 48hrs of purchase) | >70% | Product analytics |
| Diagnosis satisfaction ("Did this feel specific to your swing?") | >80% "Yes" | In-app survey |

### Lagging Indicators (Measurable Within Phase 2-3)
| Indicator | Target | Measurement Method |
|-----------|--------|-------------------|
| Founders Club enrollment (total) | 1,000 members | Payment processor |
| Annual vs. lifetime split | 30/70 (favoring lifetime) | Payment processor |
| Revenue per Founders cohort | $250K+ (at avg $250/member) | Finance |
| 30-day active retention | >85% | Product analytics |
| 60-day active retention | >70% | Product analytics |
| 90-day active retention | >60% | Product analytics |
| Net Promoter Score (30-day) | >50 | Survey |
| Handicap improvement (self-reported, 90-day) | >50% of engaged users report improvement | Survey + product data |
| Organic referrals from Founders | >5% of Founders refer 1+ person | Referral tracking |

### Decision Triggers
| Trigger | Threshold | Action |
|---------|-----------|--------|
| Docu-series open rate < 25% | After Episode 1 | Revise subject lines; test resend to non-openers with new angle |
| Keynote completion < 25% | After 48hrs | Test shorter keynote cut; add chapter markers; test email reminder |
| Founders page conversion < 1.5% (of visitors) | After 1,000 visitors | Revise ATF headline; add video testimonial; test price presentation |
| Founders page conversion < 1.0% (of visitors) | After 2,000 visitors | Major revision: new headline, new proof stack order, consider $199 annual-only (remove lifetime temporarily) |
| SwingScan completion < 50% within 48hrs | After 100 purchases | Trigger automated reminder; simplify onboarding; add "complete your diagnosis" push |
| Diagnosis satisfaction < 60% | After 100 diagnoses | Product intervention: review diagnostic accuracy, add coach review layer, improve output specificity |
| 30-day retention < 70% | At 30-day mark | Implement re-engagement campaign; add weekly coach check-in; survey churned users |
| NPS < 30 | At 30-day mark | HALT public launch planning. Focus on product improvement. Survey detractors for specific issues. |

### Kill Criteria
| Criterion | Threshold | Action |
|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Founders enrollment < 200 after full launch sequence | 4 weeks post-keynote | Diagnose: pricing problem, messaging problem, or product problem. If pricing: test $199 lifetime. If messaging: test entirely new angle. If product: halt and rebuild. |
| 90-day retention < 40% | At 90-day mark | Do NOT proceed to public launch. Product-market fit is not established. Return to product development with Founders feedback. |
| NPS < 20 at 60 days | At 60-day mark | Product crisis. Engage Founders directly (1-on-1 calls with top 20 detractors). Identify systemic issue. Fix before ANY further marketing. |
| Zero measurable handicap improvement in >80% of active users at 90 days | At 90-day mark | The core promise has failed. Reassess the diagnostic engine accuracy, the plan effectiveness, and the coaching layer. Do not market what doesn't work. |

## OPP-02: End the Search

### Leading Indicators
| Indicator | Target | Measurement Method |
|-----------|--------|-------------------|
| Lifetime vs. annual selection rate | >60% lifetime | Checkout analytics |
| "Last investment" messaging resonance (A/B test) | Higher CTR than alternative | Email/ad A/B test |
| Checkout abandonment rate | <40% | Checkout analytics |
| Time from keynote view to purchase | <24hrs for 50%+ of converters | UTM + timestamp analysis |
| Post-purchase sentiment ("How do you feel about this purchase?") | >80% positive | Immediate post-purchase survey |

### Lagging Indicators
| Indicator | Target | Measurement Method |
|-----------|--------|-------------------|
| Refund/chargeback rate (30-day) | <5% | Payment processor |
| "Would you buy again?" (30-day survey) | >85% yes | Survey |
| Founders who cancel annual within year 1 | <20% | Subscription analytics |
| Organic word-of-mouth mentions | >50 social mentions in 90 days | Social listening |
| Founders who complete 4+ SwingScan sessions | >60% at 60 days | Product analytics |

### Decision Triggers
| Trigger | Threshold | Action |
|---------|-----------|--------|
| Lifetime selection < 40% | After 200 purchases | Highlight lifetime benefits more prominently; add "lifetime vs. annual" comparison visual |
| Checkout abandonment > 60% | After 500 page visits | Add trust signals (guarantee, security badges); simplify checkout; test one-click purchase |
| Refund rate > 10% at 30 days | At 30-day mark | Survey refund requesters; identify pattern; if diagnosis-related, escalate to product team |
| "Would you buy again?" < 70% | At 30-day mark | The "end the search" promise is not being delivered. Investigate which part of the experience fails to match expectations. |

### Kill Criteria
| Criterion | Threshold | Action |
|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Refund rate > 20% | At any point | Emergency: product is not meeting expectations. Pause all marketing. Diagnose and fix. |
| Lifetime annual churn > 40% at renewal | Year 1 | The "end the search" positioning failed for annual members. Reassess value delivery or convert remaining annuals to lifetime at discount. |

## Combined Dashboard View (Recommended)

### Week 1 Dashboard
- Docu-series open/click/completion rates
- Keynote view count and completion
- Founders page visits and conversion rate
- Enrollment count and velocity
- Annual/lifetime split

### Week 4 Dashboard
- Total Founders enrolled
- SwingScan completion rate
- Diagnosis satisfaction score
- 7-day and 14-day retention
- Refund rate
- Early NPS (informal)

### Week 12 Dashboard
- 30/60/90 day retention
- Formal NPS
- Handicap improvement data
- Organic referral count
- Founders testimonial pipeline
- Public launch readiness score (composite of all lagging indicators)
