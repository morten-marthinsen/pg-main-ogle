# Action Sequence — PG1 Founders Launch

**Date:** 2026-03-16
**Skill:** 3.3-B Action Sequencer
**Tier 1 Opportunities:** OPP-01 (Root Flaw Diagnosis), OPP-02 (End the Search)

---

## PHASE 1: IMMEDIATE (0-2 Weeks)

### RESEARCH
- R1: A/B test "root swing flaw" vs. "root cause" vs. "blind spot" in email subject lines to PG's list (1,000 sample per variant). Validate which framing produces highest open + click rates.
- R2: Survey 200 PG customers with one question: "What's the #1 thing you wish a golf app could tell you about your swing?" Validate diagnosis-first positioning.

### CREATE
- C1: Finalize docu-series content pieces 1-6. Ensure each piece maps to its intended belief layer (Confession→honesty, War Room→real stakes, $3M Bet→investment credibility, Coaches→coaching quality, Breakthrough→authenticity, 72 Hours→urgency).
- C2: Build pre-sale / content page to house docu-series episodes. Structure as episodic content hub with countdown to keynote.
- C3: Build Founders launch page with ATF (fast-action buyer: headline + CTA + price) and BTF (full sales argument: proof stack, objection handling, offer architecture, FAQ).
- C4: Draft 6-email keynote build-up sequence aligned with docu-series release cadence.
- C5: Create founders badge visual asset and numbered certificate mockup.

### TEST
- T1: Internal team walkthrough of the full buyer journey: docu-series → keynote → founders page → checkout. Identify friction points.
- T2: Test SwingScan diagnosis flow on 10 internal testers. Verify the diagnosis moment delivers a specific, personal, non-generic result within 60 seconds.

### LAUNCH
- L1: Soft-launch docu-series Episode 1 ("The Confession") to a 5,000-person segment of PG's email list. Measure open rate, watch completion, reply sentiment.

### MEASURE
- M1: Track docu-series Episode 1 metrics: email open rate, click rate, video play rate, video completion rate, reply/forward rate.
- M2: Establish baseline conversion expectations: target 3-5% of docu-series viewers converting to Founders at launch.

### OPTIMIZE
- O1: Based on Episode 1 performance, adjust subject lines, send times, and video length for subsequent episodes.

---

## PHASE 2: SHORT-TERM (2-6 Weeks)

### RESEARCH
- R3: After 100 Founders sign up, survey them: "What convinced you to join?" and "What almost stopped you?" Map to objection categories. Identify any unaddressed objections.
- R4: Monitor GolfWRX, Reddit r/golf, and social media for organic discussion of PG1 launch. Capture verbatim reactions for future ad creative.

### CREATE
- C6: Build 5-7 ad creative variations for retargeting non-converters. Test: diagnosis-first hook, financial comparison hook, 60-year-golfer social proof hook, "end the search" emotional hook, founders-identity hook.
- C7: Create post-purchase onboarding email sequence (Days 1, 3, 7, 14, 30). Each email ties to the root flaw diagnosis and progress milestones.
- C8: Produce 3 short-form video testimonials from early Founders showing their diagnosis moment and early results. Target: genuine reactions, not scripted.
- C9: Build "Your Root Flaw Report" shareable asset — a visual summary Founders can show playing partners (organic word-of-mouth driver).

### TEST
- T3: Split test Founders page: Version A (ATF leads with "Root Flaw Diagnosis" headline) vs. Version B (ATF leads with "End the Search / Last Investment" headline). Measure conversion rate difference.
- T4: Test $299 vs. $399 lifetime pricing on a 50/50 split (if Brixton's $399 recommendation is still in consideration). Measure conversion rate AND revenue per visitor.

### LAUNCH
- L2: Release remaining docu-series episodes on schedule (2 per week for 2 weeks).
- L3: Release keynote video to full docu-series audience.
- L4: Open Founders Club enrollment immediately after keynote. Countdown timer to 1,000-member cap.
- L5: Send targeted "last chance" emails at 500/750/900/950 members enrolled.

### MEASURE
- M3: Track Founders enrollment velocity: members per day, conversion rate from keynote viewers, annual vs. lifetime split.
- M4: Track docu-series completion funnel: Episode 1 → 2 → 3 → 4 → 5 → 6 → Keynote → Founders page → Purchase. Identify biggest drop-off point.
- M5: Track Founders activation: % who complete SwingScan diagnosis within 48 hours of purchase.

### OPTIMIZE
- O2: If conversion rate < 2% after keynote, deploy urgency email sequence emphasizing remaining spots and founder testimonials.
- O3: If annual/lifetime split is too annual-heavy, test highlighting lifetime benefits more prominently on the Founders page.
- O4: If diagnosis completion rate < 70% within 48 hours, trigger automated "Complete your SwingScan" push notification and email.

---

## PHASE 3: MEDIUM-TERM (6-12 Weeks)

### RESEARCH
- R5: At 30-day mark, survey all Founders: Net Promoter Score, feature requests, "Has your root flaw improved?", "Would you recommend PG1?"
- R6: Analyze SwingScan diagnostic data across all Founders: What are the most common root flaws? Which flaws show fastest improvement? Use for future marketing proof points.
- R7: Research expansion audiences beyond PG's existing list. Identify cold traffic segments and channels (YouTube pre-roll, Facebook/Instagram golf audiences, Google "golf improvement" search).

### CREATE
- C10: Produce Founders success stories — minimum 5 video testimonials with before/after handicap data and root flaw identification.
- C11: Create "Founders Results Report" — aggregate anonymized data showing average improvement, most common root flaws, satisfaction scores. Use as social proof for post-Founders public launch.
- C12: Develop Tier 2 opportunity creative: "Founders Identity" ad campaign targeting the aspirational early-adopter segment.
- C13: Build public launch page (post-Founders) with higher pricing and Founders testimonials as proof stack.

### TEST
- T5: Test cold traffic ad creative using Founders testimonials. Measure cost per acquisition vs. warm-list conversion rate.
- T6: A/B test public launch pricing: $299/yr vs. $399/yr vs. $499 lifetime vs. $599 lifetime.

### LAUNCH
- L6: If 1,000 Founders reached: close Founders enrollment. Announce public waitlist.
- L7: Launch retargeting campaign to non-converting docu-series viewers with Founders testimonials and "public pricing coming soon" messaging.
- L8: Begin planning public launch campaign (post-Founders) with validated messaging, proven creative, and real user results.

### MEASURE
- M6: Track 30/60/90 day retention rates for Founders. Target: 85%+ active at 30 days, 70%+ at 60 days, 60%+ at 90 days.
- M7: Track handicap improvement data for Founders who opt in to sharing. Target: measurable improvement for 50%+ within 90 days.
- M8: Track NPS score. Target: 50+ (excellent). Below 30 = product intervention needed before public launch.
- M9: Calculate Customer Acquisition Cost for warm list (Founders) vs. projected cold traffic CAC for public launch.

### OPTIMIZE
- O5: If retention drops below targets, identify where in the user journey engagement fades and intervene (push notifications, coach outreach, content updates).
- O6: If NPS < 30, halt public launch planning and focus on product improvement based on Founders feedback.
- O7: Based on Founders data, refine positioning for public launch. If "root flaw" language resonated strongly, double down. If "end the search" converted better, lead with that for cold traffic.


## Risk Mitigations Integrated

- **Competitive risk** (OPP-01): Addressed by Phase 1 speed — establish position before competitors react.
- **Execution risk** (OPP-01, OPP-02): Addressed by T2 (diagnosis testing), M5 (activation tracking), O4 (diagnosis completion optimization).
- **Messaging risk** (OPP-01): Addressed by R1 (language A/B test), T3 (headline split test).
- **Retention risk** (OPP-02): Addressed by C7 (onboarding sequence), M6+M7 (retention/improvement tracking), O5 (intervention).
