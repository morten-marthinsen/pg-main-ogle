## How to Use This File

Each pair connects a "WHY it fails" quote (showing a misconception or failed approach) with a "HOW to fix" quote (showing the correct understanding or better approach), linked by an educational frame. These are the "aha moment" building blocks for:
- VSL education sections (mechanism reveal)
- Email nurture sequences (drip-fed belief shifts)
- Advertorial content (teach before you sell)
- Ad hooks (curiosity-driven reframes)
- Sales page body copy (logical case building)
- FAQ objection handlers

Unlike the transformation pairs (2.5-A), which work on emotional contrast (Pain to Hope), educational pairs work on logical causation. They teach prospects WHY they have been stuck and HOW the right approach resolves the root cause. Every pair is mechanism-ready -- it sets up PG1's root-flaw diagnosis as the logical next step.

Priority tags indicate copy utility:
- **GOLD** = Category-defining insight. Reframes the entire problem. Use for VSL mechanism reveals, sales page "aha moment" sections, and lead magnet headlines.
- **SILVER** = Strong educational content. Clear misconception-to-truth arc. Use for email sequences, ad hooks, advertorial body copy, competitive differentiation.
- **BRONZE** = Supporting evidence. Valid educational arc, useful for proof stacking, secondary content, and objection handling.
