## RESEARCH BRIEF ADAPTATION
This project uses a non-standard brief format. The brief describes retention mechanics
research across subscription apps with golf-specific application. The 6 buckets map as:

- **PAIN**: User/subscriber frustrations, churn reasons, why people cancel, engagement drop-off complaints
- **HOPE**: Desired retention outcomes, "I wish my app would...", ideal engagement experiences
- **ROOT CAUSE**: Why retention fails — underlying systemic issues in subscription apps
- **SOLUTIONS TRIED**: What apps/companies have tried that works or fails — retention tactics in the wild
- **COMPETITOR MECHANISM**: How specific competitors (Duolingo, Peloton, Strava, 18Birdies, etc.) implement retention mechanics
- **VILLAIN**: What users blame for churn — bad UX, notification spam, lack of value, seasonal gaps
