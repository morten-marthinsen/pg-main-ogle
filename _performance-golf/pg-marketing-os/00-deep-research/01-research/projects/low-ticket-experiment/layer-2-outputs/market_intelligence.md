### Competitive Positioning Opportunity

**No competitor uses specimen-based calibration.** The entire market is stuck in one of four paradigms:
1. Post-processing (humanizers) -- fix AI output AFTER generation
2. Template-based -- constrain AI with pre-built structures
3. Prompt-based -- improve instructions to AI
4. Hybrid human-AI -- heavy manual editing after AI draft

Our approach (specimen-based pre-calibration) is a genuinely new category. This is a Stage 4-to-5 sophistication leap.
