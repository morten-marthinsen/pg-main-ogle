# Skill 2.2-C: Now-After Grid (E5 Tool)

**Version:** 2.0
**Layer:** 2 (Intelligence)
**Type:** E5 Method - Transformation Mapping
**Dependencies:** scored_quotes.json, web_analysis.json, belief_analysis.json
**Output:** now_after_grid.json

---

## Purpose

Map the complete transformation from current state (NOW) to desired state (AFTER). This creates the transformation promise and identifies the specific changes prospects want to experience.

---

## Grid Framework

```
THE NOW-AFTER GRID:

Two states, multiple dimensions:

NOW (Pain State)          AFTER (Desired State)
-----------------         --------------------
What they feel            What they want to feel
What they think           What they want to think
What they do              What they want to do
What they have            What they want to have
What they experience      What they want to experience
Who they are              Who they want to become

The gap between NOW and AFTER is the transformation promise.
```

## Analysis Logic

### Step 1: Map NOW States

```
FROM pain quotes and current state indicators:

FOR each dimension:

FEELING (NOW):
- Extract emotional states
- "I feel frustrated..."
- "I'm anxious when..."
- "The stress is..."

THINKING (NOW):
- Extract thought patterns
- "I keep thinking..."
- "I worry about..."
- "I don't understand why..."

DOING (NOW):
- Extract behaviors
- "I keep doing..."
- "I always..."
- "I can't stop..."

HAVING (NOW):
- Extract possessions/results
- "I have a losing account..."
- "I only have $X..."
- "I have no confidence..."

EXPERIENCING (NOW):
- Extract experiences
- "Every day I..."
- "When I trade, I..."
- "My life is..."

BEING (NOW):
- Extract identity
- "I am a..."
- "I'm the kind of person who..."
- "People see me as..."
```

### Step 2: Map AFTER States

```
FROM hope quotes and desired state indicators:

FOR each dimension:

FEELING (AFTER):
- "I want to feel..."
- "I dream of feeling..."
- "Imagine feeling..."

THINKING (AFTER):
- "I want to think..."
- "If only I could stop worrying..."
- "I wish I believed..."

DOING (AFTER):
- "I want to be able to..."
- "I wish I could..."
- "Imagine being able to..."

HAVING (AFTER):
- "I want to have..."
- "My goal is to have..."
- "I dream of having..."

EXPERIENCING (AFTER):
- "I want my days to be..."
- "Imagine experiencing..."
- "I wish I could experience..."

BEING (AFTER):
- "I want to become..."
- "I wish I was..."
- "I want to be seen as..."
```

### Step 3: Define Transformations

```
FOR each dimension:

CALCULATE transformation:
transformation = NOW state → AFTER state

ARTICULATE the change:
- What specifically changes?
- What shifts?
- What disappears?
- What appears?

EXAMPLE:
FEELING:
NOW: "Constant anxiety about losing money"
AFTER: "Calm confidence in my process"
TRANSFORMATION: "From anxiety to calm confidence"
```

### Step 4: Gap Analysis

```
ANALYZE gaps between NOW and AFTER:

BIGGEST GAPS:
- Which dimensions have largest distance?
- Which are most emotionally charged?
- Which are most frequently mentioned?

MOST DESIRED CHANGES:
- Rank by frequency of mention
- Rank by emotional intensity
- Rank by specificity of desire

MOST PAINFUL NOWS:
- Rank current states by pain level
- Identify most acute suffering
- Note urgency indicators
```

### Step 5: Extract Messaging Opportunities

```
FROM grid analysis:

NOW HOOKS:
- Opening lines that resonate with NOW state
- "Are you tired of..."
- "Do you find yourself..."
- "If you're feeling..."

AFTER PROMISES:
- Desired outcome statements
- "Imagine..."
- "What if you could..."
- "Picture yourself..."

TRANSFORMATION STATEMENTS:
- Before/after summaries
- "Go from X to Y"
- "Transform your..."
- "Leave behind X, embrace Y"
```

---

## Quality Criteria

1. **Completeness** - All 6 dimensions mapped
2. **Evidence** - Quotes support each state
3. **Specificity** - States are concrete, not vague
4. **Balance** - NOW and AFTER equally developed
