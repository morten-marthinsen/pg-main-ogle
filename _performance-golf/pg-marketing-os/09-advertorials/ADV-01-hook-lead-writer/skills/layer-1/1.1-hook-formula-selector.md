# 1.1-hook-formula-selector.md

> **Layer:** 1 (Architecture)
> **Skill:** ADV-01-hook-lead-writer
> **Purpose:** Select hook formula(s) based on advertorial type and editorial angle
> **Outputs:** `hook_formulas` object with selected formulas and application guidance

---

## FUNCTION

Select the hook formula(s) best suited to the advertorial type and editorial angle. Map specimen patterns to formula options. Each advertorial type has natural formula affinities -- this microskill enforces those affinities while allowing creative variation.

---

## HOOK FORMULA LIBRARY

| Formula | Description | Best Types | Example Pattern |
|---------|-------------|------------|-----------------|
| **Curiosity Statistic** | Open with surprising data point | Native, Editorial, Sponsored | "73% of Americans over 50 have this compound in their blood -- and most doctors never test for it." |
| **Contrarian Statement** | Challenge common belief | Native, Editorial, PAS | "Everything your doctor told you about cholesterol may be based on a 40-year-old mistake." |
| **Narrative Scene** | Open in middle of a story | Blog, Native, Sponsored | "Dr. Sarah Chen was reviewing patient scans at 2 AM when she noticed something that made her call her colleague immediately." |
| **Discovery Frame** | Frame as recent finding | Native, Editorial, Sponsored | "Researchers at Johns Hopkins have identified a compound that appears to reverse a process once thought to be irreversible." |
| **Question Hook** | Ask a question reader must answer | Listicle, Blog, PAS | "What if the morning routine doctors have recommended for decades is actually accelerating the problem?" |
| **Pattern Interrupt** | Contradict reader's expectation | Native, Editorial, Review | "The supplement your doctor recommends most may be blocking the one nutrient your body actually needs." |
| **Social Proof Open** | Lead with collective behavior | Listicle, Blog, Review | "Over 2 million Americans have quietly switched to a morning ritual that cardiologists are calling 'the most significant dietary change in a decade.'" |

---

## SELECTION PROTOCOL

### Step 1: Filter by Type Compatibility

```
FROM strategy.advertorial_type:
  FILTER formulas to type-compatible options:
    listicle -> [curiosity_statistic, question_hook, social_proof_open]
    native_article -> [curiosity_statistic, contrarian_statement, discovery_frame, pattern_interrupt]
    blog_style -> [narrative_scene, question_hook, social_proof_open]
    product_review -> [pattern_interrupt, contrarian_statement, social_proof_open]
    PAS -> [contrarian_statement, question_hook, curiosity_statistic]
    sponsored_content -> [curiosity_statistic, discovery_frame, narrative_scene]
    editorial_feature -> [curiosity_statistic, contrarian_statement, discovery_frame, pattern_interrupt]
```

### Step 2: Match to Angle

```
FROM strategy.editorial_angle.type:
  RANK filtered formulas by angle alignment:
    curiosity_gap -> prioritize curiosity_statistic, question_hook
    pattern_interrupt -> prioritize contrarian_statement, pattern_interrupt
    story_driven -> prioritize narrative_scene
    discovery_framed -> prioritize discovery_frame, curiosity_statistic
```

### Step 3: Cross-Reference Specimens

```
FROM specimen_patterns.hook_patterns:
  IDENTIFY which formulas appear in type-matched specimens
  BOOST formulas that align with proven specimen patterns
  NOTE novel formulas not in specimens (higher risk, higher potential)
```

### Step 4: Select Primary + Secondary Formulas

```
SELECT:
  primary_formula: Highest-ranked formula (used for main generation)
  secondary_formula: Second-ranked formula (used for variation generation)
  wildcard_formula: One formula outside natural affinity (for Arena diversity)

DOCUMENT rationale for each selection
```

## FAILURE MODES

| Mode | Trigger | Remediation |
|------|---------|-------------|
| No compatible formulas | Type-formula mapping returns empty | Expand to adjacent types |
| All formulas score equally | No clear winner | Select based on specimen frequency |
| Specimen patterns contradict formulas | Specimens use non-standard patterns | Honor specimen patterns over theory |
| Angle conflicts with formula | Hook direction doesn't fit any formula | Adapt formula to serve angle |
