# 4.1-quality-scorer.md

> **Layer:** 4 (Scoring)
> **Skill:** ADV-05-advertorial-editorial
> **Purpose:** Score final advertorial against editorial quality criteria
> **Outputs:** `quality_score` object with per-criterion and weighted scores

---

## FUNCTION

Score the final revised advertorial against 7 editorial quality criteria. This is the definitive quality assessment that determines publication readiness. The minimum weighted score is 8.0/10. Below this threshold, the piece is not publication-ready.

---

## SCORING PROTOCOL

### Step 1: Score Each Criterion

```
FOR each criterion:
  READ relevant sections of advertorial
  COMPARE against specimen benchmarks
  APPLY scoring rubric (from ARENA-LAYER.md)
  DOCUMENT specific evidence for score

CRITERIA:
  1. Editorial Authenticity (25%): Score 1-10
  2. Hook Strength (15%): Score 1-10
  3. Voice Unity (10%): Score 1-10
  4. Proof Integration (10%): Score 1-10
  5. Bridge Naturalness (10%): Score 1-10
  6. Compliance Readiness (10%): Score 1-10
  7. 5 Laws Satisfaction (20%): Score 1-10
```

### Step 2: Calculate Weighted Score

```
weighted_total =
  editorial_authenticity * 0.25 +
  hook_strength * 0.15 +
  voice_unity * 0.10 +
  proof_integration * 0.10 +
  bridge_naturalness * 0.10 +
  compliance_readiness * 0.10 +
  five_laws_satisfaction * 0.20

IF weighted_total >= 8.0: STATUS = PASS
IF weighted_total < 8.0: STATUS = FAIL
  IDENTIFY weakest criterion
  PROVIDE specific improvement guidance
```

### Step 3: 5 Laws Individual Assessment

```
FOR each law:
  Law 1 (Looks like content): PASS/FAIL with evidence
  Law 2 (Reads like article): PASS/FAIL with evidence
  Law 3 (Editorial smell test): PASS/FAIL with evidence
  Law 4 (One CTA, text link): PASS/FAIL with evidence
  Law 5 (Bridge = recommendation): PASS/FAIL with evidence

IF any law FAIL:
  Overall 5_laws_satisfaction score capped at 6.0
  DOCUMENT which law failed and why
```

## FAILURE MODES

| Mode | Trigger | Remediation |
|------|---------|-------------|
| Score < 8.0 | Weighted total below threshold | Identify weakest criteria, iterate revision |
| Law failure | Any of 5 Laws not satisfied | Target specific law violation |
| Score inflated | Evidence doesn't support score | Re-score with specific evidence |
