# Microskill 4.5: Distinctiveness Pass (Advertorials)

**Layer:** 4 (Fix Application)
**Purpose:** Detect whether editorial cleanup has collapsed advertorial copy into obvious sponsored content — losing the editorial disguise that makes advertorials work
**Sequence:** Runs AFTER quality scoring (4.1), word-count validation (4.2), and output packaging (4.3)

---

## WHY THIS PASS EXISTS

Advertorial editorial focuses on compliance, word count, and quality scoring — all pushing copy toward safe, disclosure-heavy language that screams "ad." This pass checks whether the editorial disguise survived cleanup.

**References:**
- `~system/humanization-specimens/SIGNATURE-MOVES.md` (Advertorials section)
- `~system/humanization-specimens/engine-packs/advertorial-pack.md`
- Campaign Soul.md
