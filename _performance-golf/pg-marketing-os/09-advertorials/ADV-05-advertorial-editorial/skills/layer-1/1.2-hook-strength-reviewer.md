# 1.2-hook-strength-reviewer.md

> **Layer:** 1 (Auditing)
> **Skill:** ADV-05-advertorial-editorial
> **Purpose:** Re-validate hook scroll-stop quality in context of full piece
> **Outputs:** `hook_review` object with strength assessment

---

## FUNCTION

Re-validate the hook's scroll-stop quality in the context of the complete assembled piece. A hook that tested well in isolation may lose power when followed by a particular lead/body combination. This review checks whether the hook's promises are fulfilled, whether its curiosity gap is properly resolved, and whether it still stops the scroll.

---

## HOOK REVIEW PROTOCOL

### Step 1: Scroll-Stop Re-Assessment

```
READ hook in context of full piece:
  SCORE:
    - pattern_interrupt_strength (1-10): Still compelling?
    - curiosity_gap_strength (1-10): Gap still engaging?
    - specificity (1-10): Concrete elements still impactful?
    - platform_compliance (1-10): Still within limits?

  COMPARE to ADV-01 scores (if available):
    IF score decreased by > 1 point on any dimension:
      FLAG: "Hook weakened in context -- [dimension] dropped from [X] to [Y]"
```

### Step 2: Promise Fulfillment Check

```
IDENTIFY what hook promises to reader:
  - What curiosity gap does it create?
  - What information does it imply the reader will receive?
  - What benefit/outcome does it suggest?

VERIFY fulfillment in body:
  - Is the curiosity gap resolved? Where?
  - Is the promised information delivered? Where?
  - Is the benefit/outcome addressed? Where?

IF any promise unfulfilled:
  FLAG: "Hook promises [X] but body never delivers"
  SEVERITY: moderate (reader feels cheated)
```

### Step 3: Hook-Lead Coherence

```
READ hook + first 2 lead paragraphs as unit:
  EVALUATE:
    - Does lead naturally follow hook?
    - Is there a jarring shift?
    - Does lead sustain hook's momentum?

  SCORE coherence (1-10)
  MINIMUM: 7
```

---

## FAILURE MODES

| Mode | Trigger | Remediation |
|------|---------|-------------|
| Hook weakened in context | Score dropped > 1 point | Strengthen specific dimension |
| Promise unfulfilled | Body doesn't deliver on hook | Add fulfillment or adjust hook |
| Hook-lead disconnect | Coherence < 7 | Smooth transition |
