# Microskill 3.4: Craft Quality Audit

**Layer:** 3 (5-Tier Evaluation)
**Purpose:** Evaluate line-level writing craft and polish

---

## TIER 4 OF 5: CRAFT QUALITY

This tier evaluates the quality of execution at the sentence and word level—the "craft" of the copywriting.

---

## WRITING CRAFT DIMENSIONS

### Dimension 1: Clarity & Precision

```yaml
clarity_precision:
  sentence_clarity:
    assessment: "[STRONG | ADEQUATE | WEAK]"
    unclear_sentences:
      - quote: "[unclear sentence]"
        location: "[where]"
        issue: "[why unclear]"
        clear_rewrite: "[improved version]"

  word_precision:
    assessment: "[STRONG | ADEQUATE | WEAK]"
    imprecise_word_choices:
      - word: "[imprecise word]"
        location: "[where]"
        precise_alternative: "[better word]"

  jargon_management:
    assessment: "[STRONG | ADEQUATE | WEAK]"
    unexplained_jargon:
```
