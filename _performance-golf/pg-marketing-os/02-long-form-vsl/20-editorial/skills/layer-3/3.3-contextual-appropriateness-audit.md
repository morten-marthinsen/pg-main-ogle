# Microskill 3.3: Contextual Appropriateness Audit

**Layer:** 3 (5-Tier Evaluation)
**Purpose:** Verify copy is appropriately calibrated for its specific context

---

## TIER 3 OF 5: CONTEXTUAL APPROPRIATENESS

This tier evaluates whether the copy is properly calibrated for:
- Target audience
- Market sophistication
- Product/price point
- Platform/medium
- Brand voice

---

## AUDIENCE CALIBRATION

### Target Audience Match

```yaml

  defined_target:
    demographics: "[age, gender, location, income]"
    psychographics: "[beliefs, values, pain points]"
    awareness_level: "[unaware to most-aware]"
    sophistication: "[naive to expert]"

  copy_calibration:
    language_complexity:
      reading_level: "[grade level]"
      appropriate_for_audience: "[yes | too complex | too simple]"
      violations:
        - quote: "[language that doesn't fit]"
          issue: "[too complex | too simple | wrong register]"

    cultural_references:
      references_used: ["[list references]"]
      audience_familiarity: "[high | medium | low | unknown]"
      violations:
        - reference: "[unfamiliar reference]"
          issue: "[why it doesn't fit]"

    pain_point_accuracy:
      pains_addressed: ["[list]"]
      match_to_target: "[exact | close | off-target]"
      violations:
        - pain_point: "[misaligned pain]"
          issue: "[why it doesn't resonate]"

    aspiration_alignment:
      aspirations_targeted: ["[list]"]

```
