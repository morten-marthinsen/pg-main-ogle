# Microskill 5.1: Second-Read Assessment

**Layer:** 5 (Validation & Output)
**Purpose:** Fresh read of edited copy to validate improvements and catch remaining issues

---

## SECOND-READ PROTOCOL

After all fixes have been applied, perform a complete fresh read—similar to Layer 1's blind read, but now assessing the EDITED copy.

**RULE:** Read the ENTIRE edited copy straight through without stopping. Do NOT cross-reference with earlier versions during the read. Experience it fresh.

---

## REMAINING ISSUE DETECTION

### New Issues Introduced

Sometimes fixes create new problems. Scan for:

```yaml
new_issues_scan:
  flow_disruptions:
    found: "[yes | no]"
    locations: ["[where new flow issues appeared]"]
    severity: "[minor | major]"

  voice_inconsistencies:
    found: "[yes | no]"
    locations: ["[where voice became inconsistent]"]
    severity: "[minor | major]"

  logic_gaps:
    found: "[yes | no]"
    locations: ["[where logic broke]"]
    severity: "[minor | major]"

  thread_breaks:
    found: "[yes | no]"
    locations: ["[where threading was disrupted]"]
    severity: "[minor | major]"

  tone_shifts:
    found: "[yes | no]"
    locations: ["[where tone shifted unexpectedly]"]
    severity: "[minor | major]"
```

### Unfixed Issues

Issues that were supposed to be fixed but weren't:

```yaml
unfixed_issues:
  - original_issue_id: "[ID]"
    expected_fix: "[what should have been done]"
    current_state: "[still problematic | partially fixed | different problem]"
    action_needed: "[re-fix | accept | escalate]"
```

### Persistent Issues

Issues that remain despite attempts:

```yaml
persistent_issues:
  - issue: "[description]"
    location: "[where]"
    fix_attempts: "[what was tried]"
    recommendation: "[accept | try alternative | flag for human review]"
```
