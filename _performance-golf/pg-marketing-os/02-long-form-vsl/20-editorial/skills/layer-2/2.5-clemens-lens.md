# Microskill 2.5: Clemens Lens

**Layer:** 2 (Multi-Expert Critique)
**Purpose:** Evaluate copy through Craig Clemens' mechanism architecture and scientific positioning principles

---

## CLEMENS EXPERTISE PROFILE

**Master of:** Mechanism architecture, institutional proof stacking, reader vindication
**Signature Approach:** Complex science made graspable + External failure attribution + Paradigm-shift positioning
**Core Philosophy:** "Give them a new enemy and a scientific weapon to defeat it"

**Source Material:** Gundry MD, Golden Hippo (LA-3, Patriot Power Greens), Beverly Hills MD, Pro Biodex America

---

## CLEMENS 10-POINT CRITIQUE CHECKLIST

### 1. Disruptive Promise
**Question:** Does the core promise challenge conventional wisdom?

```yaml

  assessment: "[STRONG | ADEQUATE | WEAK]"
  promise_identified: "[the main promise]"
  disruption_level:
    challenges_belief: "[strongly | somewhat | not really]"
    creates_cognitive_dissonance: "[yes | somewhat | no]"
    feels_new: "[breakthrough | interesting | familiar]"
  evidence:
    - quote: "[promise statement]"
      location: "[where]"
  clemens_principle: "The promise must make them question what they thought they knew"
```

## CLEMENS GOLD STANDARD EXAMPLES

Reference these patterns from Clemens' work:

**Gundry MD (Leaky Gut):**
- Binary reframe: "It's not your metabolism—it's lectins attacking your gut lining"
- Graspable metaphor: "Tiny holes in your intestine letting toxins leak into your bloodstream"
- External blame: Food industry, modern farming, misinformation

**Golden Hippo LA-3:**
- Mechanism name: "Activated AMPK"
- Institutional proof: Salk Institute research, Nobel Prize connection
- Complex/simple: "3 Nobel Prize-winning discoveries in one simple capsule"

**Beverly Hills MD Dermal Repair:**
- Credentials: "The doctor Hollywood celebrities trust with their faces"
- Fear-forward: Aging skin damage before solution
- Mechanism: Hydrolyzed collagen penetration

---

## EVIDENCE REQUIREMENTS

Every critique point MUST include:
- **Quote:** Exact text from the copy
- **Location:** Section or paragraph reference
- **Problem:** Specific diagnosis
- **Fix:** Concrete recommendation

**NO VAGUE CRITIQUES.** "Mechanism could be clearer" is unacceptable. "The mechanism explanation in paragraph 4 uses 'cellular optimization' without a graspable anchor—add a metaphor like 'think of your cells as tiny batteries that have been running on low charge for years'" is acceptable.
