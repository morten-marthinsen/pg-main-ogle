# Microskill 4.5: Distinctiveness Pass

**Layer:** 4 (Fix Application)
**Purpose:** Detect whether cleaned, polished copy has drifted toward generic competence — the "convergence toward mean" detector
**Sequence:** Runs AFTER 4.3 (anti-slop) and 4.4 (polish), which both push copy toward safety/smoothness and can inadvertently strip voice distinctiveness

---

## WHY THIS PASS EXISTS

Slop removal and polishing are subtractive — they remove bad patterns. But removal without replacement drifts copy toward "any competent copywriter." This pass checks whether the campaign's distinctive voice survived the cleanup.

**References:**
- Campaign Soul.md (voice/register definition)
- `~system/humanization-specimens/SIGNATURE-MOVES.md` (named positive techniques)
- Engine pack specimens (structural qualities)
