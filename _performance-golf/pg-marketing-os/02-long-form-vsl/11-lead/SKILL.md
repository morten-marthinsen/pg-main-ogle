# 11: LEAD WRITING

## The First 200-500 Words That Determine Everything

---

## PURPOSE

Hook the reader after the headline and pull them into the body copy. The lead
establishes the emotional tone, introduces the Big Idea, and creates enough
momentum to carry the reader through the entire piece.

**Output:** Lead section copy
**Requires:** Campaign Brief (Skill 09), Headlines (Skill 10)

**Success Criteria:**
- Lead hooks the reader within the first 100 words
- Emotional resonance established (reader feels understood)
- Open loop or curiosity mechanism planted
- Voice register matches Soul.md constraints
- Output: lead prose section produced

---
