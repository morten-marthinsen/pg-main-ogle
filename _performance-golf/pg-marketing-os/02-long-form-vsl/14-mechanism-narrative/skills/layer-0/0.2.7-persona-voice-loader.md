### Selection Priority for This Skill

Since this skill uses `generative_full_draft` arena mode, prioritize **actual selling copy specimens** that show each writer's voice, vocabulary, rhythm, and persuasion techniques in the specific type of copy this skill generates.

For mechanism narratives, this is where Clemens SHINES above all other personas. His VSLs are BUILT around mechanism explanation — the entire persuasion architecture revolves around making a complex biological or scientific mechanism graspable to a mass audience. Clemens' signature techniques include binary reframes ("it's either on or it's off"), 12-year-old language (if a kid can't understand it, simplify further), and "just think about it" moments that redirect the reader's attention to their own intuitive understanding. Schwartz's ads show sophisticated mechanism framing where the mechanism is introduced through the lens of a new scientific understanding. Makepeace's controls show mechanism-to-product bridges — how to move smoothly from "here's how it works" to "here's how you get it." Bencivenga's persuasion equation (Maxim 12) provides the proof framework for mechanism credibility — every mechanism claim must be supported by evidence positioned in the right order.
