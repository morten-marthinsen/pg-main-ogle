# Microskill 3.2: Avatar Resonance Checker

**Layer:** 3 (Refinement)
**Purpose:** Ensure proof resonates with target avatar's identity, concerns, and aspirations

---

## CORE PRINCIPLE

Proof must feel like it's about PEOPLE LIKE ME. If testimonials feature people the avatar can't identify with, if data addresses concerns the avatar doesn't have, if outcomes don't match avatar aspirations — proof fails regardless of how strong it is objectively.

## RESONANCE AMPLIFICATION TECHNIQUES

### Technique 1: The "Just Like You" Bridge

**Add explicit connection to avatar:**
```
"Sarah is 54. Works a demanding job. Takes care of her aging parents. Sound familiar?

She'd tried every diet, every supplement, every exercise program...

And like you, she was about to give up."
```

---

### Technique 2: The Outcome Translation

**Translate proof outcomes to avatar-specific terms:**

Raw outcome: "Lost 34 pounds"

For busy professional avatar:
```
"Lost 34 pounds — which meant she could finally keep up with her kids and still have energy for evening work sessions."
```

For appearance-focused avatar:
```
"Lost 34 pounds — which meant she finally felt confident walking into any room."
```

---

### Technique 3: The Struggle Validation

**Show proof subject had same doubts:**
```
"[Name] was skeptical too. After years of disappointment, who could blame her?

But she decided to give it one more try..."
```

---

### Technique 4: The "What It Meant For Them" Frame

**Connect outcomes to life impact:**
```
"For John, the 62% improvement in blood flow meant he could play 18 holes without needing to rest after every hole.

For Mary, it meant she could finally walk her daughter down the aisle without a wheelchair.

For you, it might mean [avatar-specific outcome]..."
```

## RESONANCE AUDIT CHECKLIST

### For Each Testimonial

```
[ ] Subject age within 10 years of avatar (or framed appropriately)
[ ] Subject situation relatable to avatar
[ ] Subject struggle matches avatar struggle
[ ] Subject outcome matches avatar aspiration
[ ] Subject voice matches avatar communication style
[ ] "That could be me" reaction achievable
```

### For Each Data Point

```
[ ] Data addresses avatar's concern (not abstract benefit)
[ ] Data presented in avatar-accessible terms
[ ] Data implication spelled out for avatar
[ ] "So what?" answered for avatar specifically
```

### For Each Authority Proof

```
[ ] Authority credible TO THIS AVATAR (not just objectively)
[ ] Authority communicates in avatar-appropriate register
[ ] Authority's experience relevant to avatar's situation
```
