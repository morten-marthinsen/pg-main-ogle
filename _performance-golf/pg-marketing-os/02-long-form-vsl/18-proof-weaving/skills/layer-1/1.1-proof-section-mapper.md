# Microskill 1.1: Proof Section Mapper

**Layer:** 1 (Architecture)
**Purpose:** Map proof placement to EVERY section of the copy structure

---

## CORE PRINCIPLE

Proof is not just for "the proof section." Elite copy weaves proof throughout EVERY section. This microskill creates a complete map of where proof belongs.

---

## SECTION-BY-SECTION PROOF MAPPING

For each section in the structure-package, determine:

### Lead Section

| Proof Opportunity | Purpose | Element Type |
|-------------------|---------|--------------|
| Authority Anchor | Establish right to speak | Credentials, track record |
| Credibility Hook | Create immediate trust | TV appearance, publication, scale |
| Promise Validation | Support opening claim | Transformation hint, testimonial fragment |

**Density Target:** 1-2 elements
**Placement:** Within first 500 words

---

### Story Section

| Proof Opportunity | Purpose | Element Type |
|-------------------|---------|--------------|
| Transformation Proof | Show change is possible | Before/after, specific outcomes |
| Relatable Suffering | Validate reader's pain | Protagonist struggle details |
| Expert Validation | Introduce credibility | Yoda figure's credentials |

**Density Target:** 2-3 elements
**Placement:** Woven through narrative beats

---

### Root Cause Section

| Proof Opportunity | Purpose | Element Type |
|-------------------|---------|--------------|
| Failure Explanation | Prove why nothing else worked | Logic proof, data |
| Villain Evidence | Support villain accusation | Studies, data points |
| Counter-Intuitive Validation | Prove surprising claim | Expert citation, demonstration |

**Density Target:** 1-2 elements
**Placement:** Supporting each root cause claim

---

### Mechanism Section

| Proof Opportunity | Purpose | Element Type |
|-------------------|---------|--------------|
| Mechanism Validation | Prove mechanism works | Study citations, demonstrations |
| Scientific Backing | Add institutional weight | Research references |
| Simplification Proof | Show mechanism is simple | Before/after logic |
| Results Demonstration | Show measurable outcomes | Specific numbers, timeframes |

**Density Target:** 3-4 elements (HEAVIEST non-proof section)
**Placement:** After each mechanism claim

---

### Proof Section (Dedicated)

| Proof Opportunity | Purpose | Element Type |
|-------------------|---------|--------------|
| Testimonial Cascade | Build social proof momentum | 3-5 testimonials in sequence |
| Scale Proof | Show pattern, not fluke | Customer counts, sales figures |
| Variety Demonstration | Show works for different people | Diverse testimonials |
| Transformation Parade | Stack outcome evidence | Before/after series |

**Density Target:** 6-8+ elements (HEAVIEST)
**Placement:** Concentrated section

---

### Product Introduction Section

| Proof Opportunity | Purpose | Element Type |
|-------------------|---------|--------------|
| Formula Validation | Prove product efficacy | Component proof, demonstration |
| Development Story Proof | Show expertise behind product | Expert credentials |
| Results Preview | Hint at outcomes | Single strong testimonial |

**Density Target:** 2-3 elements
**Placement:** Supporting product claims

---

### Offer Section

| Proof Opportunity | Purpose | Element Type |
|-------------------|---------|--------------|
| Value Testimonial | Support price justification | "Worth every penny" type |
| Risk Removal Proof | Support guarantee | Satisfaction stats |
| Bonus Validation | Prove bonus value | Results from bonus use |

**Density Target:** 1-2 elements
**Placement:** Near price reveal, near guarantee

---

### Close Section

| Proof Opportunity | Purpose | Element Type |
|-------------------|---------|--------------|
| Transformation Reminder | Callback to earlier proof | Reference to lead/story transformation |
| Scale Callback | Remind of mass validation | Reference to customer counts |
| Future Pacing Proof | Show what's possible | "Imagine" scenarios grounded in real results |

**Density Target:** 2-3 elements
**Placement:** Between CTAs, in P.S.

---

## DRAMATIZATION OPPORTUNITY SCAN

Beyond standard placements, scan for ADDITIONAL dramatization opportunities:

### Claim Proximity Check
For every significant claim in copy, check:
- [ ] Is there proof within 2-3 sentences?
- [ ] If not, can proof be added?
- [ ] What proof type would strengthen this claim?

### Objection Anticipation Points
Where might reader become skeptical?
- [ ] Bold promise → Need proof immediately after
- [ ] Price reveal → Need value proof
- [ ] Mechanism explanation → Need validation proof
- [ ] Urgency claim → Need scarcity proof

### Emotional Peak Points
Where is reader most engaged?
- [ ] Story climax → Transformation proof reinforcement
- [ ] Mechanism "aha" → Validation proof
- [ ] Close decision point → Final proof push
