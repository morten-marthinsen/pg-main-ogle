### Selection Priority for This Skill

Since this skill uses `generative_full_draft` arena mode, prioritize **actual selling copy specimens** that show each writer's voice, vocabulary, rhythm, and persuasion techniques in the specific type of copy this skill generates.

For product introductions, prioritize specimens showing the mechanism-to-product bridge — the most dangerous transition in any promotion. This is where education becomes selling, and the copy must make the product feel like a natural, inevitable conclusion to the mechanism narrative rather than a jarring commercial pivot. Makepeace's controls show smooth product reveals where the product enters as the logical answer to everything the reader just learned. Ogilvy's ads are product-centered with credibility positioning — he makes the product itself carry authority through manufacturing quality, engineering details, and institutional backing. Bencivenga's promo letters show product-as-hero avoidance — the product is always positioned as the delivery vehicle for the mechanism, never the star of the show. Clemens' VSLs show supplement component reveals with proof packages where each ingredient carries its own mini-evidence stack.
