### Selection Priority for This Skill

Since this skill uses `generative_full_draft` arena mode, prioritize **actual selling copy specimens** that show each writer's voice, vocabulary, rhythm, and persuasion techniques in the specific type of copy this skill generates.

For stories, prioritize specimens with strong narrative sections — pieces where the writer tells a story that carries persuasive weight. Halbert's Boron Letters are IDEAL for this skill: they are father-son storytelling at its most raw and personal, demonstrating how narrative creates emotional connection and trust. The letters ARE stories — real ones that teach through experience. Makepeace's Speed Profits has a proof story / live experiment narrative showing how data can be woven into a gripping story arc. Schwartz's ads contain discovery and reveal narratives where the mechanism is uncovered through a story structure. Bencivenga's maxims are anecdotal — story-wrapped teaching where each principle is illustrated through a specific example or case study. Ogilvy's ads have product-story integration showing how to make the product itself the protagonist of a compelling narrative.
