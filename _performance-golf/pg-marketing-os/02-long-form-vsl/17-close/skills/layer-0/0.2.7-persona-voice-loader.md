### Selection Priority for This Skill

Since this skill uses generative_full_draft arena mode, prioritize actual selling copy specimens that show each writer's voice, vocabulary, rhythm, and persuasion techniques in the specific type of copy this skill generates.

For close copy, prioritize specimens showing closing techniques — the final persuasive push that converts consideration into commitment. Makepeace controls show all 6 foundational close elements (benefit summary, guarantee as confidence, repeated asks, action instructions, P.S. sections, sidebars) with graceful transitions between them. Bencivenga's promo letters show proof-cascade closes where evidence does the heavy lifting in the final push. Halbert's Boron Letters show personal-stake urgency and raw, emotional closing with direct asks. Ogilvy ads show dignified close approaches where the final ask feels like a natural conclusion rather than a sales push. Schwartz ads show sophisticated urgency calibrated to market awareness level. Clemens VSLs show branded guarantee presentation and binary choice crossroads execution. Priority: closing sections of full controls and any specimen that demonstrates the art of asking for the sale repeatedly without becoming patronizing.
