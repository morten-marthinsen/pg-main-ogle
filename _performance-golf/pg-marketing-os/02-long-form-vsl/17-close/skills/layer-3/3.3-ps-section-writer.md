---

## PURPOSE

Writes the P.S. section of the close — the second-most-read section of the entire promotion after the headline. Many prospects skip directly from the headline to the P.S., making this prime selling real estate. Clayton Makepeace identifies 6 specific P.S. techniques, and this microskill selects and executes 1-3 of them to create a P.S. section that could nearly standalone as a powerful sell.

The P.S. must do one or more of: introduce a fast-reply bonus, offer a call-in bonus, reveal an additional bonus, present the strongest testimonials, provide additional reasons to act NOW, or restate the guarantee. The best P.S. sections take up almost a full page and serve as a final, concentrated burst of selling power.

This microskill uses the P.S. strategy selected in Layer 1 (microskill 1.4) and writes the actual prose. It includes a CTA within the P.S. itself, maintaining the repetition that Makepeace identifies as the master closer's secret.

---

## IDENTITY

**This microskill IS:**
- The P.S. section prose writer for the close
- An executor of Makepeace's 6 P.S. techniques
- A prime selling real estate optimizer — P.S. is second-most-read
- A final-ammo deployer — saves the BEST material for the P.S.
- A CTA-within-P.S. integrator — the P.S. includes its own ask for the sale
- A multi-P.S. builder — can create P.S., P.P.S., and P.P.P.S. sections

**This microskill is NOT:**
- A P.S. strategy selector (that is 1.4 — strategy already chosen)
- A closing theme writer (that is 2.3)
- A CTA sequence designer (that is 2.4 — though P.S. contains CTAs)
- An urgency creator (that is 3.1 — though P.S. amplifies urgency)
- The guarantee writer (that is 2.2 — though P.S. may restate it)

## PROCESS

### Step 1: Review P.S. Strategy Assignment

1. Load the P.S. strategy from 1.4 output
2. Confirm which 1-3 techniques are assigned:

| Technique | Purpose | Best Material |
|-----------|---------|---------------|
| A: Fast-reply bonus | Light urgency fire | Time-limited bonus with strong desire pull |
| B: Call-in bonus | Reduce friction, increase accountability | Phone-exclusive bonus, human connection |
| C: Additional FREE bonus | Surprise delight, pile on value | Previously unrevealed bonus with fascinations |
| D: Most powerful testimonials | Soothe final doubt | 2-3 strongest proof assets |
| E: Additional reasons NOW | Push urgency with logic | 3-5 compressed reasons why |
| F: Restate guarantee | Reinforce zero-risk | Guarantee tied to specific performance promise |

3. Confirm what "best ammo" was reserved for the P.S.

### Step 2: Write P.S. #1

4. For the FIRST P.S., use the primary assigned technique:

**If Technique A (Fast-Reply Bonus):**
```
P.S. — I almost forgot... When you order within the next [timeframe],
I'll also include [bonus name] — a [$value] value — absolutely FREE.
[2-3 fascination bullets about this bonus]. But this bonus is only
available for the next [X] orders, so [action language].
```

**If Technique B (Call-In Bonus):**
```
P.S. — Here's a special reason to call in your order TODAY... When you
call [phone number], you'll also receive [exclusive bonus] — something
we simply can't offer through the website. [Explain why calling is better].
Our team is standing by right now at [phone number].
```

**If Technique C (Additional FREE Bonus):**
```
P.S. — I didn't mention this earlier, but when you order today, you'll
also receive [bonus name] — absolutely FREE. [Romance the bonus with
2-3 fascination bullets]. This alone is worth [$value], and it's yours
FREE when you [action language].
```

**If Technique D (Testimonials):**
```
P.S. — Don't just take my word for it. Here's what [name, credentials]
had to say: "[Testimonial quote]" And [name] isn't alone... [Additional
testimonial]. These are REAL people experiencing REAL results. You could
be next — [action language].
```

**If Technique E (Additional Reasons):**
```
P.S. — Let me give you [X] more reasons why you should act on this
right now: [Numbered list of compressed reasons, each 1-2 sentences,
including savings, exclusivity, proof, scarcity, guarantee]. What are
you waiting for? [Action language].
```

**If Technique F (Guarantee Restatement):**
```
P.S. — Remember, you're protected by our [branded guarantee name].
You must [specific performance promise] — or I'll rush you a complete
refund. [Tie guarantee to specific dollar outcome or benefit]. When was
the last time someone made you an offer like this? [Action language].
```

5. Write the prose naturally — not filling a template but using it as a structural guide
6. Include a CTA within the P.S. (this counts toward the 6-10+ CTA requirement)
7. Ensure the P.S. action language differs from CTAs already used in the close

### Step 3: Write P.P.S. (If 2+ P.S. Planned)

8. For the second P.S., use the secondary assigned technique
9. The P.P.S. should escalate from the P.S. — raise the stakes or intensity
10. Include its own CTA with yet another emotional angle
11. P.P.S. can be shorter than P.S. — concentrated punch

### Step 4: Write P.P.P.S. (If 3 P.S. Planned)

12. The third P.S. is the FINAL text of the entire promotion
13. Make it powerful, concise, and action-oriented
14. This is literally the last chance to close — make it count
15. Often works best as a guarantee + urgency combination

### Step 5: Verify P.S. Real Estate Usage

16. Quality check the P.S. section:
    - Is the P.S. using PRIME ammo (not leftovers)?
    - Could a prospect who reads ONLY the headline and P.S. be compelled to act?
    - Does each P.S. advance the close (not just restate information)?
    - Is there at least one CTA within the P.S. section?
    - Does the P.S. feel like a concentrated burst of selling power?

17. Word count check:
    - Single P.S.: 50-150 words
    - P.S. + P.P.S.: 100-250 words combined
    - P.S. + P.P.S. + P.P.P.S.: 150-350 words combined
    - Elite P.S. sections can take almost a full page

| Criterion | Pass Condition |

| CTA language varied | P.S. CTAs differ from close-body CTAs |

## FAILURE MODES

### Failure Mode 1: Leftover Ammo in P.S.
- **Symptom:** P.S. mentions weak benefits or minor bonuses
- **Cause:** Best ammo was used in the close body, P.S. got what was left
- **Prevention:** 1.4 strategy selector RESERVES best material for P.S.
- **Recovery:** Move strongest proof/bonus from close body to P.S., replace with second-tier

### Failure Mode 2: P.S. as Administrative Text
- **Symptom:** "P.S. — To order, call 1-800-XXX-XXXX or visit website.com"
- **Cause:** P.S. treated as instructions, not selling
- **Prevention:** Step 5 checks that P.S. ADVANCES the close, not just provides logistics
- **Recovery:** Rewrite P.S. with selling technique, put logistics in action instructions section

### Failure Mode 3: P.S. Repeats Close Body
- **Symptom:** P.S. restates the guarantee identically to the guarantee section
- **Cause:** Writer ran out of material, defaulted to repetition
- **Prevention:** Step 5 checks that P.S. adds NEW selling power
- **Recovery:** If restating guarantee, tie it to a NEW angle (e.g., specific dollar outcome)
