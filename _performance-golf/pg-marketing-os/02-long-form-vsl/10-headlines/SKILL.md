# 10: HEADLINE GENERATION

## The Most Critical Copy Element

---

## PURPOSE

Produce headline candidates that stop, hook, and compel the reader to continue.
Every headline is scored against proven criteria and ranked for testing priority.

**Output:** Headline package with ranked variants
**Requires:** Campaign Brief (Skill 09)

**Success Criteria:**
- 10+ headline variants generated across 3+ formula categories
- Each variant scored against proven criteria and ranked
- Top 3 headlines pass the "would I click this?" test
- Specimen decomposition completed for reference headlines
- Output: headline package with ranked variants
