# Performance Golf Copywriting System

## Brand Foundation

**Idea Worth Rallying Around:** Love Your Game

**Promise:** Your path to playing better golf.

**Mission:** Transform the games, and lives, of millions of golfers with the world's simplest personalized golf improvement system.

**Core Values:**
- **Transformation** - Meaningful long-term change, not quick fixes
- **Empowerment** - Simple tools + confidence to succeed
- **Accessibility** - Golf improvement within reach for everyone

**Ultimate Identity Transformation:**
- FROM: "I'm a frustrated golfer stuck in scattered confusion"
- TO: "I'm someone who loves their game and gets better every time I play"

## The Enemy & The Weapon

**THE ENEMY: The Scattered Playbook**
- Random YouTube tips that contradict each other
- Magazine drills with no context
- Standing over the ball with 12 swing thoughts
- Creates paralysis and confusion

**THE WEAPON: One Connected System (PG1)**
- Personalized path for THEIR game
- Right thing, right time, right order
- Adapts as they improve
- Eliminates guesswork

**Linguistic Pattern:**
"You've been [struggling with symptom]. Not because you [lack talent]. But because The Scattered Playbook has been [keeping you confused]. Once you see it, you can replace it. And [PG1's personalized system] is designed specifically to defeat The Scattered Playbook."

## Voice Quick Reference

**Voice Persona:** Brixton - Passionate golfer who found the path and wants to share it. Encouraging without rah-rah. Confident without dismissive.

**Brand Archetype:** The Magician - Turns frustration into confidence, confusion into clarity.

**Voice Pillars:**
1. **UNWAVERING** - Calm certainty about transformation. "It will happen."
2. **CONSTRUCTIVE** - Personalized, pragmatic, clarifying. No tech jargon.
3. **UPLIFTING** - Joy-focused. Those magical moments.

**Signature Phrases:**
- "Love your game"
- "Your path to playing better golf"
- "Make progress permanent"
- "Less guesswork. More fireworks"
- "That magical feeling"

For complete voice guidelines, word kit, and elevator pitches, see [[PG - Brixton's Voice + Messaging]].

## Copy Workflow

### Step 1: Identify the Belief to Shift
Every piece of copy should shift one or more of the 10 core beliefs. See [[belief-framework]].

Key beliefs:
1. **Loving Your Game** - Joy comes from the journey, not just destination
2. **The Real Problem** - The Scattered Playbook is the enemy
3. **Time** - Progress becomes predictable with the right system
4. **Money** - NOT doing this costs more
5. **Method** - ONE Connected System beats scattered tips
6. **Help** - Getting the right guidance is smart, not weak
7. **Personalization** - MY game needs MY path
8. **Transformation** - Real change, not band-aids
9. **Integration** - One connected system transforms together
10. **Identity** - Built for everyday golfers like me

### Step 2: Choose Story Type
Match story type to belief. See [[_Performance Golf/storytelling]].

- **Universal Story (Parable)** - Analogy that reinforces belief
- **Personal Story (Hell to Heaven)** - Brixton's journey
- **Client Story (Case Study)** - Someone like them who transformed
- **External Authority** - Research/data that validates
- **Internal Data** - Results across multiple clients
- **Other Area Transfer** - They already believe this elsewhere

### Step 3: Write with Physical Reality
Name specific moments, emotions, and physical sensations:
- "Death grip on the club, tension creeping up forearms"
- "Saturday morning, 8:47am. Three foursomes waiting behind you."
- "That frustrated walk back to the cart"

### Step 4: Apply the 11-Point Checklist
Before publishing, verify all 11 points. See [[copy-checklist]].

Quick version:
1. Named the transformation (FROM → TO)
2. Named specific emotions (frustrated, embarrassed, confident)
3. Included physical sensations (tight chest, death grip)
4. Put them in a specific moment (Saturday morning, first tee)
5. Made progress feel attainable (not fantasy)
6. Emphasized personalization without tech jargon
7. Led with feeling, then showed data
8. Named The Scattered Playbook as enemy
9. Positioned PG1 as weapon against confusion
10. Used Brixton's voice
11. Focused on loving the game

### Step 5: Check Restrictions
Before finalizing, verify against forbidden language and required attributions. See [[PG - Copy Restrictions]].

Critical restrictions:
- Hank Haney = "Tiger's FORMER coach" (never current, never "ex-coach")
- Jack Nicklaus = "Golfer of the Century Jack Nicklaus"
- Never say "USGA approved" → "conforms to the rules of golf"
- Never mention: Golf Fanatic, Moe Norman, Dustin Johnson, Titleist, Sir Nick Faldo

## Audience: Ari (The Everyday Golfer)

- Smart, successful, confident in most things—but golf humbles them
- Tried the tips, bought the clubs, watched the videos
- Golf is their thing, they'll make time for what works
- Wants: Clear path that's trustworthy and built for THEM
- NOT tour pros. Real players with real lives, day jobs, weekend tee times.

## Forbidden Patterns

❌ Overhype ("CRUSH 50 YARDS!!!")
❌ Tech jargon ("AI-powered ML algorithms")
❌ Stern/dismissive tone
❌ Golf clichés ("take your game to the next level")
❌ Promises of perfection
❌ Robotic/clinical voice
❌ Data before emotion
❌ Focus on pros (this is for everyday golfers)

## Good vs Bad Examples

**Transformation:**
- ❌ "Get better at golf with our training system"
- ✓ "From standing over every shot second-guessing yourself to knowing exactly what to do"

**Emotion:**
- ❌ "You're not happy with your game"
- ✓ "That frustrated walk back to the cart after you chunk another wedge shot"

**Personalization:**
- ❌ "Our AI-powered algorithm analyzes your biomechanics"
- ✓ "Your game improvement plan—built for where you are now"

**Brixton's Voice:**
- ❌ "Utilize our comprehensive methodology to enhance performance metrics"
- ✓ "Look, you're better than your current scores show. Let's get you there with a clear path that actually makes sense."

## Product Positioning

**PG1 (Personalized Game Improvement):**
- The weapon against The Scattered Playbook
- ONE connected system vs scattered random tips
- "Know exactly what to work on next"
- "No more sifting through chaos"

**72-Hour Golf Transformation Academy:**
- Root flaw identification (vs treating symptoms)
- "Fix the root, everything else falls into place"
- For committed golfers ready to eliminate confusion

## Balance Formula

- Emotion + Data = Compelling
- Personal + Universal = Relatable
- Confident + Humble = Trustworthy
- Technical + Human = Accessible

## Final Check

Before publishing, ask: **Does this help everyday golfers love their game?**

If yes, you're on brand.
