# Script 1: VIP Coaching — Coach Chat Introduction

**Voice:** Martin Chuck (Golf Digest Top-10 Coach)
**Format:** On-camera intro → voiceover on screen share
**Length:** ~110 seconds
**Placement:** Coaching introduction video embedded in Connect / My Coach section of PG1 Pulse Smart Path
**Goal:** Get the user to upload their first swing video
**Review:** Brixton Albert

---

## Script

[Martin Chuck on camera — warm, direct, confident]

Hey — I'm Martin Chuck, and this is how coaching works inside Performance Golf.

Here's the truth about why most golfers stay stuck. It's not effort. It's not talent. It's that they're working on the wrong thing.

Every swing has what we call a root flaw — one thing that, when you fix it, three or four other problems start fixing themselves. It's the lead domino. And until somebody identifies yours, you're guessing. You're putting in the work, but you're putting it in the wrong place.

That's what your coach is here to find.

[Transition to screen share — My Coach page]

This is your coaching page. You've got an assigned PG1 Certified Coach right here — real person, real expertise, here specifically for your game.

They're not giving you generic advice. They're watching YOUR swing, finding YOUR root flaw, and building a plan around fixing that one thing first — so everything else starts falling into place.

You can also message them anytime. Ask questions, share what you're working on, tell them what's frustrating you. This isn't a one-time review — it's an ongoing coaching relationship.

[Screen highlights the "Upload Swing Video" button]

But here's where it all starts. Upload a swing video. Takes about 60 seconds, and I'll show you exactly how.

[Screen: click "Upload Swing Video" — phone mock appears showing camera roll]

Tap "Upload Swing Video." Your camera roll opens. Pick a video you've already recorded, or go film one right now.

[Screen: visual showing the two camera positions]

Two videos are ideal. For the first one, set your phone up a few feet behind you, pointed at your target — so the camera sees the same thing you're aiming at. For the second, put the phone in front of you, facing you straight on.

Keep each one under a minute. That's all your coach needs to see what's going on.

Hit submit. Done.

[Screen: swing analysis overlay — Swing Flaw identification, personalized breakdown]

Within 48 hours, your coach sends you a live video breakdown of YOUR swing. Not a text message. Not a generic tip. A real video — showing you exactly what your root flaw is, why it's been holding you back, and the specific drills to start fixing it.

This is what changes everything. You stop guessing. You stop working on the wrong thing. You start practicing smarter — not more — because you finally know exactly what to work on.

[Screen: chat input area]

And your coach is always right here. As you practice, as things start clicking — or if they don't — send them a message. That's what they're here for.

[Martin on camera — or final voiceover, warm close]

Upload your first swing right now. Two quick videos, under a minute each.

Your coach is ready to find the one thing that's going to unlock everything else.
