## 2. The Swing DNA Score Architecture

### Core Definition

**Swing DNA Score** = How dialed-in your swing is to YOUR personalized optimal model (0-100)

This is NOT:
- A comparison to pro golfers (creates anxiety)
- A generic swing rating (not actionable)
- A handicap proxy (outcome-based, not process-based)

This IS:
- Your personalized blueprint based on YOUR body
- A measure of alignment to YOUR optimal mechanics
- A tangible number that improves with deliberate practice

### The Personalization Engine

Your Swing DNA profile is built from:

| Input Category | Data Points | Source |
|----------------|-------------|--------|
| **Physical Profile** | Height, weight, arm length, flexibility | Onboarding assessment |
| **Mobility Assessment** | Hip rotation, shoulder turn, spine angle | In-app movement screen |
| **Skill Baseline** | Current handicap, typical misses, goals | Self-reported + SwingScan |
| **Swing Fingerprint** | Natural tempo, plane tendency, power sources | SwingScan AI 2.0 |

**Output:** A personalized optimal swing model—the swing YOUR body is built to execute.

### Score Components

Swing DNA Score is calculated from five stations (matching SwingScan AI 2.0):

| Station | Weight | What It Measures | Why It Matters |
|---------|--------|------------------|----------------|
| **Setup** | 15% | Grip, posture, alignment, ball position | Foundation for everything |
| **Backswing** | 20% | Takeaway path, arm structure, turn | Sets up power and plane |
| **Top** | 20% | Position, arm plane, wrist conditions | Transition readiness |
| **Impact** | 30% | Club delivery, face angle, path | Where the ball is hit |
| **Finish** | 15% | Balance, extension, rotation completion | Indicator of swing health |

**Total:** 100 points possible = 100% Swing DNA alignment

### Score Display Philosophy

Following Oura/WHOOP best practices:

- **Single Number:** 0-100, shown prominently
- **Color Coding:** Red (0-50), Yellow (51-75), Green (76-100)
- **Trend Indicator:** Arrow showing 7-day trajectory
- **Breakdown Available:** Tap to see station-by-station analysis
- **Actionable Insight:** Each session ends with "Focus on X to improve your score"

## 3. SwingScan AI 2.0 Integration

### FinRes Technology Capabilities

SwingScan AI 2.0 (powered by FinRes) provides Trackman-level data from a phone camera:

| Data Point | Accuracy | How It's Used |
|------------|----------|---------------|
| **Club Speed** | ±2 mph | Power efficiency calculation |
| **Ball Speed** | ±3 mph | Energy transfer analysis |
| **Estimated Distance** | ±5 yards | Consistency tracking |
| **Tempo Ratio** | Precise | Rhythm alignment to DNA |
| **P-Positions** | 5 stations | Station-by-station scoring |

### Two Modes, One Score

**Mode 1: Analyze My Swing**
- User records swing
- AI analyzes against Swing DNA model
- Returns station-by-station breakdown
- Updates overall Swing DNA Score
- Recommends Accelerator if applicable

**Mode 2: Dial In Distances**
- User hits multiple shots
- AI tracks consistency
- Returns distance calibration
- Updates "consistency" component of score
- Tracks improvement over time

### The Data Flywheel

```
User Scans Swing
      ↓
AI Compares to Swing DNA Model
      ↓
Score Updated (0-100)
      ↓
Weakness Identified
      ↓
Accelerator Recommended
      ↓
User Purchases/Uses Accelerator
      ↓
User Scans Again
      ↓
Score Improves
      ↓
Identity Reinforced ("I'm a 78 Swing DNA golfer")
      ↓
REPEAT
```

## 4. The Acquisition-to-Retention Bridge

### The Dual-Metric Architecture

| Phase | Metric | User Experience | Business Objective |
|-------|--------|-----------------|-------------------|
| **Discovery** | Swing Match % | "Your swing is 73% like Adam Scott" | Viral sharing, curiosity |
| **Onboarding** | Swing DNA Profile | "Let's build YOUR optimal swing model" | Investment, personalization |
| **Ongoing** | Swing DNA Score | "You're at 82—up 4 points this month" | Retention, identity |

### The Psychological Bridge

**Acquisition Hook (Swing Match):**
- *"I match Adam Scott!"* → Share to social
- *"I'm curious who I swing like"* → Download app
- *"My friends want to try"* → Viral loop

**Retention Engine (Swing DNA):**
- *"I'm an 82"* → Identity anchor
- *"I was 78 last month"* → Progress protection
- *"I need to hit 85 before my trip"* → Goal-driven return

**The Transition:**
> "Knowing you swing like Adam Scott is fun. But becoming the best version of YOUR swing? That's the real game. Let's build your Swing DNA."

### Identity Theory Application

From behavioral psychology research:

| Identity Marker              | Swing DNA Equivalent              | Retention Impact                      |
| ---------------------------- | --------------------------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| "I'm a runner" (not "I run") | "I'm an 82 Swing DNA golfer"      | 3.4x retention vs. non-identity users |
| Streak protection (Duolingo) | Swing Score protection            | Users return to prevent decline       |
| Progress visibility (Oura)   | Weekly/monthly swing score trends | Creates narrative of improvement      |
