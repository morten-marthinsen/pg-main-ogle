## WHAT SWING MATCH % IS

A single percentage that shows golfers how close their swing matches the golfer they aspire to be.

- User uploads a swing
- Selects their target identity (favorite PRO swing, Tiger, Rory, Scottie, etc.)
- Receives one clear number: **"Your Swing Match: 58%"**

No angles. No charts. No confusion. Just direction.

*"I'm already partway there."* That's the first emotional hook.

## WHY THIS CHANGES EVERYTHING

### The Problem We Solve

Progress in golf today is:
- **Internal** (felt, not seen)
- **Abstract** (confidence, control, stability)
- **Fragmented** (many metrics, no anchor)
- **Private** (nothing shareable)

When progress is only *felt*, it's easy to dismiss. A single bad session erases weeks of belief. That's why users churn.

### The Breakthrough

**Swing Match % makes progress visible, recognizable, and identity-defining.**

When golfers can *see* themselves changing—when they watch their before/after and see the transformation—belief locks in. Stopping the app feels like giving something up.

*That's retention that doesn't require convincing.*
