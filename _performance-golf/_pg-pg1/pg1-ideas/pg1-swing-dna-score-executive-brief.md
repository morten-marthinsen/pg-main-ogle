## The Solution: Swing DNA Score

### What It Is
One unified score (0-100) that tells you how dialed-in your swing is to *your* personalized optimal model—not Tiger's, not Rory's, but yours.

Your Swing DNA is calculated from your:
- Body type and physical constraints
- Flexibility and mobility profile
- Current skill level and goals
- Swing tendencies and natural patterns

### How It Works

**SwingScan AI 2.0** (powered by FinRes) captures Trackman-level data from your phone:
- Club speed, ball speed, distance
- Tempo and timing ratios
- 5 swing positions: Setup → Backswing → Top → Impact → Finish

Each session updates your Swing DNA Score. Every product becomes an **Accelerator**—a tool designed to improve specific components of your score.

### Why It's Different

This isn't comparing you to pros. That creates anxiety (your concern was valid).

This is comparing you to **the best version of yourself**—the swing your body is built to execute. Your Swing DNA is your personalized blueprint. Your score measures how close you are to it.
