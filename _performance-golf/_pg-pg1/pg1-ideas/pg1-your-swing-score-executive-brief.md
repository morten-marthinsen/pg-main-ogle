This is NOT:

- A generic swing rating (not actionable)

This IS:
- Your personalized perfect swing based on YOUR body
- A measure of alignment to YOUR optimal mechanics
- A tangible number that improves with deliberate practice

### The Personalization Engine

Your Swing DNA profile is built from:

| Input Category | Data Points | Source |
|----------------|-------------|--------|
| **Physical Profile** | Height, weight, arm length, flexibility | Onboarding assessment |
| **Mobility Assessment** | Hip rotation, shoulder turn, spine angle | In-app movement screen |
| **Skill Baseline** | Current handicap, typical misses, goals | Self-reported + SwingScan |
| **Swing Fingerprint** | Natural tempo, plane tendency, power sources | SwingScan AI 2.0 |

**Output:** A personalized optimal swing model—the swing YOUR body is built to execute.

| Station | Weight | What It Measures | Why It Matters |

| **Top** | 20% | Position, arm plane, wrist conditions | Transition readiness |
| **Impact** | 30% | Club delivery, face angle, path | Where the ball is hit |
| **Finish** | 15% | Balance, extension, rotation completion | Indicator of swing health |

**Total:** 100 points possible = 100% Swing DNA alignment
