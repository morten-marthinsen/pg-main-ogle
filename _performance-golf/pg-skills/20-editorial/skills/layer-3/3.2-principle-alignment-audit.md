# Microskill 3.2: Principle Alignment Audit

**Layer:** 3 (5-Tier Evaluation)
**Purpose:** Verify copy execution aligns with core copywriting principles

---

## TIER 2 OF 5: PRINCIPLE ALIGNMENT

This tier evaluates whether the copy correctly applies fundamental copywriting principles, regardless of structure.

---
