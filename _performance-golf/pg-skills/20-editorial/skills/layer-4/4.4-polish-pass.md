# Microskill 4.4: Polish Pass

**Layer:** 4 (Fix Application)
**Purpose:** Final refinement for professional-grade finish

---

## POLISH PASS OBJECTIVES

The polish pass elevates copy from "good" to "elite" through:
1. Rhythm refinement
2. Word-level upgrades
3. Punch enhancement
4. Technical cleanup
5. Consistency verification

---

## POLISH AREA 1: RHYTHM REFINEMENT

### Sentence Rhythm Polishing

```yaml
rhythm_refinement:
  sentence_length_optimization:
    current_distribution:
      short: "[percentage]"  # <10 words
      medium: "[percentage]"  # 10-20 words
      long: "[percentage]"  # >20 words

    target_distribution:
      short: "20-30%"
      medium: "50-60%"
      long: "15-25%"

    adjustments_needed:
      - section: "[where]"
        current: "[pattern]"
        adjustment: "[what to change]"
        revised_text: "[polished version]"

  punch_line_placement:
    short_punchy_sentences_at:
      - after_long_paragraphs: "[check: placed? yes/no]"
      - at_section_ends: "[check: placed? yes/no]"
      - after_major_claims: "[check: placed? yes/no]"

    punches_added:
      - location: "[where]"
        punch: "[short punchy sentence added]"

  paragraph_length_variation:
    current_pattern: "[assessment]"
    adjustments:
      - location: "[where]"
        action: "[split | combine | rebalance]"
```
