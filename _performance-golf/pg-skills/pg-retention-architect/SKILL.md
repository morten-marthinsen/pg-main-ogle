## Core Operating Principles

### The Money Lives in the Backend

**Backend Multiplier Effect:**
- Acquisition costs money (CAC)
- Backend makes money (profit)
- Every dollar of effort on existing customers returns 5-10x more than new customers

**Revenue Formula:**
```
Backend Revenue = Customers × Repeat Rate × AOV × Margin
```

### The Three Retention Levers

1. **Keep them longer** (reduce churn, extend lifetime)
2. **Buy more often** (increase purchase frequency)
3. **Spend more per order** (increase AOV through bundles, upsells, subscriptions)

### Speed Wins

- First 48 hours determine lifetime retention
- First 5 minutes after lead capture = 80% conversion difference
- First 30 days determine second purchase rate (the most critical metric)

### Segmentation Multiplies Everything

Generic campaigns fail. Segment-specific campaigns win 2-3x better.

**RFM is non-negotiable:**
- Recency: Days since last purchase
- Frequency: Purchase count
- Monetary: Total spend

Champions get different treatment than At-Risk customers. Always.

### Proof Beats Promise

Deploy testimonials, case studies, results, and social proof at every stage.

"437 people bought this week" beats "best product ever" every time.

## Decision Trees (When to Apply Which Framework)

### Revenue Problem Diagnostic

```
PROBLEM: Revenue is declining
├── Fewer customers buying? → 5 Horsemen (Retention) [hormozi-playbooks.md]
├── Lower spend per customer? → Crazy Eight (LTV levers) [hormozi-playbooks.md]
├── Leads not converting? → Four Pillars (Lead Nurture) [hormozi-playbooks.md]
└── Need money NOW? → Fast Cash Plays [hormozi-playbooks.md]

PROBLEM: Repeat rate is stuck
├── New customers not coming back? → Horseman 1 (Onboarding) [campaign-architecture.md]
├── Loyal customers slowing down? → Crazy Eight (Frequency + AOV levers)
└── High-value customers leaving? → Can't Lose segment strategy [rfm-segmentation.md]

PROBLEM: LTV isn't growing
├── Purchase frequency flat? → Replenishment flows [backend-monetization.md]
├── AOV flat? → Bundle/upsell optimization [backend-monetization.md]
├── Churn too high? → 5 Horsemen + Win-back flows [campaign-architecture.md]
└── Referral rate low? → VIP referral program [backend-monetization.md]

PROBLEM: Campaigns underperforming
├── Open rates low? → Subject line testing [cro-testing.md]
├── Click rates low? → CTA optimization [cro-testing.md]
├── Conversions low? → Offer/segment mismatch [rfm-segmentation.md]
└── Unsubscribes high? → Frequency/content audit [campaign-architecture.md]
```

### Testing Priority Framework

```
START HERE: Headlines/Subject Lines (20-40% lift potential)
    ↓

    ↓
THEN: CTAs (15-30% click rate lifts)
    ↓
THEN: Body Copy (10-20% engagement)
    ↓
LAST: Design/Layout (5-15% incremental)
```

## Key Metrics to Track

### North Star Metrics
- **Repeat Purchase Rate** (% of customers who buy again)
- **Purchase Frequency** (average orders per customer)
- **Customer Lifetime Value** (total revenue per customer)
- **Retention Curves** (D7, D30, D60, D90, D180)

### Channel Metrics
- **Email**: Click rate, CTOR (click-to-open), RPR (revenue per recipient)
- **SMS**: Click rate, conversion rate, opt-out rate, revenue per message
- **Flows**: Flow revenue, conversion rate, exit rate by step

### Segment Metrics
- **RFM Scores** (555 = Champions, 111 = Hibernating)
- **Segment Revenue %** (Champions should be 60-70% of revenue)
- **Reactivation Rate** (% of At-Risk who return)
- **Churn Risk Score** (predictive model)
