**Claimed Root Cause:** Poor contact from lack of forgiveness in traditional wedges
**Marketing Message:** "Chunk a little less" / "Elite forgiveness"
**Implicit Belief:** The problem is off-center strikes; perimeter weighting helps
**Weakness:** "A little less" admits you'll STILL chunk

## Cleveland Smart Sole
**Claimed Root Cause:** Wrong sole design for greenside shots
**Marketing Message:** "Get out of sand FIRST TIME"
**Implicit Belief:** Wide sole prevents digging
**Weakness:** Unusual appearance, perceived as "extreme game improvement"

## Square Strike
**Claimed Root Cause:** Traditional wedge swing is too complex
**Marketing Message:** "ACS 2 Low Friction Anti-Chunk Slide Ramp Sole with No-Dig Leading Edge"
**Implicit Belief:** Putter-style stroke is easier
**Weakness:** Perceived as gimmick, stigma of "chipper"

## Callaway CB / Mack Daddy CB
**Claimed Root Cause:** Lack of forgiveness in blade wedges
**Marketing Message:** "Incredible short game forgiveness"
**Implicit Belief:** Cavity back = better for amateurs
**Weakness:** "For the everyday golfer" = stigma

## Mizuno ES21
**Claimed Root Cause:** CG location in traditional wedges is sub-optimal
**Marketing Message:** "Enhanced Spin" / "Extreme-CG forged wedge"
**Implicit Belief:** Center CG = better results on mishits
**Weakness:** Technical/engineering focus, not emotional

## PING Glide 4.0
**Claimed Root Cause:** Inconsistent partial shots
**Marketing Message:** "Specially designed grip" / "Low centre of gravity"
**Implicit Belief:** Precision engineering solves problems
**Weakness:** Too technical, expensive

## Tour Edge Hot Launch Vibrcor
**Claimed Root Cause:** Poor turf interaction
**Marketing Message:** "Beveled leading edge prevents digging"
**Implicit Belief:** Sole design creates forgiveness
