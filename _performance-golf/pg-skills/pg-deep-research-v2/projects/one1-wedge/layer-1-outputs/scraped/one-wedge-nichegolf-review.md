# Niche Golf ONE Wedge Review
**Source:** https://www.nichegolf.com/a-truly-amazing-golf-wedge-that-does-it-all/
**Author:** Marc Heyman (11 handicap, 25+ years playing)
**Date:** June 4, 2024
**Scrape Date:** 2026-01-16

## Reviewer Background
- 11 handicap, 25+ years of golf
- Previous wedge: Callaway PM Grind (loved them, hadn't replaced in years)
- Self-described: "my short game is what keeps me in every hole"
- Skeptical stance: "for a wedge to come into my bag it must truly be a phenomenal wedge"

## Initial Impressions
- "Slightly heavier than my current wedge"
- "Sole seems to be a little wider than most others"

## Testing Protocol
1. Chipping area with super thick grass, first cut, and fairway grass
2. Hit ~12 shots from super thick grass
3. Hit 30-40 balls from first cut/fairway (~20 minutes)
4. Real course play for almost a month

## Performance Observations

### From Super Thick Grass:
> "I was amazed at how easy the golf ball popped out. Usually, I have to open the face a bit and swing a little harder, not with The ONE Wedge."

### From First Cut/Fairway:
> "The ONE Wedge was just popping everything up quickly and landing softly, I was really amazed!"

### On-Course Results:
- "First shot perfect to about 10 feet from a medium heavy grass lie"
- "Next shot from an almost buried lie, and the ball again popped right out"
- "Gotten me up and down on numerous occasions"

## Key Outcome
**Replaced his beloved Callaway PM Grind wedge after almost a month of testing**

> "I didn't think I would ever take my Callaway PM Grind wedge out of my bag, but I have."
