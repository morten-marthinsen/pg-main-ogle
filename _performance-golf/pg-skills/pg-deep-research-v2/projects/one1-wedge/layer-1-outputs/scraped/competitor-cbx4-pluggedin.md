**Scrape Date:** 2026-01-16

## Summary (50 Words)
"The Cleveland CBX 4 ZipCore wedge gives golfers help on mishits without taking anything away in spin. Soles designed for each loft. All the face technology of Cleveland's Tour wedges."

## Key Product Features

### Forgiveness Technology
- Cavity back design for "help on mishits"
- "Help higher handicap golfers survive the fat shots and the off-center contacts with less pain"
- Ball speed and carry distance more consistent on mishits

### Spin Technology
- HydraZip for consistent spin in wet conditions
- UtiliZip - deeper, more tightly packed grooves
- "Spin that's at the high end of average across all wedges"
- **"You don't have to give up any spin to get increased forgiveness"**

### Sole Designs (Pre-assigned by loft)
- **V Sole** (44-52°): Square position, relief on leading/trailing edges
- **S Sole** (54-56°): More relief on trailing edge/heel for bunker opening
- **C Sole** (58-60°): Aggressive relief on heel, toe, trailing edge
- **All have 12-14° bounce** - "quite good at resisting digging"

## Performance Claims
- "Mishits are not going to miraculously end up in the cup. But in the area of the game where every inch matters, cavity back wedges can really help."
- "Significantly" helps on mishits vs traditional wedge
- Cleaner look in bag than predecessor

## Voice of Customer (from comments)

### User Kevin (mid-handicapper, Florida):
> "While I had my heart set on a forged set of tour wedges it was undeniable looking at my Foresight launch monitor data and results at my clubs practice area that the CBX4's were the wedges for me. Not sure if the many years with Ping wedges ISI, i3 O-Size and G425 had something to do with this but with the CBX4s I'm longer, more consistent, more accurate and hitting the ball with more spin control resulting in better scoring opportunities."

### User Bill asking about trajectory:
> Concern about launch height from cavity back design

### Matt's response:
> "The CBX wedges will launch a bit higher than the RTX because of the lower CG"

## Competitive Intelligence

### CBX 4 vs Edison 2.0
- Matt Saternus prefers Edison: "The sole is more versatile for me, and I find the higher CG to be better"
- Both similar in forgiveness
- Edison has highest CG of any wedge

### Pricing
- Standard retail pricing for premium wedge category
