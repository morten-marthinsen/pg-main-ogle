**Frame Claim:** Most golfers miss putts not because of bad reads or a bad stroke, but because the putter’s face naturally drifts open the moment they start moving it. This is due to a common flaw in traditional putter design called face drift. And face drift can pull the ball off-line even when everything else is right. Unfortunately, nearly all putters have this face drift flaw built in because traditional putter design has the shaft and most of the head’s weight sitting behind the face. This common design flaw is what makes the putter face want to open as soon as it’s moved. Which leaves the golfer to have to do the work to keep the face square instead of the putter doing the work of starting square for the golfer. The RS1 with Roll Straight Technology™ fixes this by eliminating face drift instead of causing it. Its forward-weighted design, forward-rotating face orientation, and more upright stroke angle help the face stay square longer, so golfers can finally start the ball on their intended line with the stroke they already have. No more fighting with the putter to keep the club face square; RS1 does the work for you and gives the golfer better putting outcomes without requiring them to change their stroke or work harder.

**Unique Mechanism:** Roll Straight Technology™

Roll Straight Technology™ is a forward-balanced design that eliminates face drift and keeps the putter face square through the stroke, so golfers can start more putts on line and roll the ball straighter with the stroke they already have.

## **Hero Features (Simplified):**

* **Forward Axis Balance™**  
  The putter naturally sits with the face tilted forward instead of opening up. This forward tilt helps the face resist drifting open during the stroke, so the putter works to stay square without the golfer having to fix it.  
* **High Forward MOI Front**  
  Most of the putter’s weight is packed into the very front of the head. With more weight in front, the face becomes harder to twist, which helps the putter hold a square position as it moves.  
* **Forward Center of Gravity**  
  The heaviest point of the putter sits in front of the shaft instead of behind it. When the CG is forward, the putter naturally lines up with its path and keeps the face from turning open while the golfer swings it.  
* **Square-to-Square Lie Angle (74°)**  
  A more upright lie angle creates a stroke that moves straighter back and through with less face rotation. This helps the putter keep the face square longer, instead of falling open during a flatter arc.  
* **Face-Square Focus Alignment System**  
  Three long lines and one short front line guide the eyes to square the face at setup. When the face starts square, the putter’s forward-balanced design can hold that square position more easily through the stroke.  
* **Face-Forward Shaft Position**  
  The shaft enters the head from behind the face, giving a clear view of the full hitting surface. This setup creates a true pendulum-style swing that reduces twisting and helps the face stay pointed where the golfer aims.  
* **Multi-Material Construction**  
  A heavy steel front, light carbon top, and light aluminum back push more mass toward the front of the head. This front-biased build helps stabilize the face and supports the other features that fight face drift.
