# Micro-Skill 2.2-E: WEB Analysis Deep

**Layer:** 2 - Intelligence
**Purpose:** Systematically excavate Wants, Emotions, and Beliefs from extracted quotes to create actionable messaging intelligence
**Dependency:** Layer 1 extraction complete (tagged_quotes.json, quantified_buckets.json)
**Parallel:** Can run simultaneously with 2.2-A through 2.2-D
**Version:** 1.0
**PRD Reference:** [[RESEARCH-PRD#Section 4.2]]
**E5 Reference:** Todd Brown's Prospect Examination - Tool 2: WEB Analysis (Most Important Tool)

---

## CRITICAL: WEB Analysis Is The Foundation of All Messaging

Todd Brown states: "The WEB Analysis is the most valuable and frequently used tool for understanding prospects."

This skill transforms raw quote data into structured intelligence about:
- **W**ants and Desires (what they truly want, not just what they say)
- **E**motions and Feelings (current state AND desired state)
- **B**eliefs and Identifications (pre-existing beliefs that filter your message)

**NEVER** synthesize messaging framework without complete WEB Analysis.

---

```yaml
tagged_quotes: [output from 1.5-B]
```

## WEB Analysis Process

### WANTS ANALYSIS

#### Step 1: **Excavate** Stated Wants from Quotes

From hope_quotes and pain_quotes, **scrutinize** what prospects explicitly say they want.

```yaml
extraction_patterns:
  - "I want to..."
  - "I wish I could..."
  - "I'd love to..."
  - "If only I could..."
  - "My goal is to..."
  - "I just need to..."
  - Inverse of pain statements (pain = chunking → want = not chunking)
```

#### Step 2: **Excavate** Secret Desires Behind Stated Wants

For each stated want, **ruminate** on: "What FEELING do they really want?"

```yaml
vic_schwab_five_categories:
  GAIN:
    - Health, time, money, popularity
    - Improved appearance, comfort, leisure
    - Pride of accomplishment, advancement
    - Increased enjoyment, self-confidence, personal prestige

  BE:
    - Good parents, social, hospitable, up to date, creative
    - Proud of possessions
    - Influential over others
    - Efficient, first in things
    - Recognized as authorities

  DO:
    - Express their personality
    - Not be dominated by others
    - Satisfy curiosity
    - Emulate admirable people
    - Appreciate beauty
    - Win others' affections
    - Improve themselves

  SAVE:
    - Time, money, work
    - Discomfort, worry, doubts
    - Risk, personal embarrassment

  AVOID:
    - Trouble, criticism, effort
    - Misfortune, worry, doubt
    - Risk, embarrassment
```

#### Step 3: Create Wants Hierarchy

Rank wants by frequency and intensity in quotes.

```yaml
hierarchy_criteria:
  - Frequency of mention
  - Emotional intensity in language
  - Connection to deeper desires
  - Urgency indicators
```

---

### EMOTIONS ANALYSIS

#### Step 4: Map Current Emotional State

From pain_quotes, identify emotions they're experiencing NOW.

```yaml
core_emotions_to_identify:
  - Fear (disgusted, disappointed, nervous, anxious)
  - Anger (frustrated, upset, irritated)
  - Greed (wanting more, entitled)
  - Envy (jealous of others' abilities)
  - Embarrassment (humiliated, ashamed)
  - Inadequacy (incompetent, helpless)
  - Guilt (should be better)
  - Escape (wanting out of current state)
```

#### Step 5: Map Desired Emotional State

From hope_quotes, identify how they want to FEEL.

```yaml
target_emotions_to_identify:
  - Confidence
  - Control
  - Pride
  - Relief
  - Joy
  - Respect
  - Competence
```

#### Step 6: Define Emotional Transformation Arc

```yaml
arc_template:
  FROM: [current dominant emotions]
  TO: [desired emotional state]
  messaging_implication: "Copy must acknowledge FROM state AND paint picture of TO state"
```

---

### BELIEFS ANALYSIS

#### Step 7: Extract Beliefs About the Problem

What do they believe CAUSES their problem?

```yaml
belief_extraction_patterns:
  - "I think the problem is..."
  - "It's because I..."
  - "The reason is..."
  - "It must be..."
  - Implicit beliefs in their explanations
```

#### Step 8: Extract Beliefs About the Marketplace

What do they believe about golf equipment marketing, brands, solutions?

```yaml
marketplace_belief_areas:
  - Equipment marketing in general
  - Big brands vs small brands
  - Price vs quality
  - Marketing claims vs reality
```

#### Step 9: Extract Beliefs About Solution Types

What do they believe about different solution categories?

```yaml
solution_belief_areas:
  - Game improvement equipment (stigma?)
  - Lessons and instruction
  - Practice aids
  - Specific product categories
```

#### Step 10: **Calibrate** Beliefs to Disrupt vs Align

**Scrutinize** each belief to determine strategic approach:

```yaml
disruption_criteria:
  - Belief prevents them from seeing your solution
  - Belief is inaccurate or incomplete
  - Disrupting creates opening for your mechanism

alignment_criteria:
  - Belief validates their identity
  - Belief shows you understand them
  - Alignment builds trust before disruption
```

## Quality Criteria

- [ ] Stated wants extracted with frequencies
- [ ] Secret desires mapped using Vic Schwab categories
- [ ] Wants hierarchy established
- [ ] Current emotional state mapped with sub-emotions
- [ ] Desired emotional state mapped
- [ ] Emotional transformation arc defined
- [ ] Beliefs categorized (problem, marketplace, solutions, vendors)
- [ ] Beliefs to disrupt identified with approach
- [ ] Beliefs to align with identified with language
- [ ] Synthesis complete with copy implications
- [ ] Verbatim evidence provided for all claims

## Failure Protocol

```
IF wants unclear:
  - Return to hope_quotes for deeper extraction
  - Look for inverse of pain statements
  - Search for aspiration language

IF emotions shallow:
  - Analyze language intensity and word choice
  - Look for emotional spillover in problem descriptions
  - Consider implicit emotions behind stated frustrations

IF beliefs insufficient:
  - Search for "because" and "reason" language
  - Look at solutions_tried quotes for implicit beliefs
  - Analyze root_cause quotes for attribution patterns

IF synthesis doesn't gel:
  - Re-examine highest frequency wants/emotions/beliefs
  - Look for connecting threads
  - Flag for human review if contradictions persist
```

- Return to this analysis frequently throughout research
