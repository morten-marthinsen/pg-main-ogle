## CRITICAL: Awareness Level Determines Messaging Strategy

Before scraping begins, you MUST diagnose where the MAJORITY of your target market sits on the awareness pyramid. This determines:
- How to lead your marketing (promise vs mechanism vs experience)
- What copy angle will resonate
- How long/complex your marketing needs to be
- Where differentiation matters most

**NEVER** proceed to Layer 2 intelligence without awareness mapping complete.

---

## The Five Levels of Awareness (Eugene Schwartz)

```
                    ┌─────────────────┐
                    │   MOST AWARE    │ ← Smallest segment, easiest to convert
                    │   (Level 5)     │   They know YOU and your product
                    ├─────────────────┤
                    │  PRODUCT AWARE  │ ← They know solutions exist in your category
                    │   (Level 4)     │   Comparing options
                    ├─────────────────┤
                    │ SOLUTION AWARE  │ ← They know solution TYPES exist
                    │   (Level 3)     │   Evaluating categories
                    ├─────────────────┤
                    │  PROBLEM AWARE  │ ← They know they have a problem
                    │   (Level 2)     │   Not actively seeking solutions
                    ├─────────────────┤
                    │   COMPLETELY    │ ← Largest segment, hardest to convert
                    │    UNAWARE      │   Don't know they have a problem
                    │   (Level 1)     │
                    └─────────────────┘
```

## Diagnostic Process

### Step 1: Scrutinize Search Behavior Signals

Examine how prospects search for information related to the problem/solution.

```yaml
search_behavior_signals:
  completely_unaware:
    - No searches related to the problem
    - May search general category ("golf tips", "lower scores")

  problem_aware:
    - "Why do I keep [problem]?"
    - "How to stop [problem]"
    - "[Problem] causes"
    - "[Problem] tips"
    - Technique/instruction searches

  solution_aware:
    - "[Solution type] vs [solution type]"
    - "Best way to fix [problem]"
    - "[Solution category] reviews"
    - Comparing approaches

  product_aware:
    - "[Brand] vs [Brand]"
    - "[Product name] review"
    - "Best [product category]"
    - Specific product comparisons

  most_aware:
    - [Your brand name] searches
    - "[Your product] discount/sale"
    - Direct navigation to your site
```

### Step 2: Excavate Forum/Community Language Patterns

Analyze how prospects discuss the problem in forums.

```yaml
language_pattern_signals:
  problem_aware_language:
    - "I've been struggling with..."
    - "I can't seem to fix..."
    - "Anyone else have this problem?"
    - "What am I doing wrong?"
    - Focus on THEIR actions, not solutions

  solution_aware_language:
    - "Has anyone tried [approach]?"
    - "I'm thinking about getting [solution type]"
    - "What's better - [option A] or [option B]?"
    - Focus on SOLUTIONS to evaluate

  product_aware_language:
    - "[Product] vs [Product]"
    - "Anyone gaming the [specific product]?"
    - "Thinking about switching to [brand]"
    - Focus on SPECIFIC PRODUCTS
```

### Step 3: Assess Root Cause Belief Patterns

Where they attribute the problem reveals awareness level.

```yaml
root_cause_attribution:
  problem_aware_attribution:
    - "I'm doing something wrong" (technique)
    - "I just need more practice"
    - "Maybe I need lessons"
    - Attribution to SELF, not external solutions

  solution_aware_attribution:
    - "Maybe equipment could help"
    - "I've heard [solution type] works"
    - "Thinking about trying [approach]"
    - Attribution to SOLUTION CATEGORIES

  product_aware_attribution:
    - "[Brand] might be better for me"
    - "I've heard [product] is forgiving"
    - "Wondering if [specific product] is the answer"
    - Attribution to SPECIFIC PRODUCTS
```

### Step 4: Calculate Distribution Estimate

Based on evidence, estimate percentage at each level.

```yaml
distribution_calculation:
  methodology:
    1. Count forum threads by language pattern type
    2. Analyze search volume patterns (instructional vs product)
    3. Review quote bucket distribution (technique beliefs vs equipment beliefs)
    4. Weight by engagement (more engagement = larger segment)

  typical_distributions:
    mature_market_existing_products:
      completely_unaware: 5-10%
      problem_aware: 30-40%
      solution_aware: 25-35%
      product_aware: 15-25%
      most_aware: 5-10%

    emerging_category_new_product:
      completely_unaware: 20-30%
      problem_aware: 40-50%
      solution_aware: 15-25%
      product_aware: 5-10%
      most_aware: 0-5%
```

### Step 5: Determine Primary Target Level

Select the level to optimize messaging for.

```yaml
target_selection_rules:
  default_recommendation: "PROBLEM_AWARE"
  rationale: "Low on pyramid (good scale) but significantly easier to convert than Completely Unaware"

  exceptions:
    target_solution_aware_when:
      - Problem is universally recognized
      - Solution categories are well-known
      - Market is actively comparing approaches

    target_product_aware_when:
      - Category is mature
      - Your brand has strong awareness
      - Competition is primary focus

  never_target:
    - completely_unaware: "ROI too low - requires too much education"

  cascade_principle:
    description: "Copy created for lower levels works for ALL levels above"
    implication: "Target Problem Aware → copy works for Solution, Product, and Most Aware too"
```

### Step 6: Define Strategic Implications

Map awareness level to messaging strategy.

```yaml
awareness_to_strategy_map:
  completely_unaware:
    lead_with: "Curiosity/story that surfaces the problem"
    copy_length: "VERY LONG - must educate on problem existence"
    mechanism_importance: "LOW - they don't care how it works yet"

  problem_aware:
    lead_with: "Problem agitation + belief disruption"
    copy_length: "LONG - must move them through awareness levels"
    mechanism_importance: "MEDIUM - helps establish credibility"
    must_establish:
      - "The problem is NOT what you think"
      - "Solutions you've tried fail for THIS reason"
      - "Equipment CAN solve this (if correct type)"

  solution_aware:
    lead_with: "Why THIS solution category is superior"
    copy_length: "MEDIUM - comparing approaches"
    mechanism_importance: "HIGH - differentiates from other solution types"

  product_aware:
    lead_with: "Why THIS product is superior in category"
    copy_length: "MEDIUM - comparing specific products"
    mechanism_importance: "VERY HIGH - primary differentiator"

  most_aware:
    lead_with: "Direct offer, reminder, urgency"
    copy_length: "SHORT - they already know and trust"
    mechanism_importance: "LOW - already sold on it"
```

## Failure Protocol

```
IF awareness level unclear:
  - Default to PROBLEM_AWARE (safest assumption)
  - Flag for validation with additional research
  - Document uncertainty in output

IF evidence contradictory:
  - Note the contradiction
  - Estimate distribution that accounts for both signals
  - May indicate bimodal market (two primary awareness levels)

IF market is emerging/new:
  - Higher percentage at Completely Unaware and Problem Aware
  - Longer copy will be required
  - Education-first approach mandatory
```
