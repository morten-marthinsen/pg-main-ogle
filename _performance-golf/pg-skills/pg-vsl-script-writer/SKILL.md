# VSL Script Creator - RMBC Framework

Create high-converting Video Sales Letter (VSL) scripts using the proven RMBC framework. This system guides you through creating compelling long-form sales copy that converts viewers into buyers.

## Very Simple Sales Letter Outline

1. Hook/Microlead
2. Lead
3. Background (Credibility and Origin Story)
4. Discovery
5. UMP/UMS (Unique Mechanism of Problem/Solution)
6. Product Build-Up and Reveal
7. Close
8. FAQ

## Detailed Sales Letter Framework

**Important Notes:**
- Support points don't all need to be used
- Order within sections can mix and vary
- Open loops with fascinations ("I'll get to that in just a moment...")

---

### 1) Pre-Lead/MicroLead

A. Hook heavy or attention-grabbing opening line
B. Quickly related to the pain-point prospect faces
C. Quick related to the promise of a solution

---

### 2) Lead (Elements can mix - not strictly ordered)

a) **Call Out Problem** - Name the pain directly
b) **Promise Solution** - To pain point + promise to save time/money
c) **Tease Emotional Discovery Story** - Hook them with narrative
d) **Tease Unique Mechanism** - Behind the solution
e) **Tease Contrarian Nature** - "This flies in the face of what you've been told"
f) **Work in Fascinations** - As incentive for continuing ("I'll answer this riddle for you")
g) **Briefly Mention Credibility** - Builders and authority
h) **Qualifiers** - Who this works for. Speak to your market.
i) **Briefly Address Skepticism** - Acknowledge doubts
j) **Include Broad Testimonials** - About benefits (don't give away product)

---

### 3) Background Story

a) **Who I Am** + Credibility builders when appropriate (doctor, expert)
b) **"Me or Someone Close Was Just Like You"** - Emotional story about pain
c) **Traditional Solutions Not Working** - What they've tried that failed
d) **Trigger Event** - Problem escalated to where avatar realizes something MUST change
e) **Search for Truth/Answers** - The quest begins
f) **Optional: Meets Wise "Sensei"** - Who promises to reveal hidden truth

---

### 4) Unique Mechanism of the Problem (UMP)

a) **Explaining the "Real Cause"** - You've had 99% of the answer but this is the missing 1%
b) **Should Be Surprising** - Possibly even counterintuitive
c) **Backed by Credibility and Proof** - Elements that validate the claim

---

### 5) Unique Mechanism of The Solution (UMS)

a) **Now That You Know the Real Cause** - This is the "Real Solution" on a MACRO level
b) **Logically Connected to the Problem** - Clear cause-effect relationship
c) **Dig Deep to Prove The Theory** - Get into the MICRO details
d) **Backed By Credibility and Proof** - Including citations and scientific sources

---

### 6) Product Build-Up + Reveal

a) **Avatar Looks For "Out-of-the-Box" Version** - Something to just solve it
b) **Discovers Out-of-the-Box Is Flawed** - Existing solutions don't work
c) **Has to Do It Himself** - Create novel new solution
d) **Starts Testing It** - Problems and setbacks along the way
e) **Eventual Success/Breakthrough** - The moment it all clicked
f) **Proof Product Works** - Evidence and results
g) **Others Asking For It** - Demand from others
h) **PRODUCT Is Born!** - The reveal

---

### 7) Close

a) **Product Details** - What's included, what makes it special, USPs
b) **Discuss and Dismiss Alternatives** - Expensive, ineffective, side-effects, poor quality, unproven
c) **Keep Building Up Value** - Stack the value
d) **Additional Testimonials** - More social proof
e) **How to Use The Product** - Simple explanation
f) **Scarcity** - Demand is high, supplies limited, out-of-stocks common
g) **Personal Mission** - Avatar's mission tied to emotion
h) **Price Justification & Reveal** - Other solutions more expensive, consequences of inaction costly
i) **This Solution is Better** - "Normally $X which is a bargain, but right now just $Y!"
j) **Smart People Buy More** - Upsell positioning

---

### 8) First Call to Action

a) **What Happens After You Click** - Set expectations
b) **Optional: Valuable Free Bonuses** - Revealed to add value
c) **Guarantee** - "Great experience, but if not, no risk and no hassle"

---

### 9) Second Call to Action

a) **Two Choices** - "Go on suffering, or take action today and change your life forever"
b) **Urgency** - "You must hurry, no guarantee how long offer will be available"

---

### 10) Third Call to Action

a) **Thank You For Watching** - Gratitude and final push

---

### 11) FAQs

a) **Back to Being Their Friend** - Help clear up any confusion
b) **Remind Them** - What product is, what it's used for, importance of action, guarantee terms, how to buy

## Process

### Step 1: Gather Product Information
Request details about:
- Product name and type (physical/digital)
- Target audience and their pain points
- Key benefits and features
- Unique mechanism/technology
- Pricing and offer structure
- Available testimonials
- Credibility/authority elements

### Step 2: Analyze Examples
Review relevant examples from the `examples/` folder that match the product type.

### Step 3: Draft VSL Script
Follow the framework section by section, creating compelling copy for each element.

### Step 4: Review and Refine
Ensure all elements flow naturally and build toward the close.

## Quality Standards

- Every section should connect logically to the next
- Fascinations and open loops keep viewers engaged
- Proof elements back up every major claim
- Emotional resonance throughout
- Clear, compelling calls to action
- CEO-level quality output
