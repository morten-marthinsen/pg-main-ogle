# Elite Supplement Sales Page Analysis System

Systematically research and analyze the most effective e-commerce sales pages from top-performing supplement businesses to extract replicable conversion mechanisms, copywriting patterns, and design strategies.

## Overview

Analyze sales pages from leading supplement companies (Ryze, Goli, Armra, Ancestral Supplements, etc.) to identify high-performing conversion elements, copywriting patterns, psychological triggers, and design strategies that drive supplement sales in the direct-response marketing space.

## Trigger

Upon receiving request to analyze supplement sales pages or research ecommerce conversion strategies, immediately begin the systematic research and analysis process outlined below.

**User can provide:**

1. **Specific company list** - Focus on particular brands
2. **Product category** - Narrow to specific supplement types
3. **General research request** - Analyze top performers across supplement space
4. **Competitive analysis** - Compare specific competitors

## Process

### Step 1: Company Research & Page Discovery

Identify the actual sales pages (not homepages) for target companies:

**Priority Research Companies:**
- Ryze (mushroom coffee)
- Mars Men (men's supplements)
- Rosabella Moringa (superfood supplements)
- Goli (apple cider vinegar gummies)
- Dose (daily wellness)
- Norse Organics / Froya Organics (Nordic supplements)
- Arctic Goddess (women's wellness)
- Spartan Hair (hair growth supplements)
- Opal Whipped Tallow (skincare supplements)
- Gruns (green supplements)
- Armra (colostrum supplements)
- Petlab Co (pet supplements)
- Primal Queen (women's primal wellness)
- Ancestral Supplements (organ meat supplements)
- Carpe (antiperspirant/wellness)
- Everyday Dose (daily wellness blends)

**Complete company details with verified URLs:** See [references/companies.md](_Performance%20Golf/pg-skills/supplement-sales-page-analyzer/references/companies.md) for:

- Official website links
- Example sales page URLs
- Company backgrounds and target audiences
- Notable conversion elements
- Product details and key features

**Page Discovery Strategy:**
- Locate primary product sales pages (not just homepage)
- Find highest-traffic product pages
- Identify recent campaign landing pages
- Document specific URLs for verification
- Prioritize pages with clear conversion optimization

### Step 2: Sales Page Analysis Framework

Analyze each page systematically using the comprehensive framework:

**Conversion Elements Analysis:**
- Headline structure and emotional hooks
- Problem/solution positioning framework
- Social proof strategy and implementation
- Scarcity/urgency tactics employed
- Scientific backing and credibility signals
- Pricing psychology and anchor strategies
- Call-to-action optimization (placement, language, frequency)

**Visual & UX Design:**
- Page flow and information hierarchy
- Visual proof elements (before/after, demos, charts)
- Mobile optimization approach
- Trust signals (badges, guarantees, certifications)
- Media integration (VSL, product demos, customer stories)

**Copywriting & Messaging:**
- Unique Selling Proposition articulation
- Core mechanism/story narrative
- Objection handling techniques
- Emotional triggers targeted
- Target audience demographics/psychographics
- Language patterns and tone

### Step 3: Pattern Identification

Extract patterns across high-performing pages:

**Winning Formula Identification:**
- Headline formulas that convert
- Social proof types most effective
- Common scarcity/urgency mechanisms
- Scientific positioning strategies
- Pricing psychology patterns
- CTA language and placement patterns

**Cross-Company Analysis:**
- Elements appearing across multiple top performers
- Industry-standard conversion tactics
- Unique differentiators by brand
- Competitive positioning strategies

### Step 4: Scoring & Ranking

Rate each page comprehensively:

**Scoring Criteria (1-10 Scale):**
- Overall conversion optimization
- Copywriting effectiveness
- Visual design and UX
- Social proof strength
- Scientific credibility
- Mobile experience
- Trust signal implementation

**Ranking Methodology:**
- Identify top 5-10 highest-performing pages
- Document specific standout elements
- Explain why each page scores highly
- Note unique strategies worth replicating

### Step 5: Actionable Intelligence Extraction

Transform analysis into implementation guidance:

**For Each Top Page:**
- Standout elements (3-5 specific items)
- Primary conversion strategy
- Key psychological triggers
- Copywriting insights
- Unique differentiators
- Replicable mechanisms

**Across All Pages:**
- Top 3 conversion elements to implement
- Top 3 copywriting patterns to adapt
- Top 3 design/UX elements to test
- Red flags to avoid

### Step 6: Output Generation

Create comprehensive analysis report using the structured format:

**Required Components:**
1. Executive Summary (2-3 sentences)
2. Top 5-10 Highest-Performing Sales Pages
3. Pattern Analysis Across Top Pages
4. Actionable Recommendations
5. Competitive Intelligence Insights

**Reference Template:**
See `examples/output-template.md` for complete output structure.

## Quality Control

**Quality Standard Context:** Analysis must be immediately actionable for creating high-converting supplement sales pages. CEO-level quality expected.

Verify before delivering:

- [ ] All URLs are active and accessible
- [ ] Minimum 10 active, high-quality sales page URLs analyzed
- [ ] Exact copy/paste of headlines, CTAs, and key phrases (not paraphrased)
- [ ] At least 5 distinct conversion patterns identified
- [ ] Specific, actionable copywriting examples included
- [ ] Cross-references between similar strategies across companies
- [ ] Quantifiable observations where possible
- [ ] Competitive intelligence that wasn't obvious before research
- [ ] No generic advice without specific examples
- [ ] No homepage analysis (sales pages only)
- [ ] No outdated or inactive pages
- [ ] Flagged any currently inaccessible pages

## Validation Checkpoints

Stop and verify after:

1. ✓ After company research and URL discovery
2. ✓ After analyzing first 5 pages
3. ✓ After pattern identification across all pages
4. ✓ Before generating final output
