## Research Usage Notes

**For Sales Page Analysis:**

1. **Always verify current URLs** - Some companies may have multiple domains or updated sites
2. **Look for product-specific landing pages** vs homepage - Often best conversion elements are on dedicated product pages
3. **Check for special offer pages** (BOGO, bundle deals) - These often have most aggressive conversion tactics
4. **Review About/Story pages** - Origin stories are often key differentiators
5. **Note guarantee pages** - Trust-building mechanics vary significantly

**Priority Analysis Targets:**

**Tier 1 (Best Direct Response Examples):**
- Goli (pioneered category)
- Ancestral Supplements (established market leader)
- Armra (premium positioning)
