## CAPTION

The range lies to you. Every. Single. Day.

.
.
.

I used to hit wedge shots at my buddy's feet. On purpose.

Not anger—training. If I could commit fully with someone standing there, tournament pressure would feel like relief.

That's the secret nobody talks about: golfers who perform under pressure aren't mentally tougher than you. They've just trained under harder conditions.

Make practice harder than the real thing—and competition becomes the easy part.
