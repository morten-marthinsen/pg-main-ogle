A billionaire shared four sentences that have been worth $10MM+ to my future.

I've carried them with me ever since.

In a world full of distractions pulling you in every direction, I've found clarity in these sentences.

A simple north star and a few daily reminders can keep you centered and progressing toward your potential.

Here they are:

1️⃣ Whatever you can't overcome, your kids probably won't either.

The dragons you don't slay will get passed down.
↳ Fears you don't confront. 
↳ Habits you don't break. 
↳ Potential you leave untouched.

Reframe: Fight for something bigger than yourself.

2️⃣ Mastering your emotions might be the greatest aim in life.

Not ignoring them, but mastering them. Knowing:
↳ When to push. 
↳ When to stay calm. 
↳ When to respond intentionally instead of react emotionally.

Reframe: See this as a challenge to be elite, not a difficult obligation.

3️⃣ Some people say "roger that." Others say "roger that, expect results."

There's a difference between acknowledging something and committing to it.
↳ The first one is loose. You might do it.
↳ The second one is a commitment.

Reframe: Speak it into existence. Then execute. 

4️⃣ Lots of great starters. Few great finishers.

Everyone has ideas. Everyone gets excited at the beginning. 
But most people quit when the excitement wears off and the work remains.

Reframe: If you get the ball rolling, see it through to the end. Do this alone and seperate yourself from 99% of the world.

I have these on my computer screen to make sure I imprint these into my DNA daily.

Not because I’ve mastered them, but to set the intention daily.
