How far can someone go if they stop taking the safe/traditional path?

I've spent the last 33 years trying to find out.

Earlier this year, my team put together a docuseries on the real story behind Performance Golf.

But Episode 1 wasn't really about golf. 
It was about time.

About realizing at four years old that life moves fast. 

What it was like growing up with that awareness and asking the question: 
How far can someone actually go if they stop taking the safe path?

It was about trying to reach my potential and learning what it really takes to earn belief when you're starting from behind.

Episode 2 pulls the camera back.

This one shows the days behind the obsession:

- The 100-hour weeks. 
- The physical and mental pressure. 
- What it actually looks like to build a company while pushing personal limits at the same time.

And how that intensity eventually turned into a mission: 

To build the world's smartest way to help golfers improve and love this game again.

If you've ever felt like you're in the middle of something hard and wondering if it's worth it, this might resonate.
