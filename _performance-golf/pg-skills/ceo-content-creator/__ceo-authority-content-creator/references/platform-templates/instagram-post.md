# Instagram Post Template

**Formats:** Carousel, Single Image, Reel
**Voice Calibration:** 70% Casual, 30% Professional (warmer than LinkedIn)

---

## Format 1: Carousel (8-10 Slides)

### Structure

| Slide | Purpose | Content |
|-------|---------|---------|
| 1 | Hook | Bold statement or question that stops scroll |
| 2 | Setup | Why this matters / the problem |
| 3-7 | Content | One point per slide, large text |
| 8 | Summary | Quick recap (optional) |
| 9 | CTA | Save, share, follow |

### Slide-by-Slide Template

**SLIDE 1 (Hook):**
```
[BOLD CONTRARIAN STATEMENT]

or

[QUESTION THAT STOPS SCROLL]

---
Swipe to learn [promise] →
```

**Design:** Large text, minimal words (10-15 max), high contrast

**Examples:**
- "STOP PRACTICING MORE"
- "Why do you play worse on the course?"
- "The lie you've been told about improvement"

---

**SLIDE 2 (Setup):**
```
Here's the thing...

[Why this matters in 2-3 lines]

[The problem no one talks about]
```

**Design:** Clean, readable, builds curiosity

---

**SLIDES 3-7 (Content):**
```
[NUMBER]. [POINT IN 3-5 WORDS]

[1-2 sentences explaining]

[Why this matters]
```

**Design Rules:**
- ONE point per slide
- Large text (readable on mobile)
- Max 30 words per slide
- Consistent visual style across all content slides

---

**SLIDE 8 (Summary - Optional):**
```
Quick recap:

✓ [Point 1 in 5 words]
✓ [Point 2 in 5 words]
✓ [Point 3 in 5 words]
✓ [Point 4 in 5 words]
✓ [Point 5 in 5 words]
```

---

**SLIDE 9 (CTA):**
```
Save this for later 💾

Share with someone who needs it 📤

Follow @brixtonalbert for more
```

## Format 3: Reel Script (15-30 seconds)

### Structure

| Timing | Section | Content |
|--------|---------|---------|
| 0-3 sec | Hook | Pattern interrupt, stop scroll |
| 3-25 sec | Body | 3 rapid points, keep momentum |
| 25-30 sec | CTA | Follow, save, Part 2? |

### Script Template

**HOOK (0-3 seconds):**
```
[Pattern interrupt - say something unexpected]

Options:
- "Stop [thing everyone does]."
- "This is for [specific group]..."
- "Nobody talks about [thing]..."
- "The reason you [problem]..."
```

**BODY (3-25 seconds):**
```
[Point 1 - delivered fast, 3-5 seconds]

[Point 2 - keep momentum, 3-5 seconds]

[Point 3 - the payoff, 5-7 seconds]
```

**CTA (25-30 seconds):**
```
Options:
- "Follow for more."
- "Part 2?"
- "Save this."
- "Link in bio."
```

### Example Reel Script (The Experimenter)

```
HOOK (0-3 sec):
"When I was 6, a speech therapist told me I'd need a year of therapy."

BODY (3-25 sec):
"I told her she'd see me for a week.

I went home, figured out which sounds gave me trouble, and drilled them until they were automatic.

A week later? Done.

Here's what I learned: if everyone says something takes a year, ask how to do it in a week."

CTA (25-30 sec):
"That's systems over effort. Follow for more."
```

## Instagram Voice Adjustments

### Compared to LinkedIn:
- Warmer, more personal
- More visual language ("picture this...")
- Shorter sentences
- More story, less framework
- More emotion, less data

### Signature Phrases (Adapted for IG):
- "Love your game" → Works as-is
- "Systems over effort" → Works as-is
- "Right things, right order" → Works as-is
- "The Scattered Playbook" → May need brief explanation

### What to AVOID:
- Corporate language
- Long explanations
- Dense text
- External links in captions (use "link in bio")

---

## Instagram Algorithm Signals (2025)

**Optimize for:**
- DM shares (strongest discovery signal)
- Saves (high-value indicator)
- Watch time / completion rate (Reels)
- Early velocity (first 30-90 minutes)

**Best Practices:**
- Carousel: 6-10 slides optimal
- Reels: 7-15 sec for viral, 30-45 sec for tutorials
- Post when audience is active (test and learn)
- Hashtags: 5-10 relevant, mix broad and niche
