# YouTube Script Template

**Formats:** Shorts (10-60 sec), Long-form (8-12 min)
**Voice Calibration:** Conversational, direct to camera, energetic but grounded

---

## Format 1: YouTube Shorts (10-60 seconds)

### Structure

| Timing | Section | Purpose |
|--------|---------|---------|
| 0-2 sec | Hook | Immediate value promise or pattern interrupt |
| 2-50 sec | Value | Deliver content fast, each point 5-10 sec |
| 50-60 sec | CTA | Subscribe, full video, end abruptly |

### Script Template

**HOOK (0-2 seconds):**
```
[Pattern interrupt OR value promise]

Options:
- "Here's why your [X] isn't working..."
- "Stop doing [thing]. Do this instead."
- "[Bold claim in 5 words]"
- "I learned this the hard way..."
```

**VALUE (2-50 seconds):**
```
[Point 1 - delivered with energy, 10-15 seconds]

[Point 2 - keep momentum, 10-15 seconds]

[Point 3 - the payoff/insight, 15-20 seconds]
```

**CTA (50-60 seconds):**
```
Options:
- "Subscribe for more."
- "Full breakdown on my channel."
- "Comment if you want Part 2."
- [End abruptly for loop]
```

### Example Shorts Script (The Experimenter)

```
HOOK (0-2 sec):
"At 6 years old, I was told I'd need a year of speech therapy."

VALUE (2-50 sec):
"I told her I'd be done in a week.

I went home, figured out exactly which sounds gave me trouble, isolated them, and drilled them until they were automatic.

A week later? Fixed.

Here's what that taught me: If everyone says something takes a year, ask how to do it in a week.

Not by working harder. By finding the right things and doing them in the right order."

CTA (50-60 sec):
"That's systems over effort. Subscribe for more."
```

---

### Shorts Hook Patterns

| Pattern | Formula | Example |
|---------|---------|---------|
| Pattern Interrupt | [Unexpected visual or statement] | "I've been lying to you." |
| Identity Callout | "This is for [specific group]" | "This is for golfers stuck at 15 handicap." |
| Proof First | [Show result, then explain] | "I went from [X] to [Y]. Here's how." |
| Controversy | "I'll get hate for this but..." | "I'll get hate for this, but practice less." |
| Curiosity Gap | "Nobody talks about [X]" | "Nobody talks about why your swing works on the range but not the course." |
| Tutorial | "In 30 seconds I'll show you..." | "In 30 seconds, I'll show you the one thing killing your consistency." |

## Format 2: Long-Form (8-12 minutes)

### The HIVES Framework

| Section | Timing | Purpose |
|---------|--------|---------|
| **H** - Hook | 0-30 sec | Stop scroll, pattern interrupt, promise value |
| **I** - Intro | 30 sec - 1 min | Context, who it's for, what you'll cover |
| **V** - Value | 5-9 min | Main content with clear sections |
| **E** - Engagement | Throughout | CTAs every 2-3 min (like, comment, subscribe) |
| **S** - Strong CTA | Last 30 sec | Summary, next action, end screen |

---

### Section-by-Section Template

#### H - HOOK (0-30 seconds)

**Purpose:** Stop scroll, create curiosity, establish credibility

**Formula:**
```
[Bold claim or question that challenges conventional wisdom]

"If everyone else says [X], I ask [Y]."

"By the end of this video, you'll know exactly how to [specific outcome]."
```

**Example:**
```
"I'm going to tell you something that goes against everything you've heard about improving at golf.

Ready?

Less is more.

Not less effort. Less chaos. Less jumping from tip to tip.

By the end of this video, you'll know exactly why most golfers never reach their potential—and what to do instead."
```

---

#### I - INTRO (30 sec - 1 min)

**Purpose:** Set context, establish who this is for, preview what's coming

**Formula:**
```
"Here's why this matters:"

[Brief context on the problem—2-3 sentences]

"This video is for [specific audience]."

"Here's what we'll cover:
1. [Topic 1]
2. [Topic 2]
3. [Topic 3]

Let's get into it."
```

---

#### V - VALUE (Main Content, 5-9 minutes)

**Structure:** 3-5 main sections, each 1-2 minutes

**Per-Section Formula:**
```
[SECTION HEADER - State the point clearly]

"First... [Point with context]"

[Story or example that illustrates—be specific]

[The insight or takeaway]

"Here's what this means for you..."

[Transition to next section]
```

**Pacing Guidelines:**
- New visual/cut every 3-5 seconds
- B-roll or graphics to illustrate points
- Vary energy—build to key moments
- Specific examples > abstract concepts

---

#### E - ENGAGEMENT (Throughout)

**Insert CTAs every 2-3 minutes:**

**Options:**
- "If this is landing, hit subscribe—I post videos like this every week."
- "Drop a comment if you've experienced this."
- "Quick question—what's been your biggest struggle with [topic]?"
- "Like this video if you want Part 2."

**Placement:**
- After making a strong point
- Before transitioning to new section
- Natural pauses, not forced

---

#### S - STRONG CTA (Last 30 seconds)

**Purpose:** Summarize, direct next action, use end screen

**Formula:**
```
"Here's what to remember:

[Key point 1]
[Key point 2]
[Key point 3]

If you want to go deeper, watch this video next—[point to end screen]—where I break down [related topic].

And if you haven't already, subscribe. I'll see you in the next one."
```

## YouTube Voice Adjustments

### Compared to Written:
- Write for speaking, not reading
- Shorter sentences
- More "you" and "I"
- Contractions always
- Energy cues: [pause], [emphasis], [lean in]

### Speaking Patterns:
- "Here's the thing..."
- "Let me break this down..."
- "And here's where it gets interesting..."
- "Watch this..."
- "Ready?"

### What to AVOID:
- Reading a script word-for-word (sounds stiff)
- Long sentences
- Passive voice
- Filler words (umm, like, you know)
- Monotone delivery

## YouTube Algorithm Signals (2025)

### Shorts:
- Completion rate (watch to end)
- Loop rate
- Shares
- Comments

### Long-Form:
- Click-through rate (thumbnail + title)
- Average view duration / % watched
- Session time (did they watch more after?)
- Subscriber conversion

**Best Practices:**
- Thumbnail and title are 80% of success
- First 30 seconds determine retention
- Consistent niche builds authority
- 8-12 minutes is sweet spot for monetization
