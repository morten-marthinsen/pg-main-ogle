## Questions to Surface Ideas

When Brixton says "I don't have an idea," ask these questions:

### Recent Experience Questions
- "What happened in the last week that stuck with you?"
- "Any matches or practice sessions that revealed something?"
- "Something a golfer asked you that made you think?"

### Frustration Mining
- "What's something that frustrated you recently?"
- "What do you wish people understood about [golf/business/life]?"
- "What advice do you keep giving that people ignore?"

### Belief Reinforcement
- "Which core belief feels most relevant right now?"
  1. There is almost always a smarter way to do things
  2. Pressure reveals what actually works
  3. Progress comes from working on the right things in the right order

### Pillar-Specific Questions

**The Experimenter:**
- "What have you been testing or experimenting with lately?"
- "What's something that worked faster than expected?"
- "What's a 'conventional wisdom' you've proven wrong?"

**The Arena:**
- "Any recent rounds that taught you something?"
- "What did pressure reveal recently?"
- "A moment where your thinking was tested?"

**The Innovator:**
- "What do golfers get wrong that you see over and over?"
- "What would you tell someone just starting to improve?"
- "How would you explain [concept] to someone stuck?"

## Idea Generation: Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Identify the Pillar
"Which pillar are we speaking from today?"
- The Experimenter
- The Arena
- The Innovator

### Step 2: Check Story Bank
Search `BA - Brixton Content Ideas.md` for matching stories.

Present 3-5 options:
```
"Here are 3 stories that fit [Pillar]:

1. **[Story Name]** — '[Core message in one line]'
2. **[Story Name]** — '[Core message in one line]'
3. **[Story Name]** — '[Core message in one line]'

Which resonates, or want me to surface more?"
```

### Step 3: If No Story Fits, Ask Questions
Use the questions above to mine for ideas.

### Step 4: If Still Stuck, Use Distribution Matrix
Combine:
- Selected pillar + distribution theme + core belief

Example:
- Pillar: The Innovator
- Distribution: Contrarian Golf Truth
- Belief: Right things, right order
- Result: "Why your practice routine is making you worse"

### Step 5: Confirm Before Moving On
Never draft without a clear idea. Get explicit confirmation.
