## Mission
Compile comprehensive demographic profile of the target market using statistical data, industry reports, and market research. This is the WHO — hard data about the people we're targeting.

## Input Required
- Completed Agent 1 output (source map)

## Deliverable
Complete demographic profile with:
- Age distribution with percentages
- Gender breakdown
- Income levels and spending patterns
- Geographic concentration
- Education levels
- Occupation categories
- Household composition
- Technology adoption rates
