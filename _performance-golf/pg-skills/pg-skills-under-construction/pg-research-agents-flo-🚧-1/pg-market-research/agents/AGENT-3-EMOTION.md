## Enhanced Emotion Taxonomy (NEW in v5.0)

### FRUSTRATION FAMILY

| Emotion | Definition | Language Markers | Example |
|---------|------------|------------------|---------|
| **Exasperation** | "I've tried EVERYTHING" | Repeated failed attempts | "Nothing works no matter what I try" |
| **Confusion** | "I don't know what I'm doing wrong" | Lack of clarity | "Every tip makes sense but nothing clicks" |
| **Betrayal** | "YouTube ruined my swing" | Trusted source failed them | "I followed exactly what he said and got worse" |
| **Self-doubt** | "Maybe I'm just not athletic enough" | Identity threat | "Some people just have it, I don't" |
| **Method fatigue** | "I'm tired of contradictory advice" | Information overload | "Every pro says something different" |

### EMBARRASSMENT FAMILY

| Emotion | Definition | Language Markers | Example |
|---------|------------|------------------|---------|
| **Social shame** | "Can't play with my boss anymore" | External judgment | "I'm the one holding up the group" |
| **Self-judgment** | "I should be better by now" | Internal standards | "After 5 years I should be breaking 90" |
| **Comparison shame** | "Everyone else gets it" | Relative failure | "My friends picked it up in months" |
| **Performance anxiety** | "I freeze when people watch" | Situational | "I can do it on the range but not on the course" |
| **Identity threat** | "Maybe I'm not a golfer" | Existential | "I should just quit and find another hobby" |

## Intensity Indicators

### Language Markers for High Intensity:
- **ALL CAPS** usage
- Multiple punctuation (!!!, ???)
- Profanity
- Specific numbers ("I've taken 47 lessons")
- Time references ("for 3 years now")
- Superlatives ("the WORST", "every single time")
- Quantified frustration ("out of 10 rounds...")

### Intensity Scale:
| Level | Description | Indicators |
|-------|-------------|------------|
| 1-3 | Mild | Mentioned in passing, neutral tone |
| 4-6 | Moderate | Topic focus, some emphasis |
| 7-8 | Strong | Emotional language, emphasis |
| 9-10 | Intense | CAPS, profanity, specific numbers, desperation |

## The Six-Layer Dig (Enhanced from Three-Layer)

For each top desire/frustration, dig through all six layers:

```
LAYER 1 - SURFACE: What they say
    ↓
LAYER 2 - DEEPER: Why they want that
    ↓
LAYER 3 - DEEPEST: Identity level need
    ↓
LAYER 4 - ASPIRATION: Who they want to become
    ↓
LAYER 5 - AVOIDANCE: Who they don't want to be
    ↓
LAYER 6 - CORE FEAR: Root fear driving behavior
```

### Example Dig:

```
SURFACE: "I want to break 90"
    ↓
DEEPER: "I want to feel good at something I've invested in"
    ↓
DEEPEST: "I want to prove I'm capable of mastering a skill"
    ↓
ASPIRATION: "A legitimate golfer who earns respect on the course"
    ↓
AVOIDANCE: "The hacker who holds everyone up and embarrasses himself"
    ↓
CORE FEAR: "I'm fundamentally incapable and wasting my time/money"
```
