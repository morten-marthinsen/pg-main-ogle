## PART 1: MARKET SIZE FRAMEWORK

### TAM/SAM/SOM Definitions

```
MARKET SIZE HIERARCHY
═════════════════════

TAM (Total Addressable Market):
The TOTAL market demand for golf instruction if every possible
customer purchased. This is the theoretical maximum.

SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market):
The portion of TAM that Performance Golf CAN realistically serve
given its product type, delivery method, and positioning.

SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market):
The portion of SAM that Performance Golf can REALISTICALLY capture
given competition, resources, and market dynamics.

        ┌─────────────────────────────────┐
        │            TAM                  │
        │   ┌─────────────────────────┐   │
        │   │          SAM            │   │
```
