I'm exhausted. Not from golf — from starting over. Every single time.

If I'm going to do this — really do it — I need it to actually work. I need a program that doesn't abandon me after day two. I need follow-up. I need someone who's invested in my success beyond a single lesson. Not just for two days, but an entire year of game planning and coaching on the backend.

This is probably my last shot. If this doesn't work, I'm done trying to improve. I'll just accept that this is as good as I'll ever be. But I don't want to accept that. Not yet. Not if there's one more option that might actually work.

Show me something different. Show me why this won't be another waste. Because I'm sold on the idea — I'm all in — I just need to believe this time is different.
