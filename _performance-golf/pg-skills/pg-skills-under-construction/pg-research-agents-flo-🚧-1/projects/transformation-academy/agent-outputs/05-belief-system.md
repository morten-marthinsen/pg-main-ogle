## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This belief analysis reveals a HIGH-TICKET SPECIFIC belief structure that differs fundamentally from frontend buyer psychology.

**BREAKTHROUGH FINDING:** The dominant beliefs are NOT about golf mechanics. They're about:

1. **PERMANENCE SKEPTICISM** — "Lessons never stick for me"
2. **INSTRUCTION BETRAYAL** — "Every instructor says something different"
3. **SPOUSE PARTNERSHIP** — "We make financial decisions together"
4. **LOGISTICS REALITY** — "My schedule is genuinely complicated"

The campaign-powering inversion is NOT about golf technique — it's about PERMANENCE:

> **BELIEF:** "Golf instruction doesn't stick — I always regress."
> **INVERSION:** "Instruction doesn't stick because you're abandoned after the lesson. With a 12-month follow-up system, permanence is guaranteed."
