# **THE DAILY 5**

**If you spend your time like this, you will grow. Your game will improve. It's that simple.**

Most golfers plateau because they don't have a system. They practice randomly. They forget the key thing their coach told them. They skip fitness. They drift.

**Academy members make progress permanent because they follow The Daily 5\.**

Simple. Repeatable. Takes \~20 minutes a day.

**Do the Daily 5, make progress. Skip the Daily 5, plateau.**

## **☐ 1\. YOUR DRILL (5 minutes)**

**What:** The ONE drill your coach gave you 

**Why:** This drill addresses your root flaw. Do it daily and the new pattern becomes automatic.

**How:**

* 5-10 reps with a club (or training aid if recommended)  
* Focus on the FEEL, not perfection  
* Indoors or outdoors, range or backyard

**Not sure what to work on?** Submit a swing and ask your coach.

## **☐ 2\. YOUR 15TH CLUB (13 minutes)**

**What:** Golf-specific fitness routine.

**Why:** Your body is your 15th club. If you can't rotate, turn, or maintain posture, those other 14 clubs don't matter.

**How:**

* Follow the golf fitness program in your portal  
* 15 minutes, 3x/week minimum  
* Simple routines, no gym required

## **☐ 3\. LOG IT (1 minute)**

**What:** Track what you worked on today.

**Why:** Progress you don't track disappears. Tracking creates awareness and accountability.

**How:**

* Keep a simple log (notebook, phone notes, app)  
* Write: Date, drill worked, reps, how it felt  
* 30 seconds per session

## **☐ 4\. MONDAY FORM (3 minutes \- Weekly)**

**What:** Your weekly progress check.

**Why:** Helps us track your improvement and catch issues early.

**How:**

* Every Monday, you get an email with 5 questions  
* Takes 3 minutes  
* Don't skip it

## **☐ 5\. SUBMIT WHEN OFF (2 minutes \- As Needed)**

**What:** Send a swing to your coach when something feels wrong.

**Why:** Regression happens. Catching it early prevents backsliding.

**How:**

* Open the app  
* Record your swing  
* Submit with notes: "This feels off, what am I missing?"  
* Your coach reviews and responds (24-48 hours)

**Foundation:** 12 swings total (use them wisely)  
 **Transformation/Elite:** Unlimited swings (use them freely)

## **THE RULE**

**Do 1-3 daily.**  
**Do 4 weekly.**  
**Do 5 when needed.**

**Total time:** \~25 min/day \+ 3 min/week

**Miss a day?** Get back on the wagon. Don't quit because you missed one day.

**Make progress permanent.**
