## MARKET OPPORTUNITY IDENTIFICATION

### The "Forgiveness Gap"

**Key Insight from Cleveland:**
> "84% of golfers play cavity-back irons, yet most wedge offerings are blade designs"

**Market Opportunity:**
- Premium forgiveness wedge market is UNDERSERVED
- Cleveland CBX is primary competitor in this space
- ONE.1's "anti-chunk" positioning has minimal direct competition
- No major brand explicitly sells "eliminate chunking" as primary benefit

### Unmet Needs in Wedge Market

| Need | Current Solutions | Gap |
|------|-------------------|-----|

| Anti-chunk technology | None explicitly | **MAJOR GAP** |

| Confidence building | No brand messaging | **MESSAGING GAP** |
