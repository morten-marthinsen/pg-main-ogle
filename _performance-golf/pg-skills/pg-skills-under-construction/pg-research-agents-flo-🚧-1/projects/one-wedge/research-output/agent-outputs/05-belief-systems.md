## LIMITING BELIEFS

### BELIEF #1: "Chipping requires special technique"

**The Belief:**
> "Good chipping requires mastering multiple techniques for different lies and situations"

**Origin:**
- Golf instruction industry
- Tour player demonstrations
- YouTube complexity
- Lesson experience

**Evidence They'd Cite:**
- "Phil Mickelson has 15 different chip shots"
- "My pro taught me 6 different short game shots"
- "You need high shots, low shots, bump-and-runs..."

**Emotional Attachment:** HIGH
- Validates their failure ("This is HARD")
- Explains gap vs tour pros ("They have technique")
- Provides hope ("If I learn enough techniques...")

**Inversion Strategy:**
> **FLIP:** "Tour pros have technique because they practice 8 hours a day. For weekend golfers, ONE swing with the right equipment beats 15 techniques you can't execute under pressure."

**Copy Application:**
> "Forget the 15 different chip shots. ONE swing. ANY lie. Every time."
