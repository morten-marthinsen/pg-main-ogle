## COMPETITIVE AD LANDSCAPE OVERVIEW

The golf equipment advertising ecosystem operates across three primary channels:
1. **Direct Response (DTC):** YouTube pre-roll, Facebook/Meta, email
2. **Brand Awareness:** PGA Tour sponsorships, golf publication ads
3. **Retail Support:** In-store displays, retailer co-op

ONE.1 Wedge competes in the **Direct Response/DTC** channel where Performance Golf has expertise.

## COMPETITOR ADVERTISING ANALYSIS

### TITLEIST VOKEY (Minimal Direct Response)

**Advertising Approach:** Brand/aspirational, minimal DTC
- Primary: Tour player endorsements, "Most Used on Tour" positioning
- Channels: Golf publications, PGA Tour branding, retail presence
- Budget: High overall, low direct response

**Ad Messaging:**
- Focus on tour stats and professional validation
- Feature-forward (grooves, grinds, finishes)
- Minimal emotional pain/gain language

**Funnel:**
- Awareness → Retail consideration → In-store purchase
- Limited direct email marketing to consumers
- Relies on retail partner relationships

**Weakness for ONE.1 to Exploit:**
- Doesn't speak to amateur pain points
- No direct response education
- Premium price without DTC justification

## DTC GOLF EQUIPMENT ADVERTISING PATTERNS

### Successful DTC Golf Ads (Industry Benchmarks)

**Format Analysis:**
- **Long-form VSL:** 20-45 minutes (high commitment products)
- **Short-form hooks:** 30-60 seconds (Facebook/Instagram)
- **YouTube pre-roll:** 15-30 seconds (awareness)
- **Email sequences:** 7-12 touch points (launch campaigns)

**Hook Patterns That Work:**
1. **Problem Identification:** "Are you struggling with [specific issue]?"
2. **Curiosity Gap:** "The one thing tour pros know that you don't"
3. **Pattern Interrupt:** "Forget everything you know about [topic]"
4. **Social Proof:** "Why 50,000 golfers switched to [product]"
5. **Demonstration:** Video showing product in action

**Conversion Patterns:**
- Demonstration videos outperform static images 3:1
- Before/after sequences drive action
- Testimonials from "people like me" most effective
- Risk reversal (guarantee) increases conversion 15-25%

## RECOMMENDED AD STRATEGY FOR ONE.1

### Phase 1: Hook Testing (Week 1-2)

**Test 6-8 Hooks:**

**HOOK A - Direct Problem:**
> "Do you chunk chips? You're not alone. And it's not your fault."

**HOOK B - Belief Challenge:**
> "What if the problem isn't your technique... it's your wedge?"

**HOOK C - Specific Scenario:**
> "Standing over an easy chip with your playing partners watching..."

**HOOK D - Social Proof:**
> "Why thousands of golfers are ditching their blade wedges"

**HOOK E - Demonstration:**
> [Video: Traditional wedge digging vs. Auto-Glide gliding]

**HOOK F - Identity:**
> "For golfers who can hit a 7-iron 165 but can't chip to save their life"

### Phase 2: VSL Deployment (Week 3-4)

**Primary VSL Structure:**
- 28-32 minutes
- Problem-Agitation-Solution-Proof-Offer
- Frank story as narrative anchor
- Auto-Glide demonstration as centerpiece

**VSL Placement:**
- Dedicated landing page
- YouTube unlisted (for email traffic)
- Facebook lead generation

### Phase 3: Email Sequence (Week 4-6)

**7-Part Sequence:**
1. **Day 0:** Problem identification email
2. **Day 2:** "What no one tells you about chunking"
3. **Day 4:** Mechanism introduction (Auto-Glide)
4. **Day 6:** Frank's story (testimonial)
5. **Day 8:** Demonstration video
6. **Day 10:** Offer + guarantee
7. **Day 12:** Final urgency

### Phase 4: Retargeting (Ongoing)

**Facebook/Meta Retargeting:**
- Website visitors → Demo video ads
- VSL viewers (25%+) → Testimonial ads
- Cart abandoners → Guarantee + urgency ads
- Email non-openers → Hook variation ads

## AD CREATIVE RECOMMENDATIONS

### Video Ad Specs

**Short-Form (15-30s):**
- Hook: Problem identification (0-3s)
- Demo: Auto-Glide vs. dig (4-15s)
- CTA: "Watch the full demonstration" (16-20s)
- Captions: Required (80% watch silent)

**Long-Form VSL (28-32 min):**
- Shot on green/practice area
- Mix of presenter + B-roll demonstration
- Testimonial interviews intercut
- Screen capture for graphics/proof

### Static Ad Specs

**Facebook/Instagram:**
- 1:1 ratio for feed
- 9:16 for Stories/Reels
- Bold text overlay on demo images
- Before/after comparison format

### Email Specs

**Subject Line Testing:**
- Problem-focused: "Why you chunk chips (it's not what you think)"
- Curiosity: "The wedge secret tour pros won't tell you"
- Direct: "Stop chunking chips. Here's how."
