## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### The Opportunity
The ONE.1 Wedge enters a **$200M+ U.S. wedge market** where **84% of golfers play cavity-back irons but use blade wedges for their most difficult shots**. No major competitor explicitly positions against the #1 stated fear in golfer forums: **chunking**.

### The Big Idea
**"THE ANTI-CHUNK WEDGE"**

> Your technique isn't the problem. Your wedge is.

### The Primary ICP: Frank Morrison
- 58 years old, semi-retired financial advisor
- 17 handicap stuck for 4 years
- $15,000 invested in equipment, $8,000 in lessons
- Chunked a chip in front of his biggest client 4 years ago
- Would pay $500 for a wedge that chips without fear

### The Core Belief Inversion
**FROM:** "I chunk chips because my technique is flawed"
**TO:** "I chunk chips because my wedge is fighting me"

### The Unique Mechanism
**Auto-Glide Sole** - 4-way cambered design that accelerates through any lie instead of digging.
