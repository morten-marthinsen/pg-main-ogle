## Key Messaging Assets (From Research)

### Primary Headlines
- "The slice-fixing driver that doesn't sacrifice distance"
- "Everything you loved about SF1. Now with tour-level speed."
- "Same slice-fix. Now with serious distance."
- "Finally: straighter AND longer."

### The Upgrade Story
> "The SF1 proved that equipment can fix your slice without changing your swing. But we heard you; you wanted straighter shots AND more distance. So we rebuilt it from the ground up with 3-piece forged titanium. Same slice-fixing technology. Now with tour-level speed."

### Target Customer Profile
Golfers who slice the ball and have:
- Tried lessons, YouTube tips, and "anti-slice" drivers without lasting success
- Lost confidence off the tee
- Aimed left into trouble just to keep ball in play
- Lost distance due to the slice curve
- Experienced frustration with every "banana ball"

### Key Stats for Copy
- 96% of golfers want to eliminate their slice
- Only 8% currently hit a draw
- Expected distance gains: 20-30 yards (from titanium speed AND straighter ball flight)
