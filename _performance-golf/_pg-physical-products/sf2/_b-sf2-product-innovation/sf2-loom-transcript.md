## Specifications

### Three-Piece Forged Titanium Head

Forged titanium - forging is another word for stamping. It's a little different. It's different than forging an iron, but it's the same process. You press the pieces into shape with forged titanium. Force and pressure. That's forging.

So, this is a three-piece driver: crown, sole, face. Each of those pieces is made independently, forged or stamped, and then welded together into a product.

### Key Specs

- **Head Size:** 445 cc speed geometry head
- **Length:** 45" men's, 44.5" women's (a little bit less than a typical driver - really hard for an average player to square up longer drivers)
- **Face Angle:** 3° closed face angle spec
- **Loft:** 12° loft @ 3° closed FA; 15° loft @ 0° square FA
- **Lie:** 60° (a little bit more upright - helps square the face up as the shaft wants to droop during the swing)
- **Swingweight:** D2 (not too heavy, not too light)
- **Shaft:** Counterbalanced shaft (more weight up in hands for better speed and face closure)

### Shaft Flex Options

- 65 X (Extra Stiff)
- 60 Stiff
- 57 Regular
- 55 Senior
- 50 Senior Soft / Ladies

The X-Flex was developed because there are going to be some stronger players, now that we have a titanium head, that will want to use this product. Senior Soft is a men's length, Ladies Flex is a ladies length (half inch shorter).
