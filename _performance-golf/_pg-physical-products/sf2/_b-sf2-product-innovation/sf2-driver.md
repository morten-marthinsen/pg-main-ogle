# SF2 Driver

*The slice-fix titanium driver where all features stack together to square the face and speed the club for longer, straighter drives.*

---

## Problem & Solution

**PROBLEM:** If you struggle with a slice you'll never have full confidence on the golf course. Good golf always starts with a good drive, and no good drive ever starts with a slice.

**SOLUTION:** Designed with Slice Fix Technology a unique combination of slice fixing features that work together to square the face for you. All SF2 features stack together to square and speed the club for longer, straighter drives. The speed geometry design delivers more club speed with a square face on the course where and when it matters the most.

## Product Features

*The long distance Slice-Fix Driver that squares the clubface to your path for you!*

- **Anti-Slice Speed Geometry Design** - 445cc aerodynamic head with a unique slice fixing, speed enhancing feature stack
- **Aero Square Crown** - channels turn the heel around the shaft to square the face
- **Speed Geometry Toe Slot** - speeds the toe closed to square the face
- **Counter-Slice Keel** - angled heel weighting helps power the slice fix effect
- **Draw Biased CG** - location, deep and in the heel, to dynamically closes the face
- **Draw Calibrated Face Angle** - makes reducing your slice effortless
- **Draw Spin Face Bulge** - guarantees less slice spin across the face, heel-to-toe
- **Square Launch Specs** - with the right loft, lie, length and face angle to fix your slice
- **Takeaway Tracks** - provide visual guidance to start the club back on the perfect path
- **Premium Counterbalanced Shaft** - puts more control in your hands to close the face
- **Bold & Beautiful** - premium finish details

## Notable Changes/Improvements vs Original SF1

### 3pc Forged Titanium Head for Speed
- Thinner, faster face, max allowable face flex (COR/CT)
- Improved ball speed, longer distance, likely +20-30 yards

### 3pc Forged Titanium Head for Weight Distribution
- Thinner, stronger walls create discretionary weight
- For precise weight placement, CG location management
- Thicker heavier keel structure
- Improved dynamic face closure

### Improved Aerodynamics
- New toe slot for faster toe closure
- Improved, angled crown slots

### Refined Bulge and Roll Face Curvature
- Increased gear effect on toe hits (more draw spin)
- Reduced gear effect on heel hits (less cut spin)

### Improved Set Up Appearance
- Topline-crown channel interaction
- Less hooked look

### Improved and Amplified Hero Cosmetics, Graphics and Finishing
