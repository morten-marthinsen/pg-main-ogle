# POST-PURCHASE JOURNEY

## Stage 5: First Use Experience

**Critical Moments:**
- Unboxing (first impression of quality)
- First range session (expectation vs. reality)
- First on-course use (social validation)

**Success Indicators:**
- Noticeable ball flight change in first session
- Positive feedback from playing partners
- Confidence increase on tee

**Failure Risk Points:**
- Doesn't see immediate difference → doubt → return risk
- Overcorrection (hook) → "wrong driver" conclusion
- Playing partners skeptical → social doubt

**Intervention Opportunity:**
- Post-purchase email sequence:
  - Day 1: Setup/adjustment guide
  - Day 3: "What to expect in first range session"
  - Day 7: "First course round tips"
  - Day 14: "How's it going?" check-in
