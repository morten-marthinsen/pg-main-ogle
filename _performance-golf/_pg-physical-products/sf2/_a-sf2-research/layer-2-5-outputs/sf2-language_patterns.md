# SECTION 5: DO / DON'T VOCABULARY GUIDE

## DO USE (Audience-Aligned)

| Category | Approved Language | Why |
|----------|-------------------|-----|
| **Slice** | slice, curve right, push-slice, pull-slice | Technical but accessible |
| **Miss** | 2 fairways right, adjacent fairway, way right | Specific, visual |
| **Feeling** | fear, dread, frustration, embarrassment | Authentic emotions |
| **Equipment** | draw bias, draw driver, slice-fixing | Solution-oriented |
| **Results** | fairway, straight, consistent, reliable | Outcome-focused |
| **Speed** | immediate, instant, first 5 swings | Time-to-result |

## DON'T USE (Audience-Misaligned)

| Category | Avoid | Why |
|----------|-------|-----|
| **Corporate** | "revolutionary," "game-changing," "cutting-edge" | Sounds like marketing |
| **Overpromise** | "never slice again," "guaranteed fix" | Unbelievable |
| **Technical jargon** | "coefficient of restitution," "moment of inertia" | Over-complex |
| **Judgmental** | "your bad swing," "terrible mechanics" | Insulting |
| **Vague** | "better," "improved," "enhanced" | Meaningless without specifics |
