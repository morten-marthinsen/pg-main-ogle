# MEASUREMENT BY FUNNEL STAGE

## Top of Funnel (Awareness)

**Primary Metrics:** CTR, Video View Rate, CPM
**Secondary Metrics:** Reach, Frequency, Engagement

**Dashboard:**
- Daily: CTR, CPM, Spend
- Weekly: Video completion rates, Top performing creatives
- Monthly: Audience saturation, Creative fatigue

---

## Middle of Funnel (Consideration)

**Primary Metrics:** Landing Page CVR, Time on Page, Scroll Depth
**Secondary Metrics:** Email opt-in, Blog engagement

**Dashboard:**
- Daily: Sessions, Bounce rate
- Weekly: Page performance comparison, Heat maps
- Monthly: Funnel analysis, Drop-off points

---

## Bottom of Funnel (Conversion)

**Primary Metrics:** CVR, AOV, Cart Abandonment
**Secondary Metrics:** Checkout completion, Payment method success

**Dashboard:**
- Daily: Revenue, Orders, CVR
- Weekly: Cart analysis, Checkout optimization
- Monthly: Cohort analysis, Customer value

---

## Post-Purchase (Retention)

**Primary Metrics:** Return Rate, NPS, CSAT
**Secondary Metrics:** Support tickets, Review sentiment

**Dashboard:**
- Weekly: Returns, Support volume
- Monthly: NPS survey results, Customer feedback themes
- Quarterly: Lifetime value, Repeat purchase
