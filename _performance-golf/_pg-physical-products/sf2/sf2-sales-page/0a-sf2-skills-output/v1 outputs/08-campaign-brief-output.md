## Language Dos & Don'ts

**DO USE:**
- "slice" (not "fade")
- "military golf"
- "physics"
- "finally"
- Specific numbers (96%, 5.7, etc.)
- Direct Golf Digest attribution

**DON'T USE:**
- "game improvement" (stigma)
- "beginner" (insulting)
- "crutch" or "band-aid"
- "never slice again" (too absolute)
- Vague qualifiers ("many," "some")
