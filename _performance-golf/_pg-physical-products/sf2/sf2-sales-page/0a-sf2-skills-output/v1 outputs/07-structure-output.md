# SECTION BRIEFS

## Section 1: LEAD

### Purpose
Hook attention, create intrigue, preview promise

### Key Elements
- **Hook:** "96% of golfers fight a slice. Only 8% hit draws."
- **Authority tease:** "PING's VP calls it 'the best-kept secret in golf'"
- **Promise preview:** "Fix your slice without sacrificing distance"
- **Curiosity gap:** What is this secret?

### Copy Direction
Open with the 96% statistic immediately. Use it to create instant identification ("that's me"). Tease the secret without revealing it. Promise transformation but create curiosity about HOW.

### Proof to Include
- P-047: "96 percent want to hit anything but a slice"
- H-032: "best-kept secret in golf" (teaser only)

---

## Section 2: PROBLEM AGITATION

### Purpose
Resonate with pain, validate frustration, deepen problem

### Key Elements
- **Pain scenes:** Military golf, adjacent fairways, 90-degree slice
- **Failed solutions:** Lessons that didn't work, endless practice, equipment graveyard
- **Emotional resonance:** Fear, embarrassment, frustration, defeat
- **Pattern recognition:** "Sound familiar?"

### Copy Direction
Use verbatim language from research. Paint vivid pictures of the slice experience. List everything they've tried. Make them feel understood before offering solution.

### Proof to Include
- P-001: "military golf (left, right, left, right)"
- P-021: "2 fairways to the right"
- P-046: "mentally prepared to hit a bad shot"
- P-118, P-119: lessons failed

---

## Section 3: ROOT CAUSE REVEAL

### Purpose
Shift blame from golfer to equipment, create "aha" moment

### Key Elements
- **Reframe:** "It's not your swing. It's your equipment."
- **Evidence:** You can hit irons, just not driver
- **Why nothing worked:** Equipment was fighting you
- **Relief:** "It's not your fault"

### Copy Direction
This is the emotional turning point. Remove self-blame. Explain WHY lessons and practice couldn't work (physics). Create moment of revelation and relief.

### Proof to Include
- RC-017: "Most off-the-shelf drivers are 45-46\""
- Driver-specific pain quotes

---

## Section 4: MECHANISM PRESENTATION

### Purpose
Explain HOW Square-Lock Face Technology works

### Key Elements
- **Mechanism name:** Square-Lock Face Technology
- **Physics explanation:** Heel CG placement
- **Expert validation:** TaylorMade CTO quote
- **Differentiation:** Fixed > adjustable
- **Simplicity:** "The club does the work"

### Copy Direction
Make the physics simple. Use the expert quotes to build credibility. Show contrast with competitors (adjustable weights). Emphasize: no swing changes required.

### Proof to Include
- RC-024: "When you move the CG to the heel..."
- V-020: "fixed internal weighting" > adjustable

---

## Section 5: PROOF STACK

### Purpose
Build overwhelming evidence that this works

### Key Elements
- **Third-party authority:** Golf Digest testing
- **Expert endorsement:** PING VP quote
- **Quantified results:** 70%, 5.7 yards, <5 swings
- **Testimonials:** Real transformations
- **Competitor contrast:** PING loses distance

### Copy Direction
Layer proof systematically. Start with Golf Digest (highest authority). Add PING VP endorsement. Stack specific numbers. Include emotional testimonials. Contrast with competitor weakness.

### Proof to Include
- H-029: "70% improved, 5.7 yards farther"
- H-030: "less than five swings"
- H-032: "joyous to watch... best-kept secret"
- H-171, H-090, H-112: testimonials
- CM-030: "PING eight yards shorter"

## Section 6: PRODUCT INTRODUCTION

### Purpose
Formally introduce the SF2 Driver

### Key Elements
- **Product reveal:** "Introducing the SF2 Driver"
- **Technology recap:** Square-Lock Face Technology
- **Build quality:** Materials, construction, design
- **Confidence at address:** AeroSquare Crown

### Copy Direction
Transition from mechanism proof to product presentation. Show the club. Highlight the technology features. Build desire for physical product.

## Section 7: OFFER PRESENTATION

### Purpose
Present complete offer and value stack

### Key Elements
- **Core offer:** SF2 Driver
- **Bonus stack:** Training, guide, cheat sheet
- **Value comparison:** vs. lessons, vs. Callaway
- **Price reveal:** Strategic positioning
- **Guarantee:** 90-day satisfaction, no subscription

### Copy Direction
Build value before price reveal. Stack bonuses. Compare to alternatives (lessons, premium drivers). Reveal price as "incredible deal." Strong guarantee presentation.

---

## Section 8: CLOSE

### Purpose
Drive action, overcome final hesitation

### Key Elements
- **Future pacing:** Imagine hitting fairways
- **Risk reversal:** Nothing to lose
- **Urgency:** Production limited
- **Call to action:** Order now
- **Identity close:** "Join the elite 8%"

### Copy Direction
Paint picture of transformed golf life. Address "what if it doesn't work" with guarantee. Create urgency without being pushy. Strong CTA. Close with identity transformation.
