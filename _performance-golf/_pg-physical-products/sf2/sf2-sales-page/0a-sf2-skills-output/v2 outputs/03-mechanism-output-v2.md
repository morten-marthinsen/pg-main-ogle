# LAYER 4: OUTPUT PACKAGE

## Mechanism Statement

> **"SliceFix SpeedTech™"** — The dual-action engineering system that fixes your slice AND adds distance. Precision heel-weighted CG squares your face automatically, while 3-piece forged titanium construction delivers maximum ball speed. Unlike previous draw-bias drivers that traded distance for accuracy, SliceFixed SpeedTech delivers BOTH.

## Core Explanation

### How It Works (E5 Format)

**1. The Slice-Fix Component:**
> When the center of gravity is positioned in the heel, the clubface naturally wants to rotate closed through impact. That's physics — the same physics TaylorMade's CTO confirmed: "When you move the CG to the heel, no matter where you hit it, it produces more draw."

**2. The Speed Component:**
> 3-piece forged titanium construction allows a thinner, faster face pushed to maximum allowable flex. This is the same premium construction used in $600 Big Golf drivers. The result: explosive ball speed even as the face squares.

**3. The Combination:**
> Previous draw-bias drivers had to choose: put weight in the heel for accuracy (losing distance) OR thin the face for speed (losing accuracy). Titanium changed the equation. Now we can do BOTH — heavier heel weighting AND faster face — in the same club.

### Analogy

> "Imagine a race car with exceptional handling AND top-end speed. Before advanced materials, you had to choose one or the other. Titanium is the SF2's advanced material — it lets us build in the accuracy features without sacrificing the speed."
