> SliceFix SpeedTech™ is the dual-action engineering system that fixes your slice AND adds distance. Unlike previous draw-bias drivers that traded distance for accuracy, SliceFix SpeedTech delivers BOTH.

- 3-piece forged titanium construction

- Same premium material as $600 flagship drivers

**The Breakthrough:**

# SECTION 3: THE ROOT CAUSE

## Named Problem

> **"The Slice-Distance Trap"**

### The Trap Explained

For decades, golfers have been told to CHOOSE:
1. Fix your slice with a draw-bias driver... but sacrifice 15-20 yards of distance
2. Keep your distance driver... and keep slicing into the trees

**This isn't a choice. It's a trap.**

### Why The Trap Exists

Draw-bias technology required heavy heel weighting, which:
- Shifted CG away from face center
- Reduced ball speed
- Cost 10-20 yards of distance

Golfers accepted this tradeoff as physics.

### How SF2 Breaks The Trap

Titanium construction allows:
- Heavier heel weighting (more draw)
- Thinner, faster face (more speed)
- BOTH in the same club

Golf Digest confirmed: Draw drivers hit **5.7 yards FARTHER** on average.
