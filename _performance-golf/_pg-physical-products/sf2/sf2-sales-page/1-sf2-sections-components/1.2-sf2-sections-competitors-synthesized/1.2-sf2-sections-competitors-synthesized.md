## KEY INSIGHTS

### Insight 1: Social Proof Gap
OEMs dramatically under-index on customer reviews (42%) and star ratings (42%). DTC brands (80%) make reviews a HERO element. **SF2 should lean into DTC-style review prominence.**

### Insight 2: Problem Acknowledgment
DR brands openly name the problem ("slice," "accuracy"). OEMs use euphemisms ("forgiveness," "draw-bias"). **SF2 should directly acknowledge the slice problem like DR while backing it with OEM-level technology credibility.**

### Insight 3: Trust Architecture
- OEMs rely on: Brand authority + tech specs
- DTC relies on: Customer reviews + value messaging
- DR relies on: Guarantees + editorial endorsements

**SF2 needs ALL THREE:** Tech credibility (OEM) + Review density (DTC) + Guarantee prominence (DR)

### Insight 4: Price Positioning
| Category | Range | SF2 Opportunity |
|----------|-------|-----------------|
| OEM | $449-$699 | Undercut significantly |
| DTC | $319-$499 | Competitive with more features |
| DR | $179-$549 | Premium DR positioning |

**SF2 likely position:** $197-$297 (DR pricing with DTC quality perception)

## STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS FOR SF2

### Adopt from OEM Baseline:
1. Technology section with named proprietary tech
2. Detailed specifications table
3. Multiple product images (5-8 minimum)
4. Face technology explanation
5. Materials callouts (carbon, titanium equivalent)
6. Configuration options

### Adopt from DTC:
1. **Customer reviews as HERO element** (not buried)
2. Star rating visible immediately
3. Verified buyer badges
4. Third-party review links/quotes
5. Lead time/shipping transparency
6. Financing options (Affirm, Shop Pay, etc.)
7. Expert help CTA

### Adopt from DR:
1. **Problem hook opening** ("Are you tired of...")
2. **Slice-specific positioning** - own the problem
3. **Money-back guarantee PROMINENT** - above fold
4. **Editorial review quote** in hero area
5. **Feature checklist format** for scanning
6. **Free shipping** as trust badge

### Avoid from DR:
- Excessive urgency (countdown timers)
- Vague "independent testing" claims without data
- Celebrity endorsements without substance
- Non-conforming equipment positioning
- Before/after claims without proof
