4. MyGolfSpy "Most Wanted 2025" recognition
5. 460cc traditional shape with modern enhancements
6. Custom built to order
7. Direct-to-consumer = better value

**Target Audience:**
- Value-conscious golfers seeking OEM-quality performance at lower price

- Tech-savvy buyers who research before purchasing

**Trust Builders:**

7. Named designer credibility (Bob Renegar)
8. True Temper Fitting Center badge
9. 23 answered customer questions

**Urgency/Incentives:**
1. Free shipping over $199 (product qualifies)

4. "In stock" availability status

**Risk Reducers:**

3. Contact options (phone, email, chat)
