## NOTABLE OBSERVATIONS

**Fitting-Centric Approach:**
PXG heavily emphasizes custom fitting throughout the page - fitting CTAs appear in sticky navigation, hero section, and dedicated section. This suggests they see fitting as a key conversion driver and differentiator.

**Model Segmentation:**
Three driver variants (0311, Tour-1, Ultra-Lite) with clear visual differentiation based on Forgiveness/Launch/Spin characteristics. This allows them to capture different player segments.

**Premium Brand at Accessible Price:**
PXG has transitioned from ultra-premium ($1000+) to accessible premium ($499). The page maintains premium messaging while highlighting the value proposition.

**No Customer Reviews:**
Notable absence of customer reviews on the product page - relies heavily on tour pro testimonials and press quotes instead. This may be intentional to maintain premium positioning.

**Veteran Identity:**
"Proud To Be Veteran-Owned" badge and Heroes Program prominently featured - differentiator from other DTC brands.
