## DR-SPECIFIC OBSERVATIONS

### Direct Response Techniques Used:
1. **Ecosystem Lock-in** — Driver optimized for Polara balls (requires additional purchase)
2. **Technical Authority** — Extensive specs create credibility
3. **Value Stacking** — Included torque wrench adds perceived value
4. **Adjustability Promise** — Multiple adjustment options suggest premium product
5. **Educational Content** — Video tutorial builds confidence

### What's Missing (vs. typical DR pages):
- No long-form sales copy
- No before/after testimonials
- No celebrity endorsements
- No urgency timers
- No bonus stacking
- No comparison to competitors
- No satisfaction guarantee prominently displayed
- No discount pricing

### Page Style:
**Product-focused e-commerce** with technical emphasis. Less "salesy" than typical DR, more focused on specifications and ecosystem integration. The cross-sell strategy (driver + balls) is the main DR element.

### Critical Observations:
1. **Non-Conforming Warning** — Openly states it exceeds USGA limits, which limits competitive play
2. **PayPal Exclusion** — No returns for PayPal orders is unusual and potentially concerning
3. **Cross-Sell Products Sold Out** — All recommended Polara balls show "Sold out" which undermines the ecosystem pitch
4. **Right-Hand Only** — Limits addressable market
