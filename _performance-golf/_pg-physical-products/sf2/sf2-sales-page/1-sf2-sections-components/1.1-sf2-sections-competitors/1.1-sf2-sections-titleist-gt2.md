## LEVEL 2: DEEP ANALYSIS (Marketing Angle & Conversion Hypothesis)

### MARKETING POSITIONING

**Primary Message:** Maximum forgiveness for players who don't always find the center of the face

**Key Claims:**
1. "Speed & Forgiveness Across The Face" — Both performance and forgiveness
2. "High-MOI driver without sacrificing speed" — No trade-off messaging
3. "Consistently impressive distance from any impact point" — Off-center forgiveness
4. "Signature Titleist Sound and Feel" — Premium experience
5. "Split Mass Construction" — Named proprietary technology
6. "Speed Ring® VFT Face" — Trademarked face technology

**Target Audience:** Mid-handicap players who value forgiveness but don't want to sacrifice Tour-level performance or brand prestige

### CONVERSION ELEMENTS

**Trust Builders:**
- Titleist brand authority
- Detailed technology explanations
- Multiple shaft options from Tour-trusted manufacturers
- Model comparison tool
- Fitting integration

**Purchase Enablers:**
- Direct pricing ($449)
- Add to cart capability
- Multi-step configuration (Club → Shaft → Grip → Review)
- Lead time transparency (6 weeks)
- Custom options PDF link

**Missing Elements (vs DR/DTC):**
- NO customer reviews visible on page

- NO price anchoring (no strikethrough)
- NO guarantee language visible

### CONVERSION HYPOTHESIS

**HELPING:**
- Clean, professional presentation builds trust
- Detailed specs satisfy research-oriented golfers
- Comparison tool reduces decision paralysis between models
- Multi-step configurator guides purchase decision
- Fitting integration adds value and reduces returns

**HURTING:**
- No social proof (reviews, usage stats) on product page
- 6-week lead time may deter impulse purchases
- No urgency elements
- Price with no anchor may feel high without context
