### Why These Elements Are Unique

**NLS Context:** NLS is optimized for supplement/wellness PDPs where:

- Products are consumable (ongoing purchase)
- Results are internal/physiological
- Competition is other supplements
- Trust comes from ingredients/formulation

**SF2 Context:** SF2 is a golf equipment page where:

- Product is one-time purchase (driver)
- Results are visible/measurable (distance, accuracy)
- Competition is big-brand OEMs (Titleist, Callaway, TaylorMade)
- Trust comes from technology + expert endorsement + third-party data

The 12 UNIQUE elements address SF2-specific challenges:

- **Unknown brand competing with OEMs** → Media Credibility Bar, Anti-OEM positioning
- **Higher price point single purchase** → Value stack, countdown, guarantee depth
- **Golf is situational** → Use Case Scenarios, Challenge Section
- **Golfers fear change** → NO/NO/NO trio, "without changing swing"
