## SECTION 6: TECHNOLOGY FEATURES

**Section Headline:** Kill The Slice. Keep The Speed.

7 Slice-Fix features work together to square the face and maximize ball speed. Here's how each one helps you hit more fairways:

---

### FEATURE 1: Speed Geometry Toe Slot

**Headline:** Titanium Tames The Toe

**Copy:** The aerodynamic slot carved into the toe reduces drag as the club moves through impact. The toe catches up to the heel faster, so the face squares itself — while the reduced drag maintains clubhead speed that powers the fast face.

This means you'll hit straighter drives without manipulating your swing. No timing the face. No "holding it off." Just swing naturally and Trust In The Titanium Construction.

**Visual:** [Feature animation showing airflow and face squaring]

---

### FEATURE 2: Fast Face Construction

**Headline:** Straight Is Far, And Far Is Fun

**Copy:** 3-Piece Forged Titanium construction creates a thinner, faster face that generates higher ball speed with the same swing speed. PLUS: straight shots travel farther than slices — so you gain distance two ways.

Built-in ball speed means longer drives without swinging harder. Shorter approach shots, easier scoring opportunities, and more birdie looks.

**Visual:** [Face flex animation showing ball speed generation]

---

### FEATURE 3: Counter-Slice Weighting

**Headline:** Let Physics Work For You

**Copy:** Because 3-Piece Forged Titanium is light, we saved weight on the clubface (keeping it thin and fast for explosive ball speed) and redistributed that weight deep and in the heel. This creates a Draw Biased CG location that dynamically closes the face without shrinking the sweet spot.

Even when your swing leaves the face open, the club closes it FOR you. Which means, you hit fairways even on your bad swings.

**Visual:** [Weight distribution diagram showing CG location]

---

### FEATURE 4: AeroSquare Crown Channels

**Headline:** Stay In Play.

**Copy:** Compared to our first slice-fix driver, SF1, we completely re-engineered SF2 with deeper directional-control channels and more aggressive angles, optimized for the faster clubhead speed created by 3-piece forged titanium. The air flow itself helps close your clubface.

This squares the clubface for you even when you don't release the club early. So even your worst drives stay in play.

**Visual:** [Airflow animation over crown channels]

---

### FEATURE 5: Draw-Calibrated Face Angle

**Headline:** Fix Your Slice Before You Swing

**Copy:** The face is pre-set 3° closed, but titanium's precision manufacturing lets us engineer the face geometry so it actually looks square when you're standing over it. No ugly "hooked" appearance — and no sacrificing distance because the thin, fast face that delivers maximum ball speed.

Every swing starts with a built-in margin for error. You can swing free instead of thinking about your grip, face, path, or tempo.

**Visual:** [POV: Address view showing square appearance]

---

### FEATURE 6: Draw Spin Face Bulge

**Headline:** Bad Swing? Good Drives.

**Copy:** The asymmetric face bulge design reduces slices because the heel side is flatter (less cut spin on heel hits) while the toe side is rounder (more draw spin on toe hits). Combined with the fast titanium face, your mis-hits fly straighter AND farther.

Your mis-hits are now playable. Instead of watching the ball curve into trouble, you'll see it working back toward the fairway.

**Visual:** [Face bulge diagram with spin direction arrows]

You get more clubhead speed without swinging harder — the sleeker design cuts through the air for you. Which means you'll pick up yards without changing a thing about your swing.
