### 5.3 Core Explanation

#### CORE LOGIC (78 words)

> Standard drivers are designed for tour players who close the face naturally at 115 mph. SliceFix Technology is a 7-point system that closes the face FOR you. Each feature attacks a different aspect of what causes your slice: aerodynamic drag, face angle, weight distribution, swing path. When they work together, the face squares automatically at impact. No swing change required. No practice needed. The technology does the work your timing can't.

#### ANALOGY — PRIMARY (34 words)

> "It's like power steering for your driver. You don't muscle the wheel to steer straight—the technology makes the correction for you. SliceFix does the same thing for your face angle."

#### ANALOGY — SECONDARY (31 words)

> "Think of it like 7 bodyguards, each protecting you from a different threat. One guards the face angle. One guards the swing path. Together, nothing gets through."

#### STEP-BY-STEP EXPLANATION (The 7 Points)

**POINT 1: AeroSquare Crown Channels**
> Angled channels carved into the crown reduce air resistance on the toe. This allows the toe to accelerate through impact, catching up with the heel for faster face closure.

**POINT 2: Counter-Slice Keel**
> Heavy, fixed weighting positioned on the heel that pulls the clubhead closed through impact. Unlike adjustable weights, this is permanently positioned for maximum slice correction.

**POINT 3: Speed Geometry Toe Slot**
> An aggressive slot carved into the toe that reduces drag. As the toe encounters less resistance, it speeds up and helps the face square faster.

**POINT 4: Draw Spin Face Bulge**
> Asymmetric face curvature—more rounded on the toe, flatter on the heel. Toe hits add draw spin. Heel hits reduce cut spin. Either way, the ball curves less right.

**POINT 5: Draw-Calibrated Face Angle**
> The face is set 3° closed at address—built-in insurance that gives you a head start on squaring the face. But the topline is engineered so it still looks square at setup.

**POINT 6: Takeaway Tracks**
> Visual alignment guides on the crown that help you start the club back on the right path. A better backswing leads to a better downswing and easier face closure.

**POINT 7: Counterbalanced Shaft**
> Weight shifted toward the grip for a more stable, controlled swing. Less wobble means more consistent face angle through impact.
