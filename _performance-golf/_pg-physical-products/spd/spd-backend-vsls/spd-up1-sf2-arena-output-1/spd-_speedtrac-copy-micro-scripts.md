## Category

---

## Dominant Selling Idea (DSI)

> **"The only swing trainer that gives you speed AND accuracy at the same time"**

### SIBMO Validation:
| Criteria | Assessment |
|----------|------------|
| **S - Superlative** | "Only" - No competitor trains both. They all focus purely on swing speed. |
| **I - Important** | Solves THE problem: "I gained 15 mph but my slice got worse." Speed without accuracy = faster miss. |
| **B - Believable** | Y-Trac Weight System creates non-axial CG (like a real driver). 15 configurations train face awareness. |
| **M - Measurable** | Speed gains + accuracy gains. "Show up on the scorecard, not just the radar." |
| **O - Ownable** | SuperSpeed/Stack/Rypstick all use shaft-axis weight. None address face control. White space is clear. |

---

## Binary Frame

> **"Fast AND Straight vs. Fast OR Straight"**

Competitors force golfers to choose between speed and accuracy. SpeedTrac eliminates the choice.

---

## Positioning Statement

SpeedTrac is the only swing trainer that gives you speed AND accuracy at the same time. While every other speed trainer on the market focuses purely on swing speed - leaving golfers faster but wilder - SpeedTrac's Y-Trac Weight System trains face control simultaneously with speed. The result: distance gains that actually show up on the scorecard, not just the radar.

---

## Micro-Pitch (APPROVED - 78 words)

> Every speed stick on the market trains you to swing faster. None of them train you to hit it straighter. That's why your gains never show up on the course. SpeedTrac is the only swing trainer giving you speed and accuracy at the same time. The Y-Trac Weight System creates real driver feel with 15 configurations that train speed and face control simultaneously. One tool. One system. Finally, speed training that transfers to the moments that matter most.
