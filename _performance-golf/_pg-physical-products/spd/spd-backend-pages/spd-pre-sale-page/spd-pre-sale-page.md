## [SECTION 5: THE PROBLEM — AND THE SOLUTION]

# THE SPEED GAP TRAP

[speed apex animation from SRSW demonstrating the gap]

You're not hitting shorter, less accurate drives because you're swinging slower.

You’re losing distance and accuracy because your highest swing speed ***(called your Speed Apex)*** is happening AFTER you hit the ball.

The wider the gap is between your top swing speed and the moment of impact, the shorter (and wilder) you hit your drives.


## Golf Is A Game Of Inches

[speed apex animation from SRSW demonstrating the difference as the gap gets wider]

❌ Speed Apex one inch before impact? You lose 10 yards and leak a few shots.

❌ Speex Apex three inches before impact? 30 yards gone and you can't hit a fairway.

❌ Speed Apex six inches before impact? That's when your driver goes the same distance as a 5-iron...

**And the only thing you're losing faster than golf balls – is *confidence.***


## Swing Tips Won't Save You

Swing tips rarely work because no swing tip closes your speed gap.

❌ Dropping your right foot back doesn't move your speed apex closer to impact
❌ Flaring your feet doesn't close the gap
❌ Swinging from the inside, keeping your left arm straight, keeping your shoulder high...

None of these tips address WHERE you release your speed.

**Bottom line:**

**Until you close your "Speed Gap" you will NEVER hit the ball as far or straight as you're capable of.**


## The Solution:
# RELEASE YOUR SPEED AT IMPACT

[speed apex animation from SRSW demonstrating closing the gap]

When you sync your speed apex with the moment of impact…

Two incredible things happen.

**#1:**

Your fastest swing speed happens AT IMPACT... so all your power transfers into the back of the ball.

**#2:**

Because you're releasing your speed at the ball instead of after it, your face is more square naturally…

Eliminating slices and hooks.

**That's how you gain distance AND accuracy at the same time.**
