## Warnings and Limitations

1. **emotional_intensity_map.json was not available.** Emotional intensity was estimated from text analysis (exclamation marks, CAPS, intensifier words, emotional tag detection). Actual intensity scores would improve contrast scoring precision.

2. **physical_tag and emotional_tags were inferred** from text content using market_config aspect definitions. These are estimates, not human-validated classifications.

3. **GOLD pair count is high (21/38 = 55%)** relative to typical distributions. This reflects the strong emotional content in the golf speed training market. The GOLD criteria were applied consistently per the skill specification.

4. **Uncovered categories are numerous** (28 categories). Many of these are specific sub-categories that simply didn't have sufficient quote volume in both PAIN and HOPE to form valid pairs. This is not a data gap issue but a granularity issue in the category taxonomy.
