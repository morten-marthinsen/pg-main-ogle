## Downstream Usage Guide

### For Headline Generation (Skill 08.5)
- Use GOLD pairs TP-001 through TP-005 as primary transformation hooks
- The `copy_angle` field provides ready-to-use headline seeds
- The `marketing_formats.headline` and `marketing_formats.subhead` provide starting frameworks

### For Story Construction (Skill 10)
- GOLD and HIGH pairs with `Emotional_Confidence` type are ideal for story arc construction
- The `before_after` marketing format maps directly to story beginning/ending
- `age_decline` to `age_defying` pairs align with the SpeedTrac target demographic (35-65)

### For Root Cause Narrative (Skill 11)
- `speed_plateau` PAIN quotes provide evidence for the "brain's speed governor" root cause
- `wasted_investment` PAIN quotes support the "wrong approach" countersell narrative
- `Physical_Resolution` type pairs validate the mechanism effectiveness

### For VSL/Landing Page Copy
- Lead with GOLD TP-003 (Distance Recaptured) — highest emotional contrast score (10/10)
- Use TP-001 (Age Reversal) for demographic-targeted segments (50+ golfers)
- Use TP-005 (Finally Works) for skeptic-targeted segments
- Stack 3-5 HIGH pairs as supporting proof/testimonial sections
