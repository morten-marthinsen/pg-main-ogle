## **1\. SUPERSPEED GOLF TRAINING SYSTEM**

**What It Is/How It Works:** A three-club overspeed training system with different weighted clubs (light, medium, and heavy). Uses overspeed training methodology to "trick" your body into moving faster than it thinks it can. Golfers swing each weighted club in a specific sequence to build neuromuscular speed patterns.

**Link:** [https://superspeedgolf.com/products/mens-set-2-0](https://superspeedgolf.com/products/mens-set-2-0)

**Price:** $129.99-$199.99 (currently on sale from $199.99)

**Main/Differentiating Features:**

* Three separate weighted clubs for progressive training  
* Simplest to get started \- no tech required  
* Free mobile app with 5 levels of training protocols  
* 15-minute workouts, 3x per week  
* Used by PGA Tour professionals including Padraig Harrington (3x Major Champion)  
* Named "2025 Best Training Aid for Improving Speed"

**Market Performance:**

* Widely regarded as the most trusted speed training program  
* Extensive PGA Tour player usage  
* Strong brand recognition as the "OG" of speed training

**Feedback:**

*Positives:*

* Simple, easy to use with clear protocols  
* Proven results: Users report 5-8% speed gains (5-10 mph increase)  
* No learning curve \- straightforward from day one  
* Strong educational support with video guidance  
* Results seen quickly (3-8% increase in first session, sustained with continued training)  
* Works well for beginners too

*Negatives:*

* Three separate clubs are bulky to transport and store  
* Takes up too much space in golf bag  
* Nearly impossible to travel with all three clubs  
* Higher time commitment rotating through each club  
* No adjustable weights (must use all three clubs)  
* Generic protocols compared to more advanced systems  
* Only 14-day return policy  
* Need to purchase speed measurement device separately  
* Some users report it can exacerbate existing swing faults
