## Core Market Lessons for SPD

1. Category baseline is already strong on hero commerce and mechanism storytelling.
2. Category baseline is weak on complete end-to-end conversion utility consistency.
3. Most pages sell speed first and treat control as secondary or implicit.
4. Transfer-to-course confidence remains under-explained or assumed.
5. Risk reversal and trust utilities are still under-leveraged relative to skepticism level.

## SPD-Relevant Opportunity Windows from Competitor Reality

- Win window 1: own the speed-plus-control narrative (not speed-only).
- Win window 2: deliver cleaner mechanism-to-transfer bridge than competitors.
- Win window 3: tighten operational trust layer (guarantee, support, payment, localization, utility coherence).
- Win window 4: use proof-governance discipline to out-credibility hype-heavy pages.
