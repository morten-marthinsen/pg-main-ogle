## Marketing System Breakdown

### Message/Angle Clusters
- Speed outcome angle: increase clubhead speed through variable inertia training.
- Mechanism angle: weighted hardware + app-guided dynamic programming.
- Authority angle: Dr. Sasho MacKenzie science framing and high app-store review rating.
- Practicality angle: family sharing, guided timers, hands-free logging, cross-platform access.

### Funnel Logic (Observed)
1. Brand legitimacy + deep navigation/support options.
2. Product certainty and purchase path (hero gallery + ATF price/variant/CTA).
3. Mechanism and protocol explanation (hardware specs + app training system).
4. Objection handling (FAQ help center + device/platform/subscription answers).
5. Risk control (shipping/returns/warranty page details).
6. Persistent footer trust utilities and social/support links.

### Offer Architecture
- Core hardware offer at `$299.00` with adult/junior variants.
- Membership inclusion anchor: `Includes 1-Year Membership to TheStack App`.
- Post-year app economics clarified in FAQ (`$99/year`; wedging+putting tier references).

### Proof Model
- On-product quantified proof: `Rated 4.9 with 5,500+ Reviews on App Store`.
- Dedicated review surface: `Real Stackers. Real Reviews.`
- Expert attribution: repeated Dr. Sasho MacKenzie references.

### Risk Reversal Model
- Hardware: 2-year defect warranty + 30-day return request window.
- Radar accessory: 30-day return with restocking-fee caveat.
- Returns operations: explicit processing and refund timing disclosures.

### Conversion Strengths
- Strong ATF commerce clarity and mechanism specificity.
- High FAQ depth for onboarding, compatibility, subscription, and training objections.
- Detailed shipping/returns/warranty transparency.

### Conversion Weaknesses
- No visible promo urgency layer for price-sensitive buyers.
- No visible email capture module in extracted pages.
- No visible currency selector or payment icon stack in extracted text.
