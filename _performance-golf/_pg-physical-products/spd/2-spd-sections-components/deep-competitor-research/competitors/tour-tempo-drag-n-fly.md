## Marketing System Breakdown

### Message/Angle Clusters
- Speed-power-distance outcome framing.
- Mechanism-first explanation (resistance tail + phase-by-phase motion effects).
- Authority-assisted trust (John Novosel + major champion association).
- Low-friction offer framing (discounted anchor price + free USA shipping + guarantee).

### Funnel Logic (Observed)
1. Navigation legitimacy and shopping context.
2. Product certainty via media, title, anchored pricing, and CTA.
3. Mechanism education with phase-specific movement instructions.
4. Trust/risk support via quote, stock status, and money-back guarantee.
5. Footer policy utilities and safety disclaimer.

### Offer Architecture
- Single-SKU front-end with explicit markdown (`$299.99` -> `$227.00`).
- Value enhancer through included lessons/drills (`$100 Value`).
- Free-shipping cue at product-form level.

### Proof Model
- Primary proof is authority/experience claim plus attributed quote.
- Secondary proof appears on supporting pages (major champion statistic and press-style claims).

### Risk Reversal Model
- Clear, explicit guarantee language (`30-Day Money-Back Guarantee`).

### Conversion Strengths
- Clear ATF price/value architecture.
- Strong mechanism specificity tied to swing phases.
- Good trust utilities (guarantee, policy links, safety language).

### Conversion Weaknesses
- No visible countdown/promo urgency block.
- No visible payment icon stack.
- No visible currency/localization controls.
- FAQ layer not observed on this product flow.
