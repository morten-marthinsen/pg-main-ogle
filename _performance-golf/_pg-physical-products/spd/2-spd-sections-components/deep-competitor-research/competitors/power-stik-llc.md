### Message/Angle Clusters
- Distance gap angle: `Tired Of Being 30 Yards Behind On Your Drive?`

- Strength + flexibility transfer angle: weighted resistance to golf-specific muscles.
- Path correction angle: inside-out swing framing and slice-cure narrative.
- Authority angle: orthopedic-surgeon origin + review publication references.

### Funnel Logic (Observed)
1. Credibility primer (`TWENTY TWO YEARS OF SALES-ZERO RETURNS`) + basic nav.
2. Problem framing and mechanism explanation on Home.
3. Protocol page (`Fitness Routine`) for compliance and repeat use.
4. Review framing + technical details on `Video Reviews`.
5. Direct variant pricing + shipping terms on `Buy Now`.

- Single core product with three grip variants (`Reg Grip`, `RH Trainer Grip`, `LH Trainer Grip`) at parity pricing (`$119.95 USD`).

### Risk Reversal Model
- Explicit refund/guarantee policy language not observed in captured pages.
- Implied reassurance appears via `OF SALES-ZERO RETURNS`.

### Conversion Strengths
- Clear pain-point headline and strong benefit density.

### Conversion Weaknesses
