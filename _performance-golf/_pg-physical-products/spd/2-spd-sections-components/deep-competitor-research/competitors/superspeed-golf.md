## Marketing System Breakdown

### Message/Angle Clusters
- Distance-first outcome framing (`gain 20 yards`).
- Mechanism-first framing (overspeed protocol + weighted sticks + app guidance).
- Authority framing (tour pro endorsement + media logos + testimonial library).
- Offer framing (bundle upsell + trade-in credit + shipping threshold value).

### Funnel Logic (Observed)
1. Brand legitimacy + promo anchor (header/nav + free-shipping strip)
2. Product certainty and immediate purchase path (hero media + ATF form)
3. Mechanism and usage clarity (`What's Included`, `How It Works`, cadence)
4. Mid-flow monetization levers (bundle switch + trade-in value)
5. Trust reinforcement (`AS SEEN IN`, coach/pro testimonials, review block)
6. Risk/objection handling (return policy, app support, shipping policy)
7. Footer conversion utilities (email capture, localization, payment icons, policy links)

### Offer Architecture
- Core offer: `Speed Sticks Pro / No Tech - $199.99`.
- High-ticket upsell: `Speed Sticks Pro / PRGR Launch Monitor - $399.99`.
- Dynamic AOV lever: bundle prompt with `save $30`.
- Salvage lever: trade-in program credit (`GET $30`) for competitor systems.

### Proof Model
- On-product-page proof: review count + on-page testimonial + media publication logos.
- Extended proof module: dedicated testimonial page with coach and user quotes.

### Risk Reversal Model
- Explicit 30-day return policy with no restocking fee and published support contact path.
- Operational reassurance via shipping policy transparency and app support SLA language.
