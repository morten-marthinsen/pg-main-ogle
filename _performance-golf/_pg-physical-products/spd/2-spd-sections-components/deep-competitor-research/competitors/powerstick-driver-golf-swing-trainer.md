## Marketing System Breakdown

### Message/Angle Clusters
- Distance-first claim: more speed -> more distance.
- Practical utility angle: warm-up + strength/flexibility.
- Broad-fit angle: all levels, men and women.
- Risk-reversal angle: money-back + lifetime guarantee.

### Funnel Logic (Observed)
1. Logistics reassurance (`Free shipping in continental US and Canada`)
2. Product certainty (image carousel + title + sale price)
3. Short mechanism/benefit stack (distance + speed + use cases)
4. Risk reversal and proof (guarantees + review aggregate)
5. Catalog expansion + email capture + footer trust utilities

### Offer Architecture
- Single-SKU hero with visible discount anchor (`$69.95` compare-at vs `$49.95` current).
- Native cross-sell module (`More from this collection`) adds bundle/upgrade paths (`Bundle 1: Swing Align + Power Stick`, `Swing Align + Power Stick Pro`).

### Proof Model
- Review aggregate (`Based on 47 reviews`) plus visible rating distribution.
- Inline review cards (for example `Great Product`, `More distance.`) provide qualitative outcome proof.

### Risk Reversal Model
- `90 Day No Questions Asked Money Back Guarantee`
- `Lifetime Guarantee`

### Conversion Strengths
- Clear, short above-the-fold commerce path.
- Strong risk reversal relative to price point.
- Cross-sell and newsletter modules preserve downstream value.

### Conversion Weaknesses
- No dedicated urgency/countdown construct.
- Limited long-form mechanism education for skeptical buyers.
- No dedicated on-page ask-a-question form block.
