## Marketing System Breakdown

### Message/Angle Clusters
- Distance + straightness outcome promise.
- Mechanism-led story (flex shaft + feedback).
- Transfer narrative (practice to course).
- Authority-assisted trust (awards/tour mentions).
- Offer-driven urgency (coupon + countdown).

### Funnel Logic (Observed)
1. Trust + promotion (header/promo bars)
2. Product certainty + purchase path (hero gallery/form)
3. Mechanism explanation + logistics reassurance (description/shipping)
4. Mid-page urgency recapture (flash sale timer)
5. Long-form persuasion + authority
6. Guarantee + email capture + footer trust utilities

### Offer Architecture
- Single-product hero price + layered coupon promotions.
- Mid-page promo reinforcement to catch non-converters.
- Bundling cue in shipping offer (`2+ clubs` free shipping with code).

### Proof Model
- Primarily authority + qualitative persuasion.
- Limited visible hard data proof blocks on-page.

### Risk Reversal Model
- Explicit money-back guarantee with low-friction return framing.

### Conversion Strengths
- Strong above-the-fold commerce clarity.
- Multiple urgency touchpoints.
- Clear mechanism and transfer narrative.
- Strong trust utilities (awards, tour references, payment icons, policies).

### Conversion Weaknesses
- Limited quantified evidence hierarchy.
- Heavy reliance on authority references vs structured performance proof.
- Potential promo fatigue from multiple discount layers.
