## Marketing System Breakdown

### Message/Angle Clusters
- Core promise: better golf through speed + tempo + control.
- Portability angle: indoor/outdoor, all-weather, breakdown carry-case utility.
- Flexibility angle: adjustable length and weight for skill-level fit.

### Funnel Logic (Observed)
1. Simple nav + direct hero promise.
2. Visual proof of physical product form.
3. Product + price + CTA repetition.
4. Mechanism explanation + attributes list.
5. Reviews/testimonials + guarantee.
6. Newsletter, contact, social, and policy utilities.

### Offer Architecture
- Single-SKU price anchor (`$129.99`) repeated across home, category, and PDP.
- Benefit-rich copy around a single primary CTA (`Add to Cart` / `Add to cart`).

### Proof Model
- Qualitative proof: testimonials and one visible customer review.
- Mechanism proof: adjustable components and concrete weight/size details.

### Risk Reversal Model
- Front-end guarantee mention (`Lifetime Guarantee`) backed by dedicated refund/returns policy.

### Conversion Strengths
- Extremely clear product focus (single-SKU flow).
- High copy/media repetition of core promise and use case.
- Strong portability + simplicity positioning.

### Conversion Weaknesses
- No visible urgency architecture.
- No dedicated FAQ block.
- No visible localization or payment-badge confidence layer.
