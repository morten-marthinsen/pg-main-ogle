### Funnel Logic (Observed)
1. Store legitimacy and utility navigation.
2. Promo-value preframe (`Free shipping ...`).
3. Hero media plus immediate price/CTA.
4. Mechanism and benefits bullets.
5. Guarantee stack and review proof.
6. Footer utilities (support, policy, newsletter, payment confidence).

### Offer Architecture
- Core product offer shown as discounted stack (`$49.95 $69.95`).
- Cross-sell support via `Related Products` and catalog-level `On sale` presentation.
- No visible bundle-switch or in-flow upsell modal in extracted PDP flow.

### Proof Model
- First-party proof: customer review snippets and review counts.
- Authority proof: company background and golf-industry experience claims.
- Mechanism proof: concrete feature claims tied to each side of the device.

### Risk Reversal Model
- Product-page guarantees: 90-day improvement refund + lifetime defect replacement.
- Terms-page reinforcement: 30-day returns for any reason, then manufacturing-defect coverage through six months.
