Face Forward Design positions the shaft behind the face for full unobstructed visibility of the entire striking surface — which means complete alignment clarity before you take the putter back. No hosel, no shaft in the way. Just face and ball.

T-Wing Tail saves weight from the rear and redistributes it forward — which means every gram removed from behind the shaft is a gram added where it matters: driving Forward Axis Weighting and keeping the face square for you.
