## PART 2: PRODUCT RESEARCH & STRATEGIC FRAMEWORK

### 📝 RS1 PRODUCT: ROLL STRAIGHT TECHNOLOGY™
- **Product:** RS1 Putter with Forward Axis Balance™.
- **The "Magical Sauce":** Weight forward of the shaft (Forward Axis Balance™). Positions CG in front of the shaft to create a "pulling" relationship.
- **Physics:** Creates "Face-Down Balance" (demonstrable by the table test).
- **Technical Specs:** 360g head, 74° Square-to-Square Lie Angle (most upright in market), CNC-machined steel front, carbon composite crown.

### 🎯 THE "ENEMY": FACE DRIFT
- **Root Cause:** 95% of putters have CG *behind* the shaft, creating a "pushing" relationship that causes the face to rotate open. This is **Face Drift**.
- **Pain Point:** Golfers blame themselves ("I pulled it"), but the physics of their equipment is sabotaging them.

### 📈 THE POSITIONING NARRATIVE (THE EVOLUTION)
1. **Phase 1 (Traditional):** Heel-weighted, weight in back. Putter flops open + human opens face = Double Failure.
2. **Phase 2 (LAB Golf):** Zero Torque/Neutral. Fixed the equipment flop, but left 100% of face control to the golfer. 
3. **Phase 3 (RS1):** Forward Weighting. Eliminates equipment flaw **AND** creates a counteracting force that helps the golfer stay square. Technology that "helps you human."
