## The Problem SF1 Solves

**Root Cause of Slice:** An open club face relative to the path of your swing at impact.

**The Amateur Struggle:**
- Big-name drivers are designed for pros who don't need help squaring the face
- 90% of amateur golfers slice the ball
- Traditional fixes require drills, range time, and swing changes
- Most golfers lack time and consistency to fix their slice through practice

## The Solution: Square Face Technology

The SF1 combines **7 slice-correcting features** that work together to create the "Slice Fix Effect" - automatically squaring the face at impact without any swing changes.

### The 7 Features

| # | Feature | What It Does |
|---|---------|--------------|
| 1 | **AeroSquare Crown** | Aerodynamically forces the toe to close faster in your downswing |
| 2 | **Anti-Slice Face Curvature** | Produces guaranteed draw-spin, even when you miss the center of the face |
| 3 | **Fixed Counter-Slice Weights (x2)** | Balance the clubhead's center of gravity so the face auto-squares in the bottom of your swing arc |
| 4 | **Takeaway Tracks** | Visual aid on the crown to guarantee your club starts and STAYS on a square path from setup to impact |
| 5 | **Draw-Calibrated Face Angle** | Sits at 3 degrees SHUT so you stand over the ball knowing your shot is going straight or drawing left |
| 6 | **Counterbalanced Shaft** | Puts weight closer to the grip so the clubhead releases earlier and squares the face faster through your downswing |
| 7 | **Customizable Shaft Options** | Match YOUR swing speed or average total driving distance |
