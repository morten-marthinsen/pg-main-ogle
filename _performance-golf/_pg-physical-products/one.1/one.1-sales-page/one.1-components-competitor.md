## DETAILED BREAKDOWN

---

## SECTION 1: HERO/PRODUCT SECTION
**Priority: Non-negotiable**
*Location: Above the fold | All 12 competitors have this section*

**Contains 7 components:**

**Product Image Gallery** — High-quality multi-angle product shots with zoom capability. Vokey shows 5+ angles; Cleveland shows lifestyle + studio shots; DTC brands use fewer images.

**Product Title** — Clear product name with model identifier. Format: "[Brand] [Model] [Type]" (e.g., "Vokey Design SM10 Wedge")

**Tagline/Value Proposition** — Single-line benefit statement. Examples: "Spin. Control. Versatility." (Vokey), "Maximum Spin From Every Lie" (Cleveland)

**Star Rating Display** — Aggregate review score shown prominently. Typically 4.5-5 stars with review count. Sub70 and Haywood feature this prominently; some OEMs bury it.

**Price Display** — Clear pricing with any "starting at" qualifiers. Range: $80 (Haywood) to $399 (PXG). Most OEMs at $179-199.

**Configuration Selectors** — Dropdowns/buttons for: Loft, Grind, Bounce, Hand, Shaft, Grip. Vokey has the most options; DTC brands have fewer.

**Primary Add-to-Cart CTA** — High-contrast button. "Add to Cart" (most common), "Buy Now" (PXG), "Add to Bag" (TaylorMade)

---

## SECTION 2: PRODUCT DESCRIPTION
**Priority: Brief, benefit-focused**
*Location: Below hero, above technology | 10 of 12 competitors have this*

**Contains 1 component:**

**Product Description Copy** — 2-4 sentence overview of the wedge's key benefits and positioning. Often includes target player type. Mizuno and Callaway have the most detailed descriptions.

---

## SECTION 3: TECHNOLOGY/FEATURES SECTION
**Priority: Your differentiation story**
*Location: Mid-page primary content | All 12 competitors have this section*

**Contains 4 components:**

**Technology Section Header** — Headline introducing the tech story. Examples: "Performance Technologies" (Vokey), "Tour-Proven Technology" (Cleveland), "Engineered For Spin" (TaylorMade)

**Technology Feature Blocks** — Individual cards/sections for each technology. Typically 3-6 blocks per page. Each contains: Name, Icon/Image, 2-3 sentence explanation. Examples: "Spin Milled Grooves," "ZipCore," "Snakebite Grooves"

**Technical Diagrams/Cutaways** — Visual explainers showing internal construction or face technology. Vokey shows groove geometry; PXG shows forging process; Cleveland shows face milling patterns.

**Materials/Construction Callouts** — Steel type, forging method, finish options. Examples: "8620 Carbon Steel" (Vokey), "S20C Forged" (Haywood), "3X Forged" (PXG)

---

## SECTION 4: GRIND GUIDE/SELECTION SECTION
**Priority: Critical for wedge conversion**
*Location: Mid-page, after technology | 10 of 12 competitors have this*

**Contains 3 components:**

**Grind Comparison Grid** — Visual comparison of all available grinds. Shows sole shape, bounce angles, intended use. Vokey's is the most comprehensive (6 grinds); Callaway Opus shows 5 grinds clearly.

**Player Type Matching** — "Which grind is right for you?" guidance. Connects swing type, course conditions, and skill level to grind recommendations. TaylorMade and Ping do this well.

**Shot Type Guidance** — Shows which grind excels at which shots (bunker, flop, pitch, chip). Often includes turf interaction visuals. Cleveland and Cobra include this.

---

## SECTION 5: SPECIFICATIONS SECTION
**Priority: For the detail-oriented buyer**
*Location: Mid-to-lower page | All 12 competitors have this*

**Contains 2 components:**

**Specifications Table** — Full loft/lie/bounce matrix. Includes: Loft options (typically 46°-62°), Bounce options per loft, Lie angle, Length, Swing weight. Usually in expandable/tabbed format.

**Available Options Summary** — Quick-reference of all configurations. Shaft options, grip options, custom stamping availability. Vokey and Cleveland offer the most customization.

---

## SECTION 6: SOCIAL PROOF SECTION
**Priority: Build trust**
*Location: Lower-mid page | 11 of 12 competitors have elements here*

**Contains 4 components:**

**Customer Reviews Module** — Star ratings with written reviews. Filterable by rating/verified purchase. Sub70 features video reviews; Haywood shows photo reviews.

**Tour Player Usage/Endorsements** — "Trusted by [X] Tour Players" or specific player callouts. Vokey leads with "Most played wedge on Tour"; Cleveland mentions tour wins; PXG lists specific pros.

**Awards/Recognition Badges** — Golf Digest Hot List, MyGolfSpy awards, Editor's Choice. Cleveland and Callaway display these prominently.

**Trust Badges** — Secure checkout icons, payment method logos, "Authentic Product" guarantees. More prominent on DTC sites (Sub70, Haywood, Takomo).

---

## SECTION 7: SUPPORT/INFO SECTION
**Priority: Reduce purchase friction**
*Location: Lower page | 9 of 12 competitors have this*

**Contains 4 components:**

**FAQ Accordion** — Common questions about the product. Topics: Fitting, grinds, customization, care. Vokey has the most comprehensive FAQ; DTC brands often skip this.

**Shipping/Delivery Information** — Expected delivery timeline, shipping costs, international availability. DTC brands highlight "Ships in 1-2 days"; OEMs often link to separate page.

**Return Policy/Guarantee** — Trial period, satisfaction guarantee, return window. Sub70 offers 60-day playability guarantee; Haywood offers 60-day returns; PXG offers 30-day.

**Warranty Information** — Defect coverage, wear policy, claim process. Usually linked rather than displayed in full. Most OEMs offer 2-year; varies by brand.

---

## SECTION 8: CONVERSION/UPSELL SECTION
**Priority: Maximize AOV**
*Location: Bottom of page | 8 of 12 competitors have elements here*

**Contains 3 components:**

**Secondary/Sticky CTA** — Persistent add-to-cart that follows scroll or appears at bottom. PXG and TaylorMade use sticky headers; Sub70 uses bottom bar.

**Complementary Product Recommendations** — "Complete Your Bag" or "Pairs Well With" section. Shows matching irons, other wedge lofts, accessories. Cleveland and Callaway do this well.

**Bundle/Set Offers** — Discounted multi-wedge packages. "Buy 2, Save 10%" or "3-Wedge Set" pricing. More common on DTC sites (Sub70 offers 3-wedge bundle).

---

## SECTION 9: COMPARISON SECTION
**Priority: Optional — If you have multiple models**
*Location: Variable - some mid-page, some bottom | 5 of 12 competitors have this*

**Contains 1 component:**

**Product Comparison Table** — Side-by-side comparison with other models in the line or competitor products. Shows specs, features, price differences. Sub70 compares their 4 wedge models; some OEMs compare current vs previous gen.

---

## SECTION 10: URGENCY/SCARCITY ELEMENTS
**Priority: Optional — Use sparingly for credibility**
*Location: Variable - often in hero or near CTAs | 3 of 12 competitors use this*

**Contains 1 component:**

**Scarcity/Urgency Indicators** — Low stock warnings, limited edition callouts, "selling fast" messaging. Takomo shows "Only [X] left in stock"; DTC brands use this more than OEMs.
