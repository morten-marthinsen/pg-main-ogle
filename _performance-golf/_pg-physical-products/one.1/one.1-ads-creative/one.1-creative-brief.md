# ONE.1/ONE.S Creative Brief

Launch Target: 2/16/2026  
Category: Physical product — Wedges / Short Game Equipment

**Background**  
Most golfers struggle around the green because **traditional wedges punish imperfect lies** (thick rough, uneven turf, bunkers, tight fairways, and tight lies around the green). The result is a “confidence crisis”: tension, deceleration, chunks/blades, and lost strokes. ONE.1 is designed to make short game contact more forgiving and predictable by solving the club-to-ground problem instead of asking golfers for perfect technique. ￼

**Goal**  
Drive new customer acquisition in wedges at a $150–$250 price point while establishing PG as the brand that solves the \#1 short game frustration: inconsistent contact from real-world lies.  ￼  
   
**Audience**

* Primary: Men & women golfers, 40–70, 12–28 handicap.  
* Mindset: “I can’t trust my wedge unless it’s a perfect lie. I’m thinking ‘don’t chunk it’ before I swing.” They’re skeptical of gimmicks but desperate for credible proof.  ￼


**Key Takeaway**  
The ONE.1 Promise: one simple swing that works from fairway, rough, sand, and tight lies.  ￼

**Tension**

* “This is technique, not equipment.”  
* “Easy to use \= for beginners / not legit.”  
* “Does it sacrifice spin/control?”  
* “Will I need to learn a new technique?”  ￼

**Reason(s) To Believe**   
The ONE.1  includes five key features:

1. Auto Glide Sole: Four-way cambered shape that increases bounce surface area, reduces digging, and maintains club momentum through turf and sand  
2. Pitch Control Weighting: Heavy sole wing \+ high toe weight placement that stabilizes off-center contact and preserves ball speed across the face  
3. Square and Shoot Alignment: Straight leading edge \+ center-face alignment marks that make squaring the club simple at address  
4. Spin-Friction Face: Traditional grooves for full shots combined with laser-etched surface texture for friction on partial swings  
5. Motion Swing Weighted: Stepless steel shaft with 40g counterweight that improves balance and reduces flipping/deceleration ￼

Core technology features to translate into creative:

* Auto Glide Sole: four-way cambered sole to resist digging \+ maintain momentum  
* Pitch Control Weighting: stabilizes off-center contact \+ preserves ball speed  
* Square & Shoot Alignment: straight leading edge \+ alignment marks  
* Spin-Friction Face: grooves \+ laser-etched texture for partials  
* Motion Swing Weighted: stepless steel shaft \+ 40g counterweight to reduce flipping/deceleration

**Messaging Territories (build a mix across the funnel)**  
We want to be diverse here so that we can hit all parts of the funnel:

1. Problem / Reframe: “The hidden reason you chunk wedge shots (it’s not your swing).”  
2. Mechanism: “Auto Glide Sole: designed to glide, not dig.”  
3. Confidence / Emotion: “Swing freely—without fear of chunking.”  
4. Any Lie / One Swing: “Same swing from fairway, rough, sand, tight lies.”  
5. Legitimacy: “Forgiveness tech without feeling like a ‘beginner club.’”  
6. Performance: “Predictable spin \+ distance control across the face.”  ￼
