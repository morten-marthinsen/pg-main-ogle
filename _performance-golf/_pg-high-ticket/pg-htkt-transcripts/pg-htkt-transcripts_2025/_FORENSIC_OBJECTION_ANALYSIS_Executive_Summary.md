## 🎯 CRITICAL INSIGHT FOR OPTIMIZATION

**Prospects cite LOGISTICS more than 3X as often as PRICE.**

The primary barrier to enrollment is NOT affordability—it's the **perceived difficulty of fitting this into their life** (schedule, travel, spouse coordination, work conflicts).

### **Optimization Priorities (Ranked by Impact):**

1. **Lead with scheduling flexibility and travel convenience** - Address the #1 objection immediately in sales scripts and VSLs
2. **Address spouse/partner early** - Position as a couples experience or vacation opportunity
3. **Frame value proposition around time efficiency** - Emphasize 12-month follow-up, not just the 3-day event
4. **Emphasize risk-reversal** - 100% refundable deposits, guarantees, and no-commitment holding periods
5. **Differentiate from prior golf school failures** - Show what makes this different from past experiences
6. **Reduce commitment friction** - Flexible payment terms matter, but less than flexible scheduling
7. **Create urgency without pressure tactics** - Limited spots messaging backfires when prospects feel rushed
8. **Showcase follow-up support as unique differentiator** - The 12-month coaching is a major value driver
