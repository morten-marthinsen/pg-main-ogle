> *"I'm just not gonna accept a $5,500 package right now. I love the game. I respect the game. I wanna get better at it."* — Frank (transcript_2b589151)

## KEY INSIGHTS FOR SALES OPTIMIZATION

1. **Price anchoring is critical** — Prospects anchor to local lesson prices ($100-250) and are shocked by $5,000+

2. **Spouse approval is the #2 gatekeeper** — Nearly every married male prospect says "I need to check with my wife"

3. **Payment plans help** — Reps frequently offer $2,250 split payments or "$1,000 deposit" to reduce sticker shock

4. **The "think about it" objection** is often a proxy for price or spouse concerns

5. **Core demographic is 58-84 years old** — Many have physical limitations that could be positioned as reasons TO attend (specialized instruction for aging bodies)

6. **Frequency of play objection** can be reframed — Less frequent players arguably need more efficient learning, not less
