## **How Does the Quiz Fit Within the FY26 Strategic Focus?**

1. **The Quiz Is a Day 1+ LTV Lever, Not JUST an Acquisition Tactic**

*“How does this quiz fit into the broader growth plan?”*

The quiz is designed specifically to improve Day 1+ monetization by **creating voluntary engagement inside the first 60 days.** It does this by installing belief, setting a personalized path, and making PG1 and future purchases feel like a continuation — not a forced decision.

3. **The Quiz Is Built to Drive Buying Momentum the First 60 Days**

*“What problem is the Quiz solving right now?”*

The quiz is designed to front-load confidence, quick wins, and buying momentum because we know the first 60 days are where 70% of LTV is captured. **The quiz shortens time-to-value.**

4. **The Quiz Lets Prospects Experience PG1 Before They Opt In**

*“Why would someone voluntarily opt into PG1?”*

The quiz gives golfers a real taste of the PG1 experience before they ever see the offer. Instead of just talking about personalization like we’ve done in long VSLs in the past, we actually show it — their goals, their constraints, their practice rhythm, their first win, and their personalized path. So when PG1 is presented, it feels like a continuation of something they’re already experiencing, not a leap of faith.
