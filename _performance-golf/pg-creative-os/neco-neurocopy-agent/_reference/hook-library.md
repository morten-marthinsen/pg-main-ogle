## Curiosity Gap Hooks

### 1. The Hidden Mistake
**Formula**: The [specific] mistake [audience] makes that [negative outcome]
**Example**: "The 11pm mistake killing your sleep (it's not screens)"
**Use when**: Audience unaware of problem

### 2. The Secret Weapon
**Formula**: The [unexpected thing] [successful group] use to [desirable outcome]
**Example**: "The $3 product destroying $300 skincare routines"
**Use when**: Positioning against expensive competitors

### 3. The Revelation
**Formula**: [Time frame] ago, I discovered [unexpected thing]. [Implied transformation]
**Example**: "3 months ago, I discovered why my ads never worked. Now I can't make them fail."
**Use when**: Personal story + result

### 4. The Incomplete Method
**Formula**: The [number]-step [method] for [outcome] (most people skip #[number])
**Example**: "The 5-step cold email formula (most people skip #3)"
**Use when**: Educational content

### 5. The Forbidden Knowledge
**Formula**: What [authority figures] don't want you to know about [topic]
**Example**: "What financial advisors don't want you to know about index funds"
**Use when**: Positioning against established players

### 6. The Counter-Intuitive Truth
**Formula**: Why [common belief] is actually [opposite]
**Example**: "Why 'eating less' actually makes you gain weight"
**Use when**: Challenging conventional wisdom

### 7. The Silent Problem
**Formula**: The silent [problem] destroying your [important thing]
**Example**: "The silent leak draining your ad budget every day"
**Use when**: Hidden problem angle

### 8. The Unexpected Source
**Formula**: How [unlikely source] taught me [valuable skill]
**Example**: "How my 6-year-old taught me to close $50K deals"
**Use when**: Story-based content

## Identity/Tribal Hooks

### 9. The Identity Call-Out
**Formula**: Why [specific identity] are [unexpected behavior]
**Example**: "Why smart investors are pulling out of stocks right now"
**Use when**: Speaking to aspirational identity

### 10. The In-Group Signal
**Formula**: [Specific group] know this. Everyone else is still [mistake]
**Example**: "7-figure agency owners know this. Everyone else is still chasing retainers."
**Use when**: Creating exclusivity

### 11. The Tribal Split
**Formula**: There are two types of [people]: [type A] and [type B]. Which are you?
**Example**: "There are two types of marketers: those who test, and those who guess. Which are you?"
**Use when**: Forcing self-identification

### 12. The Aspirational Identity
**Formula**: If you're the kind of person who [behavior], you need to see this
**Example**: "If you're the kind of person who refuses to settle for average, you need to see this"
**Use when**: Calling out high-achievers

### 13. The Rejection Frame
**Formula**: This isn't for [out-group]. It's for [in-group].
**Example**: "This isn't for hobbyists. It's for people building real businesses."
**Use when**: Qualification/positioning

### 14. The Shared Enemy
**Formula**: [Frustration they share] ends today
**Example**: "Unpredictable income ends today"
**Use when**: Problem-aware audience

### 15. The Belonging Promise
**Formula**: Join [number] [identity markers] who [desirable action]
**Example**: "Join 47,000+ marketers who get our best insights every Monday"
**Use when**: Community/newsletter

## Pattern Interrupt Hooks

### 16. The Contradiction
**Formula**: I [action that seems negative]. [Unexpected positive outcome]
**Example**: "I fired my best employee. Revenue doubled."
**Use when**: Maximum attention grab

### 17. The Confession
**Formula**: I have a confession: [admission]. And it [unexpected result]
**Example**: "I have a confession: I stopped posting for 6 months. My business grew 40%."
**Use when**: Authenticity + surprise

### 18. The Impossible Claim
**Formula**: [Extreme outcome] in [short timeframe]. No [expected requirement].
**Example**: "6 figures in 90 days. No ads. No team. No audience."
**Use when**: Transformation stories (must have proof)

### 19. The Reversal
**Formula**: Everything you know about [topic] is wrong
**Example**: "Everything you know about SEO is wrong (and Google wants it that way)"
**Use when**: Disruptive positioning

### 20. The Stop Command
**Formula**: Stop [common behavior]. Here's why.
**Example**: "Stop optimizing for conversions. Here's why."
**Use when**: Challenging best practices

### 21. The Admission
**Formula**: [Credential/success] but I still [relatable struggle]
**Example**: "Built a $2M agency but I still hate sales calls"
**Use when**: Humanizing authority

## Authority Hooks

### 22. The Expert Reveal
**Formula**: [Specific credential] reveals [insight]
**Example**: "Ex-Facebook ads buyer reveals the metrics that actually matter"
**Use when**: Insider knowledge angle

### 23. The Data Point
**Formula**: After [specific experience], here's what [insight]
**Example**: "After 847 sales calls, here's what actually closes deals"
**Use when**: Experience-based authority

### 24. The Borrowed Authority
**Formula**: The [method/tool] used by [impressive examples]
**Example**: "The negotiation framework used by FBI hostage negotiators"
**Use when**: Association with respected source

### 25. The Case Study Lead
**Formula**: How [specific example] achieved [specific result] in [timeframe]
**Example**: "How a burned-out freelancer hit $30K/mo in 4 months"
**Use when**: Proof-forward content

### 26. The Research Reveal
**Formula**: [Institution] study reveals [surprising finding]
**Example**: "Stanford study reveals why most productivity advice backfires"
**Use when**: Science-backed content

### 27. The Behind-the-Scenes
**Formula**: Inside the [exclusive thing] of [impressive entity]
**Example**: "Inside the email sequence that generated $1.2M in 5 days"
**Use when**: Transparency/access angle

## Threat/Loss Hooks

### 28. The Warning
**Formula**: Warning: [behavior] is [negative consequence]
**Example**: "Warning: Your 'brand awareness' campaign is burning money"
**Use when**: Problem-aware audience

### 29. The Cost of Waiting
**Formula**: Every [time period] you wait costs you [specific amount]
**Example**: "Every month you wait costs you $3,400 in lost revenue"
**Use when**: Urgency angle

### 30. The Mistake Reveal
**Formula**: The #1 mistake [audience] make (and how to fix it)
**Example**: "The #1 mistake new course creators make (and how to fix it)"
**Use when**: Educational content

### 31. The Hidden Danger
**Formula**: [Common practice] is secretly [negative outcome]
**Example**: "Your 'engagement posts' are secretly hurting your reach"
**Use when**: Awareness content

### 32. The Countdown
**Formula**: [Timeframe] left before [negative consequence]
**Example**: "72 hours left before this pricing disappears forever"
**Use when**: Deadline urgency

### 33. The Comparison Loss
**Formula**: While you [current behavior], [competitor] is [better approach]
**Example**: "While you're writing blog posts, your competitors are building email lists"
**Use when**: Competitive angle

## Story Hooks

### 34. The Origin Moment
**Formula**: The moment I realized [insight that changed everything]
**Example**: "The moment I realized my 'dream client' was destroying my business"
**Use when**: Personal story content

### 35. The Unlikely Beginning
**Formula**: [Low point/unlikely starting place]. [Impressive result].
**Example**: "$37 in my account. 2 years later: 7-figure exit."
**Use when**: Transformation story

### 36. The Conversation Hook
**Formula**: "[Quote from conversation that creates intrigue]"
**Example**: "'You're leaving money on the table,' he said. I had no idea how right he was."
**Use when**: Dialogue-based story

### 37. The Unexpected Teacher
**Formula**: [Unexpected person/source] taught me [valuable lesson]
**Example**: "A Uber driver taught me more about marketing than my MBA"
**Use when**: Counterintuitive insight

### 38. The Before/After
**Formula**: [Time period] ago: [pain state]. Today: [desired state].
**Example**: "12 months ago: burned out, broke, and begging for clients. Today: booked 3 months out with a waitlist."
**Use when**: Transformation proof

### 39. The Failure Opening
**Formula**: I failed [number] times before [success]
**Example**: "I failed 23 product launches before the one that made millions"
**Use when**: Authenticity + persistence

## Contrarian Hooks

### 40. The Unpopular Opinion
**Formula**: Unpopular opinion: [contrarian take]
**Example**: "Unpopular opinion: Personal branding is overrated"
**Use when**: Thought leadership

### 41. The "Actually" Reversal
**Formula**: [Common advice] is actually [opposite truth]
**Example**: "More content isn't the answer. Less (strategic) content is."
**Use when**: Challenging best practices

### 42. The Expert Disagreement
**Formula**: [Impressive credential] but I disagree with [common belief]
**Example**: "Harvard MBA but I think business school is a waste for entrepreneurs"
**Use when**: Credentialed contrarian take

### 43. The Counterintuitive Result
**Formula**: I [did opposite of expected] and [positive result]
**Example**: "I raised my prices 3X and got more clients"
**Use when**: Business insight

## Specificity Hooks

### 44. The Exact Number
**Formula**: How I [achieved specific result] in [specific timeframe]
**Example**: "How I added 2,847 email subscribers in 14 days without ads"
**Use when**: Proof-based content

### 45. The Specific Method
**Formula**: The [specific name] method for [specific outcome]
**Example**: "The 'Reverse Launch' method for filling courses without a list"
**Use when**: Named framework

### 46. The Breakdown
**Formula**: [Result]: Here's exactly how
**Example**: "$147,000 from one email: Here's exactly how"
**Use when**: Detailed case study

### 47. The Step-by-Step
**Formula**: [Number] steps to [outcome] (steal this)
**Example**: "7 steps to $10K/mo freelancing (steal this)"
**Use when**: Actionable content

### 48. The Template Offer
**Formula**: The exact [asset] I used to [achieve result]
**Example**: "The exact cold email I used to land a $50K contract"
**Use when**: Swipe file content

### 49. The Time-Boxed Challenge
**Formula**: [Outcome] in [short timeframe]: a step-by-step breakdown
**Example**: "First client in 7 days: a step-by-step breakdown"
**Use when**: Challenge/tutorial

### 50. The Metric Reveal
**Formula**: The [metric] that [impressive result] (and how to get it)
**Example**: "The open rate that generated $127K (and how to get it)"
**Use when**: Data-driven content

## Hook Selection Guide

| Audience Temperature | Best Hook Types |
|---------------------|-----------------|
| Cold (unaware) | Curiosity Gap, Pattern Interrupt, Story |
| Problem-aware | Threat/Loss, Contrarian, Identity |
| Solution-aware | Authority, Specificity, Case Study |
| Ready to buy | Specificity, Urgency, Social Proof |

| Content Format | Best Hook Types |
|---------------|-----------------|
| Short-form video (TikTok/Reels) | Pattern Interrupt, Contradiction |
| Long-form video | Story, Authority, Curiosity Gap |
| Email subject line | Curiosity Gap, Specificity |
| Ad primary text | Identity, Threat/Loss, Curiosity |
| Sales page headline | Specificity, Identity, Authority |
