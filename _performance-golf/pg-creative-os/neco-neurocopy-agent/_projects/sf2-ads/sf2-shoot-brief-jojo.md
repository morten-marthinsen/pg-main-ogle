## Shoot 3: Friday Testimonial Shoot (3 golfers)

**What**: Authentic first reactions from 3 golfers trying SF2 for the first time.

**Direction:**
- Man-on-the-street style — hand them the club, capture natural reactions
- **NO scripted lines. Zero.**
- Close-ups of facial reactions on hit (the "face lights up" moment)
- Wide shot: full swing + ball flight
- Before/after: their regular driver vs. SF2 (same golfer, same session)

**What NOT to do:**
- No fake testimonials
- No "say this" direction
- No coaching them on what to say
