## General Instructions

This is the ONLY brand-first script in the SF2 campaign — sf2-0001 and sf2-0002 are DR-forward. The goal is to give SF2 a premium brand presence. Body tone = TV commercial (philosophy leads, product enters late). Hook tone = mixed branded and DR per Andromeda testing — some hooks match the commercial feel, others use DR pattern interrupts that pivot into the brand body. No AIVO or visual-only hooks — all 5 are Christopher VO.
