## Key Insight

This ad is a **branded origin story** — NOT a typical DR ad. It reads like a 90-second TV commercial. The narrative follows PG's engineering philosophy through three values (Balance, Synergy, Control), reveals the F1-inspired technology, introduces Chris McGinley as the craftsman, and lands the "first-ever" claim before pivoting to a proof/CTA close. Product doesn't appear until beat 7 of 10. The first 6 beats sell the company's philosophy. Proof is atmospheric (visuals, reactions, data tease) rather than explicit stats.
