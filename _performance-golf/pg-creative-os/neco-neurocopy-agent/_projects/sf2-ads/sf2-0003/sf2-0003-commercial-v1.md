## General Instructions

This is the ONLY brand-first script in the SF2 campaign — sf2-0001 and sf2-0002 are DR-forward. The goal is to give SF2 a premium brand presence. Tone should feel like a TV commercial: claims are atmospheric, not statistical. Proof is visual, not explicit. Philosophy leads, product enters late. No hook variations — single linear script, one VO track (Christopher).
