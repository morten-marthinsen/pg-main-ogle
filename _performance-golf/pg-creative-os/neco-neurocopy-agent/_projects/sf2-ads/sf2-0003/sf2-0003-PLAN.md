## Approach

Beat-for-beat adaptation of the SF1-0003 branded commercial. This is the ONLY brand-first script in the SF2 campaign — sf2-0001 and sf2-0002 are DR-forward. The goal is to give SF2 a premium brand presence.

**Tone rule:** This script should feel like a TV commercial, not a direct response ad. Claims are atmospheric, not statistical. Proof is visual, not explicit. The product enters late. Philosophy leads.

**Key SF2 elements to weave in (subtly):**
- 3-piece aerospace-grade forged titanium (replaces carbon fiber / F1 racing)
- SR-71 Blackbird as borrowed-authority metaphor (replaces F1 racing cars)
- "Straight AND long" — distance + accuracy (SF2's core differentiator vs SF1)
- SliceFix SpeedTech (evolved from Square Face Technology)
- Six slice-correcting features (down from seven)
- Evolution framing (sequel, not from-scratch)

**What NOT to change:**
- Three-value philosophy (Balance / Synergy / Control)
- Beat sequence and emotional arc
- Chris McGinley as craftsman
- Identity close ("relaxed and confident from the tee box")
- Cinematic production direction style
- Minimal claim density

**What to keep OUT (belongs to other scripts):**
- 60,000+ golfers stat (sf2-0002's territory)
- 96% slice stat (sf2-0001's territory)
- "Most popular" framing (sf2-0002's angle)
- Heavy testimonial sections
- Gifting/shortcut angle
