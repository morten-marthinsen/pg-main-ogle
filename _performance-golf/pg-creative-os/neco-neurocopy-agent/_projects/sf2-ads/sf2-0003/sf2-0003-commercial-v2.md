## General Instructions

This is the ONLY brand-first script in the SF2 campaign — sf2-0001 and sf2-0002 are DR-forward. The goal is to give SF2 a premium brand presence. Tone should feel like a TV commercial: claims are atmospheric, not statistical. Proof is visual, not explicit. Philosophy leads, product enters late. No hook variations — single linear script, one VO track (Christopher).

## v1 → v2 Change Log

| Beat | What Changed | Why |
|------|-------------|-----|
| 1+2 → 1 | Merged. Reverted to SF1 opening structure: "When PG sat down to redesign..." → subversion → promise. | v1 drifted too far from proven SF1 pattern. |
| 3 (Philosophy) | Dropped Synergy. Restructured to Balance / Control / Distance. Control reverted to SF1 steering wheel line. Distance is new. | SF2's core differentiator is straight + long. Distance earns its own value beat. |
| 4 (Craftsman) | Reframed McGinley. "Built the SF1... set out to surpass it" replaces "brought in craftsmen." Lower third: RENOWNED CLUB ENGINEER. | McGinley led SF1 development — he wasn't brought in. He's head of product innovation. |
| 6 (Product Hero) | "First anti-slice driver that gives you the same distance as big brand drivers." Reverted to SF1's "We've produced — through endless experimentation" structure. | Christopher confirmed distance parity claim is verifiable. SF1 language was stronger. |
| 9 (Close) | "Relaxed, confident, and dominant off the tee box." | Added power language per Christopher's direction. |
