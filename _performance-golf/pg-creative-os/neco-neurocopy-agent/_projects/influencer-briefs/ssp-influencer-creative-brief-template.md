## **Target Audience Profile**

**Who We're Speaking To**: Male recreational golfers, ages 45-65

### **Psychographics**

* Played golf for 10-30+ years \- it's part of their identity  
* Noticing decline in performance year over year  
* Pride prevents them from admitting they're "getting old"  
* Have tried lessons, equipment upgrades, stretching \- nothing worked  
* Have disposable income for premium solutions  
* Want to keep up with (or beat) their buddies

### **Physical Experience (USE THIS LANGUAGE)**

* Joints that "pop, crack, or grind" during the swing  
* Shorter backswing than 5-10 years ago  
* Lower back ache after 18 holes  
* Stiffness that takes longer to warm up  
* "Death grip" on the club to compensate for lost rotation  
* That frustrated walk back to the cart after a bad shot

### **Emotional Experience (TAP INTO THESE)**

* **Shame** of being outplayed by buddies who practice less  
* **Fear** of golf "slipping away" with age  
* **Frustration** that their body won't do what their mind knows  
* **Nostalgia** for the swing they had in their prime  
* **Hope** that it's fixable if they find the right solution

[10-25 seconds] PRODUCT BRIDGE - Name Swing Smooth Pro, explain WHY it works

## **Do's and Don'ts**

### **DO:**

* Use your natural speaking voice  
* Share personal experience when possible  
* Hold the product naturally  
* Let pauses happen (they create authenticity)  
* Film in genuine settings  
* Include the compliance disclaimer  
* Transition smoothly from hook to product

### **DON'T:**

* Read from a script word-for-word  
* Oversell or make outrageous claims  
* Film in a "studio" setting that feels fake  
* Rush through the message  
* Make medical claims ("cures arthritis" etc.)  
* Drop the product name without context
