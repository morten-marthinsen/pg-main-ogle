# PGB VSL Shortening Plan — Christopher's Version

## Context

**Problem:** The PGB (Performance Golf Beginners) VSL script is a 25-35 minute full-length script written by Tony Flores, featuring Martin Chuck as presenter. We need a shortened 12-18 minute version for a younger audience (25-40), presented by Christopher (35-year-old golfer, student of Martin's who flew out for a real lesson — footage exists).

**Why:** Younger audience has shorter attention tolerance. A peer presenter (Christopher) creates stronger identification than an authority figure (Martin). The "student who discovered it" frame is more powerful for this demographic than "expert who invented it."

**Constraints:**
- Tony's copy stays VERBATIM wherever it's kept — no rewriting his lines
- Transition lines are the ONLY new copy, used to bridge cuts
- Martin's first-person credibility lines (cannot be delivered by Christopher) get reframed to third-person narration or replaced with Christopher's personal testimony
- **Offer structure changes**: One-time payment for the One Swing System as standalone product (modeled after PGF's "Off-Season Swing Saver"). NO mention of PG1 in the VSL. PG1 is a checkout page upsell only.
- Guarantee: TBD (current script says 30-day; PGF model uses 365-day)
- Must maintain structural integrity per TonyFlo CopywritingEngine and Neco Golf Suggestibility Principle
