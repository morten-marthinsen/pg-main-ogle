## Features

*6 Slice-Fix features built on 3-Piece Forged Titanium construction*

### 1. Speed Geometry Toe Slot
- **CLAIM:** Squares the face faster by reducing drag on the toe
- **PROOF:** Aerodynamic slot carved into the toe reduces drag as the club moves through impact
- **BENEFITS:**
- **Functional:** You'll hit straighter drives without manipulating your swing. No timing the face. No "holding it off." Just swing naturally and watch the ball fly down the middle.
- **Dimensionalized:** Step up to the first tee knowing the club handles the hard part for you. Your playing partners will wonder what lesson you took.
- **Emotional:** You'll finally trust your driver — because for the first time, it's actually working WITH your swing instead of against it.

### 2. Counter-Slice Weighting
- **CLAIM:** Forces the toe to close faster than the heel through impact.
- **PROOF:** Because 3-Piece Fused Titanium is light and saves weight on the clubface, we could redistribute weight deep and in the heel, creating a Draw Biased CG location that dynamically closes the face without shrinking the sweet spot.
- **BENEFITS:**
- **Functional:** Even when your swing leaves the face open, the club closes it FOR you
- **Dimensionalized:** You hit the fairway even on your bad swings, setting up easier approach shots and more stress-free pars.
- **Emotional:** You'll feel the freedom to swing without bracing for disaster. "Zero f's given," as one golfer put it.

### 3. Aero Crown Channels // Directional Control Channels
- **CLAIM:** Guides airflow towards the heel so the face squares faster.
- **PROOF:** Completely re-engineered with deeper channels and more aggressive angles, optimized for the faster clubhead speed created by 3-piece forged titanium.
- **BENEFITS:**
- **Functional:** Squares the clubface for you even when you don't release the club early.
- **Dimensionalized:** Even your worst drives stay in play.
- **Emotional:** Instead of walking up to tight tee shots expecting to hit a bad shot, you have total confidence on ANY tee box.

### 4. Draw-Calibrated Face Angle
- **CLAIM:** Pre-sets the face 3° closed, but looks square behind the ball.
- **PROOF:** Titanium's precision manufacturing lets us engineer the face geometry so this closed face actually looks square when you're standing over it.
- **BENEFITS:**
- **Functional:** Every swing starts with a built-in margin for error — so when your swing isn't perfect, you've still got room to miss.
- **Dimensionalized:** You can stop worrying about having perfect technique, and swing free instead of thinking about your grip, face, path, or tempo.
- **Emotional:** You'll feel the relief of not needing a perfect swing every time. Finally, permission to be human out there — and still hit fairways.

### 5. Draw Spin Face Bulge
- **CLAIM:** Turn your mis-hits into draws with face closure built into every strike.
- **PROOF:** Asymmetric bulge design reduces slices because the heel side is flatter (less cut spin on heel hits) while the toe side is rounder (more draw spin on toe hits). 
- **BENEFITS:**
- **Functional:** Your mis-hits are now playable — instead of watching the ball curve into trouble, you'll see it working back toward the fairway.
- **Dimensionalized:** You'll stop cringing after contact. Even when you know you caught it a little off-center, you'll watch the ball do something it's never done before: stay in play.
- **Emotional:** You'll feel permission to swing freely — because even your bad ones end up okay.

- **PROOF:** Same 445cc as SF1, but sleeker, lower profile, more of a clamshell shape. Designed specifically around titanium's properties.

- **Functional:** You get more clubhead speed without swinging harder — the sleeker design cuts through the air for you.
