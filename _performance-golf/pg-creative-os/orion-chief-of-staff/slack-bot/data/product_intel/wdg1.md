# ONE.1 Wedge — Product Intel

**Category:** Auto-Correct Wedge
**Binary Frame:** Glides vs Digs
**Dominant Selling Idea:** The only wedge with an Auto-Glide Sole that glides through turf instead of digging into it.

## Positioning
84% of golfers use forgiving cavity-backed irons but then grab a punishing bladed wedge for their most important scoring shots. The ONE.1 looks like a normal premium wedge but has an invisible advantage: its Auto-Glide Sole manages ground contact FOR you — glides through turf instead of digging, even on steep, quick swings. No special technique needed.

## Key Features
1. **Auto-Glide Sole (Hero)** — 4-way cambered shape glides through turf instead of digging, consistent contact from any lie
2. **Distance Control Weighting** — Heavy sole wing + high toe weight stabilizes off-center hits, predictable distance on mishits
3. **Square and Shoot Alignment** — Straight leading edge + center-face marks, instant aim at address
4. **Spin-Fast Face** — Max conforming grooves + laser-etched texture, checks and stops on full swings AND chips
5. **Motion Swing Weighted** — Stepless shaft + 40g counterweight reduces flip/deceleration, solid contact feel
