# Click Stick — Product Intel

**Category:** Release + Speed Training Tool
**Binary Frame:** Feel vs Think
**Dominant Selling Idea:** One sound tells you everything — if it clicks at impact, you're releasing correctly and building speed.

## Positioning
Golfers are drowning in swing tips, data, and mechanics. The Click Stick condenses all of that into one sound: a magnetic click at the release point. No complex understanding required. Heavier than a standard club so it builds strength with every swing — when you switch back to your regular club, it feels like a feather. Instant feedback. Zero confusion.

## Key Features
1. **Magnetic Click Feedback** — Internal magnet slides and clicks at the correct release point. One sound = you're doing it right. No sensors, no app, no complexity.
2. **Weighted Training Effect** — Heavier than a standard club, builds strength and speed with every rep. Neural adaptation means your regular club feels lighter and faster after training.
3. **Feel-Based Learning** — No data screens or complex mechanics. Sound-based feedback works for ALL learning styles, especially feel-based learners.
4. **Power Transfer Training** — Teaches proper energy transfer through the swing. Common issue: arm swing without weight transfer. Click Stick teaches you to MOVE THROUGH the swing.

## Proof Points
- Weighted club builds strength AND speed simultaneously
- Magnetic feedback is binary: click = correct, no click = adjust
- No technology to charge, sync, or break
- Works for beginners through advanced players
- Especially effective for women and feel-based learners
- Slow-motion shows magnet sliding and clicking at impact point

## Key Audiences
- **Women golfers** — Feel-based, not mechanics-based. Respond to simplicity and power/confidence messaging. Avoid all technical jargon (no "load," "lag," "release" — use "transferring power/energy," "moving through your swing").
- **Beginners** — Overwhelmed by information. One feedback mechanism eliminates overload.
- **Speed seekers** — Weighted training effect builds distance through every rep.
