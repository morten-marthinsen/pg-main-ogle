# SpeedTrac — Product Intel

**Category:** Speed PLUS Accuracy Training System
**Binary Frame:** Fast AND Straight vs Fast OR Straight
**Dominant Selling Idea:** The ONLY swing trainer that gives you speed AND face control at the same time.

## Positioning
Most speed trainers are weighted sticks — round shaft-axis weight, no face, no feedback. You gain speed but amplify whatever your face is already doing: slicers get faster slices. SpeedTrac is the first tool with off-axis clubhead weight on a heel-to-toe track inside a real clubhead shape. Three positions: heel (slicers), toe (hookers), center (neutral). Train speed AND face control simultaneously.

## Key Features
1. **Y-Trac Weight System** — Off-axis weight in clubhead (not on shaft), adjustable 3-position track (heel/center/toe), creates face loading conditions specific to YOUR miss
2. **Speed Spoon Head** — Real mini-driver clubhead shape, gives visual face target, enables neuromotor transfer to actual golf
3. **SpeedTrac Stack** — Concentric stackable weights (light overspeed → heavy overload → driver weight), progressive training protocol
4. **Driver Look & Feel** — Visual priming: brain fires golf motor program when seeing clubhead, not a stick. Real club geometry = real transfer
