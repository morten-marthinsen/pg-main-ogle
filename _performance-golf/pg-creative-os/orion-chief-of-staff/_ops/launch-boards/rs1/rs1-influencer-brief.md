## 5 Key Themes to Call Out

These are the features and stories that matter most. You don't need to hit all 5 in every video — pick the ones that feel natural to you.

### 1. Forward Axis Weighting
75%+ of the head weight sits in front of the shaft. That's the opposite of every other putter on the market. It eliminates "face drift" — that tendency for the putter face to drift open during your stroke. With the RS1, gravity holds the face square for you.

### 2. "Pull It Back, Let It Fall"
This is how the RS1 works. You pull it back, and gravity pulls it through. The putter wants to stay square through the entire stroke. It's not something you have to think about or practice — the physics do the work. Show this on camera: take the putter back and let gravity take over.

### 3. Face-Down Balance (The Visual Proof)
This is the best demo for video. Place the RS1 face-down on any flat surface — a table, the floor, the putting green. It stays face-down. Now try it with any other putter — the face flops open. That's face drift in action. The RS1 is the only putter where gravity holds the face square. This is the single most shareable moment you can capture.

### 4. Chris McGinley's Story
The RS1 was designed by Chris McGinley — over 30 years of club design experience, with designs in the hands of 11 world #1 players. He's worked inside the engineering departments that built the putters your favorite Tour pros rely on. The RS1 is the putter he's always wanted to build. This isn't a golf brand adding a putter to the lineup — it's a world-class putter designer building the putter he believes in.

### 5. "Science of the Stroke"
Most putters only give you better equipment. The RS1 actually helps your putting technique. The forward weighting creates a natural pendulum motion — less hand manipulation, less grip pressure, less overthinking. You'll feel it immediately: your stroke gets quieter, simpler, more consistent. The tagline says it all: "Do less. Make more."
