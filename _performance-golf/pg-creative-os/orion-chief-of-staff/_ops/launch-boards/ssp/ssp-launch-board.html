The Root Problem: Stuck Joints

“Stuck joints” is a systemic breakdown caused by MMP protein enzymes that build up starting around age 45. Golf accelerates it — swinging a golf club causes nearly 3x more joint impact than running.

  • Gnaw through cartilage — both damaged and healthy tissue
  • Drain synovial fluid — chew holes that let natural lubricant leak out
  • Stiffen connective tissue — turn loose, flexible tissue into dried-out, ragged elastic
  • Trigger muscle guarding — neurological reflex tells your body “DANGER — STOP MOVING”

Result: Every year you lose 2–3 degrees of rotation. Backswing gets shorter. Contact suffers. Consistency deteriorates.
