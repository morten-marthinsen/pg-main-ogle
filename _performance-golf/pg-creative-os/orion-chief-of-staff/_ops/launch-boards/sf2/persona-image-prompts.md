# SF2 Launch Board — Persona Image Generation Prompts

> **Purpose**: AI image generation prompts for the 5 SF2 launch personas. Paste directly into ChatGPT, DALL-E, or Midjourney.
> **Destination**: Section 4 of the Figma launch board.
> **Aspect ratio**: 16:9 (landscape, fits Figma card layout)

## Global Style Direction

Realistic photography style. Natural lighting. American male golfer, 45-65 years old. Candid moment, not posed. No text, no logos, no golf brand visible. Warm, slightly desaturated color palette. Shot at a public/municipal course (not a country club). 16:9 aspect ratio.

---

## P1 — The Chronic Slicer

> A realistic photo of a frustrated male golfer in his mid-50s standing on a tee box, watching his ball curve sharply right into the trees. He's wearing a casual polo and baseball cap. His body language shows resignation — shoulders slightly slumped, club hanging loosely at his side. Public municipal course, morning light. The expression isn't anger — it's the tired look of someone who's seen this happen a thousand times.

---

## P2 — The Equipment Skeptic

> A realistic photo of a skeptical male golfer in his late 50s standing in a golf pro shop, holding a brand new modern driver in both hands and examining the clubhead with a doubtful, unimpressed expression. He is turning the driver over, inspecting the face closely, clearly unconvinced by the marketing claims. He wears a casual polo shirt, no hat. The driver is clearly recognizable as a golf driver with a large titanium clubhead. His body language and facial expression say prove it to me. Well-lit retail golf store environment with other clubs visible on racks in the background. Realistic photography, natural lighting, 16:9 aspect ratio, warm slightly desaturated color palette.

---

## P3 — The Tech/Innovation Enthusiast

> A realistic photo of an engaged, curious male golfer in his late 40s at an outdoor driving range, standing upright and relaxed in a hitting bay, looking at a launch monitor screen with genuine excitement and fascination. His hands are empty, one hand gesturing slightly toward the data on the screen. A golf bag with clubs is visible leaning against the bay divider behind him. He stands tall with confident natural posture. He wears a clean modern polo shirt. Morning light, open sky visible behind the range. His expression is wonder and curiosity — the look of an early adopter who loves the technology side of golf. He has a slight grin, clearly impressed by the data on the screen. Realistic photography, natural lighting, warm slightly desaturated color palette, 16:9 aspect ratio.

---

## P4 — The Weekend Warrior

> A realistic photo of a relaxed, easygoing male golfer in his early 50s walking down a fairway with his bag, mid-conversation with an unseen playing partner. He's smiling — this is his one round this week and he's enjoying it. Casual outfit (maybe shorts, relaxed polo). Late morning light on a public course. The vibe is "I'm not trying to go pro, I just want to have fun out here."

---

## P5 — The Comeback Golfer

> A realistic photo of a male golfer in his early 60s pulling his clubs out of a garage or car trunk, as if heading to the course for the first time in a while. Slightly nostalgic expression — eager but a little uncertain. The clubs look like they haven't been used recently. Suburban driveway, morning light. The story in the image: a man returning to something he loved.
