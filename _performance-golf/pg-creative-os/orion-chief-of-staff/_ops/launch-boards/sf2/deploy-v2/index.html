Slice-Fix SpeedTech™ — 7 Features

1
Speed Geometry Toe Slot
Aerodynamic slot carved into the toe reduces drag, accelerates toe through impact, squares face automatically without timing the release.
NEW TO SF2 — titanium enables aggressive slot geometry
2
Fast Face Construction
3-piece forged titanium creates a thinner, faster face at max COR/CT (legal limit). Higher ball speed with the same swing speed. Straight shots also travel farther than slices — distance gains come two ways.
CORE ADVANTAGE — titanium allows thinner face without structural compromise
3
Counter-Slice Weighting
Weight saved from the thin face is redistributed deep in the heel — draw biased CG that dynamically closes the face. Hits fairways even on bad swings.
UPGRADED — deeper/heavier than SF1 keel from titanium weight savings
4
AeroSquare Crown Channels
Deeper directional-control channels with more aggressive angles than SF1, optimized for faster clubhead speed. Airflow itself helps close the face.
UPGRADED — deeper angles + re-engineered for titanium ball speed
5
Draw-Calibrated Face Angle
Pre-set 3° closed, but engineered to look square at address. No "hooked" appearance. Built-in margin for error on every swing.
RETAINED — titanium precision manufacturing holds tighter tolerances
6
Draw Spin Face Bulge
Asymmetric bulge: flatter heel (less cut spin on heel hits) + rounder toe (more draw spin on toe hits). Mis-hits fly straighter AND farther.
UPGRADED — 3-piece forged allows zone-specific face geometry
7
445cc Speed Geometry Head
Sleeker, lower-profile clamshell shape. Same 445cc as SF1, redesigned around titanium's properties for aerodynamic efficiency and more clubhead speed without swinging harder.
REDESIGNED — new shape optimized specifically for titanium
