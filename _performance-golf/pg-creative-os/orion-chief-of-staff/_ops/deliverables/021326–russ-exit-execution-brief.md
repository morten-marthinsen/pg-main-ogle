## 8. Morton Evaluation Trigger

John deferred the Morton conversation to "after Russ exits." This means the evaluation conversation should happen **week of Feb 17** (first full week post-exit).

### Why This Matters

From the John sync analysis: "How you handle Morton = how John evaluates whether you can build the org through the AI lens." This is a live demonstration of Christopher's VP-level talent evaluation capability.

### Evaluation Framework (3 Dimensions)

**Dimension 1: Teach/Train Ability**
- Can Morton elevate junior editors, not just do the work himself?
- Evidence: Has he trained anyone? Can he articulate his editing methodology?
- Test: Ask Morton to walk through how he'd train a new editor in his workflow

**Dimension 2: AI Adoption Leadership**
- Is Morton willing to integrate AI tools (VEDA, AI-assisted editing) into his workflow?
- Evidence: Has he experimented with any AI tools? What's his attitude?
- Test: Show Morton VEDA's capabilities (hook stacks, cutdowns). Gauge reaction — curiosity vs. resistance
- VEDA-specific examples to demo: hook stack generation, scroll stopper creation, automated cutdowns

**Dimension 3: Leadership at Scale**
- Can Morton operate as a lead, not just a senior IC?
- Evidence: Does he think about team output, not just personal output?
- Test: "If you had two junior editors, how would you structure their work?"

### Conversation Structure (30-45 min)

1. **Open:** "With Russ's departure, the team structure is evolving. I want to talk about your role and where I see it going." (Positive framing — opportunity, not interrogation)
2. **Explore:** Walk through the three dimensions naturally. Let Morton talk. Listen for signals.
3. **Challenge:** Share VEDA demo. Watch his reaction. This is the most diagnostic moment.
4. **Close:** "Here's what I need to see over the next 30 days..." (Specific, measurable expectations)

### Decision Matrix

| Signal | Interpretation | Action |
|--------|---------------|--------|
| Curious about AI + willing to train others | Strong candidate for elevated role | Title/raise conversation at Day 30 |
| Curious about AI but prefers IC work | Valuable contributor, not a leader | Keep as senior IC, hire a lead above |
| Resistant to AI | Misaligned with creative OS direction | Have the harder conversation. May not be long-term fit. |
| Strong teach/train but resists AI | Partial fit — needs coaching | Give 30-day window with specific AI adoption milestones |

### Prep Before the Conversation
- [ ] Review Morton's recent work output (quality, turnaround, complexity)
- [ ] Prepare VEDA demo (2-3 concrete examples of AI-assisted editing)
- [ ] Draft specific 30-day expectations for each dimension
- [ ] Align with John on what outcome he wants from this conversation
