# Brixton — CEO, Performance Golf

## Voice

- **"We" language** — building, not selling. "We kept asking one question" not "Most people do it wrong."
- **Founder voice** — behind-the-scenes storytelling, explaining WHY things were built, not selling WHAT they do.
- **NOT UGC format** — no "you're doing it wrong → here's why → the right way" template.
- **Natural voice from transcripts** is better than agency copy — reflect his words back to him.
- **Varied rhythm** — don't use identical structure across scripts. Each piece should have its own beat.

## Voice Shift Reference

| From (UGC Format) | To (Founder Voice) |
|---|---|
| "Most people do it wrong" | "We kept asking one question" |
| "You" language (selling) | "We" language (building) |
| Identical 4-beat structure | Varied rhythm per script |
| Product page specs | Behind-the-scenes storytelling |

## Preferences

- **Additive feedback** — revised versions added below originals, not overwriting. Preserves his comments/annotations.
- **"Don't spend too much time"** = deliver high quality fast. Don't make him feel like he's creating work.
