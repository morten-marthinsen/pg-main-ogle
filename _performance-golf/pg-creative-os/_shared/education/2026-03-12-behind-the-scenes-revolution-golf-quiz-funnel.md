## Key Tactical Takeaways

1. **Big Idea = Diagnostic + Promise**: "Fix your #1 power leak → unlock 15-30 yards." The quiz positions itself as a personalized diagnostic, not a generic lead magnet.

2. **Progressive Personalization**: Each question builds on previous answers. After answering gender (man), age (under 30), and drive distance (200-250 yards), the next screen says: "Men who are under 30 who drive the ball between 200 and 250 yards typically suffer from one of the same three power leaks." The quiz literally recites the user's inputs back to them.

3. **Reason-Why Framing on Sensitive Questions**: "Are you a man or a woman?" is justified with "men and women tend to suffer from different power leaks." Every potentially intrusive question has a reason-why tied to the diagnostic.

4. **Branch Logic Mirrors User Language**: When the user selects "distance" as their biggest challenge, the next question uses the word "distance" — not a synonym. This creates the feeling of a real conversation.

5. **Diagnosis → Named Result**: The output is "Power Leak Number One" — a named, numbered diagnosis that implies a system and authority. Not "you might have a problem with X."

6. **Specific Quantified Promise at Diagnosis**: "Typically costs men on average 15 to 30 yards in every tee shot" — concrete, measurable, and repeated from the initial hook.

7. **Video Fix Delivered via Email**: The opt-in mechanism is "we'll deliver your fix video to your email so you can watch it at your leisure." This is a value-first email capture, not a hard gate.

8. **$17 Front-End Offer on Thank-You Page**: Immediately after opt-in, the prospect lands on a sales page with a $17 offer — low-friction tripwire before upsell sequence.

9. **Upsell Sequence Follows**: Baldasso estimates $97 or $67 upsell, plus a downsell and second upsell — standard ascension ladder after the tripwire.

10. **Google Display as Primary Traffic Source**: Revolution Golf is described as "one of the biggest advertisers on Google Display" — suggesting this quiz funnel was optimized for cold display traffic, not just warm search or social.
