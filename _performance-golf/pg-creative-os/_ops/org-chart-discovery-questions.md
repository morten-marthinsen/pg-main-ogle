# Creative OS — Org Chart Discovery Questions

**Purpose:** Use this document when meeting with each key team member in the Creative OS department. Capture their answers (and/or share transcript) so the PRD and each member's input into Creative OS can be updated accordingly.

**Key team members:** Jojo, Jenny, Fatima, Morten, and other creative leaders.

---

## Role & Responsibilities

1. What is your primary role and key responsibilities?
2. What is your official title, and does it match what you actually do day to day?
3. What creative outputs do you produce? (List everything: ad types, formats, channels.)
4. What creative inputs do you need from others to do your job?

---

## Time & Friction

5. What tasks take up most of your time currently?
6. What tasks do you find most frustrating or time-consuming?
7. What tasks do you wish you could delegate or automate?
8. What meetings or Slack threads consume the most time?
9. What would you do more of if you had more time?

---

## Information & Decisions

10. What information do you need to do your job effectively? Where do you get it today?
11. What decisions do you make independently vs need approval for?
12. Who do you typically need to align with before something can ship?
13. What tools and systems do you use daily?

---

## Effectiveness & Sub-Agents

14. What would make you 2x more effective in your role?
15. If you could have an AI assistant (sub-agent) that handled one thing for you, what would it be?
16. What repeatable tasks could a sub-agent own so you could focus on higher-value work?
17. Where do you see the biggest gaps between what’s asked of you and what you have time to do?

---

## Optional / Deeper Dive

18. How do you currently know if your work is aligned to our two brand threads (smarter journey / stories of innovation)?
19. What would make it easier to stay aligned with campaign themes and the rest of the team?
20. Is there anything else about your role, workflow, or goals that would help us design Creative OS around you?

---

## How to Use This

- Schedule 1:1s with each key team member.
- Go through the questions that fit the conversation (you don’t have to ask every one verbatim).
- Capture answers in notes or transcript.
- Feed the answers/transcript back into the AI so it can help update the PRD and each member’s place in the Creative OS.
