## **The 4-Step System to Win the Second Purchase (First 30–45 Days)**

### **1️⃣ Win the First Product Experience (Days 0–14)**

Goal: Create an emotional win fast.

* Remove friction.

* Focus on one clear improvement.

* Help them feel progress.

* Reinforce that they made a smart decision.

If they don’t feel early improvement, they won’t come back.

This phase is about belief, not selling.

---

### **2️⃣ Identify the Golfer’s Real Gap (Days 10–20)**

Goal: Diagnose before pitching.

Use simple signals:

* What product they bought

* Engagement behavior

* One-click check-in questions (“What’s still frustrating you?”)

* Optional phone outreach for high-value cohorts

You are not guessing what to sell next.  
 You are surfacing their biggest gap.

Clarity beats complexity.

---

### **3️⃣ Educate Around the Gap (Days 20–35)**

Goal: Build conviction.

Once you know their gap:

* Send focused education.

* Show why this issue is holding them back.

* Explain the mechanism.

* Demonstrate proof.

Do not overwhelm.  
 Do not present multiple options.

Guide them to see the next step themselves.

---

### **4️⃣ Present One Logical Next Purchase (Days 30–45)**

Goal: Make the second purchase obvious.

Not 5 products.  
 Not “you might also like.”  
 One clear next move based on their gap.

Position it as:  
 “The natural next step in your game.”

If you get this right, the second purchase becomes progression, not promotion.
