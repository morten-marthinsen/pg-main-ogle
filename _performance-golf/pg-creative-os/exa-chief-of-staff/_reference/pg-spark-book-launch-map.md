## Weekly Review Protocol

Every week, the `launch_tracker` sub-agent reviews this map and answers:
1. Which launches moved forward? (stage progression)
2. Which launches are stalled? (no movement in >2 weeks = FLAG)
3. Which launches are at risk for their timeline? (BLOCK if critical)
4. What ClickUp tasks were created, assigned, or completed?
5. What's the "desk drawer" status — how many ideas have no ClickUp task or owner?
