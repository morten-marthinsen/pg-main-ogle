For decades, golfers have accepted a strange contradiction in their bags.

You figured it out years ago with your irons — cavity-back designs help you hit straighter, more consistent shots without perfect technique. That's why 84% of golfers play game-improvement irons.

But around the green? You're still fighting blade wedges designed for Tour pros who practice 40 hours a week.

Traditional wedges dig into turf unless you deliver the exact angle of attack they demand. One degree off, and you chunk it fat or blade it thin. The result? Dread builds in your backswing. Panic hits at impact. You start avoiding certain shots entirely — praying your ball lands in the bunker so you won't have to chip.

It was never your fault. You were using equipment designed for different golfers.

Performance Golf built the ONE.1 Wedge to fix this contradiction. The secret is the Auto-Glide Sole — a four-way cambered surface engineered to glide through turf instead of digging into it. It manages ground contact FOR you, eliminating the precise angle-of-attack control traditional wedges demand.

No technique changes. No hours of practice. Just square the face, swing, and let the sole do the work.

The ONE.1 delivers consistent contact from tight lies, thick rough, and sand — without changing your swing. Even your mishits land on the green instead of short in the bunker.

And unlike chippers or "game improvement" wedges that look like training wheels, the ONE.1 has a traditional wedge appearance with a premium black finish. Your playing partners will only see the results.

Available in 50°, 56°, and 60° to cover your entire short game.

Stop fighting equipment designed for someone else. The Auto-Correct Wedge is here.
