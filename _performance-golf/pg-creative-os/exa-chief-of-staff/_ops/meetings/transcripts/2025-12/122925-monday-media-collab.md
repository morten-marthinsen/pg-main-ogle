    *   Katherine Green raised concerns regarding inconsistencies in the full-screen focus settings, autoplay configurations, and overall landing page experiences across various funnels.
    *   The discussion emphasized standardizing these settings to ensure that conversion data is reliable and that differences in user experience do not skew performance metrics.

    *   The team discussed the balance between WPSS and the quiz funnels, with media insights suggesting potential cannibalization due to overlapping audience demographics.
    
*   **Standardization of Ad Asset Quantity & Future Funnel Strategy** Idea
    *   The team debated the need for establishing standard numbers of video and image assets for each funnel (e.g., a set number of videos for VSL and product-focused ads) to streamline planning and testing.
    *   Opinions varied with an emphasis on balancing creative diversity (different hooks and video lengths) with resource constraints, and decisions would be data-driven in subsequent meetings.
