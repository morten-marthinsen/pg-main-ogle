### Overview

The team reviewed the digital campaign performance as they transitioned from Black Friday messaging to holiday-focused promotions and evaluated strategies to clear significant physical inventory. They discussed detailed ad spend reductions, creative adjustments, and plans to clear out high-stock items, particularly the Thriver product, while considering current resource limitations.

### Key Takeaways

*   The team decided to continue lowering and consolidating digital ad spend and to shift creative focus from Black Friday to holiday messaging to approach a break-even point.
*   They agreed to explore strategies such as checkout bump offers and bundled promotions (e.g., buy-one-get-one free) for clearing physical inventory, with a special emphasis on the Thriver product.
*   The group recognized the need to balance digital adjustments with physical inventory clearance while addressing potential resource and development constraints.

    *   Danielle Schwolow confirmed that Thriver inventory was the highest and suggested prioritizing closeout offers for this product, potentially executing a campaign in February.

    *   Concerns were raised regarding the limited development resources available, especially during December, potentially delaying new feature implementations.
    *   Discussion focused on whether dev tickets for modifying checkout pages or creating new offer pages could be prioritized or if such changes might need to be deferred until January or February.

*   **Next Steps and Action Items** Decision
    *   Samuel Mercado planned to continue monitoring and adjusting digital ad spend and creative strategies to stabilize campaign performance and reach break-even.
    *   The inventory clearance strategy was to be further explored, with Danielle Schwolow tasked to provide comprehensive inventory details and prioritize products for clearance.

    *   A follow-up on development resource capacity was deemed necessary to determine which initiatives could be implemented immediately versus those that need to be postponed.
