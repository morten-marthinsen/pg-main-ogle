### Overview

The team reviewed ad campaign performance metrics and discussed creative tests ahead of the upcoming Black Friday period. They focused on identifying underperforming elements, reallocating budgets, and refining ad content to improve engagement and conversion rates.

### Key Takeaways

*   The team agreed to retest creatives with proven winning elements instead of discarding them outright.
*   They decided to rework and optimize creative assets by enhancing the initial 25% of the ad to better capture viewer interest.
*   The group resolved to shift advertising spend to higher-performing regions, such as Australia and the UK, while reducing spending in the US until updated Black Friday messaging is in place.
*   They recognized the need to review new footage offline to extract more authentic and engaging content that differentiates from repetitive shots.
*   The team acknowledged the importance of gathering detailed landing page behavior analytics to better understand conversion challenges.

### Key Topics
*   **Ad Campaign Performance Update** Update
    *   Samuel Mercado presented a performance overview of the current ad campaigns, noting metrics such as net loss per trial, cost per click (CPC), add-to-cart costs, and overall ad spend across products like 357 and the click stick.
    *   He highlighted improvements on product 357, where the net loss per trial dropped from 64 to 36, and mentioned adjustments made to align performance targets, especially ahead of Black Friday.
    
*   **Creative Testing and Hypothesis Discussion** Idea
    *   The team reviewed several underperforming creatives that were based on a proven winning script featuring Gary, discussing reasons behind high CPC and add-to-cart costs despite using familiar elements.
    *   Will McIlroy emphasized learning from these underperformers rather than discarding them, suggesting the need to test them further to understand if timing, algorithm behavior, or target audience saturation affected performance.
    *   Morten Marthinsen and Christopher Ogle contributed hypotheses regarding early engagement issues, particularly pointing out low performance in the first 25% of the ad, which could be harming CTR and overall effectiveness.
    
*   **Geographical Budget Allocation and Market Performance** Decision
    *   There was a detailed discussion on differing market performances, with Australia and the UK showing better results compared to the US, where spend and performance were lower.
    *   Samuel Mercado indicated that the majority of the ad spend would be allocated to the better performing geographies while reducing spend in the US until updated Black Friday messaging becomes available.
    *   This decision was informed by seasonal factors, local consumer behavior, and current performance metrics from various campaigns.
    
*   **Creative Asset Quality and Production Concerns** Problem
    *   Cameron Fair and Paul Schlegel raised concerns about the authenticity and quality of new B roll footage used in recent creative tests.
    *   They noted that newer footage from the second shoot did not capture genuine reactions compared to earlier shoots, leading to a potential decrease in ad engagement.
    *   The team discussed the need to re-evaluate, re-edit, and possibly revisit the available footage to extract more authentic and engaging clips to improve the ad's performance.
    
*   **Retesting Strategies and Action Items** Decision
    *   The team agreed to retest promising creatives that contain winning elements, with a focus on improving the hook in the first 25% of the ad to boost engagement and CTR.
    *   Action items included refining and updating the creative deck with detailed hypotheses, reviewing footage offline with production teams (involving Cameron and Paul), and closely monitoring behavioral data on landing pages to better understand conversion issues.
    *   There was consensus on using the current underperformance as a learning opportunity to adjust creative strategies, audience targeting, and campaign timing ahead of the key Black Friday period.
