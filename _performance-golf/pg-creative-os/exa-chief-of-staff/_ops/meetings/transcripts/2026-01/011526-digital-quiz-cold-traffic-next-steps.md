    *   There was consensus that once the VSL and bump offer are updated, the funnel’s performance (especially conversion rate, which is already above 2%) should improve.
    *   The team remains cautious about increasing spend until the funnel optimization is completed.
    
*   **International Targeting and Creative Adjustments** Idea
    *   The discussion addressed lower international conversion rates compared to the U.S., with metrics typically showing about half the U.S. performance.
    *   Suggestions included using localized creative elements, such as Canadian or Australian presenters and influencers, to better target these markets.
    *   Omer committed to providing historical conversion benchmarks by country in the next meeting to guide future creative strategies.
    
*   **Start Page Creative Options** Idea
    *   Two creative options for the start page were discussed: Option A features a smaller image with the query “Do you know your swing circle?” above the fold, and Option B would incorporate a testimonial image below the male/female selection.
    *   Maeve noted that previous data from Option A was available, suggesting testing Option B to compare its performance.
    *   The team agreed to experiment with these options to determine which would better engage users without causing issues such as ad disapproval.
    
*   **Underperforming DQFE Ads** Decision
