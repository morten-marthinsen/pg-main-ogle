John used the phrase "marketing OS" and "creative OS" multiple times. He sees TESS + Neco + VEDA + the Donnie/Ben research pipeline as a single system. Your current PRD treats them as separate integration points. **You need a name and a tracking mechanism for the unified system.** John will evaluate you partly on whether this system is real, adopted, and producing connected outputs — not just on whether individual agents work.

**Recommendation:** Name it. Call it the Creative OS (or whatever you prefer). Add it to the scorecard as a measurable capability: "By Day 60, the Creative OS produces a connected campaign package (ad angles + landing page microscripts + organic content ideas + influencer brief) for RS1 that all teams receive from the same source."
