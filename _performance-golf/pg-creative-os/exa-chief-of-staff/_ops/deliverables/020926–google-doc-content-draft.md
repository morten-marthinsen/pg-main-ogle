## 1. Creative OS Overview

### What It Is

The Creative OS is an AI-powered operating system for PG's creative department. Four specialized AI agents coordinate data intelligence, copywriting, video production, and strategic oversight — all under one roof, all connected through shared data and angles.

### The Pipeline

```
Exa (strategic orchestration — sits above all)
├── Tess (intelligence — what's working, what to make next)
│   ├──→ Veda (production — creates the video assets)
│   └──→ Neco (copy & briefs — how to say it)
└── Neco ──→ Veda (future: copy scripts feed production)
```

### Agent Summary

| Agent | Role | What It Does | Sessions |
|-------|------|-------------|----------|
| **Exa** | Strategic Chief of Staff | Scorecard tracking, delegation, meeting prep, weekly updates, challenger protocol | 14 |
| **Tess** | Intelligence Engine | Ad performance data, naming conventions, root angle identification, asset registry | 123 |
| **Veda** | Production Engine | Video editing, hook stacks, cutdowns, Iconik integration, automated variations | 40 |
| **Neco** | Copy Engine | Ad copy, influencer briefs, script generation, claim verification | 13 |

### Connection to Marketing OS

Creative OS connects to Donnie and Ben's Marketing OS via shared research, product concept docs, and campaign themes. Marketing OS provides research, product intelligence, and GTM campaign structure. Creative OS takes that input and produces creative direction, ad assets, copy, and influencer briefs. The two systems share a common data layer so every team operates from the same strategic foundation — one engine, not two separate systems.

## 8. Creative Team Telos Map

The Creative OS isn't just about AI agents — it's about empowering each person on the team to operate at their highest potential. The traditional org chart asks "what boxes do we need." This asks "what's each person's superpower, and what's keeping them from it?"

For each team member, we identify their **telos** (highest-potential operating state) and their **drag** (day-to-day tasks that reduce their potential). The drag tasks become automation targets — each person eventually gets their own AI agent that handles the drag so they can operate at telos.
