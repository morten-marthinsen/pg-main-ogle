bile in stool that may be caused by a bile duct obstruction
or a liver problem.
Black stool is a serious warning sign for bleeding in
the upper gastrointestinal tract-possibly the stomach.
Bright red stool may indicate bleeding in the lower
intestinal tract-possibly the large intestine or rectum.
Hemorrhoids are usually the source of the bleeding.
