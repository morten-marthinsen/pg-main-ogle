# Why Drugs Should Be Your Last Resort:


## 1. Drugs are excessively expensive.

Drug prices are already scandalously inflated and pro
tected by laws that forbid any competition. This means most people have no real choice. This is great for
the manufacturers. But it's a raw deal for most people living in today's tight economy on fixed incomes.

Drug companie have yet to invent
a drug that corrects what causes blood pressure or blood sugar to rise. Today's drugs merely lower your
readings artificially, while the underlying problem gets worse. As a result, most patients must remain
on them for life.

## 3. Many drugs can be dangerous.

Drugs can be life-savers in emergency situations. (That's
the only way I'll use them.) But plenty of studies have found that today's drugs produce uncomfortable
side effects and long-term health damage. These problems include: heart arrhythmias ... sexual dysfunction
... elevated cholesterol... severe weakening of the heart muscle ... even heart failure. Some of these drugs
actually INCREASE the risk of heart attack and cancer, and can cause vision loss.

## 4. They're often unnecessary.

This is true in 80% of all hypertension, and in 90% of type 2
diabetes cases. Cholesterol drugs have been proven to be irrelevant because the majority of all heart attacks
occur in people with NORMAL levels, whether they're on a drug or not.
