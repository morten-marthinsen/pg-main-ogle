Reducing road noise
The vibration tracks at Vizzola
are rough, potholed, undulating.
Their job is to induce the worst
kind of juddering discomfort
found on the world's roads.
Noise measurements are
taken both inside and. outside the
vehicle. The gathered sound is
then run through Pirelli's
frequency analyzers where the
source of each noise wave is
traced and measured.
This enables Pirelli's
engineers to develop tires that
are both comfortable and quiet.
How do 150 mph tires
help at 65 mph?
Speed limits on European super
highways are much higher than
our own maximum national
speed limit of 65 miles-per-hour.
On the German autobahn, for
example, the "advisory" speed
limit is 81 mph. But you can
drive as fasfas your car will
take you.
The only catch is that you are
required to fit your car with tires
rated to perform at the vehicle's
maximum speed. If all four tires
are V-speed rated you're legal at
speeds up to 150 mph. If they're
Z-rated you're legal at any
speed.

With our 65 mph speed limit in
the US, you won't find many
places where you can drive your
car at its maximum speed.
So what purpose do speed
ratings serve in the US?
Speed ratings are not so much
a measure of speed as they are a
measure of performance. If you
must suddenly stop or swerve '
your car, a speed-rated perfor

control. Which could mean the
