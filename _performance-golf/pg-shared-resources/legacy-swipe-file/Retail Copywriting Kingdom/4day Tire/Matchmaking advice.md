A word about trailer tires
Most trailer tires require higher
inflation pressures than
passenger car tires, a fact not
generally known by the corner
gas jockey.
Bleeding air from trailer tires
in a misguided attempt to match
the lower inflation pressures of
passenger cal'. tires may result
in uneven treadwear, trailer
instability at high speeds and·
extreme heat build-up. ·
Give tires a rest
When recreational vehicles are
out of service for long periods of
time, the vehicle should be put
on blocks and the tire/wheel
assemblies removed and stored
in a cool, dry place where the
tires will not be exposed to direct
sunlight, which will cause
weather cracking. The air
pressure of the stored tires
should be reduced to 10 psi.
