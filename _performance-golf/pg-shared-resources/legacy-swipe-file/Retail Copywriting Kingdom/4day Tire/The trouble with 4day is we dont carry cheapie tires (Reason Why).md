After thousands of miles on
the road, tires and wheels have a
tendency to go slightly out of
shape. Ride quality deteriorates
so gradually that you may
hardly notice.
When you replace the tires,
wheel balancing will certainly
improve the car's ride. However,
because new tires assume the
shape of the old wheels, it is
impossible for balancing alone to
restore that new car ride.
That's precisely what Accu
Balance does. AccuBalance is a
process that matches the total
wheel-&-tire assembly to near
perfect concentricity by preci
sion buffing microscopic rubber
shavings from the tire's shoulder
under simulated driving condi
tions. This corrects variations in
sidewall flexing which can cause
a rough, uneven ride.
The tire-&-wheel assembly is
then computer spin balanced for
the ultimate in vibration-free
driving.
AccuBalance is now available
at all 4day stores. Our price is
$12 (cash/check) for tires
purchased from 4day, $14.25
(cash/check) for tires purchased
elsewhere. We also offer the

purchased at 4day, $8.25
(cash/check) for tires purchased
elsewhere.
