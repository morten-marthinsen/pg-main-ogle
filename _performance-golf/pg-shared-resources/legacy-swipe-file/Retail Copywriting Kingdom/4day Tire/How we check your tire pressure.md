To get you In & outfaster
Checking your tires at frequent
intervals enables us to help you
correct any problems which
might be developing.
Also to help you maintain the
correct inflation pressures.
Our mgrs are required to
place a numbered cone on every
car that drives in. All work is
started in the sequence of
arrival.
However, if you're there just
for your regular 4,000 to 6,000
mile inspection, a tab that says
"Inspection Only" is placed on
the numbered cone.
This signals our mgr to send
his next available man to in
spect your tires now, ahead of
the next numbered customer. It
takes only moments and doesn't
inconvenience those who are
waiting.
He doesn't have to put your
car in a stall or on a lift. He
brings the air to your car in a
can.
We call this pressurized can
our portable Inflation Station.
It has a large dial. Look over
his shoulder. You can see if
you've been driving on incor
rectly inflated tires.
The can automatically cor
rects inflation in seconds.
If your tires are wearing
evenly, they won't need rotat
ing. If they do need it, or you
request it, the "Inspection
Only" tab is removed and you
wait your numbered turn on the
lift.
If you miss an Inspection
or are a few miles late for it, you
automatically reduce the
treadwear portion of the guar
antee, though you still keep the
road hazard and free flat repair
portion in effect.
But frankly, some customers
shouldn't buy our 4day guaran
tee. If it's too long a drive or for
any other reason you find it
inconvenient to drive in for
frequent inspections, why spend
the extra 5%?
It's a good guarantee, on
which we break even or make a
negligible profit.
We advise you to buy it, but
we don't push it.
