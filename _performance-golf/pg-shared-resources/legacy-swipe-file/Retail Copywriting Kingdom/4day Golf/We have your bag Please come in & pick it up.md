surgery to save his arm, which

practice run down the Santa Pod

Gwynn was taken, said they could
not determine the extent of any
paralysis until swelling went down
around his spinal cord.
"All things considered, Darrell is
in excellent spirits," his father,
Jerry Gwynn, said through a
spokesman. "He has some very
serious neck and back injuries
along with the arm.

critical. He has some feeling in his
right hand, which is encouraging,
but has no feeling below the waist.
The doctors said that was not
unusual after an accident like he
had."

crew chief and team manager since
Darrell began racing in 1980. Be

England from four to 14 weeks, a
