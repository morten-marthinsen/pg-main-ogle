## Low style vs. High style.

Unlike most stores, Dorman-Winthrop carries all
. the major _styles worn by men ..
Some of our sport coats, for example, are very high
style. The new Edwardian cut. The shaped look.
Inverted pleats. The one-button coats.
But the opposite of high style is not low style. It's
just a little more conservative.
In this category, we put the two and three button
