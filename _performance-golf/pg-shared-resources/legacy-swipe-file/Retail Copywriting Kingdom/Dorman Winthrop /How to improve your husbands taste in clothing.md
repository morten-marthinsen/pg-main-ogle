# How to improve your


# husband's taste in clothing

He's so sophisticated.
He always chooses the best of everything.
Music. Wine. Cars.
But he has one shortcoming. His taste in
clothing. Oh, it's not bad-but it's not super-
lative. He just doesn't know the difference be-
tween good men's clothing and-the-very best.
Yet.
You're going to teach him. So he'll never
settle for anything but the best again.
You hesitate to just go out and buy him a
$1525 suit for Christmas. Until he's used to
fine clothing, that may startle him a bit.
Even if you tell him you got it at Dorman-
Winthrop for just $1147.75.
You need to work him up to it slowly.
Begin with something small. A cotton shirt.
Not just any kind of cotton shirt, though.
An $85 Sea Island cotton shirt. Woven of
extra-long, silky cotton fibers. One touch of
the luxurious fabric and he'll know it's
special.
Dorman-Winthrop sells this $85 shirt for
$63.02.
Or warm him up with a muffler. A $75 pure
cashmere muffler made in Scotland. Avail-
able in 9 solid colors. At Dorman-Winthrop
for$52.57.
Or choose any other item from our haber-
dashery department.
We don't carry the conventional selection
of underwear, pajamas, handkerchiefs and
the like.
We carry only those items that you might
select to harmonize with the suits and sport
coats you buy here.
Dress shirts. Sports shirts. Ties. Belts. A
few pullovers.
Llke everything else at Dorman-Winthrop,
these items are the most expensive lines.
From the most prestigious US and European
designers.
Your man will get so used to the very best
haberdashery that he won't want to wear it
with his nice, but oh-so-ordinary suits, sport
jackets and slacks.
Then you'll have won him over to Dorman-
Winthrop quality.
Not to mention how much more under-
standing he'll be about your expensive tastes
in clothing for yourself.

# • • •

While we have your attention:
Don't forget about our women's tailored
apparel and accessories.
Or how about a D-W gift certificate for
Christmas? After all, good things come in
small packages.
