The men's clothing business, as a whole, is
slow. Yet clothing sales at our three Dorman
Winthrop stores arc up 12% over last year.
Did we hold extra promotions to pump up
sales?
No. We just keep doing what we've done all
along. And apparently more and more men
arc noticing.
Our huge selection. Dorman-Winthrop has a
larger selection from the top makers, in your
size, than any store in the West. In more
types and models. Including European, con
temporary, square shoulder, California and
natural shoulder traditional.
We're even adding a full topcoat depart
ment this Fall.
We charge for alterations. Some alterations
are included when you buy elsewhere. That
doesn't mean they're free. Just that the price
has been padded to cover alteration costs.
But some customers don't need as many
alterations as others. So why charge them
the same as those that need more? We
charge only for needed alterations. At our
cost. It's fairer. And it saves you money.
Our cut prices. Most men's stores work on a
55% to 60% profit margin on suits, jackets
and slacks. The difference between our sell
ing price and the wholesale price (including
freight and hanger) is only 33-1/3%. Priced to
the penny by our computer.
Top notch
service.
The employ
ees at Dorman
Winthrop are
knowledgeable
about men's fine
clothing. And, un
like at some stores,
you won't have to look
hard to find them.
If you ever do receive
less than superior service,
we ask you to let our president,
Jay Winthrop, know. At the bottom of
this and every ad, we ask you to call him at
any of our stores.
Our salespeople aren't pushy. We offer no
spiffs to our employees to sell the slower
moving goods. Instead we reduce the prices
further on these items and identify them with
red tags.
Cash discount. Processing a credit card ·costs
money. About 2%. Why charge our cash cus
tomer for that? So we give a 2% discount if
you pay by cash or check.
But that's not all. If a customer pays a
deposit at time of purchase and then waits to
pay the balance when he picks up the cloth
ing, that costs us a little too. Pay us the full
amount when we write up your sales ticket
and that 1% is also yours.
Total, 3% off. A tidy savings.
