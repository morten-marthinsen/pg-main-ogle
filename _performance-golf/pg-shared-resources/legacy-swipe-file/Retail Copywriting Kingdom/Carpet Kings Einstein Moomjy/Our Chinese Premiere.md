Albert Moomjy presents the Great Rugs of China. A limited engagement now thru Nov.10th.
Albert Moomjy, our Chinese Premier, announces
one of the most dramatic moments in the history of Sino
Moomjy relations.
Bigger even than our last Chinese first; our historic
introduction of Chinese rugs to American floors eight
years ago.
It's the Einstein Moomjy Chinese Rug Fair, featur
ing the great rugs of China. With a supporting role played
by Chinaruhsu (China National Native Produce & Animal
By-Products Import & Export Corp.), the carpet export
agency of the People's Republic of China.
There are rugs that cover the entire mainland of
China; from Peking to Shanghai to Tientsin to Sinkiang to
Tibet. And rugs that cover the entire history of China.
There are hand made, hand woven rugs of Chinese
sheepwool, goat's hair and silk.
Each as different from the other as Yin is from Yang.
There are geometrics and round rugs, Savonneries
and Aubussons, Peacocks, Peonies and an olympian
collection of Oriental runners.
Not to mention the great wall hangings of China.
There are Chinese artifacts, Chinese delicacies like
Jasmin tea and Chinese know-it-alls like our salespeople.
And special off-the-wool prices. (Off the goat's hair
and silk, too.)
So come to Moomjy's Chinese Theatre for this
important Premiere. Even if it's just to say hello. We
promise you'll leave with a good buy.
