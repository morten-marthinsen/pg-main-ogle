The Thailand Floor Show at Einstein Moomjy. Now thru November 24th.
At Einstein Moomjy, we're turning our
eyes, our floors and our walls to Thailand, a
nation that's been weaving its way into power.
Come see a collection of Thai rugs fit for a
King of Siam. It's called the Siamese Collection.
There are exquisite oriental designs (as you
can see in the picture above) in exquisite colors
(as you can't see in the picture above).
For example, there are classics like the
Cinabar Dragon, the Sea Foam Green Panda, the
Creamy Plum Blossom and the wheat-colored
Peking. There are also designs with a french
accent. Like the pastel Ivory Aubusson and the
pastel Blue Aubusson; our Siamese twins.
They're all lustre washed, hand carved and
pure hand tufted virgin wool. And each comes
with a handsome fringe benefit; a fringe.
What's more, thanks to good old Thai
ingenuity and good old Walter Moomjy, our
Ambassador to Thailand, these priceless oriental
classics are now priced less than oriental rugs.
The 4x6 size is $349 (was $440), the 6x9 size is
$749 (was $940) and the 8' 3"xl2 size is $1299
(was $1625). (All sizes in all stores.)
These rugs can be shipped all over the
U.S. And they're available for immediate delivery.
So deliver yourself to Einstein Moomjy immedi
ately. Or at least deliver a phone call or a letter.
Oh, by the way, dress for this event is
optional. We'll be wearing the Thais.
