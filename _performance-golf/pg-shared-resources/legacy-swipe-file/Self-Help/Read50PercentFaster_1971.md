# Why rapid readers retain


# more than others

Are you frequently annoyed at yourself for forget
ting things you've read only a few hours or even
minutes before? Names in a news story, for exam
ple. Or the contents of a memorandum. Or the
quotable line in a book you just put down.
Surprisingly enough, this may happen because.
you read too slowly. Actual tests show that when
you read slowly, both your eyes and your mind
tend to wander. Your concentration slackens.
Rapid reading, on the other hand, is accomplished
when your eyes and mind are focused on the page,
and. your concentration is greatest. Once consid
ered superficial skimmers, rapid readers score con
sistently higher not only in reading speed but in
comprehension and retention as well.
