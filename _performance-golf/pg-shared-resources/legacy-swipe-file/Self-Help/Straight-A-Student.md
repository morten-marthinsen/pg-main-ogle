## Former D student gets Ph.D.

"All of us are capable of much more than we
achieve", says author Gordon Green. "I learned
this lesson the hard way-through my own ex
periences as a student. My grades in high school
were so poor that I had to go through a special
remedial program to get into college, and once
in college I received several D's in my freshman
year."
"But things changed. I went on to get a Ph.D.
in Economics, and in the process I made an A in
every course I took-in fact, I made an A on
every test in every course I took! At the time I
was attending classes at night, working at a full
time job, and taking care of home and family
responsibilities-but I still had plenty of time
left over for leisure activities."
