## A FEW FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS ABOUT BITCOIN:

What the heck is a bitcoin?
Bitcoins are simply a unit of account in a ledger that exists on the computers of every user in the Bitcoin
network. Bitcoin transactions are verified by thousands of users with specialized software called "miners"
to ensure there is no fraud or double-spending. The security and integrity of the network is ensured through
cryptography - the same computer science that banks, big companies, and government agencies use to
keep their own information secure.
How do bitcoins work?
The same way as dollars: You can transfer ownership of bitcoins through the Internet in exchange for
goods and services. Most dollars are just numbers in a bank's computer network. Bitcoin's creator was the
first to figure out that you can make such a network public and decouple money ownership from personal
information, making it anonymous. Your ownership is recorded by your online wallet and broadcast to the
Bift."'Oin network without your name attached to anything.
Why should I buy bitcoins?
The Bitcoin technology is an incredibly significant leap in the evolution of banking. For the first time in
history, you can retain control over your money and send it anonymously and with little or no cost over
the Internet. When you spend bitcoins, you never have to put your credit, debit, or other financial infor
mation on some website where it can be hacked. We believe Bitcoin to be an essential step in enabling
people to protect themselves from both criminal threats and bad government decisions.
