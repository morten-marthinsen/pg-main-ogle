INDUSTRY OVERVIEW
The Chinese government is emphasizing increased access
to healthcare for more citizens, particularly those in rural areas.
Consequently, total expenditure on pharmaceutical products in
China, which almost doubled from 2002 to 2006, hitting
$47.3 billion, is expected to grow at a rate of 23.5% annually
between 2007 and 2011, reaching $126.8 billion in 2011.
Operating in a highly fragmented pharmaceutical industry,
China Baicaotang Medicine Limited is well-positioned to capi
talize on this growing marketplace.
COMPANY BRIEF
China Baicaotang Medicine Limited ("China BCT") is a
vertically integrated enterprise, engaged in pharmaceutical dis
tribution, retail pharmacies and the manufacture of pharma
ceuticals and medical-related products through three sub
sidiaries, all of which are located in Guangxi Province, China.
The Company has major manufacturing capacity in six
key formulation areas: granular, paste, pills, capsule, liquid and
raw material; owns 76 production licenses; and is the sole pro
ducer in China for a hepatitis treatment drug.
China BCT is one of the largest regional wholesale net
works in China. The Company has about 4,000 suppliers and
offers more than 8,000 products. Included in their supplier net
work are 1,200 under exclusive wholesale contract at the provin
cial level. Customers include hospitals, clinics, other wholesalers
and retail pharmacy groups.
China BCT operates the largest regional retail network
in Guangxi Province consisting of 66 directly owned stores,
supplying both Western medicines as well as traditional
Chinese medicines.
