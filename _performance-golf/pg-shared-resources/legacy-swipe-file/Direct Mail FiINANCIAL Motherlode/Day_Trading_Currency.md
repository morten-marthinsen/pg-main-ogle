# What Is Forex?

Forex (Foreign Exchange) is the name given to the "direct access" trading of
foreign currencies. It is the simultaneous buying of one currency and selling
of another.
Forex is a new way for individual, online traders
to
make money trading
the $1.9 trillion daily foreign exchange (forex) currency market. Forex
is 20 times bigger than the New York Stock Exchange and NASDAQ
- combined. Forex is 63 times bigger than all the futures markets in the
world - combined. (Total daily volume in futures is $30 billion.) As a result,
many traders believe Forex is the most liquid and fastest-moving market to
trade. You can open up an account in minutes online, fund it instantly with
your credit card - and trade mini-contracts for as little as $300 deposit. It is
an electronic, over-the-counter market where buyers and sellers - from small
online traders
to
billion-dollar money center banks - trade foreign currencies
directly.
