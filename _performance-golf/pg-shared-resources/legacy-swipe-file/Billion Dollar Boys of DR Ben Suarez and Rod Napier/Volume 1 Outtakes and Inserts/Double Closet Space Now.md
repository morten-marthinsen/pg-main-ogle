TIP ONE: Remove all
plastic dry cleaner bags from
clothing. Plastic bags do not
allow fabrics to breathe and
they keep the dry cleaning
chemicals in the clothing.
TIP TWO: A set of good
quality hangers that are not
plastic or wire is a must-have
item to keep your closet neat
and help preserve your expen-
sive clothing.
Study for this article
revealed why metal hangers are
virtually free to consumers...they
are basically worthless.
Not only do they get tangled
and bent, but a wire hanger
leaves bumps in shoulders and
arms of clothing making them
very unattractive to wear.
Plastic hangers sag and
break with any type of heavy
clothing. Plus they can also
create bumps in the shoulder
area of clothing and cause
wrinkling of slacks when the
plastic sags.
After investing a lot of
money on apparel it is very
important to make sure clothes
are protected in the best way
possible with hangers like Ultra
Hangers.
TIP THREE: Hang all of
your similar clothing items
together by color.
For example, hang all of
your long sleeve shirts or
blouses in a row, then hang
the short-sleeved ones next.
Make sure clothes are on
Ultra Hangers to ensure there
are no lumps or bumps in the
shoulders and to maintain the
shape of the garment.
Then place all sleeveless
tops on Ultra Hangers because
they will not slip off and land
in a wrinkled mess on your
closet floor.
Never rewash wrinkled
garments or lake time to iron
them when it can be prevented
with Ultra Hangers.
TIP FOUR: Fold and
place slacks on an Ultra
Hanger and hang similar col-
ors together to find them in a
flash.
TIP FIVE: Hang all long
garments such as coats, robes
or long dresses to one side of
the closet. This will allow
room for items such as shoes
and baskets to be placed under
the shorter clothing for easy
viewing.
TIP SIX: It is always
beneficial to hang all of your
matching hangers together in
the same direction. For this
reason the Ultra Hanger has a
pivotal hook at the top to aid
with this step.

loose items in labeled baskets
