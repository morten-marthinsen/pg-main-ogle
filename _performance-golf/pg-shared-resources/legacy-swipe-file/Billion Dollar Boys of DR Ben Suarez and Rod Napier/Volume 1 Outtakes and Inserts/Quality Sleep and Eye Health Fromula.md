## DROWSY AND DREARY

When your body is deprived of adequate sleep, it shows — especially
in your eyes. How your eyes look tells you they need help in the form
of proper, restorative sleep.

closed, which helps the retinal membranes recharge after a too-long
day of seeing and overuse. The puffiness is caused by a fluid retention,
or edema, under the eye from elevated blood pressure that causes
vessels to constrict. Dark rings under the eyes are actually circulating
blood in the soft tissues beneath the skin. It’s believed that sleep
deprivation causes blood to pool under the eyes.
