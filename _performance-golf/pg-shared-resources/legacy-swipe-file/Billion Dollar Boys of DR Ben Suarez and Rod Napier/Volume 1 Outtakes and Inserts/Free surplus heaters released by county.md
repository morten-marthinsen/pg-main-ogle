lucky Michigan residents.
That’s because U.S. based Heat Surge ® just
announced that they are giving away brand
new portable heaters that help slash high heat
bills to residents of the state of Michigan.
This is all happening to help Michigan res-
idents slash high heat bills and save money.
That’s why the surplus heaters are being giv-
en away free to lucky Michigan residents who
find their county listed in today’s publication
and beat the 2-day deadline to order the hand-
crafted Amish fireplace mantle for just one
hundred ninety-nine dollars in either Light

hand-made in the USA by real Amish crafts-
men. That makes the solid oak mantle a real
steal at just one hundred ninety-nine dollars
and shipping/handling because the first 9,786
Michigan residents who find their county list-
ed in today’s publication are getting the brand
new Efficiency Plus Wide Screen Touch heat-
er for free.
Company spokesman, David Martin, con-
firms, “We only have 9,786 surplus heaters to
give away right now, so residents of the state
of Michigan who find their county listed in to-
day’s publication need to immediately call the
Michigan Distribution Hotline to get the free
heaters.”
“Everyone’s sick and tired of paying high
heat bills. But what most people don’t realize
is that their heat bills are so high because their
furnace constantly runs and runs to heat areas
of their home that they’re not even using, cost-
ing them a significant amount of money every
single year,” Martin said.
Demand for Heat Surge heaters has sky-
rocketed ever since consumer reviews sur-
faced showing just how much money peo-
ple are actually saving on high heat bills us-
ing ultra-efficient zone heating. That’s why the
decision was made to widely advertise this an-
nouncement to the general public through local
and national newspaper publications.
Here’s how it works. The brand new
Efficiency Plus ™ Hybrid-Thermic ® heater helps
eliminate high heat bills and saves consumers
a substantial amount of money by out smarting
their home thermostat. This little heating gen-
ius uses super advanced Hybrid-Thermic heat
technology that lets you set and keep the actu-
al temperature in any room a bone-soothing 74°
so you’ll never be cold again.
But here’s the real miracle. It keeps the room
a constant 74° even when the home thermostat
is turned clear down to 59°. That means you’ll
never be cold again and you’ll save a substan-
tial amount of money on heat bills with zone
heating since your furnace rarely ever kicks
on.
“It just doesn’t make any sense to keep pay-
ing high heat bills. That’s because with all the
money you can save with these new Hybrid-
Thermic portable heaters that produce ultra-
efficient zone heat they actually pay for them-
selves in no time. After that, they just keep
on putting cash in your pocket month after
month,” Martin said.
“You see, with your furnace you are paying
to heat areas of your home that you’re not even
using. And since the new Efficiency Plus heater
only uses pennies an hour of electricity on the
standard setting the potential energy savings
are absolutely incredible,” said Martin.
“We don’t want anyone to get left out, but
residents need to hurry because we only have
9,786 surplus heaters to give away. For now,
we’re allowing Michigan residents to get two
free heaters just as long as they call within the
next two days,” Martin said.
Getting the new Efficiency Plus
Hybrid-Thermic heater is the smart way for
consumers to save money on heat bills and
stay warm all winter long. And getting the
Hybrid-Thermic heater free is like putting two
hundred and sixty bucks in your pocket and
you can save even more on heat bills.
“We’re bracing for all the calls because
everyone wants to save money on heat bills and
stay warm,” Martin said.
“So if lines are busy keep trying, all calls will
be answered. And we’ll get a delivery truck
out to you in no time so you can start saving
money,” said Martin. ■
