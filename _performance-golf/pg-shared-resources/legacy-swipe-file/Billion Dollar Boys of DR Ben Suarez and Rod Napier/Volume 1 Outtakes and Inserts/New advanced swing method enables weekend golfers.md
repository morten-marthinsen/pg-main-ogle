By JERRY HEARD
PGA Golf Professional,

Over five years ago we
revolutionalized golf for
thousands of people across
the country. Because many
weekend golfers may have
missed our announcements I
have decided to again publish
this notice.
After years of intense
research and experimenta-
tion, my staff and I have
finally developed a golf
swing that I believe not only
produces the longest and
most accurate driver, wood
and iron hits ever but also is
the quickest and easiest to
master.
We have named this new
advancement "The Heard
Super Swing."
Not only have I witnessed
the amazing results of many
amateurs who have tried this
swing technique, but I con-
tinue to receive unbelievable
letters of thanks.
The Heard Super Swing
was developed from a num-
ber of sources.
You are probably well
aware of the swing method
used by Mo Norman that has
been widely publicized in the
golf magazines. There is also
a major golf school that inde-
pendently and scientifically
developed the type of swing
used by Mo Norman. This
type of golf swing is based on
the naturalization of the golf
swing that better adapts to the
human body.
But we found this more
natural golf swing, although
much easier to learn, still had
its drawbacks with regard to
distance and several other
factors concerning the ability
to work the ball left to right
and up and down.
We have also found major
flaws in the standard golf
swing which is generally
referred to as the "Vardon
Grip" golf swing.
The "Vardon Grip," as you
know, has the club being held
in the crease where the fin-
gers meet the palm in the left
hand. The right hand is then
crossed extensively over the
left hand and then the last fin-
ger of the right and first fin-
ger of the left are interlocked
or overlapped. The problem
with the "Vardon Grip" is
that it causes unnatural swing
motions with the human
anatomy and causes the
swing to come in on multiple
planes, which unnecessarily
over-complicates the swing
making it difficult to master.
Most amateurs never master
the "Vardon Grip" swing and
seldom improve their game
no matter how many years
they play.
But we found that the new
natural swing also had major
flaws. The stance, address
and length of the backswing
caused distance and control
problems as mentioned.
We modified the natural-
type swing and we have
added in new advancements
to the golf swing that we had
been experimenting with.
What we came up with,
we believe, is the ultimate
golf swing, The Heard Super
Swing. We have been able to
teach many amateur golfers
this swing in a matter of min-
utes. I have seen driver,
wood and iron shots improve
almost instantly. They were
able to master this swing in a
matter of days and in some
cases in a matter of hours.
With The Heard Super
Swing, the distance and accu-
racy of the wood and iron
shots of these amateur golfers
improved so substantially
that I was literally shocked.
One amateur golfer I taught
used to drive the ball only
150 to 180 yards with numer-
ous far left or right shots and
numerous ground balls.
After being taught The Heard
Super Swing, the same golfer
started hitting the ball
straight down the middle,
200-to 250 yards, and over
250 yards in some cases. I
have seen some amateur
golfers drive the ball 270
yards using The Heard Super
Swing.
I saw these same amateurs
hit iron shots that were
sprayed all over the place,
with hits going sometimes
low and sometimes high.
After being taught The Heard
Super Swing, they quickly
started hitting the ball
straight, high and holding the
green with a backspin.
Although The Heard
Super Swing is the most
advanced and most effective
swing ever developed, it is
simple to use. You never
think of more than one thing
at a time. There is a certain
thing you do to modify your
grip, a certain thing you do to
modify your stance and it's
an easy change from the tra-
ditional swing. It's as easy as
1-2-3, and that's it.
