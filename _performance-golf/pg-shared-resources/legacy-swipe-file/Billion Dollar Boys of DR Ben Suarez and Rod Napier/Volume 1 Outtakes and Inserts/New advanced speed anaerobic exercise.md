(Canton, OH) If you want to
lose weight slowly, do aerobic exer-
cise. If you want to lose weight fast,
do anaerobic exercise.
Many studies have shown that
anaerobic exercise burns more
calories and thus more fat than
aerobic exercise. But, a number of
recent studies by major universities
have proven that anaerobic exer-
cise actually burns more than
450% more calories than aerobic
exercise after only 15 hours (see
Rgure 1). But then anaerobic exer-
cise keeps on burning calories after
15 hours for reasons that will be
discussed shortly, which yield over
5 times more calories burned by
anaerobic exercise than aerobic
exercise.
Also, aerobic exercise can typi-
cally bum 25% muscle and 75% fat
for body energy. Anaerobic exer-
cise bums 100% fat for body ener-
gy. The reason for this will be given
shortly.
Because of its many calorie
burning attributes, one hour of
anaerobic exercise is worth more
than 5 hours of aerobic exercise to
burn calories and thus fat and
anaerobic exercise brings you
many other fitness and health ben-
efits that aerobic exercise does not.
Actually, in many cases, anaer-
obic exercise can burn as much as
7 times more calories than aerobic
exercise if you match the way
many typical people do aerobic
exercises versus doing proper
anaerobic exercises. And there is
yet another factor that could cause
anaerobic exercise to bum over 10
times more fat than aerobic exer-
cise.

between aerobic and anaerobic
exercise is.this: Aerobic exercise is
that which causes the body to uti-
lize oxygen to create energy.
Anaerobic exercise is that which
causes the body to create energy
without oxygen because the
demand for energy is so fast and
large that the body must create it
from numerous natural body chem-
icals. Aerobic exercise is generally
associated with non-resistance
exercises such as aerobic classes,
jogging, treadmills, ski machines,
air gliders, exercise bikes and vari-
ous sports such as racquetball.
Anaerobic exercise is associated
with resistance exercise especially
using weights which in turn con-
sumes many calories and builds
muscle and bones and in the
process also rejuvenates organs.
But for the person that wants to
lose weight, it is anaerobic exercise
that will give them the most fat
burned for every minute of exercise
versus aerobic exercise.
A[so, it is a myth that anaerobic
exercise done with weights will put
bulky male-type muscles on
women. Women do not have the
hormones for that to happen. The
female bodybuilders seen on TV
bodybuilding contests get those
muscles by taking hormonal sup-
plements and doing super exten-
sive weight training that is not nec-
essary to achieve weight loss and
a well-shaped body. Women can
add muscle without increasing size
because muscle weighs so much
more than fat. Women will actually
reduce their size.
Anaerobic exercise fatigues and
breaks down some of the muscle
and stresses bones. This causes
muscle and bones to build or
rebuild and become stronger. First
of all the body must rebuild the bro-
ken down muscle and then it actu-
ally strengthens the muscle and the
bones because it perceives that the
body will need extra strength in the
future. The process of building and
rebuilding new muscle and bone
requires a substantial amount of
calories. It is the same situation
that exists in children and adoles-
cents who are constantly building
new muscle, bone and organs and
therefore, can consume a great
deal more calories and not gain
weight.
Professional bodybuilders, for
example, can jump the amount of
calories they burn per day from
2,500 calories a day to over 9,000
calories per day in some cases due
to anaerobic exercise.
But the benefits of anaerobic
exercise do not stop there. The
building of muscle and bone
requires organs to engage in extra
performance which in turn revital-
izes the organs. The building of
muscle and bone also requires the
body to produce youth related hor-
mones. Besides building muscle
and bone, these youth-related hor-
mones, as documented in scientific
studies from the University of
California, San Diego, also
increase the sex drive and provide
better sexual performance. T';
a
mind also benefits in that people
who do anaerobic exercise also
have been shown to have better
mental performance according to
scientific studies.
On the other side of the coin,
people who do not engage in
anaerobic exercise as they get
older will lose muscle, lose bone
mass, gain fat and experience the
deterioration of internal organs and
the cardiovascular system. A new
Tufts University study found that
the average woman after the age
of 30, every 10 years loses 5
pounds of.muscle, loses 10% of
her bone mass and gains 10
pounds of fat. By age 50, the aver-
age woman is nearly 50% body fat
and has lost 20% of her bone
mass.
The Tufts study also found that
you are never too old to get anaer-
obic exercise benefits. It was found
that anaerobic exercise increased
the muscles and bone density of
nursing home patients in their 80s
and 90s, increased their strength
by 175% and many of them got rid
of their walkers.
Another reason anaerobic exer-
cise is so important with regard to
losing excess fat is this: When you
diet and do not do anaerobic exer-
cise, the body is designed to per-
ceive that it is fat, not muscle, that
will be needed in the future and the
body actually starts burning a sub-
stantial amount of muscle. When
you do anaerobic exercise, the
body perceives that it is muscle
that it will need in the future and
starts burning excess fat at a very
high rate. This is why aerobic exer-
cise usually bums 25% muscle and
75% fat while anaerobic exercise
bums 100% fat.
