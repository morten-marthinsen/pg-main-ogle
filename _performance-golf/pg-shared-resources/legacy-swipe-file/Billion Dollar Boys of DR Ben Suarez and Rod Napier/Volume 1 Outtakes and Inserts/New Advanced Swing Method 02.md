# enables weekend golfers to learn in 30


# minutes to hit 230 to 270 yard drives,

# And Dramatically improves wood and iron play, too.

By Jerry Heard
PGA Golf Professional
South Seas Plantation
(Captiva Island, FL)
I am writing to notify golfers
about a recent development here at
The Heard Golf Academy that I
believe is the biggest advancement in
golf in the past 50 years. After years
of intense research and experimenta­
tion, I have finally developed a golf
swing that I believe not only pro­
duces the longest and most accurate
driver, wood and iron hits ever but
also is the quickest and easiest to
master.
We have named this new
advancement ‘The Heard Super
Swing.”
I am personally testifying to you
today that this new advanced swing
has dramatically improved my game,
even though my old swing carried me
to many PGA Tour championships. I
have also personally witnessed that it
has dramatically improved the game
of many amateur golfers to whom we
have taught it here at the Heard Golf
Academy.
The Heard Super Swing was
developed from a number of sources.
You are probably well aware of
the new swing method used by Mo
Norman that has been widely publi­
cized in the golf magazines. There is
also a major golf school that indepen­
dently and scientifically developed
the type of swing used by Mo
Norman. This type of golf swing is
based on the naturalization of the golf
swing that better adapts to the human
body.
But we found this more natural
golf swing, although much easier to
learn, still had its drawbacks with
regard to distance and several other
factors concerning the ability to work
the ball left to right and up and down.
The Heard Golf Academy has
also been working on our own new
advanced golf swing over the past 5
years. We have also found major
flaws in the standard golf swing
which is generally referred to as the
“Vardon Grip” golf swing.
The ‘‘Vardon Grip,” as you know,
has the club being held in the crease
where the fingers meet the palm in
the left hand. The right hand is then
crossed extensively over the left hand
and then the last finger of the right
and first finger of the left are inter­
locked or overlapped. The problem
with the ‘‘Vardon Grip” is that it caus­
es unnatural swing motions with the
human anatomy and causes the swing
to come in on multiple planes, which
unnecessarily over-complicates the
swing making it difficult to master.
Most amateurs never master the
‘‘Vardon Grip” swing and seldom
improve their game no matter how
many years they play.
But we found that the new natural
swing also had major flaws. The
stance, address and length of the
backswing caused distance and con­
trol problems as mentioned.
For the past 2 years, we have
modified the natural-type swing and
we have added in new advancements
to the golf swing that we had been
experimenting with over the past 5
years.
What we came up with, we
believe, is the ultimate golf swing,
The Heard Super Swing. We have
been able to teach many amateur
golfers this swing in a matter of min­
utes. I have seen driver, wood and
iron shots improve almost instantly.
They were able to master this swing
in a matter of days and in some cases
in a matter of hours.
With The Heard Super Swing, the
distance and accuracy of the wood
and iron shots of these amateur
golfers improved so substantially that
I was literally shocked. One amateur
golfer I taught used to drive the ball
only 150 to 180 yards with numerous
far left or right shots and numerous
ground balls. After being taught The
Heard Super Swing, the same golfer
started hitting the ball straight down
the middle, 200 yards to 250 yards
and over 250 yards in some cases. I
have seen some amateur golfers drive
the ball 270 yards using The Heard
Super Swing.
I saw these same amateurs hit
iron shots that were sprayed all over
the place, with hits going sometimes
low and sometimes high. After being
taught The Heard Super Swing, they
quickly started hitting the ball
straight, high and holding the green
with a backspin.
Although The Heard Super
Swing is the most advanced and most
effective ever developed, it is simple
to use. You never think of more than
one thing at a time. There is a certain
thing you do to modify your grip, a
certain thing you do to modify your
stance and an easy change to the tra­
ditional swing. It s as easy as 1-2-3,
and that’s it.
Here are just a few testimonials:
This is going to be hard to
believe, but it is the truth. I received
your Video and instruction on
Wednesday, played golf that day
(Wednesday) with my old swing and
shot 105, my handicap is 27. Then,
after watching the video and reading
the instruction Wednesday evening, I
went to the driving range Thursday
and hit a bag of balls, then played
golf on Friday using the new swing
and shot 89, also with my first hole in
one in sixty years. The change was
almost unbelievable if I wasn’t doing
it myself.
