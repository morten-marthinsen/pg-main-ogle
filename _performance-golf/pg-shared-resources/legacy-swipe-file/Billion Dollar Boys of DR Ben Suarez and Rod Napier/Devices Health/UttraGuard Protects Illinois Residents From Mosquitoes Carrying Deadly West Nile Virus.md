alike. There are actually over

States.
Next, mosquito adults feed on
all sorts of (lower nectar, juices

Plus, people are not always
the primary source for these
blood meals. Horses, cattle,
birds, cats and dogs to name a
few are also acceptable sources
of blood for these female mos-
