President James Hull said the
union's most recent offer was
essentially the same as its previ-
ous proposals.
"And there was no screaming or
yelling," Hull added
Zumbrook acknowledged that the
union's latest offer still proposes
salary hikes of 5, 5 and 6 percent,
as well as step increases based on
seniority, over three years
Hull said the school board is
sticking by its offer of a 12 percent
hike, including the step increases,
spread over three years.
But he also said the teachers
union must drop the handful of
unfair labor practice claims it has
filed with the state as part of any
settlement, a point Zumbrook said
the union cannot agree to
The school board has reopened
Round Lake High School for
juniors and seniors and kinder-
garten through third-grade classes
using a combination of substitutes,
administrators and teachers who
have crossed the picket line to
teach classes

last day teachers could have come
back without pushing the school
calendar past June 30
