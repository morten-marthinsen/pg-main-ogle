and ingestible pain reliev­
ers on the market?
A. Typical ingestible pain
relief drugs only relieve pain
by temporarily interrupting
the pain impulse in the
nerves. They do not go to the
source of the pain. These
pain relief drugs can produce
harmful side effects like nau­
sea, bleeding and internal tis­
sue damage.
PainNOT™ works by
going to the source of the pain
and the cause. Most pain is
caused by inflammation which
then causes so much swelling
that it puts pressure on the
nerves. PainNot™ quickly
reduces the inflammation and
thus reduces the pain.
When there is excessive
inflammation, blood flow is
reduced to the area. When
PainNOT™ reduces the
inflammation, blood flow
increases to the damaged area
which also speeds up healing.
