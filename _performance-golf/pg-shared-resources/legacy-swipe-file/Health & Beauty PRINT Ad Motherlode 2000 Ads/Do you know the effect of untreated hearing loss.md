>> When a person loses the ability to
hear, a breakdown in communication
occurs in the brain.
>> The ear becomes unable to send
accurate information to the brain
thereby affecting a person’s ability
to understand what is being “heard”.
>> Left untreated, prolonged hearing
loss results in the deterioration of
the part of the brain that processes
sounds.
>> Hearing aids stimulate brain activity
and prevents auditory deprivation.
