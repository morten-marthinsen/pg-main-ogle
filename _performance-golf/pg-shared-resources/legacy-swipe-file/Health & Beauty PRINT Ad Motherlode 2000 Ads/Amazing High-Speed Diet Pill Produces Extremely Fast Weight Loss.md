PEACH OF A PEACH CAKE
I package white cake mix
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
I teaspoon baking powder
3 eggs
I (19-ounce) can peach pie fill­
ing
1/2 teaspoon lemon extract
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
Topping:
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon ground cinna­

1/4 cup butter, softened
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Grease a 13x9x2-inch pan.
In a large mixing bowl, combine
cake mix, flour, baking powder,
eggs, pie filling, lemon extract and
nuts. Beat on medium speed for 2
minutes or until well blended.
Spread batter evenly in prepared
pan.
To prepare the topping, in a
small bowl, combine sugar, flour
and cinnamon. With a pastry
blender or fork, cut in butter until
crumbly. Sprinkle evenly over bat­
ter.
Bake 40-45 minutes or until a

out clean. Cool at least 30 minutes
in pan on wire rack before cutting.
Makes 16 servings.
