# Feeling Tired, Stressed or Fatigued?

By: Dr. Queenie Mei,
DC, CTN and Dipt Acup
Do you feel stressed, tired, irritable and overwhelmed?
Do you feel like you're aging fast and are wondering
what you can do to feel less stressed? The adrenal
glands are important organs that help you deal with
aging and stress.
ADRENAL GLANDS are two small glands, the size
of a walnut, that sit on top of each kidney. Adrenal
glands secrete a hormone called Cortisol. Hormones are
substances that are produced by many different organs
in your body and they communicate with each other so
the body can function properly.
WHAT ARE THE FUNCTIONS OF THE
ADRENAL GLANDS?
Energy Production and Quality of Sleep - Do you
need caffeine to help you start your day? Do you have
trouble falling asleep, or keep waking up during the
night?
• Adrenal glands produce the highest cortisol level
in the morning and lowest at night (See Figure I).
Poor adrenal function can cause low cortisol level in
the morning and as a result an individual has poor
energy in the morning.
• When you start your day feeling tired, your body
will try to produce more cortisol. High cortisol
values at night will occur as a result and interrupt
sleep quality. Lack of good quality of sleep can
reduce the body’s ability to repair, mental vitality
and vigor, leading to depression.
