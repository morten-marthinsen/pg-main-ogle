reality, the surface of the skin is a very important bio sensor. The stratum

the bacteria world. It also controls the storage and balance of water within
it's tissues. On a cosmetic level it is what the world sees. The condition of
your stratum corneum is what gives your age away.

prevent moisture loss While these types of products may give the skin a
temporary nicer appearance, they do nothing to directly hydrate the skin.
