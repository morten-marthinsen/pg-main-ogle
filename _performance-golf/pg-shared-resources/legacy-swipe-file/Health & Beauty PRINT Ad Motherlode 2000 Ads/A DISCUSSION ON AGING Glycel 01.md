quality of life has long been one of my primary interests.
''For in all my years of clinical experience I have found that the
effects of aging can be devastating.
''Unlike a defect in the heart, the manifestation of aging is readily
apparent to everyone.
''It is something that cannot be satisfactorily hidden. From your-
