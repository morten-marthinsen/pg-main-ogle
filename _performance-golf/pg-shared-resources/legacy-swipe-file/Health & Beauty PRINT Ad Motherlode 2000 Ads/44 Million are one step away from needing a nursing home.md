# OPINION

ORLANDO, FLA.—
This is hardly breaking news to orthopedic
surgeon Dr. Charles T. Price. “Every year,
500,000 people are hospitalized with bone
injuries, and I am surprised it’s not more than
that,” says Dr. Price.
According to the U.S. Surgeon General,
half of women and one quarter of men over 50
years have below optimum bone health, and
consequences of poor bone health may lead to a
downward spiral in physical and mental health.
Improving low bone mass is easier said than
done, but a recent discovery by Dr. Price and
a handpicked team of orthopedic specialists at
the Institute of Better Bone Health promises to
change all of that.
Dr. Price has discovered that certain forms
of silicon, when combined with the right bone-
friendly nutrients, can help transform the health
of bones; making them harder, stronger, and
more resilient.
It is a subject that he knows much about.
Family Tragedy
A few years ago, a tragedy occurred in Dr.
Price’s family.
His mother fell and broke her hip, which trig-
gered a downward spiral in her physical health.
Ten months later, she passed away from
complications caused by her injury.
“When my mother fell and broke her hip, I
knew she was in deep trouble,” he says.
Not too long after her passing, a still-grieving
Dr. Price received more bad news.
His wife of 43 years was also developing low
bone density and might be headed down the
same path.
He knew it would be physically impossible
for him to watch over her and prevent falls, but
there was one thing he could do.
As an experienced researcher and orthopedic
surgeon, he would devote his time and talents to
helping his wife’s bone health without resorting
to drugs that can have serious side effects.
In fact, the U.S. Surgeon General’s report
recommends “focusing on lifestyle changes,
including physical activity and nutrition” to
support optimal bone health.
Dr. Price encouraged his wife to exercise
regularly, and consume a healthier diet while
he began a quest for a superior bone health
supplement.
It was a daunting task, He knew he couldn’t
do it by himself, so he enlisted four of the
brightest, most brilliant doctors and surgeons in
the field, and formed a prestigious brain trust,
the Institute for Better Bone Health.
Calcium Alone is
Not Enough for Optimal Bone Health
“We knew that calcium and vitamin D were
important, but by themselves, they were not
enough,” says Dr. Price.
At the time, he was doing research with
advanced bone graft materials.
One of the substances was silicon.
He was amazed at what he saw. “Silicon was
making a tremendous difference in bone health.”
Amazing Bone-Friendly Discovery
“In fact, our bodies need silicon and bone
can’t form without silicon,” he says.
But not any silicon will do, says Dr. Price
because the silicon in meteorites, like silicon
that’s in sand or rocks can’t be digested.
The right kind of silicon is needed to get into
the body in order to attract calcium to the bone
and strengthen the protein in bone.
An Irresistible Attraction
Dr. Price discovered that only some types of
silicon can be used by the body.
Although some foods and beverages have a
useful form of silicon, the typical American diet
doesn’t provide enough for optimum bone health.
“Men and women who consume greater
amounts of silicon have better bone density than
men and women who don’t.” says Dr. Price.
With their target in sight, the specialists at
the Institute for Better Bone Health set about
developing a totally new formula; one that provides
an abundance of healthy silicon along with key bone
health nutrients to provide multi-faceted support to
fortify bones.
Tough, Durable Bones
The end result was a groundbreaking
proprietary formula called Silical ® that contains
beneficial amounts of bone-nourishing silicon,
magnesium, calcium, and vitamin D.
Further research led to the addition of vitamin
K2, which is crucial for bone toughness, adds Dr.
Price.
“We also added boron to help improve the
retention of magnesium and calcium,” says Dr.
Price, “as well as vitamin C and inositol, because
studies show they are also important for bone
health.”
Since Silical ® was introduced into the market,
its impact has been profound.
Former schoolteacher and avid gardener,
62-year-old Susan M. is a prime example.
Having to live with poor bone health was
simply unacceptable to Susan.
“I stayed active and exercised my entire life,
but I knew I could do more” she says.
She tried calcium and vitamin D.
Then, she started taking Dr. Price’s Silical ®
formula.
In the weeks that followed, she says there was
an overall difference in the way she felt.
“I became more active and confident. A visit
to my doctor was met with amazement.”
“When I started taking Silical ® ,” smiles
Susan, “it felt like my body was starving for
what was in the capsules.”
“It was like a sponge sucking up water.”
Get Active with Healthy Bones
Today, you’ll find Susan chasing down her
grandkids at the park, or taking power-strolls
with her friends.
“I tell everyone to stay active, to exercise and
enjoy every day like it’s your last.”
“If it wasn’t for Silical ® , I feel like my life
wouldn’t be much fun at all,” she laughs.
Lynne D., 73 years old from Tucson,
Arizona, wanted to do all that she could to
support her bone health.
She read a newspaper article about Dr. Price’s
silicon-based bone health formula and knew that
was the answer. “I started on Silical ® ,” says Lynne.
Five months later, she received some
great news.
“When the report came back,” smiles Lynne,
“my doctor told me to keep doing what I was
doing. I was shocked and thankful!”
