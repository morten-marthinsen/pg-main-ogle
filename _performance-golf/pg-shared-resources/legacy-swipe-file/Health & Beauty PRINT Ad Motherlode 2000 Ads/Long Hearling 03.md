# Have You Thought About Invisible?

Starkey SoundLens is the first and most popular custom fit Invisible-in-the Canal (IIC) hearing aid ever made. It is
custom fit to your unique ear canal for all day comfort, and rests deep inside your ear where no one can see it.
SoundLens come with Starkey’s innovative features, including the industry’s most proven feedback canceller, and
a noise reduction speech preservation system that is designed to help you hear clearly and easily, even in noisy
environments like restaurants. We can now fit this technology on a wide range of hearing losses and the hearing
aids are available at three technology levels and price points to suit your lifestyle and your budget. Please give our office a
call if you would like to come in for an appointment to discuss this exciting technology.
