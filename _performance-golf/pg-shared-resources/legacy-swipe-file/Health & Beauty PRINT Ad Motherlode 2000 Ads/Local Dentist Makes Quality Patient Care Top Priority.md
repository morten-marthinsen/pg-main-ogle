or the past seven years,
local dentist Luis E. Carrero,
DDS, has made quality
family esthetic dentistry and
excellent patient care his primary
mission at his full-service practice
New Image General & Cosmetic
Dentistry.
At New Image's location on
Bloomingdale Avenue in Valrico
(near Bloomingdale High School),
Dr. Carrero continues to provide
exceptional dental care for area
residents of Valrico, Brandon,
Riverview, Lakeland and the
surrounding Tampa areas. The
dentistry welcomes new patients
of all ages and treats emergencies
promptly.
"We value family and the
importance of maintaining and
restoring health," says Dr. Carrero.
"We treat our patients as if they're
part of our extended family."
The family practice offers a wide
range of treatment options that
can be personalized to meet your
unique needs and expectations.
Their comprehensive dental
services include preventative
care, like dental cleanings,
regular checkups and X-rays;
non-mercury fillings; nitrous and
oral sedation; cosmetic crowns
and veneers; smile restoration
and enhancement treatments,
such as teeth whitening,
bonding, bridges and dentures;
periodontal treatment; and root
canals. New Image also utilizes
the partnership of a local, highly-
qualified Prosthodontist to offer
implants at the practice.
"We're not the type of dentistry
that routinely pushes patients out
the door to see other specialists,"
Dr. Carrero explains. "We try to
take care of all of your dental care
needs under one roof."
Dr. Carrero and his highly-
trained, personable team
focus on making your dental
experience as comfortable as
possible. They will help you better
understand the cause of your
current dental condition and help
you develop a long-term plan to
ultimately achieve a higher sense
of well-being and self-esteem
by enhancing your appearance,
comfort and ability to sustain
outstanding dental health.
The first step toward achieving
a beautiful, healthy smile is to
schedule an appointment. To do
so, or for more information, call
New Image today at (813) 436-
9813. Visit the dentistry online at
NewImageGeneralDentistry.com.
New Images accepts most forms
of insurance.
