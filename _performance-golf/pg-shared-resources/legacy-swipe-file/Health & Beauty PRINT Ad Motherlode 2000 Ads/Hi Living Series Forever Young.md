1. Eat yourself young

such as fish.
2. Choose a healthy lifestyle
Most of the life-threatening diseases that
can develop with age are the result of an
unhealthy lifestyle. Stress, lack of sleep, poor
diet, excessive alcohol intake, smoking and
sunbathing can accelerate the ageing of your
vital organs as well as your skin, especially facial
skin. The good news is that all of these are
within your control and thus preventable!
