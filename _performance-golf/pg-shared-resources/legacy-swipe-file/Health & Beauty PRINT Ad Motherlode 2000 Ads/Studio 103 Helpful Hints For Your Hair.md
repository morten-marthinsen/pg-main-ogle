WHEN it comes to talking about Mount
Carmel Clinic’s second annual Car Rally
for Kids, coordinator Carolyn Rickeyisn’t
saying much at all.
It’s not that she doesn’t want to talk
about the event, which is designed to
raise money for the clinic that offers a
variety of health and social programs to
residents of north Winnipeg. And it’s not
that she doesn’t want to share the excite-
ment she feels for what will surely be a
fun and memorable event.
It’s the details surrounding the rally
itself that compels her to be close-
mouthed.
“I don’t want to give too much away,”
she laughs.
As coordinator of the event, it was
Rickey’s task to develop the rally that will
have registered participants and their
cars driving all over north Winnipeg
completing various tasks, collecting items
and solving riddles.
Rickey says she’s already given the
rally a test run by monitoring the
progress of two of her associates. She
says she found that it went according to
plan — almost.
