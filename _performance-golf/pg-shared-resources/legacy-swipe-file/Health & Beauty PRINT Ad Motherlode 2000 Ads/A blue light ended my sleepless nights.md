Chop up watermelon into
cubes and sprinkle with mint or
basil leaves for a spike of flavor.

For a surprising and sweet

pitted black olives. These
unexpected but complementary

any party.

## Tip3

lime juice and white rum; strain
over ice for a pink mojito (below).

vodka for rum, combine with ice in
a shaker, strain, and you have a
watermelon martini.
