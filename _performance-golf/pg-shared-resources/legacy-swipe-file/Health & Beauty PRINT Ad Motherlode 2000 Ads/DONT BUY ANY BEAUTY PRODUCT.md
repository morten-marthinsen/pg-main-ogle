## What is Collagen? How Does it Affect Skin?

Collagen is a type of protein, fibrous In nature, that connects and supports other body tissues,
such as skin, bone, tendons, muscles, and cartilage It also supports the internal organs and is even
present in teeth. There are more than 25 types of collagen that occur naturally in the body
Collagen is one ot the most plentiful proteins present in the bodies of mammals. Including humans
In fact, it makes up about 25-30% of the total amount of proteins in the body. Collagen is sometimes
considered the glue that holds the body together Without It, the body would literally tall apart
Regular protein foods or protein powders do not contain enough collagen components, such as
the amino acids glycine and proline. Ingesting a lot of collagen from gelatin is benettcial to provide
building blocks for collagen In the body However, supplementing with Collagen Revive is essential
because this ingredient has special properties ot stimulating collagen production in the body
Often, collagen is discussed in relation to the skin. It works with keratin to provide the skin with
strength, flexibility end resilience
As people age, however, collagen degradation occurs, leading to wrinkles and facial sagging.
Therefore, collagen is an important substance to counter the visible effects of aging on the skin.
