Plastic surgery, specifically breast

Many women who consult with us want

Mothers who consult with us sometimes

It’s important to be realistic about your
expectations if you considering breast
enhancement. You want proportionate,

Once you decide you want breast
augmentation surgery, there are two choices for
implants: silicone gel-filled or saline filled.
Silicone gel-filled implants are filled with
silicone gel, a semi-solid. Saline-filled implants
are filled with salt water, the same kind used in
I.V. fluids. Each has its benefits, depending on
your body and personal preferences.
Implant placement is another decision. Your
breast shape and lifestyle may determine which
implant position is best for you.
Just be sure you communicate your goals
with your surgeon, and together you can make
the right decision for you.
