got together, occured during the 10th annual Truly family reunion held recently at The Frostburg
Community Park. Jones, seated, is shown holding her great-great-granddaughter. Standing, from
left, are Brian Lewis Clark, Jones' great-grandson; Donna Lee Clark, daughter; and Victor Clark Jr.,
grandson, all of Barton.
