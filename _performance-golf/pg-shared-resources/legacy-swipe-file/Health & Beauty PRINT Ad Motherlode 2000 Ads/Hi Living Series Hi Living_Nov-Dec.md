7. Balance emotions
Your emotions have a direct effect on how

Make sure you look after yourself by

a harmonious space in your home so that
you can retreat to it when you need to.
Have an aromatherapy bath, listen to some
relaxing music or chant. Do whatever
nurtures your mind, body and spirit!
