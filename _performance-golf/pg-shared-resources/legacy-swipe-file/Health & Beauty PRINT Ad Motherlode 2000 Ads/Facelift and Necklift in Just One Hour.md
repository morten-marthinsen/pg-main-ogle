What is a One Hour Facelift? "The One Hour
Facelift & Necklift combines the latest advances
in non-surgical technology with the safest,
most effective minimally invasive suspension
techniques. The goal of the procedure is to lift
the cheeks, jowls and neck which have fallen
over time. These techniques achieve the results
of a full face and necklift in about an hour while
enabling most patients to return to work the
day afterthe procedure. Because this procedure
lifts the Skin, Muscle and Adipose (or fat) with
ReducedTension in about an hour, we call it the
One Hour SMART Facelift'.'
How does this compare to other facelift
procedures? "Most facelift procedures
require incisions and stitches around the ear
-- significant downtime is usually associated
with these procedures. Limited incision
procedures, such as endoscopic facelifts, still
result in substantial swelling. To make things
more confusing, some surgeons (who are not
even certified by the American Board of Plastic
Surgery) are promoting "Lifestyle" facelift
procedures which are actually mini-facelift
procedures and require incisions around the
ear which can leave visible scars. In contrast,
the One Hour SMART Facelift eliminates the
incisions and downtime associated with these
and other traditional facelift procedures. And,
because there is limited tension on the skin, we
can avoid a pulled or wind-swept appearance'.'
Who performs the One Hour Facelift?
"Patients should always consider having their
facelift performed by a surgeon who is certified
by the American Board of Plastic Surgery and
who routinely performs many different types
of facial rejuvenation procedures. The last thing
you want to do is to have your facelift performed
by someone who is not highly experienced in
this area. And, I would avoid going to a surgeon
who performs only one procedure (such as
a "Lifestyle" procedure), because you may not
be the best candidate for the single procedure
they offer. Ideally, your surgeon should spend
a significant amount of time evaluating your
concerns and desires while educating you
about the available options'.'
Who are the typical patients? "I have
performed the One Hour SMART Facelift on
men and women ranging from 35 years old
to 70 years old. Because the procedure is
affordable and has very little downtime, it
appeals to many individuals. Typical patients
include homemakers, school teachers, doctors,
attorneys and public figures'.'
How long does the procedure last? "Our
experience with hundreds of patients over the
past decade has demonstrated that patients
enjoy good results for 5-10 years'.'
How is the One Hour Facelift & Necklift
procedure different than a regular
facelift? "Because patients experience
little to no discomfort, there is no need for
a general anesthetic. This greatly reduces
both the risks and costs associated with the
procedure. In fact, most patients are able to
engage in normal activities such as driving
immediately afterward'.'
