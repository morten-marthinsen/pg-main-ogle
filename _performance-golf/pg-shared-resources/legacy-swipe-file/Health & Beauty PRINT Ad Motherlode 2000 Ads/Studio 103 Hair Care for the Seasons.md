# Hair Care for the Seasons

Sponsored by Studio 103
by KEN CORI
Your hair reacts differently under
different conditions. In the winter
when the air is cold it is also drier.
Thus our hair seems dry and fly
away and we get static in the hair.
This isn’t to say that your hair is
necessarily dry. In order to
maintain control over your hair
you have to adjust your products
or change the products you use to
offset these conditions. The creme
rinse, leave-in conditioners, or
even deep-conditioning may not
seem to help. There are products
we use and sell to help control
your hair in cold weather
conditions.
In the summer your hair usually
feels better because the humidity
in the warmer air reacts with your
hair to make it feel softer and you
have more control. Although your
hair feels better in the summer,
you must use products to protect
your hair against some of the other
elements such as sun, wind or
water (especially pool water). If
you spend time in the sun, using
products with sun screen will
dramatically reduce sun damage in
If you have perms on a regular
basis, you might have noticed that
some perms seem to be better
than others even though your
stylist is using the same perm.
The reason is weather. In the
winter, the perm appears
somewhat drier but reacts better
in the summer.
Tinted, hi-lited, bleached and
permed hair always need more
care and conditioning than virgin
hair which has no chemical
treatments. So make sure you are
using the proper conditioners and
which case your hair will be in
much better condition for the
winter. If you are a swimmer, the
chlorine in pools can also damage
your hair so you must use products
to protect against chlorine.
