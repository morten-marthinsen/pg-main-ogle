of today have abandoned their
traditional child-rearing role in favor
of making their way in the
workplace—right?
Wrong, according to the authors
of Whafs a Smart Woman Like

Dittmer and Cheri Loveless, who
pooled their talents and their
experiences to produce this 192-

housewife or in arranging their lives

generation."
