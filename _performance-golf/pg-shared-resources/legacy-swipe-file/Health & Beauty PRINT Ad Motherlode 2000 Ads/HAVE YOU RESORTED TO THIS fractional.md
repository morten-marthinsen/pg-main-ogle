(cid:127) Running or jogging: Warm up with a 3- to 5-min-

(cid:127) Swimming: Practice various styles of swimming.

ner knee, hip and back problems.
(cid:127) Surfing: Use the proper equipment. Consider us-

which can prevent contact injuries or cushion the blow
from a wipeout.
(cid:127) Bicycling: Bring the bicycle in for a proper fit. Han-

individual, to avoid both long- and short-term injuries.
(cid:127) Basketball: Proper basketball shoes are key. Sneak-
