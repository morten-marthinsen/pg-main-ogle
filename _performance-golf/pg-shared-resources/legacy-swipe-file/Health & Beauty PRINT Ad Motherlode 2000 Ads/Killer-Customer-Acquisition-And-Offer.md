## JACK A. MARKELL TRAIL

LOCATION
Wilmington to New
Castle
DISTANCE
7.9 miles
Completed in 2018, this greenway
connects downtown Wilmington’s
popular Riverwalk to the historic
town of New Castle. The Jack A.
Markell Trail features a 2,300-foot
boardwalk over wetlands in the
Russell Peterson Wildlife Refuge
as well as a pedestrian- and
cyclists-only arched bridge across
the Christina River. It proceeds
through underpasses and forests
along a former rail corridor to
the cobblestone streets of New
Castle.
