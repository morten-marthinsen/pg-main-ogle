One of the most significant advances in skin
rejuvenation in decades looks set to make

who have developed a new technology capable
of regenerating skin in just a matter of days using
stem cells.
Up until recently the use of stem cells was
shrouded with ethical concerns, as the focus has
been on embryonic stem cells which can only be
collected from unborn foetuses.
But a recent advancement in the field has shifted
the focus from embryonic stem cells to adult
stem cells.
“Unlike ordinary cells which have a limited life
span, adult stem cells can reproduce an infi nite
number of times and act as a repair system
for the body.” Explains leading Bio-Scientist,
Dr. Curtis Crasto.
“What’s really exciting is that this has catapulted
stem cell technology to a new level.”
Possible conditions that might benefit from stem
cells are skin conditions, diabetes, heart disease,
cancer, spinal cord injury, and genetic diseases.
One revolutionary new process is the treating of
burns victims. Stem cells harvested from a small
patch of healthy skin, are put into a solution and
sprayed back on to the affected area. Within days,
doctors conducting the study reported that severe
burns had been completely healed.
And this is only the tip of the iceberg in terms of
stem cell development.
“This breakthrough stem cell technology
could be the holy grail of skin regeneration.”
Dr. Crasto confi rms.

CELLUGEN ® Ultra Night Repair is based upon

The Malus Domestica apple is unique in that it
possesses amazing regenerative properties. Unlike
ordinary apples that turn brown and shrivel once
picked, the Malus Domestica apple stays fresh for
weeks and actually repairs itself.
When scientists discovered the rare Swiss apple’s
secret to its prolonged youth lay in its resilient stem
cells, they set out to discover what effect it could
have on human skin.
But first, they had to overcome a problem… it is
impossible to incorporate live genetic material into
cosmetic creams.
As a result, the scientists developed award winning
technology to enable them to cultivate an extract
from the apple’s powerful long living stem cells.
The results were nothing short of astounding.
And now, this phyto stem cell extract looks set
to unlock the youth code.
Scientifically proven to reduce wrinkle depth &
improve skin health, studies have confi rmed the
Malus Domestica apple’s stem cells have the same
repairing and protecting effect on human cells.
In one clinical trial by Mibelle Biochemistry in
Switzerland, subjects applied the Malus Domestica
extract to the crow’s feet around their eyes, and
after just four weeks, the result was a signifi cant
reduction in wrinkle depth and size in 100% of
test subjects.
In further laboratory tests, when the extract
was applied to human stem cells, it actually
increased the number of stem cells present by
a staggering 80%.
That means the Malus Domestica apple extract
helped to grow new cells, and new cells produce
collagen and elastin, to give skin a soft, elastic feel.
In addition, the rare Swiss apple extract
appeared to protect and repair the human stem
cells from environmental stress such as UV light
and pollution.
