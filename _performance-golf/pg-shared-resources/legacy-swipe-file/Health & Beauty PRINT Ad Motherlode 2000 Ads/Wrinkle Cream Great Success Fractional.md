The teachers' cdhtract has

secretaries, classroom aides and
others. The raises these workers
get this year will be the same as
the teachers'.
Last year, the base salary for a
first-year teacher with a bache-
lor's degree was $22,110. The base
salary for a teacher with 13 years
of experience and a master's
degree was $39,798.
Negotiators from the teachers'
union and the school district met
about 10 times in April, May and
June and then took a break for
vacations and other commitments.
Negotiations resumed Aug. 5.
Tuesday's meeting was the second
session since the break.
The last several teacher con-
tracts were concluded before the
start of school, and both sides
want to continue the tradition,
Mundorf said.
