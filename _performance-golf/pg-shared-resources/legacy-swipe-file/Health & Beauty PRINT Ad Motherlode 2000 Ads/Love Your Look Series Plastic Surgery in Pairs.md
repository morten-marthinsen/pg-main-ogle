# Plastic Surgery in Pairs

It's always more fun to do something
with a friend.
Many of our patients tell us they prefer
having cosmetic surgery about the same
time as a close friend or relative. Some
consider it a bonding experience -a new
and exciting adventure for couples, friends,
mothers and daughters.
Plastic surgery buddies can provide
moral and emotional support before and
after the procedures. Perhaps the most
practical benefit is that they can help each
other while they recover from surgery.
Our patients, it seems, reflect a national
trend. One study reports that in 2005, 34
percent of patients surveyed had plastic
surgery with a companion.
It's not unusual for girlfriends to
have surgery about the same time. Often
they choose to have tummy tucks and/or
breast surgery. They come in together
for consultations before the surgery,
and sometimes they schedule follow-up
appointments together.
Some mothers and daughters schedule
their surgeries at the same time so the
husband/father can help during their
recuperation.
