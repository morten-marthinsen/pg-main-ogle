# PENNY DREADFUL


## Premieres Sunday, May 11, at 10PM on Showtime

Timothy Dalton, Eva Green and Josh Hartnett star in this new
Victorian London–set fantasy series as a mismatched set of
allies battling the forces of darkness. Those forces are made up
of famous fictional villains, including Dr. Victor Frankenstein
(Harry Treadaway), Dorian Gray (Reeve Carney) and characters
from Dracula in one big horror-origins mashup. Dalton plays
a nobleman whose daughter has been kidnapped by a ring of
vampires; Green is his partner, though it’s never quite explained
where she gets her semi-supernatural abilities. Hartnett is a
traveling American with a Wild West show who’s quick with a
six-gun. But while the story starts strong in the first of the two
episodes released for review, it collapses in the second, losing
focus on the most interesting characters to introduce more story
lines. There’s a difference between being spooked and being
confused; hopefully this series will figure it out.
