# Adult softball

Join the El Dorado Hills CSD’s coed or men’s
softball winter leagues. Seven weeks of games fol-
lowed by playoffs for the top four teams are played
at the new Promontory Community Park. Coed
teams play on Tuesdays, Sept. 25-Nov. 13; men’s
leagues play Thursdays, Sept. 27-Nov. 15; and
men’s 35 and older leagues play Fridays, Oct. 12-
Dec. 7. The fee is $405 per team. Registration
opened Monday. The deadline to enter is Friday,
Sept. 14, or when full. Call (916) 933-6624 to reg-
ister or (916) 614-3223 for more information.

# Memorial golf tourney

The first Scott Russell memorial golf tournament is
scheduled for Monday, Sept. 24, at Empire Ranch Golf
Course in Folsom. The four-person scramble tees off at 8
a.m. and all proceeds benefit local youth programs and
scholarships. The entry fee includes golf, cart, range balls,
polo shirt, lunch and raffle prizes. To enter, list players in
your foursome if you have one along with each player’s
shirt size and a check for $150 per person made out to Scott
Russell Family Memorial Trust Fund and send the entry to
Brad Parks, 705-2 East Bidwell St., No. 221, Folsom,
95630. For more information call (916) 302-7110, e-mail
brad_parks@sbcglobal.net. or call the Placerville California
Highway Patrol office. The deadline is Sept. 17.

While vitamins do not generally
reduce wrinkles, the topical vitamin
A-based drugs known as “retinoids”
are the exception. Introduced in the
1970s as an acne treatment, tretinoin
(Retin-Aand Renova) is the most
common retinoid used in skin care. It
demonstrated its ability to fade actinic
keratoses (scaly patches that appear
on sun-exposed older skin) and to
speed th turnover of superficial skin
cells. In 1996, the FDAapproved
Renova, which contains a 0.05%
concentration of tretinoin, as the
first drug to treat wrinkles. Tretinoin
reduces fine lines and wrinkles by
increasing the production of new
collagen. It also stimulates the
production of new blood vessels in
the skin. Additional benefits include
fading of “liver spots”and softening
actinic keratoses.
