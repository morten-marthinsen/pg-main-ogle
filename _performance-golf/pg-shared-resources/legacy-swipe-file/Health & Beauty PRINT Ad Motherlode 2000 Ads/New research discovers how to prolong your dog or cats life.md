# You can extend your dog or cat’s life by 20% to 60% longer

By John Whitacre, Media Service
New research has now
discovered how to extend
your dog or cat’s life by 20%
to 60% and avoid many dis­
abilities.
To find out how, I inter­

Ph.D. Dr. Tsengas has writ­

interview follows.
Q. Dr. Tsengas, how
long is it possible to extend
the life of a dog or cat using
your methods and prod­
ucts?
A. Depending on the dog
or cat, you can increase their
life by 20% to 60% over
average life spans of the
past. We are not extending a
dog’s or cat’s normal life
with these new develop­
ments. Rather, we are
extending their short abnor­
mal life span and allowing
them to live to their full nor­
mal life span.
It was once believed that
dogs and cats, based on their
breed, had a certain life span.
But the data to calculate their
life span was taken from
household pets.
Research has found that
household pets have a shorter
life span because of the same
things that shorten the life
span of humans. The things
that cause the shortening of
life span in both humans and
animals are bad nutrition,
lack of proper exercise, lack
of mental stimulation and
other mental problems.
Q. Let’s start with
nutrition. I have a dog, and
most of my family mem­
bers have both dogs and
cats. The dog and cat food
we buy claims to be totally
balanced in nutrition. Is
that not so?
A. That is definitely not
so. And, humans have the
same problem, even if
they’re eating a so-called
balanced diet. Here is why.
New scientific research
has now found that most
complex animals, which
would include humans, dogs
and cats, need 122 nutrients
in order to live out their life
span and to live a healthy life
span. It is now proven that
the human life span is actual­
ly 120 years old, not 60
years old.
So why do humans and
their pets die at half their
normal life span? One reason
is that most of us get only an
average of 40 of the 122
nutrients needed to live out a
full and healthy life.
Q. But, how do people,
who think they are giving
their pets a balanced diet,
miss out on the other 82
nutrients?
A. Those other 82 nutri­

tude of reasons.
First of all, the soil from
which crops are farmed in
most of the United States has
been depleted of most of the
122 nutrients. Farmers only
replenish about 40 of the 122
nutrients needed to live a full
healthy life span. Another

If it takes too long for

Food processing and

more nutrients. For example,
the most nutritious part of
wheat, rice, sugar, etc. is sepa­

nutrition void by-products of

Another major nutrition

food. Most cooking, espe­

kill off many nutrients. Many
pet foods are overcooked.

to the exercise problem.
How in the world do you
exercise a dog or a cat?
A. There are ways that
you can exercise your dog or
cat. One way to exercise your
dog is a daily walk. The walk
provides quality time and
exercise for you and your
dog. A simple game of fetch
in the yard or park also keeps
your dog active and happy.
On the other hand, cats
are more independent and
when outside prefer to exer­
cise alone. Cats can remain
active if the owner uses a toy
that creates interest like my
Play-N-Squeak Wand.
I also have more
advanced ways to exercise
your dog or cat that I provide
in my book.

Q. What other mental
problems shorten life span?
A. Your pet’s body pro­
duces a neurotransmitter
which is a kind of brain food
that assists brain function. If a
mental activity decreases, for

for long periods, the body
will cease to product the neu­

physical stimulation decrease.
Bones and muscles atrophy
from the lack of stimulation.
When a dog comes into a
family, it looks upon the fami­

ever feeds the dog and gives it
instruction becomes the alpha
person or leader. Long periods
of separation cause anxiety,
aggression and leads to other
problems like overfeeding and
obesity. These issues all lead
to a poor quality of life and
shorter life span. I tell people
how to avoid these problems
