This is a true story.
About five months ago, my ten-year-old
daughter, Jenny, came home from school.
Unlike her usual routine, she ran upstairs and
went right to her room. Even downstairs I
could hear her crying. So I went up to see what
was wrong.
“I don’t want you to take me to school anymore!”
she blurted. “Some of the kids said you looked
like a cow! I even heard Mrs. Fogarty whisper
‘moo’ to some of the other moms as you walked
by. Mom. can’t you please be thin again?”
I went to my bedroom and began to cry, too.
After I quit sobbing. I took a long hard look at
myself in the mirror. Jenny was right. I was a
“cow.” I’m 5’ 6" and I weighed 225 pounds.
About ten days later I came across an article
in Reader’s Digest about a brand new medical
discovery which allows doctors to treat obese
patients with almost unbelievable results.
This wasn’t a fad diet.
This was science. Pure science.
In three separate university studies, subjects
were given the same basic diet for a period of
60 days. There was only one difference. The
subjects who were in Group A were given food
supplements containing certain types of dietary
calcium. The subjects in Group B were not.
Amazingly, doctors found that the participants
in Group A lost 70% more weight than the
subjects in Group B.
Not in just one study.
In three totally independent trials!
The news of the discovery didn’t take long to
reach the medical community. These amazing
results were reported in numerous medical
