# The Simple Segmentation Process for Mailing To Your Email List Without Pissing Off Your Prospects

**Date:** 2014-06-11 | **Duration:** 11m | **Folder:** _unfiled

Hey everybody, Todd Brown here and I'm regularly asked this question about what's the best way
to email your prospect list on a regular basis with a variety of different offers
without pissing off your prospects, right? Without inundating them with offer after offer and
without putting offers out in front of them that they maybe just aren't interested in. And so I decided to put together this little video to just show you an ultra simple segmentation
process that you can use to mail to your prospect list on a regular basis, identify who is interested
in what so that you can follow up with those individuals with targeted messages and not
the other prospects that might not have an interest in that particular topic. This is the exact process
that we use internally here at MFA and this is the exact process that I teach to coaching clients. So let me walk you through this entire process.

you yet. So these individuals could have come in through a variety of different opt-in channels,
could be from JV partners, media buying, cold traffic, whatever, right? These are all individuals
that have gone through some kind of initial marketing campaign or hopefully an initial marketing funnel.
