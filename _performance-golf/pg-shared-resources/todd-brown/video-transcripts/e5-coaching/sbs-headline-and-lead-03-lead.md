**Date:** 2021-06-27 | **Duration:** 0m | **Folder:** E5 COACHING

In the campaign lead, remember that you are going to write something that grabs your prospects' attention emotionally
and inflames their interest and desire to consume the remainder of your campaign. For a refresher of what Todd does, here's a convenient link. Start by writing down things about your unique mechanism. Use these boxes to collect that information. Fortunately, we unpacked and interrogated the unique mechanism already in a previous step-by-step guide.

On page two, put your big marketing idea in this box. And finally, using the information from the first page and your big idea and these rules,
write a lead that gets your prospect emotionally excited about your unique mechanism. Feel free to share your lead during the group coaching call.
