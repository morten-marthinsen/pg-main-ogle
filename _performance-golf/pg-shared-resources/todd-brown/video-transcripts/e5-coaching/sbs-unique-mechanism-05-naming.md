# SBS - Unique Mechanism - 05 Naming

**Date:** 2021-05-26 | **Duration:** 1m | **Folder:** E5 COACHING

---

In this step, let's start by looking at our entire unique mechanism and coming up with a name for it. I have four rules for this. One, the name is not easily found in a search engine. Two, the name is not hyperbolic or flashy. Three, the name is not familiar sounding.

And four, the name does not sound like it's the name of a product, course, or service. Ideally, your name should be a term instead of a title. So, for example, cash flow compounding rather than the cash flow compounding formula. Lumbar reactivation instead of the lumbar reactivation protocol. While there's nothing wrong with having a term followed by the word protocol or blueprint or system,
when you use a term, it can make coming up with a big idea for your marketing a little bit easier.

We'll discuss this later when we work on your big idea. In my case, I could call my mechanism authority exchange. Or perhaps authority partnering. Because a big part of my unique mechanism is about using the clout of existing experts to help with branding. For now, come up with a name for your mechanism and try not to stress out about it.

The reality is that the name is much less important than the actual breakdown of the mechanism itself.
