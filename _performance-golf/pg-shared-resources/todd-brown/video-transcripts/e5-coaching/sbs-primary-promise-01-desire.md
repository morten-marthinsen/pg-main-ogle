# SBS - Primary Promise - 01 Desire

**Date:** 2021-06-04 | **Duration:** 2m | **Folder:** E5 COACHING

---

When I stepped you through prospect analysis you ended up with a nice list
of desires and wants from your prospects and put them here in the web analysis
worksheet and because we did research correctly we ended up with desires that
were specific and easy for anyone to visualize for example in my case you see
a nice list of desires that are in the actual words of my prospects and when I
did my research instead of accepting responses like I want to be a best
selling author I dug into that statement during the interview and I got them to
admit this I'd love to be a top 10 bestseller in a top-level Amazon category
and stay there for at least two years but look at this one it says I want to be
successful that could mean so many things to different people to some it could
mean being published to others it could mean being a best-selling author and to
some it might mean fame interviews on television hosting live events so that
particular desire is useless to me for the purposes of creating a campaign anyway
as I look through these wants and desires I realized that there are many things I
can promise to do for people according to this list I can help them with writing or
authority or creating a digital platform or publishing or even having a status
like bestseller or admiration and respect from family friends or colleagues
honestly I could write as many as five different campaigns one for each of these
desires but as I think about these things I consider everything I learned
about my prospects and I consider everything I learned about my competitors and the
kind of things that they offer and then I decide on something that I believe that
they would all want what I'm saying is that I just pick one it almost doesn't
matter which anyway based on what I've learned I want to create a campaign about
the first desire the one about becoming a best-selling author and I picked that
one for a couple of reasons number one I'm good at making that happen and number
two I have a pretty unique process for getting it done so what I want you to do is
pick one of the desires from your web analysis worksheet you can also get
ideas from other worksheets like the now and after grid or the ideal client
exercise but pick one and only one copy it and then paste it somewhere on the
primary promise worksheet and then go to the next video
