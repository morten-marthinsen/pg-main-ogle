# SBS - SIN Offer - 02 List Final Deliverables

**Date:** 2021-06-06 | **Duration:** 2m | **Folder:** E5 COACHING

---

in this step we will categorize our deliverables the first thing to do is
group your deliverables by benefit the reality is that if there is more than
one deliverable that offers the same benefit such as speed or ease of
implementation then your prospects are going to wonder why they need all of
them if each of your deliverables offers discrete benefits well then that's great
you can present each of them however if a few of your deliverables make things
easy such as templates then I recommend that you bundle them together into a
single deliverable as an example look at my list there are a lot of deliverables
here since I assume my prospects already want my mechanism I don't need to spend
a whole lot of time talking about all these my goal is to build value with
benefits until my offer clearly exceeds the price the higher the price the more
value I'll need which means I'll need more benefits so I have a couple of
templates that make it easy to create special kinds of web pages I have a few
more templates that make it easy to format a book correctly for publication I
have three different resource lists that I could bundle together into a single
deliverable and I have cheat sheets for speeding things along I even have a
couple of ways to offer support as they publish and promote their book so by
grouping these things I went from having 23 deliverables to having 15 but
there's one more exercise I want you to do before filling out the sin offer
worksheet I want you to separate the core deliverables from the bonuses and the
criteria I want you to use is to ask yourself which things are essential for
delivering the primary promise that you made in the thesis statement and
which things are there to make things easier faster and more convenient
but without those items they could still achieve what you promised so I've
done this to my list but I want you to notice two things first of all I broke up
my resource bundle by moving two of the three resource lists to the bonus list I
didn't have to but I wanted another bonus but before I did that I made sure that I
met the criteria I mentioned before which is that all my deliverables do have
different benefits when I'm done the second thing I want you to see is that there are
four different courses in the core deliverables they're all needed to
achieve a long-lasting best-seller status so now I'm wondering whether I should
bundle these into a single deliverable after all nobody wants training they want
the results that they get from the training I'm thinking that maybe the
prospects will be discouraged by all the training they need to go through so
maybe I should bundle them together into a single training suite anyway once
you've completed categorizing your list move on to the step three video
