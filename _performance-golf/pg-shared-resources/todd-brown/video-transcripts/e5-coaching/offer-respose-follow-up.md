In this lesson, we're going to cover the offer response follow-up sequence. Now, as a reminder, there are four stages to your campaign follow-up. The first stage, which we already covered, is campaign consumption or message consumption. This sequence is designed to drive your prospects to consume as much of your marketing message,
as much of your campaign argument as possible. Remember, the greater the percentage of your campaign argument your prospects consume,
the higher their conversion rate will be.

Next up is what we call offer response. Now, this is the next stage in your campaign follow-up. Specifically, this is follow-up for prospects who've seen the offer and haven't purchased yet,
ultimately driving them to buy. So again, this part, this stage is triggered once people have seen your offer. So if they've watched enough of your video, let's say, where they've seen the offer,
they've gotten to the offer, and they have not purchased, this is the follow-up sequence
that is now triggered.

This is now about the offer. We're talking to the prospect about your offer because by this stage, they've seen the offer. So now we're no longer talking about your marketing argument. We're no longer talking about the content pieces or the beliefs that you introduced in
your marketing message, in your campaign argument. Now, we're focused on the offer for two to three days to drive sales conversions.
