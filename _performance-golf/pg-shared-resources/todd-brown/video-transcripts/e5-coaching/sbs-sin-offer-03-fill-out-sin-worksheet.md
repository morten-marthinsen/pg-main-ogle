# SBS - SIN Offer - 03 Fill Out SIN Worksheet

**Date:** 2021-06-06 | **Duration:** 1m | **Folder:** E5 COACHING

---

In this step, use the SIN Offer Worksheet to plan how you will present your deliverables. Start by filling in your thesis statement so you can easily reference it as needed. Then, come up with a name for your entire offer, which includes everything your prospect will get when they buy. And then get the deliverables from your list and put them into the SIN Offer Worksheet. However, when you do that, go ahead and rename each deliverable with a title, just like Todd described in his lesson on deliverables.

You can click on this link to refresh your memory about how to name your deliverables. Then, enter the main feature or features of the deliverable and why that feature is needed. For example, I've renamed my bundle of courses Perpetual Best Seller University. What is it? It's a multimedia training suite.

Why do my customers need that feature? They need it so they can publish and promote a book to bestseller status and keep it there for as long as they like. Notice that these three things are simply short notes about my deliverable. I haven't written a long description in here. These are just notes because the writing for the presentation comes later.

Now, for a benefit, I ask myself what the big payoff or transformation in their life will be once they use this feature. Then, I make a quick note about a functional, dimensionalized, and emotional benefit. Click on this link here for a review of the three levels of offer benefits. When you have put all your deliverables and bonuses in the worksheet, go on to the next step.
