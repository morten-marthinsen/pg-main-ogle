# SBS - Big Marketing Idea - 01 Big Idea

**Date:** 2021-06-10 | **Duration:** 0m | **Folder:** E5 COACHING

---

To create your big marketing idea, first put your thesis statement into the big marketing idea worksheet. Make sure that you reference it often as you work, because ultimately your goal is to get your prospects to believe and accept your thesis statement as true. Okay, the second step is to research, research, research, and here's a link to Todd's advice and training about how to do that. For this step, you cannot have too many ideas. Personally, I come up with dozens of ideas as I research.

The third step is to select one of your ideas from step two and develop it into a big marketing idea. Todd's advice for doing that is right here at the other end of this link. If you have any questions or you would like to ask for help from others, the group call is a great place for that.
