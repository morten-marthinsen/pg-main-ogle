# SBS - Unique Mechanism - 02 Delineate

**Date:** 2021-05-26 | **Duration:** 1m | **Folder:** E5 COACHING

---

In the delineate step of unpacking your unique mechanism, write down how your mechanism works. The trick is to imagine that you're creating written instructions for someone to follow. What steps would they take? Or if it's a physical product or software, how and why does it work? For example, imagine that I have a brand marketing plan for owners of businesses in the pet supply market.

And my mechanism is a three-step process. The three steps are authority attraction, marketing message, and positioning. Now, this isn't unique, but that's okay because we're just getting started. Anyway, we put these three steps into the delineate step in the unique mechanism worksheet. So what I want you to do is take a good long look at the promise you're making to your prospect,
then write down how your product or service is able to make that promise come true.

But when you do this, be careful not to talk about your product or service. This confuses a lot of people who work on their unique mechanism first time. For example, if I included steps like watch the video training about authority attraction
and request the social media post from our team,
then my unique mechanism starts to become more about my product and service
and less about how and why it works to deliver the primary promise. If you have questions about this, just show your work during the campaign coaching call. In step two, we'll interrogate this mechanism further.

Thank you.
