And so let's start with this, that as an entrepreneur, as a business builder, you are an investor. You're really investing. You're investing in the acquisition of assets, just like any other
investor is investing in the acquisition of assets, whether it be stocks or real estate. Only in our
case, those assets for you are your customers, your clients. And just like any asset, there is a
value today, what that asset is worth to you today.

In our case, when we're talking about customers or
clients, we're talking about how much money they spend with you the day that they become a buyer. So how much money they spend with you during their first transaction. Let's say that that's 49 bucks. Well, every asset, when an investor is acquiring, it has a future value. The reason why an investor

And so there is a future value for us. This future value is all of the other transactions
that your clients, that your customers, that your buyers invest in with you, all the transactions
