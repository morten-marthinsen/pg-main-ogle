# SBS - SIN Offer - 06 Call Offer

**Date:** 2021-06-06 | **Duration:** 1m | **Folder:** E5 COACHING

---

If you want your prospects to get on a call with you or your sales team in order to make a sale,
then you actually have to have two SIN offers. The one you've put together so far is something that you can use on the actual sales call. But for the purposes of your actual E5 campaign, your SIN offer is actually the call,
not the deliverables we talked about before. What that means is that you have completely different deliverables for your sales page. And the deliverables now become things that you will talk about during the call.

So let's look at the SIN offer worksheet I'm doing for my sales call. It starts off the same, but look at my offer name. It's now called the Lucid Attraction Implementation Call because I'm offering them a call and that's it. Now since my prospects want my unique mechanism,
the topic of the call is going to be how I can help them get that unique mechanism
into their business, into their life as easily and as quickly as possible. Look at how I set up my deliverables.

I have a single deliverable with two features. The single deliverable is the call and the two features are the two things that I'm going to talk about. Also, I want you to understand that even though the deliverable is a call,
I can still have terms, I can have bonuses, and I can have risk reversal. So fill out the worksheet for your call. As always, if you have any questions, bring them up during the weekly coaching call.
