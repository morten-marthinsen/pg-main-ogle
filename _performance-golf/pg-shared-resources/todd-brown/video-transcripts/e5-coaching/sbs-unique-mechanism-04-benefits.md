# SBS - Unique Mechanism - 04 Benefits

**Date:** 2021-05-26 | **Duration:** 3m | **Folder:** E5 COACHING

---

One of the biggest problems that new marketers have is determining the
benefits, the transformations, the results that their prospects get from
their unique mechanism. Because what happens is that they end up using one or
two benefits centered around their primary promise. In other words, they
keep repeating the same benefit over and over throughout their marketing message
and they have a hard time coming up with more. For example, how many benefits can
you think of right now about your mechanism? Two?

Three? Well in this video
we're gonna fix that problem. So start by copying and pasting everything from the
interrogation step into the benefits step. And one by one think of as many
benefits to each step of the unique mechanism as you can. So for example, in
the first step of authority attraction clients use video.

So what is the big
payoff or transformation in their life if they use videos exactly the way I tell
them to. So to help us let's look at all the extra little things we came up with
when we interrogated our unique mechanism. So when my prospects follow my specific
video creation process, they connect with their audience, they build trust by being
helpful, they attract more clients, they are seen all over the internet, they
eliminate content creation stress because they're using short videos, they're seen
as an authority in as little as a few hours, they end up with a short buying
time due to omnipresence, and they have a short buying time due to being top of
mind. Now let's look at the next step in authority attraction. I have a process for
partnering with authorities, respected vendors, and influencers.

This could be
influencers in social media, it could be dog food companies, it could be
veterinarians. If my clients follow my unique mechanism and do this
collaboration exactly as I describe it, what kind of benefits would they get from
it? Well they would reach more prospects, they would have a better return on
investment on their money and their time, they would gain instant authorities from
other authorities, they would have an instant insurgence of leads, they would become
friends with industry leaders, they would be able to test marketing ideas more
quickly and they would be able to create products together with those vendors and
those influencers and those authorities and so on. Now if we continue to do this
for the six remaining steps of my unique mechanism, at this rate we stand to uncover
more than 60 different benefits of using my unique mechanism. So instead of coming up
with only three or four different versions of the primary promise as a benefit and
using that benefit over and over in my marketing message I use dozens and
dozens of great benefits and they're all different.

So what I'd like you to do is
come up with as many different benefits as you can think of for every step or
element of your unique mechanism as you possibly can and then move on to the
last step in creating your unique mechanism in the next video.
