# SBS - Headline and Lead - 01 Overview

**Date:** 2021-06-27 | **Duration:** 0m | **Folder:** E5 COACHING

---

In this step-by-step guide, you will create your headline and your lead, and there's a worksheet for each of these. At the top of each worksheet is a link in case you want to re-watch Todd's training. Some people like to create their headline first, other people like to create their lead first. There's no right or wrong way. Personally, I've done it both ways.

In the next video, I'll show you how to use the headline worksheet, and in the third video, I'll show you how to use the lead worksheet. But again, it doesn't matter which one you do first, so go ahead and watch one and fill out the worksheet. As a side note, the group coaching call is a great place to run your headline or lead by the group and get their feedback.
