# SBS - SIN Offer - 01 Brainstorm Deliverables

**Date:** 2021-06-06 | **Duration:** 1m | **Folder:** E5 COACHING

---

it's best to start with your thesis statement the reason is that by the time
your prospect reaches this point of your campaign they already want your unique
mechanism so keep that in mind your prospect also wants what you've
promised in your primary promise so start the send offer process by asking
yourself how can you give your customer everything they need in order to achieve
what you promised what I'd like you to do first is get out a blank sheet of
paper or open up a blank document on your computer then put your thesis
statement at the top of the page and underneath simply list all of the things
you know that your prospect will need in order to achieve what you promised in
your thesis statement in my case I came up with a lot of things I started by
putting my thesis statement at the top of the page I know that I want to give my
customers everything they need to be able to become and remain a best seller so I
list everything I can think of I talked to some of my publisher friends for
ideas I talked to customers for ideas I look at my unique mechanism for ideas and
I definitely look at competitors for ideas since I want my offer to be superior
to theirs now don't worry about what you can or cannot deliver right now this
exercise is meant to identify a really great offer whether you can deliver it or
not when you've exhausted all ideas go to the next step
