# Module 2 Review

**Date:** 2014-02-28 | **Duration:** 1m | **Folder:** 6FFF Lessons

---

Okay, so here's what we covered in module two. We covered the difference between the front end and
the back end, the purpose of both the front end and the back end, how the A players in marketing
view each, how they view the front end and the back end. We talked about the three objectives
of your front end offer. We talked about front end product objectives, the actual objectives of the
product itself, not the offer. And then we talked about how to set up the sale of your first back
end offer.

And we finished by talking about how to actually build your product with the hook in mind. Here's what to do now. First, begin brainstorming ideas for your front end product, taking into
account marketplace sophistication, what the market has seen, what they're being offered right now,
and begin brainstorming ideas for your front end product based on your hook and promise and the
elements that we talked about. Begin jotting down ideas for how and when you'll use the pre-selling
techniques in your front end product to set up the sale and create desire for your first back end
product, the first offer that you're going to make to new customers after their front end purchase. And then finally, of course, begin thinking about what that first back end offer and product is going
to be that you will make to customers who, again, purchase your front end product.
