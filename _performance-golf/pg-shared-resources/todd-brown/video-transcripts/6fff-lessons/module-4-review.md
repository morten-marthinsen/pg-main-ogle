# Module 4 Review

**Date:** 2014-03-01 | **Duration:** 0m | **Folder:** 6FFF Lessons

---

Okay, so here's what we covered in this module. First, we covered the elements of the customer
captivation offer. Then we talked about constructing the customer captivation offer,
presenting your customer captivation offer, and then building your customer captivation page
using something like lead pages or 10-minute pages. Here's what to do now. Step one,
write your word-for-word VSL script.

Step two, record the audio for the VSL. Then turn your script
into your VSL slides. Then record and render your VSL. Upload your VSL to your streaming service
like Vimeo or Wistia. And then create your customer captivation offer page using something like
lead pages, 10-minute pages, or the page creation application of your choice.
