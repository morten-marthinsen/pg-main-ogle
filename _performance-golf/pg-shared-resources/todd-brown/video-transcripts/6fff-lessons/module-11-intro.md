**Date:** 2014-03-01 | **Duration:** 0m | **Folder:** 6FFF Lessons

Welcome to module 11. In this module, we're going to talk about testing for six-figure funnel
formula optimization. We're going to start by talking about why you need to test. Then we're
going to talk about what to focus your testing on and why. Then I'm going to show you some simple
testing with a free tool, and then I'm going to show you a little bit more extensive testing with
a paid tool.
