# Module 2 Introduction

**Date:** 2014-02-26 | **Duration:** 0m | **Folder:** 6FFF Lessons

---

Welcome to module two. In this module we're going to start by talking about the keys to the perfect
six-figure funnel formula product. Most marketers don't understand the role that their front-end
product, their first product sold to a prospect, plays. And so we're going to start by talking
about the purpose of the front-end product. Then we're going to go on to talk about what's called
strategic front-end product development.

How to actually develop the perfect first product that
you sell to a prospect so that it fulfills its role and purpose. And then we're going to talk about
how to incorporate your hook and promise into the development of this first or front-end product.
