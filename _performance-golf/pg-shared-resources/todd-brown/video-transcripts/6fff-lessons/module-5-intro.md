process. We're going to start by covering the elements of the ideal order form. Then I'm going
to introduce you to a killer application from Brian Moran called SamCart which allows marketers
who don't have a merchant account to easily and quickly accept credit cards online. It is awesome.

It takes care of your upsells, your downsells, your front-end offer, your list segmentation. It is
killer. And so I'm going to introduce you to SamCart and then I'm going to have Brian Moran
walk you through how to actually use SamCart. Again, if you want to get started collecting
credit card payments online without any kind of merchant account, all at extremely low rates.
