# Module 1 Introduction

**Date:** 2014-02-26 | **Duration:** 1m | **Folder:** 6FFF Lessons

---

Welcome to module one. Now in this module, we're going to cover the foundation of your six-figure
funnel. Specifically, we're going to be talking about selecting your niche or marketplace and
the criteria that you need to use to assess viability. We're going to be talking about
something called marketplace sophistication and how to assess that level of sophistication for each
of the potential niches or markets that you're considering. We're going to talk about how to
assess the competitive landscape, and then we're going to talk about developing your six-figure
funnel formula hook and promise.

Let me tell you, of all of the modules, this module is probably the
least exciting for most marketers. It's more strategy than it is tactics, and there's a whole
bunch of quote-unquote conceptual information. But I've got to tell you that the information that
you're going to get in this module is some of the most critical information you will hear throughout
the rest of the six-figure funnel formula training. Because your entire six-figure funnel gets built
on what you'll learn in the four lessons within module one. And so I highly, highly encourage you
to go through these modules, pay close attention, take notes, because you'll be using this information
throughout the rest of the modules in the six-figure funnel formula.
