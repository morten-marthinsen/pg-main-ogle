# Module 1 Review

**Date:** 2014-02-26 | **Duration:** 1m | **Folder:** 6FFF Lessons

---

Okay, so here's what we covered in module one. First, we covered the elements of a good niche
or market. We talked about assessing the marketplace sophistication. We talked about
developing a unique mechanism. We talked about assessing the competitive landscape,
developing your six-figure funnel formula hook, and developing your six-figure funnel formula
promise.

Here's what to do now. Step one, start by analyzing your niche market ideas, the ideas
that you have for different niches, for different markets against the criteria that I gave you. Step two, review the current and past hooks and promises of the top 10 competitors to understand
your marketplace's sophistication or your market sophistication level. In step one, you're going
to analyze your niche market ideas against the criteria that we talked about. You're going to
pick your niche, and then what you're going to do is you're going to review the current and past
hooks and promises of who would be your top 10 competitors to understand your market's
sophistication level.

Then you're going to review those top 10 competitors again, but now you're
going to review their current offers to better understand the competitive landscape. Then
you're going to begin to brainstorm your six-figure funnel formula hook based on the elements that
I gave you. And finally, you're going to brainstorm your six-figure funnel formula promise also based
on the elements that I gave you.
