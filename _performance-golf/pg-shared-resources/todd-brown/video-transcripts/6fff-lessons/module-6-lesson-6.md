In this lesson, we're going to talk about the thank you page gift offer add-on. This is a
special add-on offer that comes all the way at the end of the transaction maximization sequence. So you can see just as a quick recap that we send our traffic to our lead magnet offer page. This
is where we generate the lead and then they immediately go to the customer captivation
offer page. The individuals that take this offer then immediately get brought to the reverse add-on,
then the soft offer add-on, then the mid-tier add-on and land on this thank you page.

And as
you're going to see in a second, this thank you page has an additional offer. This offer is for
some sort of continuity offer, a newsletter, a membership site, etc. But the way it's presented
makes this extremely savvy and the take rate is through the roof. And so let's go over the elements
of this thank you page add-on offer. Number one, it's presented on a mock thank you page.

We could
really call it a thank you page. What we're really doing is after the mid-tier add-on offer, whether
they take that or not, they are brought to a thank you page. And this is not to look like the other
transaction maximization offer pages. Now we want the customer to feel like the transaction is done. We want them to let their guard down and we want them to feel like they've reached the end of the
sequence.

And this is the thank you page. And so it needs to look and feel like a thank you page,
and it should have a thank you statement at the top. So in this case, we want the customer to feel
like the transaction is over. Now this particular offer gets presented with a VSL with no call to
action delay. And you're going to see that it's not an add to cart button that you utilize with this
offer.

This is an offer that is presented as a thank you gift for new customers. And so I want
you to think about it for one second. A new customer came in, purchased the customer captivation offer,
purchased one or more of your transaction maximization offers, maybe all of them, and then landed on this
thank you page. Now there's a video that auto plays that thanks them for their purchase,
thanks them for becoming a customer and basically says, as my way of over delivering value, as my way
of showing you how much I appreciate having you now as a new customer, I want to offer you a thank you
gift. Then what gets presented is a free trial, a free membership, but it's not presented as a trial.

It's presented as I want to give you a free month in my XYZ membership, or I want to give you a free
month to my XYZ newsletter. And so in essence, it is a trial continuity offer, but it's presented to
the customer as a thank you gift, as a gift. Now this 30 day trial continuity offer is priced
up to 27 to $47 a month. The call to action button has copy that reinforces that this is a gift offer. And so it doesn't say add to cart.

It says, yes, I'll take my free month or however you need to word
it for your particular continuity offer. It's a 14 day trial or that sort of thing. Now, let me be clear
on something. Am I saying that at no point, we tell them that they will be charged at the end of the
month? Absolutely not.

You do want to, you do need to, you must be completely 100% upfront with
customers that at the end of the month that they will be billed unless they tell you prior. And so
it gets presented as, again, a thank you gift as my way of thanking you for trusting in me. I want to
give you a gift. I want to give you one month free to XYZ membership site. Now it's normally $27 a
month.

And what you'll get access to is boom feature advantage benefit right now. And then you,
you go on to say at the end of the month, if you love it and you're thrilled with the content,
do nothing. And you'll be billed at the charter rate of just $27 a month going forward. If for
whatever reason, you don't want to continue beyond that free month, just shoot me an email. Let me know
your membership will be canceled and you won't pay anything.

So go ahead, grab your free gift,
enjoy it. Again, it's my way of saying, thank you. Click the button below that says, yes,
I'll take my free month. And what happens is they actually get a free month. And then if they don't
cancel, if they don't do anything, they're billed at the end of the month and then ongoing.

And again,
this is something that can very easily be set up in a variety of different CRM applications. It's very
easy to set up in Sam card if that's the route that you choose to go. But this is an incredible
offer to put at the end of your sequence because of how it's presented. The take rate on this is also
through the roof.
