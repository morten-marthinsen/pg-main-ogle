# Module 12 Review

**Date:** 2014-03-01 | **Duration:** 0m | **Folder:** 6FFF Lessons

---

Here's what we covered in this module. First, we talked about how the real business of direct
response is after the first sale. Then we talked about the big thinking mistake that most marketers
make, the idea of more of the same. We talked about optimizing your backend. We talked about
why you must be prolific with product and offer creation.

So here's what to do now. First, decide
what your first backend offer is going to be. Then write the sales copy for the offer, of course
developing a hook and promise first. Then create that backend product and test the offer to your
customer list.
