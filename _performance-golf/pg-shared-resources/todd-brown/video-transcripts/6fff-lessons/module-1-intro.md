# Module 1 Intro

**Date:** 2014-02-26 | **Duration:** 1m | **Folder:** 6FFF Lessons

---

Welcome to module one. In this module, we're going to talk about the foundation of your
six-figure funnel formula, what everything actually gets built on. And specifically,
we're going to start by talking about how you actually go about selecting a viable niche or
marketplace for your business, your product, and what it is that you're going to be offering. Then we're going to talk about how do you actually assess what's called marketplace sophistication. How do you understand what it is that the market has seen, has been presented with in terms of
promises and offers?

So you can cut through the clutter and make sure that your marketing message
is at the sophistication level appropriate for the market. Then we're going to talk about assessing
the competitive landscape, how to uncover what it is that your competitors are offering so that we
can create a superior offer for the marketplace. And finally, we're going to talk about developing
your six-figure funnel formula, hook and promise. This is actually the foundation of your overall
marketing message. And it's critical that you nail both the hook and promise and fully understand both.
