# 32 The Purple Protocol-Process

Hey and welcome to the Purple Notebook. I am Sam Woods and I'll be showing you exactly how this
works and what you'll be doing. In this video we are once again looking at the Purple 48-hour
protocol and we're going to look deeper into the process that we're going to follow. The
Purple 48-hour protocol consists of three steps. Step number one is when you go live and drive
traffic to your Purple sales page.

Once enough traffic has come through and you have some data
and metrics to look at you're going to move on to step number two where you diagnose what's going
on with the Purple model so you can decide what to change and how to change it. Then you're going to
move on to step number three where you expand your traffic sources and you drive more traffic to the
Purple sales page. So step number one this happens in the first eight to twelve hours of you creating
everything. You're going to start driving visitors to the Purple sales page from your choice of traffic
source. You can do paid ads.

You can promote this to your email list or to someone else's email list. You can use social media or YouTube and video. There are plenty of traffic sources you can use but this
is the first step. Once everything is set up and ready to go you go into drive traffic and you're going to
do this with at least three Purple traffic drivers that we spoke about in a previous video and you're going
to start with just one traffic source for now and use any one of those seven frames for your purple
traffic drivers and again in the purple model worksheet you see the full list. Now depending
on the traffic source you should reach 1,000 visitors to the sales page within eight to 12 hours if not
sooner.

Again this depends on a bit on the traffic source. If you're using paid traffic it can happen
pretty quick. If you are deciding to focus on search traffic or SEO traffic then it'll take a lot longer
than eight to 12 hours because your page has to rank and so on. So I recommend that you don't start using
a slow traffic source like SEO but rather focus your efforts on either paid traffic, videos on YouTube,
social media posts, or email list promotion. Now again paid ads drive traffic much quicker and in larger
volumes than I post on social media so choose your source just have a reason for the source that you
are working.

Once you have reached 1,000 visitors to the purple sales page you're going to record your
metrics in the purple metrics spreadsheet and then continue to step number two and in a minute I will
show you what that metrics sheet looks like and how to proceed with evaluating those numbers but in step two
you're going to diagnose what's going on with your purple model. So once you record your metrics in
the purple metric spreadsheet you're going to follow the prompts below or in that protocol so you can
uncover whatever bottlenecks exist and then decide on your next steps. So in order for your sales to reach
$1,000 per day or more there are certain benchmarks that you're aiming for. So your purple sales page
conversion rate should convert at 10% or more and your purple checkout page conversion rate should be at
50% or more. These are benchmarks so you want to get as close to them as possible.

There are no strict rules
when it comes to these these are more benchmarks and guidelines for you to aim for. The cost of your
offer the cost of traffic source and so on these things all play a role in your success so don't take
these as strict rules but more so as guidelines that will help you decide on what to do. And if either one of your conversion rates are hitting the benchmark you work on the conversion
rate that does not hit the benchmark. So if your conversion rate for your sales page is at 10, 11,
or 14 percent but your checkout page is only at 50 percent or is less than 50 percent then you want to
focus your effort on the checkout page for example. Once you have worked through the protocol that I'll
show you in a minute and you have optimized the different parts of your either purple sales page
or purple checkout page or both of them you then move on to step number three which is the expand step.

So once those are optimized your goal is to expand your traffic and increase the number of quality
number and quality of your purple sales page visitors. So you do this by expanding both the
number of purple traffic drivers that you're using and expanding into more traffic sources. That means
that if you have three of those traffic drivers for one source you are then going to use the other four
of the seven purple traffic drivers and then you're going to use those in a new traffic source. So let's pretend for a minute that you had three traffic drivers and you're using paid ads and then
you expand with four more traffic drivers still with paid ads. You are then going to take the winners
out of those seven traffic drivers and you're going to try them as for example emails or you're going to try
them as short videos on youtube or just regular posts on social media.

So all of these steps are carefully
laid out for you in the purple 48 hour protocol and in the next video I will show you side by side
how to look at your metrics sheet and then how to make decisions with the protocol.
