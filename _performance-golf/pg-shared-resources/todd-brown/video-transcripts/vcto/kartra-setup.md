# Kartra Setup

**Date:** 2018-06-01 | **Duration:** 2m | **Folder:** VCTO

---

Okay, so in this video we're going to quickly walk through the Kartra setup. And I say quickly because it's actually pretty well designed and pretty simple to walk through
the process. But just as an overview, again, Kartra is a fully featured marketing system platform. It includes the ability to build all of your funnel pages, sort of like ClickFunnels. As I mentioned before, I still do like ClickFunnels editor a little bit better.

But Kartra has some other features that ClickFunnels does or doesn't have and makes things a little
bit easier. So this is a more full-fledged solution. This can replace Infusionsoft and ClickFunnels or ActiveCampaign and ClickFunnels. So this has your full email list management. It has multiple automation functionality to be able to create sophisticated campaigns.

It can host all your videos, host all your lead magnets, all your downloadable files, you
can build membership sites, you can build webinars. So it's a pretty full-featured product. But the nice thing about it, like I said, is that it does come with a complete tutorial. So once you start your account and log in, you'll see here this Cartridge Tutorial tab
on the right. So there's a pretty full-featured tutorial here that walks you through each different
section and what you need to do to kind of fully set up your account.

So just completing your profile, creating your first list, creating your first tag, setting
up your payment gateway, configuring your products, how to set the broadcast, things
like that. And everything in here is pretty self-explanatory. Under profile, you're going to put in your username and your password and your contact details
and your logo and your address and your Facebook URLs, things like that. When you create your list, it'll walk you through the process of clicking the button to create a
list, enter the name of the list. It's got good help icons over here to tell you what's what.

So this is pretty straightforward. I don't think I need to dive into too much detail. Just walk through this entire process and you'll quickly be up and running. And then in future videos, I'll dive deeper into how to set up campaigns in here and other
things. There's also a good academy available as well, which is the same login as your account, which
will walk you through some other trainings as well.

So that's the quick overview on the cartridge setup. background music
