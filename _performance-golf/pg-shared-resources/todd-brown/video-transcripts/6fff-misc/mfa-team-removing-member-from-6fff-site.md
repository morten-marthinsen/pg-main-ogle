# MFA Team - Removing Member from 6FFF Site

**Date:** 2014-12-20 | **Duration:** 3m | **Folder:** 6FFF (Misc.)

---

In this video, you'll learn how to remove a canceled member from the Six Figure Funnel Formula training area. And so the first thing that we do is we log in to the admin panel of the WordPress site,
and you'll have your own username and password. Once inside, you want to go over and click on, hover your mouse cursor over Wishlist Member,
and then over Members. This is actually the plugin that we use to manage all of our active clients and students in this program. Once hovering over Members, you want to left-click on your mouse,
and that'll bring you to a page that looks like this.

You want to then go ahead and you want to paste in the client or member's email address that you were given
that you want to remove from this platform in here. You paste their email address right in here, and then you click this little magnifying glass. Once you do that, it'll bring up the individual's account. The next thing that you want to do is you want to just left-click on their username right here. That'll bring you to a page that looks like this.

The first thing that you want to do is you want to uncheck the box next to Membership Level. So we uncheck that box right there, and you want to scroll down and click Update User. That'll bring you back to this page, and we want to paste their email address in here again. Click on the magnifying glass. It'll bring us back here, and then we want to again left-click on their name.

Now you'll see that next to their membership level that there is nothing selected, which is good. Then what we want to do is we want to click on Add to Blacklist. Now what I'm going to do, and I'm doing this on the Mac, is I'm going to hold down the Command key,
and I'm going to left-click on this so that it actually opens in a separate window over here. Once we do that, we just click Save Setting, and then we go back to this page. And then we want to do the same thing over here.

And what this is doing, the reason why we're doing this is so that they can't log back in on some future date
and try to access the portal again, or so that they can't re-register under a different bogus email address
or something like that. So we click over here. We scroll down, and we click Save. And then if we see that there is a different IP address that they've used,
so in this case there's two different IP addresses that they've used,
we're going to then click again over here on that one. Scroll down here, click Save Settings,
then scroll down here one more time, and click Update User, and that's it.

And then that user has been removed,
and their email address and IP addresses have been flagged so that they can't re-log back in.
