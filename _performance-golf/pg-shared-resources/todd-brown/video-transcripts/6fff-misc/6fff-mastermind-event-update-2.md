in Delray Beach, Florida, and I am super excited. Over the last handful of days, we've gotten a
couple of emails, Facebook messages from some of the folks that are going to be in attendance
asking what they should do to get prepared. And so I figured I'd make you this quick little
two-minute video to kind of answer that question. And so first and foremost, you heard me say before
in either a prior video or an email that this event, this Mastermind, even though it's going to be fun
and there's going to be a lot of networking and food and hanging out, it's going to be intense,
right?

And my goal is to make sure that some of the best content possible gets shared, some incredible
insights, the stuff that is working right now for some extremely sharp marketers that are going to
be in attendance. And so the very first thing that I want you to think about in preparation for our
Mastermind is what is working really well for you right now or really well for clients, consulting
clients or coaching students? What is some of the best tactics, some of the best strategies that you've
seen produce phenomenal results over the last handful of months? Because each and every person
in attendance is going to have the opportunity to share over 15, 20 minutes each what's working right
now that they feel the group can really benefit from. And so that's the first thing that I want you to
think about in preparation for our Mastermind.

The second thing is what is the number one constraint,
the number one obstacle that you have right now in your business, the number one thing that's holding
you back from experiencing the growth that you want, the explosive growth that you're looking for, or
what's the number one constraint that's holding you back from having a well-oiled machine for a company? Because in the second part of day two, you're going to have the opportunity to share that number one constraint
constraint with the group and get everybody's insights on what they would do to overcome your
obstacle or what they would do to eliminate that constraint. So you're going to have the opportunity
to hear from 11, 12, 13 savvy marketers as it relates to how they would tackle and eliminate your specific
problem. And so I highly encourage you to think a little bit about what is that number one obstacle that
you have right now in your business that you want the group as a whole to tackle for you. So your
constraint, your number one obstacle, and of course your best practices, the things that are working
really well right now in your business.

Give some serious thought to those two things and get prepared to
generously share, participate, give your insights, give your thoughts. Because I have no doubt that if each and
every one of us approach this event with that type of mentality, that type of generous thinking,
and we come armed with, again, the stuff that's working really well right now, this is going to be
an incredibly valuable event for everybody. Not to mention, of course, we've got food,
we're going to have a lot of networking, we're going to have a great time, but it's going to be intense,
some killer stuff right from day one. And so get ready, bring a pad, bring a pen, bring your laptop,
get all caffeined up, get ready to learn and share. And I look forward to seeing each and every one of
you on this Wednesday.

And so I'll talk to you soon and I'll see you in a couple of days.
