# The Other Type Of Prospect "Problem" Your Marketing Must Address

**Duration:** 1:50 | **Published:** 5 years ago

**URL:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nESVSXbzuxg

---

so most savvy entrepreneurs and marketers have learned that when it comes to their marketing and advertising they should be addressing a problem that their prospect has but what is a problem in this context what do we consider to be a problem well certainly the existence of any kind of pain or

discomfort or problem that your prospect wants removed from their life that your product or service can remove for them that is certainly a problem but that's not the only type of problem that we are addressing we're also addressing any unmet need or desire and so if there's something missing from your prospects

life if they want maybe to make more money or to grow bigger tomatoes or they want to learn a skill and they don't know how to learn that skill if they want more of something that they don't have enough of right now that is what we call an unmet need or desire that is also in a marketing context considered a

problem so you have to understand that your product or service is solving a problem your prospects buy products and services not because they want products and services but because they want a problem solved whether that is getting out of pain or whether that is an unmet need or

desire before you write a single word of copy for your marketing campaign you have to identify what is the problem that your product or service is solving for the prospect whether it be a get out of pain solve a problem solve an issue type of problem or whether it be an unmet need or desire regardless you have

to identify what is it that your prospect is trying to solve and how your product or service will solve that
