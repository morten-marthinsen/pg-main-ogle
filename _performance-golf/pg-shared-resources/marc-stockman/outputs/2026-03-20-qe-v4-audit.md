# qe v4 audit — gap analysis against marketing os

**date:** 2026-03-20
**auditor:** claude (opus 4.6)
**source:** quality-engine-v4 vs pg-marketing-os current state
**purpose:** identify everything in marketing os not captured in qe v4, to inform v5

---

## summary

qe v4 is ~70% complete as a generic system. strong architecture (gates, phases, adapters, protocols) but missing the psychological/persuasion foundation layer and several integration patterns marketing os has discovered through real-world use.

**16 gaps found:**
- 3 critical (missing entirely)
- 7 high (protocol exists but incomplete)
- 4 medium (examples/templates needed)
- 2 low (domain customization — not core gaps)

## critical gaps (missing entirely)

### 1. persuasion principles — layer 0 of the 3-layer model

**what it is:** WORKSPACE.md contains 25+ psychological principles on how readers process information, how belief forms, how persuasion sequences, and how copy lands at sentence level. these are extracted from real draft-to-final editing sessions and trace back to psychological mechanisms — not surface-level writing rules.

**where in marketing os:** WORKSPACE.md "copywriting principles — persuasion craft" section (lines 83-156)

**qe v4 status:** MISSING — qe v4's humanization protocol catches what NOT to do (slop words, structural patterns) but doesn't cover what TO do. the 3-layer model has layers 1 (word-level) and 2 (structural) but no layer 0 (psychological mechanisms for how belief actually forms).

**what's missing specifically:**
- how readers process information (cognitive friction, effort-payoff pairing, zoom in/out principle, passive voice rules, jargon earned vs unearned)
- how belief forms (root cause vs symptoms, analogy placement, binary frame structure, recognition vs proclamation, consequence + reason = insight, social proof through shared experience, matching frame to market emotional state)
- how persuasion sequences (frame claim → lens → problem, idea real estate matching weight, section-to-section setup, headline dual function, throat-clearing elimination, clarity over clever, price after value, milestone payoff before next challenge, never introduce doubt, counter-sell outpositioning)
- how copy lands at sentence/paragraph level (one thought per line, shorter = authority, lead with the thing, punctuation as delivery instruction, close on benefit not mechanism, break complex ideas into beats, repetition vs redundancy, every element earns its spot, show don't tell, sequence final punch, reader as analogy subject, vague action language, kill cliches, complete proof points, data scale and delta)

**recommended for v5:** create `protocols/PERSUASION-PRINCIPLES-PROTOCOL.md` as layer 0 of the humanization model. four sections matching the four categories above. each principle states the mechanism, explains why it works, and is concrete enough for any writer to apply.

**what's missing specifically:**

**qe v4 status:** MISSING — qe v4's system-core has forbidden behaviors and mc-check but does not include an explicit verification mandate for factual claims.

**recommended for v5:** add to `core/SYSTEM-CORE.md` as a universal law or standalone section.
