## Science / Mechanism Section

### Eyebrow

### Headline
"We've cracked the code on sous vide speed"

### Body
"Quick refresher: Sous vide works by setting the temperature of water bath in which your food is cooked to the same temperature you want your food cooked. It's a thoroughly awesome way to get perfect food that never overcooks. The only downside is this kind of cooking to equilibrium can take a long time -- especially those last few degrees. Why? At that point, the heat is flowing slowly because the difference in temperature between your food and the water is pretty small."

## Algorithm & Technology Explanation

"Using some sophisticated mathematical algorithms, Joule Turbo -- in tandem with the Breville+ app -- can give you those same perfect results in as little as half the time. It works by raising the water temperature to speed up cooking and lowering it back down again to the perfect temperature just as the food itself arrives there. Bonus: Despite cooking faster and to perfect doneness, the food can still stay in the water until you're ready, just like traditional sous vide."
