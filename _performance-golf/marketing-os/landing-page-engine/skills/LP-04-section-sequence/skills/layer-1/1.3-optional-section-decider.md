# 1.3 — Optional Section Decider

**Skill:** LP-04-section-sequence
**Position:** Layer 1, Microskill 3
**Purpose:** For each optional section in the selected template, make an explicit include/exclude decision based on the product brief, offer details, and proof inventory. Every optional section gets a binary decision with written rationale — no section stays undecided.
**Effort Level:** medium
**Dependencies:** `template-selection.md` (1.1), `mandatory-check.md` (1.2), `page-brief.json`
**Output:** `optional-decisions.md`

---

## Input Schema

```yaml
input:
  optional_sections_list: "[from template-selection.md]"
  brief: "[page-brief.json — especially offer_summary, proof_inventory, audience]"
```

---

## Decision Criteria Per Optional Section

For each optional section in the list, evaluate against these criteria:

### `story` (Type A)
- **Include if:** Product has origin story, founder story, discovery story, OR clinical researcher story. Brief mentions a named person, a trip, a discovery moment.
- **Exclude if:** Pure formula/product, no human story behind it, or awareness stage 4–5 (warm/hot audiences skip stories).
- **Replace with:** If excluded, expand the Root Cause Narrative to carry emotional weight.

### `proof_block_post_ingredients` (Type A)
- **Include if:** Proof inventory has 8+ testimonials OR the ingredient section is long (5+ ingredients). Provides pacing break.
- **Exclude if:** Proof inventory is thin (<5 testimonials). Consolidate into Proof Block 2 instead.

### `value_stack` (Type A)
- **Include if:** Offer includes 2+ bonuses OR product has multiple delivery components (main product + bonuses + members area etc.)
- **Exclude if:** Product is single-item, no bonuses, price under $40. Merge value framing into product introduction.

### `urgency_scarcity` (all types)
- **Include if:** There is a JUSTIFIED urgency/scarcity lever — real limited batch, legitimate time-bound promotion, subscription introductory rate ending, ingredient supply constraint.
- **Exclude if:** No legitimate justification exists. Generic countdown timers without reason destroy trust.
- **RULE:** Never include fabricated urgency. If excluded, note: "No justified urgency lever found in brief — section excluded to protect credibility."

### `comparison_table` (all types)
- **Include if:** Awareness stage 3–4 (Solution-Aware or Product-Aware), competitive market, product has clear differentiation points, multiple competitors use same category language.
- **Exclude if:** Awareness stage 1–2 (naming competitors educates them about alternatives), monopoly-style positioning, or brief explicitly avoids comparison.

### `before_after` (Type B)
- **Include if:** proof_inventory.has_before_afters = Y AND ≥3 before/after sets available.
- **Exclude if:** No before/afters exist. Section creates expectation gap without content to fill it.

### `upsell_bundle` (Type B)
- **Include if:** Product has clear companion products, multi-pack options, or subscription upgrade. Brief mentions bundle deals.
- **Exclude if:** Single SKU, no related products, or ecomm platform doesn't support upsell modules.

### `related_products` (Type B)
- **Include if:** Client has a product catalog of 3+ relevant related products.
- **Exclude if:** Single-product brand or unrelated catalog.

## Quality Criteria
- Every optional section in the template has an explicit INCLUDE or EXCLUDE decision
- Every decision has a specific rationale (not "it seemed right")
- Urgency/scarcity decision explicitly cites whether justification exists
- No section left as "undecided" or "TBD"

## Failure Modes
- Brief doesn't contain proof inventory info → Default to EXCLUDE for proof-dependent sections, flag for human review
- Urgency section: if justification is ambiguous → EXCLUDE with note "Insufficient justification found — excluded to protect credibility"
