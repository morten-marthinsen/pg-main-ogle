# 3.1 — Social Proof Volume Audit

**Skill:** PDP-05-social-proof-review-system
**Position:** Layer 3, Microskill 1
**Purpose:** Verify minimum review counts per section, validate star distribution realism against the Realism Table, check coverage across review system components (histogram, UGC, featured reviews, general pool, Q&A), and flag any gaps that would undermine trust. This is the quantitative health check — does this review system have ENOUGH content to be credible?
**Effort Level:** medium
**Dependencies:** `input-verification.md` (0.1), `review-system-architecture.md` (1.1), `ugc-carousel-architecture.md` (1.2), `review-histogram-spec.md` (1.3), `keyword-filter-spec.md` (1.4), `featured-reviews.md` (2.1), `keyword-topics.md` (2.2), `reviewer-personas.md` (2.3), `qa-section-architecture.md` (2.4), `review-card-template.md` (2.5)
**Output:** `volume-audit.md`

## Execution Logic

**Step 1:** Define minimum volume thresholds by scope:

| Component | Full | Standard | Minimal | Generation Only |
|-----------|------|----------|---------|-----------------|
| Featured reviews | 4-5 | 3-4 | 3 | 3-5 |
| General review pool (specified) | 30-50 | 15-30 | 10-15 | 15-30 |
| Total review count displayed | 100+ | 50-100 | 10-50 | 50-100 |
| UGC photos (if available) | 8-12 | 6-10 | 4-6 | 6-10 |
| UGC videos (if available) | 5-7 | 3-5 | 0-3 | 3-5 |
| Q&A pairs | 10-15 | 8-12 | 0 (hidden) | 8-15 |
| Keyword filter chips | 6-8 | 5-7 | 5-6 | 5-8 |
| Reviewer personas | 7-8 | 5-6 | 4-5 | 5-6 |

**Step 2:** Count actual volumes from Layer 2 outputs:
- Count featured reviews in `featured-reviews.md`
- Count general review pool references in `review-system-architecture.md`
- Count UGC slots specified in `ugc-carousel-architecture.md`
- Count Q&A pairs in `qa-section-architecture.md`
- Count keyword chips in `keyword-topics.md`
- Count personas in `reviewer-personas.md`

**Step 3:** Compare actual volumes against thresholds. For each component:
- PASS: actual >= minimum threshold for scope
- FAIL: actual < minimum threshold — flag with specific shortfall count

**Step 4:** Validate star distribution realism. Extract distribution from `review-histogram-spec.md` and compare against the Realism Table:

| Product Quality Signal | 5-star | 4-star | 3-star | 2-star | 1-star | Avg Rating |
|----------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| Excellent product | 55-65% | 20-25% | 8-12% | 3-5% | 2-4% | 4.3-4.6 |
| Good product | 40-50% | 25-30% | 12-18% | 5-8% | 3-6% | 4.0-4.3 |
| Average product | 25-35% | 25-30% | 20-25% | 10-15% | 5-10% | 3.5-4.0 |

Checks:
- 5-star percentage within expected band? (FAIL if > 65% or < 25%)
- 1-star percentage present? (FAIL if 0% — looks censored)
- Average rating within realistic range? (FAIL if > 4.7 or < 3.0)
- Distribution shape plausible? (FAIL if uniform or bimodal without explanation)
- FORBIDDEN: 95%+ five-star, 0% one-star, perfectly uniform distribution

**Step 5:** Check coverage completeness:
- Does the histogram cover all 5 star ratings (including zero-count bars)? [Y/N]
- Do keyword chips cover 60%+ of research-derived topics? [Y/N]
- Do featured reviews span at least 3 different reviewer personas? [Y/N]
- Does the Q&A section cover all 5 question types (efficacy, usage, shipping, comparison, ingredient)? [Y/N]
- Are all mandatory archetype personas represented (Enthusiast, Analytical, Skeptic Convert, Practical, Long-Term)? [Y/N]

**Step 6:** Check for problematic patterns:
- All featured reviews same star rating? (FAIL — need variety, at least one 4-star)
- All featured reviews from same age group? (FAIL — need demographic spread)
- All Q&A pairs brand-answered only? (FAIL — at least 30% need community answers)
- UGC photos all from same reviewer? (FAIL — need diversity from 4+ unique customers)
- No 3-star-or-below reviews specified? (FAIL — merchant response section would be empty)

**Step 7:** Calculate volume audit score (0-10 scale):

| Dimension | Weight | Score Logic |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Featured review count | 15% | Met threshold = 10, shortfall proportional |
| General pool adequacy | 15% | Met threshold = 10, shortfall proportional |
| Star distribution realism | 25% | All bands within range = 10, per deviation -2 |
| Coverage completeness | 20% | All 5 checks pass = 10, per fail -2 |
| Diversity (personas, demographics, Q&A types) | 15% | All diversity checks pass = 10, per fail -2 |
| UGC adequacy | 10% | Met threshold = 10, omitted (no UGC available) = 7, shortfall proportional |

Volume audit score = weighted average. Minimum passing: 7.0/10.

**Step 8:** Document all findings, pass/fail per component, and the overall volume audit score.

## Quality Criteria
- All component volumes counted and compared against scope-specific thresholds
- Star distribution checked against Realism Table with specific band comparisons
- Coverage completeness checked across all 5 dimensions
- Problematic patterns identified (same-star featured, homogeneous demographics, brand-only Q&A)
- Volume audit score calculated with weighted dimensions
- Score >= 7.0 required for PASS
- All FAIL items have specific remediation actions documented

## Failure Modes
- Volume audit score < 7.0 -> HALT. Address all FAIL items before proceeding to Layer 4.
- Star distribution outside all Realism Table bands -> HALT. Regenerate distribution using the Realism Table.
- 0% one-star reviews -> HALT. Looks censored. Add realistic 1-star percentage.
- 95%+ five-star reviews -> HALT. Looks fake. Redistribute per Realism Table.
- All featured reviews same star rating -> HALT. Vary ratings (include at least one 4-star).
- No 3-star-or-below reviews -> HALT. Merchant response section requires at least some low-rating reviews.
- All Q&A brand-answered only -> HALT. Add community answers to at least 30% of Q&A pairs.
- Featured reviews from fewer than 3 personas -> HALT. Increase persona diversity.
