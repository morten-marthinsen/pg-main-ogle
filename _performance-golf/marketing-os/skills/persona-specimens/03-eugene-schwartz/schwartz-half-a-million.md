## Schwartz Techniques Visible

- **Challenge/dare structure:** "Do You Have the Courage" -- transforms a desire (money) into a test of character
- **Identity play:** Makes the reader prove they're WORTHY, not just interested
- **Specific income claim:** "Half a Million Dollars Per Year" -- large enough to be transformative, specific enough to be believable
- **Question format:** Forces internal dialogue -- the reader must answer YES or NO
- **Ego engagement:** Saying "no" admits cowardice; saying "yes" compels reading further
- **Implicit qualification:** Only certain people deserve this; are YOU one of them?
- **Business opportunity market:** 1967 -- the era of mail-order opportunity seekers
