## Headline

"Should every corporation buy its president a Rolls-Royce?"

## Context & Background

Of all the ads in Ogilvy's campaign for Rolls-Royce, this one is the most unique in that it targets businesses vs. individual businessmen and their families. The objective was to create an ad that justified the car as a sound business investment. Ogilvy highlighted multiple points to make his argument including the idea of the car being better for their president's health and the company's brand image being elevated because of its association with Rolls-Royce. Unlike other ads in this campaign, the larger limousine model is also advertised, marketed as being able to "accommodate a whole board of directors in club-like comfort and quiet."

## Key Specimen Value

- Same product, completely different angle: B2B targeting for a consumer luxury product
- Question headline that reframes the purchase as a business decision, not a personal indulgence
- Health angle: the car is better for the president's wellbeing
- Status transfer: company brand elevated by association with Rolls-Royce brand
- Shows Ogilvy's ability to find new audiences for the same product

**Headline:**

Should every corporation buy its president a Rolls-Royce?

**Subhead:**

There is much to be said for it. It is a prudent investment. It enhances the public image of the company. And rank is entitled to its rewards.

**Body Copy:**

A great many of the Rolls-Royce and Bentley cars sold in England are sold to corporations for the use and benefit of their top executives. "The Bentley is a company perk," is a saying well observed by British businessmen.

What makes the Rolls-Royce the best investment car in the world? There are several factors:

**Longest guarantee**

The Rolls-Royce is guaranteed for three years—twelve times as long as the usual motor car warranty.

A good part of the cost can often be written off in five years or less. The car will then be in the infancy of its career. When driven in the normal way, it will last for many more years.

The Rolls-Royce obviates the common practice of trading in cars to avoid the prospect for high repair bills. This costs you money each time a change is made. With the money that is wasted in a change of ordinary cars you could have bought a Rolls-Royce.

**No "planned obsolescence"**

The exterior of a Rolls-Royce is not changed in appearance from year to year. Only an expert can tell whether it was manufactured this year or last.

Rolls-Royce dealers throughout the country, service its perfection.

**Your president will live longer**

Send for Rolls-Royce to fetch your president at the airport the next time he will reach the office in better shape. During the day, you can immunize him from wailing his taxis in street congestion. At night, send him home in the Rolls-Royce; he can be his own chauffeur, put up a map, nap. Do these things, and let the Rolls-Royce take care of the president. He will also live five years longer.

**A safe car**

On bad roads, in heavy traffic, the Rolls-Royce can be handled like a sports car. It will not normally be driven by a chauffeur. But such driving shows the fine safety ability of this noble car to perfection.

The heater box is tipped in a fine precision instrument. There are three independent brake linkages. Should one fail (an unlikely contingency), the others will keep the car under perfect control.

**A source of contentment**

There is satisfaction in owning such an exquisite piece of machinery. To handle a Rolls-Royce, to look at it, to use it oneself in business, can add to pleasure and contentment.

But the automobile of a company should not be denied. After all, a company car for executive showing at Rolls-Royce can be purchased at a Bentley. It is exactly the same car, except for the radiator. It costs less $300.

**Why buy a Rolls-Royce?**

The price of a new Rolls-Royce Silver Cloud II is $14,395. Before you decide that this is more than you can afford to invest, consider the following arithmetic:

1. Annual depreciation is low and you can keep your Rolls-Royce or Bentley for years if you choose.
2. A Rolls-Royce never becomes out of style. You are assured of a continuity of styling that protects your investment.
3. Every Rolls-Royce and Bentley engine and chassis is guaranteed for three years—twelve times as long as most American cars.
4. You can pay by installments over one, two, or three years. One bank allows six years.

If you would like more information, write Rolls-Royce, 30 Rockefeller Plaza, New York 20. If you would like to try driving a Rolls-Royce or Bentley, get in touch with one of the dealers listed on page 106.

**Paid for quality.** Of all luxury cars, it is the least ostentatious.

**Free from exhibitionism**

An executive's car, like his office, should subtly influence public opinion toward his company. The Rolls-Royce inspires trust, confidence and good taste.
