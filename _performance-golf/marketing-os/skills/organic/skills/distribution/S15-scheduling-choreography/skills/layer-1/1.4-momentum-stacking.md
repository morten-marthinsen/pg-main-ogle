# 1.4 Momentum Stacking Protocol

## Purpose
Define rules for responding to viral moments and building content series momentum.

---

## Input Requirements

From Layer 0:
- `0.2-teachings-loaded.yaml` (momentum teachings)

From Layer 1:
- `1.2-content-type-timing.yaml` (content schedule)

---

## Execution Protocol

### Step 1: Define Viral Response Protocol

```yaml
viral_threshold:
  metric: "[e.g., engagement rate, views vs average]"
  threshold: "[e.g., 3x average performance]"
  timeframe: "[e.g., first 60 minutes]"

viral_response_sequence:
  within_30_minutes:
    - "Increase engagement intensity (S16)"
    - "Post supporting story/content"
    - "Reply to high-value comments"
    - "Notify network for amplification (S17)"

  within_2_hours:
    - "Create follow-up content referencing success"
    - "Cross-post tease to other platforms"
    - "Screenshot/celebrate in stories"

  within_24_hours:
    - "Create Part 2 or response video"
    - "Compile and respond to top comments"
    - "Adjust next week's calendar to capitalize"
```

### Step 2: Content Series Stacking

Identify related content pieces and design posting sequence:

```yaml
series_stacking:
  series_id: "[ID]"
  related_content: []

  posting_strategy:
    spacing: "[24-48 hours between pieces]"
    escalation_pattern:
      part_1: "Introduce concept (Awareness)"
      part_2: "Deepen with examples (Engagement)"
      part_3: "Actionable framework (Conversion)"

    callback_hooks:
      - "Reference previous part performance"
      - "Use comments from Part 1 in Part 2"
      - "Create anticipation with previews"
```

### Step 3: Weekly Momentum Rhythm

Design weekly content rhythm:

```yaml
weekly_rhythm:
  monday: "Week opener - strong hook content"
  tuesday: "Educational/value content"
  wednesday: "Community engagement focus"
  thursday: "Controversial/opinion content"
  friday: "Lighter/entertaining content"
  saturday: "Repurposed best performers"
  sunday: "Behind-the-scenes/authentic content"
```
