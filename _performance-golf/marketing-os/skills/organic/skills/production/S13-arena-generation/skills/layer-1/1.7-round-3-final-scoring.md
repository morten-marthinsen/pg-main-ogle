# Microskill 1.7: Round 3 Final Scoring
Skill: S13 Arena Generation | Layer: 1 | Model: claude-opus-4.6 | Version: 1.0

## Purpose
Compile final Arena scores, calculate composite score, identify improvement opportunities for synthesis.

## Input
s13-1.6-round-3-scores.yaml, s13-1.4-round-2-scores.yaml, s13-1.2-round-1-scores.yaml

## Process
### MUST
1. Compile all 7 persona final scores
2. Calculate composite score (average across personas)
3. Identify consensus strengths (6+ personas agree)
4. Identify consensus weaknesses (6+ personas agree)
5. Flag synthesis opportunities (combine strengths, address weaknesses)
6. Document score evolution across rounds

### NEVER
- Skip composite calculation
- Ignore consensus patterns
- Proceed without synthesis framing

## Output
**File:** `s13-1.7-final-scores.yaml`

```yaml
final_arena_scores:
  timestamp: 2026-03-05T16:15:00Z

  persona_scores:
    volume_machine: 6
    value_architect: 8
    virality_engineer: 7
    community_builder: 6
    brand_purist: 7
    algorithm_hacker: 7
    storyteller: 5

  composite_score: 6.6

  consensus_strengths:
    - "Value density high (Value Architect 8, Brand Purist 7)"
    - "Brand alignment strong (Brand Purist 7)"

  consensus_weaknesses:
    - "Story resonance weak (Storyteller 5, Community Builder 6)"
    - "Volume sustainability concerns (Volume Machine 6)"

  synthesis_opportunities:
    - "Strengthen narrative arc (address Storyteller weakness)"
    - "Add emotional hook (elevate Community Builder score)"
    - "Simplify for scale (address Volume Machine concern)"

  score_evolution:
    round_1_avg: 7.3
    round_2_avg: 6.9
    round_3_avg: 6.6
    trend: "Declining — Critic challenges surfaced weaknesses"
```
