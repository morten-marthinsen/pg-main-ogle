# 1.3 Narrative Arc Planning

## Purpose
Design multi-day/week story arcs that span personas. Each persona tells their piece of a larger narrative. Requires timeline coordination, perspective alignment, and consistency verification.

## Model Assignment
**Model:** claude-opus-4-20250514

## Input Requirements
- Persona Bibles (backstory, personality, voice)
- Network calendar sync (from 1.1)
- Teaching summary (narrative sequencing)

## Execution Protocol

### MUST
- Design narrative arc structure: beginning/middle/end across multiple personas and days
- Assign narrative pieces: which persona tells which part, why they're qualified
- Create timeline coordination: who posts when, what knowledge they have at each point
- Ensure perspective alignment: how each persona views events from their angle
- Verify narrative consistency: no contradictions, timeline conflicts, or knowledge gaps
- Design discovery moments: how audiences piece together full story
- Plan story resolution: how arc concludes, what audience learns
- Include fallback plans if one persona's content underperforms

### NEVER
- Create narrative arcs without timeline mapping
- Allow perspective contradictions across personas
- Skip knowledge boundary verification (what each persona can know when)
- Design arcs that require all personas participate (need flexibility)
- Ignore how arc respects each persona's niche and voice
- Forget resolution planning

## Output
**Filename:** `narrative-arc-planning.md`

**Format:** Markdown narrative coordination

**Minimum Size:** 5KB

**Required Sections:**
1. Narrative Arc Structures (multi-day story concepts, 3-5 arc templates)
2. Persona Piece Assignments (who tells what, rationale for assignment)
3. Timeline Coordination (posting sequence, knowledge progression)
4. Perspective Alignment (how each persona views events from their angle)
5. Consistency Verification (conflict check, timeline check, knowledge boundaries)
6. Discovery Moments (how audience pieces together full story)
7. Fallback Plans (if one persona's content underperforms)

## Quality Gates
- [ ] Narrative arcs include timeline coordination
- [ ] Persona assignments respect niche and voice
- [ ] Perspective alignment verified (no contradictions)
- [ ] Consistency check complete (timeline, knowledge boundaries)
- [ ] Discovery moments designed (audience engagement)
- [ ] Fallback plans included
