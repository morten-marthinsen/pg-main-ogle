# 1.4 Growth Strategy

## Purpose
Design organic growth tactics per persona × platform. Include specific hashtag strategies, engagement tactics, collaboration plans, and trending topic participation. Must be tactical, not generic advice.

## Model Assignment
**Model:** claude-opus-4-20250514

## Input Requirements
- Platform selections (from 1.1)
- Persona Bibles (niche, archetype, voice)
- Teaching summary (growth frameworks, algorithm insights)
- Content calendar frameworks (from 1.3)

## Execution Protocol

### MUST
- Design hashtag strategy per persona × platform: specific hashtags with follower counts, mix of broad/niche/branded
- Create engagement tactics: specific accounts to engage with (names, not types), when/how to engage
- Plan collaboration strategy: types of creators to collaborate with, specific targets if possible, pitch angles
- Define trending topic participation approach: how persona joins conversations while staying on-brand
- Include platform-specific growth mechanics: Twitter threads, LinkedIn carousels, TikTok duets, Instagram Reels
- Set growth milestones: follower targets by timeframe with tactics per milestone
- Design measurement plan: which metrics matter, how to track, when to pivot

### NEVER
- Use generic advice like "use relevant hashtags" or "engage with audience"
- Create hashtag lists without follower count research
- Define engagement tactics without specific account targets
- Skip collaboration planning
- Ignore platform-specific growth features
- Set growth targets without tactical path to achieve them
- Forget measurement and optimization plan

## Output
**Filename:** `growth-strategy-tactics.md`

**Format:** Markdown growth tactics playbook

**Minimum Size:** 6KB

**Required Sections:**
1. Per-Persona × Platform Hashtag Strategy (specific tags, follower counts, rotation schedule)
2. Engagement Tactics (specific accounts to engage, how/when, value-add approach)
3. Collaboration Strategy (creator types, specific targets, pitch angles, partnership formats)
4. Trending Topic Participation (how to join conversations on-brand)
5. Platform-Specific Growth Mechanics (features to leverage per platform)
6. Growth Milestones (follower targets, timeframe, tactics per stage)
7. Measurement Plan (metrics, tracking, optimization triggers)

## Quality Gates
- [ ] Hashtag strategy includes specific tags with follower counts
- [ ] Engagement tactics name specific accounts (10-20 minimum)
- [ ] Collaboration strategy includes target types and pitch angles
- [ ] Platform-specific mechanics leveraged (not generic advice)
- [ ] Growth milestones with tactical roadmap
- [ ] Measurement plan defines metrics and optimization process

Feeds into: 1.5 Cross-Promotion Map (growth strategy + cross-promotion = network effects)
