# 1.5 Voice & Hook Strategy

## Purpose
Provide specific voice guidance and approved hooks for content creators. This ensures brand consistency and proven engagement patterns.

---

## Input Requirements

From Layer 1:
- `1.1-synthesis.yaml` (BVF and HLF context)

---

## Execution Protocol

### Step 1: Voice Summary for Campaign

Distill BVF into actionable creator guidance:

```yaml
voice_summary:
  tone_for_campaign: |
    [2-3 sentences describing how to sound]
    Example: "Use conversational authority—like a knowledgeable friend,
    not a guru. Direct address ('you') with empathy. Confident without
    being preachy. Think: helpful coach, not salesman."

  key_phrases_to_use:
    - "[phrase 1]"
    - "[phrase 2]"
    - "[phrase 3]"
    # 7-10 phrases that should appear regularly

  phrases_to_avoid:
    - "[banned phrase 1]"
    - "[banned phrase 2]"
    - "[banned phrase 3]"
    # 5-10 phrases that violate voice

  energy_direction: |
    [High/medium/low with context]
    Example: "Medium-high energy. Enthusiastic without being manic.
    Match the excitement level of someone sharing a discovery with a
    friend, not a hype man."

  personality_notes: |
    [Campaign-specific personality adjustments]
    Example: "For this campaign, lean into the 'myth-buster' persona.
    Call out bad advice directly but without attacking specific people."
```

---

### Step 2: Approved Hook Strategy

Select 3-5 hook types to use repeatedly:

```yaml
hook_strategy:
  primary_hook_types:
    - type: "Pain Amplification"
      when_to_use: "Awareness content about common frustrations"
      example: "You've been doing [X] wrong your entire life..."

    - type: "Authority Challenge"
      when_to_use: "Myth-busting content"
      example: "Every golf pro says [X]. Here's why they're wrong..."

    - type: "Transformation Reveal"
      when_to_use: "Proof and case study content"
      example: "I couldn't [X] 30 days ago. Now I [Y]. Here's what changed..."

    # [3-5 total]
```

---

### Step 3: Pre-Approved Hooks

Provide 10-15 specific hooks ready to use:

```yaml
specific_hooks_approved:
  - hook: "You've been told to [X]. Here's why that's making it worse..."
    for_content_type: "Myth-busting Reels"
    pillar: "Performance Fundamentals"

  - hook: "3 years. 47 students. Same result: [transformation]. Here's the pattern..."
    for_content_type: "Case study content"
    pillar: "Results & Proof"

  # [10-15 total, specific and ready to use]
```

---

### Step 4: Hook Testing Plan

How hooks will be A/B tested:

```yaml
hook_testing_plan:
  approach: |
    [How testing will work]
    Example: "Test 2 hook variations per week on similar content.
    Track first-hour engagement rate. Winner becomes default for
    that content type. Losing hook gets retired or reworked."

  metrics_tracked:
    - "Hook view-through rate (% who watch past 3 seconds)"
    - "Engagement rate in first hour"
    - "Share rate (indicator of hook compelling enough to share)"

  testing_schedule: |
    [When testing happens]
    Example: "Weeks 1-2: Test Pain Amplification vs Authority Challenge
    Weeks 3-4: Test winning hook vs Transformation Reveal
    Ongoing: Test new hooks monthly, retire bottom 20%"
```

---

### Step 5: Voice Check Protocol

How to ensure content stays on-voice:

```yaml
voice_check_protocol:
  before_publishing:
    - [ ] "Read script out loud—does it sound like me?"
    - [ ] "Check for banned words/phrases"
    - [ ] "Energy level matches guidance"
    - [ ] "Tone is [specified tone], not [off-voice tone]"

  if_off_voice:
    action: "Rework before publishing—voice violations damage brand"
```
