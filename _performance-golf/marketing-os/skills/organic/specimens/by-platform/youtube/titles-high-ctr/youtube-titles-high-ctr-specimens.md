# YouTube Titles — High-CTR Specimen Bank

> 50+ title specimens from videos that achieved outsized click-through rates. Each specimen is dissected for pattern, word count, keyword strategy, and the psychological mechanism driving clicks.

---

## How to Use This File

1. **Identify patterns** that match your content type and niche.
2. **Study the mechanism** — understand WHY each title works, not just WHAT it says.
3. **Adapt the formula** — never copy titles verbatim; adapt the structure to your topic.
4. **Cross-reference** the playbook (`youtube_titles_high_ctr_playbook.md`) for deeper strategy.
5. **Test** — run your adapted title against 2 alternatives before publishing.

### TI-001: How to Invest in Index Funds (Complete Beginner's Guide)
- **Channel:** Graham Stephan
- **Views Estimate:** 4.2M+
- **Title Pattern:** How-To + Parenthetical qualifier
- **Word Count:** 9
- **Keyword Position:** "Invest" at word 3, "Index Funds" at words 4-5
- **Power Words:** "Complete," "Beginner's Guide"
- **Why It Works:** "How to" signals clear utility. "Index Funds" is a high-search-volume keyword placed early. "(Complete Beginner's Guide)" in parenthetical does three things: (1) reassures beginners they won't be lost, (2) signals comprehensive value, and (3) adds a long-tail keyword. The title promises a specific, deliverable outcome.
