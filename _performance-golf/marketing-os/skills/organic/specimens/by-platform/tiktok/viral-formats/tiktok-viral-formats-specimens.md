## Key Patterns Across All Specimens

1. **Dual-Track Attention**: The highest-performing formats layer two engagement channels (visual + narrative, audio + text, process + story)
2. **Participation Architecture**: Formats that invite replication (dances, sounds, templates) achieve 3-10x the views of consume-only formats
3. **Emotional Polarity**: Content at emotional extremes (awe, humor, anger, warmth) outperforms neutral-tone content by 5-8x in shares
4. **Language Independence**: 4 of the top 10 specimens use zero spoken language, enabling global reach
5. **Specificity Paradox**: Hyper-specific scenarios ("Italian grandma + Dominos") outperform generic scenarios because specificity triggers recognition in broader audiences
