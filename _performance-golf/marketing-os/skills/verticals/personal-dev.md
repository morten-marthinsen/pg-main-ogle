## Personal Dev Market Context

### Audience Profile
- Adults 25-55, more gender-balanced than other verticals
- Range from beginners seeking change to experienced self-improvers
- Sub-niches: meditation, communication/voice, productivity, relationships, confidence, habits
- Often burned by "guru" promises — increasing skepticism
- High emotional investment — personal identity tied to growth journey
- Willingness to pay for transformation, not information

### Market Sophistication
- Varies dramatically by sub-niche:
  - Meditation: Stage 3-4 (mechanism-stage — neuroscience differentiation works)
  - Productivity: Stage 4-5 (highly saturated — identification-stage)
  - Communication/Voice: Stage 2-3 (education-stage still viable)
  - General self-help: Stage 5 (maximum sophistication — need radical differentiation)

### Proof Architecture (Personal Dev-Specific)
- Before/after transformation stories (specific, detailed, time-bounded)
- Neuroscience/psychology research (peer-reviewed, credible journals)
- Expert credentials (degrees, published research, clinical experience)
- Student/practitioner results with specifics
- Celebrity/notable person endorsements
- Measurable outcomes where possible (test scores, performance metrics, biometric data)
