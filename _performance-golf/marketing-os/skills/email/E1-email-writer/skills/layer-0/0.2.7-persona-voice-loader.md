### Selection Priority for This Skill

Since this skill uses `generative_full_draft` arena mode, prioritize **actual selling copy specimens** that show each writer's voice, vocabulary, rhythm, and persuasion techniques in the specific type of copy this skill generates.

Email writing is the MOST CONVERSATIONAL form of direct response copy. Unlike sales pages or VSLs, emails must feel like a one-to-one communication -- a person writing to another person. This makes voice calibration critically important because any "copywriter sound" or "formal writing" feel will destroy the intimate register that makes email effective. Halbert's newsletters and Boron Letters are the gold standard here -- they were literally personal letters that happened to sell. Bencivenga's Marketing Maxims are structured as personal essays to subscribers. Makepeace's controls show how to maintain relationship while selling. For email, prioritize specimens that demonstrate conversational authority -- writers who sound like real humans, not copywriters.
