# 2.8: Round 2 Targeted Revision

## Purpose
Same revision protocol as Round 1. Each persona receives their Round 2 critique and revises ONLY the identified weakness. Revision addresses the specific fix direction. Persona perspective maintained. Maximum scope: targeted fix.

## Inputs
- 2.6-round2-persona-evaluation output (Round 2 evaluations)
- 2.7-round2-adversarial-critique output (Round 2 critiques)

## Process
```
STEP 1: Load Round 2 evaluations and critiques
  - READ: layer-2-outputs/2.6-round2-persona-evaluation.md
  - READ: layer-2-outputs/2.7-round2-adversarial-critique.md

STEP 2: FOR EACH concept:
  FOR EACH persona (7 total):

    A. LOAD persona's Round 2 evaluation
    B. LOAD Critic's Round 2 critique for this evaluation
    C. IDENTIFY specific weakness and fix direction

    D. REVISE the identified weakness ONLY
      - Targeted fix: address the specific criterion weakness
      - DO NOT rewrite entire evaluation
      - MAINTAIN persona perspective

    E. RE-SCORE affected criterion only
      - Update critiqued criterion score
      - Keep other scores unchanged
      - Recalculate weighted total

    F. DOCUMENT revision (what changed, why, score impact)

CRITICAL RULES:
  - Same targeted revision protocol as Round 1 (2.3)
  - Persona perspective MUST be maintained
  - Only critiqued criterion score changes
  - Effort level: max
```
