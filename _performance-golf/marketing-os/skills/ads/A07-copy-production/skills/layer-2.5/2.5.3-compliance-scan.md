# 2.5.3: Compliance Scan

## Purpose
Scan every variant for compliance violations based on vertical profile constraints and platform-specific ad policies. Health ads have different compliance requirements than finance or golf ads. Platform policies vary (Meta restricts before/after images, TikTok restricts certain health claims). Every variant must pass compliance before entering the variant matrix.

## Inputs
- All variants (from Layer 1 and Layer 2 outputs)
- Vertical profile compliance constraints (from 0.0.1)
- Platform ad policies (from 0.5)
- Variant inventory (from 2.4)

## Process
```
STEP 1: LOAD compliance constraint set.
  From 0.0.1 (vertical profile):
    - Vertical-specific claim restrictions
    - Required disclaimers
    - Banned language patterns

  From platform policies:
    - Meta: no before/after images, health claim restrictions, "results may vary"
    - TikTok: community guidelines on health/finance claims
    - YouTube: advertiser-friendly content policies
    - Google: ad policy violations for specific verticals

STEP 2: For EACH variant in the inventory:

  CHECK A: VERTICAL COMPLIANCE
    Health vertical:
      - No disease cure claims ("cures diabetes" is VIOLATION)
      - No unapproved medical claims
      - "Results may vary" present where needed
      - No guarantees of specific health outcomes
    Finance vertical:
      - Required regulatory language present
      - No "guaranteed returns" claims
      - Risk disclosures present
      - No specific profit predictions without disclaimers
    Golf vertical:
      - Distance claims cite conditions ("in testing" / "average")
      - No performance guarantees
    Technology vertical:
      - Technical claims substantiated
      - No misleading comparisons

  CHECK B: PLATFORM COMPLIANCE
    - Meta: before/after restrictions, health claim limits
    - TikTok: community guideline alignment
    - YouTube: advertiser-friendly compliance
    - Google: keyword and claim policies

  CHECK C: GENERAL AD COMPLIANCE
    - No misleading claims
    - No unsubstantiated statistics
    - Testimonials properly attributed (if used)
    - Price claims accurate

STEP 3: For each violation found:
  - Identify the variant
  - Classify the violation type
  - Specify the violating text (verbatim)
  - Recommend specific fix

STEP 4: PRODUCE the Compliance Audit table.
```
