### Specimen D01
- **Brand:** Gundry MD (MCT Wellness)
- **Persona:** DR Strategist
- **Platform:** Meta (Facebook/YouTube)
- **Vertical:** Health/Supplement
- **Format:** Video (long-form VSL, 30-60 min)
- **Hook Type:** A6: Secret/Insider
- **Framework:** Edutainment (Teach-Then-Sell)
- **Estimated Run Duration:** 3+ years continuous (multiple variants)
- **Source:** cdn.gundrymd.com text version (verbatim)
- **Confidence:** VERBATIM

#### Verbatim Copy

**Hook (Opening):**
"Are you drinking coffee wrong?"

**Mechanism Section (Core Script):**
Introduces "Caloric Bypass" as the central mechanism — a biological process allegedly activated through specific foods. Dr. Gundry explains mitochondria as cellular "power plants" that process energy, then describes how certain compounds signal cells to waste excess calories rather than store them as fat.

Three "Mito-Boosters" presented:
1. Polyphenols — compounds in dark berries and spices
2. Ketones — produced through fasting or specific foods
3. MCTs (medium-chain triglycerides) — fats that convert directly to ketones

**CTA (Verbatim):**
"Click the 'Next Step' button below, and let's get started. When you get to our order page, you'll see order options for MCT Wellness on the right side of the page."

**Offer Stack:**
- Single jar: $49.95
- 3-jar package: $44.95/jar with free shipping
- 6-jar package: up to $165 off retail

**Guarantee (Verbatim):**
"If you're unsatisfied with the results for any reason whatsoever, you'll get a complete refund of your purchase price" — 90-day window.

#### Why This Specimen
This is the Craig Clemens / Gundry model at its purest — authority figure + named mechanism + education-first structure. The hook uses a daily habit question (coffee) to create an information gap. The mechanism ("Caloric Bypass") follows the health DR playbook: named, simple, science-backed. The CTA is specific and instructional ("click the Next Step button"). Multiple price tiers with descending per-unit cost create the classic DR offer architecture. This is the archetype for the DR Strategist persona in health/supplement vertical.

### Specimen D03
- **Brand:** Dollar Shave Club
- **Persona:** DR Strategist
- **Platform:** YouTube
- **Vertical:** DTC (Grooming)
- **Format:** Video (90 seconds)
- **Hook Type:** A5: Contrarian
- **Framework:** AIDA
- **Estimated Run Duration:** Launched March 2012, ran continuously, 28M+ views
- **Source:** manifestowriting.com full script (verbatim)
- **Confidence:** VERBATIM

#### Verbatim Script

"Hi, I'm Mike. Founder of dollar shave club dot com. What is dollar shave club dot com? Well, for a dollar a month we send high quality razors right to your door. Yeah, a dollar. Are the blades any good? No. Our blades are fucking great.

Each razor has stainless steel blades and aloe vera lubricating strip and a pivot head. It's so gentle a toddler could use it.

We are dollar shave club dot com and the party is on.

SUPER: Shave time. Shave money."

#### Why This Specimen
The most important DR video ad of the modern era. Every second serves the conversion path: (1) Authority via founder presence, (2) Price anchor ($20/mo vs $1/mo), (3) Enemy framing (brand name razors with unnecessary features), (4) Benefit reframe (savings), (5) CTA (the website). The contrarian hook ("Are the blades any good? No.") is a masterclass in pattern interrupt that leads to a stronger claim. The humor serves the sale — it never exists for its own sake. This is the DR Strategist's gold standard for video ad conversion architecture.

The closing CTA includes price ("it's $29") which pre-qualifies buyers and reduces friction. The numbered format also increases rewatchability (viewers often rewatch to catch all 3 points).
