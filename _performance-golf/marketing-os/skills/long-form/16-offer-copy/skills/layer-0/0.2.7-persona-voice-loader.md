### Selection Priority for This Skill

Since this skill uses generative_full_draft arena mode, prioritize actual selling copy specimens that show each writer's voice, vocabulary, rhythm, and persuasion techniques in the specific type of copy this skill generates.

For offer copy, prioritize specimens showing offer construction and price psychology. Bencivenga's 100-seminar-letter is essential — it is a 46-page high-ticket offer with qualification selling, 7 Master Secrets, and sophisticated value stacking that demonstrates how to present a premium offer without desperation. Makepeace controls show multi-buy cascades and closing momentum with urgency that builds through the offer section. Ogilvy ads show premium value positioning and how to frame price as reasonable for quality. Schwartz ads show offer urgency framing calibrated to market sophistication. Clemens VSLs show supplement offer stacks with mechanism-backed value demonstrations. Halbert's Boron Letters show personal-stake offer framing and direct request psychology.
