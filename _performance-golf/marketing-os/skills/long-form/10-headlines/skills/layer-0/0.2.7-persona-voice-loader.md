### Selection Priority for This Skill

Since this skill uses `generative_full_draft` arena mode, prioritize **actual selling copy specimens** that show each writer's voice, vocabulary, rhythm, and persuasion techniques in the specific type of copy this skill generates.

The headline skill is the MOST specimen-dependent skill in the entire engine — headlines are pure voice expression compressed into a single line or phrase. Every word carries maximum weight. For headlines, Schwartz's 204-headline-compendium is essential as a pattern library showing how the greatest headline writer in history structured attention-grabbing first lines across dozens of niches. Makepeace's controls show his Six Maxims in action through their headlines and subheadlines. Halbert's newsletters demonstrate raw hook power — the ability to stop a reader cold with a single opening. Ogilvy's ads contain some of the most legendary headlines ever written (Rolls-Royce "At 60 miles an hour...", Hathaway shirt man). Bencivenga's homepage sales letter has a masterfully crafted headline that promises specific value. Because headlines are the most compressed form of copy, voice calibration here matters more than in any other skill.
