# Copy Editing Checklist

Before publishing any content, run through this checklist to ensure it's on-brand and effective.

## The 11-Point Copy Checklist

### 1. Did I name the transformation?

Don't just talk about "improvement." Call out the shift: from frustration to confidence, confusion to clarity.

**Example:**
- ❌ "Get better at golf with our training system"
- ✓ "From standing over every shot second-guessing yourself to knowing exactly what to do before you even address the ball"

---

### 2. Did I name the specific emotion?

Don't describe around it. Name it: frustrated, embarrassed, defeated, panicked, relieved, confident, fired up.

**Example:**
- ❌ "You're not happy with your game"
- ✓ "That frustrated walk back to the cart after you chunk another wedge shot that should've been easy"

---

### 3. Did I include physical sensations?

The body knows: tight chest, death grip on the club, shoulders climbing, stomach dropping, jaw clenched.

**Example:**
- ❌ "You feel nervous before important shots"
- ✓ "Your hands are squeezing that grip so tight you can feel the tension creeping up your forearms before you even start your backswing"

---

### 4. Did I put them in a specific moment?

Name the actual situation. Saturday morning. First tee. Playing with clients. The moment it all goes sideways.

**Example:**
- ❌ "You'll drive better"
- ✓ "Saturday morning, 8:47am. Three foursomes waiting behind you. Driver in hand. And that little voice saying 'don't slice it into the woods like last time'"

---

### 5. Did I make progress feel attainable?

Focus on what's achievable, not perfect. Real gains. Permanent improvement.

**Example:**
- ❌ "Break 80 in just 3 weeks"
- ✓ "Your personal path to hitting 5 more fairways per round and cutting those three-putts in half—progress that shows up in your scores"

---

### 6. Did I emphasize personalization without tech jargon?

This isn't one-size-fits-all. It's THE RIGHT guidance for THEIR game.

**Example:**
- ❌ "Our AI-powered algorithm analyzes your biomechanics"
- ✓ "Your game improvement plan—built for where you are now and exactly what you need to work on next"

---

### 7. Did I lead with the feeling, then show the data?

Emotion first, proof second. Paint the magical moment, then back it up.

**Example:**
- ❌ "Increase your smash factor by 8% for 12 additional yards"
- ✓ "That pure contact that sends it 12 yards further than you've ever hit your 7-iron—and the data that shows you exactly how to find it again"

---

### 8. Did I name The Scattered Playbook as the enemy?

Call out the villain: conflicting YouTube tips, scattered magazine advice, random buddy recommendations.

**Example:**
- ❌ "Try a new approach to learning golf"
- ✓ "Done with YouTube videos that contradict each other? One says 'keep your head down,' another says 'rotate through'—meanwhile you're standing over the ball with 12 different swing thoughts"

---

### 9. Did I position PG1 as the weapon against confusion?

Make it clear: One Connected System defeats The Scattered Playbook.

**Example:**
- ❌ "Our comprehensive training system helps you improve"
- ✓ "Instead of 47 random tips fighting each other, you get ONE personalized path built for where your game is right now and where it needs to go next"

---

### 10. Did I use Brixton's voice?

Would Brixton say this? Encouraging without being rah-rah. Confident without being dismissive.

**Example:**
- ❌ "Utilize our comprehensive methodology to enhance performance metrics"
- ✓ "Look, you're better than your current scores show. Let's get you there with a clear path that actually makes sense"

---

### 11. Did I focus on loving the game?

The ultimate goal isn't just lower scores. It's the joy. The confidence. Looking forward to the next round.

**Example:**
- ❌ "Achieve your performance goals"
- ✓ "The best part? You'll actually start texting your buddies asking when the next tee time is instead of making excuses to skip Saturday morning"

---

## Forbidden Patterns

| Pattern | Why It's Wrong | What To Do Instead |
|---------|----------------|---------------------|
| Overhype ("CRUSH 50 YARDS!!!") | Creates distrust, feels fake | Focus on attainable progress |
| Tech jargon ("AI-powered ML algorithms") | Intimidating, not human | "Your personalized path" |
| Stern/dismissive tone | Breaks connection | Stay encouraging, never talk down |
| Golf clichés ("Take your game to the next level") | Dead, meaningless | Use specific, vivid language |
| Promises of perfection | Unrealistic, breeds skepticism | Progress, not perfection. Real gains |
| Robotic/clinical voice | Feels cold, impersonal | Brixton = encouraging + real |
| Data before feeling | Misses emotional connection | Lead with emotion, data justifies |
| Focus on pros | Wrong audience | This is for everyday golfers |

## Quick Application Guides

### Email Subject Lines
- Lead with signature phrases or emotional hooks
- Keep it short and punchy
- Test variations of key messaging themes

**Examples:**
- "Your straight shot to better golf"
- "Make progress permanent this season"
- "Less guesswork. More fireworks."

### Product Descriptions
Structure:
1. Emotional hook (the magical moment)
2. The benefit (what it does for their game)
3. How it works (personalized, data-driven, clear)
4. Call to action (confident, attainable)

### Social Posts
- Short, conversational, authentic
- Focus on magical moments and real progress
- Use signature phrases naturally
- Avoid overhype or clichés

**Tone Examples:**
- ✓ "That feeling when your drive splits the fairway exactly like you pictured it."
- ✓ "Progress you can feel. Results you can measure. Golf you can love."
- ❌ "CRUSH YOUR DRIVES LIKE NEVER BEFORE!!!" (too rah-rah)
- ❌ "Our AI-powered ML algorithms optimize your swing plane" (too technical)

---

## Final Brand Checklist

Before publishing anything, ask:

✅ **Voice:** Would Brixton say this?
✅ **Tone:** Is it Unwavering, Constructive, and Uplifting?
✅ **Audience:** Does it speak to everyday golfers like Ari?
✅ **Promise:** Does it support "Your path to playing better golf"?
✅ **Differentiators:** Does it reinforce our three uniques?
✅ **Balance:** Emotion + Data? Personal + Universal? Confident + Humble?

**The Ultimate Test:**
Does this help everyday golfers love their game?

If yes, you're on brand.
